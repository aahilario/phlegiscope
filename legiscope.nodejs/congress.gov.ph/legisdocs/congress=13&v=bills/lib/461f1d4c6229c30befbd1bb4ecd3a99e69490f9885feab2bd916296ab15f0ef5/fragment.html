House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02841
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REDUCING THE INCOME TAX RATES OF INDIVIDUAL TAXPAYERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CHAPTER III, SECTION 24 (A) (1) (c) OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to reduce the income tax rates of individual taxpayers to a minimum of 2.5% on income below PhP10,000 and a maximum of 32% of income above PhP12 million.
