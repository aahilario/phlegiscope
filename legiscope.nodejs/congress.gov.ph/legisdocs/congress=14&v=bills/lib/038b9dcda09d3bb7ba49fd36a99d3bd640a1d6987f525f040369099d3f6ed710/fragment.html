House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00341
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1185, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "FIRE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES", PROVIDING FOR EFFECTIVE IMPLEMENTATION THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : To strengthen the accountability of public officers charged to implement the Fire Code, this bill proposes to amend the Fire Code of the Philippines by providing administrative sanctions as well as criminal penalties to acts which result in loss of life and/or damage to property.
