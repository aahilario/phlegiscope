House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02855
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AMEND REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7900 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'HIGH VALUE CROPS DEVELOPMENT ACT OF 1995' AND FOR OTHER PURPOSE
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to include in the coverage of RA 7900 (High Value Crops Development Act of 1995) various-sized farm owners, not just small landholders, upland dwellers, lowland tenants, indigenous peoples, CARP beneficiaries, farmers organizations and cooperatives, among others.
