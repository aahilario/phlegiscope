FULL TITLE : AN ACT REORGANIZING THE NATIONAL FOOD AUTHORITY INTO THE NATIONAL FOOD CORPORATION AND THE FOOD DEVELOPMENT AND REGULATORY ADMINISTRATION, REDEFINING THEIR POWERS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Food Authority Reorganization Act of 2003
ABSTRACT : "National Food Authority Reorganization Act of 2003." The bill creates the Corporation with a P15 billion capitalization to maintain and manage the annual rice reserve or buffer stock of the government.
