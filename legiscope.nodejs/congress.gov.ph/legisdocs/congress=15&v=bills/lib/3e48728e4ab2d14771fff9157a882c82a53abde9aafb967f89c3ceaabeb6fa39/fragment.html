House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00016
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE DEPARTMENT OF INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATIONS TECHNOLOGY, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Department Of Information And Communications Technology Act
ABSTRACT : “Department of Information and Communications Technology Act.” Prescribes the mandate of the Department and its organizational structure, defining its task of promoting, developing and overseeing the ICT systems thus ensuring reliable and cost-efficient communications facilities and services.
