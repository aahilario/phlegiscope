HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00443
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE DESIGNATION OF A ONE METER WIDE LANE FOR THE EXCLUSIVE USE OF BICYCLE RIDERS IN ALL NATIONAL ROADS OF THE COUNTRY, PRESCRIBING THE PENALTY FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to create a bicycle lane in designated roads so as to provide biking enthusiasts a safe and well-deserved byway where they can pass without obstruction or danger from vehicular traffic. A successful promotion of biking as a means of mass transport will bring about a healthy populace free from respiratory ailments and will drastically cut fuel expenses. It will also declog the streets from too much traffic.
