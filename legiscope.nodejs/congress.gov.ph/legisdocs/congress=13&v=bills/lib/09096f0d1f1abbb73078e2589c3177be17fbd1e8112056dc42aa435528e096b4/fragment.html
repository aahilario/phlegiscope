House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00387
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR A THREE THOUSAND PESOS (PHP 3,000.00) DAILY ACROSS-THE-BOARD WAGE RATE INCREASE FOR EMPLOYEES AND WORKERS IN THE PUBLIC SECTOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Public Sector Workers' Wage Rate Increase Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Public Sector Workers' Wage Rate Increase Act of 2004". The measure seeks to grant all public sector employees and workers a PhP 3,000.00 daily across-the-board wage increase effective immediately. The proposed wage increase would partially restore the lost purchasing power of the workers' wages.
