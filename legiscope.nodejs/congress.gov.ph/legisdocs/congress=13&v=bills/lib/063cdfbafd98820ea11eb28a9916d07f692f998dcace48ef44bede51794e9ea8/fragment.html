House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02652
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING TAX AMNESTY ON ALL UNTAXED INCOME/WEALTH AND UNPAID TAX LIABILITIES FOR THE TAXABLE YEAR 2002 AND PRIOR YEARS
SHORT TITLE : The Tax Amnesty Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "The Tax Amnesty Act of 2005." The measure proposes to grant a tax amnesty to delinquent taxpayers to enable them to settle their delinquent accounts on a voluntary basis. The proposed tax amnesty shall cover all revenue taxes.
