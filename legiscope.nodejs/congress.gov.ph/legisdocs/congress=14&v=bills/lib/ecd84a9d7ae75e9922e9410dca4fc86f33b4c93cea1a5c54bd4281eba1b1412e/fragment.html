HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01029
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL RAILWAY AUTHORITY TO ACCELERATE THE DEVELOPMENT OF A NATIONAL RAILWAY SYSTEM, PRESCRIBING ITS POWERS, FUNCTIONS AND DUTIES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "National Railways Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "National Railways Act of 2007". The National Railways Authority (NRA) shall formulate and adopt a comprehensive national railways development plan and implement the same. The NRA shall control, develop, construct, operate and maintain the railway facilities.
