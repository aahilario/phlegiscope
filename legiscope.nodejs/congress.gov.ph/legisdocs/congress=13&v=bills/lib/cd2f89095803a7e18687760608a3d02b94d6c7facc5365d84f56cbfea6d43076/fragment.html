SHORT TITLE : "Validation of Duly Issued Sales Certificates Covering Lots Within the Friar Lands of 2005."
ABSTRACT : �Validation of Duly Issued Sales Certificates Covering Lots within the Friar Lands of 2005�. The bill declares the duly issued sales certificates covering lots within the Friar lands as sufficient for the conveyance of the lots and as basis for the issuance of the corresponding transfer certificates of title to the sales applicant or his successors or transferees-in-interest.
