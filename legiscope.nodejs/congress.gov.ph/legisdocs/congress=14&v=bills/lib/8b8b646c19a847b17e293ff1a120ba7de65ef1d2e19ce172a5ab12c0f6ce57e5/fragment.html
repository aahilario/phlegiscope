HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02477
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMOTING AND INSTITUTIONALIZING THE ONE-TOWN-ONE-PRODUCT PROGRAM IN ALL PROVINCES NATIONWIDE AND RATIONALIZING THEIR SPECIALIZATION TO ENCOURAGE THE PROMOTION, DEVELOPMENT AND GROWTH OF A MILLION SMALL ENTERPRISES IN THE RURAL AREAS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill institutionalizes the one-town-one-product program whereby towns which specialize in the production of particular products shall receive support from the government in the form of incentives and product design.
