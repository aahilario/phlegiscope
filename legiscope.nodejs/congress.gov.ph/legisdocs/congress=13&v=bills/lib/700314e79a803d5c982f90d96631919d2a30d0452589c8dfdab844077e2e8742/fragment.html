HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01340
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ANNUAL INCREASE OF THE AMOUNT OF PENSION PAID TO ALL RETIRED GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES BY AT LEAST AN AMOUNT EQUIVALENT TO THE RATED ANNUAL INCREASE IN THE NATIONAL LIVING INDEX AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The rising cost of living has made it increasingly difficult for retired government employees to provide for their family's daily sustenance from their monthly pension. The bill seeks to grant retirees an automatic increase in their pension equivalent to the annual rate of increase in the cost of living index.
