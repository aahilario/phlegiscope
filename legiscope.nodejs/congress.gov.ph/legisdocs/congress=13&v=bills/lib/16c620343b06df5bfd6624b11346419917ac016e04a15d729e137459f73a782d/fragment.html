FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ENCOURAGE THE DONATION OF FOOD FOR CHARITABLE PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to address the legal liability in the donation of food surplus by exempting donors from any civil or criminal liability arising from their charitable acts.
