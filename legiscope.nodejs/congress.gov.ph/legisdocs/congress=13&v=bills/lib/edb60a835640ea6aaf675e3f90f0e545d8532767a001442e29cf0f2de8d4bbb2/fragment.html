House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03603
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT 7722, ENTITLED "AN ACT CREATING THE COMMISSION ON HIGHER EDUCATION, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES", STRENGTHENING THE COMMISSION ON HIGHER EDUCATION (CHED),AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Higher Education Act of 1994
ABSTRACT : "Higher Education Act of 1994." The bill seeks to rationalize, reinforce and strengthen the Commission on Higher Education to enable it to discharge its functions and missions properly and effectively in accordance with the intent of RA 7722.
