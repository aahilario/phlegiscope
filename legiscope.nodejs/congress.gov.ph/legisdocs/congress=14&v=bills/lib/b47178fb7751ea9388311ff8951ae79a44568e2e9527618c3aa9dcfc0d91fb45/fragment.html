HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01277
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROHIBIT THE ASSIGNMENT OR CONTINUANCE IN AN ASSIGNMENT OF CERTAIN OFFICIALS OF THE COMMISSION ON ELECTIONS IN JURISDICTIONS WHERE THEY ARE RELATED BY CONSANGUINITY OR AFFINITY, WITHIN THE FOURTH CIVIL DEGREE, TO ANY INCUMBENT ELECTIVE PUBLIC OFFICIAL OR ANY CANDIDATE FOR A PUBLIC OFFICE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits the assignment to or continuance in any assignment of election officers or registrars in jurisdiction where an incumbent public official is related to him by blood or by affinity.
