House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00741
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A ROAD DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Road Development Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Road Development Act of 2004." The bill seeks to create the Road Development Authority (RDA) which shall be responsible for the construction and/or rehabilitation, maintenance and operation of selected and desirable infrastructure projects where there are no takers from the private sector. It grants the RDA the power to raise and/or borrow funds from local and international financial institutions for the purpose of financing its projects and programs.
