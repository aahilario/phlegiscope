House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00082
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A COLLEGE EDUCATION TRUST FUND FOR CHILDREN OF GSIS AND SSS MEMBERS
SHORT TITLE : "College Education Trust Fund Act"
ABSTRACT : "College Education Trust Fund Act". The bill seeks to establish within the Government Service Insurance System and the Social Service System a special trust fund called the "College Education Trust Fund" to cover the college education of the children of members of both systems.
