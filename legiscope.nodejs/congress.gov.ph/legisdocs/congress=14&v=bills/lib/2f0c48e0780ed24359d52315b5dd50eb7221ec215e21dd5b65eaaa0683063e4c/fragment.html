HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02187
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS TO CONDUCT A QUARTERLY BREAST CANCER DETECTION PROGRAM
SHORT TITLE : "Breast Cancer Detection Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Breast Cancer Detection Act of 2007". The bill directs the local government units to hold, quarterly, manual or mammogram breast cancer screening program through the local health officers.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.
DATE FILED : 2007-08-22
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
