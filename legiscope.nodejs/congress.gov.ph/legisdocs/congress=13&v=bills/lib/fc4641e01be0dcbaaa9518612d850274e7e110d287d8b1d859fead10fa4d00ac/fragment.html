House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00447
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ENSURING SECURITY OF TENURE FOR AGRARIAN REFORM BENEFICIARIES OVER LANDS AWARDED TO THEM UNDER THE COMPREHENSIVE AGRARIAN REFORM PROGRAM, PROVIDING GUIDELINES FOR THE CANCELLATION OF LAND OWNERSHIP AWARDS, PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The rampant cancellation of Emancipation Patents (EPs) and Certificates of Land Ownership Awards (CLOAs) issued to farmer-beneficiaries under the land reform program has become alarming. The cancellations occur mainly because the subject pieces of land were erroneously covered by the program. This bill seeks to reaffirm that EPSs and CLOAs are land titles under PD 1527. The measure provides for compensation to landowners whose lands were erroneously covered by the land reform program. It ensures that the farmer-beneficiaries do not bear the consequences of the Department of Agrarian Reform's errors in distributing lands that should have been exempted from the coverage of the program.
