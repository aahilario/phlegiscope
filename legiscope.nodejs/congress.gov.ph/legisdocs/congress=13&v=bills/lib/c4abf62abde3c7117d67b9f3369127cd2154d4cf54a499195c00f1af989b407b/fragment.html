House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01459
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REQUIRE INDUSTRIES, FACTORIES, COMPANIES, OR ANY INSTITUTION, FIRM OR ESTABLISHMENT USING CHEMICALS AND THEIR BY-PRODUCTS FOR PRODUCTION, TO DEVELOP AND PROMOTE A COMMUNITY ENVIRONMENT INFORMATION PROGRAM
SHORT TITLE : Community Environmental Information Program Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Community Environmental Information Program Act of 2004". The bill directs all industries, corporations, business firms and establishments that derive raw materials and those that produce and discard their by-products in the community, to ensure that their activities or operations do not pollute water and land bodies.
