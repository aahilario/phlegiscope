HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB06101
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE DISCOUNT PRIVILEGE OF SENIOR CITIZENS FROM 20% TO 34%, FURTHER AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT 7432 OR "AN ACT TO MAXIMIZE THE CONTRIBUTION OF SENIOR CITIZENS TO NATION BUILDING, GRANT BENEFITS AND SPECIAL PRIVILEGES," AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the discount being enjoyed by senior citizens on the purchase of goods and services, medical and dental services, transport fares, funeral and burial services, among others, from 20% to 34%.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CUENCO, ANTONIO V.
