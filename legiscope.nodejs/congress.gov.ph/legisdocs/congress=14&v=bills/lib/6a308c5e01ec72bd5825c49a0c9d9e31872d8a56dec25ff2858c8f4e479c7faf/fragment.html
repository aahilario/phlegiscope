HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00643
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE REPRESENTATION OF COOPERATIVES IN THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS OF THE LAND BANK OF THE PHILIPPINES, AMENDING THEREFORE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 3844, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The measure provides for an additional member in the Board of Directors of the Land Bank to represent the cooperative sector thus increasing the number of Board members from 9 to 10.
