HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02827
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING SMUGGLING AS HEINOUS CRIME, PROVIDING THE DEATH PENALTY FOR THE CRIME OF SMUGGLING, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill declares smuggling as an act of economic sabotage and a heinous crime. It imposes the penalty of death and a fine of twice the value of the smuggled goods or Php20 million whichever is higher.
