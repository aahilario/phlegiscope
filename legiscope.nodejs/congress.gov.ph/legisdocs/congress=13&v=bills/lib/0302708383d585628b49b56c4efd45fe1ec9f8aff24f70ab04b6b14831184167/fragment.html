HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00419
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE INCLUSION OF ALL ELECTRIC COOPERATIVES ORGANIZED AND EXISTING UNDER PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 269, AS AMENDED, AND REGISTERED WITH THE NATIONAL ELECTRIFICATION ADMINISTRATION (NEA) WITHIN THE COVERAGE OF TAX EXEMPTION PRIVILEGES AND INCENTIVES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : Sections 193 and 234 of the Local Government Code (LGC) effectively withdrew the tax exemption benefits enjoyed by electric cooperatives. The withdrawal of the incentive caused electric cooperatives to suffer heavy financial losses. This bill seeks to exempt electric cooperatives from the payment of real property taxes and other fees imposed by the local government units. It seeks to include all electric cooperatives organized under PD 269, as amended, and registered with the National Electrification Administration, within the coverage of tax exemption privileges and incentives granted under the LGC.
