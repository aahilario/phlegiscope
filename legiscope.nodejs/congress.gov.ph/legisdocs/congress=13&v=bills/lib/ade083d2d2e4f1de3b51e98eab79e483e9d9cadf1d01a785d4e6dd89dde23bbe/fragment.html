House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04726
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE NATIONAL MUSEUM LEGISLATIVE ENDOWMENT FUND
SHORT TITLE : "National Museum Legislative Endowment Fund Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "National Museum Legislative Endowment Fund Act of 2005". The bill automatically appropriates annually P100,000 from the Priority Development Assistance Fund (PDAF) of each Congressmen and P300,000 from the PDAF of the each Senator to fund the programs, projects and activities of the National Museum.
