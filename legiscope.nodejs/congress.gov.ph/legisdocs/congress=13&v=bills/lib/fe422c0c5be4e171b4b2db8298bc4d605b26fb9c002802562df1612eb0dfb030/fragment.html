HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02582
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXTENDING THE TERM OF BARANGAY AND SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN OFFICIALS AND SETTING THE DATE OF ITS ELECTION, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT 9164
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the holding of the synchronized barangay and sangguniang kabataan elections on the 2nd Monday of May 2007 to coincide with the national and local officials and every three years thereafter. It also extends the terms of those elected on July 15 2002 to June 30, 2007.
