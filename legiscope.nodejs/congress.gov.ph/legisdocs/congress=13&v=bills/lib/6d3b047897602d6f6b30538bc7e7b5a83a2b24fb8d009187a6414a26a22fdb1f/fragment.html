HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02273
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE AUTOMATIC RELEASE OF THE TEN PERCENT (10%) ALLOCATION TO THE SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN FROM THE GENERAL FUND OF THE BARANGAY AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS 329 AND 331 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to free the Sangguniang Kabataan from having to beg to the Barangay Captains for the release of the ten percent (10%) allocation due them.
