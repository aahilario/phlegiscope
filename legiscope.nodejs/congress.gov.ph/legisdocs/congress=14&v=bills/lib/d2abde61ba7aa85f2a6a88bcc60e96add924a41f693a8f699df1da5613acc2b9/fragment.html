House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01821
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER TWENTY-FIVE OF EVERY YEAR AS "NATIONAL CONSCIOUSNESS DAY FOR THE ELIMINATION OF VIOLENCE AGAINST WOMEN"
SHORT TITLE : Declaring November Twenty-Five of Every Year as "National Consciousness Day for the Elimination of Violence Against Women"
ABSTRACT : The proposed nationwide observance of the National Consciousness Day for the Elimination of Violence Against Women in November 25 of every year aims to raise the public's awareness on the problem of violence and the elimination of all violence against women.
