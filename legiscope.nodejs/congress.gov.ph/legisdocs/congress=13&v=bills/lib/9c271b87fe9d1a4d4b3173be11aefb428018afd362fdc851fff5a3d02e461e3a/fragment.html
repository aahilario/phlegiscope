House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00600
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING EARLY CHILDHOOD DEVELOPMENT AND EDUCATION CENTERS FOR CHILDREN AGED ZERO (0)TO FIVE (5)YEARS, CREATING THE IMPLEMENTING MACHINERY THEREOF, PROVIDING GUIDELINES, INCENTIVES, GOVERNMENT FINANCIAL SUPPORT AND TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Early Childhood Development and Education Act
ABSTRACT : "Early Childhood Development and Education Act." The bill seeks to establish Early Childhood Development Centers which will develop a program of activities that will focus primarily on the total development of children with their first language as their medium of instruction; provide the parents with training programs which focuses endeavor to inculcate responsble parenthood and provide guidance in child rearing, health, nutrition and education.
