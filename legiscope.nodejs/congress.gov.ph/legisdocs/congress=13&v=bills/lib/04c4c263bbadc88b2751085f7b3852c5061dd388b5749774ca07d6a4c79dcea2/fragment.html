House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04080
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE MOVIE AND TELEVISION CLASSIFICATIONS BOARD (MTCB), REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1986, "CREATING THE MOVIE AND TELEVISION CLASSIFICATIONS BOARD," AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Movie and Television Classifications Board or MTRCB Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Movie and Television Classifications Board or MTCB Act of 2005". The bill seeks to abolish the Movie and Television Review and Classification Board (MTRCB) and creates, in its place, the MTCB. It provides for the adoption of the Miller's doctrine in classifying a film as "X". It creates the Review and Classification Board and abolishes the Appeals committee.
