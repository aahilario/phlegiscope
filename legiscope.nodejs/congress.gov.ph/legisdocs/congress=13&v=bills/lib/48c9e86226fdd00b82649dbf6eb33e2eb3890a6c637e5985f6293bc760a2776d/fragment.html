HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01465
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THAT ALL LIQUOR PRODUCTS CARRY A WARNING LABEL THAT ALCOHOL INTAKE DURING PREGNANCY IS HAZARDOUS TO THE LIFE AND HEALTH OF THE UNBORN CHILD
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes a fine ranging from PhP10,00 to PhP200,000 on liquor manufacturers and/or distributors who fail to print on the label of their liquor products a warning that alcohol intake is dangerous to the life and health of the unborn child.
