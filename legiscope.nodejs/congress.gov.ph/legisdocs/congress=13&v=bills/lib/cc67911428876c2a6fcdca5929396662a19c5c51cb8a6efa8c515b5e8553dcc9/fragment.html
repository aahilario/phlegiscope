House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03380
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE INTERNAL AFFAIRS OFFICE OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the creation of the Internal Affairs Office in the Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP) to enable AFP members to clean up their own ranks and strengthen the image of the Filipino workers.
