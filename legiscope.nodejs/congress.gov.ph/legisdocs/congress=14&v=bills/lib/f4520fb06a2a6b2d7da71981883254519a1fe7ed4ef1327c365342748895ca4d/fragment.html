House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03030
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING CENTRALIZED PROCUREMENT OF PETROLEUM IN THE COUNTRY
SHORT TITLE : "Centralized Petroleum Procurement Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill institutionalizes a centralized procurement system of imported crude oil and refined petroleum products including the creation of buffer supplies to cushion consumers against drastic increases in petroleum prices.
