House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03693
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PRE-NEED CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : Pre-Need Code of the Philippines
ABSTRACT : "Pre-Need Code of the Philippines." The bill direct pre-need companies to maintain their trust fund at a level equivalent to the actuarial reserve liability. It provides for a longer grace period of five months, termination values in the event the planholder opts to terminate his policy and the creation of a planholders' protection fund.
