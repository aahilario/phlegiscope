House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01468
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR COMPREHENSIVE HEALTH REFORM OF THE PHILIPPINES TO INCREASE AND MAKE EQUITABLE ACCESS TO HEALTH CARE SERVICES, CREATING A NATIONAL HEALTH POLICY COUNCIL, A NATIONAL HEALTH AND HOSPITALS CORPORATION AND A NATIONAL HEALTHCARE FINANCING POLICY CLUSTER, AMENDING THE INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY CODE (REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8293), AND OTHER RELEVANT LAWS, SUPPLEMENTING THE NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE ACT (REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7875), THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE (REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160) AND OTHER RELEVANT LAWS FOR THAT PURPOSE
SHORT TITLE : "Comprehensive National Health Reform Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Comprehensive National Health Reform Act of 2007". The bill seeks to provide a framework for a multi-stakeholder collaboration in the structured reform of our health care system with the goal of achieving equitable and sustainable access to quality health care and services in the country.
