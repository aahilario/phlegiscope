House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03700
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE OFFICE OF EDUCATION ATTACHE OF THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION
ABSTRACT : The bill creates the Office of Education Attache to supervise and oversee international school managed and operated by Philippine embassies and/or private institutions.
