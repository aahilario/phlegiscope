House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05003
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 290 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
SHORT TITLE : Amending Section 290 of RA 7160, Otherwise Known as the Local Government Code of 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill directs natural and juridical persons engaged in the utilization and development of national wealth to pay the share of local government units directly to the host local government unit and the remaining share to the national government.
