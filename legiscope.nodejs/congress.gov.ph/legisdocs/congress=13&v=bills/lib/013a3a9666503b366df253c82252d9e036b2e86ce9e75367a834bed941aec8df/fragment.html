House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05743
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ACCELERATING THE COMPLETION OF THE COMPREHENSIVE AGRARIAN REFORM PROGRAM (CARP), BY PROVIDING AUTOMATIC APPROPRIATION THEREFOR, AND ADDRESSING MAJOR IMPLEMENTATION PROBLEMS,AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6657, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE COMPREHENSIVE AGRARIAN REFORM LAW OF 1988"
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the appropriation of additional amounts for the Comprehensive Agrarian Reform Program (CARP) through automatic annual appropriation in an amount not lower that 1.6% of the total national budget.
