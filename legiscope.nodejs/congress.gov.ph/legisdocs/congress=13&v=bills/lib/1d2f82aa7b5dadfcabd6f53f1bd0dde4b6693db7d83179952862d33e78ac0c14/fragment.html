House Bill/Resolution NO. HB06097
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING INCENTIVES TO THE SHIPBUILDING AND SHIP REPAIR INDUSTRY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Shipbuilding and Ship Repair Industry Incentives Act of 2002
ABSTRACT : "Shipbuilding and Ship Repair Industry Incentives Act of 2002". The bill seeks to grant shipbuilding and ship repair yards duly registered with the Maritime Industry Authority (MARINA) exemption from import duties and taxes and from the value-added tax.
