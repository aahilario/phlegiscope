HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04530
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING FRESH MILK AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Fresh Milk Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Fresh Milk Act of 2005". The bill defines fresh milk as pasteurized fresh milk with limited shelf life and requires refrigeration. It declares UHT (ultra high temperature) milk or any milk other than fresh milk, represented or purported to be fresh milk, as mislabeled food.
