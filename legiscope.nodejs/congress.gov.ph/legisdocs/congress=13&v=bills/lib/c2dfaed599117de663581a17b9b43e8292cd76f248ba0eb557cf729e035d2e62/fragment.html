House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01884
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE OPERATION OF TAXI AND MEGA TAXI UNITS
SHORT TITLE : Taxi Regulation Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Taxi Regulation Act of 2004." This bill is designed to put together relevant regulations and combine them with new and responsive ones for the smooth operation of taxi and mega taxi units. It enumerates the schedule of fines and penalties for violators of this Act.
