House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00564
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ELEVATING THE PHILIPPINE NORMAL UNIVERSITY TO BECOME THE COUNTRY'S NATIONAL UNIVERSITY FOR TEACHER EDUCATION, ESTABLISHING A SYSTEM OF NATIONAL TEACHER TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine Normal University (PNU) Modernization Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Normal University (PNU) Modernization Act of 2007". The bill establishes the National Teachers Training and Development Program that will provide teachers opportunities for in-service training programs in the following areas: a) information and communication techologies; b) competencies in various fields of specialization; and c) innovative strategies and alternative modalities of teaching and learning.
