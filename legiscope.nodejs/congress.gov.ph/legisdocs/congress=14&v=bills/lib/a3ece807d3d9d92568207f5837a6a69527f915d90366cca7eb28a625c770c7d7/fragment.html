HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00392
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 20 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBERED 1638, AS AMENDED BY PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBERED 1650, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES RETIREMENT LAW
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to grant a separation pay equivalent to one month base pay and longevity pay for every year of active service to the AFP's enlisted personnel who are honorably separated from service before rendering 20 years of active service.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : GOLEZ, ROILO S.
