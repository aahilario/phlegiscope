House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04835
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 53, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS 'AN ACT TO EXEMPT THE PUBLISHER, EDITOR OR REPORTER OF ANY PUBLICATION FROM REVEALING THE SOURCE OF PUBLISHED NEWS OR INFORMATION OBTAINED IN CONFIDENCE' BY INCLUDING WITHIN ITS COVERAGE JOURNALISTS FROM BROADCAST, NEWS AGENCIES AND INTERNET PUBLICATIONS
SHORT TITLE : Amending RA No. 53, As Amended, by Including Within Its Coverage Journalists From Broadcast, News Agencies and Internet Publications
ABSTRACT : The bill includes media practitioners from the broadcast, news agencies and internet publication in the coverage of the protection extended to their infomation sources under RA 53. Ra 53, as amended, limited the protection only to the print media industry.
