HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02448
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING MANUFACTURERS AND DISTRIBUTORS OF PROCESSED FOODS, SNACKS AND BEVERAGES TO DETERMINE AND PRINT ON THE BOX, PACK, BOTTLE OR CONTAINER OF PROCESSED FOODS, SNACKS OR BEVERAGES THE NUTRITIONAL FACTS OF THEIR PRODUCTS WHICH ARE MANUFACTURED, DISTRIBUTED OR SOLD IN THE PHILIPPINES AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates manufacturers and distributors of processed foods and beverages to determine the nutritional value of their products and to print the nutritional facts on the label of their packaging.
