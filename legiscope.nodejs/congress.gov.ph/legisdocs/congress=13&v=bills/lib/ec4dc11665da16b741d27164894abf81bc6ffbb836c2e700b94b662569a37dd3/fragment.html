House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02068
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE DEPARTMENT OF HOUSING, PLANNING, AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, RATIONALIZING THE GOVERNMENT HOUSING SYSTEM, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the powers, functions and organizational structure of the Department. It places the National Home Mortgage Finance Corporation, Home Guaranty Corporation, Home Development Mutual Fund, National Housing Authority and the Housing and Land Use Regulatory Board under the control and supervision of the Department.
