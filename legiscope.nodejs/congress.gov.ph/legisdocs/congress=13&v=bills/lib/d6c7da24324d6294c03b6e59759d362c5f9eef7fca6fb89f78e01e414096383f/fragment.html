House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03316
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PHILIPPINE MEDICAL SCHOLARSHIP AND SERVICE PROGRAM AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Medical Scholarship and Service Act
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Medical Scholarship and Service Act." The bill provides for an annual selection of 'medical scholars' from among qualified graduating students. It creates the Medical Scholarship and Service Board which shall administer the Phillipine Medical Scholarship and Service Program.
