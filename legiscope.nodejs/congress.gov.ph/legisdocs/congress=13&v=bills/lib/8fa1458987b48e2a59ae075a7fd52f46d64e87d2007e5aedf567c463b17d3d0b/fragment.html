FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE DAILY SUBSISTENCE ALLOWANCE OF ALL UNIFORMED PERSONNEL OF THE PHILIPPINE COAST GUARD (PCG), AND PROVIDING FOR ITS PERIODIC ADJUSTMENT
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes to increase the daily substistence allowance of uniformed personnel of the Philippine Coast Guard from P60 to P120.
