HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04195
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AMEND THE NATIONAL BUILDING CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES AND TO INCORPORATE IN CHAPTER 3 THEREIN A PROVISION ON THE STANDARD MINIMUM REQUIREMENT FOR TERMITE PROOFING IN ALL PRIVATE AND PUBLIC BUILDINGS IN THE PHILIPPINES AND TO INSTITUTE THE OPERATING GUIDELINES FOR ITS IMPLEMENTATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the institution of a pre-construction termite proofing in all private and public structures. It requires all applicants for a building permit to submit a "written contract for pre-construction termite proofing with a duly licensed pest control operator".
