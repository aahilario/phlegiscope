House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03407
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 209, ALSO KNOWN AS THE FAMILY CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, ARTICLES 221 AND 236
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the liability of parents and guardians to be only subsidiary for injuries and damages caused by the acts or omissions of their children or wards between the ages of eighteen and twenty-one years provided that they continue to live with their parents or guardians.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SANTIAGO, NARCISO III D.
