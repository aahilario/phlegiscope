House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01537
FULL TITLE : THE BUDGET IMPOUNDMENT CONTROL ACT OF 2004
SHORT TITLE : The Budget Impoundment Control Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Impoundment" refers to a refusal of the President, for whatever reason, to spend funds made available by Congress. The bill seeks to curtail the practice of presidential impoundments of funds by establishing a mechanism for Congress to review the deferral of releases prior to its exercise.
