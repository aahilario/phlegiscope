House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00981
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE CULMINATION OF MUSLIM ANNUAL HAJJ A NATIONAL HOLIDAY FOR THE OBSERVANCE OF EIDUL ADHA, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 26, CHAPTER 7 OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 292, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE ADMINISTRATIVE CODE OF 1987, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Declaring the Culmination of Muslim Haji a National Holiday for the Observance of Eidul Adha
ABSTRACT : The bill declares the Muslim's annual religious observance of EIDUL ADHA as a national holiday to help advance the peace effort of the government.
