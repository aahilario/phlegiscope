HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03440
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING THE SALE OF ELECTRICITY TO MARGINALIZED CONSUMERS FROM THE VALUE-ADDED TAX, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 109(1) OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to exempt from the ambit of the value-added tax the sale of electricity to marginal consumers whose monthly electric consumption does not exceed 100 kwh.
