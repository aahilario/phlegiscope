House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01364
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RECOGNIZING THE POTENTIALS OF THE FILIPINO YOUTH TOWARD NATION-BUILDING, PRESCRIBING THE FREE EXERCISE OF CERTAIN RIGHTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Magna Carta for the Youth
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta for the Youth." Proposes to grant the youth the following rights: a) access to labor and employment; b) access to education; c) academic freedom; d) access to health care and services; e) free expression and information; f) organization and free assembly; g) paricipation in policy formulation; h) freedom of thought, conscience and religion; and i) political expression.
