HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03977
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPOSING A FIFTY CENTAVO (P0.50) SPECIFIC TAX ON (EXCLUDING VALUE-ADDED TAX) SHORT MESSAGING SERVICE USING THE CELLULAR NETWORK TO BE BORNE BY CELLULAR PHONE COMPANIES, CREATING A SPECIAL INFRASTRUCTURE FUND FOR PUBLIC WORKS PROJECTS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes a P0.50 specific tax on every short messaging service (SMS) to be shouldered by the cellular phone companies. The tax proceeds shall be used to fund the upgrading, modernization and improvement of the country's infrastructures.
