HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02681
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE DISTRIBUTION TO THE PUBLIC OF VIOLENT VIDEO PROGRAMMING DURING HOURS WHEN CHILDREN ARE REASONABLY LIKELY TO COMPRISE A SUBSTANTIAL PORTION OF THE AUDIENCE
SHORT TITLE : "Children's Protection from Violent Programming Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to protect children from violent video programming by prohibiting its airing to the public during hours when children are likely to comprise a substantial portion of the audience.
