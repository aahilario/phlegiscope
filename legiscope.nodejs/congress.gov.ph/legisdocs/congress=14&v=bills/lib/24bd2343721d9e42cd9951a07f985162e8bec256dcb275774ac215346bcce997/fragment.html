House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00097
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MAGNA CARTA FOR THE WORKERS IN THE INFORMAL SECTOR, INSTITUTIONALIZING MECHANISM FOR IMPLEMENTATION THEREOF AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Magna Carta for the Workers in the Informal Sector"
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta for the Workers in the Informal Sector". The proposed Code seeks to introduce an integrated, holistic and comprehensive policy instrument that shall serve as the springboard for the informal sector to join the mainstream of the Philippine economy.
