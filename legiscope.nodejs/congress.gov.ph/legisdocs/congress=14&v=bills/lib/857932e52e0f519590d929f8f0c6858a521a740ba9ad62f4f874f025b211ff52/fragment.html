HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03398
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE SANGGUNIANG PANLALAWIGAN, SANGGUNIANG PANLUNGSOD AND THE SANGGUNIANG BAYAN TO DECLARE WORKING OR NON-WORKING SPECIAL HOLIDAYS IN THEIR RESPECTIVE AREAS TO COMMEMORATE SIGNIFICANT HISTORICAL EVENTS OR PAY TRIBUTE TO IMPORTANT PERSONALITIES FOR THEIR CONTRIBUTION TO THE GROWTH AND DEVELOPMENT OF THE POLITICAL SUBDIVISIONS OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the sangguniang panlalawigan, the sangguniang panglungsod and the sangguniang bayan to declare, by ordinance, special holidays.
