HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00902
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE AUTOMATIC DISMISSAL OF DORMANT COURT CASES
ABSTRACT : This bill aims to decongest the courts of cases which have not been prosecuted for six month from the last hearing and filing of last pleading. Covers Municipal Trial courts, Municipal Circuit Trial Courts, Regional Trial Courts and Court of Appeals.
