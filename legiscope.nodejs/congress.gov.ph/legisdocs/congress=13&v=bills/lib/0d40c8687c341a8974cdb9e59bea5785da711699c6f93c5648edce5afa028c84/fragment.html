HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05836
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS TO ALLOCATE FIVE PERCENT (5%) OF THEIR INTERNAL REVENUE ALLOTMENT FOR THE RELOCATION AND RESETTLEMENT OF UNDERPRIVILEGED AND HOMELESS CITIZENS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7279, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE URBAN DEVELOPMENT AND HOUSING ACT OF 1992
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to assist local government units in providing relocation and resettlement areas by setting aside 5% of their respective internal revenue allotment for this purpose.
