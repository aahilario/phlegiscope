HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00205
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPROVING PHILIPPINE COMMITMENT TO HUMAN RIGHTS PROMOTION AND PROTECTION BY PROVIDING THE NECESSARY MECHANISMS FOR THE PREVENTION OF THE OCCURRENCE AND THE PROTECTION FROM ADVERSE EFFECTS OF INTERNAL DISPLACEMENT AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Internal Displacement Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Internal Displacement Act of 2010.” Seeks to embody the two-pronged approach of protection and assistance that is the moving spirit behind the United Nations Guiding Principles on Internal Displacement formulated by the United Nations Commission on Human Rights during its 54th session.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : TAÑADA, LORENZO III R.
