HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01591
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING INCENTIVES TO PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS WHO ARE ASSIGNED OUTSIDE OF THEIR TOWNS AND PROVINCES APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Public School Teachers' Incentives Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Public School Teachers' Incentives Act of 2005". The bill proposes to grant public school teachers an incentive allowance of P1,000 when assigned to another town within the same province and P2,000 allowance when assigned to a town in another province.
