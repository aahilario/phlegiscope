House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00517
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TRANSFERRING THE PHILIPPINE COAST GUARD TO THE DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION AND COMMUNICATIONS AS AN ATTACHED AGENCY
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine Coast Guard Law of 2004"
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Coast Guard Law of 2004". The bill establishes the Philippine Coast Guard as a distinct, armed and uniformed commission service attached to the Department of Transportation and Communications.
