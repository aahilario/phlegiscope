House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01367
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO RATIONALIZE THE TEACHING OF RELIGION IN THE PUBLIC SCHOOLS
SHORT TITLE : "Act to Rationalize the Teaching of Religion in the Public Schools"
ABSTRACT : "Act to Rationalize the Teaching of Religion in the Public Schools". The measure provides for the conduct of religion classes in public elementary and high schools to students or pupils whose parents or guardians have requested to have their children or wards taught the religion of their choice.
