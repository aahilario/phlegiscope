FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE NATIONAL EDUCATION COORDINATING COUNCIL, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "National Education Coordinating Council Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to establish the National Education Coordinating council that shall shall integrate and coordinate the policies, plans, programs and prjects in the areas of formal and non-formal education in all levels.
