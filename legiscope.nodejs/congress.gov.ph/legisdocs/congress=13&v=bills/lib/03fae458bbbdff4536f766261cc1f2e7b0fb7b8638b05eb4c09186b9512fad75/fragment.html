House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02292
FULL TITLE : AN ACT UPGRADING THE MINIMUM SALARY GRADE LEVEL OF PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS IN ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY LEVELS FROM GRADE 10 TO GRADE 17 AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : This bill aims to entice competent and highly qualified educators to join the public teaching force and recognizes the critical social responsibility that they shoulder by upgrading their minimum salary levels from Grade 10 to Grade 17.
