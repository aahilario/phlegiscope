House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00386
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE MOVEMENT TO HELP ERADICATE RAPE AND OTHER FEMALE ABUSES '(MOTHER)', MANDATING THE COMPULSORY TEACHING OF SELF-DEFENSE TO ALL FEMALE CONSTITUENTS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Movement to Eradicate Rape and Other Female Abuses (MOTHER) Act
ABSTRACT : "Movement to Eradicate Rape and Other Female Abuses (MOTHER) Act". Violence against women has a devastating effect on the physical and emotional well-being not only of the victim but also the people around them as well as future generation. To address this concern, the bill seeks to mandate that basic self-defense techniques be taught to enable females to protect themselves in the event of imminent danger. Equipping women with self-defense techniques will also address another sensitive issue - heinous crimes.
