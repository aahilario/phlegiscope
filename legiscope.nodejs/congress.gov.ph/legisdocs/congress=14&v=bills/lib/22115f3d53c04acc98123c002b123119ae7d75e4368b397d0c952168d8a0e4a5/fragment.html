HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02099
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE OVERSEAS FILIPINO WORKERS PENSION AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Overseas Filipino Workers Pension Fund Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Overseas Filipino Workers Pension Fund Act of 2007". The bill mandates all registered overseas Filipino contract workers to remit 5% of their gross monthly income to the proposed Fund for five years. It provides for the creation of the Overseas Filipino Workers Pension Fund Board, its composition and powers and functions.
