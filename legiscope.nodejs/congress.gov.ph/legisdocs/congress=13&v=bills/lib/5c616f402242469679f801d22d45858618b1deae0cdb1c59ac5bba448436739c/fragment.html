FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A STUDENT LOAN PROGRAM, ESTABLISHING THE STUDENT LOAN FUND TO BE ADMINISTERED BY THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Student Loan Program Act
ABSTRACT : "Student Loan Program Act." The bill establishes a Student Loan Program to provide financial assistance to qualified applicants. It prescribes the requirements, procedures and terms and conditions for the availment of the student loan.
