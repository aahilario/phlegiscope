HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02374
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING THE GROSS INCOME TAXATION SYSTEM FOR CORPORATIONS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to impose a 15% tax on the gross income of corporations and a 10% tax on the gross income of proprietary educational institutions and hospitals.
