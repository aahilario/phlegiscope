HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00323
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING STIFFER PENALTIES TO MAKERS, DRAWERS AND ISSUERS OF BOUNCING CHECKS
ABSTRACT : At present, the number of cases involving violations of BP 22 have unduly clogged the court dockets. This is because of the "light" penalty provided under the existing law (BP 22). To deter individuals from issuing bouncing checks, the bill seeks to impose stiffer penalties to makers, drawers and issuers of bouncing checks.
