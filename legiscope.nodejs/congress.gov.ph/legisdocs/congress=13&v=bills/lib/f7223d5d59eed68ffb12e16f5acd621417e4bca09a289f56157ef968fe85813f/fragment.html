House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01957
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A NEW COMMITTEE ON OVERSEAS WORKERS AFFAIRS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREOF AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : An Act Creating a New Standing Committee on Overseas Workers Affairs
ABSTRACT : "An Act Creating a New Standing Committee on Overseas Workers Affairs". To advance the interests, rights and welfare of overseas Filipino workers and their families, the bill seeks the creation of a separate standing committee on overseas workers affairs. The committee shall have 25 members and 2 chairpersons.
