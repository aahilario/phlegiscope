House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01110
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING A PHILIPPINE MARITIME AND OCEANS POLICY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine Oceans Act of 2000"
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Oceans Act of 2000". The bill clearly sets forth the government's policies on coastal area and ocean area management. It proposes the establishment of a National Commission on Maritime and Oceans Office that shall oversee the implementation of the law.
