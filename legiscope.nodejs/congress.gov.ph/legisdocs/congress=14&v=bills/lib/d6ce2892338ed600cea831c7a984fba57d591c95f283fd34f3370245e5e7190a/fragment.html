House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00421
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE PROMOTION OF BREASTFEEDING PRACTICES AND INSTITUTING MEASURES THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Breastfeeding Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Breastfeeding Act of 2007". The bill provides a national policy to encourage, protect and support the practice of breastfeeding. It also provides an atmosphere that is physically, emotionally and psychologically conducive to rooming-in and breastfeeding.
