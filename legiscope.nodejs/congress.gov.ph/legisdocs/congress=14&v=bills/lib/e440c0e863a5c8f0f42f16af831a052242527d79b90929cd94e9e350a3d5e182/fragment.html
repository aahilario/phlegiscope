HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00996
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PLACING THE PROVINCIAL, SUB-PROVINCIAL AND DISTRICT JAILS UNDER THE JURISDICTION OF BUREAU OF JAIL MANAGEMENT AND PENOLOGY (BJMP), AMENDING SECTIONS 61, 62, AND 63 OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTY-NINE HUNDRED AND SEVENTY-FIVE OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT ACT OF 1990" AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to remove from the provincial governments the supervision and control over provincial and sub-provincial jails and place them with the Bureau of Jail Management and Penology (BJMP) to avoid confusion as to which government agency should be held responsible for the improvement of these jails and the discipline of its jail personnel.
