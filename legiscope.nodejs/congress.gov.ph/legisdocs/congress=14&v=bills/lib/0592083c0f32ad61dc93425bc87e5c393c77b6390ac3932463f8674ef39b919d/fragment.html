House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00190
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING CYBERCRIME, PROVIDING FOR PREVENTION, SUPPRESSION AND IMPOSITION OF PENALTIES THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2007". The bill defines cybercrimes, identifies punishable acts involving computers , determines the legal procedures for the investigation and prosecution of cybercrimes, clarifies jurisdictions, provides for a clause on mutual assistance and cooperation, and identifies a local body that shall be responsible for providing a 24/7 assistance to foreign entities in the resolution of cybercrime cases.
