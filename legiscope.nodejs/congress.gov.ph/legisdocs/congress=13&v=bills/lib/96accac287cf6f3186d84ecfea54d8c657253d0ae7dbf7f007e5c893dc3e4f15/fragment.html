House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00614
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER AMENDING CERTAIN SECTIONS OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1177, AS AMENDED, IN ORDER TO INSTITUTE LINE-ITEM BUDGETING WITH RESPECT TO THE APPROPRIATION FOR PERSONAL SERVICES
ABSTRACT : The bill requires every agency of the government to itemize personal services according to position title, number of employee and basic salary.
