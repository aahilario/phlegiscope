HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00401
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE IMPOSITION BY THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT OF AN IDLE LAND TAX, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE THE PERTINENT PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : Studies on local finance show that the idle land tax is not being implemented by local government units due to certain operational difficulties. It is believed that if the national government were to impose the idle land tax, local officials will most likely implement the same. The bill seeks to provide for the imposition by the national government of a 10% idle land tax based on the zonal value or the fair market value of the land, whichever is higher.
