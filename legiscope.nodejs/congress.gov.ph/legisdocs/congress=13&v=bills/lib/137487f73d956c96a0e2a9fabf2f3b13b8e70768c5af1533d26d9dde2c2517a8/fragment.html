House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01074
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING THE COOPERATIVE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES TO BE KNOWN AS THE "PHILIPPINE COOPERATIVE CODE OF 2004"
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Cooperative Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Cooperative Act of 2004." It has been noted that the growth and development of cooperatives have remained slow despite the enactment of the Cooperatives Code. The bill seeks to strengthen the Cooperatives Code of the Philippines to attract more groups to organize themselves into cooperatives.
