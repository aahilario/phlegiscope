FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 19 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NATIONAL WORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN HONOR OF THE MARTYRDOM OF YOUTH LEADER LEANDRO L. ALEJANDRO
