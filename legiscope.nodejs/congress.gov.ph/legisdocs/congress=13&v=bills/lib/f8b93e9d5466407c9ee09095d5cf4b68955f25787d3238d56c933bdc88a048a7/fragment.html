REPUBLIC ACT NO. RA09433
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MAGNA CARTA FOR SOCIAL WORKERS AND SOCIAL WELFARE AND DEVELOPMENT WORKERS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Magna Carta for Social Workers and Social Welfare and Development Workers"
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta for Social Workers and Social Welfare and Development Workers". The bill institutionalizes social work as a distinct profession. It mandates that social work positions in all social welfare and development agencies be given to social workers. It also requires the creation of a social welfare and development officer position in the provincial, city or municipality governments.
