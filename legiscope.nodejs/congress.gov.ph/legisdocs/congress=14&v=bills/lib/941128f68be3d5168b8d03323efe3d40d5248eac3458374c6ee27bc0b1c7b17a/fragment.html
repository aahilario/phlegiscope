HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02926
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 12-B OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 1161, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8282, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SOCIAL SECURITY LAW OF 1997 TO ENTITLE UNDERGROUND MINE WORKERS RETIRING AT AGES SET UNDER REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8558 OF THE BENEFITS PROVIDED UNDER THE FORMER ACT
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to amend the Social Security Law of 1997 by expressly providing for the lowering of the retirement age of underground mine workers to 50-60 years of age.
