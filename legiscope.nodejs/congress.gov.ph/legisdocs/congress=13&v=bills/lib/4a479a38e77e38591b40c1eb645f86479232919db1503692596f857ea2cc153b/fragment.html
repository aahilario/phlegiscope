HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02738
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO INSTITUTIONALIZE THE ONE-STOP CENTER FOR ACTION RESPONSIVENESS (OSCAR) IN EVERY DEPARTMENT OR AGENCY OF THE GOVERNMENT AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : To improve the delivery of services, the bill proposes the establishment of a one-stop center for action responsiveness which shall be authorized to accept, process and dispose of applications for certification, license, permit, clearance and other forms of documentation.
