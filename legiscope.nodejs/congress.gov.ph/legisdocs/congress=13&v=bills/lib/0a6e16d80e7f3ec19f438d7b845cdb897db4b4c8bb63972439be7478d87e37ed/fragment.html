HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04951
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN THE NATIONAL HOME MORTGAGE FINANCE CORPORATION (NHMFC) FOR MASS HOUSING ENDEAVORS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1267, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to increase the capitalization of the National Home Mortgage Finance Corporation and to convert a portion of its loan with the Social Security Service and the Home Development Mutual Fund representing accrued interests into preferred shares. It also increases the appropriation for the Community Mortgage Program to enable it to serve more beneficiaries.
