House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00180
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9136, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE ELECTRIC POWER INDUSTRY REFORM ACT OF 2001 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill calls on power generators and distributors to adopt certain measures to keep them from passing on the costs of their inefficiency, mistakes and onerous contracts to the end-users.
