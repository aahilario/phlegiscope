House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02380
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROTECTING THE RIGHT OF STUDENTS OF COURSES REQUIRING PROFESSIONAL LICENSING EXAMINATIONS TO ENROLL IN REVIEW CENTERS OF THEIR CHOICE AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to penalize and revoke the permit to operate of any school that compels its students to take board review classes in a particular review center.
