HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01085
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXPANDING THE PROHIBITED ACTS OF DISCRIMINATION AGAINST WOMEN ON ACCOUNT OF SEX, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLES 135 AND 137 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : This bill aims to expand the prohibited acts of discrimination against women to include: a) giving preference to a male employee over a female employee in the hiring process; and b) favoring a male employee over a female with respect to dismissal of personnel.
