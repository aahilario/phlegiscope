HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00391
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE PHILIPPINE COCONUT AUTHORITY, INCREASING THE SMALL COCONUT FARMERS' PARTICIPATION IN, AND THEIR BENEFITS FROM, THE COCONUT INDUSTRY, AND BOLSTERING THE INDUSTRY'S DEVELOPMENT, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED AND SIXTY EIGHT, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED COCONUT INDUSTRY CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the sustainable development and growth of the coconut and other palm oil industries and ensures that the coconut farmers, especially the small coconut farmers, tillers, workers and owner cultivators, become direct participants and beneficiaries of the growth of the industry. It seeks to establish the Coconut Industry Rehabilitation and Development Fund from the proceeds of the coconut levy collections.
