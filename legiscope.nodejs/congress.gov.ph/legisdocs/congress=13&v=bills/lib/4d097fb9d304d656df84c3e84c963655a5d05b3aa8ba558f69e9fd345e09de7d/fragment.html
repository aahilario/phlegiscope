House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03848
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE WORKERS' COMPENSATION PROGRAM, THE WORK CONTIGENCY INSURANCE FUND, THE PHILIPPINE WORKERS' COMPENSATION AUTHORITY AND DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS AND AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE ARTICLES 166 TO 208-A OF TITLE 2 BOOK IV OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 442, AS AMENDED OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Workers' Compensation Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Workers' Compensation Act of 2005". The bill proposes a comprehensive and integrated implementation of the employees' compensation program that focuses not only on benefits payment but also on work contingency prevention and, in situations where workers would eventually suffer from work-connected sickness or injury, their rehabilitation into productive members of society.
