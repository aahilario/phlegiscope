HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03001
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE WORKERS' COMPENSATION PROGRAM, THE WORK CONTINGENCY INSURANCE FUND, THE PHILIPPINE WORKERS' COMPENSATION AUTHORITY AND DEFINING THEIR POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE ARTICLES 166 TO 288-A OF TITLE 2 BOOK IV OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES", AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE
SHORT TITLE : "Workers' Compensation Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes a tax-exempt Workers' Compensation Program that will promote a culture of safe and healthy work environment in order to minimize the incidence of work contingencies, ensure prompt and adequate compensation in the event of work contingencies and provide occupationally-disabled workers with rehabilitation services.
