HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02441
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING HONORARIA AND OTHER BENEFITS TO ALL ACCREDITED BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7883, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS INCENTIVES AND BENEFITS ACT'
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to grant accredited barangay health workers a monthly honorarium of P2,000 and insurance coverage of not less than P10,000 by the Government Service Insurance System or any accredited company.
