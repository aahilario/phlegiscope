FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING CALAYAN RAIL LOCALLY KNOWN AS 'PIDING' AS A PROTECTED BIRD IN THE PHILIPPINES, PROVIDING FOR THE PRESERVATION OF THE SAME AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATIONS OF FUNDS FOR THE PURPOSE
SHORT TITLE : Calayan Rail Act
ABSTRACT : "Calayan Rail Act." The bill seeks to declare the Calayan rail or "piding" as an endangered specie to save it from extinction.
