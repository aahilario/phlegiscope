House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00003
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN THE UNIVERSITY OF THE PHILIPPINES AS THE PREMIER STATE UNIVERSITY
SHORT TITLE : "The University of the Philippines Charter of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The University of the Philippines Charter of 2007". The bill seeks, among others, to: a) create a University System Council; and b) distribute the functions of governance to the Board of Regents, the University President and other administrators, and the University Council.
