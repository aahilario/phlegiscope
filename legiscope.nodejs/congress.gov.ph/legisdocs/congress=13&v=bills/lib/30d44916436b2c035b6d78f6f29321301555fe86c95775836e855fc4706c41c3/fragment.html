HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02099
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING AN ACROSS THE BOARD SALARY GRADE INCREASE OF PUBLIC LEARNING FACILITATORS, INCLUDING NON-TEACHING POSITIONS IN THE NATIONAL, REGIONAL, DIVISION, SCHOOLS DISTRICT, AND SCHOOL LEVEL, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREOF AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to upgrade by two salary grades the present salary grade levels of all public learning facilitators, including non-teaching positions, in the national, regional, district and school levels.
