House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05067
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING ARTICLES ONE HUNDRED SEVENTY-SEVEN AND ONE HUNDRED SEVENTY-NINE OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSE
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the penalty for usurpation of authority or official functions and for the illegal use of uniforms or insignia of the military, police or public safety personnel.
