House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01531
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A NATIONAL BOOK DEVELOPMENT TRUST FUND TO SUPPORT FILIPINO AUTHORSHIP
SHORT TITLE : National Book Development Trust Fund Act
ABSTRACT : "National Book Development Trust Fund Act". The bill seeks to establish the Trust Fund to support and promote Filipino authorship especially in science and technology and in subject areas wherein locally authored books are few or non-existent.
