House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00446
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN AND ENHANCE THE USE OF ENGLISH AS THE MEDIUM OF INSTRUCTION IN PHILIPPINE SCHOOLS
SHORT TITLE : "Strengthening and Enhancing the Use of English as the Medium of Instruction Act"
ABSTRACT : "Strengthening and Enhancing the Use of English as the Medium of Instruction Act". The bill mandates the use of English as the medium of instruction in pre-schools, elementary and high schools. In the college level, the teaching of English shall focus on the language and terminology of business and industry.
