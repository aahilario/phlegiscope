HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00034
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING ALL AGRICULTURAL LANDS WITH NOT LESS THAN TEN PERCENT (10%) AND NOT MORE THAN THIRTY PERCENT (30%) PLANTED TO TREES FROM THE COVERAGE RA 6657, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE COMPREHENSIVE AGRARIAN REFORM LAW, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE
ABSTRACT : The measure exempts all agricultural lands with ares of not less than 10% and not more than 30% planted to trees from the Comprehensive Agrarian Reform Law of 1988.
