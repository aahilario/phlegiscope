House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03775
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 3 (B) OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3518, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7169, ENTITLED "AN ACT CREATING THE PHILIPPINE VETERANS BANK AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill limits the ownership of shares of stock in the Veterans Bank to 1,000 shares per veteran, widow or compulsory heir. It also proposes to extend ownership of the Bank's stocks to veterans other than World War II veterans.
