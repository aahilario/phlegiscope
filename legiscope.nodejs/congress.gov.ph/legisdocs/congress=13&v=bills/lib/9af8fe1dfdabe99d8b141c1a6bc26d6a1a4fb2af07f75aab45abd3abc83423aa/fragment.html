House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01638
FULL TITLE : THE MAGNA CARTA FOR PRIVATE SCHOOL TEACHERS
SHORT TITLE : Magna Carta for Private School Teachers
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta for Private School Teachers." The bill seeks to give private school teachers the following: a) security of tenure, b) the right against discrimination, c) academic freedom, d) the right to self-organization, and e) benefits related to health and study leaves.
