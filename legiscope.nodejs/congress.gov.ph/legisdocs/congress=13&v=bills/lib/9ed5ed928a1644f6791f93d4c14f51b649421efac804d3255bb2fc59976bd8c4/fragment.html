HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02357
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL MEMORIAL CEMETERY IN EVERY REGION OR MAJOR ISLAND IN THE PHILIPPINES AND PROVIDING FOR THEIR ADMINSITRATION AND MAINTENANCE
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to establish in each region and major island at least one national cemetery which shall serve as a memorial and final resting place for veterans, military personnel and others who rendered distinguished service to the country.
