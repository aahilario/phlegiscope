House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01490
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL PROVINCES AND HIGHLY-URBANIZED CITIES TO ESTABLISH A TECHNICAL VOCATIONAL AND SKILLS TRAINING SCHOOLS
SHORT TITLE : "Technical Vocational and Skills Training School Establishment Act"
ABSTRACT : "Technical Vocational and Skills Training School Establishment Act". The bill mandates all provinces and cities to establish technical vocation and skills training schools to make quality technical vocation education accessible to out0of0school youth and the general public.
