HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02522
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 144 OF BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 68, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CORPORATION CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES BY INCREASING THE RANGE OF PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS OF ITS PROVISIONS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill increases the penalties for violations of the Corporation Code to a fine of not less than P10,000 but not more than P100,000 and imprisonment of not less than six (6) years but not more than twelve (12) years.
