FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8291, AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1146, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "REVISED GOVERNMENT SERVICE INSURANCE ACT OF 1977," AS AMENDED
