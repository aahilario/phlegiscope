House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01453
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MORE RESPONSIVE AND COMPREHENSIVE REGULATION FOR THE PRACTICE, LICENSING, AND REGISTRATION OF ENVIRONMENTAL AND SANITARY ENGINEERS IN THE PHILIPPINES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 1364, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : New Environmental and Sanitary Engineering Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "New Environmental and Sanitary Engineering Act of 2004." The measure creates the Board of Examiners for Environmental and Sanitary Engineers to issue certificates of registration for the practice of sanitary engineering. It provides for the powers and duties of the Board and the qualification of its members. It further provides for the requirements and qualifications of applicants to obtain a license.
