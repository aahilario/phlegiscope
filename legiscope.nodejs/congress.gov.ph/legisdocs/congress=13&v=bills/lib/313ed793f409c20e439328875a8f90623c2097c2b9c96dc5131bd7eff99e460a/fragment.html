FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO INTERCONNECT THROUGH THE INTERNET ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES, PROVINCES, CITIES AND MUNICIPALITIES IN THE PHILIPPINES, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes the interconnection through the internet of all government agencies, provinces, cities and municipalities to solve the perennial problem of lack of communication facility.
