HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01971
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PARAMETERS OF ECONOMIC, SOCIAL AND CULTURAL RELATIONS BETWEEN THE PEOPLE OF THE PHILIPPINES AND THE PEOPLE IN TAIWAN
SHORT TITLE : Taiwan Economic and Cultural Relations Act
ABSTRACT : "Taiwan Economic and Cultural Relations Act." The bill seeks the creation of the Philippine Economic and Cultural Center to promote and protect the interest of Filipinos in Taiwan, providing for its powers and functions. It also aims to foster a closer relationships between the people of the Philippines and Taiwan.
