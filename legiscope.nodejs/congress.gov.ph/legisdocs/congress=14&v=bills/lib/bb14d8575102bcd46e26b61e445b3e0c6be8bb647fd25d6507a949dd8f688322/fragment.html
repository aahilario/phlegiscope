House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00245
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE PUBLICATION OF LEWD PHOTOGRAPHS AND SEX STORIES AND ARTICLES IN TABLOID AND BROADSHEET NEWSPAPERS, PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to prohibit the publication of lewd photographs and articles in tabloids and broadsheet newspapers to avoid the evolution of our dailies into a smut-dominated medium of information.
