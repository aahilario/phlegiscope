HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02860
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DISQUALIFYING ANY PERSON WHO HAS BEEN DEFEATED IN THREE CONSECUTIVE ELECTIONS FROM RUNNING FOR THE SAME PUBLIC OFFICE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 881, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE OMNIBUS ELECTION CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to disallow a person who has been defeated in three consecutive elections from running again for the same public office.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ESCUDERO, SALVADOR III H.
DATE FILED : 2007-10-09
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
