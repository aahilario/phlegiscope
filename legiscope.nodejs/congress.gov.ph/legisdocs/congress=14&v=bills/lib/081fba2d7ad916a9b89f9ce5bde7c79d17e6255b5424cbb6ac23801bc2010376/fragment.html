HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03516
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROMOTE AND HASTEN THE DEVELOPMENT OF MICROFINANCE NGOs IN THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : "Microfinance NGOs Development Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill grants non-stock, nonprofit BIR accredited non-government organizations or foundations engaged in the provision of microfinance services the following benefits: a) donee institution status; and b) exemption from liability for past due and unremitted taxes.
