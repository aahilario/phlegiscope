HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00593
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING THE FEES AND CHARGES ON FIREARMS, AMMUNITIONS, SPARE PARTS, ACCESSORIES, COMPONENTS, EXPLOSIVES, EXPLOSIVE INGREDIENTS, PYROTECHNICS AND FIRECRACKERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 256, S. 1995
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to adjust the rates and charges being collected by the Firearms and Explosives Division (FED) of the PNP. It proposes to retain 20% of the total collections from firearms and explosives fees and charges to fund the direct and operational expenses of FED.
