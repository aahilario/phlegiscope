House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02459
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING THE COMPREHENSIVE CRIME PREVENTION AND CRIMINAL JUSTICE ACT OF 2004, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Comprehensive Crime Prevention and Criminal Justice Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Comprehensive Crime Prevention and Criminal Justice Act of 2004." The bill provides for an integrated system of planning, implementation and enforcement of crime prevention and criminal justice policies, programs and projects based on United Nations Standards and Norms in Crime Prevention and Criminal Justice. It provides for the creation of the Crime Prevention and Criminal Justice Synergism Board and the National Action Center.
