House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01212
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING BUSINESS ONE-STOP SHOP (BOSS) CENTERS FOR SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES IN ALL REGIONAL AND PROVINCIAL CENTERS IN THE COUNTRY
SHORT TITLE : Business One-Stop-Shop (BOSS) Center Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Business One-Stop-Shop (BOSS) Center Act of 2004." This bill mandates all government agencies involved in business start-ups to situate themselves, through a branch or satellite office, in the so-called Business One-Stop-Shop or BOSS Center to assist prospective entrepreneurs in securing documentary/business requirements/permits.
