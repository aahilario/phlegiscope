House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01633
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REGULATE THE OPERATION OF ALL REVIEW CENTERS FOR BOARD AND OTHER FORMS OF LOCAL AND INTERNATIONAL PROFESSIONAL EXAMINATIONS NATIONWIDE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Review Centers Regulation Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Review Centers Regulation Act of 2004." The bill mandates the Commission on Higher Education to establish a system of registration and accreditation of public and private review centers which offer review classes, seminars and programs to prepare for a professional board examination.
