House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04140
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AMEND REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINE HUNDRED AND TEN, AS AMENDED, AND TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO IMPLEMENT ITS PROVISION
ABSTRACT : The bill grants the surviving spouse of a justice or a retired justice of the Supreme Court and the Court of Appeals a monthly pension equivalent to 80% of the amount received by the justice or retired justice-pensioner until he/she remarries or dies.
