HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01071
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RENAMING THE BOY SCOUTS OF THE PHILIPPINES TO SCOUT PHILIPPINES, DEFINING ITS OBJECTIVES, POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE COMMONWEALTH ACT NO. 111, AS AMENDED
SHORT TITLE : Scouts Philippines Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Scouts Philippines Act of 2004." To enhance the practice of scouting in the country, the bill proposes the inclusion of girls into the Boy Scouts of the Philippines (BSP) and rename the same to the Scouts Philippines. It proposes to grant the organization tax exemptions and to name the President as the Chief Scout of the Scouts Philippines.
