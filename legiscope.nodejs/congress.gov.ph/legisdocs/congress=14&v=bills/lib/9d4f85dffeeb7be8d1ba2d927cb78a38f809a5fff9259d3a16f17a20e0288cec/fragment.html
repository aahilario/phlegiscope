House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00071
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS FOR FILING OF CERTIFICATES OF CANDIDACY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SEC. 74 OF BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 881, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE OMNIBUS ELECTION CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill requires the presentation of a National Bureau of Investigation (NBI) clearance and Police clearance in the filing of certificates of candidacy of persons aspiring to run for public office.
