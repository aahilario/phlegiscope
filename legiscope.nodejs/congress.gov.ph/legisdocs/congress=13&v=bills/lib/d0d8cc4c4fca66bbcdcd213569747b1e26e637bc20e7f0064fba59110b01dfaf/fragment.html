HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01365
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AMEND CERTAIN SECTIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SEVEN THOUSAND NINE HUNDRED SIXTEEN, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE ACT OF 1995, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Seeks to strengthen the PEZA (Philippine Economic Zone Authority) Law by imposing stiffer requirements for those who will be nominated and appointed to manage the PEZA. The bill also ensures that appropriate and due compensation is given to persons or organizations affected and/or displaced by the establishment or expansion programs of the PEZA.
