HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03425
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE USE OF PHILIPPINE MADE MATERIALS IN CONTRACTS FOR PUBLIC WORKS AND PROVIDING FOR THE BLACKLISTING OF CONTRACTORS VIOLATING SUCH REQUIREMENT
SHORT TITLE : "Preference for Philippine Materials in Public Contracts Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the blacklisting of contractors, subcontractors, materialmen and suppliers who fail to use Philippine made products, materials and supplies in any public building or public work.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.
DATE FILED : 2008-01-28
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
