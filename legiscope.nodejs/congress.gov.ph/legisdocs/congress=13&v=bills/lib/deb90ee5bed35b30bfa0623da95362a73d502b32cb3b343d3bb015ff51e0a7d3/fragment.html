HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00398
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXPANDING THE TAXING AND OTHER REVENUE-RAISING POWERS OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The proposed amendment to the Local Government Code (LGC) expands the exclusive authority of municipalities to grant fishery privileges in the municipal waters by explicitly including lakes and gulfs within the coverage of the LGC. The bill further expands the authority of local government units to grant tax exemption privileges by including any and all taxes levied and imposed by LGUs.
