House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00216
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING ARTICLE 16 OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 209 AS AMENDED BY EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 227, OTHEWISE KNOWN AS THE FAMILY CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, MAKING IT MANDATORY FOR COUPLES TO UNDERGO COUNSELING PRIOR TO THE ISSUANCE OF A MARRIAGE LICENSE
SHORT TITLE : Mandatory Marriage Counseling Act
ABSTRACT : "Mandatory Marriage Counseling Act". The present law only prescribes marriage counseling in cases where parental consent or parental advise is needed. This bill aims to provide for mandatory counseling for all couples intending to marry. It seeks to educate couples on the rigors of marriage and how to cope with the challenges that will face them everyday for the rest of their lives.
