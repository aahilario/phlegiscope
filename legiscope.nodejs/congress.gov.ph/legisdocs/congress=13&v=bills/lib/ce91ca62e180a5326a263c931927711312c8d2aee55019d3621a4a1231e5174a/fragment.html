HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01353
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING GREATER PROTECTION TO LOCAL INDUSTRIES THROUGH THE IMPOSITION OF ANTI-DUMPING DUTY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 301, PART 2, TITLE II, BOOK 1 OF THE TARIFF AND CUSTOMS CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8752
ABSTRACT : The bill deletes the provision in the Tariff and Customs Code of the Philippines which allows the Tariff Commission to decide whether or not to impose a definitive anti-dumping duty even when all the requirements for the imposition of said duty have been fulfilled. It further provides that 50% of the dumping duties collected from a particular dumping protest shall be returned to the petitioner.
