House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03600
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PHILIPPINE AIR FORCE ACADEMY (PAFA) AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Air Force Academy Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Air Force Academy Act of 2004." The bill seeks to establish the Philippine Air Force Academy (PAFA) in the Air Forces of the Philippines (AFP). The PAFA shall provide AFP cadets with instruction and prepare them for military service.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : EULOGIO "AMANG" R. MAGSAYSAY, Ph.D.
