House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01707
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING FROM THE EXPANDED VALUE ADDED TAX THE SALE OF GOODS AND SERVICES TO SENIOR CITIZENS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9257, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'EXPANDED SENIOR CITIZENS ACT OF 2003'
ABSTRACT : The bill exempts the sale of goods and services to senior citizens from the expanded value-added tax to enable them to enjoy the full 20% discount privilege.
