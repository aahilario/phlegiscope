HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00494
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 10(1) AND SECTION 11 OF REPUBLIC ACT 7156 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'MINI-HYDROELECTRIC POWER INCENTIVE ACT'
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for 60% of the special privilege tax collected from mini-hydroelectric power developers to be remitted directly to the host local government unit as its share and for the remaining 40% to be remitted to the national government.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : DOMOGAN, MAURICIO G.
