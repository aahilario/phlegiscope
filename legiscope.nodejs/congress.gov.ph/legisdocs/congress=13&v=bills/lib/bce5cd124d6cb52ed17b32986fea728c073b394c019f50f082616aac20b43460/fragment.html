HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01337
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO UPGRADE THE SALARY SCALE OF A RETIRING GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEE BY ONE GRADE FOR PURPOSES OF HIS/HER RETIREMENT
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to offer a decent retirement benefit package to government personnel by upgrading the salary grade of a retiring employee by one grade on the date of his retirement. The upgraded salary scale shall then be used as the basis in computing the retirement benefits of the retiring employee.
