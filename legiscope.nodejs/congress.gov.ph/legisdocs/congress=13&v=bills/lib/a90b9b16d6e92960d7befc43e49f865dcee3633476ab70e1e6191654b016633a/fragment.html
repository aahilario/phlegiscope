HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03137
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPOSING A TEN PERCENT (10%) AD VALOREM TAX ON FLAVORED OR COLOURED SYRUPS EXCLUSIVELY USED IN THE MANUFACTURE OF SOFTDRINKS BY INSERTING A NEW SECTION, THEREBY AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CHAPTER VI, TITLE VI OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes a 10% ad valorem tax on flavored or coloured syrups exclusively used in manufacturing softdrinks. The tax shall be based on the manufacturer's selling price, or the total value of importation.
