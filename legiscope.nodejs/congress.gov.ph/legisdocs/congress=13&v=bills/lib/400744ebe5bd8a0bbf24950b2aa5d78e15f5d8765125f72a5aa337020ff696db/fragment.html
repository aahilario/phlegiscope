HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04645
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING ALL PUBLIC SCHOOL LIBRARIES INCLUDING STATE COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES TO PROVIDE NEWSPAPERS AS PART OF THEIR REFERENCE MATERIALS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill requires all public elementary and secondary schools and state universities and colleges to subscribe to newspapers of general circulation for their library.
