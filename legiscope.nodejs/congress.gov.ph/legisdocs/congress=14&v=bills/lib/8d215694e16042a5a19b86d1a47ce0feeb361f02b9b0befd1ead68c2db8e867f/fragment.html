House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01890
REPUBLIC ACT NO. RA09503
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ENLARGING THE ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE OF THE COURT OF TAX APPEALS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CERTAIN SECTIONS OF THE LAW CREATING THE COURT OF TAX APPEALS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Enlarging the Organizational Structure of the Court of Tax Appeals
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to add a Third Division to the Court of Tax Appeals and increase its membership to nine members composed of a Presiding Justice and 8 Associate Justices sitting en banc or in three Divisions with 3 members each.
