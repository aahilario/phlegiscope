HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03850
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING THE JURISDICTION OF LABOR CASES BETWEEN THE NATIONAL LABOR RELATIONS COMMISSION (NLRC) AND THE REGIONAL OFFICES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF LABOR AND EMPLOYMENT (DOLE), AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE, ARTICLES 128 AND 217 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBER FOUR HUNDRED FORTY-TWO (PD 442), AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to transfer the jurisdiction over labor cases from the National Labor Relations Commission to the regional offices of the Department of Labor and Employment REGARDLESS OF THE AMOUNT OF CLAIM.
