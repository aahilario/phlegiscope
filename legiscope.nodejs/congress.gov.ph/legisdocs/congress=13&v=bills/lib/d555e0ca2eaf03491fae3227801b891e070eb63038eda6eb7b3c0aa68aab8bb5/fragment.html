House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03495
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PHILIPPINE AIR FORCE ACADEMY (PAFA) AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks the establishment in the Philippine Air Force (PAF) of an educational institution to be known as the Philippine Air Force Academy that shall provide PAF cadets instruction and preparation for military service.
