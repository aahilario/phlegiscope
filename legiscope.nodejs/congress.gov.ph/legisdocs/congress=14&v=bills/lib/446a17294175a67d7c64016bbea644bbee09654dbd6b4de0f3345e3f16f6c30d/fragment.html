HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01242
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE REGISTRATION, LICENSURE AND PRACTICE OF OCCUPATIONAL THERAPY, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine Occupational Therapy Law"
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Occupational Therapy Law". The bill aims to upgrade and update the competence, knowledge, skills and attitudes of Filipino therapists and physical therapists to make them globally competitive and nationally accessible to the disabled.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : GONZALES, AURELIO JR. D.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-23
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
