HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01243
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ABOLISHING THE SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE THE PERTINENT PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill calls for the abolition of the sangguniang kabataan to enable the youth to concentrate on their studies rather on politics where they can easily be victimized or pursuaded to do anomalies acts.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : GUNIGUNDO, MAGTANGGOL T.
