HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03091
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE EQUAL REPRESENTATION OF THE NFA, RICE MILLERS AND FARMERS IN THE IMPORTATION OF RICE AND PRODUCTION OF LOCAL CROPS AND PROVIDING FOR A TREASURY CROP CERTIFICATE SYSTEM IN THE PROCUREMENT OF GRAINS BY THE NATIONAL FOOD AUTHORITY GUARANTEED BY THE DEPARTMENT OF FINANCE, FOR THE REALIZATION OF A HARMONIOUS RELATIONSHIP AMONG THE THREE SECTORS
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes rice millers to import and distribute imported rice and to buy the palay produce of farmers at ceiling prices set by the National Food Authority (NFA). It directs the NFA to issue Treasury Crop Certificates which are negotiable instruments that can be sold, bartered, purchased and/or issued as collateral for any loan or debt incurred.
