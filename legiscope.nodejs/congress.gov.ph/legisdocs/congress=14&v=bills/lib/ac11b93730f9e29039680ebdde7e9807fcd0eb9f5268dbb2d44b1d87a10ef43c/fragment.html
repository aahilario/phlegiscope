HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02075
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION (DEPED) AND/OR THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNIT (LGU) WHERE THEY ARE LOCATED, AUTOMATIC, ABSOLUTE AND INCONTROVERTIBLE OWNERSHIP, TITLING AND REGISTRATION OF SCHOOL SITES FOR THE EXCLUSIVE USE OF PUBLIC ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY SCHOOLS OF THE PHILIPPINES AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to grant the Department of Education and/or the local government unit automatic, absolute and inconvertible ownership of the land where public elementary and secondary schools are situated.
