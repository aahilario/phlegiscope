HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01630
FULL TITLE : AN ACT POSTPONING THE BARANGAY AND SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN ELECTIONS AND SYNCHRONIZING IT WITH THE NATIONAL ELECTIONS TO BE HELD ON THE SECOND MONDAY OF MAY 2010 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to reset the barangay and sangguniang kabataan elections and synchronize it with the national and local elections to be held on the second Monday of May 2010.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : MADRONA, ELEANDRO JESUS F.
