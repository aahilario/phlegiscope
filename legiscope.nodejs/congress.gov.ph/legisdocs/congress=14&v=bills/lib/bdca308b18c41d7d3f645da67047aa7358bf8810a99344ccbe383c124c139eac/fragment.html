House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02881
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1185, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE FIRE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes criminal liability on the actions or omissions of public officers who, through negligence or malice, the same resulted to or failed to prevent the loss of life and/or damage to property.
