House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03159
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROMOTE THE USE OF ETHYL ALCOHOL OR ETHANOL AS ALTERNATIVE TRANSPORT FUEL, ESTABLISHING FOR THE PURPOSE THE NATIONAL FUEL ETHANOL PROGRAM, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Fuel Ethanol Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Fuel Ethanol Act of 2004." The bill provides for the use of ethanol as an alternative fuel through the Fuel Ethanol Program. The Program shall launch the production of unconventional and replenishable sources of energy and fuel.
