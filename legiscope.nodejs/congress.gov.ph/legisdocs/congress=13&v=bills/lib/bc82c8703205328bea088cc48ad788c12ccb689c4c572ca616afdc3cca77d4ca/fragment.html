HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03699
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE CONFERMENT OF BACHELOR OF LAWS DEGREE AS EQUIVALENT TO A MASTERS DEGREE
ABSTRACT : The bill declares the conferment of a Bachelor of Laws degree equivalent to a Master degree for purposes of appointment to positions requiring such educational attainment.
