House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02073
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR DEBT-RELIEF MEASURES TO REHABILITATE FINANCIALLY DISTRESSED ENTERPRISES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Corporate Recovery Act
ABSTRACT : "Corporate Recovery Act." The bill offers distraught enterprises debt-relief measures that will secure their survival and, at the same time, protect the rights of creditors with duly registered liens against the assets of these enterprises.
