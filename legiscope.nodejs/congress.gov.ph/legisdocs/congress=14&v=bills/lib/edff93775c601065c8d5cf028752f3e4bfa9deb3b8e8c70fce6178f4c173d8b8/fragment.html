House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01202
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING THE ARCHIPELAGIC BASELINES OF THE PHILIPPINE ARCHIPELAGO, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3046, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5446
SHORT TITLE : "Archipelagic Baselines Law of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : "Archipelagic Baselines Law of the Philippines". The bill seeks to amend RA 3046, as amended by RA 5446, by drawing straight baselines on the outermost points of Scarborough Shoal and joinging them with the baselines defined under RA 3046, as amended.
