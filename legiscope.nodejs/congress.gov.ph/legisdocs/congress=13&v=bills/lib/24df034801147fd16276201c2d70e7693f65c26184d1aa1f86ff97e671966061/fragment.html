House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01460
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE PLANTING OF TREES IN OPEN SPACES OF SUBDIVISION PROJECTS DEVELOPED FOR RESIDENTIAL, INDUSTRIAL, OR COMMERCIAL PURPOSE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Greening Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Greening Act of 2004." The bill requires land developers to reserve at least 30% of the gross area to be developed as an open space for parks, playgrounds, recreational uses, schools, roads, churches and other similar amenities.
