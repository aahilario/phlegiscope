House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01100
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 4 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7432, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9257, TO COMPENSATE THE NEGATIVE EFFECT OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9337 ON THE DISCOUNT PRIVILEGE OF SENIOR CITIZENS PROVIDED THEREUNDER AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to increase the discount privilege granted to senior citizens on their purchase of goods and services from 20% to 34%.
