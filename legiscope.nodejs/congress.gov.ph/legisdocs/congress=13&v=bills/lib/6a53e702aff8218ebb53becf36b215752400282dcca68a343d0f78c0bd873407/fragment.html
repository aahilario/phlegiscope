HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00124
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8799, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SECURITIES REGULATION CODE
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to amend certain provisions of RA 8799, otherwise known as the Securities Regulation Code, in order to promote a more vibrant and healthy capital market. The pertinent provisions, among others, are a) Section 19.1 - ON MANDATORY TENDER OFFERS; b) Section 28.4; (b) - ON MINIMUM CAPITAL REQUIREMENTS; c) Section 30.1 - ON BROKER-DIRECTOR PROHIBITION; and d) Section 33.2 (c) - ON LIMITATION ON OWNERSHIP OF AN EXCHANGE.
