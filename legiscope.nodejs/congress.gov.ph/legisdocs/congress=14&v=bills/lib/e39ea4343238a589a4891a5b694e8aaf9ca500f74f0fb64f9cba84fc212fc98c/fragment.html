HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01238
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING SURVIVORSHIP BENEFITS TO THE MEMBERS OF THE JUDICIARY AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill grants the surviving spouse of a justice or judge who dies while receiving the retirement pension a monthly survivorship pension equivalent to 80% of the basic monthly pension of the justice or judge.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CASTRO, FRED H.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-23
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
