House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01207
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A NATIONAL ECOTOURISM POLICY, ESTABLISHING A FRAMEWORK FOR ITS INSTITUTIONALIZATION, CREATING MECHANISM FOR ITS IMPLEMENTATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Ecotourism Policy Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "National Ecotourism Policy Act of 2004." This bill identifies the areas comprising the ecotourism sites which include, but are not limited to, areas within the Integrated Protected Areas System as identified under RA 7586 and other areas with significant and relevant cultural heritage to be identified by the National Ecotourism Commission.
