FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING FARMERS AND FISHERMEN A DISCOUNT ON PETROLEUM PRODUCTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Farmers' and Fishermen's Petroleum Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The Farmers' and Fishermen's Petroleum Act of 2007". The bill grants qualified farmers and fishermen a one peso discount for every liter of petroleum purchased.
