HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00493
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING MALL AND STORE OWNERS ENGAGING IN THE SALE OF CONTRABAND PRODUCTS
ABSTRACT : Smuggled and pirated goods not only distort normal pricing mechanisms but also affect other market forces that contribute to the decline of the local economy. This bill seeks to clamp down on these illegal activities by penalizing store/stall owners who engage in the selling of contraband products. It also provide penalties for mall owners who tolerate the selling of contraband products by their tenants.
