HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01922
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR EDUCATIONAL BENEFITS FOR THE MINOR CHILDREN OR LAW ENFORCEMENT OFFICERS WHO ARE KILLED BY INSURGENTS OR SLAIN IN THE LINE OF DUTY, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides educational benefits for minor children and dependents of law enforcement officers killed by insurgents or slain in the line of duty. The benefits shall include tuition fee and a reasonable allowance for books, school supplies, transportation, clothing and food.
