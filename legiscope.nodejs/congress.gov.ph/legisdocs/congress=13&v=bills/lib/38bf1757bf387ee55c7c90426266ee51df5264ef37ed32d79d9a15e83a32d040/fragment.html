HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04405
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING MANPOWER AND LIVELIHOOD TRAINING CENTERS IN ALL THE PROVINCES OF THE COUNTRY AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The measure creates the Manpower and Livelihood Training Center in all provinces throughout the country. These training centers shall function under the scope and supervision of the TESDA and shall be based on a program prepared by the TESDA in coordination with the local development councils.
