HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03013
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF INTEGRATED REFORMATORY CENTERS FOR METROPOLITAN MANILA AND OTHER HIGHLY URBANIZED CENTERS IN THE COUNTRY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION SIXTY-THREE OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6975, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT ACT OF 1990" AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the Bureau of Jail Management and Penology to establish integrated reformatory centers in Metro Manila and other highly urbanized centers and to transfer the existing jails to a common site. It also provides for the Supreme Court to establish court rooms in the integrated reformatory centers.
