HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04371
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING LARGE CITIES AND MUNICIPALITIES TO ASSUME RESPONSIBILITY OVER FIRE PROTECTION SERVICES, AMENDING SECTION 56 OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTY NINE HUNDRED AND SEVENTY FIVE OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT ACT OF 1990" AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes large cities and municipalities to assume the responsibility for fire protection services to enable local government units to improve and modernize their fire protection facilities and equipment.
