House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03866
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE INTERNAL REVENUE AUTHORITY, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Internal Revenue Authority Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Internal Revenue Authority Act of 2004." The measure provides for the restructuring of the Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR), organizational structure of the proposed Internal Revenue Authority, the composition and powers of the Internal Revenue Board and the absorption of BIR employees.
