House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01791
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING ADDITIONAL INSURANCE BENEFITS TO BARANGAY CAPTAINS, PROVIDING FUNDS FOR THE PAYMENT OF PREMIUMS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Expanded Barangay Captains' Insurance Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Expanded Barangay Captains' Insurance Act of 2007". The bill grants barangay captains the following additional insurance benefits: a) P250,000 death benefits if caused by assassination, accident or other similar circumstances; b) 50,000 burial benefit; and c) P100,000 reimbursement of actual medical expenses.
