House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02961
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE CONSTITUTIONAL RIGHT TO SECURITY OF TENURE
SHORT TITLE : "Security of Tenure of the Workers Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to penalize the misclassification of regular employees into other kinds of employees. It aims to harmonize the laws on subcontracting and regular employment.
