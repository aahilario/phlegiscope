HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04630
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE IMPOSITION OF OR EXACTION OF DORMANCY FEES ON INACTIVE DEPOSIT BANK ACCOUNTS OR OF ARBITRARILY CHARGING SERVICE FEES FOR SAVINGS ACCOUNTS BELOW THE MINIMUM BALANCE REQUIREMENT WITHOUT THE PRIOR APPROVAL OF THE DEPOSITOR AND PRESCRIBING PENALTIES THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits banks from imposing, exacting, collecting or charging a) dormancy fees on inactive bank deposits and b) service fees on savings accounts whose balance fall below the bank's minimum balance requirement.
