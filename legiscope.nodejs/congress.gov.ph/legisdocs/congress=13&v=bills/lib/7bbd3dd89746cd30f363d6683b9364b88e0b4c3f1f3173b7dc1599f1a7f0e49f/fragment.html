HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00452
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING REAL ESTATE DEVELOPERS TO PLANT AT LEAST FIFTY (50) SHADY TREES FOR EVERY HECTARE OF THE SUBDIVISION PROJECT THEY ARE DEVELOPING AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to require real estate developers to plant at least 50 shady trees per hectare of the subdivision/residential areas they are developing. This will provide ecological balance and add up to the aesthetic appearance of the place. It will also greatly help in preventing natural disasters such as flooding and landslides.
