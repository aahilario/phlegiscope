House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00430
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 53, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS 'AN ACT TO EXEMPT THE PUBLISHER, EDITOR OR REPORTER OF ANY PUBLICATION FROM REVEALING THE SOURCE OF PUBLISHED NEWS OR INFORMATION OBTAINED IN CONFIDENCE' BY INCLUDING WITHIN ITS COVERAGE JOURNALISTS FROM BROADCAST, NEWS AGENCIES AND INTERNET PUBLICATIONS
SHORT TITLE : Includes Within the Coverage of RA 53 Journalists From Broadcast, News Agencies and Internet Publications
ABSTRACT : The bill includes within the coverage of RA 53 (Sotto Law) journalists from broadcast stations, news agencies and internet publication. With their inclusion in the ambit of RA 53, journalists can not be compelled to reveal the source of a confidential news report or information except when a court or or committee of Congress finds that the revelation is demanded for the security of the State.
