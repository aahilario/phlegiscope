HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00035
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING FROM WITHHOLDING TAX THE INTEREST EARNINGS ON BONDS ISSUED BY THE LANDBANK OF THE PHILIPPINES TO LANDOWNERS ON THE SALE OF THEIR LANDS COVERED BY THE COMPREHENSIVE AGRARIAN REFORM LAW
ABSTRACT : The bill exempts from the withholding tax all interest earnings on bonds issued by the Land Bank to landowners for the sale of their lands covered by the Comprehensive Agrarian Reform Law.
