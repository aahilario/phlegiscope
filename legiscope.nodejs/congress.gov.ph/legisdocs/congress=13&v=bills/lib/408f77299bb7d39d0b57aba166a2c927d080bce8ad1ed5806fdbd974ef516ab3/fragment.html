HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00843
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING THE DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE THE AUTHORITY TO SETTLE MINOR CRIMINAL OFFENSES
ABSTRACT : This bill authorizes to the Department of Justice (DOJ) through the Office of the Chief State Prosecutor, to bring together to an extra-judicial settlement the parties to a criminal action involving minor offenses. Minor offenses are offenses punishable by imprisonment not exceeding one (1) year and/or a fine not exceeding Five Thousand Pesos (P5,000.00).
