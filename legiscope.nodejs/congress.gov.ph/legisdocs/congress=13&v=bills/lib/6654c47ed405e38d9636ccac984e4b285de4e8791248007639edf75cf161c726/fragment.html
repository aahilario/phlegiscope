House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01001
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REGULATE THE SALE, PURCHASE AND POSSESSION OF TOXIC AND HAZARDOUS SUBSTANCES, INCLUDING SODIUM CYANIDE PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Toxic and Hazardous Substances Control of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Toxic and Hazardous Substances Control of 2004." To preserve our aquamarine resources and ensure our food supply, the bill seeks to regulate the sale/purchase, possession, use, transport, storage, transfer and disposal of toxic substances by requiring a permit from the Philippine National Police and the Department of the Interior and Local Government.
