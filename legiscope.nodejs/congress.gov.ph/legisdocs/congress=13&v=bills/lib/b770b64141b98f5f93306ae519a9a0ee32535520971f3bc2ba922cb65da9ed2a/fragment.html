House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04218
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE CONCURRENCE OF THE SANGGUNIANG PANLALAWIGAN OR PANLUNGSOD, AS THE CASE MAY BE, FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF TERMINALS, OUTLETS, OR AGENTS OF ANY GAME OF CHANCE AUTHORIZED UNDER EXISTING LAWS
SHORT TITLE : Requiring the Concurrence of the Sangguniang Panlalawigan or Panlungsod for the Establishment of Any Game of Chance
ABSTRACT : The bill requires the concurrence of the sangguniang panlalawigan or panlungsod for the establishment of lotto terminals, jai alai fronton stations, bingo outlets or agents of any game of chance.
