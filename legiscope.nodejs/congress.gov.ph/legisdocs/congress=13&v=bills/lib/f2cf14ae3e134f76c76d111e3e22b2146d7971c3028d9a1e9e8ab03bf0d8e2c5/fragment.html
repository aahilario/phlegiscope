House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01018
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING MATERNITY LEAVE BENEFITS TO UNMARRIED PREGNANT WOMEN WHO ARE IN GOVERNMENT SERVICE
ABSTRACT : Unmarried pregnant women in the private sector enjoy maternity benefits under RA1161. This bill seeks to extend the same benefits to unmarried pregnant women in the government service by granting them maternity leave benefits equivalent to 60 days for normal delivery and 78 days in case of caesarian delivery.
