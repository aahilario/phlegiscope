HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02845
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING THE PRESENCE OF EMERGENCY OR EXTRAORDINARY CIRCUMSTANCES AS EFFECTS OF NATURAL AND MAN-MADE CALAMITIES AND PROVIDING FOR THE PROCEDURE FOR AND ESTABLISHING GUIDELINES ON THE IMPLEMENTATION OF INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS OF GOVERNMENT DURING OR IMMEIDATELY FOLLOWING THE EMERGENCY OR EXTRAORDINARY CIRCUMSTANCES
ABSTRACT : The bill defines the emergency and extraordinary cases where immediate response by infrastructure agencies are warranted. It sets the guidelines and procedures for a fast-track reaction by concerned agencies, especially in engineering design, procurement and implementation, and funding for the required infrastructure project.
