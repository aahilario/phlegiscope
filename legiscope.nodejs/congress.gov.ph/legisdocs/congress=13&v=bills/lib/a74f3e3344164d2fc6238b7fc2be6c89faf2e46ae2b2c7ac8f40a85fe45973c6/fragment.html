HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01416
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR ADDITIONAL BENEFITS AND PRIVILEGES TO PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS, AMENDING FOR THESE PURPOSES REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FORTY-SIX HUNDRED SEVENTY (REPUBLIC ACT NO. 4670), OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE MAGNA CARTA FOR PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure grants hardship allowance equal to 25% of monthly salary to teachers exposed to danger due to distant locations, natural calamities or armed conflict. Allows teachers one-year study leave after three years of service to pursue graduate studies.
