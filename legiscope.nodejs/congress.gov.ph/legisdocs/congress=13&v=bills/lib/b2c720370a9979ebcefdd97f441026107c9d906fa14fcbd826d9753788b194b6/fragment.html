House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02894
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ADOPTION OF A NEW BILINGUAL PROGRAM IN PHILIPPINE SCHOOLS AND THE USE OF ENGLISH AS MEDIUM OF INSTRUCTION
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the use of English as the medium of instruction in all subjects in the elementary and secondary levels, except in Pilipino taught as a subject.
