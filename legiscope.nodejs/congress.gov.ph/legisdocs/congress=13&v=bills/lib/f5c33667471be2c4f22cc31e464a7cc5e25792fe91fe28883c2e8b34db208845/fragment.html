HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00409
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SEVEN THOUSAND NINE HUNDRED FORTY-ONE, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS 'THE PARTY-LIST SYSTEM ACT'
ABSTRACT : RA 7941 failed to address the following concerns: 1) the procedure in allocating seats for party-list representatives; and 2) the liberal interpretation which allow mainstream political parties and organizations and non-marginalized sectors to join and participate in the party-list elections. This bill seeks to propose the following: a) a mechanism that will bridge the gap in the existing law; and b) disallow mainstream political parties and non-marginalized sectors from participating in the party-list elections.
