HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00612
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROMOTE THE PRODUCTION OF AROMATIC RICE AND FANCY RICE VARIETIES AND TO AUTHORIZE THE EXPORTATION THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The measure authorizes the exportation of sinampaguita, denorado, milagrosa, sinandomeng, maligaya selections 3,6, and 8, college #22, UPLB rice #5 and 7 and other such varieties of rice as the National Food Authority may determine.
