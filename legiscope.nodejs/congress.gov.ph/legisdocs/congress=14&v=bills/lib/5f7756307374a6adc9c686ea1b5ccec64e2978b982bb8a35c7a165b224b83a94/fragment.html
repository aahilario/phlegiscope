House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01311
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL GOVERNMENT ISSUED IDENTIFICATION CARDS, CERTIFICATES AND LICENSES TO INCLUDE A PORTION INDICATING THE BLOOD TYPE OF THE HOLDER THEREOF AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : "Blood Type Identification Act of 2004"
ABSTRACT : "Blood Type Identification Act of 2004". The measure seeks to prevent blood transfusion delays by requiring government IDs to include information on blood type.
