FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING DISCRIMINATION AGAINST A PERSON BY REASON OF AGE UPON HIRING FOR AND DURING EMPLOYMENT, PENALIZING ANY VIOLATION THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Anti-age Discrimination In Employment Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Anti-Age Discrimination in Employment Act of 2010.” Seeks to prohibit discrimination against any individual with respect to compensation and terms and conditions of employment on account of age.
