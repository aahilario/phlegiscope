HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00210
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING THE RIGHT TO FRANCHISEES TO DEVELOP THE RURAL AIRPORT INFRASTRUCTURE NETWORK IN THE PHILIPPINES FOR COMMERCIAL AND OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Our government places the highest priority on poverty alleviation, particularly in the countryside. As a strategy to achieve this, the government should focus on rural development coupled with private sector participation. The establishment of private rural airport infrastructures will encourage economic activity in the hinterlands, by serving not only as transportation hubs, but also as industrial, commercial and service centers which will provide the countryside employment to uplift the standard of living in the rural areas.
