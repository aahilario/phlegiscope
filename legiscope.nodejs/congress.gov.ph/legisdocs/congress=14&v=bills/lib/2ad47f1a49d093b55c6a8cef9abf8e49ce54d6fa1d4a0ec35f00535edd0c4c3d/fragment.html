House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01250
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMOTING THE LOCAL MOVIE INDUSTRY BY EXEMPTING THEATRES AND/OR CINEMAS FROM PAYMENT OF AMUSEMENT TAX, AMENDING SECTIONS 140 AND 152 (D) OF R.A. 7160, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to promote the local movie industry by exempting operators of theaters and cinemas showing locally-produced motion pictures and movies from the payment of the amusement tax.
