House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01571
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE WITNESS PROTECTION, SECURITY AND BENEFIT PROGRAM, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6981, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "WITNESS PROTECTION, SECURITY AND BENEFIT ACT" AND PROVIDING ADDITIONAL FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill provides, among others, the following: a) the establishment of an organic security for the Witness Protection, Security and Benefits Program; b) the imposition on the witness of a separate criminal liability for violation of the Memorandum of Agreement; and c) limits the extension of rights and benefits to relatives within the first degree of consanguinity.
