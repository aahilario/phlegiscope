House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02444
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 24, PARAGRAPH 2 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6770, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to amend Section 24 of RA 6770 on the power of the Ombudsman to preventively suspend any officer/employee under his authority pending an investigation. It authorizes the Ombudsman to impose up to a maximum of 90 days preventive suspension.
