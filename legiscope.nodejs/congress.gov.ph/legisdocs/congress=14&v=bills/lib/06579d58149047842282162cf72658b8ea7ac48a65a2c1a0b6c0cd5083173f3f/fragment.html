House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02408
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REORGANIZING AND STRENGTHENING THE CAPABILITY OF THE BUREAU OF IMMIGRATION, BY EXPANDING AND STREAMLINING ITS BUREAUCRACY, CREATION OF ADDITIONAL IMMIGRATION OFFICES, ESTABLISHMENT OF INTELLIGENCE UNITS, UPGRADING EMPLOYEE SKILLS AND BENEFITS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Bureau of Immigration Empowerment Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the number of Immigration Officers and personnel and to upgrade its technical equipment to enable it to effectively regulate the influx of aliens and to streamline the administration and enforcement of alien registration laws.
