HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01750
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE BENEFITS OF RETIRED GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE COMMONWEALTH ACT NUMBERED ONE HUNDRED EIGHTY-SIX, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to grant government employees who intend to retire under RA 660 or PD 1146 a 6-year annual lump sum instead of the usual 5-year annuity. The proposed monthly annuity shall be based on the highest monthly salary received prior to retirement instead of basing it on the average monthly salary received during the last 3 years of service.
