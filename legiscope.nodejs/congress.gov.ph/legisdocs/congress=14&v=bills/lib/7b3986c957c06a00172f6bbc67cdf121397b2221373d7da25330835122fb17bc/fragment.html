House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01964
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REVERTING TO THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT THE DISCHARGE OF HEALTH FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES DEVOLVED TO CERTAIN LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to revert to the national government the health functions and responsibilities devolved to local government units in order to attain quality health care service for our people.
