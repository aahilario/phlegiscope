House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00453
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN WITHIN SMALL ISLANDS AS AGRICULTURAL LAND OPEN TO DISPOSITION FOR COMMERCIAL ECO-TOURISM AND OTHER PRODUCTIVE PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Declaring Certain Parcels of Land of the Public Domain Within Small Islands as Agricultural Land Open to Disposition for Commercial Eco-Tourism
ABSTRACT : The bill declares as agricultural lands certain parcels of land of the public domain within small islands with land areas of 50,000 hectares or less, already devoted to or with great potential for eco-tourism ventures. These lands shall be open to disposition for commercial eco-tourism purposes provided that they have been subjected to a public cadastral survey.
