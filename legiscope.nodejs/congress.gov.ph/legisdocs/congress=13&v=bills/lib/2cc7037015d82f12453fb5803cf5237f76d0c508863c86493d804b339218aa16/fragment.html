HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02437
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEVOLVING THE HEALTH CARE PROGRAMS OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT, INCLUDING FUNDS AND RESOURCES, TO THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the devolution of the national government's health care program, including funds and resources, to provinces, cities, municipalities and barangays. Each local government unit shall be made responsible for a minimum set of services and facilities.
