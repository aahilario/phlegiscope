House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00456
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE IMPOSITION OF CHARGES AND/OR FEES BY TELECOMMUNICATION COMPANIES ON TEXT MESSAGING AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits telecommunication companies and/or providers from imposing fees and/or charges on text messaging between subscribers of the same telecommunication company.
