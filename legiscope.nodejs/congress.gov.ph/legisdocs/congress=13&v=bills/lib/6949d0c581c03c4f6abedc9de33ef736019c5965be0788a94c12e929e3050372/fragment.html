House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03845
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE PENSION OF VETERANS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6948, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the old age pension of veterans by P500.00 effective January 1st of the proceeding year and every year thereafter for the next five years from the effectivity of the act.
