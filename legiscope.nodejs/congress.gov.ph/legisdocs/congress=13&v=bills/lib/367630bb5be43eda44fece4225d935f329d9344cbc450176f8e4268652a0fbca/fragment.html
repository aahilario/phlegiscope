House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02918
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING A STRATEGY FOR RURAL DEVELOPMENT, STRENGTHENING VOLUNTEERISM AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Volunteer Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Volunteer Act of 2004." The bill prescribes the role and modalities of volunteerism in the private sector and by foreign volunteer organizations. It provides for the functions of the Philippine National Volunteerism Service Coordinating Agency (PNVSCA) and the Mutli-Sectoral Advisory Body of the PNVSCA.
