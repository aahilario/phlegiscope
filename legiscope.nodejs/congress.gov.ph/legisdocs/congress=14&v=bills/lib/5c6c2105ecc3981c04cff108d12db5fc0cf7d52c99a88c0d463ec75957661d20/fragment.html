HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00337
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING ARTICLE 26 OF THE NEW CIVIL CODE TO INCLUDE AS ACTIONABLE WRONG THE DISCLOSURE OR DISSEMINATION TO THIRD PARTIES MAILING LIST OF PERSONAL INFORMATION OF PERSONS GIVEN IN CONFIDENCE
ABSTRACT : The bill pronounces as an actionable wrong the disclosure, distribution, circulation and/or transmittal, with or without consideration, to third parties through a mailing list used for commercial purposes, personal information presumed to have been given in confidence by another.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ABAYA, JOSEPH EMILIO A.
