HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02403
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE BANGKO SENTRAL NG PILIPINAS (BSP), PHILIPPINES DEPOSIT INSURANCE COMPANY (PDIC) AND BUREAU OF INTERNAL REVENUE (BIR) TO LOOK INTO DEPOSITS OF ANY BANKING INSTITUTION, THEREBY AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT 1405, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to allow bank regulators from the Bangko Sentral ng Pilpinas and the Philippine Deposit Insurance Corporation to examine the deposit records of banks to ascertain their actual financial condition. It also allows examiners from the Bureau of Internal Revenue to verify the correctness of the tax payments withheld by the banks.
