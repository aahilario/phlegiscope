HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00134
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ACCELERATE THE DEVELOPMENT OF IDLE AND UNDERUTILIZED AGRICULTURAL LANDS AS A MEANS TO GENERATE EMPLOYMENT
SHORT TITLE : "Rural Employment Generation Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Rural Employment Generation Act of 2007". The bill aims to tap one million hectares of idle and underutilized lands for modern productive agricultural resources. It envisions to create one million rural jobs with annual farm incomes of at least P25,000 per hectare being generated.
