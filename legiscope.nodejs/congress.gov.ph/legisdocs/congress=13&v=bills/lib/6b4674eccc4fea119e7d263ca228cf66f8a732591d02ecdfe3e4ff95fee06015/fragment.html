House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00699
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMOTING THE ROLE, DIRECT PARTICIPATION AND ACTIVE INVOLVEMENT OF THE COMMON PEOPLE THROUGH COOPERATIVES IN GOVERNMENT INFRASTRUCTURE CONTRACTS AND PROJECTS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : People's Role in Gov't Infrastructure/Contracts and Projects Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "People's Role in Gov't Infrastructure/Contracts and Projects Act of 2004." The measure allows cooperatives to join bids for government infrastructure projects. Prescribes the qualifications, rights and prohibitions of cooperative-contractors.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SYJUCO, JUDY
