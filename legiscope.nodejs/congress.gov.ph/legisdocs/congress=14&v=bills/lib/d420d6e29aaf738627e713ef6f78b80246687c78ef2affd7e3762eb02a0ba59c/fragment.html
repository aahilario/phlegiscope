House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01487
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR FIVE YEARS MINIMUM APPOINTMENT WITH MONTHLY SALARY, HEALTH AND RETIREMENT BENEFITS FOR DAY CARE WORKERS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : "Security of Tenure and Benefits of Day Care Workers Act"
ABSTRACT : "Security of Tenure and Benefits of Day Care Workers Act". The bill proposes to give day care workers a minimum of five (5) years appointment and salaries and benefits commensurate to their competence level.
