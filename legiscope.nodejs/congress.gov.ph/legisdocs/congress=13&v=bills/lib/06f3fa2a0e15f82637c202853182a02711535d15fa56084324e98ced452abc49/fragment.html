House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04346
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING THE PROVISIONAL RELEASE PROGRAM, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Provisional Relesase Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Provisional Release Act of 2005". The bill seeks to institute the Provisional Release Program which will enable accused persons charged of a crime, except heinous crimes, to be released from detention without having to post bail.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SOLIS, JOSE G.
