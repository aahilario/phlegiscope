HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03368
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AMEND SECTION SEVEN OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED ONE HUNDRED SIXTY-SIX, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LAW ON TRADEMARKS, TRADENAMES AND UNFAIR COMPETITION FOR THE PURPOSE OF REQUIRING THE PUBLICATION OF A LISTING OF APPLICATIONS FOR REGISTRATION IN A NEWSPAPER OF NATIONAL CIRCULATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill requires the Philippine Patent Office to publish quarterly a summary of all applications for registration of trademark or trade name received during the preceding quarter in a newspaper of national circulation. This shall supplement the publication requirement in the Official Gazette.
