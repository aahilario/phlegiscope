House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02627
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXTENDING THE PERIOD WITHIN WHICH THE AGRICULTURAL COMPETITIVENESS ENHANCEMENT FUND SHALL BE UTILIZED, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE SECTION 8 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8178, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill extends the utilization of the Agricultural Competitiveness Enhancement Fund for another ten years, after which the balance shall be transferred to the general fund.
