House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04709
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE MAINTENANCE, ESTABLISHMENT, OR CONSTRUCTION OF FUEL DEPOTS AND WAREHOUSES, OR STORAGE OF OTHER FLAMMABLE OR HAZARDOUS CHEMICALS AND SUBSTANCES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure prohibits the establishment and maintenance of fuel depots, warehouses or storages within a dangerous distance to a populated or residential area.
