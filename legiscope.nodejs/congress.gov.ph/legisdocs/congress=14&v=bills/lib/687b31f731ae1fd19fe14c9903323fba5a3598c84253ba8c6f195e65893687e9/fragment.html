HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01453
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING MANDATORY GOOD CONDUCT ALLOWANCE TO PRISONERS WHO PARTICIPATE IN LITERACY, SKILLS, AND VALUES DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMS IN PENAL INSTITUTIONS
ABSTRACT : The bill grants any prisoner who has earned or is making satisfactory progress towards earning a post-graduate or a college degree or a certificate of completion of a vocational course or a high school diploma or an elementary grade certificate, time credit applicable towards the service of his sentence.
