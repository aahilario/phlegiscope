HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02704
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBER 7227, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'BASES CONVERSION AND DEVELOPMENT ACT OF 1992' AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NUMBER 7917, ADJUSTING THE ALLOCATION OF REVENUES FROM THE SALE, LEASE, JOINT-VENTURE AGREEMENTS AND OTHER TRANSACTIONS INVOLVING MILITARY BASES RESERVATIONS, ALLOCATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for a) the inclusion of the proceeds of the sale, lease, joint-venture agreements involving military bases and reservations as part of the capitalization of the Conversion Authority; b) rationalization of the funding scheme of the Conversion Authority; c) equitable redistribution of the proceeds from transactions involving military bases, among others.
