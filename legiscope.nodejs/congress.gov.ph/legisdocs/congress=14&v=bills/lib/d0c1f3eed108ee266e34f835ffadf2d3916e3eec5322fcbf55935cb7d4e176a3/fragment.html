HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03080
FULL TITLE : AN ACT BANNING MOTORCYCLES FROM USING MAIN ROADS, HIGHWAYS, AND NATIONAL HIGHWAYS THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY
SHORT TITLE : "Motorcycle and Pedestrian Safety Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The measure bans motorcycles and other two-wheeled vehicles from traversing main roads and highways from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. and from 8:00 p.m. to 6:00 a.m. except on Saturdays, Sundays and holidays.
