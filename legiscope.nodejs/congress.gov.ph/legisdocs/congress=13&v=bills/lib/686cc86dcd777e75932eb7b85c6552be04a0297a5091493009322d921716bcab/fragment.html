HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03138
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER AMENDING SECTION 145 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997
ABSTRACT : The bill adopts a uniform tax rate on machine-packed cigarettes. It also proposes to adopt a mechanism that will provide automatic adjustments in tax rates relative to inflation every two years starting CY 2013.
