House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03789
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING THE JURISDICTION OF LABOR CASES BETWEEN THE NATIONAL LABOR RELATION COMMISSION AND THE REGIONAL OFFICES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF LABOR AND EMPLOYMENT, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE, ARTICLES 129 AND 217 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE FOUR HUNDRED FORTY-TWO (PD 442) AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to grant the Regional Director of the Department of Labor and Employment jurisdiction over labor-related claims "regardless of the amount of the claim".
