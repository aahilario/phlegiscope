House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04250
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ENHANCE THE PHILIPPINE INDUSTRIAL AND LABOR DISPUTE SETTLEMENT SYSTEM
SHORT TITLE : Industrial and Labor Dispute Settlement Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Industrial and Labor Dispute Settlement Act of 2004". The bill seeks the use of alternative modes of dispute resolution, including mediation, in settling industrial or labor disputes. It provides for the creation of the National Mediation and Arbitration Board, its composition, functions and powers and jurisdiction.
