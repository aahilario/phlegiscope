House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03483
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE USE OF MOTORCYCLES AS A MEANS OF CONVEYANCE THEREBY PROVIDING IMPORTANCE TO THE SAFETY STANDARDS AND POLICIES INCLUDING REVIEW OF THE EXISTING ROAD SAFETY STANDARD DESIGN, AND USAGE OF ROAD SAFETY MECHANISMS AND TO ESTABLISH A DATABASE FOR SAFE VEHICLES PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Motorcycle Safety Law of 2008"
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Department of Public Works and Highways to provide motorcycle lanes on all main roads and highways.
