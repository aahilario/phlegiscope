HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00787
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE ALLOCATION OF ONE HALF (1/2) OF THE TEN PERCENT (10%) OF THE GENERAL FUND OF THE BARANGAY SET ASIDE FOR THE SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN FOR PUBLIC ELEMENTARY AND HIGH SCHOOLS IN THE BARANGAY TO AUGMENT THE APPROPRIATIONS FOR THEIR MAINTENANCE AND OPERATING EXPENSES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 32 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to allocate one-half (1/2) of ten percent (10%) of the barangay general fund to existing public elementary and high schools in the barangay for their maintenance such as repair of leaking roofs, clogged toilets, sewerage systems, pathwalks, electrical and water systems, repainting of walls due to vandalism, purchase of electric bulbs, electic fans, etc.
