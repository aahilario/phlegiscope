House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02391
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE PREVENTION AND TREATMENT OF TUBERCULOSIS IN THE NATIONAL PENITENTIARY AND IN ALL CITY AND MUNICIPAL JAILS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Prevention and Treatment of Tuberculosis in Prison Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Prevention and Treatment of Tuberculosis in Prison Act of 2004." The bill provides for a monthly medical examination for tuberculosis of all inmates and detainees in the National Bilibid Prison.
