HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03988
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER INCREASING THE EXCISE TAX RATES IMPOSED ON ALCOHOL AND TOBACCO PRODUCTS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS 141, 142, 143, 144, 145 AND 288 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9334
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to further increase the taxes on alcoholic and tobacco products, a portion of the tax proceeds of which shall be used to finance the establishment of rehabilitation centers for alcoholics so that they may once more become useful and productive members of society.
