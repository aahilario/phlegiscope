HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01028
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOCATING TWENTY-FIVE PERCENT (25%) OF TRAVEL TAX COLLECTIONS IN THE COUNTRY TO THE DEVELOPMENT AND PROMOTION OF TOURISM INDUSTRY IN THE TWENTY (20) DEPRESSED PROVINCES
SHORT TITLE :
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates that 25% of the travel tax collected be earmarked for the development and promotion of tourism in the twenty depressed provinces.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : DOMOGAN, MAURICIO G.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-11
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
