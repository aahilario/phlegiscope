House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03790
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PRESCRIBING WORK ARRANGEMENTS BASED ON SECURITY OF TENURE
SHORT TITLE : "The Security of Tenure Law of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "The Security of Tenure Law of 2005". The bill seeks to institutionalize the parameters for: a) contracts of employment for an indefinite period, and b) contracts of employment for a definite period. It prescribes the security of tenure, when such contracts are allowed, the form and contents of such contracts and the rights and privileges of employees under contract.
