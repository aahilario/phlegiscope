HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02500
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING AND REFORMING THE SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN AMENDING FOR THAT PURPOSE CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991 AND REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9340 RESETTING THE BARANGAY AND SK ELECTION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Sangguniang Kabataan Reform Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The measure directs the sangguniang barangay to appropriate the sangguniang kabataan (SK) fund in lump sum. It grants the SK fiscal autonomy in its operations as to disbursements and encashment of their income and expenses.
