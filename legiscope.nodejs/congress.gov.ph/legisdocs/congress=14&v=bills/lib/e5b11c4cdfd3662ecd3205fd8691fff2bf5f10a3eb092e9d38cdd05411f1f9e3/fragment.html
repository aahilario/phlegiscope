House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01903
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A FREE MEDICINE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM FOR THE POOR AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Free Medicine Assistance Act of 2004"
ABSTRACT : "Free Medicine Assistance Act of 2004". The bill seeks to establish the program to address the health care problem of the country. The beneficiaries of the program shall be identified by the Department of Health through government hospitals in coordination with the Department of Social Welfare.
