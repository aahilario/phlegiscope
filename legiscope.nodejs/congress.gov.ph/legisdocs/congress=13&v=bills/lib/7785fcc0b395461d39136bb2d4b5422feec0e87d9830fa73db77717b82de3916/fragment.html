HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02670
FULL TITLE : AN ACT POSTPONING THE BARANGAY AND SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN ELECTIONS FROM OCTOBER 2005 TO THE SECOND MONDAY OF MAY 2007, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill postpones the barangay and sangguniang kabataan elections to the 2nd Monday of May 2007 and every three years thereafter.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : VICENCIO, ROMUALDO T.
