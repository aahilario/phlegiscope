House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00299
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REQUIRE THE TEACHING OF BASIC COMPUTER PROGRAMMING AS PART OF THE CURRICULUM OF ALL SECONDARY SCHOOLS IN THE COUNTRY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the inclusion of basic computer programming courses in the curriculum of all high schools in the country to make the youth computer literate.
