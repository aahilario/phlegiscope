House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01640
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A COMMUNITY ORCHARD IN EVERY CITY OR MUNICIPALITY OF EVERY PROVINCE OF THE PHILIPPINES, ALLOCATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes local community orchards that will serve as initial core training and research facilities and experimental plant stations with capabilities to disperse and distribute planting materials.
