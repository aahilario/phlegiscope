HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01920
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE RECOVERY AND LIQUIDATION OF FINANCIALLY DISTRESSED JURIDICAL ENTITIES
SHORT TITLE : Business Entities Recovery and Insolvency Act
ABSTRACT : "Business Entities Recovery and Insolvency Act." The bill provides for a bankruptcy legislation including initiation proceedings, administration of proceedings and determination of claims, rehabilitation receiver, management and creditors committee, termination of proceedings, remedies (court-supervised and pre-negotiated rehabilitation), dissolution and liquidation.
