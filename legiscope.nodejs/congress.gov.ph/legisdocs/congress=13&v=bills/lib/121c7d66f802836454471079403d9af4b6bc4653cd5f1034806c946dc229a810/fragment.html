HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05506
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE PENALTIES FOR NON-COMPLIANCE WITH THE PRESCRIBED INCREASES AND ADJUSTMENTS IN THE WAGE RATES OF WORKERS, AMENDING FURTHER FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6727, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "WAGE RATIONALIZATION ACT", AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Increasing the Penalties for Non-Compliance With the Prescribed Increases and Adjustments in the Wage Rates of Workers
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to increase the minimum fine on employers who fail to comply with the prescribed wage adjustments to P50,000 plus moral damages for each affected worker and the cost of litigation.
