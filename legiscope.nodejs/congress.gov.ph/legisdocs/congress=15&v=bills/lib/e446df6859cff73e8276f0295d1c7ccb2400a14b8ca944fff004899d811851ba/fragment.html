House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00081
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REDUCE POVERTY AND PROMOTE HUMAN CAPITAL DEVELOPMENT THROUGH THE CREATION OF A NATIONAL CONDITIONAL FUND TRANSFER PROGRAM
SHORT TITLE : Pantawid Pamilyang Pilipino Program Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Pantawid Pamilyang Pilipino Program Act of 2010.” Defines the program (4Ps) to mean the national poverty reduction strategy that provides conditional cash transfer to extremely poor households to improve their health, nutrition and education, particularly of children aged zero (0) to fourteen (14).
