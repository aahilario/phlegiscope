HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00925
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR SECTORAL REPRESENTATIVES FROM THE INDIGENOUS CULTURAL COMMUNITIES/INDIGENOUS PEOPLES IN THE LOCAL LEGISLATIVE COUNCILS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE 'THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991'
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the election in the local legislative council of one (1) sectoral representative from the indigenous people in communities where they are predominant.
