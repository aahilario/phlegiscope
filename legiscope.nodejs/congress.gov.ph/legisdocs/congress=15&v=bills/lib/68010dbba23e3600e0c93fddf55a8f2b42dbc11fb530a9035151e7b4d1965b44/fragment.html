HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00009
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING THE ADMINISTRATIVE CODE OF 1987 BY PROHIBITING A PERSON FROM BEING APPOINTED AS THE SECRETARY OF THE DEPARTMENT OF NATIONAL DEFENSE WITHIN THE NEXT THREE (3) YEARS AFTER RELIEF, SEPARATION OR RETIREMENT FROM ACTIVE DUTY AS A COMMISSIONED OFFICER OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES (AFP)
SHORT TITLE : Prohibiting A Person From Being Appointed As The Secretary Of The Department Of National Defense Within The Next Three (3) Years After Relief, Separation Or Retirement From Active Duty As A Commissioned Officer Of The Armed Forces Of The Philippines ( A F P)
ABSTRACT : Prohibits a former military officer from being appointed as Secretary of National Defense within three (3) years after relief from active duty as a commissioned officer from the Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP) to afford him sufficient time to adapt to civilian life.
