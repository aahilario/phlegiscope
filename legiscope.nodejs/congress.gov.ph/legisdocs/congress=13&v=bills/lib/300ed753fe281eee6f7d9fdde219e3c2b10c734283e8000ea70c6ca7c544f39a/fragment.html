HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00929
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING CAR COMPANIES TO PUT UP SUB-ASSEMBLY PLANTS FOR THE LOCAL MANUFACTURE OF CERTAIN CAR OR AUTO PARTS
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to generate employment and lower the prices for car parts by requiring auto companies to put up sub-assembly plants for local manufacturers of car parts using local raw materials and labor.
