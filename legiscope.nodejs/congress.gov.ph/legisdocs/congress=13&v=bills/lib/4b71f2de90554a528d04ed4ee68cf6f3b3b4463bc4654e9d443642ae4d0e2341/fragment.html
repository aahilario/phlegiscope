HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01019
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO CREATE A NATIONAL WOMEN'S MUSEUM, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Filipino Women's Museum Act of 2003
ABSTRACT : "National Filipino Women's Museum Act of 2003." The National Filipino Women's Museum shall be established to enshrine and commemorate the heritage, heroism and martyrdom of Filipino women who played significant roles in the country's struggle for liberation, justice, peace and prosperity.
