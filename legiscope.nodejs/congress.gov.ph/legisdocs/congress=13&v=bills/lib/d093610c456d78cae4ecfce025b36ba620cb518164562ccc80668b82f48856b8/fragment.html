HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04397
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE REGULATORY POWER OF THE DEPARTMENT OF TRADE AND INDUSTRY (DTI) TO EFFECTIVELY ENFORCE CONSUMER LAWS, ESTABLISHING ADDED MECHANISMS FOR ITS IMPLEMENTATION, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7394, ALSO KNOWN AS THE CONSUMER ACT OF THE PHILIPPINES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : DTI Regulatory Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "DTI Regulatory Act of 2005". The bill directs the bureau of Product Standards to establish standards for all products which have not been standardized and which products are not yet covered by the standardization activities of other government agencies.
