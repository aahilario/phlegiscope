FULL TITLE : AN ACT CONVERTING THE NATIONAL EDUCATION TESTING AND RESEARCH CENTER (NETRC) INTO A NATIONAL EDUCATION ASSESSMENT AND EVALUATION AUTHORITY (NEAEA), DEFINING ITS STRUCTURE, POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "National Education Assessment and Evaluation Act of 2008"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to establish the National Educational Assessment and Evaluation Authority that shall monitor educational processes and outputs, and oversee how effectively these are implemented.
