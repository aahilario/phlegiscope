FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING JULY 27 AS A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN RECOGNITION OF THE FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY OF IGLESIA NI CRISTO IN THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : "Commemoration of the Founding Anniversary of Iglesia ni Cristo Act"
ABSTRACT : "Commemoration of the Founding Anniversary of Iglesia ni Cristo Act". The bill declares July 27th of every year as a special non-working national holiday.
