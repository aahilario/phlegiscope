House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01084
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING CRISIS INTERVENTION CENTERS FOR WOMEN AND CHILDREN VICTIMS AND SURVIVORS OF ABUSE OR EXPLOITATION, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Women's and Children Crisis Act
ABSTRACT : "Women's and Children Crisis Act." The bill seeks to institutionalize in every city and province at least one Women's and Children's Crisis Center to provide temporary shelter to women and children victims of violence, assist victims needing medical care, provide guidance counseling, secure legal assistance and provide training and placement.
