House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02620
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL IDENTIFICATION SYSTEM (NIDS) IN THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : National ID Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "National ID Act of 2004." The bill provides for a tamper-proof National ID (NID) Card which may be used to transact with any government instrumentality without need of further proof as to his/her identity.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ESPINO, AMADO JR. T.
