HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00065
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A DEPARTMENT OF AQUACULTURE AND FISHERIES RESOURCES, PROVIDING FOR ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Department Of Aquaculture And Fisheries Resources Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : "Department of Aquaculture and Fisheries Resources Act of 2010." Provides for the transfer of the powers and functions, appropriations, records, properties, equipment and personnel of agencies involved in aquaculture, fisheries and marine resources from the Department of Agriculture and other related agencies to the proposed Department.
