House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01967
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CREATION AND ESTABLISHMENT OF SMALL CLAIMS COURTS IN THE PHILIPPINES, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Small Claims Court (SCC) Act
ABSTRACT : "Small Claims Court (SCC) Act." The bill provides that the SCC shall have jurisdiction over civil cases where the value of personal property or amount of the demand is not over P100,000.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : BARBERS, ROBERT ACE S.
