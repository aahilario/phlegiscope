HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02921
FULL TITLE : AN ACT SETTING ASIDE A PORTION OF ALL INCOME DERIVED FROM LABORATORY AND TESTING FEES AND SUB-SOIL EXPLORATION ACTIVITIES TO BE RETAINED AND CONSTITUTED AS TRUST FUNDS FOR THE EXCLUSIVE USE OF THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC WORKS AND HIGHWAYS (DPWH) IN THE REPAIR, MAINTENANCE AND PROCUREMENT OF RESEARCH, MATERIALS AND SUB-SOIL EXPLORATIONS EQUIPMENT
ABSTRACT : The measure aims to provide ready funds for the repair, maintenance and procurement of equipment in the laboratory and research facilities of the Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH) and for its subsoil exploration activities.
