HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01298
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MORE RESPONSIVE AND COMPREHENSIVE REGULATION FOR THE PRACTICE OF CHEMICAL ENGINEERING IN THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : Revised Chemical Engineering Law
ABSTRACT : "Revised Chemical Engineering Law." Rationale: The present advances in technology practically make all phases of engineering work fully automated. The availability of advanced, sophisticated and modern instruments has rendered Republic Act No. 318 obsolete, sometimes irrelevant and impractical. A review of the law would reveal that some provisions thereof are inapplicable and therefore prone to be violated than followed.
