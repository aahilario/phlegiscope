HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00631
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE INCLUSION OF BASIC ECONOMIC SUBJECTS AS PART OF THE CURRICULUM OF THE MILITARY AND POLICE COURSE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks the inclusion of basic economic subjects in the curriculum of military and police courses to provide an understanding on the effects of destabilizing acts and activities on the country's economy.
