HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02351
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING THE BASIS FOR THE ASSESSMENT OF CUSTOMS DUTY FOR ARTICLES THAT ARE LANDED AT ECONOMIC ZONES AND FREEPORTS AND SUBSEQUENTLY TRANSFERRED TO CUSTOMS TERRITORY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE TARIFF AND CUSTOMS CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill provides in clear and certain terms the basis in assessing the tax and duty liabilities of dutiable goods that enter customs territory through the economic zones and freeports.
