House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00639
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GOVERNING THE ESTABLISHMENT, OPERATION AND REGULATION OF LENDING COMPANIES
SHORT TITLE : Lending Company Regulation Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Lending Company Regulation Act of 2004". The bill requires lending companies to have a paid-up capital of one million pesos, be registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission, and to obtain a quasi-banking license from the Central Bank.
