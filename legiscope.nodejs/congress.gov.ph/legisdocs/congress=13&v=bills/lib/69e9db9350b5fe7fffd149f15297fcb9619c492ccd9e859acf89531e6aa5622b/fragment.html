House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03339
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN SECURITY OF TENURE AND FOR THAT PURPOSE, AMENDING CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO.
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to allow a principal/employer to engage up to a maximum of 10% subcontracted employees. It also seeks to penalize any violation to the subcontracting rules.
