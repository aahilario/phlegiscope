House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02452
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING TELECOMMUNICATIONS COMPANIES PROVIDING MOBILE TELEPHONE SERVICES TO CHARGE MOBILE TELEPHONE USERS FOR EVERY SIX SECOND PULSE OF USAGE AND TO REQUIRE SUCH SERVICE OPERATORS TO GIVE A DETAILED BILLING FOR PREPAID SUBSCRIBERS
SHORT TITLE : Truth in Mobile Telephone Billing Act
ABSTRACT : "Truth in Mobile Telephone Billing Act." The bill requires mobile telephone service providers to bill subscribers for actual air time usage, i.e., every 6-second pulse of usage which translates to one-tenth (1/10) of a minute. It also directs the service providers to furnish pre-paid subscribers with a detailed billing upon presentation of the consumed pre-paid card at designated business centers of the providers.
