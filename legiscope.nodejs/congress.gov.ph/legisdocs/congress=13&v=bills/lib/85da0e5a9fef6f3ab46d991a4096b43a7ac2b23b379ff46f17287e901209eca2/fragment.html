House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02284
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE ISSUANCE OF LEGAL INSTRUMENT TO COVER THE FUNDS FOR THE CHARATER LOANS TO THE GOVERNMENT AND PRIVATE WORKERS
ABSTRACT : This bill authorizes the Government Service Insurance System (GSIS) and the Social Security System to grant loans in the amount of One Hundred Thousand Pesos (P100,000.00) to government and private employees who have rendered fifteen (15) to twenty (20) years of continuous service.
