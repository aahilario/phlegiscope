House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00883
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE DEPARTMENT OF MARITIME AFFAIRS PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The creates the DEPARTMENT OF MARITIME AFFAIRS which shall be tasked to recommend and implement the Government's policies, plans and programs for the promotion of safety and development of ports, shipping, shipbuilding, seafaring and other activities in the maritime industry.
