House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02046
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AMEND REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9189 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE OVERSEAS ABSENTEE VOTING ACT OF 2003
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to amend RA 9189 to correct certain flaws in the law to make voting more accessible. It seeks to expand voting by mail and institutionalize voting by other means such as the internet.
