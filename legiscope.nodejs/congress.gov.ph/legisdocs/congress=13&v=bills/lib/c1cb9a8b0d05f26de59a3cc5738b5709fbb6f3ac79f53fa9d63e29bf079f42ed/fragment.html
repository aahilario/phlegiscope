HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00763
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE POSITION OF TOURISM OFFICER IN EACH PROVINCE, CITY AND MUNICIPALITY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CERTAIN SECTIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : At present, the development and promotion of tourism is confined only to the Department of Tourism. To fully develop the tourism potentials in the country, the bill seeks to create the position of Tourism Officer in every province, city, and municipality. The Tourism Officer shall monitor and evaluate the implementation of the different tourism development programs and activities in the local government unit.
