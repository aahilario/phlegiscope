HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02418
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE SPEEDY AND EFFECTIVE PERPETUATION OF THE TESTIMONY OF A VITAL WITNESS IN THE PROSECUTION FOR A CRIMINAL OFFENSE SO AS TO PREVENT ITS SUBSEQUENT RECANTATION OR SUBSTANTIAL ALTERATION
SHORT TITLE : "Anti- Recantation Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the investigating police officer to transmit the testimony of witness within three (3) days from its execution to the City or Provincial Prosecutor who has jurisdiction over the preliminary investigation of said offense.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : GARCIA, PABLO P.
DATE FILED : 2007-09-04
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
