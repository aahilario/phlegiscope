House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00201
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING FRANKING PRIVILEGES TO SANGGUNIANG BARANGAYS
ABSTRACT : Sangguniang Barangays (SBs are often required to communicate with other agencies. However, the limited budget of SBs also limit their capacity to send communications essential in the performance of their duties. To address this problem, the bill proposes to grans SBs limited franking privileges for communications sent to other government agencies.
