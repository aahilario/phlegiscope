HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02092
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING CERTAIN TOURISM-ORIENTED ESTABLISHMENTS AND TOURIST WORKERS TO SECURE ACCREDITATION FROM THE DEPARTMENT OF TOURISM AS A PREREQUISITE TO THE ISSUANCE OF LICENSE OR PERMIT TO OPERATE BY LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS
ABSTRACT : The bill requires tourism-oriented establishments and tourism workers to be accredited by the Department of Tourism prior to the issuance of a license, permit to operate or conduct a tourism-related business by the local government unit.
