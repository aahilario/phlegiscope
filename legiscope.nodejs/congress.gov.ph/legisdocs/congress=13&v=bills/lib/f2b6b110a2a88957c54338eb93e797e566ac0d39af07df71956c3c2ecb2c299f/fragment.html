HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00678
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING DIRECT AND TECHNICAL SMUGGLING AS ACTS OF ECONOMIC SABOTAGE, PRESCRIBING PENALTIES THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Imposes the following penalties for smuggling goods and articles amounting to P1 million: (1) fine equal to twice the value of the article; (2) fine equal to the taxes avoided; (3) eight years up to life imprisonment, or death; and (4) confiscation of smuggled items.
