HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02872
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE IMMEDIATE ADJUSTMENT OF INTERNAL REVENUE ALLOTMENT (IRA) OF RECIPIENT LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS (LGUs) OF RELOCATED AND RESETTLED INFORMAL SETTLERS IN THE PRECEDING FISCAL YEAR, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE URBAN DEVELOPMENT AND HOUSING ACT OF 1992
ABSTRACT : To enable local government units (LGUs) to provide livelihood programs and basic services and facilities in resettlement or relocation areas, the bill seeks to increase the internal revenue allotment of LGUs that provide resettlement or relocation sites for informal settlers.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : EDUARDO V. ROQUERO, M.D.
