House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04527
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING AN ANNUAL MANDATORY DRUG TEST FOR ALL ELECTED GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION THIRTY-SIX OF REPUBLIC ACT NINETY ONE HUNDRED SIXTY-FIVE, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "COMPREHENSIVE DANGEROUS DRUGS ACT OF 2002
ABSTRACT : The bill requires all elective government officials except the President, Vice President, Senate President and Speaker of the House of Representatives to undergo a mandatory annual drug test.
