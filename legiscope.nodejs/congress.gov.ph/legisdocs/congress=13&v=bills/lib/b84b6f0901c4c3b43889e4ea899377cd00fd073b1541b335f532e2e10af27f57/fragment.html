HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03666
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 61, 62 AND 63 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6975 PLACING FOR THE PURPOSE THE PROVINCIAL AND SUB-PROVINCIAL JAILS UNDER THE JURISDICTION OF THE BUREAU OF JAIL MANAGEMENT AND PENOLOGY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSE
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes to take away from the provincial governments the supervision and control over provincial and sub-provincial jails and place them under the Bureau of Jail Management and Penology.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ESPINO, AMADO JR. T.
