House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00165
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING ARTICLE 287 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES BY ADDING A NEW ARTICLE 287-A REDUCING THE RETIREMENT AGE OF RACEHORSE JOCKEYS FROM SIXTY (60) TO FIFTY-FIVE (55)
SHORT TITLE : The Race Horse Jockeys Retirement Act Of 2008
ABSTRACT : "The Race Horse Jockeys Retirement Act of 2008." Lowers the retirement age of race horse jockeys from 60 to 55 years old so that they will have more time to enjoy the fruits of their labor and have more opportunities to practice other horse-racing professions such as horse trainers, or even train jockeys immediately after their retirement.
