HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05066
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING PRIORITY TO RESIDENTS OF THE BARANGAY, MUNICIPALITY OR CITY WHERE THE SCHOOL IS LOCATED, IN THE APPOINTMENT OR ASSIGNMENT OF CLASSROOM PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS
ABSTRACT : The measure recommends that in the appointment or assignment of classroom public school teachers priority be given to residents of the barangay, municipality or city where the school is located.
