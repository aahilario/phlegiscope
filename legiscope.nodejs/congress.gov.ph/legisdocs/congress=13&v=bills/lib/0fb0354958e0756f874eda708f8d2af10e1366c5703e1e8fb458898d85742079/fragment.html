HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03473
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ABOLISHING THE THREE HUNDRED AND ONE (301) DAYS CRIMINAL PROHIBITION TO REMARRY AND ALLOWING THE REMARRIAGE OF A WIDOW OR A SEPARATED WOMAN FORTY DAYS AFTER THE DEATH OF HER HUSBAND OR THE ANNULMENT OR DISSOLUTION OF HER PREVIOUS MARRIAGE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLE 351 OF ACT NO. 3815, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "REVISED PENAL CODE"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to reduce the 301 days penal prohibition for women to remarry to forty days in fealty to customs and traditions.
