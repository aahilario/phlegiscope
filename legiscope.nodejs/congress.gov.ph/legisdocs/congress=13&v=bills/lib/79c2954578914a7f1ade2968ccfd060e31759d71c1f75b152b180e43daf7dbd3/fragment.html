House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01869
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE PHILIPPINE TRANSPORTATION SECURITY AUTHORITY, AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Transportation Security Authority Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Transportation Security Authority Act of 2004." This bill mandates the Authority, as the central security agency of all transport systems in the country, to encourage, promote, formulate, implement and ensure the installation of standard security and safety measures and policies that are internationally acceptable for the prevention of transportation disasters and for the protection, security, and safety of passengers, transportation facilities and properties.
