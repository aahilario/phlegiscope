HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02460
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO EXPAND THE POWERS, DUTIES AND FUNCTIONS OF A PUNONG BARANGAY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 389, CHAPTER 3 OF THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill grants the punong barangay additional powers, duties and functions, to wit: a) to enforce laws and regulations relating to the prevention, use and sale of illegal drugs; b) to perform minor repairs and maintenance of municipal and barangay roads ; and c) to conduct annually a census of the population of the barangay.
