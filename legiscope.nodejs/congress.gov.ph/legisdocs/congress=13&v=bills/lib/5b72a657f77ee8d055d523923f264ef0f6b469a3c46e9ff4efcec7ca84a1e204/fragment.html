House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01862
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE STANDARDIZATION OF THE RATES OF COMPENSATION OF THE DOCTORS, NURSES AND OTHER HEALTH WORKERS IN THE GOVERNMENT SERVICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Public Health Workers Compensation Structure of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Public Health Workers Compensation Structure of 2004." This bill declares it a State policy to establish a highly motivated salary structure for public health workers to prevent the imminent and inevitable collapse of the Health System in the country. Provides for the upgrading of the basic salary of health workers with Salary Grade 22 and below by 2 salary grades.
