HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01858
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE ESTABLISHMENT OF VIDEO ARCADES, INTERNET GAMING STATIONS, BILLIARD HALLS AND OTHER SIMILAR ESTABLISHMENTS NEAR EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS
ABSTRACT : This bill declares it unlawful for any individual to establish video arcades, internet gaming stations, billiard halls and other similar establishments near educational institutions and provides penalty for violation thereof.
