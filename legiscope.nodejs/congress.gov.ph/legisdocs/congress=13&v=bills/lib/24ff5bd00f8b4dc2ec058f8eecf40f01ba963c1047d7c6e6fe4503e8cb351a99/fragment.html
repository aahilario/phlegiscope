FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR MONTHLY ALLOWANCES FOR THE MEMBERS OF LUPONG TAGAPAMAYAPA AND THE PANGKAT NG TAGAPAGKASUNDO, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
