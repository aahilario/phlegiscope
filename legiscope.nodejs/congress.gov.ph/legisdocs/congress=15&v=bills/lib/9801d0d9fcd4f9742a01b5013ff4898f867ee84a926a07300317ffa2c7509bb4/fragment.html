HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00115
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN SECTIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SEVENTY-EIGHT THIRTY-TWO, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'ANTI-ELECTRICITY AND ELECTRIC TRANSMISSION LINES/MATERIALS PILFERAGE ACT OF 1994'
ABSTRACT : Hopes to bring better benefits to the Filipino consumers as well as to protect the properties of the power industry players from pilferage activities and destruction by adding a new provision in RA07832 thus avoiding “executive or judicial legislation” in the interpretation of the law, especially on the issue of maximum cap for recoverable systems loss.
