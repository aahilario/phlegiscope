HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02101
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AMEND SECTION 2 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 1405, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "SECRECY OF BANK DEPOSITS LAW," EXEMPTING GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES FROM THE PROHIBITION AGAINST DISCLOSURE OF OR INQUIRY INTO DEPOSITS WITH ANY BANKING INSTITUTION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to exempt government officials and employees, whether elected or appointed, from the coverage of the Bank Secrecy Law in order to equip law enforcement authorities with the tools needed to go after crooks in government.
