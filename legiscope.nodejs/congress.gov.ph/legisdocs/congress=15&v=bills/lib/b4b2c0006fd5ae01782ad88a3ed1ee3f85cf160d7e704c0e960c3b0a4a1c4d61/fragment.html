HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00270
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MANDATORY STUDY OF MORO HISTORY, CULTURE AND IDENTITY IN THE CURRICULA OF ALL LEVELS OF SCHOOLS IN THE COUNTRY
SHORT TITLE : Moro History, Culture And Identity Studies Act
ABSTRACT : “Moro History, Culture and Identity Studies Act” Mandates for the inclusion of the study and understanding of Moro history, culture and identity in the curricula of all levels of education and training in all schools in the country, starting in Mindanao, adopting the scope and treatment of the subjects or courses to the respective educational levels.
