HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02664
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROVIDE A PERCENTAGE OF THE VALUE OF FORFEITED PROPERTIES DERIVED FROM ANTI-CORRUPTION CASES PROSECUTED BY THE OMBUDSMAN TO BE EMBARKED FOR THE OFFICE OF THE OMBUDSMAN AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE SECTION 6 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 1379 KNOWN AS THE FORFEITURE LAW
ABSTRACT : The bill earmarks 10% of the value of all properties forfeited in favor of the State to augment the funding needs of the Office of the Ombudsman.
