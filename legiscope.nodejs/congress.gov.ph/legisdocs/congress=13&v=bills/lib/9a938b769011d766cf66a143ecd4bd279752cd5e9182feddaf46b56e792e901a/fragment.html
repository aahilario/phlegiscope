HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01598
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8436 ENTITLED "AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE COMMISSION OF ELECTIONS TO USE AN AUTOMATED ELECTION SYSTEM IN THE MAY 11, 1998 NATIONAL OR LOCAL ELECTIONS AND IN SUBSEQUENT NATIONAL AND LOCAL ELECTORAL EXERCISES, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to delete the requirement for an optical mark reader counting machine provided under RA 8436. It also allows the storage of election data in compact disc drives and compact discs. Finally, it authorizes the Commission on Elections to acquire the necessary materials for elections by "purchase, lease or otherwise."
