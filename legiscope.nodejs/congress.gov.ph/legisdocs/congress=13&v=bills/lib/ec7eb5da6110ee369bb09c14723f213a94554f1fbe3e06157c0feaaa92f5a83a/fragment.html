HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01619
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO INSTITUTIONALIZE A COMPULSORY ON-THE-JOB TRAINING (OJT) AS PART OF THE CURRICULAR PROGRAMS REQUISITE PRIOR TO GRADUATION
SHORT TITLE : OJT Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "OJT Act of 2004," The bill seeks to institutionalize the OJT program to supplement the academic training of our students in order to arm them with actual work experience in the industry where they intend to belong to.
