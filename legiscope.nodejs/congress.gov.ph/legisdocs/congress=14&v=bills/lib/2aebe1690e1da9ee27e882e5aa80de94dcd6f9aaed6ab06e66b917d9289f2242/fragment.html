HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02782
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REORGANIZING AND RENAMING THE PHILIPPINE RETIREMENT PARK SYSTEM, ENLARGING ITS POWERS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine Retirement Authority Act"
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to update the charter of the Philippine Retirement Authority in order to provide it with a clearer, more responsive mandate and power in promoting the growth of the alien retirement industry in the country.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : VALENCIA, RODOLFO G.
