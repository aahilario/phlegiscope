HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01741
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF ENTREPRENEURSHIP SKILLS AMONG GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES FOR AN INNOVATION-INSPIRED CORPS OF CIVIL SERVANTS AND A PRODUCTIVE POST-SERVICE CITIZENRY
SHORT TITLE : "Government Employees' Entrepreneurship Development Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Government Employees' Entrepreneurship Development Act of 2007". The bill directs all government agencies to hone the entrepreneurial skills of employees to equip them with necessary skills to enable them to earn extra income in their spare time.
