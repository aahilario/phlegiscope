HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04668
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING A CORPORATE RESTRUCTURING PROGRAM FOR THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT HOSPITAL SYSTEM, REORGANIZING THE NATIONAL CENTER FOR HEALTH FACILITIES DEVELOPMENT INTO A CORPORATE BODY TO BE KNOWN AS THE NATIONAL HOSPITAL SYSTEM DEVELOPMENT CENTER FOR THE PURPOSE, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFORE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSE
SHORT TITLE : "National Hospital System Corporation Restructuring Act".
ABSTRACT : "National Hospital System Corporation Restructuring Act". The bill mandates that all national government hospitals currently under the supervision of the Department of Health be converted to government corporate entities.
