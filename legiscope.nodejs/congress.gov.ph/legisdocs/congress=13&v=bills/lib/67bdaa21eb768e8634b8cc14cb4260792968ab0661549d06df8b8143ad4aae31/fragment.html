FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO UPDATE THE LAW ON SECURITY DEVICES BY AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 386, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CIVIL CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AND ACT NO. 1508, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CHATTEL MORTGAGE LAW
ABSTRACT : "Security Devise Act of 2001". The bill seeks to update the following areas in the Law on Security Devices: a) assignment by way of security, b) chattel mortgage and c) pledge.
