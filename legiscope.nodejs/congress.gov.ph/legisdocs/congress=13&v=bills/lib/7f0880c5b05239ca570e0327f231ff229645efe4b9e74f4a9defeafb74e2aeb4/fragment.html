HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05844
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN THE CONSTRUCTION INDUSTRY, CREATING THE PHILIPPINE CONSTRUCTION INDUSTRY DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Philippine Construction Industry Development Act"
ABSTRACT : "The Philippine Construction Industry Development Act". The bill aims: a) to set the global standards in the various sectors in the construction industry; b) to develop/implement regulatory and developmental programs for the growth of the industry; and c) to provide opportunities for Filipino participation in the local and overseas markets.
