House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01725
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY TWENTY-TWO OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY TO BE KNOWN AS NATIONAL FARMER'S DAY
SHORT TITLE : Declaring January Twenty-two Of Every Year As A Special Working Holiday To Be Known As National Farmer's Day
ABSTRACT : The bill declares January 22nd of every year as a special working holiday to honor the heroism of the peasants who were gunned down during the "Mendiola Massacre".
