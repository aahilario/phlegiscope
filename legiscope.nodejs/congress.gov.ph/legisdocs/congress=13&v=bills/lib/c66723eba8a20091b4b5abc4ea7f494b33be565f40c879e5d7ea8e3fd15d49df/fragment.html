House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03729
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FOR CONSERVING AND REHABILITATING THE FOREST OF THE COUNTRY THROUGH THE POLICY OF TOTAL LOG BAN FOR A PERIOD OF THIRTY YEARS, CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE A NATIONAL FOREST WATCH AND PROTECTION TASK FORCE AND COMMUNITY-BASED COUNCILS
SHORT TITLE : "The Forest Preservation and Rehabilition Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "The Forest Preservation and Rehabilitation Act of 2005." The bill calls for a total log ban in primary and secondary forests and in watersheds for a period of 30 years. It provides for the creation of a National Forest Watch and Protection Task Force, its composition and functions.
