HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00519
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING CENTERS FOR STREET CHILDREN IN EVERY REGION OF THE PHILIPPINES AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : Declares it a State policy to prevent the exploitation of infants and children through mendicancy and provide rehabilitative service for those already exploited or in immediate danger of exploitation, and promote the rehabilitation of minors by providing an integrated development package of preventive, interceptive and/or remedial services.
