HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02920
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING CIVIL SERVICE ELIGIBILITY UNDER CERTAIN CONDITIONS TO ALL GOVERNMENT WORKERS AND EMPLOYEES HOLDING PERMANENT, PROVISIONAL, TEMPORARY, CASUAL, CONTRACTUAL OR CO-TERMINOUS APPOINTMENTS WHO HAVE CONTINUOUSLY RENDERED A TOTAL OF FIFTEEN YEARS IN GOVERNMENT SERVICE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes to grant civil eligibility to government employees, regardless of employment status, who have rendered 15 years of government service upon the recommendation and approval of their head of office.
