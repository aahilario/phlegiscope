HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02636
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A SCHOOL NUTRITION PROGRAM AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Child Nutrition Act of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes a school nutrition program that shall provide milk and fortified snack foods to public elementary school entrants. Donor companies shall be allowed to deduct from their gross income 200% of the value of their donation.
