House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03097
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING ONE LOCAL EMERGENCY CENTER IN EVERY MUNICIPALITY THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY
SHORT TITLE : "Local Emergency Centers Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The measure establishes a local emergency center in every municipality that will extend immediate assistance in times of disasters and calamities.
