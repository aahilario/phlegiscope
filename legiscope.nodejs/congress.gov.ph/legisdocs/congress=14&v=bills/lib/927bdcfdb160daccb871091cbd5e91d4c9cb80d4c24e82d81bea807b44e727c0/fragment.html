HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02999
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ABOLISHING THE SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN AND INSTEAD PROVIDE FOR THE APPOINTMENT OF A YOUTH SECTOR REPRESENTATIVE AS MEMBER OF THE SANGGUNIANG BARANGAY, SANGGUNIANG BAYAN, SANGGUNIANG PANGLUNGSOD AND SANGGUNIANG PANLALAWIGAN, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the abolition of the Sangguniang Kabataan and the appointment of a representative from the youth sector as member of the sangguniang barangay, bayan, panlungsod and panlalawigan.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SOLIS, JOSE G.
