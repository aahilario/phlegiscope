House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01214
REPUBLIC ACT NO. RA09442
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING OTHER PRIVILEGES AND INCENTIVES TO PERSONS WITH DISABILITY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE 'MAGNA CARTA FOR DISABLED PERSONS'
SHORT TITLE : Granting Other Privileges and Incentives to Persons with Disability
ABSTRACT : This bill states that persons with disabilities are as vulnerable as senior citizens, thus they should be given the same privileges enjoyed by senior citizens. The grant of similar privileges will surely boost the moral of persons with disabilities and make them feel that they are valued members of society and not a burden to their families and their communities.
