HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03104
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REPEALING THE TAX AND DUTY EXEMPTIONS AND PREFERENTIAL TREATMENT GRANTED TO GOVERNMENT AND PRIVATE ENTITIES UNDER SPECIAL LAWS, WITH CERTAIN EXCEPTIONS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill revokes all tax and duty incentives granted to private and government entities under 20 republic acts and 3 presidential decrees. The proposal is expected to generate P5 billion in revenues to the government.
