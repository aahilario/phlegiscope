House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03983
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMOTING THE PHILIPPINE STANDARD TIME (PST) BY PROVIDING FUNDS FOR THE INSTALLATION, OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF SYNCHRONIZED TIME DEVICES TO BE DISPLAYED IN KEY PUBLIC PLACES
SHORT TITLE : The Philippine Standard Time Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "The Philippine Standard Time Act of 2005". The bill seeks to provide funds for the installation, operation and maintenance of synchronized time devices to be displayed in key public places.
