HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03376
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 12-B OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 1161 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8282, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SOCIAL SECURITY LAW OF 1997 TO ENTITLE UNDERGROUND MINE WORKERS RETIRING AT AGES SET UNDER REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8558 TO THE BENEFITS PROVIDED FOR UNDER THE SECTION
ABSTRACT : The bill allows underground mining employees to retire upon reaching the age of fifty years or more but not beyond 60 years, which is the compulsory retirement age, and to be entitled to all the retirement benefits provided in the Social Security Act.
