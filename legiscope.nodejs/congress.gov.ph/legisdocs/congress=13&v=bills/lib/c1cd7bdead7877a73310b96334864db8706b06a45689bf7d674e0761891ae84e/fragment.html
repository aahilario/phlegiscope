HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03736
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8436, ENTITLED "AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE COMMISSION ON ELECTIONS TO USE AN AUTOMATED ELECTION SYSTEM IN THE MAY 11, 1998 NATIONAL OR LOCAL ELECTIONS AND IN SUBSEQUENT NATIONAL AND LOCAL ELECTORAL EXERCISES, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to delete the requirements for an optical mark reader counting machine and, in lieu thereof, use an "appropriate technology to count votes." It allows the storage of election data in compact disc drives and compact discs. It authorizes the COMELEC to acquire the necessary materials through "purchase, lease or otherwise."
