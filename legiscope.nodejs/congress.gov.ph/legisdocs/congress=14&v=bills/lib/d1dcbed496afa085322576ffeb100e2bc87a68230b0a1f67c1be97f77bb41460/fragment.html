HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00121
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE AUTOMATIC RELEASE OF THE SHARE OF THE BARANGAY IN THE PROCEEDS OF NATIONAL TAXES AND TAXES ON THE UTILIZATION AND DEVELOPMENT OF THE NATIONAL WEALTH, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE SECTION 286, CHAPTER 1 AND SECTION 293, CHAPTER 2, TITLE 3, BOOK II, RESPECTIVELY, OF THE REPUBLIC ACT 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Under the present system, barangays have to literally beg for their rightful share of the national taxes. To discourage patronage politics and to help attain our goals of decentralization, this measure seeks to provide for the automatic release of the share of the barangays in the national taxes and in taxes imposed on the utilization and development of national wealth.
