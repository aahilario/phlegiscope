HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00040
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REORGANIZING THE NATIONAL FOOD AUTHORITY INTO THE NATIONAL FOOD CORPORATION, REDEFINING ITS POWERS, AND CREATING THE FOOD DEVELOPMENT AND REGULATORY ADMINISTRATION, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "National Food Authority Reorganization Act of 2002"
ABSTRACT : "National Food Authority Reorganization Act of 2002". The bill seeks to reorganize the National Food Authority into the National Food Corporation (NFC) and to arm the NFC with a broader mandate and wider powers to enable it to effectively perform its functions as motivator for farmers to become more productive and competitive.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : LACSON, JOSE CARLOS V.
