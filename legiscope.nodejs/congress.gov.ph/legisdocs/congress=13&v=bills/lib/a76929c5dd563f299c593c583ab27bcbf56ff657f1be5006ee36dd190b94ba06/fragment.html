FULL TITLE : RESOLUTION CALLING ON THE COMMITTEE ON NATURAL RESOURCES TO CONDUCT AN INQUIRY IN AID OF LEGISLATION ON THE MINING OPERATION OF LAFAYETTE PHILIPPINES, INC.
