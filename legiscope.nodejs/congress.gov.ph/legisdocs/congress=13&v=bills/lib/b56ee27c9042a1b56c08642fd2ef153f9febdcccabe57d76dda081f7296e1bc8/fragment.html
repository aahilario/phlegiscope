HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02210
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROHIBIT AND PENALIZE THE USE OF CERTAIN PORTABLE ELECTRONIC DEVICES ON BOARD PHILIPPINE AIR CARRIERS WHETHER COMMERCIALLY OR PRIVATELY OWNED AND/OR OPERATED FLYING OVER PHILIPPINE AIRSPACE
ABSTRACT : This bill declares it unlawful for any person to operate any portable electronic devices like amateur radio transceivers, citizens band radios, pagers, cellular phones, text messaging devices, remote control devices and all other types of portable radio transceivers, whose operation may endanger the safety of the flight of an aircraft.
