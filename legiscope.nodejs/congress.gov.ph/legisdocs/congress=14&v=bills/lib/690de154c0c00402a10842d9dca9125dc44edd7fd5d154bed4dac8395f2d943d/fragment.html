House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01247
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROMOTE AND REGULATE THE SUBSCRIBED TELEVISION SERVICE IN THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Subscribed TV Act"
ABSTRACT : "Subscribed TV Act". The measure sets down the procedures, standards and guidelines that would encourage the growth and development of subscribed television services. It differentiates between subscribed television network services and subscribed television communications services and provides specific rights to subscribed television service operators.
