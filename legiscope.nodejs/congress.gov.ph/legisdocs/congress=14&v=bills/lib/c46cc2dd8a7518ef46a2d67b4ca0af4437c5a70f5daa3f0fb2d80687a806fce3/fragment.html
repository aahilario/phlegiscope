HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01011
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A BUREAU OF INTERNAL REVENUE FIELD OFFICE IN EVERY MUNICIPALITY THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT EIGHTY-FOUR TWENTY-FOUR OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'TAX REFORM ACT OF 1997'
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes a Bureau of Internal Revenue field office in all municipalities in the country to be headed by a collection supervisor assisted by at least two personnel.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : GARIN, JANETTE L.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-10
