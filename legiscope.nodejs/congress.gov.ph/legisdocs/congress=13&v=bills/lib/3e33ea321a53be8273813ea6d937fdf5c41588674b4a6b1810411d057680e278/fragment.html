House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03821
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROVIDE THE REVIVAL OF ARBOR DAY BY VESTING IN PROVINCIAL, CITY AND MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENTS WITH THEIR COMPONENT BARANGAYS THE RESPONSIBILITIES FOR CELEBRATING THE DAY FOR TREE PLANTING AS AN ANNUAL EVENT
SHORT TITLE : Revival of Arbor Day/Tree Planting Day
ABSTRACT : The bill grants provincial, city and municipal governments the responsibility and flexibility to choose the date to celebrate Arbor Day.
