House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02654
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING AND PENALIZING THE FLYING AND/OR OPERATION OF KITES, BALLOONS, AND UNMANNED ROCKETS AND OTHER HAZARDOUS OBJECTS ON OR NEAR FLYING FIELDS
SHORT TITLE : "flying Field Operations Safety Act Of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill bans the flying of kites, balloons, unmanned rockets amd other similar objects inside any airport, air base, air strip or air field and on surrounding areas.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : NOGRALES, PROSPERO C.
DATE FILED : 2007-09-24
