House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01003
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A NATIONAL PROGRAM FOR FILIPINO GIFTED CHILDREN AND SCIENCE-ORIENTED YOUTH, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Gifted Children and Science-Oriented Youth Act
ABSTRACT : "Gifted Children and Science-Oriented Youth Act." The bill seeks to provide scholarships, grants and other forms of incentives to deserving science students and gifted children. It proposes the creation of the Center for the Gifted Children and Science-Oriented Youth which shall identify and place them in appropriate educational institutions and assist them in securing jobs.
