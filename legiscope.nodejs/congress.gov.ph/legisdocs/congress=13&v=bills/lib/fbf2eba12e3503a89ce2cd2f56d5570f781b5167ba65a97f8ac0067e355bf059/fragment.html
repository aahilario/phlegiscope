HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00427
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ADJUSTMENTS IN THE MONTHLY CONTRIBUTIONS OF PHILHEALTH MEMBERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SEVENTY EIGHT HUNDRED AND SEVENTY FIVE, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE ACT OF 1995,' AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : At present, the Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (Philhealth) covers only 5% to 10% of the total hospitalization expenses of its member. This bill seeks to adjust the monthly contributions of Philhealth members to increase the funds of Philhealth and consequently allow for a more reasonsble coverage in case a member is hospitalized.
