House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01969
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXTENDING THE TERM OF OFFICE OF BARANGAY OFFICIALS AND RESETTING THE DATE OF BARANGAY ELECTIONS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9164
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for a synchronized barangay and sangguniang kabataan elections to be held on the last Monday of October 2007 and every five years thereafter.
