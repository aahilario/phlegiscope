HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02295
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CONVERTING THE ACCUMULATED VALUE ADDED TAX (VAT) PAYMENTS OF LOW-INCOME WORKERS INTO CREDITS FOR THE COMPENSATION-BASED INCOME TAX, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CHAPTER VII, SECTION 35 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to allow employees receiving compensation income of not more than P300,000 per annum to deduct from their annual taxes VAT (value-added tax) payments on their purchase of good and services.
