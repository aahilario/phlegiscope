House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05676
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A NATIONAL ECO-TOURISM POLICY, ESTABLISHING A FRAMEWORK FOR ITS INSTITUTUIONALIZATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to create an Economic-Tourism Development Council that shall review, integrate and synchronize the policies, plans, programs and services of the government and private sectors related to tourism.
