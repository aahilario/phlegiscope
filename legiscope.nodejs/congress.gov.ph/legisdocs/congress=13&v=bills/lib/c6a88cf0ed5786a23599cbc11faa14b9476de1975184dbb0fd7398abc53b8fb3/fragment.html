HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01036
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF VALIDITY ON THE PRIVILEGES FOR MULTIPLE ENTRY VISA AND REENTRY PERMIT OF FILIPINO VETERANS AND THEIR IMMEDIATE FAMILY MEMBERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE ACT GRANTING PERMANENT RESIDENT STATUS, OTHER RIGHTS AND PRIVILEGES TO FILIPINO VETERANS OF WORLD WAR II WHO ACQUIRED AMERICAN CITIZENSHIP UNDER THE UNITED STATES IMMIGRANT ACT OF 1990 AND ANY OTHER PRIOR ACTS FOR THESE PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes that the multiple entry visa and reentry permit granted to Filipino veterans who have acquired American citizenship and their immediate family members be extended until their application for permanent resident status in the Philippines has been approved. This will save them time, money and effort in having their visa or permit renewed.
