House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03254
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REGULATE THE NUCLEAR, SECURITY AND SAFETY ASPECTS IN THE PEACEFUL UTILIZATION OF IONIZING RADIATION SOURCES THROUGH THE CREATION OF THE PHILIPPINE NUCLEAR REGULATORY AUTHORITY APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Comprehensive Nuclear Regulations Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill creates the Philippine Nuclear Regulatory Authority that be tasked to regulate and control the utilization of nuclear energy and ionizing radiation sources for peaceful purposes.
