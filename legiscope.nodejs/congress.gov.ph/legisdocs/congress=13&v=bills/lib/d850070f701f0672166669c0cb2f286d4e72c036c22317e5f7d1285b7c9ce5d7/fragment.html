HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02355
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR AN ALTERNATIVE MODE OF PAYMENT FOR ALL SCHOOL FEES UNDER THE EDUCATIONAL BENEFITS, THEREBY AMENDING SECTION 3 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6948, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS 'AN ACT STANDARDIZING AND UPGRADING THE BENEFITS FOR MILITARY VETERANS AND THEIR DEPENDENTS'
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for an alternative mode of payment for matriculation and other fees to proprietary educational institutions that accommodate scholars of the Philippine Veterans Affairs Office.
