HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00950
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING A FEEDBACK MECHANISM IN THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES (AFP) AND THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE (PNP), CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE THE AFP/PNP WELFARE AND AFFAIRS OFFICE, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This measure creates a channel for police and military personnel to air their sentiments and grievances to arrive at prompt solutions rather than their resorting to rebellion and coup d' etat.
