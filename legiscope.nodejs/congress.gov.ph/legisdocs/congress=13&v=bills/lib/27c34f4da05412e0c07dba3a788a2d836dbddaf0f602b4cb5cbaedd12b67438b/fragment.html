House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02268
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING AND REGULATING TUITION AND OTHER SCHOOL FEES IN ALL HIGHER AND BASIC EDUCATION INSTITUTIONS, CREATING A TUITION FEE RATIONALIZING COUNCIL AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Tuition Fee Rationalization Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Tuition Fee Rationalization Act of 2004." This bill establishes a tuition fee rationalization council to be headed by a multi-sectoral body that will serve as the highest policy-making body on matters pertaining to tuition fee rates and other increases or adjustments in school fees.
