House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00232
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN SECTIONS OF THE PHILIPPINE MERCHANT MARINE OFFICERS LAW
ABSTRACT : Seeks to upgrade the marine profession by requiring the future marine officers and seafarers to undergo practical apprenticeship to be served on board seagoing vessels for a prescribed period and duly supervised by a qualified officer.
