HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04697
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING MANUFACTURERS OF LIQUEFIED PETROLEUM GAS (LPG) TO INSTALL STANDARD METER GAUGE ON EVERY LIQUEFIED PETROLEUM GAS CYLINDER TANK AND PRESCRIBING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill requires all marketers engaged in the manufacture, sale and distribution of liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) to install a standard meter gauge in every LPG steel cylinder tank to protect consumers from shortselling or underfilling of LPG tanks.
