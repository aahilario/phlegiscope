House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04623
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FACILITATING FOREIGN TRADE AND COMMERCE, AMENDING CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9280, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill allows exporters and importers to undertake the preparation of customs requisite documents for exports and imports; the declaration of customs duties and taxes; the preparation, signing, filing, lodging and processing of export and import entries.
