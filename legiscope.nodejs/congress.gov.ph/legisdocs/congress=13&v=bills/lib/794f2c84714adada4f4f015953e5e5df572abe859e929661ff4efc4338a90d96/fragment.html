HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00674
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE BENEFITS OF BARANGAY OFFICIALS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 393 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, CREATING A NATIONAL BARANGAY BENEFITS AUGMENTATION FUND, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Grants the following monthly benefits: (1) to the Punong barangay - P2,000; (2) to the Sangguniang barangay, treasurer, secretary - P1,800; (3)to the Purok president - P600; (4) to Barangay tanods - P500; and P2,000 Christmas bonus to each barangay official.
