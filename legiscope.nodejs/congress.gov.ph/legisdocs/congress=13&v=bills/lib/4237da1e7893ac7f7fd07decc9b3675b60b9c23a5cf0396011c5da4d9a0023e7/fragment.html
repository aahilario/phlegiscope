HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00333
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AMEND ARTICLE 217 OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE MAKING THE FAILURE TO LIQUIDATE CASH ADVANCE WITHIN THE PERIOD PROVIDED THEREIN A PRIMA FACIE PROOF OF MALVERSATION
ABSTRACT : The Commission on Audit has been looking into possible ways to enjoin government officials and employees to settle their outstanding cash advances promptly. In order to enforce compliance with existing laws and regulations, this bill proposes to make the failure to liquidate cash advances within the specified periods a prima facie presumption of malversation.
