HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02173
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL VOLUNTARY INFORMATION PROTECTION CARD SYSTEM TO BE COMMONLY KNOWN AS THE "VIP CARD", APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill institutionalizes a national voluntary information protection card to be know as "VIP Card" in order to facilitate and streamline governmental transactions, cut red tape and increase productivity.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : BICHARA, AL FRANCIS D.
DATE FILED : 2007-08-21
