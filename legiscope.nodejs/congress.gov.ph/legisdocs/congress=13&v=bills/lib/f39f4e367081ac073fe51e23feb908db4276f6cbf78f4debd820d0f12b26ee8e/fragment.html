House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00101
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING AN ADVANCED STUDIES DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM FOR EXCEPTIONAL EMPLOYEES FROM THE GOVERNMENT AND THE PRIVATE SECTOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The Pensionado Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "The Pensionado Act of 2004". It is imperative for the government to devise a program for overseas education and training for the brightest and most promising Filipinos who are already employed or self-employed in a branch of science or technology along identified priority areas. The proposed bill endeavors to provide the country with a continuing supply of highly trained leaders for industry, research and the academe through the coordination of these talents under a single governing body where all scholarships, under foreign and local sponsorships, are pooled together.
