FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING A THIRTEENTH-MONTH PENSION BENEFIT TO PHILIPPINE WAR VETERANS AND THEIR SURVIVING SPOUSES
ABSTRACT : The bill grants a thirteenth-month pension benefit to Philippine war veterans as an additional benefit to the benefits and assistance embodied in RA 6948, as amended.
