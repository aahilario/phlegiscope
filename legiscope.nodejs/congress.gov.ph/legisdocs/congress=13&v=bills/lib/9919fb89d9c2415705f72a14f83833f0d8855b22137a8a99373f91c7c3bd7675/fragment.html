House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02938
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR ADDITIONAL BENEFITS FOR SENIOR CITIZENS, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9257 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE EXPANDED SENIOR CITIZENS ACT OF 2003
SHORT TITLE : Revised Expanded Senior Citizens Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Revised Expanded Senior Citizens Act of 2004." The measure amends RA 9257 by granting senior citizen a 20% discount on the purchase of doctor-prescribed unbranded generic medicines and a 5% discount only on doctor-prescribed branded medicines.
