House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03406
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO SAVE THE LIVES OF MEN BY ESTABLISHING WITHIN THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AN OFFICE OF MEN'S HEALTH
SHORT TITLE : "Men's Health Act of 2008"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks the establishment of the Office of Men's Health that shall coordinate the fragmented men's health awareness, prevention and research efforts being undertaken by the Department of Health.
