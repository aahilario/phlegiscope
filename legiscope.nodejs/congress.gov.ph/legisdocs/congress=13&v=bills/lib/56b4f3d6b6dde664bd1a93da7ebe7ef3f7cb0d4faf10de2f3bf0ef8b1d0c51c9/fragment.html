HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03569
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING A ONE-TIME THREE YEAR REGISTRATION FOR ALL BRAND NEW CARS
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes that the registration of brand new cars be adjusted to cover a three-year period renewable annually after the initial three-year period. The proposed registration scheme is expected to raise much needed revenues for the government.
