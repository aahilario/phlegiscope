House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01256
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMOTING THE ROLE, DIRECT PARTICIPATION AND ACTIVE INVOLVEMENT OF THE COMMON PEOPLE THROUGH COOPERATIVES IN GOVERNMENT INFRASTRUCTURE CONTRACTS AND PROJECTS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "People's Role in Gov't Infrastructure/Contracts and Projects Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "People's Role in Gov't Infrastructure/Contracts and Projects Act of 2007". The measure allows cooperatives to join bids for government infrastructure projects. It prescribes the qualifications, rights and prohibitions of cooperative-contractors.
