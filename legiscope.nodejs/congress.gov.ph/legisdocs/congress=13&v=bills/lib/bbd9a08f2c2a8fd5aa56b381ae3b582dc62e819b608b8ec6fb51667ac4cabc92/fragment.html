House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04974
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 6,7,8 AND 24 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9337, ENTITLED AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 27,28,34,106,107,108,109,110,111,112,113,114,116,117,119,121,148,151,236,237 1ND 288 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997 AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to exempt from the value-added tax (VAT) the sales of electricity by generation, transmission and distribution companies and duly-registered electric cooperatives, and supply of power as well as the sales and importation of petroleum products. It also seeks to repeal the capping of the input VAT at 70% of output VAT.
