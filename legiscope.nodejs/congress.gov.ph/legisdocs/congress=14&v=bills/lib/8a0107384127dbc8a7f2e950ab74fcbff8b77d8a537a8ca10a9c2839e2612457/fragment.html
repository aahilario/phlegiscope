HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01807
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING FROM THE PAYMENT OF INDIVIDUAL INCOME TAX ALL MINIMUM WAGE EARNERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 32 (B) OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The measure aims to provide immediate relief to minimum wage earners and government employees under Salary Grades 1 - 3 by exempting them from paying income taxes.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SINGSON, ERIC D.
