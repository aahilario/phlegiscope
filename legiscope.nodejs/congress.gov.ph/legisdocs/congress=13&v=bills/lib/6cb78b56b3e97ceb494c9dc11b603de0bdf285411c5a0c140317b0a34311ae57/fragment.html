House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01412
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A PROVIDENT PERSONAL SAVINGS PLAN KNOWN AS THE 'PERSONAL EQUITY AND RETIREMENT ACCOUNT'
SHORT TITLE : Personal Equity and Retirement Account (PERA) Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Personal Equity and Retirement Account (PERA) Act of 2004." The measure provides that an individual may open a retirement account with a trustee or a bank accredited by the Central Bank with a initial deposit of P20,000.
