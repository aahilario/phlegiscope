HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01801
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 25 (d) OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBERED SIXTEEN HUNDRED THIRTY-EIGHT, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE AFP MILITARY AND SEPARATION DECREE OF 1979 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill reverts back to the provision of RA 340 whereby the years of service an AFP retiree who has served a fraction of at least six (6) months or more should be counted as a year of service in relation to the computation of the number of years in the service.
