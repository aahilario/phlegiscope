FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ENCOURAGE THE DONATION OF FOOD FOR CHARITABLE PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "food Donation Act"
ABSTRACT : "Food Donation Act". The bill adresses the issue of legal liability in the donation of apparently wholesome food for charitable purposes to encourage the donation of surplus food to needy people.
