HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01581
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE GOVERNMENT OR ANY OF ITS INSTRUMENTALITY, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, FROM INTRODUCING OR CONSTRUCTING ANY KIND OF IMPROVEMENT ON PROPERTIES CONDITIONALLY COVEYED BY PRIVATE PERSONS AND PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill defines "conditionally conveyed properties" as any property, real or personal, that a private person, natural or juridical, transfers to the government or any of its instrumentalities by way of donation, trust, assignment, usufruct, and other gratuitious mode of transferring ownership that imposes any condition where violation thereof shall result to the reversion of ownership or title to the private person.
