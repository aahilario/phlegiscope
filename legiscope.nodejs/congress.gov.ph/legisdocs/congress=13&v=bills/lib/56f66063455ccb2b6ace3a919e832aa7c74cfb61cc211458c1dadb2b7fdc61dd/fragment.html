HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00673
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 286, CHAPTER 1 AND SECTION 293, CHAPTER 2, TITLE 3, BOOK II, RESPECTIVELY,OF REPUBLIC ACT 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991" AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.
ABSTRACT : Provides that the shares of the barangays in national taxes and in impositions derived from the use of national resources shall not be subject to any lien or hold back imposed by provincial, city or municipal government.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ALIPIO "TIKBONG" V. BADELLES
DATE FILED : 2004-07-01
