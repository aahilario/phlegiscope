HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01960
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING PUBLIC AND PRIVATE SCHOOLS TO PROVIDE APPROPRIATE DESKS FOR LEFT-HANDED STUDENTS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The mandates all schools to provide appropriate desks for left-handed students to ensure their convenience and comfort during classes. For this purpose, the allocation for left-handed desks is set at 5% of the total student population.
