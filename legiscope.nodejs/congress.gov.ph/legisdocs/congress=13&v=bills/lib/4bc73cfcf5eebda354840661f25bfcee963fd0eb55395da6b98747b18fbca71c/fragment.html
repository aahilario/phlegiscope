House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03399
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT 8292, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE HIGHER EDUCATION MODERNIZATION OF 1997 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides additional powers and responsibilities to the governing boards of state universities and colleges (SUCs), the creation of a Research Council, the right to designate more than one vice president and the right to extend or finish the term of office of the president of the SUC.
