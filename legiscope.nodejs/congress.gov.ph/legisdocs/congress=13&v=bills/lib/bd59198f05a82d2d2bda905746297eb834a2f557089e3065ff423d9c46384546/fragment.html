House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01450
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7898, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "AFP MODERNIZATION ACT"
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes a research and development program on new technology to upgrade AFP equipment and weapons. Expands funding sources by including proceeds from lease or joint venture agreements on military camps. Exempts AFP projects from all taxes.
