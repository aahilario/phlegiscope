HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03308
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING EARLY RETIREMENT AND VOLUNTARY SEPARATION FROM THE GOVERNMENT SERVICE, PROVIDING A SYSTEM OF FUNDING THEREFORE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for incentives computed based on present salary rates to government employees who wish to avail of the early retirement and voluntary separation benefits program proposed under the measure.
