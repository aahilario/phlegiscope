House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02590
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION TO INCLUDE VEHICLE TRAFFIC EDUCATION AS PART OF THE EDUCATIONAL CURRICULUM IN THE ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY LEVEL, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the Department of Education to include in the curriculum of elementary and high schools the teaching of basic vehicle traffic rules as part of the values education subject.
