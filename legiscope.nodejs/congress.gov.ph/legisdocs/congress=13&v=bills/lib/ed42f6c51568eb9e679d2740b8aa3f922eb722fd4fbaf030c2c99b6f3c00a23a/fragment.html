House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01471
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE DISPOSAL AND/OR COMMERCIAL DEVELOPMENT OF CERTAIN MILITARY CAMPS AND RESERVATIONS, CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE THE MILITARY REAL ESTATE DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Military Real Estate Development Act
ABSTRACT : "MIlitary Real Estate Development Act." The bill creates the Authority to raise revenues for the AFP modernization by owning, holding, managing, developing, and disposing military real estate. Provides for the powers, duties and purposes of the Authority.
