HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02119
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 4566, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CONTRACTORS' LICENSE LAW
ABSTRACT : The bill allows any two or more licensees, each with a license to engage separately in the capacity of a contractor, to jointly submit a bid or act in the capacity of a joint venture as long as a license to engage or act as a joint venture is secured before the bidding.
