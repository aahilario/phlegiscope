HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01728
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REQUIRE THE MANDATORY ROADWORTHINESS INSPECTION OF VEHICLES TEN YEARS OLD AND ABOVE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to require all ten to fifteen-year old vehicles to pass a road-worthiness inspection test (RIT) every two years prior to registration by the Land Transportation office. Older vehicles shall be required to pass the RIT every year.
