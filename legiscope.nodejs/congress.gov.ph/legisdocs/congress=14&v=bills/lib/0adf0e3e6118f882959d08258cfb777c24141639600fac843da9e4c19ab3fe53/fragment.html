HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02193
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CIVIL SERVICE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Civil Service Code of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : "Civil Service Code of the Philippines". The bill seeks to codify all laws and relevant issuances governing the civil service into a single, comprehensive statute. It addresses deeply rooted problems besetting the public service such as graft and corruption, red tape, violation of government employee rights and the merit system, among others.
