FULL TITLE : AN ACT REORGANIZING AND STRENGTHENING THE PUBLIC ATTORNEY'S OFFICE (PAO), AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PERTINENT PROVISIONS OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 292 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS 'THE ADMINISTRATIVE CODE OF 1987', AS AMENDED, GRANTING SPECIAL ALLOWANCE TO PAO OFFICIALS AND LAWYERS, AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Reorganizing and Strengthening the Public Attorney's Office (PAO)
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to transform the Public Attorney's Office as an independent and autonomous office attached to the Department of Justice.
