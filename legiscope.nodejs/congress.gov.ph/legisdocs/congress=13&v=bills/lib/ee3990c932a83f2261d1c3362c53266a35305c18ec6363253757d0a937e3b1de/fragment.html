HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03851
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXPANDING THE EXCEPTIONS FROM THE NIGHT WORK PROHIBITION OF WOMEN EMPLOYEES, THEREBY AMENDING ARTICLE 131 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBER FOUR HUNDRED AND FORTY-TWO (PD 442), AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : To allow more women to work during nighttime, the bill seeks to lift certain prohibitions identified in Article 131 of the Labor Code, such as: a) in cases of emergency; b) in case of urgent work performed on machineries, equipment or installation, and c) where work is necessary to prevent the serious loss of perishable goods.
