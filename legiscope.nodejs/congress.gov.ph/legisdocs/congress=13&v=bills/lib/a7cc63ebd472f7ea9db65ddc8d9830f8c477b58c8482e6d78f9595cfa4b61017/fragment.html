FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING 50% OF PROFITS AND CAPITAL BY MINING COMPANIES OPERATING IN THE PHILIPPINES TO BE RETAINED IN GOVERNMENT BANKS FOR A MINIMUM PERIOD OF TWENTY-FIVE (25) YEARS, ESTABLISHING MECHANISMS FOR ITS PROPER RETENTION AND ADMINISTRATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Mining Profits Withholdings Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Mining Profits Withholdings Act of 2005." The bill presrcibes the rules that shall govern the repatriation of profits and capital by foreign mining companies.
