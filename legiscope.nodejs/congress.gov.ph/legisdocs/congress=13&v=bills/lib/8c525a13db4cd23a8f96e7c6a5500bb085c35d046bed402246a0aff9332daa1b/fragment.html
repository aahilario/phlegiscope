FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING REFORMS IN LAND ADMINISTRATION
SHORT TITLE : "The Land Administration Reform Act of 2005."
ABSTRACT : "The Land Administration Reform Act of 2005". The bill consolidates under the Land Administration Authority land administration functions such as surveying, mapping, land classification, titling, disposition and registration.
