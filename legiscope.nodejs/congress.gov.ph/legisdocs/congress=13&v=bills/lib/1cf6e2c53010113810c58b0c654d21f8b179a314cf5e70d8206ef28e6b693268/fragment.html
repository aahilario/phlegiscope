HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03779
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING ANY ADVERTISING AGENCY, TELEVISION OR RADIO STATION AND PUBLICATION WHICH EXPLOIT WOMEN AND GLORIFY SEXUAL VIOLENCE IN ITS ADVERTISEMENTS
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes on advertising agencies, television or radio stations and publications, which exploit women and glorify sexual violence, a fine of: a) for the 1st offense: P50,000 - P100,000; b) for the 2nd offense: )101,000 - P300,000; and c) for the 3rd offense: P301,000 - P500,000 plus cancellation of license or permit.
