House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00138
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A SCHOOL NUTRITION PROGRAM AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Child Nutrition Act of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : "Child Nutrition Act of the Philippines". The bills aims to institutionalize a School Feeding Program in all public elementary schools. It also seeks to grant incentives to private companies donating milk, juices, food and the like to the Program.
