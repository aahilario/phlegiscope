House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01347
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROMOTE THE DEVELOPMENT, UTILIZATION AND COMMERCIALIZATION OF RENEWABLE ENERGY SYSTEMS USING INDIGENOUS RESOURCES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Renewable Energy Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "National Renewable Energy Act of 2004." Promotes the production, commercialization and utilization of renewable energy systems using indigenous resources such as biomass, solar, wind, geothermal, hydro and ocean energy. The use of alternative resources of energy, which are in abundant supply in the country, will lessen our dependence on imported fuel.
