House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03097
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A SOCIALIZED AND LOW-COST HOUSING LOAN RESTRUCTURING PROGRAM, PROVIDING GUIDELINES THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Socialized and Low-Cost Housing Loan Restructuring Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Socialized and Low-Cost Housing Loan Restructuring Act of 2004." The measure grants qualified borrower-applicants of the Socialized and Low-Cost Housing Loan Restructuring Program the option to restructure their current housing loan accounts should they incur at least 6 months of unpaid monthly amortizations.
