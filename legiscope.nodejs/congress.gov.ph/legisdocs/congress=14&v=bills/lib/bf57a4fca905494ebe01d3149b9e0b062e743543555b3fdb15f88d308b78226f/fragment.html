HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02084
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING ALL MANUFACTURERS AND IMPORTERS OF HYBRID VEHICLES FROM THE PAYMENT OF CERTAIN TAXES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill exempts from the payment of import duties; customs and tariff duties; excise, value-added and ad valorem taxes the manufacture or importation of fuel-saving hybrid cars inclsuind its parts and components.
