HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02863
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE COMMISSION OF ACTS OF TORTURE AND OTHER CRUEL, INHUMAN AND DEGRADING TREATMENT OR PUNISHMENTS, PRESCRIBING PENALTIES THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Torture Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill defines and classifies torture into physical, mental or psychological torture. It criminalizes acts of torture and provides victims or their families legal assistance.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ESCUDERO, SALVADOR III H.
DATE FILED : 2007-10-09
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
