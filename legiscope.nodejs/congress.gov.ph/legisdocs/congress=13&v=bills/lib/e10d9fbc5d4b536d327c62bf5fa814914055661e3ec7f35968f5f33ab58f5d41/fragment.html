HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02628
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING A PRE-SCHOOL PROGRAM IN THE PUBLIC EDUCATION SYSTEM AND INTEGRATING VALUES FORMATION INTO THE CURRICULUM OF PUBLIC PRE-SCHOOL, ELEMENTARY AND HIGH SCHOOL EDUCATION
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Department of Education to design a pre-school program that shall be made part of the curriculum of public schools and shall be considered as a requisite to elementary education. It also directs the integration of values formation in the curriculum of all public preparatory, elementary and high schools.
