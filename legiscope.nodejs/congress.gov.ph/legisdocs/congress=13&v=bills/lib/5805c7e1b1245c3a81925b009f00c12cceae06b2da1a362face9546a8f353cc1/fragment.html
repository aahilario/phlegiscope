HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02341
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ANY PERSON RUNNING FOR AN ELECTIVE NATIONAL OR LOCAL POSITION TO UNDERGO A NEURO-PSYCHIATRIC EXAMINATION PRIOR TO FILING A CERTIFICATE OF CANDIDACY
SHORT TITLE : Improved Qualifications Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Improved Qualifications Act of 2004." The bill aims to require all persons seeking to run for an elective public office to undergo a neuro-psychiatric examination and to attach the result of thereof to his certificate of candidacy.
