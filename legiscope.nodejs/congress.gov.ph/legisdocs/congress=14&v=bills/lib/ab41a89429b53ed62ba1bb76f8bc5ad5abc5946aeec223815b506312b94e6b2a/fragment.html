HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03045
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 3, 4, 6, 8(e) AND 12(b) AND REPEALING SECTION 12(a) OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 51, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE NATIONAL CODE OF MARKETING OF BREASTMILK SUBSTITUTES, BREASTMILK SUPPLEMENTS AND OTHER RELATED PRODUCTS PENALIZING VIOLATIONS THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits the advertising or promotion of infant formula for those between 0-6 months. It seeks to more closely align the Milk Code with the International Code of Marketing Breastmilk Substitutes.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : PUENTEVELLA, MONICO O.
DATE FILED : 2007-11-08
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
