House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03802
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING MINIMUM WAGE EARNERS FROM INCOME TAX PAYMENTS, REDUCING THE INCOME TAX RATES OF INDIVIDUAL TAXPAYERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 24(a)(1)(c)OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE, AS AMENDED AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to exempt minimum wage earners from income taxation and to impose higher tax rates on individuals earning more than P1.0 million annually.
