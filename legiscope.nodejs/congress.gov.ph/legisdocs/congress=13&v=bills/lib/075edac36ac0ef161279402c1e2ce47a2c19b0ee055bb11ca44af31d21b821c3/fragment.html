House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00597
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE UNIVERSITY OF THE PHILIPPINES AS THE NATIONAL UNIVERSITY
SHORT TITLE : The University of the Philippines Charter
ABSTRACT : "The University of the Philippines Charter". The measure strengthens the University of the Philippines by declaring it a policy of the State to promote, foster, nurture and protect the right of all citizens to quality education.
