HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00478
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1949 ENTITLED 'ESTABLISHING A JUDICIARY DEVELOPMENT FUND' AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill democratizes the use of the Judiciary Development Fund by providing that at least ninety percent (90%) of the Fund shall be used for cost of living allowances, and not more than ten percent (10%) shall be used for office equipment and facilities in the Courts where the legal fees are collected.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : PUENTEVELLA, MONICO O.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-02
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
