HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00410
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A CEILING ON ALL PUBLIC DEBTS OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Public Debt Ceiling Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Public Debt Ceiling Act of 2004". In view of the ballooning public debt of the national government, this bill seeks to impose a debt cap or debt ceiling so as not to hamper the delivery of basic social services and other priority concerns of the government to the detriment of public interest. It sets the total public debt ceiling at PhP1.2 trillion
