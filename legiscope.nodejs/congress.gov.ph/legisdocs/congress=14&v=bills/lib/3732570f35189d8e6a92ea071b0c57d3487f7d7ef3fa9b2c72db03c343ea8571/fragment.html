House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02317
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING ONE-STOP ACTION CENTER FOR PURPOSES OF LAND REGISTRATION IN EVERY LEGISLATIVE DISTRICT THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to establish a one-stop center in every legislative district to facilitate the processing and documentation of all requirements relating to land registration.
