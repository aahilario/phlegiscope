HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00349
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FIXING THE RATE OF SUBSIDIARY IMPRISONMENT IN CASE OF INSOLVENCY OF THE CONVICT, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLE 39 OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : In cases where the convict in unable to pay the fine imposed on him, the bill proposes to increase the monetary component of the rate of subsidiary penalty to an amount equivalent to the daily minimum wage of a laborer in the national capital region.
