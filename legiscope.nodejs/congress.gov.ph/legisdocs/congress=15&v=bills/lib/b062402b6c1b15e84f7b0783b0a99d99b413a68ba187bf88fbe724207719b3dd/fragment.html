FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR MANDATORY HEPATITIS B VACCINATION OF ALL INFANTS WITHIN A REASONABLE PERIOD OF TIME AFTER BIRTH, AND OTHER VACCINE-PREVENTABLE DISEASE
SHORT TITLE : Mandatory Infant-health Immunization Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Mandatory Infant-Health Immunization Act of 2010.” Adopts a comprehensive, mandatory and sustainable immunization program for the Heptitis B and other Vaccine-Preventable Diseases for all infants.
