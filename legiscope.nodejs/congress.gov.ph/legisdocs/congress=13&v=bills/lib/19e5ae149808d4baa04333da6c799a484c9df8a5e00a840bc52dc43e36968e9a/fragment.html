House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05916
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE MEDICAL TOURISM BUREAU WITHIN THE DEPARTMENT OF TOURISM, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the creation of the Medical Tourism Bureau which shall be tasked to address the needs of foreign tourists, Filipino balikbayans and overseas Filipino workers seeking medical treatment in the country.
