FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO INTEGRATE INFORMATION COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY EDUCATION INTO THE PUBLIC ELEMENTARY AND SECODARY CURRICULA AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Information Communication Technology Education Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Information Communication Technology Education Act of 2004." Declares it mandatory for all public elementary and high school to include computer education in their curricula.
