House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01475
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PRESCRIBING A RATIONALIZED STAFFING PATTERN FOR THE SUPPORT STAFF OF PROVINCIAL/CITY PROSECUTORS OFFICE
ABSTRACT : E.O. 910 issued on September 22, 1983 rationalized the salaries of provincial or city fiscals' offices and adopted a standard staffing pattern for the support staff. The bill seeks to provide for an updated staffing pattern for each prosecutors' office since E.O. 910 has become obsolete.
