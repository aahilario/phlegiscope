House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03804
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REFORMING THE NATIONAL FOOD AUTHORITY, CONVERTING IT INTO A GOVERNMENT-OWNED SERVICE CORPORATION, DEFINING ITS POWERS TO INSURE FOOD SECURITY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : National Food Authority Reform Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "National Food Authority Reform Act of 2005." The measure seeks to convert the National Food Authority into a government-owned and controlled corporation with clearly defined powers to ensure the food security of the country.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CATALINO V. FIGUEROA
