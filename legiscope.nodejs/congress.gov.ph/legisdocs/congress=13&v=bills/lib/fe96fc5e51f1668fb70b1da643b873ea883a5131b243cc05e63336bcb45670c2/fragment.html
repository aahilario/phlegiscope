House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05837
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING RELIGIOUS OR RACIAL PROFILING OF INDIGENOUS CULTURAL COMMUNITIES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Religious or Racial Profiling Act of 2006."
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Religious or Racial Profiling Act of 2006". The bill prohibits acts that are commonly described as religious or racial profiling against Filipinos who are considered minorities by virtue of their cultural or religiuos background.
