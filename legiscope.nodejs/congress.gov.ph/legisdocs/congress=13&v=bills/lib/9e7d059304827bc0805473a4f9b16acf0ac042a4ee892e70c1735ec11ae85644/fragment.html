House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00737
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A LEGAL ATTACHE OFFICE IN THE MIDDLE EAST COUNTRIES AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8042 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "MIGRANT WORKERS AND OVERSEAS FILIPINOS ACT OF 1995", AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : As of December 2001, there are 897,000 Filipino workers in Saudi Arabia and 128,604 in the United Arab Emirates. According to Migrante International, at present, there are 44 Filipino workers in various jails in Saudi Arabia facing the possibility of beheading unless the Philippine government intervenes. This measure seeks to provide a remedy to the plight of our migrant workers by establishing a Legal Attache Office in every Middle East country particularly in Saudi Arabia and in the United Arab Emirates.
