House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00076
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MODERNIZATION OF THE GOVERNMENT ARSENAL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Government Arsenal Modernization Act
ABSTRACT : “Government Arsenal Modernization Act.” Seeks to modernize the government arsenal by empowering it to become more productive thus achieving self-reliance in the production of munitions for the country’s armed forces.
