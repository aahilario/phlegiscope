HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01767
FULL TITLE : AN ACT BANNING THE ENTRY, SALE, PROCESSING, FIELD TESTING AND RELEASE OF PRODUCTS CONSISTING OF, CONTAINING OR DERIVED FROM GENETICALLY MODIFIED ORGANISMS (GMOS) INTO AND WITHIN THE COUNTRY AND IMPOSING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : GMO Prohibition Act of 2000
ABSTRACT : "GMO Prohibition Act of 2000." The bill prohibits the entry, sale, processing, field testing and release of products containing or derived from genetically modified organisms (GMOs) for five years. It directs the National Committee on Biosafety of the Philippines to establish a procedure for granting permits and monitoring the effects of GMOs on human health and the environment.
