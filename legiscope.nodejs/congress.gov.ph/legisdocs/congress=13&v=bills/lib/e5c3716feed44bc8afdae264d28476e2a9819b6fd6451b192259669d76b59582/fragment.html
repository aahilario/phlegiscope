House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00082
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A NATIONAL HEALTH CODE OF 2004
SHORT TITLE : National Health Code of the Philippines of 2004
ABSTRACT : "National Health Code of the Philippines of 2004". The measure seeks to unify the health care delivery system of the country by emphasizing the meaningful partnership of each health component - the government units, the private sector, the nongovernment units and the community, which should perform to make health care available, accessible, acceptable and affordable. The Code will serve as an important directional reference and policy basis for planners, public and private administrators, program implementers, researchers and the general public.
