FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING HARBOR PILOTAGE SERVICE AND THE CONDUCT OF HARBOR PILOTS IN ALL PORTS IN THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Pilotage Service Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Pilotage Service Act of 2007". The bill clearly defines pilotage services and provides a mechanism to determine the following: a) pilotage rates and b) extent of maritime liability of harbor pilots.
