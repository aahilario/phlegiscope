House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02262
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE LOCAL SCHOOL BOARDS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS 98, 99 AND 100 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991" AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill strengthens the Local School Boards by increasing its membership to include a representative from an accredited nongovernment or people's organization involved in non-formal education and training programs. It further directs the Boards to formulate the school building program, subject to the approval of the concerned sanggunian, and undertake the selection of the site of the schools. It furthermore integrates non-formal education and training programs in the priorities of the annual school board budget.
