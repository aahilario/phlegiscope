FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING CASH INCENTIVES TO DIFFERENTLY-ABLED ATHLETES, COACHES AND TRAINERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9064, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "SPORTS BENEFITS AND INCENTIVES ACT OF 2001"
ABSTRACT : The measure grants cash incentives to differently-abled athletes who win gold, silver and bronze medals in paralympic games.
