HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04265
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING ARTICLE 26 OF THE FAMILY CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill grants the right to remarry to a Filipino citizen whose spouse was a Filipino citizen at the time of their marriage and who, thereafter, acquired a foreign citizenship at the time a divorce was obtained.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : Dr. REY B. AQUINO
