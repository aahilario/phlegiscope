HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02261
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE ROLE OF LOCAL HEALTH OFFICERS IN AREA-BASED HEALTH PLANNING, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 478 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OHTERWISE KNOWN AS "THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991", AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill mandates the local health officers to coordinate with the Department of Health (DOH) and other local government units (LGUs) to ensure an integrated approach in the delivery of health care services through area-based health planning. It also requires the local health officers to complement health services being provided by the DOH and the other LGUs through comprehensive health care agreements and other instruments.
