HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00189
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING SAVINGS DEPOSITS AMOUNTING TO FIFTY THOUSAND PESOS OR LESS FROM WITHHOLDING TAX, AMENDING SECTION 21 (c)(1) OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE, THEREBY, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Small Deposits Exemption Law"
ABSTRACT : "Small Deposits Exemption Law". The bill seeks to exempts from withholding taxes savings deposits of natural persons in any banking or quasi-banking institution amounting to P50,000 or less.
