HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00611
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 13-A OF THE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8291 (GSIS ACT OF 1997) BY PRESCRIBING OTHER CONDITIONS FOR ENTITLEMENT TO THE RETIREMENT BENEFITS, UNDER SECTION 13 OF THE SAME ACT, FOR GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES OR OFFICIALS WHO RETIRES OR HAVE PREVIOUSLY RETIRED AS A RESULT OF BEING PHASED OUT AND/OR NOT RETAINED BY HIS OFFICE/BUREAU/CORPORATION/PRIVATIZING/ABOLISHING HIS OFFICE
ABSTRACT : The bill provides monthly old-age pension to retirees with 20 years of service to commence after five years from the date of retirement.
