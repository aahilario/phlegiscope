HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02533
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR VACATION AND SICK LEAVE BENEFITS TO PRIVATE SECTOR EMPLOYEES, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE 442 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : For clarity, the bill seeks to abolish the service incentive leave granted under Section 95 of the Labor Code and, in lieu thereof, provide for both vacation and sick leave benefits to private sector employees and make said benefits mandatory.
