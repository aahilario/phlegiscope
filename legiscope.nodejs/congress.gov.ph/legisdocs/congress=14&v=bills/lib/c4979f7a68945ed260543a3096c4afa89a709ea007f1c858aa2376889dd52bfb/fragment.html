House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01932
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING A SOCIALIZED AND LOW-COST HOUSING LOAN RESTRUCTURING PROGRAM, PROVIDING THE MECHANISM THEREFORE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Socialized and Low-Cost Housing Loan Restructuring Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Socialized and Low-Cost Housing Loan Restructuring Act of 2005". The measure seeks to institute a restructuring program for all socialized housing loan accounts (loans amounting to P225,000 and below) and all low-cost housing loan accounts (loans amounting to over P225,000 but not exceeding P500,000) in government financing institutions.
