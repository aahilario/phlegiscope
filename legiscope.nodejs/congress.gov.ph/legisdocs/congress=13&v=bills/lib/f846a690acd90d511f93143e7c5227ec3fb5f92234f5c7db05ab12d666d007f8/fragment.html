House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05304
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TRANSFERRING THE PHILIPPINE COAST GUARD TO THE DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION AND COMMUNICATIONS AS AN ATTACHED AGENCY AND REDEFINING ITS ORGANIZATION AND PERSONNEL ADMINISTRATION, AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5173, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine Coast Guard Act of 2006."
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Coast Guard Act of 2006". The bill seeks to establish the Philippine Coast Guard as an attached agency of the Department of Transportation and Communications and provides for its powers and functions and organzational structure.
