HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01675
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF BREAST CARE CENTERS IN EVERY REGION NATIONWIDE, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill calls for the establishment of a breast cancer center in existing regional hospitals in every region to spearhead the government's fight against breast cancer. In regions where there is no regional hospital, the Center shall be attached to the district hospital most accessible to the most number of constituents.
