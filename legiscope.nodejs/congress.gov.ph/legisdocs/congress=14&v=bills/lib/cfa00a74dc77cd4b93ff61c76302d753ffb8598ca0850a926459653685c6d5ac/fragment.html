House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03071
FULL TITLE : AN ACT UPGRADING THE MONTHLY PENSION OF RETIRED JUDGES OF INFERIOR COURTS, FURTHER AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINE HUNDRED AND TEN AS AMENDED AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to grant retired judges an automatic increase in pension whenever the salary or compensation of incumbent judges is increased.
