HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04807
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE FIRST DAY OF SEPTEMBER EACH YEAR A NON-WORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE COUNTRY IN HONOR OF THE FILIPINO FAMILIES, TO BE KNOWN AS THE NATIONAL FAMILY DAY
ABSTRACT : The bill declares September 1 of every year a non-working holiday to be known as the National Family Day.
