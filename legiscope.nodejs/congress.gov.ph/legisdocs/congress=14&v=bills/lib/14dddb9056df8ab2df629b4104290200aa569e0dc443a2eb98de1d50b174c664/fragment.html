HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03386
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING ADDITIONAL INSURANCE BENEFITS TO BARANGAY CAPTAINS, PROVIDING FUNDS FOR THE PAYMENT OF ITS PREMIUMS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill grants barangay captains the following additional insurance benefits: a) death benefit - P200,000; b) attempted assassinations resulting in serious physical injury - P50,000; c) burial expense - P50,000; and d) reimbursement of actual medical expense - P100,000.
