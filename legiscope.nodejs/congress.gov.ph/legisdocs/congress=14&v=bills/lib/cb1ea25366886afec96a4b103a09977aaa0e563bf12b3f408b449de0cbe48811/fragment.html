House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00701
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE APPROPRIATION TO REPLENISH THE FUND FOR LEGAL ASSISTANCE TO MIGRANT WORKERS AS ESTABLISHED IN REPUBLIC ACT 8042, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'MIGRANT WORKERS AND OVERSEAS ACT OF 1995' AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Providing For The Appropriation To Replenish The Fund For Legal Assistance To Migrant Workers
ABSTRACT : The bill calls for an annual appropriation in the General Appropriations Act for the Legal Assistance Fund in the amount of P30 million inclusive of outstanding balances.
