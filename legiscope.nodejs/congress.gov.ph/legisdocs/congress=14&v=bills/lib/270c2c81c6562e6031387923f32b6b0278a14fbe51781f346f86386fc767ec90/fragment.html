House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02884
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMOTING INTERNET SAFETY FOR CHILDREN, AND PROTECTING THEM FROM HARMFUL INTERNET MATERIALS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Child Internet Protection Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to restrict access by children to harmful materials in the internet by requiring all commercial establishments offering internet services to provide filtered "clean feed" internet service and promote the use of similar content filtering devices for households and service providers.
