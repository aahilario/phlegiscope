House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04547
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 28 PARAGRAPH (C) OF REPUBLIC ACT 7279 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE URBAN DEVELOPMENT AND HOUSING ACT
ABSTRACT : The bill provides that eviction or demolition pending appeal shall not be granted or allowed by the courts in cases involving underprivileged or homeless citizens who are beneficiaries of the Urban and Housing Act.
