HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04664
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CANCELLING PAYMENTS OF LOANS AND RECOVERING PREVIOUS PAYMENTS INCURRED THROUGH UNJUST AND FRAUDULENT CONTRACTS BETWEEN THE NATIONAL POWER CORPORATION AND INDEPENDENT POWER PRODUCERS
SHORT TITLE : "National Power Corporation - Independent Power Producer (NPC-IPP) Debt Cancellation Act".
ABSTRACT : "National Power Corporation - Independent Power Producer (NPC-IPP) Debt Cancellation Act". The bill requires the cancellation of servicing of unjust and fraudulent debts, the refund of loans and interest payments already serviced and the refund of their equivalent in past maintenance expenses plus interest thereon, net of all settlements and negotiations previously made.
