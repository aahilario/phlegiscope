HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02577
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REORGANIZING AND RENAMING THE PHILIPPINE RETIREMENT PARK SYSTEM, ENLARGING ITS POWERS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Retirement Authority
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Retirement Authority." The bill seeks to rename the Philippine Retirement Park System to Philippine Retirement Authority. Likewise, the measure provides for the powers and functions, organizational structure, incentives and benefits, and capitalization of the Authority.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : VALENCIA, RODOLFO G.

SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
