House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03429
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR AVIATION NOISE MANAGEMENT AND REDUCTION IN RESIDENTIAL AREAS
SHORT TITLE : "Aviation Noise Limit Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill prescribes the aviation noise limits, provides for its management and reduction especially in the surrounding residential areas, and provides for a staged plan which involves zoning, land use planning and relocation.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.

SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
