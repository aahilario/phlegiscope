HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04360
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING AND INSTITUTIONALIZING PROFIT SHARING IN ALL BUSINESS ENTERPRISES IN THE COUNTRY TO ENHANCE LABOR PRODUCTIVITY AND SOCIAL EQUITY
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to institutionalize a profit-sharing scheme between management and labor whereby at least 10% of the gross income shall accrue as benefits and be equally distributed to the work force.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ROBERT "DODOT" JAWORSKI, JR.
