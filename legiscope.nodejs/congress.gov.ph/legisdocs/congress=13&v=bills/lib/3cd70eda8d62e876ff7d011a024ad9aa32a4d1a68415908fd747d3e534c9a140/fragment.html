House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02327
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REORIENTING THE CHARTER OF THE UNIVERSITY OF THE PHILIPPINES AS THE PREMIER STATE UNIVERSITY
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for a new University of the Philippines (UP) whose governance structure is transparent, democratic and participative. It proposes to replace the Board of Regents with an elected UP System Assembly as the highest governing body.
