House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03139
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING MONOPOLIES, ATTEMPT TO MONOPOLIZE AN INDUSTRY OR LINE OF COMMERCE, MANIPULATION OF PRICES OF COMMODITIES, ASSET ACQUISITION AND INTERLOCKING MEMBERSHIPS IN THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS OF COMPETING CORPORATE BODIES AND PRICE DISCRIMINATION AMONG CUSTOMERS, PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Anti-Trust Act
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Anti-Trust Act." The bill penalizes combinations or conspiracies in restraint of trade and all forms of artificial machinations that will injure, destroy or prevent full market competition.
