House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02339
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REDEFINING THE TERM "VETERAN" THEREBY AMENDING FURTHER REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6948, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to redefine the term "veteran" by restricting its application to those who rendered military service in the Armed Forces of the Philippines and have been honorably discharged or separated after a total of at least 20 years cumulative service, instead of the original requirement of only six years.
