FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 1 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9164 ENTITLED: AN ACT PROVIDING FOR SYNCHRONIZED BARANGAY AND SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN ELECTIONS, AMENDING R.A. NO. 7160, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991', AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the holding of a synchronized barangay and sangguniang kabataan elections on the first Monday of November 2007 and every three years thereafter.
