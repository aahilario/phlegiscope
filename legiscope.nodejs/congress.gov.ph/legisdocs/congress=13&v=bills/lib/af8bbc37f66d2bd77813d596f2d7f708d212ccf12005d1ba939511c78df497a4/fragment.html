HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04556
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LOWERING THE RETIREMENT AGE OF GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED EIGHTY-TWO HUNDRED AND NINETY-ONE, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED GSIS ACT OF 1997
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to reduce the compulsory retirement age of government employees to 57 years provided that he/she has rendered at least 15 years of service. It also lowers the age for entitlement of optional retirement to 53 years of age at the time of retirement.
