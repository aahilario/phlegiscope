HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04341
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE PRE-BLEND OF LOCALLY-PRODUCED METHYL ESTER INTO DIESEL FUEL TO PROMOTE CLEAN AIR AND BOOST RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT FOR RENEWABLE FUELS TO REDUCE IMPORT DEPENDENCE, OPEN A NEW EXPORT REVENUE, AND PROVIDE FUEL SECURITY FOR THE COUNTRY IN TIMES OF GLOBAL FUEL SHORTAGE
SHORT TITLE : Biodiesel Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Biodiesel Act of 2005". The bill seeks to harness coco methyl ester as a diesel enhancer to address air pollution, boost resource development, boost rural economy, reduce oil import and accelerate the development of locally-sourced renewable fuel.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : PUENTEVELLA, MONICO O.
