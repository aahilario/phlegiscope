House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01072
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 286 OF REPUBLIC ACT 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, PROVIDING FOR THE AUTOMATIC APPROPRIATION AND RELEASE OF THE INTERNAL REVENUE ALLOTMENT OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS
ABSTRACT : Section 286 of the Local Government Code provides for the automatic release of the internal revenue allotments to local government units. However, the provision does not provide for the automatic appropriations of the same. The bill provides for the automatic appropriation and release of the same to prevent delays in releases.
