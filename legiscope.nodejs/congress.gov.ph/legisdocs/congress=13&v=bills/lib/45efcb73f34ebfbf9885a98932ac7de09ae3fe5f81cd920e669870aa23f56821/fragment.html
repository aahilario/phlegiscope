House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00408
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE TRANSFER OF ANCESTRAL LAND BELONGING TO THE INDIGENOUS PEOPLE TO NON-MEMBERS, AMENDING SECTION 8 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8371, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Prohibiting the Transfer of Ancestral Land Belonging to the Indigenous People to Non-Members
ABSTRACT : RA 8371 (Indigenous Peoples' Act) does not clearly state whether the sale of ancestral lands by a member of the indigenous cultural communities (ICCs) and indigenous people to a non-member is allowed or not. In the absence of a clear prohibition, it is feared that our ancestral lands will be dissipated gradually. This bill seeks to remove the ambiguity in the law by amending RA 8371 to clearly prohibit the sale of ancestral lands by a member of the ICCs to a non-member.
