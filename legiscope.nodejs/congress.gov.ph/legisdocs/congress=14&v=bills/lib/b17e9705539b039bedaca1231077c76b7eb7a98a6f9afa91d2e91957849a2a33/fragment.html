House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01170
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE OVERSEAS FILIPINO WORKERS (OFW) MEDICAL CENTER DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks the establishment, operation and maintenance of a medical center for overseas Filipino workers (OFWs) and their families. It also provides for the government to contribute the land, building, equipment and facilities for the Center.
