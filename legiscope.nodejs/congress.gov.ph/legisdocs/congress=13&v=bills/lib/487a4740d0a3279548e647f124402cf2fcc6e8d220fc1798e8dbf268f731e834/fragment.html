FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE PENSION OF VETERANS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6948, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the old age pension of veterans by P500 upon the effectivity of the proposed act and thereafter, by P500 annually for the next 3 years.
