HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02898
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DISENGAGING PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS FROM COMPULSORY ELECTION DUTIES AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 13 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6646, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE ELECTORAL REFORMS LAW OF 1987
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the Commission on Elections to tap the participation, through accreditation, of volunteer groups and individuals to sit in the Board of Election Inspectors thus freeing teachers from this mandatory duty.
