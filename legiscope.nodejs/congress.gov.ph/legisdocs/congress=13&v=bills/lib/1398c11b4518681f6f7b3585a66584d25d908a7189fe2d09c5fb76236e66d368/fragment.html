HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00926
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE PHILIPPINE LONG DISTANCE TELEPHONE COMPANY, MERALCO, MAYNILAD/MANILA WATER DISTRICT, AND OTHER PRIVATE ENTITIES FROM CHARGING CURRENCY BILL ADJUSTMENTS TO CONSUMERS
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the PLDT, MERALCO, MAYNILAD/Manila Water District and other private entities to initiate ways and means to generate income from other sources in order to recover the currency bill adjustments and not to charge the same to consumers.
