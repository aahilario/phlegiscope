HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02594
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING GENERAL MIGUEL MALVAR THE 2ND PRESIDENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Having served as the President of the Revolutionary Government from March 23, 1901 to April 16, 1902 after Aguilnaldo's capture, the bill declares Gen. Miguel Malvar as the 2nd President of the Philippines.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : VALENCIA, RODOLFO G.
DATE FILED : 2007-09-18
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
