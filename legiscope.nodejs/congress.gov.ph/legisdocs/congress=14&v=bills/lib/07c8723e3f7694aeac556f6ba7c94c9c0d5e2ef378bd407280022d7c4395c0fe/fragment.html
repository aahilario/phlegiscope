HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02696
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE IMMEDIATE DISPOSAL AND DESTRUCTION OF CONFISCATED, SEIZED OR SURRENDERED DANGEROUS DRUGS AND PARAPHERNALIA, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE COMPREHENSIVE DANGEROUS DRUGS ACT OF 2002
ABSTRACT : The bill requires the immediate and proper disposal and destruction of confiscated , seized or surrendered dangerous drugs and paraphernalia in a public forum to prevent the same from being resold, recycled or from falling into the wrong hands.
