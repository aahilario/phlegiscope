HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03096
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING COMPREHENSIVE RENTAL CONTROL ON COMMERCIAL BUILDINGS AND SPACES, PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF AND F0R OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Comprehensive Commercial Units Rental Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Comprehensive Commercial Units Rental Act of 2004." The bill provides that beginning January 1, 2005 until December 31, 2008, the monthly rentals of all commercial units in the National Capital Region and other urban areas shall not be increased by more than the annual average of devaluation of the Philippine peso nor more than 10% cumulative in every year.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : EDUARDO V. ROQUERO, M.D.
