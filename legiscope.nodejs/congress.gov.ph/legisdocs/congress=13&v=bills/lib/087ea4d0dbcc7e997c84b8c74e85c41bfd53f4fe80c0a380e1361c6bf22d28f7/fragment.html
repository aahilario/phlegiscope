HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04142
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOWING LEGITIMATION TO CHILDREN BORN OUTSIDE OF WEDLOCK OF PARENTS WHO WERE DISQUALIFIED TO MARRY EACH OTHER BY REASON OF MINORITY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE, ARTICLE 177 OF THE FAMILY CODE
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to allow the legitimation of children conceived and born outside of wedlock to parents who are below 18 years of age at the time of conception or birth of the child.
