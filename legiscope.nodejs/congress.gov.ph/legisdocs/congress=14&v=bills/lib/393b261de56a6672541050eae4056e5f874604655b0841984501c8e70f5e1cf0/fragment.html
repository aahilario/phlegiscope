FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING ARTICLE 75 OF TITLE IV OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 209, AS AMENDED BY EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 227, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE FAMILY CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : Amending Art. 75 of Title IV of EO 209, As Amended by EO 227, Otherwise Known as the Family Code of the Philippines.
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to reinstate the system of conjugal partnership of gains as the basis for the property relations between husband and wife.
