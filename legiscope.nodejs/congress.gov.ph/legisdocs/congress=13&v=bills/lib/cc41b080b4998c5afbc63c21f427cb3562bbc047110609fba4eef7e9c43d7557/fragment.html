House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00116
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE PHILIPPINE COMPETITION COMMISSION, REGULATING AND PENALIZING TRADE PRACTICES THAT LESSEN COMPETITION AND OTHER ANTI-COMPETITIVE PRACTICES AND CONDUCT, UNLAWFUL MERGERS, ACQUISITIONS AND COMBINATIONS IN RESTRAINT OF TRADE, UNFAIR COMPETITION, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Competition Act
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Competition Act". Competition Law is the primary mechanism in establishing the rules to promote and achieve competitive markets. Its goal is to correct market failure, enhance community welfare, achieve higher economic growth and be competitive in both domestic and international markets. This bill provides for the rules of a competition law and fair trade practices which shall apply to all businesses in the country. It seeks the creation of the Philippine Competition Commission which shall administer the rules on competition.
