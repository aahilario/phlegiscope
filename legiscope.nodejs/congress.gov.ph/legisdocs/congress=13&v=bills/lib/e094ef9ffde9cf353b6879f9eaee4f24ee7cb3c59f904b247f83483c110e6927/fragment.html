House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04643
FULL TITLE : "AN ACT BANNING THE USE, PRODUCTION, SALE, DISTRIBUTION OR DISPENSATION OF ABORTIVE DRUGS AND DEVICES, DEFINING THE SAME, PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR AND AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE OF ARTICLES 256 AND 259 OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Abortive Drugs and Devices Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Abortive Drugs and Devices Act of 2005". The bill imposes the penalty of prision mayor in its maximum term and the cancellation of his license to practice his profession on any physician, midwife, nurse or any other health worker who causes or assists in causing an abortion by any means.
