House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04493
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE ALLOWABLE INPUT TAX FROM SEVENTY PERCENT (70%) TO NINETY PERCENT (90%) OF OUTPUT VALUE-ADDED TAX, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 110 (B) OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 9337
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to raise the ceiling on input tax from 70% to 90% of output value-added tax to generate revenues for the government. Furthermore, the proposed rate is deemed to be more acceptable to the business sector as it would enable them to recover more fully the tax passed on to them by their suppliers.
