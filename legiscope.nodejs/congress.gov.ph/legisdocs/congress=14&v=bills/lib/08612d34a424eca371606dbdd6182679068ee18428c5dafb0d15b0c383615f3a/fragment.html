House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03464
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING THE FINANCIAL REGULATION OF WATER UTILITIES, CREATING THE WATER REGULATORY COMMISSION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Water Regulatory Commission Act of 2008"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the creation of the Water Regulatory Commission that shall be tasked to promote the expansion, improvement and efficient provision of piped-water supply and sewerage services throughout the country, among others.
