HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01248
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING NON-WAGE BENEFITS TO MINIMUM WAGE EARNERS BY PROVIDING FOR A MINIMUM WAGE EARNER PERSONAL EXEMPTION ALLOWANCE, AMENDING SECTION 35 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill grants minimum wage earners in both the private and public sectors an additional personal exemption equivalent to the highest minimum wage in the country provided that such exemption may be claimed by one the spouses only in the case of married individuals.
