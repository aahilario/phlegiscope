HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01426
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THAT ALL EXPENDITURES FOR THE PAYMENT OF FOREIGN DEBTS BE INCLUDED IN THE ANNUAL GENERAL APPROPRIATIONS ACT
ABSTRACT : The bill claims that the government is unable to review and assess debts which may have been incurred against public interest and requires all foreign debt payments to be included in the annual budget for approval by Congress.
