House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02734
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN SECTIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7941, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'PARTY LIST SYSTEM ACT'
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to correct certain defects of RA 7941, to wit: a) the establishment of a two-stage system to fill up all reserved seats for the party-list groups; b) the listing of the three-seat limit per party; c) the adoption of the eight-point guideline of the Supreme Court for screening party-list participants, among others.
