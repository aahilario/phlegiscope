House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00953
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN THE UNIVERSITY OF THE PHILIPPINES AS THE NATIONAL UNIVERSITY
SHORT TITLE : "The University of the Philippines Charter of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The University of the Philippines Charter of 2007". The bill introduces significant amendments to UP's 1908 charter and its amendatory laws by empowering the university to perform the following: a) lead in setting academic standards and innovations in teaching, research and faculty development; b) serve as a graduate university; c) serve as a research university; d) lead as a public service university.
