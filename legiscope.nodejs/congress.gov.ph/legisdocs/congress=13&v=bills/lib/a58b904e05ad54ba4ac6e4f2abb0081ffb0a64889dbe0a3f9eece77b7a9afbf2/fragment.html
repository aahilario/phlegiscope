HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01488
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ADJUSTING PENSIONS UNDER THE GOVERNMENT SERVICE INSURANCE SYSTEM TO A MINIMUM LEVEL OF TWELVE THOUSAND PESOS (P12,000.00) UNDER AN ECONOMIC ADJUSTMENT PENSION PROGRAM
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to increase the monthly pay of GSIS pensioners from a minimum level of P1,400 to P6,000 increasing annually by P1,000 until it reaches P12,000.
