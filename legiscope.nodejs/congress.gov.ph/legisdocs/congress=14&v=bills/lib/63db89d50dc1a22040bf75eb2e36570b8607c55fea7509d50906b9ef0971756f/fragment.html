House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00151
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN EMERGENCY MEASURE TO ALLEVIATE THE PLIGHT OF COCONUT FARMERS ADVERSELY AFFECTED BY LOW PRICES OF COPRA AND OTHER COCONUT PRODUCTS, AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Coconut Emergency Measures Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Coconut Emergency Measures Act of 2007". The bill seeks to launch an income supplement program for small coconut farmers, tillers, workers and owner-cultivators that will provide them immediate relief from the adverse effects of extremely low prices of copra and husked nuts.
