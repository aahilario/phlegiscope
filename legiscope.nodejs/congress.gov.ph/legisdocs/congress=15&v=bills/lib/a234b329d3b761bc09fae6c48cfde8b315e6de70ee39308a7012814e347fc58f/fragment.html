HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00066
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN AND ENHANCE THE USE OF ENGLISH AS THE MEDIUM OF INSTRUCTION IN PHILIPPINE SCHOOLS
SHORT TITLE : Strengthening And Enhancing The Use Of English As The Medium Of Instruction Act
ABSTRACT : “Strengthening and Enhancing the Use of English as the Medium of Instruction Act.” Aims to improve the total learning process in the country’s educational system towards more valuable education for the students and stauncher preparation for their successful entrance in the corporate and industrial stage of the global commercial theater.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : VILLAFUERTE, LUIS R.
DATE FILED : 2010-07-01
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
