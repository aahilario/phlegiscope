HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00140
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EMPOWERING THE PUNONG BARANGAY TO ADMINISTER OATH OF OFFICE TO ANY GOVERNMENT OFFICIAL, INCLUDING THE PRESIDENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 420 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : Gives the incoming President of the Republic wider latitude or great opportunity to choose from among the public officials, who will administer oath to His Excellency thus giving due recognition to the untiring support extended by punong barangays to the President.
