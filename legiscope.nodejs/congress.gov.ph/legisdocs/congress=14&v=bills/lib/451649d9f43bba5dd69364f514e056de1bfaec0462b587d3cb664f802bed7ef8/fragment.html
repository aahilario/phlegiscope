HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01966
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REDUCING THE USE OF PLASTIC BAGS, STYROFOAM CONTAINERS, DISPOSABLE PLASTIC UTENSILS, AND SIMILAR ITEMS IN ALL ESTABLISHMENTS IN THE COUNTRY
SHORT TITLE : "Plastic Bag Reduction Act"
ABSTRACT : "Plastic Bag Reduction Act". The measure prohibits commercial establishments from providing customers plastic bags free of charge. It also directs commercial establishments to charge the cost of the plastic bag should their customers avail of one.
