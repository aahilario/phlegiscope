House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00111
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL GOVERNMENT-ISSUED IDENTIFICATION CARDS, CERTIFICATES AND LICENSES TO INCLUDE A PORTION INDICATING THE BLOOD TYPE OF THE HOLDER THEREOF AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : Blood Type Identification Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Blood Type Identification Act of 2010.” Requires all government-issued identification cards, certificates and licenses to include a portion indicating the blood type of the holder thereof in order to facilitate the immediate matching of the correct blood type of the holder, in case of emergency which results to excessive blood loss, to be transfused in order to save the life of the victim.
