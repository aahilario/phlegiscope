FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PRESERVE AND REHABILITATE THE FOREST BY PERMANENTLY BANNING ALL LOGGING OPERATIONS IN PROTECTED FORESTLANDS, CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE A TASK FORCE FOR THE PRESERVATION AND REHABILITATION OF FORESTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Forest Preservation and Rehabilitation Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Forest Preservation and Rehabilitation Act of 2007". The bill permanently bans commercial logging activities in all protected forests identified in the NIPAS Act.
