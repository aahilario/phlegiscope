House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04669
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 6, 7 AND 24 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9337, ENTITLED "AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 27, 28, 34, 106, 107, 108, 109, 110, 111, 112, 113, 114, 116, 117, 119, 121, 148, 151, 236, 237 AND 288 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill exempts from the coverage of RA 9337 or the Expanded Value-Added Tax (EVAT) Law the generation, transmission, distribution and supply of power as well as the sale and importation of petroleum products.
