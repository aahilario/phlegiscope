House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01532
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REENGINEER THE GOVERNMENT BUREAUCRACY BY AUTHORIZING THE PRESIDENT TO IMPLEMENT THE RE-ENGINEERING PLAN, CREATING THE COMMISSION ON GOVERNMENT RE-ENGINEERING, AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : The Re-Engineering Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "The Re-Engineering Act of 2004". The bill sets forth the re-engineering principles and guidelines for the planned re-engineering of the bureaucracy. It prescribes the composition, and powers and functions of the Commission. It provides for a compensation package for government personnel who will be affected by the proposed re-engineering.
