House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02260
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 106 AND 114 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991" AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill strengthens the Local Development Councils (LDCs) by providing for the mandatory inclusion of duly approved local development plans in the plans of higher LDCs and programs and plans of national government agencies for specific local governments.
