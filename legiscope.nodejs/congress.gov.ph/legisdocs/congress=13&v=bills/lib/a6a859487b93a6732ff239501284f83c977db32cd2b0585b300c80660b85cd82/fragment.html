HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04395
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LIFTING THE RESTRICTIONS ON ELECTRICITY GENERATING COMPANIES TO BUILD ELECTRIC TRANSMISSION FACILITIES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE ELECTRIC POWER INDUSTRY REFORM ACT OF 2001.
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to lift the restriction which disallow electricity-generating companies from owning and operating electric transmisssion facilities in order to create a more conducive business environment for prospective investors in the electric power industry.
