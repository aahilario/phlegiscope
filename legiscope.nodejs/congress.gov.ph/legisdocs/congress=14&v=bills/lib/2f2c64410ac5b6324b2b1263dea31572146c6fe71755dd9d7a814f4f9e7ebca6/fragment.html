House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00406
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN THE NATIONAL HOUSING AUTHORITY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 757, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to expand the objectives and powers and functions of the National Housing Authority (NHA)to enable it to address current issues and concerns in shelter delivery. It grants the NHA the power to condone penalties and surcharges imposed on deliquent loans.
