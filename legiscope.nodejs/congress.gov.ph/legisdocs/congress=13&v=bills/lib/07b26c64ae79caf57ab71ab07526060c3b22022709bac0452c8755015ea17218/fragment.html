HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03892
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 1 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8190, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "AN ACT GRANTING PRIORITY TO RESIDENTS OF THE BARANGAY, MUNICIPALITY, CITY OR PROVINCE WHERE THE SCHOOL IS LOCATED, IN THE APPOINTMENT OR ASSIGNMENT OF CLASSROOM PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS"
ABSTRACT : The bill gives bona fide residents of the city or municipality where the elementary school is located and the province where the secondary schools is located priority in appointment or assignment to teaching positions provided that the teacher possesses the minimum qualifications for the position.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : REYES, EDMUNDO JR. O.
DATE FILED : 2005-03-08
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
