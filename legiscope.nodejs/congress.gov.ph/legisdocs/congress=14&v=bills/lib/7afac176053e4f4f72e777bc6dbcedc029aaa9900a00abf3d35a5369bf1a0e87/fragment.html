HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01084
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A TIMBANGAN NG BAYAN CENTER IN ALL PUBLIC MARKETS NATIONWIDE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CHAPTER II OF THE CONSUMER ACT OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The measure directs all local government units to establish a Timbangan ng Bayan Center in all public markets nationwide to make weighing scales readily accessible to buyers.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : VILLAROSA, AMELITA C.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-12
