HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02491
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE SECURITY OF TENURE OF WORKERS IN THE PRIVATE SECTOR, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLES 248, 279, 280, 281, AND 288, AND INTRODUCING NEW ARTICLES 280-A AND 280-B TO PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to fix employment by the term of the project, task or work to be performed and not by a mere reference of time. It fixes the period of probationary employment to six (6) months.
