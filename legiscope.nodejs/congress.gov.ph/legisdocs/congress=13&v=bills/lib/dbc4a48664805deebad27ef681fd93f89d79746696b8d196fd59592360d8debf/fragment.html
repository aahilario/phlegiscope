HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02537
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING A NEGLIGENT LESSOR OF A BUILDING, WAREHOUSE, OR ANY EDIFICE FOUND TO BE USED IN THE MANUFACTURE OR STORAGE OF DANGEROUS DRUGS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9165, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE COMPREHENSIVE DANGEROUS DRUGS ACT OF 2002
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to impose a penalty of imprisonment from 12 years and 1 day to 20 years and a fine ranging from P100,000 to P500,000 on the owner of a building, warehouse or any edifice whose property is used for the manufacture or storage of dangerous drugs.
