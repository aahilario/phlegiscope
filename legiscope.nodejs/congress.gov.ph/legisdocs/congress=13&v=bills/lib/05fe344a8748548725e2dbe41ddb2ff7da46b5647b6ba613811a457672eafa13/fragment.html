House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03329
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING STRONGER/SPECIAL PROTECTION TO CHILDREN IN CONFLICT WITH THE LAW THROUGH RESTORATIVE JUSTICE
SHORT TITLE : Stronger/Special Protection to Children in Conflict with the Law Act
ABSTRACT : "Stronger/Special Protection to Children in Conflict with the Law Act." The bill provides a just and humane system of dealing with youth offenders through a comprehensive and integrated justice system. It emphasizes and advances efforts toward rehabilitating and reintegrating juvenile offenders into the mainstream of society as productive citizens.
