House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02568
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR SUSTAINABLE MANAGEMENT OF FOREST RESOURCES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The Sustainable Management of Forest Resources Act of 1998
ABSTRACT : "The Sustainable Management of Forest Resources Act of 1998." The bill provides for the limits and classification of forestlands; administration and management of these areas; forest protection; disposition and utilization of forest resources; forest charges, fees and rentals; and criminal offenses and penalties.
