HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02684
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ALLOW EMPLOYEES TO TAKE SCHOOL INVOLVEMENT LEAVE TO PARTICIPATE IN THE ACADEMIC SCHOOL ACTIVITIES OF THEIR CHILDREN OR TO PARTICIPATE IN LITERACY TRAINING
SHORT TITLE : "Time for Schools Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill entitles employees to a total of 24 hours of leave in one year to participate in the school activities of their children or to participate in literacy training.
