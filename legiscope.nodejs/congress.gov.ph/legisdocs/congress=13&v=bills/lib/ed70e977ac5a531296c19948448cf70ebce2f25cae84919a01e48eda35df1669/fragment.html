House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01428
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT REFORMS TO PROMOTE THE EFFICIENCY AND PRODUCTIVITY OF SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNOLOGICAL ACTIVITIES (STA)
SHORT TITLE : Financial Management Reform Act of Scientific and Technological Activities
ABSTRACT : "Financial Management Reform Act of Scientific and Technological Activities." The bill seeks to improve the system of operations in the procurement, accounting and auditing of science and technology activities. It requires the recommendation of the DOST Secretary before any fund could be released for research and development in the natural, agricultural, technological and engineering sciences to various agencies.
