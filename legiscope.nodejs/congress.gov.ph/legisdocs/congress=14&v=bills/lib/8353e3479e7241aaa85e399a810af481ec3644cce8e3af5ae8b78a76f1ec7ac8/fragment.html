House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02608
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR A STUDY AND FOR DEMONSTRATION PROJECTS REGARDING CASES OF HEPATITIS C AMONG FIREFIGHTERS, PARAMEDICS, EMERGENCY MEDICAL TECHNICIANS AND OTHER EMERGENCY RESPONSE EMPLOYEES
SHORT TITLE : "Emergency Response Employees Disease Protection Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to undertake a study that will determine the prevalence of hepatitis C among emergency medical technicians and other emergency response employees, undertake demonstration projects to provide them training, testing and treatment.
