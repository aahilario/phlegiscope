HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01294
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE RATE OF DUTY ON IMPORTED TOBACCO, CIGARETTE PAPER, TIPPING PAPER AND FILTER RODS USED IN MAKING CIGARS AND CIGARETTES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CERTAIN TARIFF HEADINGS OF THE TARIFF AND CUSTOMS CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase by 50% the specific tariff rates pertaining to tobacco (tariff heading 24.01 and 24.03), tariff heading 48.13 which describes the cigarette paper, and tariff line 5601.22.10 for wrapped cigarette tow or filter rods.
