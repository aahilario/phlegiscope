HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02984
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR MANDATORY COURSE ON HUMAN RIGHTS FOR ALL OFFICERS, MEMBERS AND TRAINEES OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES (AFP), THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE (PNP), THE NATIONAL BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION (NBI) AND OTHER LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill requires prospective members of police and military organizations to go through a course on human rights as part of recruitment and admission procedures.
