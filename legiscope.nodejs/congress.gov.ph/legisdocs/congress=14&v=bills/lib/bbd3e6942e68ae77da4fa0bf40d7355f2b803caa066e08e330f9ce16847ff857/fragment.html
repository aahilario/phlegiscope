HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00298
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO CODIFY THE LAWS AND REGULATIONS RELATING TO SECURITY DEVICES IN THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : "The Code on Security Devices of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The Code on Security Devices of 2007". The bill aims to consolidate in a single code all laws and regulations relating to security devices that may be used to enhance or support the creditworthiness of one or more parties to domestic and cross-border credit transactions.
