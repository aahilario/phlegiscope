House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00152
FULL TITLE : THE REGULATION OF ALL CIVIL AVIATION ACTIVITIES AND INSTITUTION OF SAFETY REQUIREMENTS, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Civil Aviation Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Civil Aviation Act of 2007". The bill proposes to establish a single, centralized and autonomous civil aviation authority, the Civil Aviation Authority of the Philippines, that will deal with all matters pertaining to civil aviation.
