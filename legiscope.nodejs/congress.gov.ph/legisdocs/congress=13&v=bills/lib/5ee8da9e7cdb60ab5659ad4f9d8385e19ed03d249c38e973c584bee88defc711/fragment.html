HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02076
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE RETIREMENT BENEFITS OF JUSTICES AND ALL JUDGES IN THE JUDICIARY, FURTHER AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINE HUNDRED AND TEN, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill entitles a justice or a judge, who has rendered 15 years of service in the judiciary and who retired at the compulsory age of 70 years, to a basic monthly pension for life starting at the end of the 5-year guarantee period.
