HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00935
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING A TEMPORARY MORATORIUM, THE EFFECTIVITY OF WHICH SHALL BE HEREIN PRESCRIBED, ON THE APPREHENSION, SEIZURE AND CONFISCATION OF PIRATED COMPACT DISCS, VIDEO COMPACT DISCS, AND CASSETTE AND VIDEO CASSETTE TAPES IN THE COUNTRY FOR THE PURPOSE OF ASSURING EQUITABLE ACCESS TO ENTERTAINMENT, PARTICULARLY OF THE POOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Emergency Equitable Access to Entertainment Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Emergency Equitable Access to Entertainment Act of 2004." This measure suspends for five years the confiscation of pirated compact disks and cassette tapes to rationalize the prices of these items.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SYJUCO, JUDY
DATE FILED : 2004-07-01
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
