HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02166
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO INCLUDE PETITIONS TO CORRECT CLERICAL ERROR IN THE ENTRY FOR AGE OR GENDER OF A PERSON IN HIS CIVIL REGISTRY RECORDS TO BE FILED WITH THE LOCAL CIVIL REGISTRY OFFICE OR OFFICE OF THE CONSUL, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT 9048 OR THE CLERICAL ERROR LAW AND TO INSTITUTIONALIZE THAT ALL FEES COLLECTED BY VIRTUE OF REPUBLIC ACT 9048 AND THIS AMENDING LAW SHALL ACCRUE TO THE LOCAL CIVIL REGISTRY FUNDS OR OFFICE OF THE CONSUL CONCERNED
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to remove age and gender as exceptions to petitions to correct clerical or typographical error in the civil registry records of the petitioner without the need of a judicial order.
