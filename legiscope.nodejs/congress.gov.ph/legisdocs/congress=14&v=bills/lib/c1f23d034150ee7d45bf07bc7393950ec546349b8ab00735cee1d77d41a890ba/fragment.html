House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02531
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ENHANCING THE SECURITY OF TENURE OF FARMERS THROUGH STRENGTHENING THE JURISDICTION OF THE DEPARTMENT OF AGRARIAN REFORM AND ITS AGRARIAN REFORM ADJUDICATORY BOARDS OVER AGRARIAN CASES
SHORT TITLE : "Farmers' Security of Tenure Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill extends the security of tenure to ALL farmers, regardless of whether they are covered by leasehold or tenancy status or not. It seeks to strengthen the jurisdiction of the Department of Agrarian Reform and its adjudication boards over agrarian cases and disputes.
