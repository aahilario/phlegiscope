HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02201
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 43, ARTICLE XII OF REPUBLIC ACT 9184, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "GOVERNMENT PROCUREMENT REFORM ACT"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes that WHEN THE INTERNATIONAL TREATIES OR AGREEMENTS REQUIRE THAT SUPPLIERS, MANUFACTURERS AND/OR DISTRIBUTORS BE FROM THE FUNDING COUNTRY, PROCUREMENT FROM ELIGIBLE SUPPLIERS/MANUFACTURERS/DISTRIBUTORS FROM THE FUNDING COUNTRY WILL STILL BE SUBJECT TO THE PRINCIPLES AND PERTINENT PROVISIONS OF THIS ACT.
