HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01009
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A NEW QUEDAN SYSTEM IN THE MARKETING OF PALAY AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : This bill mandates the National Food Authority (NFA) to determine the floor price for palay to assure the farmers or producers a fair return on their investment and to procure and buy from farmers their palay crops subject to the following payment terms: (a)seventy-five percent in cash, and (b) a quedan for the remaining balance which shall be paid within sixty days from the time the palay is delivered to its warehouses or within such time as may be agreed upon by the NFA and the farmers/producers.
