House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02157
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A PRODUCT STANDARDS BOARD, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to establish the Product Standards Board, which will be tasked to monitor and evaluate the manufacture and processing of consumer products. It also prescribes the composition, powers and functions of the Board.
