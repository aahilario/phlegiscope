House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02123
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ENSURE PUBLIC ACCESS TO OFFICIAL RECORDS, DOCUMENTS AND ANY OTHER INFORMATION OF PUBLIC CONCERN
SHORT TITLE : Freedom of Information Act
ABSTRACT : "Freedom of Information Act." The bill provides for absolute and mandatory access to matters of official information, public concern and public interest. It mandates all government bodies to provide the requested information within five days from receipt of a written request.
