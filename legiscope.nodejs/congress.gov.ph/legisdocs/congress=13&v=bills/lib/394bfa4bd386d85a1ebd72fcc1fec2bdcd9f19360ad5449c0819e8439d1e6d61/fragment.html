HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01503
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A NATIONAL JUNIOR POLICE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to establish a national junior police, composed of Filipino citizens between the ages of 15 and 20, who shall assist the local officials in the maintenance of peace and order and other related activities.
