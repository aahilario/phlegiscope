HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03286
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR AN ANNUAL INCREASE OF FIFTEEN PERCENT (15%) IN MONTHLY PENSION OF SSS RETIREES, PROVIDING FURTHER FOR AN OPTIONAL EDUCATION TRUST FUND FOR GRANTEES OF SSS MEMBERS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, THEREBY AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8282, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SOCIAL SECURITY LAW
SHORT TITLE : Social Security Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Social Security Act of 2004." The bill proposes to provide an educational trust fund among the grantees of SSS members, increase the minimum monthly pension of SSS pensioners and provide for an annual increase thereof.
