HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03166
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MANNER OF APPOINTMENT OF BARANGAY OFFICIALS, EXCEPT THE SECRETARY AND TREASURER, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the punong barangay to be appointed by the Governor from a list of 3 nominees submitted by the Representative of the district where the barangay belongs and for the barangay kagawads to be appointed by the Mayor from a list of 2 nominees for each kagawad position submitted by the minority and majority members of the sangguniang panglungsod.
