HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03414
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A NATIONAL CENTER FOR INFORMATION AND TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE RELATING TO ALL TYPES OF FAMILY RESOURCE AND SUPPORT PROGRAMS
SHORT TITLE : "Family Resource Act"
ABSTRACT : The measure establishes a national center that shall maintain a database on all family resource and support programs, and develop and sponsor training for family resource and support program staff, among others.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SANTIAGO, NARCISO III D.
DATE FILED : 2008-01-24
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
