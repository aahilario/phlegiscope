HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00389
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTOMATICALLY INCLUDING RETIRED SCHOOL TEACHERS TO BENEFIT FROM SALARY INCREASES GRANTED TO IN-SERVICE TEACHERS, EFFECTING CORRESPONDING ADJUSTMENTS IN THEIR RETIREMENT PENSION
ABSTRACT : After devoting their prime years and talents to the education of our children and future leaders, school teachers live their retirement years in penury because their retirement benefits are inadequate to cover their basic needs. The bill provides for the automatic adjustment in the pension benefits given to retired school teachers and to retired private school teachers to correspond to increases granted to in-service teachers.
