House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02817
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A SPECIAL PROGRAM FOR THE ARTS IN REGULAR SECONDARY SCHOOLS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Special Program in the Arts Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Special Program in the Arts Act of 2004." The bill provides a mechanism for identifying and assisting qualified students to develop their potential, talent and skills in the arts, i.e., media arts, theater arts, literary arts, visual arts, music and dance.
