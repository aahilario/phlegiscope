House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03009
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING MONOPOLIES, CARTELS AND UNFAIR COMPETITION AND ESTABLISHING THE FAIR TRADE COMMISSION, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Monopolization Law of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to encourage fair and free economic competition by prohibiting the abuse of market power by those in dominant positions. It creates the Fair Trade Commission that shall investigate, gather evidence and initiate prosecution of those engaged in unfair trade practices.
