HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03447
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOWING ANY PERSON DOING BUSINESS AS IMPORTER OR EXPORTER TO REPRESENT HIMSELF OR HERSELF AS CUSTOMS BROKER, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9280, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CUSTOMS BROKERS ACT OF 2004
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for any person doing business as an importer or exporter to represent himself before any government agency and private entities as a custom broker.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.
DATE FILED : 2008-01-29
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
