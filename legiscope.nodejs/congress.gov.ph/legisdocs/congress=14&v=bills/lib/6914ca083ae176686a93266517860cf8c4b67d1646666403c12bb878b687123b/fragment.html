House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02921
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING SMALL CLAIMS COURTS
SHORT TITLE : "Small Claims Court Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the creation of small claims courts that will have jurisdiction over minor civil disputes where the value of the claim does not exceed P100,000.
