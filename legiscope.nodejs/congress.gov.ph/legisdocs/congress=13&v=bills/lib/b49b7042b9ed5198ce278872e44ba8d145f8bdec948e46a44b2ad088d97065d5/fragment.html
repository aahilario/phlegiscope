House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00983
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE SUSTAINABLE MANAGEMENT OF FOREST RESOURCES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Sustainable Management of Forest Resources Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Sustainable Management of Forest Resources Act of 2004." The measure provides for the limits and classification of forest lands; the administration and management of these lands; reforestation program; forest protection; forestry research, education, training and extension; disposition and utilization of forest resources; forest charges, fees and rentals; and criminals offenses and penalties.
