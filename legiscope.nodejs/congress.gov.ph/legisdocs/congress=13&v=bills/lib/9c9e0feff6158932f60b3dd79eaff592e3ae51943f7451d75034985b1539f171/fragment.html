HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00697
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE POSITION OF TOURISM OFFICER FOR EVERY PROVINCE, CITY AND MUNICIPALITY IN THE PHILIPPINES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBER 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the qualifications, powers and duties of a tourism officer. The position shall be mandatory in all provinces, cities and municipalities identified by the Dept. of Tourism as tourist areas.
