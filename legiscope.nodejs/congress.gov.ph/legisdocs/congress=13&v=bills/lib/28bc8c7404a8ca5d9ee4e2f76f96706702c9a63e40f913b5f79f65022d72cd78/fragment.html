HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01495
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DIRECTING THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION, CULTURE AND SPORTS TO INCLUDE IN THE CURRICULUM OF ALL SECONDARY AND COLLEGIATE SCHOOLS AND UNIVERSITIES IN THE COUNTRY THE TEACHING OF POPULATION AND ENVIRONMENTAL DEVELOPMENT AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill requires the teaching of population and environment development in the secondary and college levels to help students realize the relationships between overpopulation and poverty.
