House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01808
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 284 OF THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, AS AMENDED, CREATING IN THE PROCESS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC RECOVERY FUND, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates that 50% of the incremental revenues generated from laws passed to combat the fiscal deficit (RA09334, RA09335, RA09337) be excluded from the computation of the internal revenue allotment.
