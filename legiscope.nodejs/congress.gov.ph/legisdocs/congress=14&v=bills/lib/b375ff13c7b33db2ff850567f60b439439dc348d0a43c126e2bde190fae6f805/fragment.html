HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00176
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING THE CRIME OF ART FORGERY, PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREOF AND CREATING THE ART AUTHENTICATION BOARD AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "art Forgeries Act Of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Art Forgeries Act of 2007". The bill aims to give a more exacting definition to the crime of art forgery to erase most of the vagueness, misinterpretations and loopholes that have plagued cases of art forgeries for years.
