HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01524
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING MONTHLY COMPENSATION OF DAY CARE WORKERS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to grant day care workers and barangay health workers a monthly compensation of not less than P5,000 to be charged against the budget of the local government unit concerned.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CUENCO, ANTONIO V.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-30
