House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00174
FULL TITLE : AN ACT BANNING THE CATCHING, SALE, PURCHASE, POSSESSION, TRANSPORTATION AND EXPORTATION OF ALL SHARKS AND RAYS IN THE COUNTRY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Sharks And Rays Conservation Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Sharks and Rays Conservation Act of 2010.” Seeks to conserve, protect and sustain the management of the country’s sharks and rays population and habitats to promote ecological balance and maintain marine biodiversity.
