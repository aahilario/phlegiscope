FULL TITLE : THE PHILIPPINE IMMIGRATION ACT OF 2010
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Immigration Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Philippine Immigration Act of 2010.” Creates the Commission on Immigration and Naturalization which shall principally responsible for the administration and enforcement of immigration, alien registration, citizenship and administrative naturalization laws.
