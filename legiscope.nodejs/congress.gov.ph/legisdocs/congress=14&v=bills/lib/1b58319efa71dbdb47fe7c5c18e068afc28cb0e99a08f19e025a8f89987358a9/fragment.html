HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02906
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO.386, ALSO KNOWN AS THE CIVIL CODE, ARTICLE 867
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to amend Article 867 of the Civil Code to make it correctly refer to Article 870 and not to Article 863. Article 870 stipulates the period limiting the prohibition on alienation at 20 years while no such period is provided in Article 863.
