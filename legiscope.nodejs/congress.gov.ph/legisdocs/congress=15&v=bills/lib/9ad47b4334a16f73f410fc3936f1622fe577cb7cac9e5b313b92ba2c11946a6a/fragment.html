House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00241
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A FILIPINO IDENTIFICATION SYSTEM AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Filipino Identification System Act
ABSTRACT : "Filipino Identification System Act" Provides for a tamper-proof national ID card which may be used to transact with any government instrumentality without need of further proof as to a person's identity.
