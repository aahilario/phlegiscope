HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00134
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 7 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8794 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS AN ACT IMPOSING A MOTOR VEHICLE USER'S CHARGE ON OWNERS OF ALL TYPES OF MOTOR VEHICLES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Seeks to amend Section 7 of RA08794 with the end in view of ensuring a more transparent disbursement of the monies collected from vehicle owners so that Motor Vehicles User’s Charge (MVUC) will be used to improve motoring in the country.
