HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03224
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 235 AND SECTION 272 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, FOR THE PURPOSE OF AUGMENTING, EXPANDING AND DIVERSIFYING THE USES OF THE SPECIAL EDUCATION FUND AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to levy and collect in provinces, cities and municipalities within the Metro Manila area an additional 1% annual tax on the assessed value of real property and in all municipalities outside of Metro Manila, 1/2%. The proceeds of the additional levy shall accrue to the special education fund of the local government unit concerned.
