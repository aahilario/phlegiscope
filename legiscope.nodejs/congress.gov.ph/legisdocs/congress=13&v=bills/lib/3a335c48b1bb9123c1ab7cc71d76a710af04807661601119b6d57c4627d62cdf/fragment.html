House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01577
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER AMENDING SECTION 13 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3019, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE ANTI-GRAFT AND CORRUPT PRACTICES ACT
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to rationalize the manner in which a public officer can be preventively suspended in office while a valid information is pending against him without, however, prejudicing the interest of the State and the general public.
