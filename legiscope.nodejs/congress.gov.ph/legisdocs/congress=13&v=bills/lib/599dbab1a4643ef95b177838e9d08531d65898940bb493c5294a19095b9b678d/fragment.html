HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03997
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS BY AMENDING SECTION 463 AND SECTION 470 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill grants the provincial governor the power to appoint the provincial treasurer, assessor, accountant, engineer, budget officer, planning and development coordinator, legal officer, administrator, health officer, social welfare and development officer, general services officer, agriculturist and veterinarian.
