HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02439
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MAINTENANCE AND ADMINISTRATION OF THE PHILIPPINE MARINE CORPS AS A SEPARATE MAJOR UNIT OF THE PHILIPPINE NAVY, PRESCRIBING ITS MISSION AND FUNCTIONS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Marine Corps Act
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Marine Corps Act." The bill provides for the Philippine Marine Corps to be administered and maintained as a separate unit of the Philippine Navy. The Corps shall provide combat armed units in amphibious and other operations.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CATALINO V. FIGUEROA
DATE FILED : 2004-08-17
