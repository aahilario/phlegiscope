House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01630
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING INCENTIVES TO PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS WHO ARE ASSIGNED OUTSIDE OF THEIR TOWNS AND PROVINCES, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Public School Teachers Incentives Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Public School Teachers Incentives Act of 2004." The bill seeks to grant a P1,500.00 monthly incentive allowance to public school teachers assigned to a school located in another municipality other than the municipality of his residence and a P3,000.00 monthly allowance if assigned to another province other than the province of his residence.
