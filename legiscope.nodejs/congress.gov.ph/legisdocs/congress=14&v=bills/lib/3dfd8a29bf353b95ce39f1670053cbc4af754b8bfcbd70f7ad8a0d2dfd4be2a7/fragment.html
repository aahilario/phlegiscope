House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01882
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPOSING HARSHER PENALTIES FOR ELECTION OFFENSES ATTENDED BY VIOLENCE, COERCION, INTIMIDATION, FORCE OR THREATS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill upholds the democratic right to vote and be voted for public office by imposing stiffer penalties for election offenses attended by violence, coercion, intimidation, force or threats.
