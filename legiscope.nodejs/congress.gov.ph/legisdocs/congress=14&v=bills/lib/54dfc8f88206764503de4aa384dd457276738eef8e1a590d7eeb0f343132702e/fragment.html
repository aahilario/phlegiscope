House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03012
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING DISCRIMINATION AGAINST PERSONS ON ACCOUNT OF ETHNIC ORIGIN AND/OR RELIGIOUS BELIEF
SHORT TITLE : "the Anti-discrimination Act Of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes a penalty of imprisonment for not less than 30 days but not more than 6 months and/or a fine of not less than P10,000 but not more than P100,000 for discriminating against any person on account of ethnic origin or religion.
