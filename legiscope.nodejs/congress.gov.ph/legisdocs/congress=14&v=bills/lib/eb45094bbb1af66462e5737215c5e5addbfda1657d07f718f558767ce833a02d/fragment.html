HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00085
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE POSITION OF MUSLIM AFFAIRS OFFICER IN ALL CITIES AND MUNICIPALITIES WHERE SIGNIFICANT NUMBERS OF MUSLIM FILIPINOS RESIDE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SEVENTY-ONE HUNDRED SIXTY, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the creation of the position of Muslim Affairs Officer in all cities and municipalities where there is a significant number of Muslims residing.
