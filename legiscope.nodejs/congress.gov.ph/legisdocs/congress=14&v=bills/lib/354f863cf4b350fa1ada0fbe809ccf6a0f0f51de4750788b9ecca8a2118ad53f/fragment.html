House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03082
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR DISCLOSURE OF FIRE SAFETY STANDARDS AND MEASURES WITH RESPECT TO CAMPUS BUILDINGS
SHORT TITLE : "Campus Fire Safety Right-to-Know Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill compels all schools to disclose their current fire safety practices and programs so that the areas of improvement can be properly identified and appropriate measures adopted.
