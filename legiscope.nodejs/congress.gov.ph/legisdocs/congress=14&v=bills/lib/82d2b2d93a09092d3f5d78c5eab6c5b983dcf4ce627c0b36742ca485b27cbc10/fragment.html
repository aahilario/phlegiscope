HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02584
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A NATIONAL POLICY ON STUDENTS' RIGHTS AND WELFARE
SHORT TITLE : "Students' Rights and Welfare Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill guarantees the following rights of students: a) right against discriminaition in educational institutions, b) right to competent instruction and relevant quality education, c) right to organize, and d) right to establish a student council, among others.
