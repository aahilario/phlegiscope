House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00800
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 337, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE GENERAL BANKING ACT, REQUIRING BANKS AND OTHER FINANCIAL INSTITUIONS TO SECURE CERTIFICATION FROM THE BUREAU OF INTERNAL REVENUE ON THE AUTHENTICITY OF DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR LOAN APPROVAL PRIOR TO GRANTING SUCH, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : It is the practice of many entities to maintain two sets of financial statements - one understating income for tax purposes and the other overstating income for loan purposes. To stop this practice, the bill requires banks and other financial institutions to approve loans based on audited financial statements duly certified by the Bureau of Internal Revenue as the same statement submitted to them.
