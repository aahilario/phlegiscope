House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02641
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY 23 OF EVERY YEAR A NATIONAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE ENTIRE COUNTRY TO COMMEMORATE THE DECLARATION OF THE FIRST PHILIPPINE REPUBLIC
SHORT TITLE : Declaring January 23 Of Every Year A National Working Holiday In The Entire Country To Commemorate The Declaration Of The First Philippine Republic
ABSTRACT : The bill declares January 23rd of every year as a working holiday to commemorate the declaration of the first Philippine Republic.
