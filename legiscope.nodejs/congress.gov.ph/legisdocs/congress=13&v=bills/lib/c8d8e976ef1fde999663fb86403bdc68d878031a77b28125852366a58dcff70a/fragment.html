HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02000
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FOR THE EXTENSION OF THE TERM OF FRANCHISE OF THE PAGCOR FOR ANOTHER TWENTY-FIVE (25) YEARS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 10, SECTION 13 (2) (a) AND SECTION 14 (1) OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1869, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE FRANCHISE AND POWERS OF THE PHILIPPINE AMUSEMENT AND GAMING CORPORATION (PAGCOR)
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the extension of the franchise of the Philippine Amusement and Gaming Corporation for another 25 years after its first 25 years of corporate existence lapses in 2008.
