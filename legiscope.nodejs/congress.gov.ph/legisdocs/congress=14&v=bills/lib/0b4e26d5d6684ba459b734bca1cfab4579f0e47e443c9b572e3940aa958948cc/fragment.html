HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03280
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE ALLOWANCES OF BARANGAY OFFICIALS, GRANTING HONORARIA AND OTHER BENEFITS TO BARANGAY TANODS AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase/grant honoraria and allowances to barangay officials, as follows: a) P3,500 per month for the punong barangay; and b) P2,500 per month for sanggunian barangay members, treasurer, secretary and tanods.
