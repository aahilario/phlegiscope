HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01715
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING PREPARATORY EDUCATION AS COMPULSORY PART OF BASIC PRIMARY EDUCATION IN PUBLIC SCHOOLS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Preparatory Education Act
ABSTRACT : "Preparatory Education Act." The bill seeks to make preparatory education (one year schooling before Grade I) mandatory for all five-year old children as part of the formal elementary education. The pre-school program shall be made available for free.
