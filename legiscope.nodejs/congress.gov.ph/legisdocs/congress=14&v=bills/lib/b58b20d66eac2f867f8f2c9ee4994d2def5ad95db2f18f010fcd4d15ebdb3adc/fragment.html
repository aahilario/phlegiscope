House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00474
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO BE KNOWN AS THE 'TRANSPORTATION SECURITY ACT OF 2007', CREATING THE PHILIPPINE TRANSPORTATION SECURITY AUTHORITY (PTSA) AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATIONS OF FUNDS THEREFORE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Transportation Security Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Transportation Security Act of 2007". The measure seeks to implement a comprehensive civil, aviation, maritime, land and rail national security program to contain and prevent, if not eradicate, incidents detrimental to human life and property.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : PUENTEVELLA, MONICO O.
