House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02812
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 18, CREATING THE SUGAR REGULATORY ADMINISTRATION
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to empower the Sugar Regulatory Administration to declare any excess importation of sugar over consumer needs as 'reserve' in order to balance local production with consumer demand and keep the prices of sugar constant.
