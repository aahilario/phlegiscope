House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00640
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CRIMINALIZING VIDEO AND PHOTO VOYEURISM AND IMPOSING PENALTIES THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Voyeurism Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Voyeurism Act of 2004". Imposes imprisonment from 18 months to 4 years and/or a fine of P200,000 to those who invade the privacy of others using a camera or a video camera.
