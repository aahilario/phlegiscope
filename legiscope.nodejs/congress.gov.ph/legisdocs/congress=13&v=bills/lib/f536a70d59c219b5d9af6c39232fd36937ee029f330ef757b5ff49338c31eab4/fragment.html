HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00374
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROTECTING THE RIGHTS TO LABOR OF THE WORKERS OF THE INTERNATIONAL RICE RESEARCH INSTITUTE (IRRI) AS ENSHRINED IN THE 1987 CONSTITUTION, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLE 3 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1620 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to ensure the protection of the basic rights of the workers of the International Rice Research Institute (IRRI). The bill once enacted into law will correct an injustice done to the workers who have been deprived of their labor rights since the signing of the Marcos decree on April 19, 1979 which granted the IRRI immunity from any penal, civil and administrative proceedings.
