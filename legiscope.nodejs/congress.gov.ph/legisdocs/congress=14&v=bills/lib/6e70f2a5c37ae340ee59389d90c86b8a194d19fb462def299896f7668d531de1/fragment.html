House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01766
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF PUBLIC RESTROOM ALONG NATIONAL AND PROVINCIAL HIGHWAYS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Land Transport Support Facilities for Hygiene and Convenient Act"
ABSTRACT : "Land Transport Support Facilities for Hygiene and Convenient Act". The bill directs the Department of Public Works and Highways to construct restrooms at 30 to 50 kilometers distance from each other along the national and provincial highways.
