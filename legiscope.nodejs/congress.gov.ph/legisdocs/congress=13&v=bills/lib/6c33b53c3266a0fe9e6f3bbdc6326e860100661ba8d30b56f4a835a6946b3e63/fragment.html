House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01620
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO INSTITUTE A PROGRAM FOR THE CONTINUING EDUCATION OF TERTIARY TEACHERS, AMENDING FOR SUCH PURPOSE R.A. NO. 7784, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "AN ACT TO STRENGTHENING TEACHER EDUCATION IN THE PHILIPPINES BY ESTABLISHING CENTERS OF EXCELLENCE, CREATING A TEACHER EDUCATION COUNCIL FOR THE PURPOSE, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to require private and public university and college teachers to under a training program in order to improve the quality of tertiary education in the country. The program shall focus on proper teaching methods and other areas of study where continuing education is necessary.
