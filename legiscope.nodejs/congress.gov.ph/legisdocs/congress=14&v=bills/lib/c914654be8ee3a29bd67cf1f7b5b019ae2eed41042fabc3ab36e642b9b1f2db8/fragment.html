HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03310
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING THE PUBLIC WITH ADEQUATE NOTICE AND EDUCATION ON THE EFFECTS OF EXPOSURE TO MERCURY THROUGH THE DEVELOPMENT OF HEALTH ADVISORIES AND BY REQUIRING THAT SUCH APPROPRIATE ADVISORIES BE POSTED, OR MADE READILY AVAILABLE, AT ALL BUSINESSES THAT SELL FRESH, FROZEN, AND CANNED FISH AND SEAFOOD WHERE THE POTENTIAL FOR MERCURY EXPOSURE EXISTS
SHORT TITLE : "Mercury Health Advisory Act"
ABSTRACT : The measure requires the development of health advisories to give the public adequate notice and education on the effects of exposure to mercury
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.
DATE FILED : 2007-12-17
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
