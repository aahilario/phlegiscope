HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02970
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOWING MEMBERS OF CONGRESS, LOCAL OFFICIALS AND REPRESENTATIVES OF NONGOVERNMENTAL ORGANIZATIONS TO PARTICIPATE IN THE PLANNING AND PREPARATION OF BUDGET PROPOSALS FOR THE NATIONAL BUDGET IN ORDER TO PROMOTE TRANSPARENCY AND EFFICIENCY IN THE BUDGETARY PROCESS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 292, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE ADMINISTRATIVE CODE OF 1987"
ABSTRACT : The bill allows members of Congress, local officials and representatives of non-governmental organizations to participate in the deliberations leading to the preparation of the national budget.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.
DATE FILED : 2007-10-18
