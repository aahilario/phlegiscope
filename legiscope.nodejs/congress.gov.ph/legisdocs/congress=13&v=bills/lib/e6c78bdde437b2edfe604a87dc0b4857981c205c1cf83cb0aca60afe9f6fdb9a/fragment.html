House Bill/Resolution NO. HB06046
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE RIGHTS OF PERSONS ARRESTED, DETAINED OR UNDER CUSTODIAL INVESTIGATION TO BE PROTECTED FROM PUBLIC DISPLAY OR PRESENTATION TO THE PRESS BEFORE FORMAL CHARGES ARE FILED IN COURT AGAINST THEM, AS WELL AS THE DUTIES OF THE ARRESTING, DETAINING, AND INVESTIGATING OFFICERS, AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF, AND AMENDING FOR SUCH PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7438, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Providing for the Rights of Persons Arrested, Detained or Under Custodial Investigation to be Protected From Public Display or Presentation to the Press Before Formal Charges are Filed in Court Against Them
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to strengthen the right of a person arrested, detained or under custodial investigation to be protected from public display or presentation to the press before formal charges are filed against said person.
