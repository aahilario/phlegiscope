HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02688
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE PENSION OF VETERANS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6948, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to increase the old age pension of veterans by P500.00 effective January 1st of the succeeding year from the effectivity of the act and thereafter, by P500.00 annually for the next five years.
