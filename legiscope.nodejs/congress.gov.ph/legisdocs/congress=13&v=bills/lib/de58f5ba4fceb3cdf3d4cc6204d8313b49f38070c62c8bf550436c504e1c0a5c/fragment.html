House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01561
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AMEND PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1638, KNOWN AS THE AFP MILITARY PERSONNEL RETIREMENT AND SEPARATION DECREE OF 1979
SHORT TITLE : Amending PD 1638, Known as the AFP Military Personnel Retirement and Separation Decree of 1979
ABSTRACT : The bill grants retirement benefits to: a) a widow who married the retiree after his retirement and their children; and b) a retiree who acquires the citizenship of another country.
