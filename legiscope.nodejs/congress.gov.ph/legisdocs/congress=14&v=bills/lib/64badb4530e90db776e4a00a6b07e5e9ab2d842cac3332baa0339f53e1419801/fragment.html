House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01739
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MAGNA CARTA OF THE BARANGAYS
SHORT TITLE : "The Magna Carta for Barangays"
ABSTRACT : "The Magna Carta for Barangays". The bill proposes the following, among others: a) that barangay officials be considered as regular employees; b) that every barangay be provided with the basic priorities like supply of potable water, schools, etc; and c) the automatic release of their share in national taxes.
