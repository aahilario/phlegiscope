House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05881
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXTENDING ELIGIBILITY FOR PAROLE TO PRISONERS WHO HAVE SERVED AT LEAST FIVE (5) YEARS OF THEIR SENTENCE AND TO PRISONERS 70 YEARS OF AGE AND ABOVE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 5 OF ACT 4103, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE INDETERMINATE SENTENCE LAW
SHORT TITLE : Extending Eligibility for Parole to Prisoners Who Have Served at Least Five Years of Their Sentence and to Prisoners 70 Years of Age and Above
ABSTRACT : The bill expands the scope of eligibility to apply for parole to: a) prisoners who have reached 75 years old; and b) prisoners who have served at least five (5) years of their sentence.
