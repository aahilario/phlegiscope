HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01620
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING FROM INCOME TAX THE OVERTIME PAY OF EMPLOYEES EARNING NOT MORE THAN [TWENTY THOUSAND PESOS (P20,000)] IN MONTHLY WAGE OR COMPENSATION
ABSTRACT : The bill exempts from income taxation the overtime pay received by employees whose monthly wage or salary is not more than P20,000.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ROMULO, ROMAN T.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-31
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
