House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02260
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING POOR LITIGANTS FROM POSTING BAIL WHEN CHARGED WITH NON-CAPITAL OFFENSES FOR THE FIRST TIME AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Poor Litigants Equalization Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to help poor litigants with bailable offenses from being jailed by exempting them from the payment of bail.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : VIOLAGO, JOSEPH GILBERT F.
