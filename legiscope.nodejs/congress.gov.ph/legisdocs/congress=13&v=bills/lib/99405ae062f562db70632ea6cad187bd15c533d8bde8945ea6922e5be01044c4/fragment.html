FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING NATIONAL HEALTH RESEARCH INSTITUTES, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Health Research Institutes Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "National Health Research Institutes Act of 2004." This bill creates the National Health Research Institutes (NHRI) that will harness the health potentials of indigenous products and materials, practices and other sources through basic and clinical biomedical research.
