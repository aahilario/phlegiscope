House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03335
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE ESTABLISHMENT OF POLITICAL DYNASTIES
SHORT TITLE : The Anti-Political Dynasty Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "The Anti-Political Dynasty Act of 2004." The bill disallows the spouse or any relative within the 2nd degree of consanguinity or affinity, whether legitimate or illegitimate, full or half blood, of an incumbent elective official seeking re-election to hold or run for an elective office in the same province in the same election.
