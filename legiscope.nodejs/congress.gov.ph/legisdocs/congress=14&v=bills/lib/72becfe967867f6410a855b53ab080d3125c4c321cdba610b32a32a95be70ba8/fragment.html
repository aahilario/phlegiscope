HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00429
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE DEPARTMENT OF HOUSING AND RESETTLEMENT, ABOLISHING THE EXISTING HOUSING AGENCIES, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Omnibus Housing and Urban Development Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Omnibus Housing and Urban Development Act of 2007". The bill proposes the creation of the Department of Housing and Resettlement, which shall serve as the primary agency that will prepare, integrate, coordinate and supervise plans, programs, projects and activities of the government relative to urban planning, development and renewal.
