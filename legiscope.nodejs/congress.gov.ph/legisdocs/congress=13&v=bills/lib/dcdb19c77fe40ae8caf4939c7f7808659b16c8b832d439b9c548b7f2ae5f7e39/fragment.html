FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 290 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill provides that persons engaged in the exploitation of national wealth shall remit 40% of their tax payments directly to the host local government and 60% to the national government.
