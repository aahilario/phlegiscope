HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00399
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 285 AND 286 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, PROVIDING FOR AN EQUITABLE DISTRIBUTION OF THE INTERNAL REVENUE ALLOTMENT (IRA) TO LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to correct this inequity in the distribution of national tax collections by introducing poverty-sensitive factors in the IRA formula in lieu of population, land area and equal sharing. These factors are population density, poverty incidence and income class of the LGU. If these factors are used to calculate the IRA shares, poorer provinces, cities, municipalities and barangays would be favored over their richer counterparts.
