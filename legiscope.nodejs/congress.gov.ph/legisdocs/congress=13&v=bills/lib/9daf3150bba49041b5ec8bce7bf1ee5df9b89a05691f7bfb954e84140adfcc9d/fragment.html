HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03506
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE PROVINCIAL, CITY AND MUNICIPAL CHIEF EXCUTIVES TO APPOINT THEIR RESPECTIVE LOCAL TREASURER, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill transfers the power to appoint the provincial, city or municipal treasurer from the Secretary of Finance to the provincial governor or city or municipal mayor, respectively.
