House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00224
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE PHILIPPINE THOROUGHBRED HORSERACING AUTHORITY PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Charter Of The Philippine Thoroughbred Horseracing Authority
ABSTRACT : “Charter of the Philippine Thoroughbred Horseracing Authority” Seeks to abolish the existing Philippine Racing Authority repealing Presidential Decree No. 42, thereby promoting and strengthening the thoroughbred horseracing industry by ensuring the professional development of the horseracing personnel, upgrading the breed of the Philippine Thoroughbred horses and ensuring the efficient and fair operation of horseracing through regulatory measures.
