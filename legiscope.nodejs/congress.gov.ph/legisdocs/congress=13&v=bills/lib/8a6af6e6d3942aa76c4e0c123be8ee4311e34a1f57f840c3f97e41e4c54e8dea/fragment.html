House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02567
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE NATIONAL LAND USE POLICY AND PLANNING FRAME WORK AND THE IMPLEMENTING MECHANISM THEREFOR, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE NATIONAL LAND USE ACT OF THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : National Land Use Act of the Philippines
ABSTRACT : "National Land Use Act of the Philippines." The bill seeks to address issues relative to the allocation and utilization of all lands, whether private or government-owned, including minerals and water therein. It proposes to adopt the following land use categories: a) protection land use; b) production land use; c) settlements development; and d) infrastructure development.
