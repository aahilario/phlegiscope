HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00712
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE USE OF THE PROCEEDS FROM GROSS RECEIPTS TAX IMPOSED ON CELLULAR PHONE COMPANIES TO FINANCE THE COMPUTER LITERACY PROGRAMS OF PUBLIC SCHOOLS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill proposes the creation of a special computer literacy education fund from the gross receipts tax imposed on cellular phone companies. The fund is intended to support the computer literacy program in public schools, from the elementary to the tertiary levels.
