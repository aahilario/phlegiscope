House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03264
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING ADDITIONAL POWERS AND AUTHORITY TO THE OMBUDSMAN, THEREBY AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6770, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE OMBUDSMAN ACT OF 1989"
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the Ombudsman to engage the services of private lawyers in the prosecution of some criminal cases and provides the Ombudsman additional funds for its operation.
