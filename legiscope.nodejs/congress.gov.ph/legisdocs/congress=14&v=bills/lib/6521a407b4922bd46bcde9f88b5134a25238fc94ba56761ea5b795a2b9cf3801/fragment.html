HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02300
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING ADDITIONAL INSURANCE BENEFITS TO ALL PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS, PROVIDING FUNDS FOR THE PAYMENT OF ITS PREMIUMS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Public School Teachers Insurance Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill grants public school teachers the following insurance coverage: a) P200,000 death benefits; b) P50,000 burial expenses; and c) P100,000 reimbursement of actual medical expenses.
