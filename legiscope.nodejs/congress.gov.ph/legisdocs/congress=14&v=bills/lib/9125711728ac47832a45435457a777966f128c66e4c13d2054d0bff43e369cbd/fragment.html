House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02977
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING MECHANISMS FOR THE REDUCTION OF THE DISTRIBUTION OF MERCURY-ADDED PRODUCTS AND ITS PROPER DISPOSAL THEREOF IN ORDER TO REDUCE THE INTRODUCTION OF MERCURY INTO THE ENVIRONMENT
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes the following, among others: a) phase-out of mercury-added products; b) control of mercury release; and c) mercury waste management.
