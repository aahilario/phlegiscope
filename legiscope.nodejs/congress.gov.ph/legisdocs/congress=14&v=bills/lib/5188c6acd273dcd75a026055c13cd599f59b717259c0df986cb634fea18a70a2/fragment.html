HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03467
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 42 OF BATAS PAMBANSA BILANG 129, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE JUDICIARY REORGANIZATION ACT OF 1981, EXEMPTING AFFECTED MEMBERS OF THE JUDICIARY AS MAY HEREIN BE QUALIFIED FROM THE CONTINUOUS SERVICE REQUIREMENT
ABSTRACT : The bill provides that justices and judges whose "courtesy resignations" were accepted during the 1987 judiciary revamp and who were thereafter reinstated shall be considered to have rendered a continuous service.
