HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02578
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A STRONGER AND ACCOUNTABLE INVOLVEMENT OF SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN IN LOCAL AFFAIRS AND NATION-BUILDING, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The measure aims to give the sangguniang kabataan (SK) fiscal autonomy and independence in its operations as to disbursements and encashment of their income and expenses. It redefines and rationalizes the duties and responsibilities of SK members vis-a-vis their own constituents in the youth sector.
