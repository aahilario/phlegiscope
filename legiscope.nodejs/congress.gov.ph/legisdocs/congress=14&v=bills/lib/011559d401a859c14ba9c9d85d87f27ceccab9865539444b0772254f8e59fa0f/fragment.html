House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00606
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING COASTAL PROVINCES, COASTAL CITIES AND FIRST CLASS COASTAL MUNICIPALITIES TO HAVE A MARINE RESERVE FOR AQUATIC RESOURCES AND FISHERIES OFFICER TO SUPERVISE THE SAME AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : "Marine Reserve Law"
ABSTRACT : "Marine Reserve Law". The measure requires all coastal cities and first class coastal municipalities to establish a marine reserve for aquatic resources. It also requires the appointment of a fisheries officer in all first class coastal municipalities and in coastal cities and provinces to manage the marine reserve.
