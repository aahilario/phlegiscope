House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00684
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING THE CRIME OF CHILD PORNOGRAPHY, IMPOSING PENALTIES THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Anti-Child Pornography Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The Anti-Child Pornography Act of 2007". The bill defines child pornography or child pornography materials to include visual depiction, audio representation, written text or material that advocates explicit sexual activity with a child.
