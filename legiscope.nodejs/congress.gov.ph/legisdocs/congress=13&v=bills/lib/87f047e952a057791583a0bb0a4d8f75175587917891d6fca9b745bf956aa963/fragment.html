House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03547
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CHAPTER VI SECTION 87 ON PROVISIONS AND PENALTIES OF RA NO. 8550 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE PHILIPPINE FISHERIES CODE OF 1998 AND PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A FISHERIES MANAGEMENT FUND
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes the Fisheries Management Fund to finance the rehabilitation or restoration of fish habitats affected by acts committed in violation of RA 8550 and other related fisheries laws.
