House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03819
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CREDIT INFORMATION BUREAU SYSTEM, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The Credit Information Bureau System Act
ABSTRACT : "The Credit Information Bureau System Act." The bill provides for the creation of a Credit Information Bureau, with the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas as the majority shareholder, that will have the power to collect and disseminate credit-related information and maintain the credit history and track record of borrowers.
