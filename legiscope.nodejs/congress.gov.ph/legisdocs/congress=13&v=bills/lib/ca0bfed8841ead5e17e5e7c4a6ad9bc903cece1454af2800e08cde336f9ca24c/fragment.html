House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04542
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE PRACTICE OF GEOLOGY AND REPEALING FOR THIS PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOUR THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED NINE (R.A. NO. 4209), OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE GEOLOGY PROFESSION LAW OF THE PHILIPPINES"
SHORT TITLE : "Geology Profession Law of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Geology Profession Law of 2005". The bill prescribes the scope of the practice of the geology profession, the examination of candidates to the profession, and the registration and licensure of successful candidates. It also provides for the creation of the Board of Geology, its composition, powers and duties, compensation and term of office.
