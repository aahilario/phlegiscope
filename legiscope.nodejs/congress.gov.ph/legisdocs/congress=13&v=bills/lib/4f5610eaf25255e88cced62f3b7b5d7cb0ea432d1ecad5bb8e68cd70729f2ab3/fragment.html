House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01225
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ENHANCE FARM PRODUCTIVITY BY PROVIDING STRATEGIC FARM IRRIGATION SUPPORTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Farm Productivity Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Farm Productivity Act of 2004." This bill provides for a strategic provision of irrigation farm support with the view of increasing farm productivity. It grants incentives for the promotion of cost-effective irrigation technologies.
