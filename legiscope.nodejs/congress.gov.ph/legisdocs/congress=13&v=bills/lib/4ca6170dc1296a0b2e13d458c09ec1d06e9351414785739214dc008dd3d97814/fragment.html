House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03446
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN AND REFORM THE SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN, AMENDING FOR THAT PURPOSE CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Sangguniang Kabataan Reform Law of 2004
ABSTRACT : Sangguniang Kabataan Reform Law of 2004." The bill seeks to bring back the minimum age requirement of SK officers to 18 years old. It also addresses the conflicts between the SK and the barangay council over SK funds.
