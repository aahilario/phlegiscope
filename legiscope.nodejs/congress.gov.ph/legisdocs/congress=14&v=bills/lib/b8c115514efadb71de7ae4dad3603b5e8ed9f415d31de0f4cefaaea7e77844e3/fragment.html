FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING ALL FILIPINO EMPLOYEES AND WORKERS NATIONWIDE RECEIVING LESS THAN FIFTEEN THOUSAND PESOS (P15,000) MONTHLY WAGE AN EXEMPTION FROM INCOME AND WITHHOLDING TAXES
SHORT TITLE : "Income and Withholding Tax Exemption of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Income and Withholding Tax Exemption of 2007". The bill exempts from income and withholding taxes employees and workers earning less than P15,000 monthly.
