House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01070
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE EDUCATION AND LICENSURE OF PHYSICIANS AND THE PRACTICE OF MEDICINE IN THE PHILIPPINES, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 2382, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Revised Philippine Medical Act
ABSTRACT : "Revised Philippine Medical Act." The bill seeks to standardize, upgrade and regulate medical courses including internship training. It provides for the supervision, control and regulation of the practice of medicine, the examination for registration and licensure of physicians, the integration of the medical profession under one national organization and the promotion of a continuing professional education program.
