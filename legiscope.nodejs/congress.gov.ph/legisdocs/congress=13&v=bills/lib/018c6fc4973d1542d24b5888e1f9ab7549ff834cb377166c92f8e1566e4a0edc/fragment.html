HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01457
FULL TITLE : AN ACT SETTING ASIDE TWENTY-FIVE PERCENT (25%) OF THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS' CALAMITY FUNDS FOR PREPARATORY ACTIVITIES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to use the Fund for future calamities to buy medicines, develop relocation centers, construct infrastructure and disseminate information to prevent death and damage to property.
