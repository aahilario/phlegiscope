HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03204
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING FISCAL AUTONOMY TO THE ENERGY REGULATORY COMMISSION, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE ELECTRIC POWER INDUSTRY REFORM ACT OF 2001
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the Energy Regulatory Commission (ERC) to draw its operational expenses from a special regulatory fee which shall be collected from electric transmission and distribution utilities and industries directly connected with the National Power Corporation. The special regulatory fee shall be equivalent to one centavo per kilowatt-hour sold.
