House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02830
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING MEASURES TO ENHANCE THE ACCEPTABILITY OF AGRICULTURAL LANDS AS SECURITY FOR LOANS FROM BANKS AND OTHER FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS THEREBY PROMOTING ACCESS TO RURAL CREDIT AND PROVIDING MECHANISMS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Enhanced Collateral Value of Farm Lands Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Enhanced Collateral Value of Farm Lands Act of 2004." The bill directs all lender banks and other financial institutions to accept agricultural lands as collateral for loans. It also limits the use of the loan proceeds exclusively for agricultural activity such as agricultural production, land development and post harvest facilities.
