House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00251
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATORILY CREATING THE POSITION OF THE MUNICIPAL GENERAL SERVICES OFFICER, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE BOOK III, TITLE TWO, CHAPTER 2, SECTION 443 (a) OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the mandatory creation of the position of Municipal General Services Officer to guarantee the uniformity of general services management of the local government units in the provincial, city or municipal levels.
