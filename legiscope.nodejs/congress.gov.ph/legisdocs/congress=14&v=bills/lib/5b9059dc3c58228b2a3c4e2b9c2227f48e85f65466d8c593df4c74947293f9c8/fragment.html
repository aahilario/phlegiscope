HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00367
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ABOLISHING THE AGE REQUIREMENT FOR APPLICANTS TAKING THE BOARD EXAMINATION FOR SOCIAL WORKERS, PROVIDING FOR THE CONTINUING SOCIAL WORK EDUCATION AND UPGRADING THE SUNDRY PROVISIONS RELATIVE TO THE PRACTICE OF SOCIAL WORK
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to give young social work graduates the opportunity to take the board examination for social workers immediately upon graduation and make them economically and socially productive.
