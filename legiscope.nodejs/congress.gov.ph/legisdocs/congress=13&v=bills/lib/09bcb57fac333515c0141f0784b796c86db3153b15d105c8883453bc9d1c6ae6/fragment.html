House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00653
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING AS A CRIME THE ACT OF DRIVING ANY MOTOR VEHICLE WHILE UNDER THE INFLUENCE OF ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES AND/OR PROHIBITED DRUGS AND PROVIDING GRADUATED PENALTIES THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Drunk Driving Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Drunk Driving Act of 2004." Declares it unlawful for any driver to operate a motor vehicle if he has a blood alcohol concentration level of 6% and/or is under the influence of a prohibited drug.
