HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03496
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING ILLEGAL LOGGING, AS DEFINED IN EXISTING LAWS, AS HEINOUS CRIME AND IMPOSING DEATH PENALTY THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The measure declares illegal logging a heinous crime, imposes the penalty of reclusion perpetua to death and a fine of not less than P500,000 but not more than P1.0 million.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SUHARTO "TENG" T. MANGUDADATU
DATE FILED : 2004-12-14
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
