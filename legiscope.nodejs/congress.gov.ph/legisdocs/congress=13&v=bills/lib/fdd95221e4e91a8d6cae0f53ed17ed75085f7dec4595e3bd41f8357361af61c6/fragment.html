HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00446
FULL TITLE : CARPARK REGULATION LAW OF 2004
ABSTRACT : The non-availability of ample and safe parking spaces has become a perennial problem for motorists and car owners in practically all government agencies and commercial establishments. This bill seeks to regulate the operation of carparks to assure the public of free and easy access to parking spaces. It will also guarantee that vehicles will be well-protected and free from any attempt of theft and other premeditated damages.
