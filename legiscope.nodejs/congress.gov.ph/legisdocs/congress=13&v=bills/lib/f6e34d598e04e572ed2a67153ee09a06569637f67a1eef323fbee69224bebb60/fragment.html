House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03207
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 4368 ENTITLED "AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A NATIONAL HISTORICAL COMMISSION, TO DEFINE ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATIONS OF FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to return to the local government of Dapitan City the control, supervision and management of the Rizal Shrine Park, Rizal Relief Map of Mindanao and other similar parks in Dapitan City.
