HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01925
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING THE OPEN ELECTRONIC LEARNING SCHOOL SYSTEM IN THE PHILIPPINES, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Open ELearning School System Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Open ELearning School System Act of 2007". The bill advances the use of alternative means of instruction such as print, radio, television, and other multi-media learning and teaching technologies that allow students to study on their own without having to regularly attend classes in the conventional classroom set-up.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : LIM, RENO G.
DATE FILED : 2007-08-08
