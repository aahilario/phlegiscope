HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02801
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991 IN ORDER TO: (A) AUTHORIZE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS TO GRANT TAX EXEMPTION PRIVILEGES FOR PRIVATE SECTOR INFRASTRUCTURE AND DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS INVOLVING ROADS AND RAILWAYS THAT WILL BE TRANSFERRED TO THE GOVERNMENT AND (B) EXEMPT SAID PROJECTS FROM REAL PROPERTY TAXES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes local government units, should they wish to do so, to grant tax incentives for build-operate-transfer projects involving roads and railways. It also proposes to exempt such projects from real property taxes.
