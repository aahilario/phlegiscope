House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02452
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING REFORMS IN THE REAL PROPERTY VALUATION AND ASSESSMENT PRACTICES IN THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Real Property Valuation Reform Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes uniform and internationally-accepted standards for the valuation of real property. It also adopts the market value as the single real property valuation base for the assessment of real property related taxes.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : VINZONS-CHATO, LIWAYWAY
