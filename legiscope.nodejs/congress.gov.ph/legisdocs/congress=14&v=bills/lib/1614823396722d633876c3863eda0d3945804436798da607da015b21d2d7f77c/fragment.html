House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01877
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A PHILIPPINE IMMIGRATION AND NATURALIZATION SYSTEM
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine Immigration and Naturalization Act"
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Immigration and Naturalization Act". The bill clearly defines the functions of each department under the Commission on Immigration and Naturalization to enable it to efficiently and effectively serve the public and its clients, avoid delays as well as eradicate red tape.
