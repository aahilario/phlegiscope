House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03253
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STREAMLINE THE ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT ASSESSMENT (EIA) SYSTEM, AMENDING P.D. 1586, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Environmental Impact Assessment Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Environmental Impact Assessment Act of 2004." The bill reinforces the objectives of the Environmental Impact Statement System and proposes measures to make the process more responsive and, in so doing, provide a major venue for multi-sector participation.
