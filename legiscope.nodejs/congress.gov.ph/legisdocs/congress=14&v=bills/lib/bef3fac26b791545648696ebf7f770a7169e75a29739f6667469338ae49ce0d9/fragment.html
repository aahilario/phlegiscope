House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02829
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REVISING THE BUILD-OPERATE-AND-TRANSFER (BOT) LAW, CREATING FOR THIS PURPOSE THE PHILIPPINE BOT AUTHORITY, AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Build-Operate-and-Transfer (BOT) Law of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill places the implementation of public-private partnerships in infrastructure or development projects under the proposed Build-Operate-and-Transfer Authority (BOTA) to ensure the timely completion and commissioning of projects.
