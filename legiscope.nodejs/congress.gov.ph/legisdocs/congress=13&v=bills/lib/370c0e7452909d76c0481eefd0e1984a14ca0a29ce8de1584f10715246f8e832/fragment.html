HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01336
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CREDIT ASSISTANCE PROGRAM FOR OVERSEAS WORKERS
ABSTRACT : The bill extends to an overseas Filipino worker, with a valid contract certified by the POEA, a PhP50,000 loan from the OWWA to defray the living expenses of his family during the first few months of his absence and his expenses incurred during the pre-employment period.
