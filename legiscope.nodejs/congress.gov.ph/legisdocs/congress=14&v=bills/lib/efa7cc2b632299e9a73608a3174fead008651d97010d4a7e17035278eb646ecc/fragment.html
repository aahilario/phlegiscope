HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02282
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE PENAL CODE COMMISSION TO REVIEW AND CODIFY ALL PHILIPPINE PENAL LAWS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Penal Code Commission Act of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : The bill creates the Penal Code Commission that shall be tasked to codify all our penal laws in a systematic and well-arranged form to make them easily accessible to everyone.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CUENCO, ANTONIO V.
