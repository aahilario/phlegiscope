HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01285
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING RETIREMENT BENEFITS TO ALL BARANGAY OFFICIALS AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 393 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE 1991'
ABSTRACT : the bill grants the punong barangay, the sanguniaang bayan members, the barangay treasurers and the barangay secretary retirement benefits from the GSIS provided they have rendred at least nine (9) years of cumulative government service.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CHIPECO, JUSTIN MARC SB.

SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
