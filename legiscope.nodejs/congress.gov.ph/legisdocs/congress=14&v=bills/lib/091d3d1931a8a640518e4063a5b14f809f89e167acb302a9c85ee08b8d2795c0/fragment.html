House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01731
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CREDIT INFORMATION SYSTEM, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Credit Information System Act"
ABSTRACT : "Credit Information System Act". The measure proposes the establishment of the Credit Infomation Corporation (CIC) and the Credit Information System. The CIC shall have the power to collect and disseminate credit-related information.
