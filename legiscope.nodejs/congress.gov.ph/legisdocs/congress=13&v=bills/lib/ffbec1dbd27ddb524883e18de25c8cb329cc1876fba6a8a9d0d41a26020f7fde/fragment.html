House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02807
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 117 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE, AS AMENDED, DELETING THE QUARTERLY MINIMUM GROSS SALES OF LAND TRANSPORTATION, PROVIDING FOR A PERIODIC REVIEW OF THE GROSS SALES OF LAND TRANSPORTATION AND SETTING THE EFFECTIVE RATE THEREOF AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Deleting the Quarterly Minimum Gross Sales of Land Transportation, Providing for a Periodic Review of Its Gross Sales and Setting Its Effective Rate
ABSTRACT : The bill empowers the Secretary of Finance to set the gross sales of land transportation based on the prevailing boundary rates imposed by public utility operators. The Department of Finance shall determine the appropriate sales once every two years.
