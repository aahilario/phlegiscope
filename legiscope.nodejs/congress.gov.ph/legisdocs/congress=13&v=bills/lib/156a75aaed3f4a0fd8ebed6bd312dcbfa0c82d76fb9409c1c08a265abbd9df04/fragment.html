HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02036
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING VARIOUS FORMS OF UNFAIR PRACTICES AS ACTS OF ECONOMIC SABOTAGE, PRESCRIBING STIFFER PENALTIES THEREFOR, GRANTING INCENTIVES AND REWARDS FOR ASSISTANCE OF WITNESSES AND INFORMANTS LEADING TO PROSECUTION THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Economic Sabotage Act
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Economic Sabotage Act." The bill declares smuggling and technical smuggling as acts of economic sabotage and imposes penalties ranging from prision correccional to reclusion perpetua depending on the amount of duties, taxes and other charges avoided.
