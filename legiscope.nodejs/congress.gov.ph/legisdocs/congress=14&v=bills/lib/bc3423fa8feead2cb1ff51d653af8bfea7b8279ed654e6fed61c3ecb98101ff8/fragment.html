HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00835
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE POSSESSION OF (TEN)10 GRAMS OF METAMPHETAMINE HYDROCHLORIDE BY LIFE IMPRISONMENT TO DEATH, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE 'COMPREHENSIVE DANGEROUS DRUGS ACT OF 2002', AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill penalizes the mere possession of ten (10) grams of methamphetamine hydrochloride by life imprisonment to death and a fine ranging from P500,000 to P10 million.
