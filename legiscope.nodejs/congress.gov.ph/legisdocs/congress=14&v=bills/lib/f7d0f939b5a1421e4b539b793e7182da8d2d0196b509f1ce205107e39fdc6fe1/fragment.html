House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03430
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO INSTITUTIONALIZE THE BASIC SERVICE OF INTEGRATED SOLID WASTE COLLECTION AND DISPOSAL IN ALL LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE THE "LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991," REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Integrated Solid Waste Collection and Disposal Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to integrate solid waste collection and disposal as a basic service and to empower the local government sanggunians to provide for this basic service.
