House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03190
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING CYBER SEX, PROSTITUTION, AND PORNOGRAPHY IN THE PHILIPPINES, PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR ITS VIOLATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Cyber Sex Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to impose the penalty of imprisonment for twenty (20) to thirty (30) years and a fine of not less than P500,000 but not more than P1 million on any publisher, financier, manager, producer or promoter of a cyber sex operation.
