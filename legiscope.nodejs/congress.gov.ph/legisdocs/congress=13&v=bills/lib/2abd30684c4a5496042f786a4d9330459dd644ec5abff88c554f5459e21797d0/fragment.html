HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02087
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING CIRCUIT CRIMINAL COURTS TO TRY AND DECIDE CASES OF HEINOUS CRIMES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : To unclog the court dockets, the bill provides for the creation of a circuit criminal court in each of the judicial district of the Regional Trial Courts (RTCs). The circuit criminal court shall have concurrent jurisdiction with the RTC to try and decide on heinous crime cases.
