House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00271
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING CENTERS FOR STREET CHILDREN IN EVERY REGION OF THE PHILIPPINES AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill provides a center for street children including exploited infants and children; medicant children; dependent, abandoned, vagrant and neglected children; and youth offenders in every region in the country.
