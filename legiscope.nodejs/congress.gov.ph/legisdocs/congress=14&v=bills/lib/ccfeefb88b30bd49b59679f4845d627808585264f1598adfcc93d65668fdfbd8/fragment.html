HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02544
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9239, AN ACT REGULATING OPTICAL MEDIA, REORGANIZING FOR THIS PURPOSE THE VIDEOGRAM REGULATORY BOARD, SO AS TO REDUCE THE PENALTY IMPOSED FOR CERTAIN ACT PROVIDED FOR THEREIN
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes to reduce the penalties to be imposed on vendors and hawkers selling not more 200 copies of pirated CDs, VCD and tapes at the time of apprehension.
