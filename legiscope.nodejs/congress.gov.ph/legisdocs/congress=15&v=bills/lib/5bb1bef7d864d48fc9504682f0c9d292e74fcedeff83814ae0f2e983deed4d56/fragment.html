HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00054
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING COMPENSATION TO VICTIMS OF HUMAN RIGHTS VIOLATIONS DURING THE MARCOS REGIME, DOCUMENTATION OF SAID VIOLATIONS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Compensation Act To Victims Of Human Rights Violations
ABSTRACT : “Compensation Act to Victims of Human Rights Violations.” Institutionalizes formal and legal processes of giving compensation to victims of human rights during the Marcos regime, the funds of which shall be sourced from the estate of the Marcoses declared as ill-gotten wealth by the government.
