HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02456
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXTENDING FREE PHILHEALTH BENEFITS TO ACTIVE CAFGU MEMBERS AND TO SURVIVING SPOUSE/BENEFICIARIES OF CAFGU MEMBERS WHO WILL DIE IN COMBAT OR IN RELATED CIRCUMSTANCES IN DEFENDING THE SOVEREIGNTY AND PATRIMONY OF THE NATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure grants Philhealth benefits to CAFGU(citizens armed forces geographical units) members in active duty and lifetime Philhealth benefits to the surviving spouse/beneficiaries of those who die in action.
