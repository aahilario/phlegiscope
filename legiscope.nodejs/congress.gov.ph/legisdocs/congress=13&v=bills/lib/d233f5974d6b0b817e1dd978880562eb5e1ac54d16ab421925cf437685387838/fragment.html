HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03747
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING THAT ELECTIVE OFFICIALS RUNNING FOR ANY OFFICE OTHER THAN THE ONE WHICH THEY ARE HOLDING SHALL BE CONSIDERED IPSO FACTO RESIGNED UPON THE FILING OF THEIR CERTIFICATE OF CANDIDACY
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to discourage elective officials from running for positions other than those they are presently holding in a permanent capacity by considering them ipso facto resigned from their respective offices upon the filing of their certificates of candidacy.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : AGARAO, BENJAMIN JR. C.
