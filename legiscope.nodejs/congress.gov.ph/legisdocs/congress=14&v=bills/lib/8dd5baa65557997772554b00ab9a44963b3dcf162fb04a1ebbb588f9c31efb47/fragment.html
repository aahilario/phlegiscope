HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02102
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE BUILD-OPERATE-TRANSFER AUTHORITY, FURTHER AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6957, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7718, ENTITLED 'AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE FINANCING, CONSTRUCTION, OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS BY THE PRIVATE SECTOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES' FOR THIS PURPOSE
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to establish a Build-Operate-Transfer Authority to rationalize program implementation; set a reasonable rate of return for solicited and unsolicited or negotiated projects; and institutionalize a fair, honest and competitive procurement process, among others.
