HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02351
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MERGING THE DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF AGRARIAN REFORM AND DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES THEREBY CREATING DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND AGRARIAN MANAGEMENT DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Environmental and Agrarian Management Act"
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes to merge the three (3) departments into one to be known as Department of Environment and Agrarian Management that will formulate, institutionalize and administer an overall resource utilization strategy.
