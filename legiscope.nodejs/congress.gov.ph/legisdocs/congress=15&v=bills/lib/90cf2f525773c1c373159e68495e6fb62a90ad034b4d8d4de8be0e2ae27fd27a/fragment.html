HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00114
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER STRENGTHENING THE ANTI-SMUGGLING MECHANISM, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1464, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE TARIFF AND CUSTOMS CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Anti-smuggling Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Anti-Smuggling Act of 2010.” Seeks to promote good governance, public accountability and transparency in the operations and workings of the Bureau of Customs and its personnel, strengthen policies that will eradicate or minimize all types of smuggling, improve the processes in the Bureau, and institute vital reforms to reinvigorate the trust of the public to the revenue-collecting government institution.
