HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01842
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CODIFICATION OF CRIMINAL LAWS IN THE PHILIPPINES, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to establish a Criminal Code Commission that will be charged to review, revise and update all existing penal laws to make them more coherent and responsive to the times and compile them in a single code.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : AQUINO, BENIGNO SIMEON III C.
DATE FILED : 2004-07-28
