FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER STRENGTHENING THE HOME DEVELOPMENT MUTUAL FUND AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Home Development Mutual Fund Law of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Home Development Mutual Fund Law of 2004". The bill strengthens the powers of the Home Development Mutual Fund of Pag-IBIG Fund so that it can meet the challenge of financing the government's national shelter program.
