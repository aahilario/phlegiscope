House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02309
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING THE INCOME REQUIREMENTS FOR THE CREATION OF A PROVINCE AND THE DECLARATION OF HIGHLY-URBANIZED STATUS IN THE CASE OF COMPONENT CITIES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991"
ABSTRACT : The bill sets the income requirement for the creation of: a) province at not less than P250 million; and b) highly urbanized city at not less than P250 million for the last two (2) consecutive years.
