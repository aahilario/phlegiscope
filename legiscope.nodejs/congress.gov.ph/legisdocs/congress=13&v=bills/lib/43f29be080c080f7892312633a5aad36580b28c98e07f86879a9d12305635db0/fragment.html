HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01119
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOWING THE IMPORTATION OF LIVE ANIMALS, MEAT AND MEAT PRODUCTS ONLY FROM ZONES DECLARED BY THE OFFICE INTERNATIONAL DES EPIZOOTIES (OIE) AS 'FMD (FOOT AND MOUTH DISEASE) FREE ZONES WHERE VACCINATION IS NOT PRACTICED'
SHORT TITLE : Foot and Mouth Disease Eradication Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Foot and Mouth Disease Eradication Act of 2004." Imposes a fine of P100,000 or imprisonment of six months to any person importing live animals with foot and mouth disease.
