House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01411
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE RETIREMENT BENEFITS OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 75 OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTY- NINE HUNDRED AND SEVENTY-FIVE ENTITLED, 'AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE UNDER A REORGANIZED DEPARTMENT OF INTERIOR AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT,' AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : PNP Retirement Benefits Act
ABSTRACT : "PNP Retirement Benefits Act." The bill provides that the retirement pay of the members of the PNP shall be equal to one month base pay and longevity pay of the grade next higher than the permanent grade last held, for every year of service, payable in one lump sum or a monthly retirement pay equal to 50% of the base pay.
