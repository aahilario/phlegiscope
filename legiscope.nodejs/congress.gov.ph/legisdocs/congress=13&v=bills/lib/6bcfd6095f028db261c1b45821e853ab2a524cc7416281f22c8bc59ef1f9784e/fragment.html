HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03509
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ENABLING RETIREES WHO HAVE NOT RECEIVED PENSIONS EITHER BY CHOICE OR OPERATION OF LAW TO RECEIVE PENSIONS AT AGE SEVENTY YEARS OR OVER, AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Special Retirement Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Special Retirement Act of 2005." The bill grants all retirees who have not received old age pension or annuity either by choice or by operation of law, an old-age pension equivalent to 50% of their average monthly compensation upon reaching the age of 70 years.
