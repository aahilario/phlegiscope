HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03809
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS TO PLANT FIVE TREES AS A PRE-CONDITION FOR THEIR GRADUATION FROM HIGH SCHOOL
ABSTRACT : The measure requires public and private high school students to plant 5 trees a year as a prerequisite for graduation from high school. It also requires grades 5, 6 and 7 pupils to plant/grow seedlings as part of their shop work in preparation for their graduation.
