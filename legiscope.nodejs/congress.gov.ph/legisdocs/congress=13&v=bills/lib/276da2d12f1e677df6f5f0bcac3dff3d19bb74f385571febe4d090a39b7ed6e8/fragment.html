House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03774
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE BABALUS MINDORENSIS, COMMONLY KNOWN AS THE TAMARAW, AS THE NATIONAL MAMMAL AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to declare the tamarraw, which can only be found in the Philippines, as the national mammal.
