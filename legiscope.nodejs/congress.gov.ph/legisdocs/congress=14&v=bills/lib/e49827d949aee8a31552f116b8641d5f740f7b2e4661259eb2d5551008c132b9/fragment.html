House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02755
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CODE OF PROFESSIONAL STANDARDS FOR THE PRACTICE OF POLITICAL PUBLIC RELATIONS
SHORT TITLE : "Political Public Relations Act"
ABSTRACT : The measure establishes the professional standards for the practice of political public relations to ensure that practitioners conduct themselves professionally with truth, accuracy, fairness and responsibility to the public.
