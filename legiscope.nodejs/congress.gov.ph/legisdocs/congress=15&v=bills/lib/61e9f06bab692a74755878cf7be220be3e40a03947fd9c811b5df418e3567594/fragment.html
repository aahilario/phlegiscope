House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00216
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE IMPOSITION OF SERVICE CHARGE BY HOTELS, RESTAURANTS AND OTHER SIMILAR ESTABLISHMENTS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Service Charge Regulation Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Service Charge Regulation Act of 2010.” Requires food and beverage outlets to conspicuously display signs that they are to impose service charges, and under what circumstances can service charges be imposed and at what amount.
