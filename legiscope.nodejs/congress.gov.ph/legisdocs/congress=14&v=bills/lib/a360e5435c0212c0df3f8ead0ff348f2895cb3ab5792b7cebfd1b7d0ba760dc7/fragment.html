FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING MONTHLY SALARY, HEALTH AND RETIREMENT BENEFITS FOR BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS, BARANGAY NUTRITION SCHOLARS AND BARANGAY SERVICE POINT OFFICERS AND APPROPRIATING FUND THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : "Salary and Benefits for Barangay Workers Act"
ABSTRACT : "Salary and Benefits for Barangay Workers Act". The measure seeks to grant barangay health workers, barangay nutrition scholars and barangay service point officers monthly compensation and benefits.
