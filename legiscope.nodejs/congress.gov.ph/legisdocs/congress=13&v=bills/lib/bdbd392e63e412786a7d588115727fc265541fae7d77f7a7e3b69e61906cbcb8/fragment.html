House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01815
FULL TITLE : THE MAGNA CARTA FOR PRIVATE SCHOOL TEACHERS
SHORT TITLE : Magna Carta for Private School Teachers
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta for Private School Teachers." This bill seeks to give security of tenure to private school teachers, right against discrimination, academic freedom, benefits related to health, study leave and the right to self-organization.
