House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00632
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A WORLD TRADE ORGANIZATION (WTO) AFFAIRS STRATEGY OFFICE (WTO-ASO) UNDER THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill creates the World Trade Organization Affairs Strategy Office (WTO-ASO) that shall be tasked to formulate policies concerning WTO matters and affairs and its rules on engagement.
