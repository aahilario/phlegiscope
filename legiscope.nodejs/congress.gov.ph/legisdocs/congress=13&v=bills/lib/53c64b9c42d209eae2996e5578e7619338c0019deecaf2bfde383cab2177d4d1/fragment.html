House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03400
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING BATAS PAMBANSA 22, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE BOUNCING CHECK LAW, CONSIDERING GOOD FAITH AS A DEFENSE IN CASES UNDER BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 22
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to prevent the use of the Bouncing Check Law to enforce payment or to victimize the drawee of a check by unscrupulous creditors.
