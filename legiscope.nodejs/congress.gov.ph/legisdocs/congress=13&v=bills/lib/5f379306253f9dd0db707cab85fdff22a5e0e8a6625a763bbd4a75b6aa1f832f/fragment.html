FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE REPRESENTATION OF COOPERATIVES IN THE BOARD 0F DIRECTORS OF THE LAND BANK OF THE PHILIPPINES, AMENDING THEREFOR REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3844, AS AMENDED
