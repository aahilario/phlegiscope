HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03302
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 7941 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "PARTY-LIST SYSTEM ACT"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase from three to six the maximum number of seats that a party-list organization shall be entitled to. It bans the five major political parties, based on party representation in the previous national elections, from participating in the party-list system.
