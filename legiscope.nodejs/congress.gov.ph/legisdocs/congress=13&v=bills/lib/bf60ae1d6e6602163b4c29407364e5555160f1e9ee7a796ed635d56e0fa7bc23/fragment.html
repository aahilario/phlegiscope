HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00524
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE MONTHLY BENEFITS OF BARANGAY OFFICIALS AND PROVIDING ALLOWANCES TO BARANGAY TANODS, BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS AND BARANGAY SOCIAL WORKERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE, THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for an increase in the monthly allowance granted to the punong barangay from PhP1,000.00 to PhP3,000.00 and to the sangguniang barangay members, barangay treasurer, and barangay secretary from PhP600.00 to PhP1,800.00. It also grants a PhP 1,500 monthly allowance to the lupong tagapamayapa, barangay tanods, barangay health workers and barangay social workers.
