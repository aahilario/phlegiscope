House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02274
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC NO. 8545, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "EXPANDED GOVERNMENT ASSISTANCE TO STUDENTS AND TEACHERS IN PRIVATE EDUCATION ACT" PROVIDING FOR AN EXPANDED VOUCHER OR COUPON SYSTEM IN SECONDARY AND TERTIARY EDUCATION, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill sets the tuition fee ceiling for participating private schools in the GASTPE (Government Assistance to Students and Teachers in Private Education) at P6,000 per semester.
