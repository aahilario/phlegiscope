House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05428
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE SUPREME COURT TO TRANSFER BRANCHES OF FIRST AND SECOND LEVEL COURTS FROM ONE JUDICIAL REGION TO ANOTHER, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 129, AS AMENDED BY R.A. 7154
SHORT TITLE : Authorizing the Supreme Court to Transfer Branches of First and Second Level Courts From One Judicial Region to Another
ABSTRACT : The measure aims to give the Supreme Court sufficient latitude in the transfer of court branches, taking into account the condition of court dockets, vacancies, geographical location of salas and availability of transportation for lawyers and litigants.
