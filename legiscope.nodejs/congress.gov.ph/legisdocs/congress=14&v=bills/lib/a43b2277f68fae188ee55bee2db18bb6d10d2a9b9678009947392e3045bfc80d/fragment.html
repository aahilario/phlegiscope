HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02903
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REQUIRE HOSPITALS TO ESTABLISH AND IMPLEMENT SECURITY PROCEDURES TO REDUCE THE LIKELIHOOD OF INFANT PATIENT ABDUCTION AND BABY SWITCHING, INCLUDING PROCEDURES FOR IDENTIFYING ALL INFANT PATIENTS IN THE HOSPITAL IN A MANNER THAT ENSURES IT WILL BE EVIDENT IF INFANTS ARE MISSING FROM THE HOSPITAL
SHORT TITLE : "Infant Protection and Baby Switching Prevention Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill directs all hospitals to establish security procedures that would prevent infant patient abduction and baby switching.
