HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03836
FULL TITLE : AN ACT OFFICIALLY DECLARING THE NATIONAL SYMBOLS OF THE PHILIPPINES, DISSEMINATING ITS CULTURAL AND HISTORICAL SIGNIFICANCE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill declares the following as the national symbols of the country: a) Jose P. Rizal, national hero; b) barong tagalog and baro't saya, national attire; c) carabao, national animal; d) bahay kubo, national house; e) bangus, national fish; f) anahaw, national leaf; g) sipa, national sport; h) tinikling, national dance; and i) mango, national fruit.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : MILES M. ROCES
DATE FILED : 2005-03-01
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
ACTIONS TAKEN BY THE COMMITTEE ON RULES
