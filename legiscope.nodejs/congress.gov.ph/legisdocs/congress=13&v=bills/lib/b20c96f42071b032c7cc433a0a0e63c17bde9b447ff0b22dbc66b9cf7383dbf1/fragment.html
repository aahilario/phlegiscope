FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE PRIVATIZATION OF THE PHILIPPINE AMUSEMENT AND GAMING CORPORATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes the privatization of the Philippine Amusement and Gaming Corporation (PAGCOR). The proposed privatization is expected to generate about P80 billion in additional funds for the government.
