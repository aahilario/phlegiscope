HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01525
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REWARD INFORMANTS WHO HELP IDENTIFY, LOCATE AND RECOVER ANY ILL-GOTTEN WEALTH OF THE LATE PRESIDENT FERDINAND E. MARCOS, AND HIS WIDOW, IMELDA R. MARCOS AND OTHER DEFENDANTS OR RESPONDENTS IN ILL-GOTTEN WEALTH CASES WHEREVER LOCATED AND BY WHOMSOEVER HELD AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill offers the following rewards to informants who help in the recovery of any Marcos ill-gotten wealth: 1) For individuals - a) 10% of the amount recovered if less than US$100 million; b) 7% of amount recovered if US$100 million but less than US$500 million; c) 5% of amount recovered if US$500 million and more; and 2) For juridical informants, 15% of amount recovered.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CAGAS, DOUGLAS RA.

SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
