HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02424
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ENSURING THE FISCAL INDEPENDENCE OF THE JUDICIAL DEPARTMENT, PROVIDING FOR A DEFINITE SHARE OF SAID DEPARTMENT IN THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUES AND THE AUTOMATIC AND PROMPT RELEASE THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the annual budget of the judiciary to be not lower than 2.5% of the national internal revenue collections of the 3rd fiscal year preceding the current fiscal year.
