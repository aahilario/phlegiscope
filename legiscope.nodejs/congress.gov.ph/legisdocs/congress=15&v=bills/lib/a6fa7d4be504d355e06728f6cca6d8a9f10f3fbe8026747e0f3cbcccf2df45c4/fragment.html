HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00031
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL GOVERNMENT OFFICES TO ENSURE THE RELEASE OF THE RETIREMENT BENEFITS OF ITS EMPLOYEES WITHIN FIFTEEN (15) DAYS FROM RETIREMENT
ABSTRACT : Directs the heads of all government instrumentalities to ensure the release of the retirement benefits and gratuities of an employee within fifteen (15) days from the date of retirement.
