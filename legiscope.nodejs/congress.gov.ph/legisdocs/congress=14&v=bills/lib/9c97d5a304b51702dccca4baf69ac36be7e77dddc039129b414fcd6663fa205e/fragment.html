HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02896
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER EDUCATION TO DISCLOSE PARTICIPATION AND PROGRAM SUPPORT EXPENDITURES IN COLLEGE ATHLETIC PROGRAMS
SHORT TITLE : "Equity in Athletes Disclosure Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill requires colleges and university to inform prospective enrollees of their athletic programs and the financial support given to participants of these programs. The colleges and universities are also required to submit an annual report on the programs to the Secretary of Education.
