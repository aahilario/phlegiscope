House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01610
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING OLD-AGE PENSION BENEFIT TO RETIREES UNDER REPUBLIC ACT NO. 1616, AS AMENDED, FOR AGED 70 YEARS OR OVER, AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Special Retirement Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Special Retirement Act of 2007". The bill grants government employees who retire under RA 1616, as amended, and who are seventy (70) years old or above, old-age pension benefit equivalent to 50% of their average monthly compensation.
