House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01720
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SME) BUSINESS CENTERS (SMEBC) IN ALL REGIONAL AND PROVINCIAL CENTERS IN THE COUNTRY
SHORT TITLE : SME Business Center (SMEBC) Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "SME Business Center (SMEBC) Act of 2004." The proposed SMEBC shall house all the government agencies involved in setting up a business from registration up to the last requirement. It shall also provide enterprenuers with all the basic information in establishing and running a business.
