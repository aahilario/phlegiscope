HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02897
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING THE BUREAU OF ALTERNATIVE LEARNING SYSTEM (BALS)
SHORT TITLE : Alternative Learning System Act
ABSTRACT : "Alternative Learning System Act." The bill renames the Bureau of Nonformal Education to the Bureau of Alternative Learning System (BALS). It mandates the transfer to the BALS of the administration of all distance education programs.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : REYES, EDMUNDO JR. O.
DATE FILED : 2004-09-14
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
