HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03665
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ADJUSTING THE RATE OF SUBSIDIARY IMPRISONMENT FROM ONE DAY FOR EACH P8.00 TO ONE DAY FOR AN AMOUNT EQUIVALENT TO THE DAILY MINIMUM WAGE OF A LABORER IN THE NATIONAL CAPITAL REGION AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLE THIRTY-NINE OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to adjust the subsidiary imprisonment which a convict with no property has to suffer from "one day for each P8.00" to "one day for an amount equivalent to the daily minimum wage of a laborer in the National Capital Region," which he can not pay.
