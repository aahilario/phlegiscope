House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00797
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MAGNA CARTA OF WOMEN
SHORT TITLE : "The Magna Carta for Women"
ABSTRACT : "The Magna Carta for Women". The bill seeks to address the wide-ranging needs and sectoral concerns of women, specially those in the marginalized sector. It concretizes women's participation and empowerment in all aspects of the family, community and society.
