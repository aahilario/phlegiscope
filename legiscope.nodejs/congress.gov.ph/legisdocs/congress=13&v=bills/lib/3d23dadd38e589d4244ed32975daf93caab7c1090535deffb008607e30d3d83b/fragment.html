HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01932
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING THE IMPORTATION OF RICE, PROHIBITING THE RE-MILLING, REPACKING AND MIXING OF IMPORTED AND LOCAL RICE VARIETIES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Provides for the "legal boundaries in the entry, storage, sale and distribution of imported rice in the country." Prohibits the importation of rice milled two months before purchase and requires only legitimate millers to possess rice polishers.
