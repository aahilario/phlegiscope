House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03855
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING THE COMPENSATION OF THE OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES OF THE COMMISSION ON AUDIT AND AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1445 ENTITLED THE GOVERNMENT AUDITING CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES WITH THE VIEW TO FURTHER ENSURING AND STRENGTHENING THE COMMISSION'S INDEPENDENCE
SHORT TITLE : COA Compensation Rationalization Act
ABSTRACT : "COA Compensation Rationalization Act." The measure exempts the compensation of the officials and employees of the Commission on Audit from RA 6758 (Compensation and Classification Act of 1989) and grants the Commission the authority to determine the same.
