HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02710
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE BENEFITS OF BARANGAY OFFICIALS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 393 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, CREATING A NATIONAL AUGMENTATION FUND, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill increases the monthly honoraria granted to barangay officials from P1,000 to P2,000 for the punong barangay, P1,800 for the sangguniang barangay members and P1,500 for the barangay treasurer, secretary and tanods.
