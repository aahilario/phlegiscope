House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01286
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL PROGRAM FOR FILIPINO GIFTED CHILDREN AND SCIENCE-ORIENTED YOUTH, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Gifted Children and Science-Oriented Youth Act
ABSTRACT : "Gifted Children and Science-Oriented Youth Act." This bill enumerates the following objectives: a) identify the gifted Filipino children and science-oriented youth; b) establish a Center for the identification and nurturance of these children; c) provide scholarship grants and other forms of assistance for gifted children; d) provide funds for research and development, curriculum development, training of parents, teachers and other personnel and the necessary infrastructure; e) provide incentives to government institutions and the private sector that participate in the implementation of this Act, and f) establish and maintain linkages with national and international institutions involved in progress on giftedness.
