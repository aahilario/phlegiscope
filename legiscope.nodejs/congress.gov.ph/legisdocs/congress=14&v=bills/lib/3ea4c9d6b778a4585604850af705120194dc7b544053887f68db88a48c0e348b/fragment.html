FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER AMENDING COMMONWEALTH ACT NO. 542, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "AN ACT TO CREATE A CORPORATION TO BE KNOWN AS THE GIRL SCOUTS OF THE PHILIPPINES, AND TO DEFINE ITS POWERS AND PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill grants the Girl Scouts of the Philippines the following tax and duty privileges: a) exemption from income tax; and b) tax and duty exemption on donations from foreign countries.
