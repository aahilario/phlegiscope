House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00371
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR PUBLIC SALE OF SHARES IN THE DISPOSITION OF GOVERNMENT CORPORATIONS UNDER THE PRIVATIZATION PROGRAM
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to avoid the concentration of economic power into the hands of a few investors by privatizing government corporations through the public offering of 60% of their shares of stock.
