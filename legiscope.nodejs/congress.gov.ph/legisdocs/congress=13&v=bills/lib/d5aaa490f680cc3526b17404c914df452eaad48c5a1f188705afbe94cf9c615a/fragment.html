HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02106
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE POSSESSION OR USE OF MOBILE OR CELLULAR TELEPHONE BY CONVICTED PRISONERS AND INDIVIDUALS WHILE INCARCERATED AND PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill bans the possession or use of cellular telephones by convicted prisoners and individuals while they are incarcerated in detention centers; in provincial, city or municipal jails; or in national penal institutions.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CUENCO, ANTONIO V.
DATE FILED : 2004-08-04
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
