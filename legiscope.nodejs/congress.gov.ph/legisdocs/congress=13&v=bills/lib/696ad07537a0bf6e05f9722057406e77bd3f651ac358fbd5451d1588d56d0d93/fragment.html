House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00709
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE OVERSEAS FILIPINO WORKERS (OFW) MEDICAL CENTER DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks the establishment, operation and maintenance of a medical center for overseas Filipino workers (OFWs) and their families. The Center shall extend medical services to OFWs and their families to help prevent, relieve or alleviate their innumerable medical afflictions and maladies. The measure provides for the government to contribute the land, building, equipment and facilities for the Center.
