House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03532
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO DEFINE ENFORCEABLE JURIDICAL RELATIONS ON OCCUPANCY ON OR POSSESSION OF PRIVATE LANDS IN THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : Defining Enforceable Juridical Relations on Occupancy On or Possession Of Private Lands in the Philippines
ABSTRACT : The bill defines the framework of enforceable juridical relations between an occupant of a private land and the owner of said land. Outside of this framework, the occupant is a deemed to be occupying the land by the mere tolerance or magnanimity of the owner.
