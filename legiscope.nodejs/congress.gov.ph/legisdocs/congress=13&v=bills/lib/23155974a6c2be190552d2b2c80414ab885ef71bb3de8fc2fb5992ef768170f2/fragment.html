HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00562
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE RESEARCH OF LOCAL HISTORIES AND INTEGRATING SUCH FINDINGS INTO THE ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY CURRICULUM OF EVERY MUNICIPALITY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Local Histories Research and Curriculum Integration Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Local Histories Research and Curriculum Integration Act of 2004". This bill seeks to remedy our youth's obvious lack of information in the context of political consciousness because of so much emphasis in the educational curriculum on national events yet we hardly know the history of our own village or municipality.
