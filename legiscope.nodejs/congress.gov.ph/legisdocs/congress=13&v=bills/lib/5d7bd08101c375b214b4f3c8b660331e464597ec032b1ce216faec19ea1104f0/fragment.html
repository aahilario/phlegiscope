HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00165
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE ALLOCATION FOR THE SPECIAL EDUCATION FUND, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : Quality education has taken second place to quantity in our educational system. Elementary and secondary schools, particularly public schools, are operating below standards due inadequate facilities and lack of well-trained educators and classrooms. This bill seeks to double the Special Education Fund in order to give schools the necessary facilities needed by children and to enable teachers to undergo skills upgrading through seminars and training.
