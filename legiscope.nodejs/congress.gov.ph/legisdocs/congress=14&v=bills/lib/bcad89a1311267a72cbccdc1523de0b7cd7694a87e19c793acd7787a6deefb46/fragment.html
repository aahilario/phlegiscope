FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A PROVIDENT PERSONAL SAVINGS PLAN, KNOWN AS THE PERSONAL EQUITY AND RETIREMENT ACCOUNT ("PERA")
SHORT TITLE : "Personal Equity and Retirement Account (PERA) Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to provide people with a savings plan for their retirement years. It encourages savings mobilization, promotes capital market development and generates investments.
