House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02581
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROMOTE AND REGULATE THE CABLE TELEVISION SERVICES IN THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Cable TV Act of 2001
ABSTRACT : "Cable TV Act of 2001." The bill seeks to lay down the legal basis for the entry of capital, acquisition of state-of-the-art CATV technology, transmission, programming and production. It rationalizes the regulation of the cable television industry by clearly delineating the powers of the regulating body and the rights of the cable television operators.
