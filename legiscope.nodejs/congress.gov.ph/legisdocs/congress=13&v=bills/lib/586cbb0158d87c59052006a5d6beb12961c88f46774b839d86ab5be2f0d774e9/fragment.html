House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05791
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING REGISTERED NURSES WHOSE EDUCATION WAS SUBSIDIZED BY THE GOVERNMENT TO RENDER TWO (2) YEARS OF SERVICE WITHIN THE COUNTRY PRIOR TO DEPARTURE FOR EMPLOYMENT ABROAD
SHORT TITLE : Requiring Registered Nurses Whose Education Was Subsidized by the Government to Render Two (2) Years of Service Within the Country
ABSTRACT : The bill requires all Filipino registered nurses who finished their nursing education in a state university or college or whose education was subsidized by the government to render at least two years of service in the country.
