HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00428
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CHANGING THE MAXIMUM RETIREMENT AGE FROM SIXTY-FIVE TO SEVENTY YEARS FOR BOTH GOVERNMENT AND PRIVATE EMPLOYEES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to change the present maximum retirement age from sixty-five to seventy years for all employees in the government and the private sectors.
