HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03731
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REVERTING BACK THE ORIGINAL INFORMER'S REWARD OF FIFTEEN PERCENT (15%) INSTEAD OF THE CURRENT TEN PERCENT (10%), AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 282 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8424, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE TAX REFORM ACT OF 1997, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the informer's reward from 10% to 15% of the revenues, surcharges or fees recovered and/or fine or penalty imposed and collected.
