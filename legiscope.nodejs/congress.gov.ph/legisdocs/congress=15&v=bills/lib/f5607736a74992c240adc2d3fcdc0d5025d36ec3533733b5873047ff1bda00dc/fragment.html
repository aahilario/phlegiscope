House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00086
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPLEMENTING THE RIGHT OF ACCESS TO INFORMATION ON MATTERS OF PUBLIC CONCERN GUARANTEED UNDER SECTION TWENTY-EIGHT, ARTICLE II AND SECTION SEVEN, ARTICLE III OF THE 1987 CONSTITUTION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Freedom Of Information Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Freedom of Information Act of 2010.” Recognizes the right of the people to information on matters of public concern to include access to official records, documents and papers pertaining to official acts, transactions, or decisions, as well as to government research data used as basis for policy development, subject to the limitations as may be provided by law.
