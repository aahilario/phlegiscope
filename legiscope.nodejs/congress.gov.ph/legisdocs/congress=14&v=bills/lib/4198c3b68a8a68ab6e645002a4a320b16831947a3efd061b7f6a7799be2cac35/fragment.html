HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01921
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO CLASSIFY THE FAKING OR FABRICATING, NEGOTIATING, TRANSACTING, CONVEYING OR MORTGAGING OF FAKE OR ILLEGALLY FABRICATED TORRENS CERTIFICATES OF TITLE AS A HEINOUS OFFENSE
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the imposition of the penalty of fifteen (15) to thirty (30) years of imprisonment on individuals found guilty of faking or fabricating Torrens Certificates of Title or of using, knowingly, fake and spurious documents.
