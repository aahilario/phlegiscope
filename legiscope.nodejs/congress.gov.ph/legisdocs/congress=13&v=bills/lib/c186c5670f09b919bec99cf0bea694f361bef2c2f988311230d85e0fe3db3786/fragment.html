HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00638
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 7309, ENTITLED "AN ACT CREATING A BOARD OF CLAIMS UNDER THE DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE FOR VICTIMS OF UNJUST IMPRISONMENT OR DETENTION AND VICTIMS OF VIOLENT CRIMES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES"
ABSTRACT : The bill increases the payment to victims of unjust imprisonment from P1,000 to P7,500 monthly while the maximum claim for expenses incurred is raised from P10,000 to P75,000.
