HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01621
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE STRICT LABELLING OF FOOD AND BEVERAGE PRODUCTS BY MANUFACTURING AND DISTRIBUTION COMPANIES
SHORT TITLE : Consumer Information and Protection Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Consumer Information and Protection Act of 2004." The measure requires all manufacturers and distributors of food and beverages in the country to provide the following information on the labels of their respective products: ingredient list, additives, genetic modification, minimum durability indication, net content, nutritional labeling, and information on country of origin, manufacturers, packers and local sole agent.
