House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00417
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9280, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CUSTOMS BROKERS ACT OF 2007, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits corporations from engaging in the business of customs brokerage or from hiring the services of an in-house customs broker for purposes of accreditation by the Bureau of Customs and facilitation of customs brokerage activities.
