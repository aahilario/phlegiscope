House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02909
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A PRE-TRIAL RELEASE FOR THE ACCUSED, CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE THE PRE-TRIAL RELEASE OFFICE, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Pre-Trial Release Act of 2002
ABSTRACT : "Pre-Trial Release Act of 2002." The bill provides for the pre-trial release (PTR) program wherein an accused under detention may be released without bail. The measure provides the conditions of release under the PTR program.
