HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01489
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE APPOINTMENT OF A REPRESENTATIVE FOR THE ELDERLY IN EVERY SANGGUNIANG BARANGAY, SANGGUNIANG BAYAN, SANGGUNIANG PANLUNSOD AND SANGGUNIANG PANLALAWIGAN
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to harness the talents, wisdom and experience of senior citizens by appointing an elderly as a representative in every sangguniang barangay, bayan, panglungsod and panlalawigan.
