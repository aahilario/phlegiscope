HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00805
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 5(a) OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3779, ENTITLED 'AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR THE REGULATION OF THE ORGANIZATION AND OPERATIONS OF SAVINGS AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS' AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill directs loans and savings associations to grant loans to qualified borrowers of an amount not exceeding Fifty Thousand Pesos (P50,000.00) or the amount deposited by the borrower plus his four (4) month's salary or regular income whichever is higher.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ABAYON, HARLIN CAST
