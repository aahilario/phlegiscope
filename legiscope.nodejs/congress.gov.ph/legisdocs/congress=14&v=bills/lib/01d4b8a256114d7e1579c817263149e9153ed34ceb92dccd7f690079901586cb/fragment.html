HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03375
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A PROGRAM REQUIRING GENERAL HOSPITALS WITH NEONATAL INTENSIVE CARE UNITS TO OFFER VACCINES AGAINST INFLUENZA VIRUS TO CERTAIN PERSONS
SHORT TITLE : "Mandatory Influenza Vaccine Program of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill requires all general hospitals with a neonatal instensive care unit (NICU) to offer vaccination against the influenza virus to caregivers of newborn babies being treated in the NICU.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SANTIAGO, NARCISO III D.
