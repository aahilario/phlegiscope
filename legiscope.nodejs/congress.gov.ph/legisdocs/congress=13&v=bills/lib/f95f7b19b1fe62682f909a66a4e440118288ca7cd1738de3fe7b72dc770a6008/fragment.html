HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04315
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE SPECIAL ENVIRONMENTAL AND REHABILITATION FUND (SERF) THROUGH REVENUES DERIVED FRON LARGE-SCALE MINING OPERATIONS OF MINING COMPANIES AND REQUIRING FOR SUCH PURPOSE THE ONE-TIME RETENTION OF TWENTY PERCENT (20%) OF OPERATING CAPITAL PRIOR TO COMMENCEMENT OF MINING OPERATIONS AND THE ANNUAL RETENTION OF THIRTY PERCENT (30%) OF PROFITS FROM SAID COMPANIES IN LOCAL BANKS OR FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS OWNED AND MANAGED BY THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES, PROVIDING THE NECESSARY MECHANISMS FOR ITS IMPLEMENTATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : SERF Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "SERF Act of 2005". The bill calls for the establishment of a Special Environmentand Rehabilitation Fund (SERF) to be used exclusively for the following: a) the rehabilitation of areas affected by large-scale mining operations, b) the building of houses and the restoration of parks and other structures affected by mining operations, c) the implementation of educational and health programs, d) support to wildlife conservation, and e) creation of scholarship grants for children of families in areas affected by mining operations.
