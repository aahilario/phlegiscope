HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02904
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING AND REGULATING TUITION AND OTHER SCHOOL FEES IN ALL HIGHER TECHNICAL-VOCATIONAL AND BASIC EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS, CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE A NATIONAL TUITION FEE RATIONALIZATION COUNCIL
SHORT TITLE : Tuition Fee Rationalization Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Tuition Fee Rationalization Act of 2004." The bill creates the National Tuition Fee Rationalization Council which shall serve as the highest policy-making body on matters pertaining to tuition fees and other increases in school fees.
