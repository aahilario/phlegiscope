House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00364
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE LOCAL SCHOOL BOARDS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS 98, 99 AND 100 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991" AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the membership in the Local School Board to include a representative from an accredited non-government or people's organization involved in non-formal education and training programs.
