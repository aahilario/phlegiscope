HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02368
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE PHILIPPINE COAST GUARD AND VESTING IN IT THE PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITY OF ENFORCING MARITIME SAFETY RULES AND REGULATIONS AS WELL AS ANY OTHER MATTER RELATED TO THE PERFORMANCE OF SAFETY OF LIFE AND PROPERTY FUNCTION WITHIN PHILIPPINE TERRITORIAL WATERS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill makes the Philippine Coast Guard primarily responsbile for the enforcement and implementation of maritime safety rules and regulations promulgated by the Maritime Industry Authority. The PCG shall be responsible for overssing the safety of life and property of all seafarers at sea.
