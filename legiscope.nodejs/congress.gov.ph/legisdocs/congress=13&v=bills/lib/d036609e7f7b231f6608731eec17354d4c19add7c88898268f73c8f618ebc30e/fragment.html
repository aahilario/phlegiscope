HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02707
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR MAXIMUM PERFORMANCE IN REVENUE COLLECTION THROUGH THE GRANTING OF SPECIAL INCENTIVES AND REWARDS FOR EXEMPLARY SERVICE AND THROUGH LATERAL ATTRITION IN REVENUE-GENERATING AGENCIES OF GOVERNMENT AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure aims to grant incentives and rewards to collection officials/officers/employees who render outstanding service while upholding the highest ethical standards. It allows expeditious action against non-performing officials/officers/empoloyees by removing them from their positions if they do not meet set standards.
