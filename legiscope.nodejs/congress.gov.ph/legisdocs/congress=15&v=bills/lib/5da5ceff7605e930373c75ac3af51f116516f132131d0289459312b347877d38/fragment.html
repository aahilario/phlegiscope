House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00164
REPUBLIC ACT NO. RA10535
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO SET THE PHILIPPINE STANDARD TIME (PST) IN ALL OFFICIAL SOURCES THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY AND TO PROVIDE FUNDS FOR THE INSTALLATION, OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF SYNCHRONIZED TIME DEVICES TO BE DISPLAYED IN KEY PUBLIC PLACES AND TO DECLARE THE FIRST WEEK OF EVERY YEAR AS NATIONAL TIME CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK
SHORT TITLE : The Philippine Standard Time ( P S T) Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “The Philippine Standard Time (PST) Act of 2010.” Sets the Philippine Standard Time (PST) in all official sources throughout the country by providing funds for the installation, operation and maintenance of synchronized time devices that are to be displayed in the key public places.
