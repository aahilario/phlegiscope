FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A GENERAL AUTHORITY FOR THE CONTRACTING OF INTERNATIONAL TRADE AGREEMENTS AND THE GENERAL LIMITATIONS THERETO
SHORT TITLE : "International Trade Agreements Act of 2006"
