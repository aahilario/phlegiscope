HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00081
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR GOVERNMENT REGULATION AND SELF-REGULATION WITHIN THE COOPERATIVE SECTOR OF COOPERATIVES ENGAGED IN FINANCIAL SERVICES, AND DEFINING THE RESPONSIBILITIES, POWERS AND FUNCTIONS OF COOPERATIVES AND COOPERATIVE FEDERATIONS ENGAGED IN SAVINGS, CREDIT AND OTHER FINANCIAL SERVICES, AMENDING THEREFOR REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 6938, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE COOPERATIVE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AND REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 6939, CREATING THE COOPERATIVE DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY
SHORT TITLE : "Financial Service Cooperative Act"
ABSTRACT : "Financial Service Cooperative Act". The bill provides for the organization, registration, responsibilities, powers and functions of savings and credit cooperatives and financial service cooperatives and federations.
