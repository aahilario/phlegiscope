HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00269
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING INCLUSION OF ENTREPRENEURSHIP AS A SEPARATE SUBJECT IN THE HIGH SCHOOL CURRICULUM
ABSTRACT : Mandates the inclusion of entrepreneurship as a separate subject at the high school level in all public and private educational institutions nationwide, such appropriate skills and values training at the early age is crucial in achieving a paradigm shift.
