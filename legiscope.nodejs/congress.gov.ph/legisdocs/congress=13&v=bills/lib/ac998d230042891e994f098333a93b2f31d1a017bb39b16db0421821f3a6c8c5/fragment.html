House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02561
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A SYSTEM OF PAYING THE ARREARS OF PENSION TO VETERANS, PENALIZING ANY FRAUD COMMITTED IN RELATION TO THIS ACT
ABSTRACT : The bill entitles all veterans, whose disability is rated from 91% to 100%, to receive a Certificate of Indebtedness amounting to P30,000. The Certificate shall be issued in lieu of the monthly pension of P1,700 plus P500 for the spouse and each unmarried minor child.
