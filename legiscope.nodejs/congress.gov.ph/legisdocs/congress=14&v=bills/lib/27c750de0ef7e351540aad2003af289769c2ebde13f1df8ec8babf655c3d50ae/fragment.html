HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03426
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE MANUFACTURE AND SALE OF IMITATION FIREARMS
SHORT TITLE : "Anti Look-Alike and Imitation Firearms Act"
ABSTRACT : The measure penalizes the manufacture or production of toy look-alike or imitation firearms unless such imitation firearms are approved by the Department of Trade and Industry.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.
DATE FILED : 2008-01-28
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
