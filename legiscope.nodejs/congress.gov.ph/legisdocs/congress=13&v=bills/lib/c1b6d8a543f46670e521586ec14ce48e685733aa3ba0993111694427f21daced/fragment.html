House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02265
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A RECYCLING CENTER IN EVERY BARANGAY NATIONWIDE, APPOPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill establishes a recycling center in every barangay, aimed at: a) promoting and providing for a healthful environment; b) reducing waste generated at sources; c) enhancing the capability of the barangay in environment and solid waste management; and d) providing for income from garbage as a revenue source for barangays.
