HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01115
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REPEALING THE AUTOMATIC APPROPRIATION FOR DEBT SERVICE BY AMENDING SECTION 31 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1177 AND SECTION 26, CHAPTER 4, BOOK VI OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 292, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "ADMINISTRATIVE CODE OF 1987" WHICH REITERATES EN TOTO SECTION 31 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1177
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to free the Filipino people from the burdens of onerous and unreasonable debts by repealing the automatic appropriation provisions under PD 1117 and EO 292.
