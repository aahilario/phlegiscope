HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03445
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXTENDING THE UTILIZATION PERIOD OF THE AGRICULTURAL COMPETITIVENESS ENHANCEMENT FUND, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE PERTINENT PROVISION OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8178, ENTITLED "AN ACT REPLACING QUANTITATIVE IMPORT RESTRICTIONS OF AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTS, EXCEPT RICE, WITH TARIFFS, CREATING THE AGRICULTURAL COMPETITIVENESS ENHANCEMENT FUND" AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill extends the lifespan of the agricultural competitiveness enhancement fund (ACEF) from nine to FIFTEEN years to relieve farmers, fishermen and agricultural workers of the need to secure other sources of funds for their competitiveness enhancement activities.
