House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00206
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REGULATE THE RATIONAL EXPLORATION, DEVELOPMENT AND UTILIZATION OF MINERAL RESOURCES, AND TO ENSURE THE EQUITABLE SHARING OF BENEFITS FOR THE STATE, INDIGENOUS PEOPLES AND LOCAL COMMUNITIES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Mineral Resources Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Philippine Mineral Resources Act of 2010.” Aims to govern the ownership, management and governance of ore minerals onshore, as well as quarry resources, sand and gravel, guano, and gemstones, and the conservation, exploration, development, utilization, processing and transportation thereof.
