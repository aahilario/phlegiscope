House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03207
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AMEND THE COOPERATIVE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES OTHERWISE KNOWN AS R.A. 6938 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to make the registration of electric cooperatives with the Cooperative Development Authority less cumbersone. It also seeks to clarify the effects of and issues attendant to such registration.
