HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00517
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING A THREE(3)-DAY NON-CUMMULATIVE LEAVE WITH PAY ANNUALLY TO EMPLOYED PARENTS TO ATTEND TO THE SPECIAL NEEDS OF THEIR CHILDREN
SHORT TITLE : Parental Leave Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Parental Leave Act of 2004". The bill institutionalizes the grant of an annual three-day parental leave of absence with pay to working parents or guardians to enable them to attend to special circumstances involving their children or ward in any school activity.
