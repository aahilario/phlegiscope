House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00675
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE AUTOMATIC PROMOTION OF GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES UPON THEIR COMPULSORY OR OPTIONAL RETIREMENT, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill upgrades the retirement pay by promoting retirees one rank higher upon retiring from government service. The adjusted salary grade shall be the basis for the computation of the benefits.
