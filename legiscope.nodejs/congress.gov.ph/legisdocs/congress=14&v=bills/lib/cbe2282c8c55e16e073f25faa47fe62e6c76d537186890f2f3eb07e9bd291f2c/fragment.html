HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02333
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ARTICLES OF MILITARY JUSTICE IN THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES REPEALING FOR THAT PURPOSE COMMONWEALTH ACT NUMBERED FOUR HUNDRED EIGHT, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE ARTICLES OF WAR, AS AMENDED, OTHER SPECIAL LAWS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the adoption of a military justice system in the Armed Forces of the Philippines to be administered, applied and interpreted by a Civilian Appellate Collegiate Court.
