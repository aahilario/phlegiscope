House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03163
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ACCORD ADDITIONAL INSURANCE BENEFITS TO ALL PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS, PROVIDING FUNDS FOR THE PAYMENT OF THE PREMIUMS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Additional Teachers' Insurance Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Additional Teachers' Insurance Act of 2004." The bill grants public school teachers the following term insurance coverage: a) P100,000 death benefits; b) P50,000 burial expenses; and c) P100,000 reimbursement of actual medical expenses.
