House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01600
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL REVENUE AUTHORITY, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Revenue Authority Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "National Revenue Authority Act of 2004." The bill seeks to restructure the Bureau of Internal Revenue into a semi-autonomous revenue authority with corporate-like features. It provides for capitalization, powers, functions and organizational structure of the Authority.
