House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00520
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING PROSTITUTION, DEFINING THE PERSONS LIABLE THEREFOR, PROVIDING MEASURES AND SUPPORT SERVICES FOR ITS VICTIMS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The Anti-Prostitution Act
ABSTRACT : "The Anti-Prostitution Act". The bill seeks to end the continuing sexual exploitation of women and children and all forms of gender-based violence; recognizing prostitution as a human rights violation which calls for a coordinated and sustained response from all agencies of the government. It imposes stiff penalties for prostitution and provides social services and programs to help victims of prostitution.
