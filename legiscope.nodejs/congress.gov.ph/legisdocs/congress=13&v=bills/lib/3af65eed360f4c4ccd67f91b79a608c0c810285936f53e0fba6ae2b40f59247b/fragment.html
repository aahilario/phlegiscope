HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02811
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING ADDITIONAL INCENTIVES TO SELLERS OF REAL ESTATE PROPERTIES, THE SUBJECT MATTER OF THE COMMUNITY MORTGAGE PROGRAM OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT AND FOR OTHER PURPOSE
ABSTRACT : The bill exempts from the payment of documentary stamp tax and trasfer tax all real estate properties acquired under the Community Mortgage Program by any of the government housing agencies or local government units.
