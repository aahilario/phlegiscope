House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00718
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING COMPULSORY COVERAGE TO ACCREDITED BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS IN THE NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE PROGRAM, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7883 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to grant compulsory enrollment in the National Health Insurance Program (PhilHealth) to barangay health workers and their dependents to enable them to receive and avail of an adequate package of necessary health services.
