HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04520
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL OFFICIAL TIME AND SYNCHRONIZING THE TIME SOURCE OF ALL ENTITIES AND INDIVIDUALS
SHORT TITLE : "Time Synchronization Act".
ABSTRACT : "Time Synchronization Act". The bill seeks to establish a national time and to synchronize the time source of all entities and individuals in order to guide them of the exact time accordingly.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : Dr. REY B. AQUINO
DATE FILED : 2005-07-13
