HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02848
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING MANUFACTURING ESTABLISHMENTS OWNING NOT MORE THAN ONE (1) INDUSTRIAL MACHINE OR EQUIPMENT ACTUALLY USED IN PRODUCTION PROCESSES AND EMPLOYING NOT MORE THAN TWO (2) TECHNICAL PERSONNEL EXEMPTION FROM INCOME TAXES FOR THE FIRST TWO YEARS OF OPERATION
ABSTRACT : To encourage and assist small-scale entrepreneurs, the bill grants excise tax exemption to manufacturers owning not more than one production machine and employing not more than two technical personnel.
