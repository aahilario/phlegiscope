House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00609
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING FIVE HUNDRED PESOS (P500.00) MONTHLY ALLOWANCE TO ALL BARANGAY TANODS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : Provides incentives to barangay tanods by giving them a PhP500.00 monthly allowance. A maximum of 10 barangay tanods in every barangay shall be covered by the incentive.
