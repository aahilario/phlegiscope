FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING ALL GOVERNMENT-OWNED COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES TO ESTABLISH A COLLEGE FOR THE DIFFERENTLY-ABLED AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Tertiary Special Education Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill directs state universities and colleges to establish a college for the differently-abled to be known as "College for the Differently-Abled."
