FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL GAMING COMMISSION, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes the National Gaming Commission that shall have regulatory power over all games of chance and other numbers games within the country.
