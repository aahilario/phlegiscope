HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02680
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE PRACTICE OF REAL ESTATE SERVICE IN THE PHILIPPINES, CREATING A REAL ESTATE COMMISSION AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Real Estate License Act of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to consolidate and integrate the laws, rules and regulations relative to real-estate practice. It creates the Real Estate Commission with appropriate duties, powers and functions that will allow it to regulate, supervise and oversee the industry.
