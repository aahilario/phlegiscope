House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03227
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING THE NATIONAL SERVICE TRAINING PROGRAM (NSTP) ACT OF 2001 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9163" AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to equally divide the Reserved Officers' Training Corps hours of tertiary level students between military training and participation in different education-related activities such as tutorials, classroom rehabilitation and the like.
