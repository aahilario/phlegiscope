House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03948
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE PROTECTION OF WHISTLEBLOWERS IN GOVERNMENT AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : The Whistleblower Protection Act of the Philippines
ABSTRACT : "The Whistleblower Protection Act of the Philippines". The measure seeks to create a positive whistleblowing culture in government by providing whistleblowers protection from reprisal and harassment and by identfying the remedies available to whistleblowers.
