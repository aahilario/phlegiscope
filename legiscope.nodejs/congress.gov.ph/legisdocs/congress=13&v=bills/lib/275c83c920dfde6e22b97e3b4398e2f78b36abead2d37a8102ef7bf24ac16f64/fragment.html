House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02588
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING GOVERNMENT HOSPITALS TO RETAIN AND UTILIZE THEIR INCOME FOR MAINTENANCE AND OTHER OPERATING EXPENSES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes all government hospitals, including medical centers and specialty hospitals, to retain and use their income for maintenance and other operating expenses, including capital outlay.
