House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04376
FULL TITLE : AN ACT APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF TWO BILLION PESOS (P2,000,000,000.00) AS A SUPPLEMENTAL BUDGET FOR TO FUND THE ACQUISITION OF NEW HELICOPTERS AND REFURBISH EXISITNG ONES IN NEED OF REPAIR IN THE PHILIPPINE AIR FORCE (PAF) OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES (AFP)
ABSTRACT : The bill appropriates P2.0 billion for the procurement of new helicopters and the rehabilitation of existing units in the Philippine Air Force.
