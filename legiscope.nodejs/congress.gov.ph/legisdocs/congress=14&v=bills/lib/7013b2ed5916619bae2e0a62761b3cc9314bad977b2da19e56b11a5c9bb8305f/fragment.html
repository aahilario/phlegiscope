House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01612
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING INCENTIVES FOR BARANGAY OFFICIALS INCLUDING BARANGAY TANODS AND MEMBERS OF THE LUPONG TAGAPAMAYAPA AND OTHER BARANGAY EMPLOYEE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : "Barangay Employees' Incentives Act"
ABSTRACT : "Barangay Employees' Incentives Act". The bill exempts the salries, wages, remuneration andother emoluments of barangay officials from income taxation. It also grants them health and retirement benefits.
