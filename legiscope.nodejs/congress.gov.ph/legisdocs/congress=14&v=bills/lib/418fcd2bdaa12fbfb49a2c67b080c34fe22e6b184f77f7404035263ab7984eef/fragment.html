HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00819
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE DEPARTMENT OF OVERSEAS FILIPINO WORKERS (DOFW), DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the abolition of the Philippine Overseas Employment Administration (POEA) and the Overseas Workers Welfare Administration (OWWA)and for the transfer of their functions to the Department of Overseas Filipino Workers.
