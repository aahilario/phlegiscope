House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02528
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING PROTECTION AGAINST COMPUTER FRAUD, ABUSES AND OTHER CYBER-RELATED FRAUDULENT ACTIVITIES, PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Computer Fraud and Abuses Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Computer Fraud and Abuses Act of 2004." The bill defines computer fraud and offenses covered by the term "computer-related fraudulent activities." It provides penalties for violations of the proposed act and authorizes the National Security Council to investigate computer-related crimes vis-a-vis their effects on national security.
