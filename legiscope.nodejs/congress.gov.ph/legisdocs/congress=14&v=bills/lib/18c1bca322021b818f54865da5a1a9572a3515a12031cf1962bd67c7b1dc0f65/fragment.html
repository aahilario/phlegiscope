HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00481
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING GRATUITY PAY TO ALL DULY ELECTED BARANGAY CAPTAINS AND BARANGAY COUNCIL MEMBERS, WHO HAVE COMPLETELY SERVED THREE CONSECUTIVE TERMS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Barangay Council Gratuity Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Barangay Council Gratuity Act of 2007". The bill grants a gratuity pay of P200,000 to barangay captains and P150,000 to council members who have served three consecutive terms.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : PUENTEVELLA, MONICO O.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-02
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
