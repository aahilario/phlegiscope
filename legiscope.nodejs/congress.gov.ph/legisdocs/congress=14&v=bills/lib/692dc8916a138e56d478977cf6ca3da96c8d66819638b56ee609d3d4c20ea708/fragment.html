HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00290
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RESTORING THE TAX EXEMPTION PRIVILEGES OF LOCAL WATER DISTRICTS UNDER SECTION 46 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 198, AS AMENDED AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to: a) restore the tax exemption privileges enjoyed by water districts under PD 193, b) exempt water districts from RA 7656 which required government-owned corporation to remit 50% of their annual net income to the government, and c) condone all unpaid taxes, duties and required remittances to the government.
