HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01595
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A TWO-LEVEL NATIONAL POLICE, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE SECTION TWENTY-FIVE (25) OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTY-NINE HUNDRED AND SEVENTY-FIVE (R.A. 6975), OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "ACT ESTABLISHING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE UNDER A REORGANIZED DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to endow the Philippine National Police (PNP) with a dual character, i.e., it calls upon the PNP to perform nation-wide police operations and local police functions.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : BIAZON, ROZZANO RUFINO B.
