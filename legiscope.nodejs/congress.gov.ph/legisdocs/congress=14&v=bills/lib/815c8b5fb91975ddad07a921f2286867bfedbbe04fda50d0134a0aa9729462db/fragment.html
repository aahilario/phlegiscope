HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01249
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE COMPENSATION AND/OR INDEMNITY OF UNLAWFULLY DETAINED PERSONS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to compensate or indemnify a person who has been wrongfully detained at a rate of P60,000 for every year of detention provided that if the detention is less than one year, the rate of compensation shall be computed at P5,000 per month or a proportion thereof.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : BICHARA, AL FRANCIS D.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-24
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
