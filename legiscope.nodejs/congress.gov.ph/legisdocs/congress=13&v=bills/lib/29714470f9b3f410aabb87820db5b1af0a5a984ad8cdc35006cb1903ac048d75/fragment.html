House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04607
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE REHABILITATION AND IMPROVEMENT OF THE PHILIPPINE CHILDRENS MEDICAL CENTER (PCMC), APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "PCMC Rehabilitation and Improvement Act"
ABSTRACT : "PCMC Rehabilitation and Improvement Act". The measure provides for the modernization of the equipment and facilities of the emergency room, intensive care unit, delivery room, operating room and other major areas of the Philippine Children's Medical Center.
