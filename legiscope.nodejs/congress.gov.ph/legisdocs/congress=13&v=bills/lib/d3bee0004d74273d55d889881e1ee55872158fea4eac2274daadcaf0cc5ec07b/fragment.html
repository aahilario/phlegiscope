House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02440
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MEMBERSHIP OF SCHOOL PRINCIPALS IN MUNICIPAL SCHOOL BOARDS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 98 (b)(3) OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to designate the school principal to sit in the School Board in lieu of the district supervisor who now simply performs staff functions as mandated under RA 9155.
