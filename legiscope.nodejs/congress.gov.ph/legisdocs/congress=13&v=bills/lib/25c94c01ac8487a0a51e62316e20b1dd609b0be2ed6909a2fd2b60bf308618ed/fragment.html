House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02840
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THE PROVISIONS OF THE 1992 INTERNATIONAL CONVENTION ON CIVIL LIABILITY FOR OIL POLLUTION DAMAGE AND THE 1992 INTERNATIONAL CONVENTION ON THE ESTABLISHMENT OF AN INTERNATIONAL FUND FOR COMPENSATION FOR OIL POLLUTION DAMAGE, PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Oil Pollution Compensation Act
ABSTRACT : "Oil Pollution Compensation Act." The bill holds the owner of a ship liable for any pollution damage caused by his ship as a result of an incident. When an incident involves two or more ships, the owners of all the ships concerned shall be jointly and severally liable for all damages.
