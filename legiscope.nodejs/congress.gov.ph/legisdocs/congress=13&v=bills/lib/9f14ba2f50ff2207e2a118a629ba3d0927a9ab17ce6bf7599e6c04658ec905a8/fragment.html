HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01368
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO EARMARK TWENTY-FIVE (25%) PERCENT OF GOVERNMENT REVENUES ACCRUED FROM TOBACCO TAXES TO FUND TOBACCO CONTROL PROGRAMS AND ACTIVITIES OF THE GOVERNMENT
ABSTRACT : In recent years, several countries have decided to fund tobacco control activities through higher tobacco taxes. In like fashion, the bill provides that 25% of the revenues generated from tobacco taxes be allocated to fund tobacco control activities and broader public health programs.
