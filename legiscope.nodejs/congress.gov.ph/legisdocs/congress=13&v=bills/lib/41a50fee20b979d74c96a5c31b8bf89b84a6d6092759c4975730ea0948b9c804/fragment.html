HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01332
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 7309 ENTITLED 'AN ACT CREATING A BOARD OF CLAIMS UNDER THE DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE FOR VICTIMS OF UNJUST IMPRISONMENT OR DETENTION AND VICTIMS OF VIOLENT CRIMES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES'
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to transfer the Board of Claims from the Department of Justice to the Commission on Human Rights. It further proposes the following amendments to RA 7309: a) expands the categories of claimants; b) increases the compensation ceiling; c) provides for safeguards against false claims.
