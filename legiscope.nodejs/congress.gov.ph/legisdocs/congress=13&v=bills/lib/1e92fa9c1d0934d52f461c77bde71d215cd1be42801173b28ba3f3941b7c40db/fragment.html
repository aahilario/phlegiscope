House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05347
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE USE OF THE PROCEEDS FROM THE DEVELOPMENT, LEASE OR USE, AS THE CASE MAY BE, OF GOVERNMENT PROPERTIES FOR VETERANS' PENSIONS AND BENEFITS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 323
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to allow the national government to pay its obligations to veterans and military retirees by utilizing proceeds of the development, lease and other use of government properties.
