House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00551
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE REGULATORY FRAMEWORK FOR THE LIQUEFIED PETROLEUM GAS INDUSTRY, DEFINING AND PENALIZING CERTAIN PROHIBITED ACTS INVOLVING LPG AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "LPG Industry Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "LPG Industry Act of 2007". The bill seeks to establish stricter guidelines in the sale, requalification, distribution and repair of liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) cylinders and criminalize violations thereof.
