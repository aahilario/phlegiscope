HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02837
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING PUBLIC OFFICERS FROM CLAIMING CREDIT THROUGH SIGNAGE ANNOUNCING A PUBLIC WORKS PROJECT
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Signage of Public Works Act
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Signage of Public Works Act." The bill prohibits the affixing of the name or image of any public official to a signage announcing a proposed or on-going public works project.
