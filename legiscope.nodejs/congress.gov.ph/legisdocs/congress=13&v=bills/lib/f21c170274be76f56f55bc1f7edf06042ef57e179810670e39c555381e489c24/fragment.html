HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00369
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PENALIZE A PARENT OR ANY PERSON IN CHARGE OF A MINOR FOUND TO BE RESPONSIBLE FOR PROMOTING, INDULGING, FORCING OR INSENUATING SUCH MINOR TO GET INVOLVED IN SEX ACTIVITIES IN EXCHANGE OF MONETARY OR ANY MATERIAL CONSIDERATION, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SUB-SECTION 3, ARTICLE 335 OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE
ABSTRACT : According to the Revised Penal Code and to People of the Philippines vs. Perez, rape is committed even if the girl under 12 years old is a prostitute. The bill seeks to hold a parent or any person in charge of a minor, who forces such minor to get involved in sex activities in exchange for monetary or any material consideration, liable for rape and not the person who had sex relation with the minor.
