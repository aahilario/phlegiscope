House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03067
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMOTING BREASTFEEDING BY ESTABLISHING AND MAINTAINING LACTATION STATIONS IN WORKPLACES FOR WORKING MOTHERS PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR ITS VIOLATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Breastfeeding Promotion Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Breastfeeding Promotion Act of 2004." The bill mandates the establishment of lactation stations in workplaces for nursing female employees. The lactation station must include a lavatory for hand washing, adequate refrigeration equipment for storing milk and comfortable chairs.
