House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01389
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING HAZARD PAY TO TEACHERS ASSIGNED AS MEMBERS OF THE BOARD OF ELECTION INSPECTORS AND BOARD OF CANVASSERS IN HAZARDOUS AREAS IN THE COUNTRY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SEVENTY-ONE HUNDRED AND SIXTY-SIX
ABSTRACT : The bill grants a hazard pay of P1,000.00 to teachers assigned as members of the Board of Election Inspectors and Board of Canvassers in hazardous areas during elections.
