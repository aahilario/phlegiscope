House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02948
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MERGING OF THE DEPARTMENT OF FINANCE AND THE DEPARTMENT OF BUDGET AND MANAGEMENT THEREBY CREATING THE DEPARTMENT OF BUDGET AND FINANCE, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Fiscal and Budget Management Reform Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Fiscal and Budget Management Reform Act of 2004." The bill proposes a structural and functional overhaul of the Department of Finance and the Department of Budget and Management by merging the two to be known as the Department of Budget and Finance.
