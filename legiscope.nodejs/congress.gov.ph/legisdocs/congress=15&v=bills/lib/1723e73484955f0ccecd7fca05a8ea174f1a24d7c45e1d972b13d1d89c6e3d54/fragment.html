HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00014
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ACCELERATE THE DEVELOPMENT OF ALL BARANGAYS BY PROVIDING FUNDS FOR MICRO ENTERPRISES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Mega-fund For Progress Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Philippine Mega-Fund for Progress Act of 2010.” Seeks to create an P80 billion Philippine Fund for Progress and allocates P2.0 million to each of the 40,000 barangays in the country for lending to deserving micro enterprises.
