House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00064
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING A NATIONAL LAND USE POLICY, PROVIDING THE IMPLEMENTING MECHANISMS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "National Land Use Act of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : "National Land Use Act of the Philippines". The bill proposes to give top priority to the use of land for food security and environmental sustainability. It provides for the setting aside of a minimum area for agricultural and other uses in each province and municipality.
