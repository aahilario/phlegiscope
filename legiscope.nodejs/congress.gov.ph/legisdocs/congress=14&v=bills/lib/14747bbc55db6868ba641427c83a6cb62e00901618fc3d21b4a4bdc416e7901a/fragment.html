HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02121
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING ALL FORMS OF ADVERTISEMENT OF NON-PRESCRIPTION OR OVER-THE-COUNTER (OTC) MEDICINES, FOOD AND HERBAL SUPPLEMENTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Ad Ban on OTC Medicines, Food and Herbal Supplements"
ABSTRACT : "Ad Ban on OTC Medicines, Food and Herbal Supplements". The bill prohibits all forms of advertisements of over-the-counter medicines, food and herbal supplements in television, radio, billboards and print media to prevent the misuse and abuse of the use of such medicines.
