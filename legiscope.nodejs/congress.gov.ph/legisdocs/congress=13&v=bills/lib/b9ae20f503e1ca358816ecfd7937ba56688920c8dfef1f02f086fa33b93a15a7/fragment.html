HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01760
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE USE OF THE AUTOMATED TELLER MACHINE (ATM) TECHNOLOGY IN THE CONDUCT OF NATIONAL OR LOCAL ELECTORAL EXERCISES, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill calls on the Commission on Elections to customize the automated teller machines (ATM) technology for use in the elections. The system shall involve the use the voter's card containing the personal identification number and automated machine equipped with a biometric identification system to determine the identity of the voter.
