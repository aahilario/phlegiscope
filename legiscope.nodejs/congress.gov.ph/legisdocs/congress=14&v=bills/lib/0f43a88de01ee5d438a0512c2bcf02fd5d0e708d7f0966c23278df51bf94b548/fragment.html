HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02615
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE MANDATORY REGISTRATION OF ALL TYPES OF HEAVY EQUIPMENT, IMPOSING REGISTRATION FEE THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill requires the registration of all types of heavy equipment like bulldozer, road grader, road roller, payloader, dumptruck, water truck, backhoe, transit mixer, one bagger mixer, crane, jack hammer, drop hammer, concrete vibrator, air compressor, TRK tractor trailer, stake truck, rock crusher, pile driver and the like.

DATE FILED : 2007-09-19
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
