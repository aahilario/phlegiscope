HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HR01702
ADOPTED AS RESOLUTION NO. HR00167
FULL TITLE : RESOLUTION STRONGLY URGING THE DEPARTMENT OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION, DEPARTMENT OF LABOR AND EMPLOYMENT, DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE, PHILIPPINE COAST GUARD, MARITIME INDUSTRY AUTHORITY, PHILIPPINE OVERSEAS EMPLOYMENT ADMINISTRATION, OVERSEAS WORKERS WELFARE ADMINISTRATION, AND OTHER RELEVANT GOVERNMENT AGENCIES TO CREATE AN INTER-AGENCY TASK FORCE, WHICH SHALL BE RESPONSIBLE FOR CONDUCTING SEARCH AND RESCUE OPERATIONS OF FILIPINO SEAFARERS DURING SEA MISHAPS AND OTHER MARITIME INCIDENTS OCCURING IN TERRITORIAL WATERS, FOREIGN WATERS AND THE OPEN SEAS; LOOKING INTO CASES OF MISSING SEAFARERS WHILE ON-BOARD THEIR VESSELS; AND ENSURING THAT THE FAMILIES OF SURVIVING, MISSING OR DEAD SEAFARERS WILL RECEIVE APPROPRIATE GOVERNMENT ASSISTANCE AND LEGAL CLAIMS FROM PRIVATE ENTITIES
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : LUSOTAN, MACNELL, MENDOZA, RAYMUND DEMOCRITO C., SAVELLANO, DEOGRACIAS VICTOR "DV" B., TULFO, JOCELYN P., PACQUIAO, ALBERTO D., GAITE, FERDINAND R., PANOTES, MARISOL C., CHATTO, EDGAR M., OUANO-DIZON, EMMARIE "LOLYPOP", TUTOR, KRISTINE ALEXIE B., LARA, JOSEPH "JOJO" L., ELAGO, SARAH JANE I., ACOSTA-ALBA, MARIA LOURDES, NATIVIDAD-NAGAÑO, MARICEL G., MD., KHO, WILTON "TONTON" T., NIETO, JOHN MARVIN "YUL SERVO" C.
