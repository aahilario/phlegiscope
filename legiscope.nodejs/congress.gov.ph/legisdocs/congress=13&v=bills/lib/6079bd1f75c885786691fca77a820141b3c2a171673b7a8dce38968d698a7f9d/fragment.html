HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01367
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO UPGRADE THE POSITION TITLE AND SALARY GRADE OF STENOGRAPHIC REPORTER II (SG 6) AND STENOGRAPHIC REPORTER III (SG 11) TO STENOGRAPHIC REPORTER IV (SG 14), APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Because of the increase in the number of cases being handled by the National Labor Relations Commissions, its stenographers are compelled to perform functions beyond their prescribed duties. To grant them equitable compensation and to correct the apparent misclassification of their positions, the bill proposes to upgrade the status of Stenographers Reporters II and III to Stenographic Reporter IV.
