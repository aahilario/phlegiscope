House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03347
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE OVERSEAS FILIPINO WORKERS PENSION FUND AND APPROPRIATING FUINDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Overseas Filipino Workers Pension Fund Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Overseas Filipino Workers Pension Fund Act of 2004." The bill mandates all registered overseas Filipino contract workers to remit 5% of their gross monthly income to the proposed Fund for five years. It provides for the creation of the Overseas Filipino Workers Pension Fund Board, its composition and powers and functions.
