House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03383
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING CHILD PORNOGRAPHY AND CHILD ABUSE MATERIALS AND PENALIZING ALL FORMS AND MEANS OF COMMISSION
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Child Pornography Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill defines child pornography; provides the duties and responsibilites of internet service providers, the duty of establishments, educational institutions and offices in installing blocking software to prevent access to and transmittal of prohibited materials, among others.
