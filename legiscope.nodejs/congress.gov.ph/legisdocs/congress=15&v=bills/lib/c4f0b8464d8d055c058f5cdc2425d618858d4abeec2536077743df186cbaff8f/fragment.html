House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00217
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE "SENTRO NG BATAS SA WIKA", DEFINING ITS FUNCTIONS AND PROVIDING FOR ITS FINANCING, ADMINISTRATION AND OPERATION, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Sentro Ng Batas Sa Wika
ABSTRACT : “Sentro ng Batas sa Wika.” Aims to produce scholarly legal materials in Filipino, the translation of laws, rules and regulations and jurisprudence of the Supreme Court in Filipino, the use of Filipino in courts of justice as official communication to ensure speedy trials and transparency in the administration of justice.
