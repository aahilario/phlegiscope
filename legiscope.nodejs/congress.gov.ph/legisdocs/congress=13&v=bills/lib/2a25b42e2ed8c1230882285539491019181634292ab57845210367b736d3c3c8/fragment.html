HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01649
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING FIXED MONTHLY HONORARIA AND OTHER BENEFITS TO ACCREDITED BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 6 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7883, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS INCENTIVES AND BENEFITS ACT OF 1995
ABSTRACT : In recognition of the vital role they play in the delivery of health services, the bill proposes to grant accredited barangay health workers a monthly honoraria of P3,000.00 and an insurance coverage of not less than P10,000.00.
