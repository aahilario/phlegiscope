HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03783
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER TWENTY-FIVE OF EVERY YEAR AS 'NATIONAL CONSCIOUSNESS DAY FOR THE ELIMINATION OF VIOLENCE AGAINST WOMEN
SHORT TITLE : Declaring Nov. 25 of Every Year as "National Consciousness Day for the Elimination of Violence Against Women"
ABSTRACT : In recognition of the need to establish a comprehensive and structured campaign for national consciousness on anti-violence against women. The bill declares November 25 of every year as the "National Consciousness Day for the Elimination of Violence Against Women".
