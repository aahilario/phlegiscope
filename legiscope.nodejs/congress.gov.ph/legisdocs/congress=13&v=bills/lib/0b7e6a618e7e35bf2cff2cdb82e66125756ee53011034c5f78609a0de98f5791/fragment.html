House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02804
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE POSSESSION OF LICENSED FIREARMS AND THE CARRYING THEREOF OUTSIDE OF RESIDENCE AND FOR OTHER PUPROSES
ABSTRACT : The bill identifies the persons allowed to carry firearms outside their homes and prescribes the standards for the issuance of a permit to possess and to carry licensed firearms.
