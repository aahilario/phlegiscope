FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING MATERNAL AND INFANT HEALTH HOME VISITING PROGRAM
SHORT TITLE : "Maternal and Infant Care Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill requires health centers to designate an appropriate health professional or a qualified non-professional to deliver maternal and child health services in the home of pregnant adolescents.
