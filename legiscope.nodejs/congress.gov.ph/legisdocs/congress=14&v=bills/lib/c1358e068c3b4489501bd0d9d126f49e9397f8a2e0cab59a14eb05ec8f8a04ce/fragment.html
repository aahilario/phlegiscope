FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE ALLOWANCE OF DAY CARE WORKERS AND PROVIDING FOR THEIR INCENTIVES AND BENEFITS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE BARANGAY-LEVEL TOTAL DEVELOPMENT AND PROTECTION OF CHILDREN ACT
ABSTRACT : The bill increases the minimum monthly allowance of barangay day care workers from P500 to P5,500.
