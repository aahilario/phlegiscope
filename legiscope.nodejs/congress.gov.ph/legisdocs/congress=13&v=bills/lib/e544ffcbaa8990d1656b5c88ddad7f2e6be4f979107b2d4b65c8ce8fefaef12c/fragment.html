House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03169
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN THE UNIVERSITY OF THE PHILIPPINES AS THE NATIONAL UNIVERSITY
SHORT TITLE : University of the Philippines Charter of 2004
ABSTRACT : "University of the Philippines Charter of 2004." The bill stipulates the purpose of the university, the composition and powers of the Board of Regents, the powers of the University Council and the tax exemption privileges of the university.
