House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01648
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING NOISE AND ITS SOURCES AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR ITS VIOLATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Noise Pollution Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Noise Pollution Act of 2007". The bill prohibits any person from willfully making any loud, unnecessary and unusual noise which disturbs the peace or quiet in the neighborhood.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SYJUCO, JUDY J.
