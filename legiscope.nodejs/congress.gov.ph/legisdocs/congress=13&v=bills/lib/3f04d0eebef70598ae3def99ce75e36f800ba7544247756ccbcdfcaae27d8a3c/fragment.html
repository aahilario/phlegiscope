House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01973
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING THE ARCHIPELAGIC BASELINES OF THE PHILIPPINE ARCHIPELAGO TO INCLUDE THE KALAYAAN ISLAND GROUP AND TO CONFORM WITH THE PROVISIONS OF THE UNITED NATIONS CONVENTION ON THE LAW OF THE SEA, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3046, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5446
SHORT TITLE : Archipelagic Baselines Law of the Philippines
ABSTRACT : "Archipelagic Baselines Law of the Philippines." The bill prescribes and defines the list of geographical coordinates with the location base points of the archipelagic baselines of the Philippines in conformity with the 1982 United Nations Convention of the Law of the Sea.
