House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01727
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE RESICADA SYSTEM IN THE COCONUT INDUSTRY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Resicada System Regulation Act"
ABSTRACT : "Resicada System Regulation Act". The bill prohibits the arbitrary deduction from the prices of copra by reason of its moisture content unless the moisture has been determined by a duly calibrated meter and was proven to have exceeded the established standards.
