House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02130
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CRISIS CENTER FOR STREET CHILDREN IN ALL CITIES AND MUNICIPALITIES IN THE NATIONAL CAPITAL REGION AND IN EVERY HIGHLY URBANIZED CITY IN THE PHILIPPINES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Street Children Crisis Center Act of 1998"
ABSTRACT : "Street Children Crisis Center Act of 1998". The bill establishes crisis centers that shall provide the following services, among others: a) emergency medical and feeding services; b) temporary shelter; c) basic education, legal and counsel services; and d) rehabilitation for prohibited substance abusers.
