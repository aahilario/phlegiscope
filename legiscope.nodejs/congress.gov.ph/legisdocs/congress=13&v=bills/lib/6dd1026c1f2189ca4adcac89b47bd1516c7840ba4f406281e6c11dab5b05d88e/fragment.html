House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00564
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CONSTITUTING A CONSTITUTIONAL CONVENTION TO AMEND OR REVISE THE PRESENT CONSTITUTION, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Constitutional Convention Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Constitutional Convention Act of 2004". The bill calls for a Constitutional Convention that will amend or revise the present Constitution in order to make it more responsive to the demands of the times and meet the requirements of a good Charter possessing the elements of clarity, comprehensiveness and brevity.
