House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00924
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PRESCRIBING THE MANDATORY INSTALLATION OF MONITORING DEVICES INSIDE AND OUTSIDE THE PREMISES OF ALL COMMERCIAL AND QUASI-PUBLIC BANKS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Bank Robbery Act
ABSTRACT : "Anti- Bank Robbery Act." This bill requires banks to install surveillance cameras to record the movements of persons around the bank continuously recording it for 72 hours.
