FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 34 (h) OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 4136 OR THEREBY REQUIRING THE MANDATORY COMPLIANCE BY ALL MOTORCYCLE OPERATORS, DRIVERS, RIDERS AND/OR PASSENGERS TO WEAR SAFETY HELMETS
SHORT TITLE : "Motorcycle Helmet Use Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Motorcycle Helmet Use Act of 2007". The measure penalizes any motorist who operates or drives and rides on any motorcycle without wearing a safety helmet of an approved type.
