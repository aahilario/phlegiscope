FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PRE-NEED PLAN CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : "The Pre-Need Plan Code of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : "The Pre-Need Plan Code of the Philippines". The bill provides the legal framework to govern and regulate the operations of the pre-need industry. It stipulates additional measures to protect the rights of planholders.
