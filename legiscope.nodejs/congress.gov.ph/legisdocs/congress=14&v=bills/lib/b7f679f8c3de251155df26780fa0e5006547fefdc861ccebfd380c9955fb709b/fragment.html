HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00757
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE PAYMENT OF FILING FEES FOR COMPLAINTS CHARGING VIOLATIONS OF BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 22, AS AMENDED, FILED WITH THE PROSECUTION OFFICES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : To discourage the indiscriminate filing of cases for violation of the Bouncing Law (BP 22) to force payment from debtors, the bill authorizes the Secretary of Justice to fix, impose and collect filing fees for every complaint filed for violation of the law.
