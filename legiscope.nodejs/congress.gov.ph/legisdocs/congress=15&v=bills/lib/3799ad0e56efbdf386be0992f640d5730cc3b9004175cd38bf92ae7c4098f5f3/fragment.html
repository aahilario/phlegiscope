House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00271
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN EDUCATION TRUST FUND FOR GRANTEES OF GSIS AND SSS MEMBERS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Education Trust Fund Act
ABSTRACT : “Education Trust Fund Act” Seeks to broaden opportunities and promote education as an accessible equalizer through the partnership of the government and the private sector , making it an effective instrument in improving the life of the majority of the Filipino people.
