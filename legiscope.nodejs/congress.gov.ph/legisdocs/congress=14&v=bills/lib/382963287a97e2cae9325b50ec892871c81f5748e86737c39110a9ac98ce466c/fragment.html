FULL TITLE : AN ACT APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF THREE BILLION PESOS FOR THE AID, RESETTLEMENT, AND REHABILITATION SERVICES FOR THE UNDERPRIVILEGED AND HOMELESS CITIZENS AFFECTED IN THE DEMOLITION OF HOUSES/DWELLINGS ALONG DANGER AREAS SUCH AS ESTEROS, GARBAGE DUMPS, RIVERBANKS, SHORELINES, WATERWAYS, AND OTHER PUBLIC PLACES SUCH AS SIDEWALKS, ROADS, PARKS, AND PLAYGROUNDS
ABSTRACT : The bill allocates P3 billion for the resettlement of displaced poor families.
