House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01366
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CHANGING THE APPOINTMENT OF THE ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES OFFICER FROM OPTIONAL TO MANDATORY AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 484 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the madatory appointment of the environment and natural resources officer (ENRO) in cities and municipalities within the Metro Manila area and its optional appointment in municipalities outside of the Metropolitan Manila area.
