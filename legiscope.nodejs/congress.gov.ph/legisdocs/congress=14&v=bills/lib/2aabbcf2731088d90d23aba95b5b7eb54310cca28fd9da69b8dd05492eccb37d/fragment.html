House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02892
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ENSURE THAT OLDER OR DISABLED PERSONS ARE PROTECTED FROM INSTITUTIONAL, COMMUNITY, AND DOMESTIC VIOLENCE AND SEXUAL ASSAULT AND TO IMPROVE OUTREACH EFFORTS AND OTHER SERVICES AVAILABLE TO OLDER OR DISABLED PERSONS VICTIMIZED BY SUCH VIOLENCE
SHORT TITLE : "Older Filipinos Protection From Violence Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the development of training programs for law enforcement officers and for health professionals that would properly equip them to address cases of elderly abuse, neglect and exploitation.
