FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL HEALTH RESEARCH SYSTEM, ESTABLISHING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL HEALTH RESEARCH FUND AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine National Health Research System Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill obliges certain government institutions to allocate a portion of their gross annual revenue or to charge additional fees for health research and development projects.
