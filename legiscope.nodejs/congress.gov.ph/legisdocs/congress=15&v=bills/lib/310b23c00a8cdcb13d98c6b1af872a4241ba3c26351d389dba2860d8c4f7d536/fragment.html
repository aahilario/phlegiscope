House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00013
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE SAFETY AND PROTECTION OF THE UNBORN CHILD AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Protection Of The Unborn Child Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Protection of the Unborn Child Act of 2010.” Aims to remedy the oversight in the statutes by recognizing that the unborn has a basic right to life and by extending the mantle of legal recognition and protection to it by defining and clarifying the basic concepts and principle of fetal development.
