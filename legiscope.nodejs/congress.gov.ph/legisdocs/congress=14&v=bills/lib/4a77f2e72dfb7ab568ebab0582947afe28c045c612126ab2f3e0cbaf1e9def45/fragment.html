House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00768
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING MINING ROY ALTIES TO GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES, ESTABLISHING THE MECHANISMS FOR ITS PROPER PAYMENT, COLLECTION AND ADMINISTRATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "State's Royalty Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "State's Royalty Act of 2005". The bill proposes to charge mineral resources extractors a mining royalty equivalent to 12% of the net sales profit of the mineral resources extractor.
