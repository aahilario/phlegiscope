House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05817
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE FIRST DAY OF SEPTEMBER OF EACH YEAR A NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE COUNTRY IN HONOR OF THE FILIPINO FAMILY, TO BE KNOWN AS 'NATIONAL FAMILY DAY'
SHORT TITLE : Declaring the First Day of September of Each Year A Nonworking Public Holiday To Be Known as 'National Family Day'
ABSTRACT : The measure declares September 1 of every year as a non-working holiday in recognition of the Filipino family as the foundation of the nation.
