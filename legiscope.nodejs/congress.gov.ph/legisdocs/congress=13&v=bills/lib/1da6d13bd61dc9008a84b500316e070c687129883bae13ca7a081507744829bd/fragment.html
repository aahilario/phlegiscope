HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02839
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROMOTE THE STUDY OF FOREIGN LANGUAGES IN THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : Foreign Languages Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Foreign Languages Act of 2004." The bill requires all private and public schools to offer Spanish, Arabic and other foreign languages as an elective subject in all levels, beginning with the primary level.
