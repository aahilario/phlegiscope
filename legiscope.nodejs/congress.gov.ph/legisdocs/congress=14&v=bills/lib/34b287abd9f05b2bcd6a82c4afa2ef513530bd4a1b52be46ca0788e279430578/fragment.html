HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03112
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE PRACTICE OF METALLURGICAL ENGINEERING IN THE PHILIPPINES REPEALING FOR THIS PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1536, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE METALLURGICAL ENGINEERING LAW OF 1978, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure prescribes stiffer penal provisions to regulate the profession to ensure the reduction, if not the elimination, of illegal activities that usually result to uncontrollable social and environmental disasters.
