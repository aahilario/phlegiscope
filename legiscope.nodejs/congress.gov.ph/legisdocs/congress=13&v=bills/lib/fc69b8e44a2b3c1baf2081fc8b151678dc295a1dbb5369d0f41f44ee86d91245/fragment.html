House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04663
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE FIRST WEEK OF DECEMBER OF EVERY YEAR AS FAMILY WEEK
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to declare the first week of December of every year as a Family Week to foster love, respect, discipline, industry and enterprise within Filipino homes and the community.
