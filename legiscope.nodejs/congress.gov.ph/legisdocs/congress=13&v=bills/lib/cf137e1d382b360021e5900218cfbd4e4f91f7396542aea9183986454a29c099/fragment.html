House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00641
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 223 OF ACT NO. 3815, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED PENAL CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill increases the penalty imposed on jail guards and public officers for allowing the escape of prisoners in their custody from "prision correctional" to "reclusion perpetua" and permanent absolute disqualification.
