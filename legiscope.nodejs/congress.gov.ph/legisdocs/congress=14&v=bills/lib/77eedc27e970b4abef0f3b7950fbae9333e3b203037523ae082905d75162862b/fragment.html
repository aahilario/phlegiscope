HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00306
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE REGIONALIZATION OF THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION PAYROLL SERVICE DIVISION AND PROVIDING FOR FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to decentralize the payment of salaries of teachers to correct excessive or unauthorized deductions, wrong computation of withholding tax, delayed release of checks and the inconvenience of getting paid by Manila-based payroll.
