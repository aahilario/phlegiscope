HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04253
FULL TITLE : AN ACT SEEKING TO IMPOSE A MORATORIUM ON LAND DISTRIBUTION UNDER THE COMPREHENSIVE AGRARIAN REFORM LAW OF 1988 AND TO SET ASIDE APPROPRIATED FUNDS FOR THE CARP TO FINANCE LIVELIHOOD PROGRAMS TO MAKE LANDS ALREADY DISTRIBUTED PRODUCTIVE
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to suspend the distribution of lands under the Comprehensive Agrarian Reform Law (CARL) and to utilize the funds earmarked for the CARL to finance livelihood programs for lands already distributed in order to make them productive.
