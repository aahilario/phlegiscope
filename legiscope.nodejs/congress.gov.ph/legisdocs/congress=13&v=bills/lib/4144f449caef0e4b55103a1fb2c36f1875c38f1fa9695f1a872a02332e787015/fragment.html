House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00046
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ABOLISHING THE DEATH PENALTY REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7659 ENTITLED 'AN ACT TO IMPOSE THE DEATH PENALTY ON CERTAIN HEINOUS CRIMES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AS AMENDED, OTHER SPECIAL PENAL LAWS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES'
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to abolish the imposition of death penalty in the country. Scientific evidence show that death penalty has not been instrumental in deterring crime. In fact, in the Philippines, the number of death row inmates continue to grow despite the execution of several death convicts already. The bill further advocates the reduction of the death penalty already imposed to reclusion perpetua.
