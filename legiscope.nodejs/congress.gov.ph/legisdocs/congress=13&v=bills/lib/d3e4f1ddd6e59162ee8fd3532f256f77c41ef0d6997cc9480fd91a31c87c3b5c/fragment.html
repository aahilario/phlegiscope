House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01248
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 70 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991 AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to improve the recall mechanism as mandated in the 1991 Local Government Code. Increases the percentage number of required voters to validate a recall proportionate to the number of the population of the local government unit whose elected official is the subject of a recall process.
