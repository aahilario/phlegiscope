FULL TITLE : AN ACT REPEALING REPUBLIC ACT 9337, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 27, 28, 34, 106, 107, 108, 109, 110, 111, 112, 113, 114, 116, 119, 121, 148, 151, 236, 237, AND 288 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
