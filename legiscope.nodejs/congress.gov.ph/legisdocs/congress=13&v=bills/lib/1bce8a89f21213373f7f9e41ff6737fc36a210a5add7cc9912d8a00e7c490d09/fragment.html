House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01805
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY 23 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE COUNTRY
ABSTRACT : The bill declares January 23 of every year a special non-working holiday in the country to commemorate the Declaration of the First Philippine Republic on January 23, 1899 held at Barasoain Church in the City of Malolos, Province of Bulacan.
