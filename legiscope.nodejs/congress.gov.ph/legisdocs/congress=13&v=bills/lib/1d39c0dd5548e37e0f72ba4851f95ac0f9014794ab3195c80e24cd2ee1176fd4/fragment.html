HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01793
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PRESCRIBING HIGHER AND SIMULTANEOUS PENALTIES OF PRISON TERM AND FINE FOR VIOLATIONS OF GAMBLING LAWS WHENEVER THE OFFENDER IS THE MAINTAINER, CONDUCTOR, BANKER OR GAMBLING LORD, AND ADDITIONAL PENALTY IF THE OFFENDER IS A POLICE OR MILITARY OFFICER OR PERSONNEL OR A LOCAL GOVERNMENT OFFICIAL AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 1 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1602
ABSTRACT : The bill increases the penalties for illegal gambling to prision mayor in its minimum term AND a fine of P100,000. If the culprit is a government official or a uniformed personnel, the penalty shall be prision mayor in its maximum term and a fine of P200,000.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : PICHAY, PROSPERO JR. A.
