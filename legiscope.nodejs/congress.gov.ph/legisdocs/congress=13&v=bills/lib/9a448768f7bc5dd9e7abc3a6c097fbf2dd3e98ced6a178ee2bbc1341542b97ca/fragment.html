HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03247
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR ADDITIONAL BENEFITS AND PRIVILEGES TO PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 4670, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE MAGNA CARTA FOR PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS", AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks the full implementation of the Magna Carta for Public School Teachers. It seeks to grant additional benefits and privileges in the form of more liberal study leaves, educational grants to the children of teachers and more comprehensive special hardship allowances.
