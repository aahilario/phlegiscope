FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING COMPREHENSIVE RENTAL CONTROL ON COMMERCIAL BUILDINGS AND SPACES, AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF

ABSTRACT : The bill allows not more than 10% annual increase in the monthly rental of commercial units in the National Capital Region and other urban areas.
