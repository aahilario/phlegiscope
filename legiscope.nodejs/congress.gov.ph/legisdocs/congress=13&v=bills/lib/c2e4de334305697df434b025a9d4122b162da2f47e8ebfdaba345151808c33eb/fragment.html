HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00405
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING THAT THE SCHEDULE OF FAIR MARKET VALUES OF REAL PROPERTY SHALL BE PREPARED BY THE LOCAL ASSESSMENT COUNCIL AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : At present, the preparation of the schedule of fair market values of real property is the sole responsibility of the provincial, city or municipal assessor. Instead of one person performing such function, it is suggested that the function be assigned to a collegial body. This bill seeks to empower the local assessment council to prepare the schedule of fair market values of real property to eliminate the possibility of drawing up a schedule which favors certain groups of sectors or is erroneously done.
