HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04644
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL HOLDERS OF PROFESSIONAL DRIVER'S LICENSE AND OWNERS OF PRIVATE OR PUBLIC UTILITY VEHICLES TO OBTAIN PERSONAL ACCIDENT AND LIABILITY INSURANCE AS PREREQUISITE FOR RENEWAL OF DRIVERS LICENSE AND CAR REGISTRATION, AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill requires all holders of professional driver's license and owners of private or public utility vehicles to obtain a personal accident and liability insurance as a prerequisite for his/her driver's license and car registration renewal.
