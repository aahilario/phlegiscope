House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04526
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING BENEFITS AND SPECIAL PRIVILEGES TO STUDENTS IN BOTH PUBLIC AND PRIVATE SCHOOLS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Students Privilege Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Students Privilege Act of 2005". The bill grants students benefits and privileges similar to those enjoyed by senior citizens such as discounts on transportation fares, medicines, food and school supplies.
