HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01355
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING POLITICAL TURNCOATISM AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION SEVENTY-ONE OF BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. EIGHTY-EIGHT HUNDRED AND ONE, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE OMNIBUS ELECTION CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : In order to minimize the politics of patronage and develop a strong multi-party system in the country, the bill provides the following penalties for political turncoatism: a) an elective official shall not be qualified to run for any elective position in any election immediately following the expiration of his term of office; b) a candidate, other than the incumbent elective official, shall not be qualified to run for any elective office immediately following his change of political party affiliation; and c) either of the above cases shall not be qualified to hold any elective office within the term of office immediately following the elections from which he was qualified.
