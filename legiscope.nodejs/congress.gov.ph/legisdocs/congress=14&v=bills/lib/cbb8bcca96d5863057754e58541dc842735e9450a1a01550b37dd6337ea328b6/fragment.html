House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00196
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR BENEFITS TO DEPENDENTS OF MEMBERS OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE, CREATING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE DEPENDENTS WELFARE OFFICE, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine National Police (PNP) Dependent's Benefits Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Philippine National Police (PNP) Dependent's Benefits Act of 2007". The bill grants dependents of members of the PNP the following: a) educational benefits for the use of qualified dependents, not exceeding four (4); b) improved housing; c) health care; and d)commissary benefits.
