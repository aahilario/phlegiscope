House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01467
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 286 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the internal revenue liability of local government units (LGUs) to be automatically deducted from their respective revenue allotments to eliminate the unncessary transfer of money from the LGUs to the national treasury and back to the LGUs.
