HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04799
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HALON BANK FACILITY IN THE PHILIPPINES AND APPORPRIATING THE AMOUNT OF TWENTY MILLION (P20,000,000.00) PESOS FOR THE PURPOSE
ABSTRACT : The measure provides for the establishment of a halon bank facility which shall develop and implement comprehensive strategies to manage existing stocks of halon (ozone-depleting substances) down to zero, advise on the disposal of halons and assist in locating halons in the country.
