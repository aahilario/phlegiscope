FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MANDATORY NATIONWIDE CHILD NUTRITION PROGRAM IN ALL PUBLIC ELEMENTARY SCHOOLS AND BARANGAY DAY CARE CENTERS
SHORT TITLE : Child Nutrition Law of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Child Nutrition Law of 2004." The bill mandates the feeding of children enrolled in all public elementary schools and barangay day care centers in keeping with the State's policy of promoting and protecting the welfare of children.
