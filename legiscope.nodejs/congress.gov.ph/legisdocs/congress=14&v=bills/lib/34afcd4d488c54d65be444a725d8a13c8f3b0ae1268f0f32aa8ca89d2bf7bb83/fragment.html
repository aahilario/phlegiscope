House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00974
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REORGANIZING AND MODERNIZING THE NATIONAL BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION AND PROVIDING NECESSARY FUNDS THEREFOR AND OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "National Bureau of Investigation Reorganization and Modernization Act of 2001"
ABSTRACT : "National Bureau of Investigation Reorganization and Modernization Act of 2001". The bill aims to reorganize the NBI to enable it to meet the standards of an investigative and detective agency. It provides for expanded organizational structure and manpower complement responsive to the demands of the times.
