House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00373
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ABOLISHING THE DEATH PENALTY AND IMPOSING THE PENALTY OF QUALIFIED RECLUSION PERPETUA IN LIEU THEREOF, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7659 OR THE DEATH PENALTY LAW, ACT 3815, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AS AMENDED, AND OTHER SPECIAL LAWS
ABSTRACT : The death penalty has not proven to be a deterrent to the commission of crime. If the death penalty is seen as a retributive act of society, keeping a convict in jail for the rest of his is a civilized substitute for it. The bill seeks to abolish the death penalty and to impose the penalty of qualified reclusion perpetua in lieu thereof.
