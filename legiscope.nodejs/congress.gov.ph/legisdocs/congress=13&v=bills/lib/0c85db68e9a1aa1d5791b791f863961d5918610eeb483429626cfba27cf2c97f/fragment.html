HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03338
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMOTING REGULAR EMPLOYMENT AND FOR THIS PURPOSE, AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE 442
ABSTRACT : The salient points of the bill are: a) the incorporation of the judicial recognition that all classes of employees are entitled to security of tenure; b) the incorporation of the workers' right to notice and hearing, and c) the clarification of what constitutes regular, casual and project employment.
