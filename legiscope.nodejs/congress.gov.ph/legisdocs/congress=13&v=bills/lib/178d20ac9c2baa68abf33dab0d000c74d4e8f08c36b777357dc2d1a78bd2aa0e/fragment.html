FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A FREE MEDICINE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM FOR THE POOR AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Free Medical Assistance Act of 2006"
