HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00385
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE PHILIPPINE OVERSEAS EMPLOYMENT AGENCY (POEA) TO INCLUDE IN ITS PROGRAM FOR OVERSEAS EMPLOYMENT THE MANDATORY TEACHING OF BASIC SELF-DEFENSE TECHNIQUES FOR WOMEN APPLICANTS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : There has been an alarming upsurge of violence especially against women including women employees working abroad. The bill requires the Philippine Overseas Employment Agency (POEA) to include in its program for overseas employment the mandatory teaching of basic self-defense techniques for women applicants. The lessons will be free of charge. With these lessons, they will not only be physically prepared but they will, at the same time, develop the "martial arts discipline".
