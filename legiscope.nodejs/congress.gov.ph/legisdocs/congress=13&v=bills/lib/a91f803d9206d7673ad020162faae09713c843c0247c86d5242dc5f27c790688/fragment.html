House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04789
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING CERTAIN SMALL ISLANDS AS ALIENABLE OR DISPOSABLE FOR COMMERCIAL (ECO-TOURISM) PURPOSES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill declares as alienable or disposable for commercial (eco-tourism) purposes small islands with land areas of 50,000 hectares or less and already devoted to or with great potential for eco-tourism ventures.
