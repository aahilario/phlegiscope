HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01404
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTOMATICALLY ASSIGNING TO GOVERNMENT INSURANCE PROCEEDS OF CORPORATIONS UNDERTAKING INDUSTRIAL PROJECTS WHENEVER MAJOR ACCIDENT, DISASTER OR CATASTROPHIC EVENT OCCURS INVOLVING THEIR INDUSTRIAL PROJECT CAUSING DAMAGE TO PERSONS, PROPERTY, AND ENVIRONMENT
SHORT TITLE : "Industrial Disaster Insurance Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Industrial Disaster Insurance Act of 2005". The bill provides for the automatic assignment to the government of the insurance proceeds paid to corporations should a catastrophic event or accident occur causing grave damage to persons, property or environment. In this manner, the government can be assured that compensation for the damage caused will be indemnified.
