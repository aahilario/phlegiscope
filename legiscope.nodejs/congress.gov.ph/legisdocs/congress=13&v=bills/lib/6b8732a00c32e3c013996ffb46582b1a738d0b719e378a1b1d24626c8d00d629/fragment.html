House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01458
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMULGATING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL STANDARDS FOR DRINKING WATER, PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Safe Drinking Water Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Safe Drinking Water Act of 2004." The bill creates a Drinking Water Quality Committee in every province to be composed of a representative from the Department of Health and the local health board. The Board shall be tasked to monitor potable water.
