House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02706
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A HIGHER STANDARD OF PROMOTION AND PROTECTION OF THE RIGHTS AND WELFARE OF FILIPINO OVERSEAS SEAFARERS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Overseas Seafarers Act
ABSTRACT : "Overseas Seafarers Act." For the protection of seafarers, the bill prescribes the recruitment and placement policies of the government, the duties of recruitment and placement services, the penalties for illegal recruitment, the conditions of employment and social welfare benefits, disciplinary and dispute settlement procedures and post employment and repatriation policies.
