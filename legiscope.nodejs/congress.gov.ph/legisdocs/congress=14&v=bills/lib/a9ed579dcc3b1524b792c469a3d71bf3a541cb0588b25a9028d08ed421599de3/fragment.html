House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00182
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE STRICT LABELING OF FOOD AND BEVERAGE PRODUCTS BY MANUFACTURING AND DISTRIBUTION COMPANIES
SHORT TITLE : "Consumer Information and Protection Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Consumer Information and Protection Act of 2007". The bill directs manufacturers and distributors to supply the necessary and accurate information on their products before their distribution and/or sale to the market.
