HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00444
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE DEPARTMENT OF TRADE AND INDUSTRY TO PROVIDE EVERY BARANGAY TEN (10) PIECES OF STANDARDIZED METAL WEIGHTS WEIGHING ONE (1) KILOGRAM EACH TO BE PUT UNDER THE CUSTODY OF THE BARANGAY CAPTAIN AND LOANED FOR FREE TO ANY BARANGAY RESIDENT WHO WISHES TO CHECK THE ACCURACY OF THE WEIGHING SCALE BEING USED IN THE MARKETING OF ANY PRODUCT WHICH PRICE IS DETERMINED BY WEIGHT AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : Despite efforts by the Department of Trade and Industry, unscrupulous sellers and traders still manipulate their weighing scales to make a commodity appear to weigh more or weigh less. To ensure that the weighing instruments being used in the trade are "honest", this bill proposes to provide every barangay 10 units of standardized metal weighing instrument weighing one kilogram each to be kept under the custody of the barangay captain and loaned for free to any barangay resident.
