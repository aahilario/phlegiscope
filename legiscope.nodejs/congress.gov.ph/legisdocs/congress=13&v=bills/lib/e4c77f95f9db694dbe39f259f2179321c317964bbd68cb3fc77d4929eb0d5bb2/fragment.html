HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03551
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING OWNERS OF SHOPPING MALLS, MARKETS, HOTELS AND OTHER SIMILAR COMMERCIAL ESTABLISHMENTS TO PROVIDE ADEQUATE PARKING AREAS FOR THEIR CLIENTELE, PROHIBITING THE EXACTION OF PARKING FEES, AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill requires all shopping malls, markets, hotels and similar establishments to provide free parking areas to their clientele.
