House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00195
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING MECHANISMS TO REGULATE THE IMPORT, TRANSIT, EXPORT, AND REPATRIATION OF CULTURAL PROPERTY INTO AND FROM THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : "Cultural Property Transfer Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Cultural Property Transfer Act of 2007". The measure provides the statutory mechanism that will govern the importation of cultural goods into the country, their transit, export and return out of the country.
