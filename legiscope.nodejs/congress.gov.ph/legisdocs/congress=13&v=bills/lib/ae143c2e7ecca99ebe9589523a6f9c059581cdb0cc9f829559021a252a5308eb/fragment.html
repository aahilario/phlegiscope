House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02291
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING ONE STEP SALARY GRADE INCREASE TO GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES ONE YEAR BEFORE COMPULSORY RETIREMENT AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : This bill hopes that government employees will be assured of an increase in their lump sum retirement benefits and monthly pensions to enable them to be better-off economically, and be able to respond to their needs and their family's as well.
