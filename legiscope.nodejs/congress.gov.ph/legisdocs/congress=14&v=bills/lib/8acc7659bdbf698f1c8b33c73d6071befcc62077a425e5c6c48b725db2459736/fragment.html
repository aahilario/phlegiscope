FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE DEMOCRATIZATION OF PUBLIC SCHOOL EDUCATION
SHORT TITLE : "Public School Democratization Law of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Public School Democratization Law of 2007". The bill guarantees children who are bona fide residents of the town or city admission to the public schools in the area.
