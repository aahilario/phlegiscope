House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00792
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE BARANGAY INTEGRATED DEVELOPMENT APPROACH FOR NUTRITION IMPROVEMENT (BIDANI OF THE RURAL POOR AS A NETWORK PROGRAM OF THE PARTICIPATING STATE COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : BIDANI Act of 2001
ABSTRACT : "BIDANI Act of 2001." The bill seeks to institutionalize the Barangay Integrated Development Approach for Nutrition Improvement (BIDANI) network program to provide a lasting solution to the problem of malnutrition. It calls for the involvement of academic institutions and local government units in promoting the importance of nutrition. The BIDANI program shall complement traditional nutrition intervention.
