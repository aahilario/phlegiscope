House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02506
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE JUDGES-AT-LARGE, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 129, AS AMENDED BY R.A. 7154, AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill creates judges-at-large for first-level and second-level courts who will not be assigned to any single station. These judges may be temporarily assigned to vacant courts until a permanent judge is appointed.
