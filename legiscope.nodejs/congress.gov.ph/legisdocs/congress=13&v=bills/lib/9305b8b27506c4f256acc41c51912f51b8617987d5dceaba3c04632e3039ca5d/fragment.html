HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00618
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REVERTING TO THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT THE DISCHARGE OF BASIC HEALTH SERVICES DEVOLVED TO LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS (LGUs) AND AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
SHORT TITLE : Re-Nationalization of Devolved Health Services and Facilities Act
ABSTRACT : "Re-Nationalization of Devolved Health Services and Facilities Act". Aims to correct the flaws of decentralization such as non-implementation of the Magna Carta for Health Workers and delays or non-payment of their salaries, by reverting to the national government the devolved functions.
