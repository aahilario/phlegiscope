House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02690
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE INTERACTIVE ENTERTAINMENT RATING BOARD
SHORT TITLE : "Interactive Entertainment Rating Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes the Interactive Entertainment Rating Board that shall provide parents with information about the nature of video games used in homes or public areas, including entertainment arcades.
