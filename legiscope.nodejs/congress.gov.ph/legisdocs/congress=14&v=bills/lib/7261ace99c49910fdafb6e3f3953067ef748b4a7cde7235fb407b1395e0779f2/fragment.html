House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02125
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE URBAN AND COUNTRYSIDE GREENING IN THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : "Greening Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Greening Act of 2007". The bill seeks to build, develop and maintain forest parks in local areas, educational institutions and public roads; promote environmental consciousness among the Filipinos; and protect existing trees from further harm.
