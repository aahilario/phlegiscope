House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02524
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO UPGRADE THE SALARY GRADE LEVEL OF TEACHER I POSITION FROM SALARY GRADE 10 TO SALARY GRADE 18, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6758, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SALARY STANDARDIZATION LAW AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : Teachers are the most overworked yet underpaid public servants in the country. This measure seeks to upgrade the salary grade (SG) of Teacher I position from SG 10 to SG 18.
