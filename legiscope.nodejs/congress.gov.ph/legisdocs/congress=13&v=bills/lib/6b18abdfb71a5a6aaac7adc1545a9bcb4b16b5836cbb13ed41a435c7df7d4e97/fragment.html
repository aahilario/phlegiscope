House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05793
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A MUNICIPAL LIVELIHOOD AND EMPLOYMENT RESOURCE CENTER IN EVERY CITY AND MUNICIPALITY AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to establish livelihood and employment resource centers in every municipality and city to complement the national livelihood programs. The centers shall focus on the production of goods that are not ordinarily included in industrial manufacturing.
