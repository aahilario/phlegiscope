HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02083
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE COMPREHENSIVE AGRARIAN REFORM PROGRAM (CARP), FURTHER AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS SIXTY-THREE AND THIRTY-SEVEN OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6657, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE COMPREHENSIVE AGRARIAN REFORM LAW OF 1988
ABSTRACT : The bill directs all financing institutions to accept as collateral for loans any duly registered emancipation patent (EP) or certificate of land ownership award (CLOA) issued by the government to farmer beneficiaries of the Comprehensive Agrarian Reform Program.
