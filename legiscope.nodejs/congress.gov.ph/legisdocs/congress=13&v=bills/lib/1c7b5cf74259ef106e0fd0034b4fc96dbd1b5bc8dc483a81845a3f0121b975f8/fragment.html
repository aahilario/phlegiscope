House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02382
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING INCENTIVES AND BENEFITS TO BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Barangay Health Workers Benefits and Incentives Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Barangay Health Workers Benefits and Incentives Act of 2004." The bill grants barangay health workers the following additional incentives and benefits: a) hazard allowance; b) subsistence allowance; c) scholarships in the form of tuition fees in state universities/colleges; and d) free legal representation and consultation services.
