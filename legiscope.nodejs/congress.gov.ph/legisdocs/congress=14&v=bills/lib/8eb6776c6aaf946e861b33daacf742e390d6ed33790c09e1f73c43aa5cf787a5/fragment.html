HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02924
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER REGULATING THE SALE OF SECOND HAND ARTICLES PARTICULARLY ELECTRONIC AND TELECOMMUNICATIONS EQUIPMENT, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1612, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "ANTI-FENCING LAW OF 1979"
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the store owner or manager to require from the seller of an electronic and telecommunications equipment proof of purchase or ownership, and clearance from the National Telecommunications Commission before buying, acquiring or accepting the same as a pawn. (cell phones)
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.
DATE FILED : 2007-10-11
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
