HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00096
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING INCENTIVES TO PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS WHO ARE ASSIGNED OUTSIDE OF THEIR TOWNS AND PROVINCES APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Public School Teachers' Incentives Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Public School Teachers' Incentives Act of 2007". The bill proposes to grant any public school teacher assigned to a town other than his own a P1,000 monthly incentive allowance. It also proposes to grant a P2,000 monthly incentive allowance to any public school teacher who is assigned to a town of another province other than his own.
