House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03511
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING EXEMPTION OF OIL AND OIL PRODUCTS, ELECTRICITY, FOOD PRODUCTS, VITAMINS, AND MEDICINE FROM VALUE ADDED TAX, SOME EXISTING EXEMPTIONS, FURTHER AMENDING THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997 AS AMENDED BY R.A. NO. 9337, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSE
ABSTRACT : The bill exempts oil and oil products, electricity, food products, vitamins and medicines from the coverage of the value-added tax.
