HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01159
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS AND COMMERCIAL ESTABLISHMENTS WHICH MAINTAIN ATHLETIC TEAMS TO ALLOW THEIR ATHLETES OR PLAYERS TO JOIN IN THE SELECTION POOL AND FORMATION OF PHILIPPINE NATIONAL TEAMS THAT WILL COMPETE IN ALL INTERNATIONAL SPORTS COMPETITIONS AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF
ABSTRACT : This bill requires all educational institutions and commercial establishments that maintain athletes and teams participating in professional or amateur sports competitions to allow their athletes or players to join in the selection pool and formation of national teams that will compete in international sports competitions.
