House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03322
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DETERRING AND PUNISHING ESTABLISHMENT AND OPERATION OF CRIMINAL STREET GANGS, RECRUITMENT OF INDIVIDUALS TO PARTICIPATE THEREIN, COMMISSION OF VIOLENT GANG CRIMES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Gang Prevention and Effective Deterrence Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the imposition of stiff penalties for the establishment and operation of criminal street gangs, recruitment of individuals to participate thereon and the commission of violent gang crimes.
