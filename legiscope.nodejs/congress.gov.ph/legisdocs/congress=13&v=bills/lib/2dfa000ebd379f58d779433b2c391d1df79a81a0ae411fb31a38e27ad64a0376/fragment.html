House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04269
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL WATERSHED MANAGEMENT FORUM AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Watershed Management Forum Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "National Watershed Management Forum Act of 2005". The measure seeks to institutionalize the National Watershed Management Forum (NWMF) in order to provide a permanent venue for organized discussion on watershed management issues. It prescribes the composition of the NWMF, its functions and the roles and responsibilities of the lead agency and its agency members.
