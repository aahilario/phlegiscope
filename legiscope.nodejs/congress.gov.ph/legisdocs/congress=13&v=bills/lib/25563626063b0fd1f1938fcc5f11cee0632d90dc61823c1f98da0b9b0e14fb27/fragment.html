House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01629
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING A PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Public School Teachers' Scholarship Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Public School Teachers' Scholarship Act of 2004." The bill seeks to create a Public School Teachers Scholarship Fund to cover the tuition and other school fees, including textbooks, school supplies, transportation expenses and monthly living allowance, of public school teachers who wish to enroll in graduate and post-graduate courses.
