HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03089
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ENABLE PHILIPPINE SCHOOLS TO USE COMPUTER HARDWARE TO INCREASE STUDENT ACHIEVEMENT AND PREPARE STUDENTS FOR THE 21ST CENTURY WORKPLACE
SHORT TITLE : "Education for the 21st Century Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the Secretary of Education to award grants to: a) public and private schools that integrate technology literacy in their curriculum; and b) students, teachers, software developers and computer experts for the development of high quality educational software and internet websites.
