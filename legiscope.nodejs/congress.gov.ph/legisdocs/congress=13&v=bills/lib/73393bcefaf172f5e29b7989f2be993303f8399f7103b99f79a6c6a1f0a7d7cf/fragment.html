FULL TITLE : AN ACT REPEALING REPUBLIC ACT 7942, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE MINING ACT OF 1995
ABSTRACT : The bill calls for the repeal of the Mining Act of 1995 in view of the consequences brought about by large scale mining on the environment, health and the rights of indigenous peoples.
