HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03236
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO INCREASE THE PENALTY FOR THEFT OR ROBBERY COMMITTED WITHIN THE PREMISES OF CHURCHES, TEMPLES AND MUSEUMS
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes that the imposable penalty for crimes involving theft or robbery of religious and cultural objects from any church, temple or public museum be set higher by two degrees than that imposable penalty for such offenses under existing law.
