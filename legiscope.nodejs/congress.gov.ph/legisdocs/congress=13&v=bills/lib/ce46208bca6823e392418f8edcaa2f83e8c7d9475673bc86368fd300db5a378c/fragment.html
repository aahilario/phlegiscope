House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00788
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR ADDITIONAL MEMBERS OF THE BOARD OF TRUSTEES OF THE NATIONAL MARITIME POLYTECHNIC, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1369
SHORT TITLE : Providing for Additional Members of the Board of Trustees of the National Maritime Polytechnic
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to provide for additional members to the Board of Trustees of the National Maritime Polytechnic to include the following: a) The Chairman, House Committee on Higher and Technical Education; b) The Chairman, Senate Committee on Higher Education, Arts and Culture; c) The Chairman, Commission on Higher Education; and d) The Regional Director, Region VII, National Economic Development Authority.
