HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03408
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING THE BANK DEPOSITS OF SENIOR CITIZENS FROM THE TWENTY PERCENT (20%) WITHHOLDING TAX ON INTEREST INCOME, AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7432
ABSTRACT : The bill exempts senior citizens from paying the final 20% withholding tax on interest income from bank deposits to enable them to fully use their savings.
