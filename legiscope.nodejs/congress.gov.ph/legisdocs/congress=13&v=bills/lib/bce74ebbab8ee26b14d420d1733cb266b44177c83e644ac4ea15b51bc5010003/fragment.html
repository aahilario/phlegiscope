HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03680
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL GOVERNMENT OFFICES TO ENSURE THE RELEASE OF THE RETIREMENT BENEFITS OF ITS EMPLOYEES WITHIN FIFTEEN DAYS FROM RETIREMENT
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the heads of all government instrumentalities to ensure the release of the retirement benefits and gratuities of an employee within 15 days from the date of retirement.
