HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03018
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING REFORMS IN THE CRIMINAL JUSTICE SYSTEM BY ENHANCING DNA TECHNOLOGY ANALYSIS AS A POTENT INVESTIGATIVE TOOL, CREATING THE DNA ADVISORY BOARD UNDER THE NATIONAL BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION AND OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "DNA Analysis Enhancement Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill creates the DNA Advisory Board that shall develop and recommend standards for quality assurance, including testing the proficiency of forensic laboratories and forensic analysis, in conducting analysis of DNA (deoxyribonucleic acid), among others.
