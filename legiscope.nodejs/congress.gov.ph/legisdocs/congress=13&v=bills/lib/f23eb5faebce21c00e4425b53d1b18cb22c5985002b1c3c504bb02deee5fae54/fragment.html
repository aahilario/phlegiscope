House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00310
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE MOVIE AND TELEVISION REVIEW AND CLASSIFICATION BOARD, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1986 ENTITLED 'CREATING THE MOVIE AND TELEVISION REVIEW AND CLASSIFICATION BOARD,' AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Movie and Television Review and Classification Board or MTRCB Reorganization Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Movie and Television Review And Classification Board or MTRCB Reorganization Act of 2004". The bill sets out to create a new Board with the primary functions of classification, review and limited censorship. It further seeks to pave the way for the film industry to undertake self-regulatory functions.
