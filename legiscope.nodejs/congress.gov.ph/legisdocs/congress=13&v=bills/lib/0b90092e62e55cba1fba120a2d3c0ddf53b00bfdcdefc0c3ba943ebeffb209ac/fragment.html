HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HR01496
FULL TITLE : A RESOLUTION EXPRESSING THE SENSE OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES THAT THE PHILIPPINE CONGRESS IMMEDIATELY CONDUCT A REVIEW OF THE VISITING FORCES AGREEMENT (VFA) IN VIEW OF THE APPARENT DISREGARD OF OUR, JUDICIAL PROCESSES AND SOVEREIGNTY AND VIOLATION OF OUR CONSTITUTION WHEN U.S. SERVICEMAN LANCE CORPORAL DANIEL SMITH, WHO WAS CONVICTED OF RAPING A YOUNG FILIPINA, WAS TRANSFERRED TO THE CUSTODY OF THE U.S. WITHOUT ANY ORDER FROM OUR COURTS AND THAT IN THE MEANTIME THE PHILIPPINE CONGRESS CALLS FOR THE IMMEDIATE SUSPENSION OF THE VFA AND THE U.S.-R,P. JOINT MILITARY EXERCISES UNTIL THE COMPLETION OF THE REVIEW
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : MAZA, LIZA
DATE FILED : 2007-01-25
