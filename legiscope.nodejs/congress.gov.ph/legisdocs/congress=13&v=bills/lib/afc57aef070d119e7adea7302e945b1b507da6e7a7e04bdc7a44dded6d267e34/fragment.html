House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01279
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CREATION OF THE NATIONAL INDEPENDENT COMMISSION AGAINST CORRUPTION, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREOF AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Independent Commission Against Corruption Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "National Independent Commission Against Corruption Act of 2004." This bill creates a Commission which is mandated to implement and coordinate, at the national level, the prevention, control and elimination of graft and corrupt practices at all levels in government.
