HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00940
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE OFFICE OF EDUCATION ATTACHE OF THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION
ABSTRACT : The bill creates the Office of the Education Attache that shall supervise and oversee international schools managed and operated by the Philippines embassies and/or private institutions.
