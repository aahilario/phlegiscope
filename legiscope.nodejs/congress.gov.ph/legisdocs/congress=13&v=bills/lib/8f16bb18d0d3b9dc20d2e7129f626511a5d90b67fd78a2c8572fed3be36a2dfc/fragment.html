HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00411
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN SECTIONS OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBERED SIX HUNDRED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE MARINE POLLUTION DECREE OF 1974
ABSTRACT : This measure seeks to increase the penalty for the unlawful dumping of wastes and other matters that create hazards to human health, harm living resources and marine life, damages amenities or interfere with the legitimate uses of the sea. It holds the head of a corporation or any partnership culpable for violations of the provisions of the proposed measure. It defines the guidelines under which the Philippine Coast Guard may allow the dumping of waste materials. It likewise,authorizes the adoption of a dumping system.
