HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03418
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING FROM TAXES THE PRIZES RECEIVED BY CONTESTANTS IN LOCAL COMPETITIONS IN THE FIELDS OF ARTS AND CULTURE, SPORTS, SCIENCE, ENVIRONMENT, AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY SPONSORED BY THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS, SCHOOLS, COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES, PARENTS-TEACHERS ASSOCIATIONS, CIVIC ORGANIZATIONS, OR NON-STOCK AND NON-PROFIT FOUNDATIONS
ABSTRACT : The bill exempts from taxation all prizes, cash or in kind, including honoraria and gifts, received from local competitions in the fields of arts and culture, sports, sciences, environment and information technology.
