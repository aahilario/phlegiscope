FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING ADDITIONAL INSURANCE BENEFITS TO BARANGAY CAPTAINS, PROVIDING FUNDS FOR THE PAYMENT OF PREMIUMS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Expanded Barangay Captains' Insurance Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Expanded Barangay Captains' Insurance Act of 2007". The bill provides supplemental benefits to barangay captains for death, injuries and sickness sustained in the conduct of their official duties.
