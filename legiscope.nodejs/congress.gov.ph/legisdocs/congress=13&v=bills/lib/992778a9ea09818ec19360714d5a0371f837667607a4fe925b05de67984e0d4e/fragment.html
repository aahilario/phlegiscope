FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A SOCIALIZED AND LOW-COST HOUSING LOAN RESTRUCTURING AND RECLASSIFICATION PROGRAM, PROVIDING MECHANISM THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Housing Loan Restructuring and Reclassification Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Housing Loan Restructuring and Reclassification Act of 2005". Undr the Housing Loan Restructuring and Reclassification Program, qualified borrower-applicants may opt to restructure and reclassify their current housing loan accounts despite having availed of the benefits of a previous condonation program.
