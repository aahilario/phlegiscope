HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01208
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROTECT AND PROMOTE THE HEALTH OF THE PEOPLE THROUGH THE PREVENTION, CONTROL AND ABATEMENT OF AIR POLLUTION CAUSED BY SMOKE-BELCHING VEHICLES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1181, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill imposes an additional requirement prior to the registration or renewal a vehicle's registration. The requirement of subjecting the vehicle to a smoke belching test will certainly minimize, if not totally eradicate, air pollution caused by smoke-belching vehicles.
