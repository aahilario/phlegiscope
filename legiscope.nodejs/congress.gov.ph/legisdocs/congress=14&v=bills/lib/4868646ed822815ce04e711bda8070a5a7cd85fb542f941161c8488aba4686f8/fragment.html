HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01002
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ABOLISHING THE PRESIDENTIAL COMMISSION ON GOOD GOVERNMENT (PCGG)
ABSTRACT : The bill abolishes the Presidential Commission on Good Government and transfers its powers and functions of investigation and prosecution of criminal and civl cases to the Office of the Special Prosecutor and the Ombudsman.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : PUENTEVELLA, MONICO O.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-10
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
