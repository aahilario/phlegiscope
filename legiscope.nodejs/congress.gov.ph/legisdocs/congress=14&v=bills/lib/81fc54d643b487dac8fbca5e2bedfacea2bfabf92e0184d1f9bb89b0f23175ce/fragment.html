HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02281
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE FILING OF A CERTIFICATE OF CANDIDACY FOR ANOTHER OFFICE A VOLUNTARY RENUNCIATION OF THE ELECTIVE OFFICE PRESENTLY HELD
ABSTRACT : The proposed bill seeks to declare any elective official, whether national or local, running for any public office than the one which he is holding in a permanent capacity as ipso facto resigned from his office upon the filing of the certificate of candidacy.
