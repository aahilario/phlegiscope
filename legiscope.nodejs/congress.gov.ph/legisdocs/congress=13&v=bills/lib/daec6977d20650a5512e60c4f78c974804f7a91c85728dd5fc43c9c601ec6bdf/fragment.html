HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01732
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN SECTIONS OF THE PHILIPPINE MERCHANT MARINE OFFICERS LAW
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the use of naval vessels, which are not engaged in emergencies, for the training of future deck officers, engineers and crewmen apart from the traditional vessels used during apprenticeship training.
