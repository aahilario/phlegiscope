House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01883
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE CAPABILITY OF THE BUREAU OF FOOD AND DRUGS (BFAD) BY ESTABLISHING ADEQUATE TESTING LABORATORIES AND FIELD OFFICES IN ALL REGIONS, UPGRADING ITS EQUIPMENT, CREATING A DRUG ENFORECEMENT UNIT (DEU) UNDER ITS OPERATIONAL AND ADMINSTRATIVE CONTROL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : BFAD Empowerment Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "BFAD Empowerment Act of 2004." This bill strengthens the Bureau of Food and Drugs (BFAD) by upgrading its equipment, creating a drug enfocement unit (DEU) under its operational and administrative control and by establishing adequate testing laboratories and field offices in all provinces and cities throughout the country.
