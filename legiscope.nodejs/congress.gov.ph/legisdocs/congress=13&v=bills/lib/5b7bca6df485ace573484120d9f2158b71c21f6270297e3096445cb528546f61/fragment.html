House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01015
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPOSING STIFFER PENALTIES TO PERSONS CAUGHT DRIVING A MOTOR VEHICLE WHILE UNDER THE INFLUENCE OF ALCOHOL/LIQUOR AND/OR PROHIBITED DRUGS
SHORT TITLE : Deadly Driver Reduction Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Deadly Driver Reduction Act of 2004." The bill imposes stiffer penalties for driving under the influence of liquor or prohibited drugs ranging from prision correccional in its minimum term and a fine of PhP20,000 to prision correccional in its maximum term and a fine of PhP80,000.
