House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04679
FULL TITLE : AN ACT SEGREGATING FROM STATE COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES IN THE COUNTRY PORTIONS OF LAND THAT ARE IN EXCESS OF THEIR ACTUAL USE, NEEDS AND REQUIREMENTS
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the segregation of lands found to be in excess of the actual use, need and requirement of state universities and colleges for distribution to the actual tillers or occupants therein.
