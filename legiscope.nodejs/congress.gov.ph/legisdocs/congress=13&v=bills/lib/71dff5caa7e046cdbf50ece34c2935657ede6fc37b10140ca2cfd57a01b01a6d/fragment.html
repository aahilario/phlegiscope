House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01613
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE MANDATORY SUBMISSION OF THE ANNUAL BUDGETS OF GOVERNMENT-OWNED AND/OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS TO THE CONGRESS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for all government-owned and controlled corporations (GOCCs) to submit their respective annual budgets to the Congress for approval within thirty days from the opening of every regular session. It further stipulates that GOCCs shall not incur any obligation or make any payment for current operating or capital expenditures without a budget approval by Congress and the President.
