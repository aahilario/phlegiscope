HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02742
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CONSTITUTING LOCAL TREASURERS AS AGENTS OF THE COMMISSIONER OF INTERNAL REVENUE, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE SECTION 12 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE, PROVIDING FOR AN INCENTIVE SCHEME FOR THE COLLECTION BY LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS OF NATIONAL TAXES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes local treasurers to collect national internal revenue taxes for remittance to the National Treasury. As an incentive, it allows local government units to retain 50% of the collection target excess.
