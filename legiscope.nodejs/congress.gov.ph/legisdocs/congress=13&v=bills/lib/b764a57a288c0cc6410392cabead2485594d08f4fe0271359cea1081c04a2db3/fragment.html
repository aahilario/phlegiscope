House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01444
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MANDATORY AND CONTINUING HUMAN RIGHTS EDUCATION FOR ALL BARANGAY TANODS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Mandatory and Continuing Human Rights Education Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Mandatory and Continuing Human Rights Education Act of 2004." The bill mandates the Commission on Human Rights to conduct regular education for barangay tanods about the Bill of Rights, Rules of Court on Arrest, and other laws on human rights.
