HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01802
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6981 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "WITNESS PROTECTION, SECURITY AND BENEFIT ACT" AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill entitles witnesses admitted into the program: a) hospitalization benefits while in the safehouse; 2) minor or dependent children free public education, from primary to college. It also seeks to explicitly provide for the perpetuation of testimonies of witnesses in consonance with Rules 134 of the Revised Rules of Court.
