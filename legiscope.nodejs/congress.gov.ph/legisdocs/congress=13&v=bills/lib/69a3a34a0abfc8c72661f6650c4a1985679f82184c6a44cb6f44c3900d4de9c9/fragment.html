HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00125
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO FURTHER STRENGTHEN CONSUMER PROTECTION BY AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7394, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CONSUMER ACT OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to make the National Consumer Affairs Council (NCAC) a truly independent body. By placing the NCAC under the Office of the President, it shall have the power to prepare its annual budget, create other positions necessary to pursue its mandate and assume the authority to represent consumers in their claims and complaints involving violations of the Consumer Act.
