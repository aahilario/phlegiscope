House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00621
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING REFORMS IN LAND ADMINISTRATION
SHORT TITLE : Land Administration Reform Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Land Administration Reform Act of 2004". Creates the Land Administration Authority under the Office of the President to conduct, integrate, regulate geodetic and geophysical surveys, land classification, mapping, aerial photography, etc. and/or dispose alienable and disposable lands under Commonwealth Act No. 141 or the Public Land Act.
