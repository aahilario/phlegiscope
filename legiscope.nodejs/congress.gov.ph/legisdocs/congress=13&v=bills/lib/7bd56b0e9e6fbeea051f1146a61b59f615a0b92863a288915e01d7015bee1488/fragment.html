HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03439
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING OLD-AGE PENSION FOR LIFE TO SENIOR CITIZENS WHO RETIRED UNDER REPUBLIC ACT 1616 AS WELL AS SURVIVORSHIP BENEFITS TO THEIR SURVIVORS
ABSTRACT : The bill allows retirees who retired under RA 1616 and who is qualified to retire under RA 660 or PD 1146, to automatically convert his gratuity retirement to a pension system and thereafter receive the old-age pension for life as well as survivorship benefits for survivors.
