HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02023
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING INCENTIVES FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF MACHINES AND TECHNOLOGY THAT WILL IMPROVE THE PRODUCTIVITY OF RURAL WOMEN WORKERS
ABSTRACT : To encourage the development of machines and technology that would enhance the productivity of women in rural areas, the bill proposes the grant of the following incentives: a) financial grant for the fabrication of models of the machines; b) exemption from all taxes, duties and fees; and c) collateral-free loans from government banks.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ECLEO, GLENDA B.
DATE FILED : 2007-08-13
