HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01978
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE SALE THROUGH PUBLIC AUCTION OF ALL KINDS OF CONFISCATED SMUGGLED GOODS AND PRODUCTS AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill bans the Bureau of Customs from selling confiscated goods and penalizes violations with one to six years imprisonment and a fine of P10,000 to P100,000. It proposes the burning and destruction of smuggled goods.
