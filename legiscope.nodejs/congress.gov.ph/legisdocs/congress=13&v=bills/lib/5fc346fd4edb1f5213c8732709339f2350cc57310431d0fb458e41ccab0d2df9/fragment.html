HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01596
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THAT AT LEAST TEN PERCENT (10%) OF GOVERNMENT-OWNED LANDS TO BE SOLD, ALIENATED OR OTHERWISE ENCUMBERED FOR DEVELOPMENT PURPOSES, BE SEGREGATED FOR SOCIALIZED HOUSING PROJECTS
ABSTRACT : The bill requires the setting aside for socialized housing projects for the underprivileged at least 10% of the government-owned lands that are to be disposed of for purposes of developing "growth centers."
