FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING ADDITIONAL RETIREMENT BENEFITS TO MEMBERS OF THE JUDICIARY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill grants retired justices and judges an automatic increase in their pension whenever incumbent judges receive in increase in their salaries.
