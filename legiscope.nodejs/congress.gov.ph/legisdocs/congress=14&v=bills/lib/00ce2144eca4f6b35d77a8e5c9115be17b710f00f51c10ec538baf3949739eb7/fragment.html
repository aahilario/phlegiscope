HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03075
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING UNAUTHORIZED INTERCEPTION AND RECEPTION OF CABLE TELEVISION SERVICES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Cable Tapping Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes the penalty of imprisonment of not less than six (6) months and not more than one (1) year or a fine of not less than P5,000 and not more than P10,000 or both for the unauthorized tapping of cable services.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SANTIAGO, NARCISO III D.
