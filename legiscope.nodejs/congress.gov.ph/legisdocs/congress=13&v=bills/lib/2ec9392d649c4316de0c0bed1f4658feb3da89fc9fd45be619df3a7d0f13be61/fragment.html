FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMOTING EXPLORATION, DISCOVERY AND PRODUCTION OF INDIGENOUS OIL AND GAS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Oil and Gas Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Oil and Gas Act of 2004." The bill seeks to revisit the fiscal incentives and contractual arrangements for petroleum exploration and projects in order to attract more investments in petroleum exploration activities.
