House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02101
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LOWERING THE RETIREMENT AGE OF GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE R.A. 8291, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED GSIS ACT OF 1997
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to lower the age for compulsory retirement to 57 years of age provided he/she has rendered at least fifteen (15) years of government service.
