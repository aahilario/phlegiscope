HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02074
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE TO AUTOMATICALLY CONSIDER A TEN (10) WHEELER TRUCK DRIVER AS CONVERTED TO A FOURTEEN (14) WHEELER TRUCK DRIVER OF A TRI-AXLE CARGO VEHICLE
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Land Transportation Office and other concerned agencies to formulate, promulgate and implement clear regulation to upgrade the license of ten (10) wheeler truck drivers to fourteen (14) wheeler truck drivers.
