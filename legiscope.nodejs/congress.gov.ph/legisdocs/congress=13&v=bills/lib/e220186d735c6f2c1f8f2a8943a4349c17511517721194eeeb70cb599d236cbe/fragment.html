FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO INSTITUTIONALIZE SKILLS DEVELOPMENT PLANNING AT THE LOCAL LEVEL, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Institutionalization of Local Skills Development Planning Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Institutionalization of Local Skills Development Planning Act of 2004." The bill mandates the local government units to prepare a skills development plan to address the skill development activities in their respective localities.
