HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03113
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING REGULATORY AND FRANCHISING POWERS TO CITIES AND MUNICIPALITIES OVER OPERATIONS OF PUBLIC UTILITY VEHICLES PLYING WITHIN THEIR TERRITORIAL JURISDICTION, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO.7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, AS AMENDED, AND OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill grants the sangguniang bayan and panlungsod the authority to regulate the operations of buses, taxis, jeepneys and tricycles and to grant franchises for their operation within their respective territorial jurisdications.
