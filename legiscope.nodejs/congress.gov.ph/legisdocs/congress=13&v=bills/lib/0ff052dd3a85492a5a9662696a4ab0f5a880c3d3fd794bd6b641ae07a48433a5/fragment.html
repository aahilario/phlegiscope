House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00681
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING THE TAX ON WINNINGS AND DOCUMENTARY STAMP TAX ON HORSE RACING, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS 126 AND 190 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED, SECTIONS 11 AND 13 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8407 AND SECTIONS 8 AND 10 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7953
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes a four percent (4%) tax to winners of the following horse racing events: Daily double, extra double, forecast, double quinella, trifecta, daily double + 1, and pari-mutuel. Imposes a 10 centavos documentary stamp on every P1.00 horse race ticket.
