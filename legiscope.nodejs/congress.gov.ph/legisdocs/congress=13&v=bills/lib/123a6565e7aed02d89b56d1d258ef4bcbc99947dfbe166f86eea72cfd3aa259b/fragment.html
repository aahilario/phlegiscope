House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02893
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR TAX AND OTHER FINANCIAL INCENTIVES TO OWNERS OF AND INVESTORS IN BUSINESSES ENGAGED IN THE EXPLORATION, DEVELOPMENT AND OPERATIONS OF WIND ENERGY
SHORT TITLE : Wind Energy Investors' Incentives Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Wind Energy Investors' Incentives Act of 2004." The bill grants the owners, developers, investors and operators of wind energy business the following tax incentives: a) exemption from the payment of customs duties on imported equipment, machinery; vehicles and spare part; b) income tax exemption for the first five years of operations; and c) accelerated depreciation of the assets of the business.
