House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01222
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE DEPARTMENT OF TOURISM TO PROVIDE AT LEAST ONE (1) TOURISM OFFICE IN EVERY MUNICIPALITY AND CITY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill aims to provide in every municipality and city one (1) Tourism Officer who would promote the economic, social and cultural development of their respective areas through income generating tourism promotions and development programs and projects.
