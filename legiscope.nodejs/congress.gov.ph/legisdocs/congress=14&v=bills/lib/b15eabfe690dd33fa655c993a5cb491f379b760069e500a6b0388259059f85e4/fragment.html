HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01283
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RECOGNIZING THE POTENTIALS OF THE FILIPINO YOUTH TOWARD NATION-BUILDING, PRESCRIBING THE FREE EXERCISE OF CERTAIN RIGHTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Magna Carta for Youth"
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta for Youth". The bill guarantees the following rights of the youth: a) access to labor and employment; b) access to education; c) academic freedom; d) access to health care and services; e) free expression and information; f) organization and free assemly; g) participation in policy formulation; h) freedom of thought, conscience and religion; and i) political repression.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CHIPECO, JUSTIN MARC SB.
