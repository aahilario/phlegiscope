HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04842
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING A CHILDREN'S WELFARE FUND FOR THE PROTECTION AND REHABILITATION OF ABANDONED, ABUSED AND SEXUALLY EXPLOITED CHILDREN, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 7610, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SPECIAL PROTECTION OF CHILDREN AGAINST ABUSE, EXPLOITATION AND DISCRIMINATION ACT
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks the creation of a Children's Welfare Fund to be used for the establishment, operation and maintenance of facilities necessary for the rehabilitation of abandoned, abused or sexually-abused children, to provide basic education and to assist in curtailing all forms of child abuse.
