HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00523
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RENAMING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE (PNP) RANKS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTY-NINE HUNDRED AND SEVENTY-FIVE, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT ACT OF 1990'
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to clarify the common confusion brought about by the change in the classification of different ranks in the Philippine National Police as provided for under RA 6975 that has resulted to an alarming, if not ridiculous, improper identification of the officers.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : VICENCIO, ROMUALDO T.
