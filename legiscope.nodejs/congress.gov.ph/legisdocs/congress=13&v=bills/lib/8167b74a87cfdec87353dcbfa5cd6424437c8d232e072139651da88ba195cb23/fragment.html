House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02711
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ANNUAL AUTOMATIC INCREASE IN THE AMOUNT OF PENSION PAID TO ALL RETIRED GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure grants retired government employees an automatic yearly increase in their pension based on the annual rate of increase of the national cost of living index.
