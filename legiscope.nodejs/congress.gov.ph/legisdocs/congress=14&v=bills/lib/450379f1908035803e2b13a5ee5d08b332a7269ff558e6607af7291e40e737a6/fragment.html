HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03202
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING BENEFITS TO DEPENDENTS OF PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBER FORTY-SIX HUNDRED SEVENTY (R.A. NO. 4670) OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE MAGNA CARTA FOR PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to grant additional benefits to public school teachers by giving their dependents the right to scholarship grants, free medical treatment and pension in case of disability of the teachers.
