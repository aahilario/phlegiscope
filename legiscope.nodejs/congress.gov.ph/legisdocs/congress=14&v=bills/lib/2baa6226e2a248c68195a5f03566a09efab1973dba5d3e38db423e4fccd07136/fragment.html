House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01025
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MAINTAINING THE PRESENT PROVISION ON PROBATIONARY EMPLOYMENT INCREASING AT THE SAME TIME THE GOVERNMENTS PROTECTION TO PROBATIONARY EMPLOYEES, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE ART 281 OF THE LABOR CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the regularization of a probationary employee who is allowed to work after his probationary period, whether broken or not.
