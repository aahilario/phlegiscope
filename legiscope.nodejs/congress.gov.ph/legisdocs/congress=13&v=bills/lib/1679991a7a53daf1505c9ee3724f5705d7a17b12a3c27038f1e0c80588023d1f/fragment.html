HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00413
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE PROMOTION OF BIO-ORGANIC FARMING IN THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The adoption of bio-organic farming methods not only helps to reduce pollution and abate the destruction of useful organisms for the agricultural ecosystem, it also helps to solve the problem of waste and garbage disposal through the use of plant and animal waste and crop residues.
