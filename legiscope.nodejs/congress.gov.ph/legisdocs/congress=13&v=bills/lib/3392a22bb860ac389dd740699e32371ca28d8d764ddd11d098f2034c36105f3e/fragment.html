HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03629
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 5, PARAGRAPH D OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 449 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure authorizes the barangay to conduct and operate a "tupada" or cockfight during barangay fiestas. The entire proceeds of the "tupada" shall accrue to the barangay treasury to fund the activities of the barangay.
