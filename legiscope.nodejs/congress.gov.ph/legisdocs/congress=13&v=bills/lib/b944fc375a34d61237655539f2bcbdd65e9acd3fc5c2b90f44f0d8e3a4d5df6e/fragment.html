House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00445
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE PUBLICATION OF LEWD PHOTOGRAPHS AND SEX STORIES AND ARTICLES IN TABLOID AND BROADSHEET NEWSPAPERS, PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to prohibit the publication of lewd photographs and articles in tabloids and broadsheet newspapers. Lewd materials should be kept away from the easy perusal and consumption of the yuoung to ensure a sane and sexually and morally-sound citizenry.
