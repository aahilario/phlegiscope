House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00296
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE SOLAR ENERGY DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY DEFINING ITS POWERS AND DUTIES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill creates the Authority that shall undertake researches on and promote the development and utilization of solar energy as an alternative to oil, hydroelectric and geothermal energy.
