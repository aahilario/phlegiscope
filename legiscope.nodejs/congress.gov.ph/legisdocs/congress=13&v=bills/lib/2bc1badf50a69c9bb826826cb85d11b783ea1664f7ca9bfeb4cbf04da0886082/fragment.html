HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01708
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REPEALING THE PROVISIONS ON VAGRANCY AND AMENDING THE LAW ON PROSTITUTION AS DEFINED AND PENALIZED IN ARTICLE 202 OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to decriminalize vagrancy, to redefine prostitution to include men, and to change the penalty for prostitution from arresto menor to destierro or banishment to enable prostitutes to start a new life. However, it proposes to impose stricter penalties for recidivist prostitutes.
