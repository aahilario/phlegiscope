HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01002
FULL TITLE : AN ACT UPGRADING THE SALARY GRADE LEVEL OF THE SCHOOLS DISTRICT SUPERVISORS FROM SALARY GRADE NINETEEN TO SALARY GRADE TWENTY-FOUR
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to correct the inequity in the present compensation scheme whereby Schools District Supervisors receive lower salaries than their underlings, the School Heads or Principals. It proposes to upgrade the salaries of District Supervisors from salary grade 19 to grade 24.
