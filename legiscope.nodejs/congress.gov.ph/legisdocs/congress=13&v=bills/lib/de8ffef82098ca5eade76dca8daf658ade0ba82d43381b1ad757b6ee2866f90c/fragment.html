HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02377
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LIFTING UNREGISTERED IMPORTED DRUG PRODUCT FROM THE CLASSIFICATION OF COUNTERFEIT DRUGS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE SPECIAL LAW ON COUNTERFEIT DRUGS
ABSTRACT : RA 8203 classifies imported medicines that are not registered with the Bureau of Foods and Drugs as counterfeit drugs and consequently bans their entry into the country. The bill seeks to lift unregistered imported medicines from the classification of counterfeit drugs to allow the entry of cheaper imported medicines.
