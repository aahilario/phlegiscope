House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01366
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE DEPARTMENT OF CULTURE, ARTS AND NATIONAL HERITAGE DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Department of Culture and National Heritage Act
ABSTRACT : "Department of Culture and National Heritage Act." The proposed department shall be mandated to: a) encourage the continuing development of our culture; b) conserve and promote the nation's historical and cultural heritage and resources; c) promote and disseminate patronage of our national culture and heritage; and d) preserve and integrate indigenous traditional culture into the mainstream of the dynamic culture of Filipinos.
