House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03195
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE DIRECT REMITTANCE TO THE HOST LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNIT OF ITS FORTY PERCENT (40%) SHARE OF THE GROSS COLLECTION DERIVED BY THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT FROM NATIONAL WEALTH TAXES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 293 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
SHORT TITLE : Providing for the Direct Remittance to the Host Local Government Unit of Its Share of the Gross Collection Dervied From National Wealth Taxes
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates any person engaged in the utilization and development of national wealth to remit 40% of the gross collection derived from national wealth taxes directly to the local government unit and the remaining 60% to the national government.
