House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01064
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THREE THOUSAND PESOS (P3,000.00) ACROSS-THE-BOARD MONTHLY SALARY INCREASE IN THE WAGE AND SALARY RATES OF EMPLOYEES AND WORKERS IN THE PUBLIC SECTOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to grant an across-the-board-increase of PhP3,000 to all employees, whether permanent, temporary, emergency, contractual or casual, of the national government, its agencies, and instrumentalities including local government units.
