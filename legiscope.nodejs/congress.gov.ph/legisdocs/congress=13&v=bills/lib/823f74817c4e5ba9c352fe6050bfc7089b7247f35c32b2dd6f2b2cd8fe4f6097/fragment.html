House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00942
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A NATIONAL ECOTOURISM POLICY, ESTABLISHING A FRAMEWORK FOR ITS INSTITUTIONALIZATION, CREATING A MECHANISM FOR ITS IMPLEMENTATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Ecotourism Policy Act of 1998
ABSTRACT : "National Ecotourism Policy Act of 1998." This bill creates the National Ecotourism Commission to formulate and implement an ecotourism master plan together with the Department of Tourism and the Department of Environment and Natural Resources.
