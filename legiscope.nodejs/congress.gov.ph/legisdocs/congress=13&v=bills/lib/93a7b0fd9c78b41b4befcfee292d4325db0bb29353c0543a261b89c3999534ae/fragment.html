FULL TITLE : AN ACT POSTPONING THE ELECTION OF BARANGAY AND SANGGUNIAN KABATAAN OFFICIALS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 1 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9164
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the holding of the synchronized barangay and sangguniang kabataan elections on the last Monday of October 2006 and every three years thereafter.
