House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00067
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REWARDS FOR INFORMATION CONCERNING TERRORISM PROGRAM AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Rewards for Information Concerning Terrorism Act"
ABSTRACT : "Rewards for Information Concerning Terrorism Act". The bill seeks to provide a reward system for persons who provide information leading to the arrest or prosecution of terrorists or the prevention of terrorist acts.
