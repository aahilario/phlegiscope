HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02453
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN SECURITY OF TENURE AND PROMOTING REGULAR EMPLOYMENT AND FOR THAT PURPOSE, AMENDING CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 422
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to fix employment by the term of the project, task or work to be performed and not by a mere reference of time. It fixes the period of probationary employment to six (6) months.
