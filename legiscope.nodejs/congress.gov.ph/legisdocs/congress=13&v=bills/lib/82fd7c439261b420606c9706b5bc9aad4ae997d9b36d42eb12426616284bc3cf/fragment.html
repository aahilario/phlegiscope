House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00736
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING ALL PUBLIC AND PRIVATE HOSPITALS TO ESTABLISH AND MAINTAIN AN ISOLATION AND QUARANTINE FACILITY FOR INFECTIOUS DISEASES, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Hospital Isolation Facility Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Hospital Isolation Facility Act of 2004". To reduce, if not entirely contain, the spread of infectious diseases, the bill mandates all government and private hospitals to establish and maintain an isolation and quarantine facility for contagious diseases.
