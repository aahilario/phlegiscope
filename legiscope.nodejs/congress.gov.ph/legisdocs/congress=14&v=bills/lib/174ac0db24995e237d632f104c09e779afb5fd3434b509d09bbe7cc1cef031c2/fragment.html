HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00831
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE SECURITY AND PROTECTION OF VENDORS IN THE WORKPLACE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to provide security and protection to vendors in their workplaces. An Inter-Agency Council on the Security and Protection of Registered Vendors shall be created to formulate and implement policies and programs for registered vendors nationwide.
