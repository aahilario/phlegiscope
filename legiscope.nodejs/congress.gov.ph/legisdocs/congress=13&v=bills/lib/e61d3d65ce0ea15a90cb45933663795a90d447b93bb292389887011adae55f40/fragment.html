HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03517
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ENFORCE WARRANTIES ON THE SALE OF MOTOR VEHICLES, AND IMPOSING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill directs manufacturers of motor vehicles to fix the defects that are originally covered under the manufacturer's warranty and reported by the owner within the lemon rights period or certain mileage as reflected by the odometer of the vehicle.
