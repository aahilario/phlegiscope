HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03669
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCLUDING MEDICAL EXPENSES AS ALLOWABLE TAX DEDUCTIONS FOR INDIVIDUAL TAXPAYERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 34 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8424, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE TAX REFORM ACT OF 1997
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to include medical fees and medicines as allowable tax deduction from gross income provided that such expenses are supported by official receipts.
