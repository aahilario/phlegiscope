HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02745
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE CREATION OR ESTABLISHMENT OF A GOVERNMENT OWNED AND CONTROLLED CORPORATION (GOCC) AND/OR OF A SUBSIDIARY OF A GOCC TO BE BY ACT OF CONGRESS
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the creation of a government-owned or controlled corporation and/or its subsidiaries to pass through congressional action and their budgets to undergo congressional scrutiny.
