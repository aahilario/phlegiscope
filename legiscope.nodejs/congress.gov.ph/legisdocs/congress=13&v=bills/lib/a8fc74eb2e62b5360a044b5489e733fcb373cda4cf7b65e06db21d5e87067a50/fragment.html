House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01063
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A P125.00 DAILY ACROSS-THE-BOARD INCREASE IN THE SALARY RATES OF EMPLOYEES AND WORKERS IN THE PRIVATE SECTOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The P125.00 Daily Acroos-the-Board Wage Increase Act
ABSTRACT : "The P125.00 Daily Across-the-Board Wage Increase Act". To alleviate the living condition of the working class, the bill mandates all employers in the private sector, whether agriculture or non-agricultural, regardless of capitalization and number of employees, to pay their workers an across-the-board increase of P125.00 daily.
