HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03064
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING SIKARAN AS THE NATIONAL MARTIAL ART OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to declare sikaran as the national martial art of the Philippines. Sikaran-arnis is an ancient art of Filipino foot and stick fighting that promotes the virtues of self-discipline and control as well as a healthy and simple lifestyle for the holistic development of the Filipino individual.
