House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00367
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PRESCRIBE A GENERAL RULE IN THE ADVERTISEMENT AND/OR PROMOTION OF CIGARETTE AND/OR TOBACCO PRODUCTS AND ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES SOLD WITHIN THE PHILIPPINES, IMPOSE CORRESPONDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : General Advertising Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "General Advertising Act of 2004". The bill requires the advertisement of cigarettes, tobacco, liquor and beer not to: a) encourage the consumption or promote the increase consumption of the above products; b) depict the act of smoking or drinking; c) appeal directly to minors; d) advertise promotional schemes like "happy hour", among others.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : BARINAGA, ROSELLER L.
