HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00101
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A NATIONAL POLICY ON REPRODUCTIVE HEALTH AND POPULATION AND DEVELOPMENT, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The Reproductive Health And Population And Development Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “The Reproductive Health and Population and Development Act of 2010.” Recognizes the critical national reproductive health situation, the increasing demand of Filipino parents, couples, women, men and young adolescents themselves, as well as the policy gaps and the government’s responsibility to address the people’s reproductive health.
