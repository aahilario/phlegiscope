HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02622
FULL TITLE : THE EQUAL EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITY ACT OF 2004
ABSTRACT : The bill declares it unlawful for an employer, employment agency and labor organization to discriminate against any person on account of his/her sex, race, creed, age, appearance, sexual orientation, regional or ethnic origin, disability, weight or height.
