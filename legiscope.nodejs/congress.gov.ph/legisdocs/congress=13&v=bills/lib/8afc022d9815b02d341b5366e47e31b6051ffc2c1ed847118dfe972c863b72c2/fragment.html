HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00680
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ESTABLISH AND MAINTAIN AN OFFICE OF VETERANS AFFAIRS IN THE PHILIPPINE EMBASSY IN THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA, PRESCRIBING ITS FUNCTIONS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to institutionalize the Office of the Veterans Affairs (OVA) in the Philippine Embassy in Washington DC established on June 21, 1990 by the Department of Foreign Affairs. The OVA shall make representations, consultations and negotiations with appropriate agencies of the US government.
