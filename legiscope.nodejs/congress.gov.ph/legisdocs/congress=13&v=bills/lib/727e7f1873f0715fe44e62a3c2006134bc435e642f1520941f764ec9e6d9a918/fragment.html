HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02189
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE PENALTIES FOR THE OFFENSE OF BRIBERY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLES 210, 211 AND 212 OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the penalties for direct and indirect bribery and to impose the same penalties to bribe-givers.
