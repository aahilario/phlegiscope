House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01394
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 34 (h) OF THE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 4136 OR THEREBY REQUIRING THE MANDATORY COMPLIANCE BY ALL MOTORCYCLES OPERATORS, DRIVERS, RIDERS AND/OR PASSENGERS TO WEAR SAFETY HELMET
SHORT TITLE : Motorcycle Helmets Use Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Motorcycle Helmets Use Act of 2004." For the safety of motorcycle drivers and passengers, the bill provides for the mandatory use by motorists (drivers and passengers) of safety helmets of an approved type. The safety helmet shall be specifically designed to cushion and protect the rider's head from the impact of a crash.
