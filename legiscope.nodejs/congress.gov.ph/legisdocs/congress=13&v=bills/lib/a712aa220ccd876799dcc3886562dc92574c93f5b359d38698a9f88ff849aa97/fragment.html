House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03257
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING R.A. 9257 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE EXPANDED SENIOR CITIZENS ACT 0F 2003
SHORT TITLE : Revised Senior Citizen Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Revised Senior Citizen Act of 2004." The bill grants senior citizens a 20% discount for purchases made from all other food serving/selling establishments in addition to restaurants. It also entitles senior citizens to a 20% discount for the purchase of all kinds of medicines, whether prescribed or over-the-counter. It limits the extension of the discount privilege to senior citizens with an annual income not exceeding P100,000.
