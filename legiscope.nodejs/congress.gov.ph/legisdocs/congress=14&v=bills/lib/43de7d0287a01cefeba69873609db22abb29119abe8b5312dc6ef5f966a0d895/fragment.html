HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03007
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING AND PENALIZING THE UNAUTHORIZED USE, EXPLOITATION, INTER-CONNECTION WITH, OR RECEPTION OF ANY SIGNAL OR SERVICE OF EXISTING CABLE TELEVISION (CATV) OR CABLE INTERNET SYSTEM NETWORK AND PRESCRIBING PENALTIES THEREFORE
SHORT TITLE : "The Anti-Cable Television Internet Theft of Signal Service Act of 2006"
ABSTRACT : The measure imposes the penalty of imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 5 years or a fine ranging from P50,000 to P100,000 or both, for the unauthorized use, exploitation, inter-connection with or reception of any signal or service of existing cable television.
