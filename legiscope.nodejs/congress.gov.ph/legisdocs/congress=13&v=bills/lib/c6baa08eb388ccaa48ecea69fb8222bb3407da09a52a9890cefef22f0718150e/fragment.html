HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01362
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO RATIONALIZE THE ADMINISTRATION AND ENFORCEMENT OF ALL LAWS ON OCCUPATIONAL SAFETY AND HEALTH STANDARDS AND ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AND OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Under the present set-up, there are no less than 10 agencies involved in the implementation of occupational safety and health and environment protections laws. The measure designates the Department of Labor and Employment as the principal agency in coordinating and monitoring the government agencies involved in the implementation of all laws promoting occupation safety and health; and the Department of Environment and Natural Resources as the principal agency in the implementation of all laws on environmental protection.
