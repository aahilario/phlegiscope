HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01738
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FREE MEDICAL SERVICES TO FILIPINO VETERANS AND RETIRED GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL IN GOVERNMENT-OWNED AND OPERATED HOSPITALS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : As a gesture of gratitude for their past services and to reassure them of the government's support for them, the bill mandates all government-owned and operated hospitals to provide free medical services to all Filipino veterans and retired government employees.
