House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01028
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A SECURED AND SEPARATE PRISON CELL FOR FEMALE PRISONERS IN EVERY DISTRICT, CITY AND MUNICIPAL JAIL, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 63 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6975
ABSTRACT : There have been a number of reports of rape and sexual harassment of female prisoners by male prisoners. The bill provides for a secured, clean, safe, sanitary and separate jail for female prisoners in every district, city and municipal.
