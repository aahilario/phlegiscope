House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02731
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE MONTH OF FEBRUARY OF EVERY YEAR AS "LIVER CANCER AND HEPATITIS-B AWARENESS AND PREVENTION MONTH
SHORT TITLE : "The Liver Cancer and Hepatitis B Awareness and Prevention Month Act"
ABSTRACT : The measure designates the month of February of every year as the National Liver Cancer and Hepatitis B Awareness Month in order to promote public awareness and attention on liver cancer and hepatitis B.
