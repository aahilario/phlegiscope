HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00291
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL PUBLIC UTILITY AGENCIES AND COMPANIES OR CORPORATIONS, BOTH PUBLIC AND PRIVATE, TO REPAIR ANY DIGGING OR DESTRUCTION MADE IN THE COURSE OF THEIR INSTALLATION OR MAINTENANCE WORK
ABSTRACT : The bill requires the punong barangay to issue a certificate of repair work completion which shall be the basis for any future activities that any public utility company may venture into in the same barangay.
