House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01013
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE ESTABLISHMENT AND OPERATION OF HEALTH MAINTENANCE ORGANIZATIONS (HMOs), PROVIDING THEM INCENTIVES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Health Maintenance Organization Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Health Maintenance Organization Act of 2004." To make quality health care available through affordable health insurance policies, the bill grants HMOs incentives to encourage the private sector to participate in the government's program. It defines the regulatory framework for the operations of health maintenance organizations (HMOs). It delineates the fundamental rights of members and the health care providers.
