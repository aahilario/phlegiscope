House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04079
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE USE OF ELECTRIC POLES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Pole Regulation Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Pole Regulation Act of 2005". The bill empowers the National Telecommunications Commission to regulate and supervise the construction, use and maintenance of electric poles by cable television operators and providers in the country.
