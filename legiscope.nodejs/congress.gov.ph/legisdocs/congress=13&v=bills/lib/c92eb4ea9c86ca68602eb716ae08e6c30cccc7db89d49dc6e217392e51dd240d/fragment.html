House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02031
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE PUBLICATION, SALE, DISTRIBUTION, IMPORTATION AND EXHIBITION OF OBSCENE AND PORNOGRAPHIC MATERIALS AND THE PERFORMANCE OF EXHIBITION OF INDECENT ACTS IN PUBLIC AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR THE VIOLATION THEREOF AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Obscenity and Pornography Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Obscenity and Pornography Act of 2004." The measure prohibits the following acts: a) the publication, sale, distribution and importation of obscene and pornographic materials; b) the portrayal, depiction, performance and exhibition of any indecent act in public; and c) the coercion of another person to listen, read or view obscene and pornographic materials in public places.
