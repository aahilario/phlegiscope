House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00727
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING COMPULSORY COVERAGE TO BARANGAY DAY CARE WORKERS IN THE NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE PROGRAM, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6972 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to grant compulsory enrollment in the National Health Insurance Program (PhilHealth) barnagay day care workers and their dependents to enable them to receive and avail of an adequate package of essential health services.
