HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01907
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THAT AT LEAST TWENTY PER CENTUM (20%) OF THE GOVERNMENT-OWNED LAND TO BE SOLD, ALIENATED OR OTHERWISE ENCUMBERED FOR DEVELOPMENT PURPOSES, BE SEGREGATED FOR SOCIALIZED HOUSING PROJECTS
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to help the under privileged and homeless by setting for aside for socialized housing 20% of government lands intended for development.
