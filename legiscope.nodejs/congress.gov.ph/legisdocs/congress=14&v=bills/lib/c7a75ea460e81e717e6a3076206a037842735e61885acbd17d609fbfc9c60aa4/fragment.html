HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00110
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING GOVERNMENT HOSPITALS UNDER THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH (DOH) TO RETAIN AND UTILIZE ALL THEIR INCOME AND INTEREST DERIVED FROM DEPOSITS, FOR MAINTENANCE AND OTHER OPERATING EXPENSES, CAPITAL OUTLAYS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to give autonomy to government hospitals in the area of physical and operational development by granting them the authority to utilize their income for maintenance and other operating expenses.
