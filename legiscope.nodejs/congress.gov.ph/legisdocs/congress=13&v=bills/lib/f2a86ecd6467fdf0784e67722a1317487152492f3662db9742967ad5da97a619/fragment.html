House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04444
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING TREE FARMING IN PRIVATE LANDS OF THE PHILIPPINES, PROVIDING FOR PROTECTION, INCENTIVES, AND EFFECTIVE IMPLEMENTATION, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The Tree Farming in Private Lands Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "The Tree Farming in Private Lands Act of 2005". The bill seeks to adopt a deregulation policy whereby tree farm owners are given the right and freedom to develop their tree farms, and utilize and market their timber products free from bureaucratic red tape and restrictive regulations.
