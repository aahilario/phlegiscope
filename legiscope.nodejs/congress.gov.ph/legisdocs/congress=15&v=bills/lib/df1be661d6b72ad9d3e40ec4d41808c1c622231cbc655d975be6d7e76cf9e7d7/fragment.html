HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00259
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXPANDING AND STRENGHTENING THE PHILIPPINE RICE RESEARCH INSTITUTE AND RENAMING IT TO THE PHILIPPINE RICE AND CEREALS RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT INSTITUTE (PhilRice)
SHORT TITLE : The Philippine Rice And Cereals Research And Development Institute (philrice) Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : "The Philippine Rice and Cereals Research and Development Institute (PhilRice) Act of 2010" Creates the Philippine Cereal Research, Development and Extension Institute (PHILCEREAL), under the Department of Agriculture, which is authorized to collect certain percentage of the Competitiveness Enhancement Fund to finance its establishment and maintenance of research, development and extension facilities and equipment for cereals in the country.
