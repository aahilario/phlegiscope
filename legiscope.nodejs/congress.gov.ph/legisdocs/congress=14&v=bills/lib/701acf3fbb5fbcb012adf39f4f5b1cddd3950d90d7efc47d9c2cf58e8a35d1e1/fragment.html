HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01784
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE PENALTIES OR FINES IMPOSABLE AGAINST ERRING PUBLIC TELECOMMUNICATIONS ENTITIES FOR VIOLATION OF TERMS OR CONDITIONS OF ANY CERTIFICATE, AUTHORITY OR OF ANY ORDER, DECISION, RESOLUTION OR REGULATION OF THE NATIONAL TELECOMMUNICATIONS COMMISSIONS (NTC)
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the National Telecommunications Commission to impose fines against violators or against erring telecommunications firms of up to the maximum of P10 million per day for everyday during which such violation continues.
