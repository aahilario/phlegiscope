HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB06060
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE NATIONAL COMMITTEE ON THE PHILIPPINE CLAIM TO AN EXTENDED CONTINENTAL SHELF TO CONFORM WITH THE REQUIREMENTS OF THE UNITED NATIONS COMMISSION ON THE LIMITS OF THE CONTINENTAL SHELF (UNCLCS) PURSUANT TO THE PROVISIONS OF THE UNITED NATIONS CONVENTION ON THE LAW OF THE SEA
SHORT TITLE : "Extended Philippine Continental Shelf Law of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Extended Philippine Continental Shelf Law of 2007". The bill provides for the creation of the National Committee on the Philippine Claim to an Extended Continental Shelf which shal be responsible for the delineation, documentation and legitimization of the country's claim to an extended shelf to be submitted to the United Nations Commission on the Limits of the Continental Shelf.
