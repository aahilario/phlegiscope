HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00720
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FIXING THE RETIREMENT AGE OF THE UNIFORMED PERSONNEL OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTY-NINE HUNDRED AND SEVENTY FIVE OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT ACT OF 1990"
ABSTRACT : An early retirement age for our police officers is recommended to: a) give him longer time for his family; and b) provide the next-in-line police officers the opportunity to assume higher ranks. To attain these objectives, the bill seeks to reduce the compulsory retirement age for police officers and non-officers from 56 years old to 54 years old.
