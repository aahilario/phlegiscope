HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03226
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING INSTRUCTION IN THE PRINCIPLES AND STATE POLICIES OF THE FILIPINO PEOPLE, SET FORTH THE CONSTITUTION, IN THE SCHOOLS OF THE NATION, PUBLIC AND PRIVATE, COMMENCING IN THE THIRD GRADE AND CONTINUING THROUGH HIGH SCHOOL GRADUATION
SHORT TITLE : "The FilipinoPrinciples and State Policies Instruction Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the integration in the values education curricula of private and public schools the teaching of the principles and state policies of the Filipino people.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ZIALCITA, EDUARDO C.
DATE FILED : 2007-12-05
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
