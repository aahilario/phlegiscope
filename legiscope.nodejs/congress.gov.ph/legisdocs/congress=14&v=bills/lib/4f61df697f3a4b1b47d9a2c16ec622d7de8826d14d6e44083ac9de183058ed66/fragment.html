HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02012
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE ADDITIONAL AD VALOREM TAX ON IDLE LANDS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 236 OF THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to increase the ceiling of the ad valorem tax on idle lands from 5% to 10% of the assessed value of the property. The current maximum tax of 5% has not proven to be an effective deterrent against the non-utilization of agricultural lands for productive purposes.
