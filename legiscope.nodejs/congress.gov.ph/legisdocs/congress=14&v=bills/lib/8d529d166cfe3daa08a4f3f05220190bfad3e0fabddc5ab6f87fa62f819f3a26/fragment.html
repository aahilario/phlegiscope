HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02593
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL LABOR RELATIONS COMMISSION DEVELOPMENT FUND THEREBY AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLE 277AND PD 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the establishment of the National Labor Relations Commission (NLRC) Development Fund that shall be used to augment the allowances of the Commissioners and Labor Arbiters of the NLRC as provided under RA 9347.
