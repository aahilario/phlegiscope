HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02712
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9164 TO EXTEND THE TERMS OF OFFICE OF BARANGAY OFFICIALS AND RESET THE DATE OF BARANGAY ELECTIONS
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to hold a synchronized barangay and sangguniang kabataan (SK) elections on the 2nd Monday of May 2007 simultaneous with the local and national elections. Subsequent synchronized barangay and SK elections shall be held every three years thereafter.
