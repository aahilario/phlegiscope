HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02183
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING THE PHILIPPINE FILM AND MOVIE INDUSTRY FROM THE PAYMENT OF CERTAIN TAXES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure exempts producers of films classified as general patronage and parental guidance 13 from the payment of amusement tax imposed by local governments. It also exmpts producers from the payment of value-added tax on importation of raw materials and equipment to be used in making films.
