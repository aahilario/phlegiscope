HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03509
FULL TITLE : AN ACT BANNING THE RE-APPOINTMENT OF A REGULAR MEMBER OF THE JUDICIAL AND BAR COUNCIL (JBC) WHO HAS ALREADY SERVED THE FULL TERM
SHORT TITLE : "Judiciary Independence Enhancing Act of 2008"
ABSTRACT : The measure provides for the absolute disqualification from reappointment to the Judicial and Bar Council of a regular member whose term has expired.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : VINZONS-CHATO, LIWAYWAY
DATE FILED : 2008-02-05
