FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING TWO THOUSAND PESOS MONTHLY ALLOWANCE TO ALL BARANGAY TANODS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to grant a P2,000 monthly allowance to barangay tanods but not to exceed 20 barangay tanods in every barangay.
