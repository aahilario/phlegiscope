HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04549
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 187, 241, 247, 325, 330, 385, 416 AND 419 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 612, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE INSURANCE CODE, AS AMENDED BY PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1455
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to: a) set a time frame for the settlement or denial of insurance claims; b) impose stiffer sanctions against insurance and adjustment companies for delayed action on insurance claims; c) increase the jurisdictional amount of the Insurance Commission, and d) increase the penalties for violation of the Insurance Code.
