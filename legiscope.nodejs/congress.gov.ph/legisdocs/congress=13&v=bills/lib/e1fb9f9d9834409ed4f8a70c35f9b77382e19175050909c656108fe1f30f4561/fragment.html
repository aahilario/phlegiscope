HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00526
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 66 OF BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 881, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE OMNIBUS ELECTION CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : This bill requires that any person holding an appointive office or position, from the Department Secretary down to the Director level or their equivalent, who intends to run for an elective office, to resign from his position two (2) years prior to the next preceding election.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CASTRO, FRED H.
