House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00037
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STABILIZE THE SUGAR INDUSTRY BY ESTABLISHING THE SUGAR INDUSTRY RESEARCH AND STABILIZATION FUND AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Sugar Industry Stabilization Act of 1997"
ABSTRACT : "The Sugar Industry Stabilization Act of 1997". The bill allocates 20% of the total value-added tax collection from sugar and sugar by-products to the Sugar Industry Research and Stabilization Fund.
