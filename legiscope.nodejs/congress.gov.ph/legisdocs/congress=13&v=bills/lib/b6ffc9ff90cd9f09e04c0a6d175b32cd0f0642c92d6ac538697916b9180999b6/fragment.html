HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01959
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE RATE OF COMBAT DUTY PAY OF OFFICERS AND ENLISTED PERSONNEL OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill increases the combat duty pay of AFP pesonnel by 25% of the base pay of a private in the Army or its equivalent in the PAF or PN.
