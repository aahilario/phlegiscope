House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02699
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING EVERY LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNIT TO ESTABLISH A BREAST CARE CENTER AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Breast Care Center Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the establishment of a breast care center in every local government unit that will spearhead a campaign to raise public awareness on breast cancer and to provide health services to fight the disease.
