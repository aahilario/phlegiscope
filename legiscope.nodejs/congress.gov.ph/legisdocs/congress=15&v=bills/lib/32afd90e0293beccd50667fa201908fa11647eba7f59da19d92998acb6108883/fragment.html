House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00041
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CRISIS CENTER FOR STREET CHILDREN IN EVERY REGION OF THE PHILIPPINES, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : “Street Children Crisis Center Act of 2010.” Mandates the Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD) to extend assistance to street children through its “Lingap” (care) Centers which implements a program aimed at developing the children’s potentials and reviving their hope for a better future.
