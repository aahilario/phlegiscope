HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00145
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT 8239, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'PHILIPPINE PASSPORT ACT OF 2007' AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to: a) simplify pasport documentation requirements and processes; b) add grounds for its amendment, suspension and revocation; c) rally for the issuance of passports using tamper proof and the latest data encryption technology; and d) provide stiffer penalties against the perpetrators of the proliferation of fake passports.
