House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02796
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE DELINEATION OF THE SPECIFIC FOREST LIMITS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "final Forest Limits Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Department of Environment and Natural Resources to undertake an actual ground survey and verification of the specific forest limits and to clearly mark the boundaries on the ground by concrete monuments.
