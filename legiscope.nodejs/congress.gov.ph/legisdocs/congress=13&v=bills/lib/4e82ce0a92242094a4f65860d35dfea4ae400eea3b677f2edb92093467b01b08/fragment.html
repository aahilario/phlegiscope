House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01418
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXPANDING THE COVERAGE OF THE EMPLOYEES COMPENSATION AND STATE INSURANCE FUND TO INCLUDE RETIRED EMPLOYEES AGED SEVENTY (70) AND BELOW WHO SUBSEQUENTLY REACQUIRED EMPLOYMENT, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED
SHORT TITLE : Expanding the Coverage of the Employees Compensation and State Insurance Fund to Include Retired Employees Aged Seventy (70) and Below
ABSTRACT : Amends Article 168 of PD 442 (Labor Code) to include 70-year old employees in the coverage of the State Insurance Fund.
