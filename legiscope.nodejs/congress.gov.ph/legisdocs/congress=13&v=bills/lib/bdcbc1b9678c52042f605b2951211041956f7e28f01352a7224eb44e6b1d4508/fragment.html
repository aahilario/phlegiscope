House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03625
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A FAMILY COURT IN EVERY MUNICIPALITY WITH A POPULATION OF ONE HUNDRED THOUSAND OR MORE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE "THE FAMILY COURTS ACT OF 1997"
ABSTRACT : To expeditiously resolve domestic problems, the bill seeks to create a family court in every municipality with a population of 100,000 or more.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : AGARAO, BENJAMIN JR. C.
