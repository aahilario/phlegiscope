HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00396
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE FEDERATION NG MGA SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN THEREBY AMENDING SECTION 436 OF REPUBLIC ACT 7160 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to strengthen the Federation ng mga Sangguniang Kabataan by granting them autonomy in determining their goals and in implementing their programs. It also provides them with an annual budget and a national headquarters with a national secretariat. It further frees the national president of the SK from his/her responsibilities in the local councils so that he/she can attent to his/her studies and functions on a full-time basis.
