HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03267
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE PRODUCTION, DISTRIBUTION, SALE, COPYING, LEASE, SHOWING OR EXHIBITION, IMPORTATION AND EXPORTATION OF VIOLENT MOVIES AND THOSE GLORIFYING CRIMES OR CRIMINALS AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits and penalizes movies that cater to violence and glorify criminals. Penalties shall range from imprisonment of not less than 3 years and a fine of not less than P100,000 to imprisonment of not more than 7 years and a fine of not more than P500,000.
