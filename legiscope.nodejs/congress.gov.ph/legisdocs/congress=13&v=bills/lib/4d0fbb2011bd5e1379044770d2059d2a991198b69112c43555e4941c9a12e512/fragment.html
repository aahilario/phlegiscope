FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE PENALTY FOR THE KILLING, WOUNDING OR TAKING AWAY OF THE EAGLE AND/OR DESTROYING, DISTURBING OR TAKING AWAY OF THE BIRD'S NESTS OR EGGS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION FIVE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTY-ONE HUNDRED AND FORTY-SEVEN
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes a penalty of prision mayor or a fine of not less than P10,000 nor more than P15,000 or both for any violation of the act.
