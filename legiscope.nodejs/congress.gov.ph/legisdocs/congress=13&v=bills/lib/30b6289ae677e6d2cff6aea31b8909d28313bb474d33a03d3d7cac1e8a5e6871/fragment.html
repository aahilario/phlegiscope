HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01549
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING PENSIONS UNDER THE SOCIAL SECURITY SYSTEM AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 12 OF THE SOCIAL SECURITY ACT OF 1997
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the minimum monthly pension of SSS members to P3,000 and to annually increase the same by P500 until the minimum monthly pension reaches P5,000.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CAGAS, DOUGLAS RA.
DATE FILED : 2004-07-21
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
