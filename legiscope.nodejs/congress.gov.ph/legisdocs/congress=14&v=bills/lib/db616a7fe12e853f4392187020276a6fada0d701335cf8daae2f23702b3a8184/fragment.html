HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02055
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MANNER AND DATE OF ELECTION OF SECTORAL REPRESENTATIVES TO THE LOCAL SANGGUNIANS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Local Sectoral Representation Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Local Sectoral Representation Act of 2007". The bill provides for 3 selected sectoral representatives each in the sangguniang panlalawigan, sangguniang bayan and the sangguniang panlungsod - 1 from the women sector, 1 from the workers and 1 from either of the following sectors: indigenous people and cultural communities; differently-abled persons, senior citizens, victims of calamities, children, and urban poor or any other sector as may be determined by the sangguniang concerned.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : DE GUZMAN, DEL R.
DATE FILED : 2007-08-14
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
