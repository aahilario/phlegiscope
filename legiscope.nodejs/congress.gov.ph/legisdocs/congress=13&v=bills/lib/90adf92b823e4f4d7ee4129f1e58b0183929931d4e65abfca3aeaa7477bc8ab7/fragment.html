House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03221
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING A NATIONAL LAND USE POLICY, PROVIDING THE IMPLEMENTING MECHANISMS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Land Use Act of the Philippines
ABSTRACT : "National Land Use Act of the Philippines." The bill seeks to arrest the deterioration of our finite land resources and provide a rational framework for sustainable land use planning which shall govern the use, allocation and management of land resources.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ROBERT "DODOT" JAWORSKI, JR.
