HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02188
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING REGULATORY POLICIES FOR THE HOUSEHOLD EMPLOYMENT INDUSTRY, ESTABLISHING STANDARDS OF PROTECTION AND PROMOTION OF THEIR WELFARE ND OF THEIR FAMILIES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PERTINENT PROVISIONS OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 442, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Magna Carta for Household Helpers" or "Batas Kasambahay"
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta for Household Helpers" or "Batas Kasambahay". The bill provides for the terms and conditions of employment of the kasambahay, i.e. minimum wage requirement, payment of 13 month pay, membership to the Social Security System and the Philhealth, working hours and days, and vacation leaves and other benefits.
