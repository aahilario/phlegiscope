HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02801
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE EXTENSION OF THE TERM OF FRANCHISE OF THE PHILIPPINE AMUSEMENT AND GAMING CORPORATION TO FIFTY (50) YEARS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 10 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1869 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS 'PAGCOR CHARTER'
ABSTRACT : In order to attract investors for the expansion and modernization program of the Philippine Gaming and Amusement Corporation (PAGCOR), the bill seeks to extend PAGCOR's franchise for another 50 years.
