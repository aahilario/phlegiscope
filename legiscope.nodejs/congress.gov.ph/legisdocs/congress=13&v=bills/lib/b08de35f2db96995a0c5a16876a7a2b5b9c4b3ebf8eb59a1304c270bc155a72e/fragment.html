HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01627
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REQUIRE LIQUOR MANUFACTURER AND/OR DISTRIBUTOR TO PUT ALL LIQUOR BOTTLE A WARNING LABEL THAT ALCOHOL INTAKE DURING PREGNANCY IS HAZARDOUS TO THE LIFE AND HEALTH OF THE UNBORN CHILD
ABSTRACT : The bill requires the printing on the label of all liquor bottles of a warning on the hazardous effect of alcohol intake on the life and health of the unborn. Penalties for violation shall range from PhP5,000.00 to PhP50,000.00.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : BARINAGA, ROSELLER L.
DATE FILED : 2004-07-26
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
