FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT 7722, ENTITLED "AN ACT CREATING THE COMMISSION ON HIGHER EDUCATION, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES", STRENGTHENING THE COMMISSION ON HIGHER EDUCATION (CHED), AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Higher Education Act of 1994"
ABSTRACT : "Higher Education Act of 1994". The bill seeks to reinforce, rationalize and further strengthen the Commission on Higher Education (CHEd) to enable the CHEd to properly and effectively discharge its functions and mission.
