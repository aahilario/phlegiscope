HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00207
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO UPGRADE THE POSITION OF SECRETARIES TO THE SANGGUNIAN, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 469 (A), OF CHAPTER 3, BOOK III, TITLE 5, ARTICLE 1 OF R.A. NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The functions, duties and responsibilities of the secretaries of the local sanggunians are vital to the program of local government units. This bill seeks to elevate the position of all secretaries to the sanggunians to an appropriate rank with a commensurate salary.
