House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00706
FULL TITLE : AN ACT BANNING THE IMPORTATION AND DUMPING OF ALL KINDS OF TOXIC WASTES AND MATERIALS INTO THE COUNTRY, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Toxic Wates and Materials Ban Act of 2003
ABSTRACT : "Toxic Wastes and Materials Ban Act of 2003'" The Philippines has been tagged as the toxic wastes "dumping capital" of the world. The measure seeks to implement a total ban on all kinds and forms of toxic wastes and materials for recycling or for any other use and purpose from entering the country. Likewise, revokes permits issued to any individual or corporation for the importation of toxic wastes and materials.
