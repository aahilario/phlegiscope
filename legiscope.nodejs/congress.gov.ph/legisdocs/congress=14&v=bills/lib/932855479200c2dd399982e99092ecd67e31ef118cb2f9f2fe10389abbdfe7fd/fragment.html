House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00645
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING THE COOPERATIVE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES TO BE KNOWN AS THE 'PHILIPPINE COOPERATIVE CODE OF 2005'
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine Cooperative Code of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Cooperative Code of 2005". The measure increases the members required to form a cooperative from 15 to 30 and raises the paid-up share capitalization to P30,000. It requires cooperatives to hire a bookkeeper. It prohibits newly organized co-ops from registering and operating as a multi-purpose cooperative.
