House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03782
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING HARBOR PILOTAGE SERVICES AND THE CONDUCT OF HARBOR PILOTS IN ALL PORTS IN THE PHILIPPINES AND RELATED PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Pilotage Services Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Pilotage Services Act of 2005." The bill seeks to institutionalize the prevailing practices in pilotage services, to rationalize pilotage fees and other charges related to pilotage services, and to define the legal relationship between harbor pilots and pilotage organizations.
