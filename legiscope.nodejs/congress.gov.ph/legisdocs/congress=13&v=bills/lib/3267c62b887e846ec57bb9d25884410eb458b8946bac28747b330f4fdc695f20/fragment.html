House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05313
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FORTY-TWO HUNDRED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE WIRETAPPING LAW
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the method of authentication of wiretapped materials to make them admissible as evidence in court. It also specifies which agency/ies of the government may be authorized to wiretap.
