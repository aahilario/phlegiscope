HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03540
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE EXEMPTION OF GOVERNMENT RAILWAYS FROM THE PAYMENT OF REAL PROPERTY TAX
SHORT TITLE : Government Railways Real Property Tax Exemption Act
ABSTRACT : "Government Railways Real Property Tax Exemption Act." The bill directs all provinces, cities and municipalities to exempt from the payment of real property tax all government railways operating within their jurisdictions. The exemption shall be deemed ipso facto revoked in the event the railways are privatized.
