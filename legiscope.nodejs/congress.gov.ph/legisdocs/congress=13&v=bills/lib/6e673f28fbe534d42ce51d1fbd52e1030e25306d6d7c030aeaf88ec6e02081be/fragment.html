HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01113
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS FROM COMPELLING THEIR GRADUATES TO TAKE REVIEW CLASSES AND/OR PRE-LICENSURE EXAMINATIONS OR REFRESHER COURSES THEREIN, AND PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to protect graduates of courses requiring board, bar or any licensing examination from being compelled to take review classes in the same school he graduated.
