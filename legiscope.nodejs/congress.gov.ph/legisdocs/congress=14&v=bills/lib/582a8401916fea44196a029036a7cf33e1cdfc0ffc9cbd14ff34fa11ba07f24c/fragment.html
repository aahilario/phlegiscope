House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00516
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING LOI 745 ON THE CREATION OF THE PHILIPPINE COMMISSION ON THE PROMOTION AND DEVELOPMENT OF SCUBA SPORTS & TECHNICAL DIVING AND PRESCRIBING THE PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "An Act Amending LOI 745 and Prescribing the Penalties Therefor"
ABSTRACT : "An Act Amending LOI 745 and Prescribing the Penalties Therefor". The bill provides standard basic dive rules to all levels/kinds of divers, regulate the sport and expand the powers of existing governmental agencies in order to further promote the sport and protect the safety of its enthusiasts through the formulation of policies and the imposition of sanctions and penalties for violations thereof.
