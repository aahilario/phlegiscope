FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE SPECIFIC TAX RATES IMPOSED ON ALCOHOL AND TOBACCO PRODUCTS AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS 141, 142, 143, 144 AND 145 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED
SHORT TITLE : Increasing the Specific Tax Rates Imposed on Alcohol and Tobacco Products
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to increase the excise tax on distilled spirits, wines, fermented liquor, tobacco products, cigars and cigarettes by 20%, to be adjusted by 3% on the 2nd and 3rd years from the date of the effectivity of the Act.
