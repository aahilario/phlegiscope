HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03681
FULL TITLE : AN ACT UPGRADING THE SALARY OF PHYSICIANS AND NURSES IN THE GOVERNMENT, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE "THE COMPENSATION AND POSITION CLASSIFICATION ACT OF 1989"
ABSTRACT : To enable them to cope with the present standard of living, the bill seeks to upgrade the salaries of government nurses from SGs 10-22 to SGs 12-24 and of government doctors from SGs 21-24 to SGs 23-26.
