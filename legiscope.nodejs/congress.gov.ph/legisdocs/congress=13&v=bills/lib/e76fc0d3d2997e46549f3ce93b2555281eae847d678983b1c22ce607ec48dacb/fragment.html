HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02691
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE IMPOSITION OF OR EXACTION OF ANY FEE FOR THE USE OF PARKING SPACES AND FACILITIES LOCATED WITHIN SHOPPING MALLS, HOTELS AND SIMILAR BUSINESS AND/OR COMMERCIAL ESTABLISHMENTS AND PRESCRIBING PENALTIES THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The measure prohibits the owners of shopping malls, hotels and other similar establishments from imposing, exacting, collecting and changing any and all fees for the use of parking spaces within their establishments.
