HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02127
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF BARANGAY DRUGSTORES, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS 'BOTICA SA BARANGAY' AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Botica sa Barangay Act"
ABSTRACT : "Botica sa Barangay Act". The bill directs the Department of Health to establish public drug stores in every barangay in the country and offer drugs included in the "core list" of the National Drug Formulary such as analgesics, anticonvulsants, anti-migraine and antipyretics.
