HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02551
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER INCREASING THE EXCISE TAX RATES IMPOSED ON ALCOHOL AND TOBACCO PRODUCTS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS 141, 142, 143, 144, 145 AND 288 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9334
ABSTRACT : The bill increases the tax on alcoholic beverages and tobacco products. It also allocates 2% of the incremental revenue generated to a trust fund for the rehabilitation of alcoholics.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ABANTE, BIENVENIDO JR. M.
