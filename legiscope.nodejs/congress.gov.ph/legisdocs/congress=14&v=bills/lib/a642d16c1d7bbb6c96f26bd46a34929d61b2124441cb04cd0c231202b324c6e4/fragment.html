HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00789
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING THE OPERATION OF GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, PROVIDING FOR THE UNIFORM COMPOSITION AND POWERS OF THEIR GOVERNING BOARDS, THE MANNER OF APPOINTMENT AND TERM OF OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OR GENERAL MANAGER AND OTHER SENIOR OFFICERS, PUTTING A CAP ON THEIR SALARIES AND ALLOWANCES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the composition, powers and functions of the Board of Directors of government owned and controlled corporations; the powers and duties of the General Manager, and the salaries and allowances of their officers. It provides for the creation of a Government Corporate Committee and its duties and functions.
