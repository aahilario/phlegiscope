House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04307
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE COMMISSION OF ACTS OF TORTURE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Torture Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Torture Act of 2005". The bill seeks to declare torture as a criminal act. It defines that acts of physical torture, mental/psychological torture and pharmacological torture and provides penalties therefor.
