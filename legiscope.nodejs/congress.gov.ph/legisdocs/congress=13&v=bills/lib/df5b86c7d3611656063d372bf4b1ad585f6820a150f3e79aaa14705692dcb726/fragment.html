HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02285
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING THE SALARIES OF THE DAY CARE TEACHERS/WORKERS WITH CORRESPONDING BENEFITS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : This bill provides for the inclusion in the regular General Appropriations Act an amount intended for the salaries and benefits of the Day Care Teachers and the Day Care Workers nationwide. The sum necessary for implementation of this Act shall be charged against the annual budget of the Department of Social Welfare and Development.
