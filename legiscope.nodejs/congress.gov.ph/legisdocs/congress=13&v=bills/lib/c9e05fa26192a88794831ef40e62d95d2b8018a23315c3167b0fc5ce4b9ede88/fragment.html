HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01757
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOCATING FIFTY PERCENT OF VALUE-ADDED TAX COLLECTIONS TO HEALTH AND EDUCATION SERVICES, AGRICULTURAL FACILITES AND SERVICES, HOUSING, ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION, AND OTHER SOCIAL SERVICES THEREBY AMENDING THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to allocate 50% of the total value-added tax collection for the following purposes: a) education - 25%; b) health - 25%; c) agricultural facilities and services - 25%; d) housing - 15%; e) environmental protection - 5%; and f) training and capability building programs - 5%.
