HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01288
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING ADDITIONAL SAFEGUARDS TO THE INTER-COUNTRY ADOPTION OF FILIPINO CHILDREN, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE INTER-COUNTRY ADOPTION ACT OF 1995
ABSTRACT : This bill prohibits MEMBERS OF THE BOARD, INCLUDING THE AGENCIES, INSTITUTIONS AND ORGANIZATIONS TO WHICH ANY MEMBER IS PRESENTLY OR WAS PREVIOUSLY ASSOCIATED, FROM RECEIVING MONEY, DONATION OR OTHER BENEFITS, DIRECT OR INDIRECTLY, BY REASON OF HIS OFFICE OF THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THE INTER-COUNTRY ADOPTION LAW.
