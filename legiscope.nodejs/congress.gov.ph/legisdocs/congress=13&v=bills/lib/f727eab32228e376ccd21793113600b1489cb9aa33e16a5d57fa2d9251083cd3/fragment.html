House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01357
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR AN OWWA CHARTER TO GOVERN THE OPERATION AND ADMINISTRATION OF THE OVERSEAS WORKERS WELFARE ADMINISTRATION, PRESCRIBE FOR PUBLIC ACCOUNTABILITY ON THE OWWA ADMINISTRATORS AND OFFICIALS THEREOF AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : OWWA Charter Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "OWWA Charter Act of 2004." To protect and promote the welfare of overseas Filipino workers, the bill seeks to provide for an OWWA Charter that will govern the operation and administration of the Overseas Workers Welfare Administration. It also prescribes for public accountability of the OWWA administrators and officials.
