HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04385
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MAKING THE CORPORATE SIGNATORIES AND OTHER CORPORATE OFFICERS LIABLE FOR BOUNCING CHECKS, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 22, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "BOUNCING CHECK LAW".
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to make the directors, officers and/or general manager of a corporation, aside from the person/s who signed the check, liable for bouncing checks.
