HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03422
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A PROGRAM ON FAMILY LIFE AND RESPONSIBLE PARENTHOOD FOR HELPING FAMILIES ACHIEVE THEIR DESIRED FAMILY SIZE AND ENHANCE THEIR FAMILY LIFE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Family Life Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Family Life Act of 2004." The measure establishes the Family Life Fund in the amount of P2.0 billion to be administered through the Family Life Program to help families achieve their desired family size.
