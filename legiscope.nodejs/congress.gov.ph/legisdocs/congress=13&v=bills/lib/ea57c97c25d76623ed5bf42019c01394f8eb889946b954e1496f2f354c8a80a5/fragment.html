HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00403
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS TO APPROPRIATE 10% OF THEIR DEVELOPMENT FUNDS TO SUPPORT THE SOCIAL REFORM AGENDA, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 324 OF THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The annual budgets of local government units are heavily focused on infrastructure development programs leaving social services and other development programs a second priority. This bill seeks to approximate an equal allotment of local government resources by requiring municipalities, cities and provinces to appropriate not less than 10% of annual development funds to support programs under the ambit of the Social Reform Agenda.
