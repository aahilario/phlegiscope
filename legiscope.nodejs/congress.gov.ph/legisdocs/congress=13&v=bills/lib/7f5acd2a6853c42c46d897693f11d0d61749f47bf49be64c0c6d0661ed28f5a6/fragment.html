HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00777
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPOSING PENALTIES ON ANY OWNER/OPERATOR OF A PUBLIC UTILITY VEHICLE VIOLATING THE OUT-OF-LINE OPERATION AND TAMPERING OF LICENSE PLATES, OFFICIAL RECEIPTS, CERTIFICATE OF REGISTRATION OF PUBLIC UTILITY VEHICLES AND/OR CERTIFICATE OF PUBLIC CONVENIENCE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill penalizes the owner/operator of a public utility vehicle (PUV) violating the out-of-line operation by revoking his Certificate of Public Convenience (CPC) issued by the LTFRB while the CPC of a PUV owner/operator found guilty of colorum operations shall be confiscated. It also provides that any person guilty of tampering with the license plate, official receipt and certificate of registration of PUVs shall be fined PhP50,000.00.
