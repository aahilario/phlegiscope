House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02730
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL FACILITY FOR IRRADIATION TECHNOLOGY AT THE PHILIPPINE NUCLEAR RESEARCH INSTITUTE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Irradiation Technology Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Irradiation Technology Act of 2004." The bill defines irradiation as a physical method of treating food and medical products by exposing them to gamma rays or electrons from radioisotopes and electron machine. The proposed facility shall be tasked to provide proper irradiation facility for commercial and large scale practical applications.
