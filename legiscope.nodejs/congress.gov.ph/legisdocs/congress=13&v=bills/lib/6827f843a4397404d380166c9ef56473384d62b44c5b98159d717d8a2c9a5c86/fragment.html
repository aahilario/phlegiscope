House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02276
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING THE OPEN LEARNING SYSTEM IN THE PHILIPPINES APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Open Learning System Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Open Learning System Act of 2004." This bill seeks to utilize the distance teaching/learning system where students shall not be required to regularly attend conventional classrooms, thus allowing these students to study while working in offices, factories, farms or other preoccupations.
