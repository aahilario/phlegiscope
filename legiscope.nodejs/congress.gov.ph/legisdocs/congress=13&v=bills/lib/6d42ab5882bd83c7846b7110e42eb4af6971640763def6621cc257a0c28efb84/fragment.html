House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00262
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 31 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6770, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS 'THE OMBUDSMAN ACT OF 1989,' AS AMENDED, ALLOWING PRIVATE LAWYERS TO ACT AS PROSECUTORS ON BEHALF OF THE OMBUDSMAN
ABSTRACT : The bill would give more teeth to the Office of the Ombudsman by allowing competent and well-trained private lawyers to act as prosecutors in cases involving former Presidents and Vice Presidents of the Philippines, incumbent and former cabinet secretaries and undersecretaires, among others.
