House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01853
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 290 AND 291 OF REPUBLIC ACT 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the share of local government units in the gross collection derived by the national government from mining taxes, royalties, forestry and fishery charges and such other taxes levied in the utilization and development of the national wealth from 40% to 60%.
