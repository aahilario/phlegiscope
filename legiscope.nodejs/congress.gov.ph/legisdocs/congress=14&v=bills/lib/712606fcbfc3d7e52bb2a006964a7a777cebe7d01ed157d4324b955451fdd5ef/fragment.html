House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03024
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE TRADING OF MINERALS AMENDING CHAPTER IX SECTION 54 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7942 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "PHILIPPINE MINING ACT OF 1995" AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill requires mineral traders to register with the Department of Trade and Industry and the Bureau of Internal Revenue and to establish an office covered with a business permit from the local government unit where the office is located.
