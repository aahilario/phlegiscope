House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00161
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CREDIT ASSISTANCE PROGRAM FOR OVERSEAS WORKERS
SHORT TITLE : "Overseas Workers Credit Assistance Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Overseas Workers Credit Assistance Act of 2007".The bill extends to an overseas Filipino worker, with a valid contract certified by the POEA, a PhP50,000 loan from the OWWA to defray the living expenses of his family during the first few months of his absence and his expenses incurred during the pre-employment period.
