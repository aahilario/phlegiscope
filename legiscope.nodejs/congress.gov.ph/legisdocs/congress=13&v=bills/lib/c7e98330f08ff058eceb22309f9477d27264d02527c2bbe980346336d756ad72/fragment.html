HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01961
FULL TITLE : AN ACT SEEKING TO IMPROVE CHILD PROTECTION AGAINST ABUSE, EXPLOITATION AND DISCRIMINATION, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7610, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7658, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "SPECIAL PROTECTION AND DISCRIMINATION ACT", APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill bans and penalizes the employment of children in all forms of hazardous occupations and aims for "greater participation of local social workers and law enforcers." Imposes penalties for child pornography through the internet. Mandates the creation of a central data bank to monitor abuses made by foreigners against children and prevent their reentry.
