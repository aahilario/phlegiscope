House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00696
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING THE TYPOLOGY OF PUBLIC HIGHER EDUCATION INSTITUTIONS AND FOR OTHER RELATED PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Public Higher Education Typology Act of 2001
ABSTRACT : "Public Higher Education Typology Act of 2001." The bill defines typology as the classification of state colleges and universities offering post-secondary, undergraduate, or post-graduate degree programs. It draws further divisions including agricultural/fisheries, technological/polytechnic, normal/teacher education.
