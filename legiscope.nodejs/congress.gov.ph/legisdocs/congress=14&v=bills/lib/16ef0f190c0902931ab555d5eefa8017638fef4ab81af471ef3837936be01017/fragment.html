House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02131
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER ENSURING THE USE OF GENERIC NAMES IN THE MANUFACTURE, PRESCRIPTION, AND DISTRIBUTION OF DRUGS AND MEDICINES AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CERTAIN SECTIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 6675, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "GENERICS ACT OF 1988", AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill creates the GenericDrug Board that shall be tasked to review, monitor and supervise the implementation of the Generic Act of 1988. It prescribes the composition and powers and functions of the Board.
