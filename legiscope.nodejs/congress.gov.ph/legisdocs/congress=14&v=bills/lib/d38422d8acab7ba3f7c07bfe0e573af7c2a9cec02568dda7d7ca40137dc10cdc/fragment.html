HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00027
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING EACH BUSINESS ENTITY, CORPORATION, FIRM, RETAIL OUTLET, ESTABLISHMENT AND ENTERPRISE IN THE PRIVATE SECTOR TO SHARE TEN PER CENT (10%) OF ITS ANNUAL NET PROFITS TO ITS RESPECTIVE EMPLOYEES AND WORKERS OVER AND ABOVE WHAT THEY RECEIVE AS WAGES AND OTHER BENEFITS PURSUANT TO EXISTING LAWS, ESTABLISHING THE MECHANISM AND PROPER STANDARDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Mandatory Profit Sharing Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Mandatory Profit Sharing Act of 2007". The measure mandates all private business entities to set aside 10% of their net profit as cash gift to be divided equally among their regular and non-regular employees.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : DOMOGAN, MAURICIO G.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-02
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
