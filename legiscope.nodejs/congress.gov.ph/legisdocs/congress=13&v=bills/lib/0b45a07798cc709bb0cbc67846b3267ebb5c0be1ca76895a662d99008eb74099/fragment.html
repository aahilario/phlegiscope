House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01282
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MODERNIZATION OF THE PHILIPPINE COAST GUARD AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Coast Guard Modernization Act
ABSTRACT : "Coast Guard Modernization Act." This bill aims to give the Philippine Coast Guard a fighting chance at being the best it can be by providing for the modernization of its equipment and training, and the allocation of the necessary funds for this purpose.
