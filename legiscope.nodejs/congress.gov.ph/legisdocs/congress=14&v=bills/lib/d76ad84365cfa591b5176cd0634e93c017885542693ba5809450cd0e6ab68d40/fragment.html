House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01790
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE COASTAL ENVIRONMENT PROGRAM, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Coastal Environment Program (CEP) Act of 2002"
ABSTRACT : "Coastal Environment Program (CEP) Act of 2002". The bill aims to promote the sustainable use of resources in coastal areas by encouraging the use of environment friendly technologies, provide livelihood oppottunites to coastal communities, promote equitable access to resources and build DENR capabilities in the management of coastal area.
