HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02134
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ABOLISH THE EXEMPTION GRANTED TO THE PHILIPPINE CHARITY SWEEPSTAKES OFFICE (PCSO) AND PHILIPPINE AMUSEMENT AND GAMING CORPORATION (PAGCOR) FROM PAYING CORPORATE INCOME TAX THEREBY, AMENDING SECTION 27(C) OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to revoke the corporate income tax exemption granted to the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office and the Philippine Gaming Corporation.
