HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03482
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING COMPENSABLE HOURLY RATE IN THE COMPUTATION OF STATUTORY MINIMUM WAGE OF EMPLOYEES AND WORKERS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Minimum Hourly Wage Rate Act of 2008"
ABSTRACT : The bill employers engaged in commerce or in the production of goods for commerce to pay their employees or workers a minimum hourly rate of not less than fifty (P50) pesos.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : LIM, RENO G.
DATE FILED : 2008-01-31
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
