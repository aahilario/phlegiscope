House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00018
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR ROUTINE HEPATITIS B IMMUNIZATION OF ALL INFANTS AT BIRTH
SHORT TITLE : "Newborn Hepatitis B Immunization Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Newborn Hepatitis B Immunization Act of 2007". The bill mandates the giving of routine Hepatitis B vaccination to all infants at birth in order to prevent the risk of acquiring the viral infection.
