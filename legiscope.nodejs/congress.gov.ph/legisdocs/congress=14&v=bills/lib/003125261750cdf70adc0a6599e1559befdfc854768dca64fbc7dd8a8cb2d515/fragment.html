House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03149
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CALLING FOR A CONSTITUTIONAL CONVENTION FOR CHANGING THE 1987 CONSTITUTION, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE
SHORT TITLE : "Constitutional Convention Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to constitute a constitutional convention for the purpose of amending the present Charter in order to make it more responsive and more relevant to the times.
