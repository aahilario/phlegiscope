House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02026
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE NAMING OF PUBLIC PROPERTIES AND GOVERNMENT SERVICES AFTER INCUMBENT ELECTED PUBLIC OFFICIALS, THEIR KIN, SPOUSES AND RELATIVES OF UP TO FOURTH CIVIL DEGREE OF CONSANGUINITY AND PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREOF AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill bans the naming of a public property, service or program after a public official or his/her relatives.
