HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02829
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE INCLUSION OF THE SUBJECT ON DRUG AWARENESS AND PREVENTION IN THE SCHOOL CURRICULA OF ALL ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY SCHOOLS
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to include in the curricula of public and private elementary and high schools students the subject of drug awareness. Knowledge of the harmful effects of dangerous drugs will discourage experimentation in and continued drug use.
