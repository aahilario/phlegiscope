House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04491
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR SELF-SUSTAINING FOREST MANAGEMENT PROGRAM, TO BE KNOWN AS TREE FOR LEGACY PROGRAM, BY GIVING ECONOMIC AND OTHER INCENTIVES TO TREE PLANTERS ON PRIVATE AND FORESTLANDS
SHORT TITLE : "Tree for Legacy Act of 2005."
ABSTRACT : The bill introduces a reforestation program (Tree for Legacy Program) covering private lands, protection forests, production forests, open public lands and other lands deemed appropriate for tree planting. The proposed program shall be administered by the local government units in partnership with the Department of Environment and Natural Resources.
