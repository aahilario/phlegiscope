HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00814
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR STIFF PENALTIES TO THE CRIME OF INFIDELITY OF PUBLIC OFFICERS IN THE CUSTODY OF PRISONERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLE 223 AND 224 OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for any public officer who shall consent or connive to the escape of a prisoner in his custody to be penalized with the unserved term of the prisoner who escaped if the fugitive shall have been sentenced by final judgment to any penalty.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SYJUCO, JUDY J.
