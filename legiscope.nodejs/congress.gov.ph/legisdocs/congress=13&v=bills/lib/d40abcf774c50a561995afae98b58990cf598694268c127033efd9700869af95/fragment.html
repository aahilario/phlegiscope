FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE LICENSURE AND THE PRACTICE OF MIDWIFERY IN THE PHILIPPINES, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7392, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE PHILIPPINE MIDWIFERY ACT OF 1992, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Midwifery Act of 2007
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Midwifery Act of 2007". The bill seeks to standardize, upgrade and regulate midwifery education; and to supervise, control and regulate the practice of midwifery.
