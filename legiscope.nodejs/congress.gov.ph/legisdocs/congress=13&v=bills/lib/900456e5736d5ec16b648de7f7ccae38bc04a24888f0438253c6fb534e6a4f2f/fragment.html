House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00450
FULL TITLE : AN ACT UPGRADING THE SALARY GRADE LEVEL OF THE SCHOOLS DISTRICT SUPERVISORS FROM SALARY GRADE NINETEEN (19) TO SALARY GRADE TWENTY-FOUR (24)
ABSTRACT : It is highly anomalous for a subordinate to receive a much higher compensation than his superior. This is exactly the case between Schools District Supervisors (SDS) and Principals or School Heads whose respective salary grades (SG) are SG 19 and SG 21. This bill seeks to correct the anomaly by upgrading the salary grade level of SDS from SG 19 to SG 24. This will give SDS compensation commensurate to their duties and responsibilities.
