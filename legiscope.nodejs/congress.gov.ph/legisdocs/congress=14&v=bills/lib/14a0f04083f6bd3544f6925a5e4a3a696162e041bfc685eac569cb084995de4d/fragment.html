HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00380
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ACCELERATE THE DEVELOPMENT OF ALL BARANGAYS BY PROVIDING FUNDS FOR MICRO ENTERPRISES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine Fund for Progress Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Fund for Progress Act of 2007". The bill creates an P80 billion Philippine Fund for Progress and allocates P2.0 million to each of the 40,000 barangays in the country for lending to deserving micro enterprises.
