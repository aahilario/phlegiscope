HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02691
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOWING LEGITIMATE CHILDREN TO BEAR THE SURNAME OF THE FATHER OR THE MOTHER, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE EXECUTIVE ORDER NUMBERED TWO-HUNDRED AND NINE (E.O. 209), AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE FAMILY CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, ARTICLE 174, PARAGRAPH 1
ABSTRACT : To avoid any confusion about his/her paternity in cases where the child uses the surname of the mother, the bill requires the father to indicate his consent thereto in the child's birth record, in a statement before a court of record or in any authentic writing.
