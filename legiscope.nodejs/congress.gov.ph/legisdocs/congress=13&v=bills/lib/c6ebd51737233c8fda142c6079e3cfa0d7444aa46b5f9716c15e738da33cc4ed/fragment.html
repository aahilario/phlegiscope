HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00767
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE PENALTIES IMPOSED FOR ILLEGAL GAMBLING AND A HIGHER PENALTY IF THE OFFENDER IS A POLICE OR MILITARY OFFICER, OR A LOCAL GOVERNMENT OFFICIAL, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1602
ABSTRACT : To deter gambling operations, the bill seeks to impose stiffer penalties to maintainers, financiers, employees and protectors of illegal gambling. The proposed penalties shall range from prision correccional in its maximum period to prision mayor in its maximum period and fines ranging from PhP50,00 to PhP500,000.
