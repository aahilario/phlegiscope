HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00775
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INVESTING IN ALL CITY AND MUNICIPAL MAYORS THE AUTHORITY TO EXERCISE EXCLUSIVE DIRECT OPERATIONAL SUPERVISION AND CONTROL OVER ALL CITY AND MUNICIPAL EMPLOYEES TASKED WITH THE DUTY OR FUNCTIONS OF ISSUING ALL KINDS OF CITY OR MUNICIPAL PERMITS AND LICENSES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The Ozone tragedy gave rise to the question as to who was responsible for the actuation of the Quezon City employees over the issuance of certain municipal permits/licenses to business establishments. To clarify the issue, the bill seeks to vest the issuance of all city and municipal permits and licenses under the exclusive direct operational supervision and control of their respective city or municipal mayor, who shall be directly responsible for any act of malfeasance, misfeasance nor non-feasance committed by city or municipal employees in the performance of their assigned tasks.
