HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04713
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 20 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY TO BE KNOWN AS THE NATIONAL CHILDREN'S DAY
SHORT TITLE : 'National Children's Day Act of 2005.'
ABSTRACT : "National Children's Day Act of 2005". The measure seeks to declare November 20 of every year as a special non-working holiday to be known as "The National Children's Day" in consonance with the United Nations General Assembly's ratification of the Convention on the Rights of the Child.
