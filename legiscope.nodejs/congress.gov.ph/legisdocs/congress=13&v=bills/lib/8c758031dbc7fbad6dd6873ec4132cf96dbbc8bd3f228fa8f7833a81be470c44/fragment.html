House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01360
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXPANDING COUNTRYSIDE CREDIT THROUGH COOPERATIVE BANKS AND ENCOURAGING THE GROWTH AND DEVELOPMENT OF COOPERATIVE BANKS BY AMENDING CHAPTER XIII OF THE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6938, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE COOPERATIVE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to strengthen the cooperative banking system, particularly in rural and remote areas. It extends to cooperative banks a similar package of incentives presently being enjoyed by thrift banks under RA 7906 and by rural banks under RA 7353.
