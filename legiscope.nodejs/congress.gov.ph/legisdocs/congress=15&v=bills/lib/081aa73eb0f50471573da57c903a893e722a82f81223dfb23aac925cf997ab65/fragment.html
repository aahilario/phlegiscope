House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00089
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING THE PRESCHOOL EDUCATION INTO THE BASIC EDUCATION SYSTEM AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Preschool Education Act
ABSTRACT : ”Preschool Education Act.” Institutionalizes preschool education as part of basic education and be made mandatory for all five (5)-year old children.
