HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00402
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE TAX ON IDLE LANDS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE, SECTION 236 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : Of the country's total land area of 30 million hectares, around 14.12 hectares are classified as alienable and disposable. Had these lands been utilized, agricultural productivity and food security would not be a critical problem today. This measure seeks to increase the ceiling of the tax on idle lands from 5% to 10% to give local government units more flexibility in its imposition. The current maximum tax of 5% has not proven to be an effective deterrent against the non-utilization of agricultural lands for productive purposes.
