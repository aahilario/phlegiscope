House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02497
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR ADDITIONAL BENEFITS AND PRIVILEGES TO PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 4670, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'MAGNA CARTA FOR PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS', AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to grant public school teachers additional benefits and privileges in the form of more liberal study leaves, educational grants to children of teachers and more comprehensive special hardship allowances.
