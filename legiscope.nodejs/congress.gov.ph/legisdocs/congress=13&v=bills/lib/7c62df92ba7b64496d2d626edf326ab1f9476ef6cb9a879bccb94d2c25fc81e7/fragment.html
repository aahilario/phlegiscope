HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05441
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL COUNCIL FOR EDUCATION, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the creation of the National Council for Education which shall serve as an advisory body to the executive and the legislative branches on all issues and concerns affecting the educational system.
