House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01073
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE POLITICAL PARTY SYSTEM AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Political Party Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Political Party Act of 2004." The bill seeks to provide the institutional framework which shall govern the system of political parties in the country to make them more responsive to the needs of their members and of their constituencies. It provides definitive guidelines to reengineer and re-invent our current system of traditional politics and discourage political chameleons and butterflies.
