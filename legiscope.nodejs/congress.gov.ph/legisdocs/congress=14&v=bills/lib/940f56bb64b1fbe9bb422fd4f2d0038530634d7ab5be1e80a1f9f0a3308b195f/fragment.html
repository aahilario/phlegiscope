House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02520
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE MAGNA CARTA OF STUDENTS
SHORT TITLE : "Magna Carta of Students"
ABSTRACT : The measure ensures the rights of students to the following: a) quality education; b) organization; c) participate in policy making; d) free expression and academic freedom; and e) due process in disciplinary proceedings.
