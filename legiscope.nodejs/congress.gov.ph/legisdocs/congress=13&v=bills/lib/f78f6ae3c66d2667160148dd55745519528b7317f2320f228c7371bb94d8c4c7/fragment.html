House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01432
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ESTABLISH CERTAIN LIMITATIONS ON ADVERTISING RELATING TO CIGARETTES AND TOBACCO PRODUCTS
SHORT TITLE : Tobacco Products Control Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Tobacco Products Control Act of 2004." The bill prohibits the advertisement of cigarettes on the following: billboard within 500 meters of a school, student newspapers, taxis, buses, trains and their terminals, cinema or video games.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : BARINAGA, ROSELLER L.
DATE FILED : 2004-07-14
