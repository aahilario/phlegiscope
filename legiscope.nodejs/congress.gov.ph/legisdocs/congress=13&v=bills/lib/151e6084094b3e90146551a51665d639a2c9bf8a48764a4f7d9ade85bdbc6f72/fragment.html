House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04804
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ENSURING PROTECTIVE MEASURES AND SUPPORT FOR THE VICTIMS OF THE SYSTEM OF PROSTITUTION, IMPOSING PENALTIES ON ITS PERPETRATORS AND AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Anti-Prostitution Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "The Anti-Prostitution Act of 2005". The bill seeks to define the punishable acts of prostitution and provides for its penalties and sanctions. It provides for the rights of persons exploited in prostitution and the responsibilities of government agencies in anti-prostitution efforts.
