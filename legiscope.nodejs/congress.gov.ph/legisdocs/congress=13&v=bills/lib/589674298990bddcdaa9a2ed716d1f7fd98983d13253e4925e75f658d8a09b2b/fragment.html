HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01576
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 16 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE NATIONAL CALENDAR TO COMMEMORATE THE DISCOVERY OF THE PHILIPPINE ARCHIPELAGO BY FERDINAND MAGELLAN AND A SPECIAL NONWORKING DAY IN THE EASTERN VISAYAS REGION
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to declare March 16th of every year as a special day in the national calendar and a non-working holiday in Eastern Visayas.
