HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02170
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING HONORARIA AND OTHER BENEFITS TO ALL ACCREDITED BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE R.A. 7883, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS INCENTIVES AND BENEFITS ACT"
ABSTRACT : The bill grants barangay health workers a fixed honoraria monthly, and provides a minimum amount of subsistence allowance and an insurance coverage from the GSIS or any accredited insurance company.
