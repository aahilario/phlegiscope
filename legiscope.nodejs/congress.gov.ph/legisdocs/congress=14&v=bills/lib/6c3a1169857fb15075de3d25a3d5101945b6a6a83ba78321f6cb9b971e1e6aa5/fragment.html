FULL TITLE : AN ACT DESIGNATING THE NATIONAL MUSIC COMPETITIONS FOR YOUNG ARTISTS (NAMCYA) AS THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL YOUTH DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM FOR MUSIC, DEFINING ITS ROLE AND FUNCTIONS, AND CREATING THE NAMCYA ENDOWMENT FUND AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "NAMCYA Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes the Philippine National Youth Development for Music that be tasked to discover outstanding musical talents and assist in their cultivation, development and advancement.
