HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00513
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING COMPULSORY COVERAGE TO ACCREDITED BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS IN THE NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE PROGRAM, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7883, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : In gratitude to the important services of the barangay health workers who are committed, willing to sacrifice financial gains and even endure countless hardships because of the tasks they do, the bill seeks to grant compulsory enrollment in the National Health Insurance Program (PhilHealth) and their dependents to receive and avail of an adequate package of necessary health services.
