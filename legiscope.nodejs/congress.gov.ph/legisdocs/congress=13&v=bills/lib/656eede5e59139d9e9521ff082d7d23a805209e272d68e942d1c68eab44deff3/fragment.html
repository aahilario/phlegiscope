House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04721
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REQUIRE ALL LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS TO ADOPT A BOY SCOUTING AND GIRL SCOUTING RECOGNITION DAY IN ORDER TO HIGHLIGHT AND GIVE RECOGNITION TO THE PRINCIPLES OF PATRIOTISM, SERVICE AND CAMARADERIE THAT SCOUTING EXEMPLIFIES
SHORT TITLE : Requiring All Local Government Units to Adopt a Boy Scouting and Girl Scouting Recognition Day
ABSTRACT : The measure mandates all cities and municipalities to adopt, by appropriate proclamation, an annual Boy and Girl Scouting Recognition Day within the months of September and October of every year.
