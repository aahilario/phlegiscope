House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02572
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING BUSINESS FOR SQUATTING HOUSING, PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Squatting Business Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill penalizes natural and juridical persons who engage in the business of squatter housing, such as selling lands or rights not belonging to them.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : BAGATSING, AMADO S.
