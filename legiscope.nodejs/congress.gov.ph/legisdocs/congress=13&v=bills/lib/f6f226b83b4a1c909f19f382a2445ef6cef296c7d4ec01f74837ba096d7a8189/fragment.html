HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02583
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROMOTE THE USE OF ETHYL ALCOHOL OR ETHANOL AS ALTERNATIVE TRANSPORT FUEL, ESTABLISHING FOR THE PURPOSE THE NATIONAL FUEL ETHANOL PROGRAM, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Fuel Ethanol Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Fuel Ethanol Act of 2004." The bill encourages and promotes the use of ethyl alcohol or ethanol as an alternative transport fuel through the Fuel Ethanol Program. The Program shall introduce the production of ethanol from sugarcane, corn, or cassava.
