HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01950
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ADOPTING A RETAIL PRICE MAINTENANCE AND UNIFORM DISCOUNTING SCHEME IN BOOK TRADE WITH THE END IN VIEW OF ENSURING AFFORDABLE, ACCESSIBLE AND QUALITY-LADEN TEXTBOOKS AND OTHER RELATED EDUCATIONAL MATERIALS TO BE USED BY PRIVATE AND PUBLIC SCHOOLS
SHORT TITLE : "Retail Price Maintenance and Uniform Discounting Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Retail Price Maintenance and Uniform Discounting Act of 2007". The bill provides for a uniform discount scheme of not lower tha 10% but not more than 25% to bookstores or private educational institutions.
