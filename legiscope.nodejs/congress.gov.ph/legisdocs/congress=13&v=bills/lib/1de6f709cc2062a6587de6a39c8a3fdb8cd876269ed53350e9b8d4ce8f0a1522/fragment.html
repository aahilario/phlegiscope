HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04147
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING A FOUR-DAY NON-CUMULATIVE LEAVE WITH PAY ANNUALLY TO EMPLOYED PARENTS TO ATTEND TO THE SPECIAL NEEDS OF THEIR CHILDREN-
SHORT TITLE : Parental Leave Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Parental Leave Act of 2005". The bill grants 4 days parental leave annually to employed parents, whether in the government or private sector, to enable them to attend school activities that require their presence.
