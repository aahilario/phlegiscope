House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00045
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR SUSTAINABLE FOREST MANAGEMENT
SHORT TITLE : Sustainable Forest Management Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : "Sustainable Forest Management Act of 2010." Provides for the limits and classification of forest lands; administration and management of these lands; reforestation program; forest protection; forestry research, education, training and extension; disposition and utilization of forest resources; forest charges, fees and rentals; and criminals offenses and penalties.
