HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01370
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LOWERING THE RETIREMENT AGE OF EMPLOYEES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION FROM 65 YEARS OLD TO 60 YEARS OLD
SHORT TITLE : "New DepEd Retirement Age Act"
ABSTRACT : "New DepEd Retirement Age Act". The bill sets the compulsory retirement age for all Department of Education teachers and non-teaching personnel at the age of 60 years.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : REYES, CARMENCITA O.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-25
