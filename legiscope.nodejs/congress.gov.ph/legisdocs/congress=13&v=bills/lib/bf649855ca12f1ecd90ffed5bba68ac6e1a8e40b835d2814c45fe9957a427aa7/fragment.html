House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00017
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MAGNA CARTA OF STUDENTS
SHORT TITLE : Magna Carta for Students
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta for Students". The proposed Magna Carta for Students seeks to respect, protect and fulfill the rights of students in the secondary, post-secondary, graduate and post-graduate courses. Among others, it provides for student representation in the governing board and other policy-making bodies of the school, the creation of multi-sectoral School Fee Board, the right to organize and run an autonomous student council, and the right to publish and circulate an independent school paper. The Magna Carta also seeks to penalize any violation of the rights of students.
