HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02217
FULL TITLE : AGRICULTURAL INNOVATION PROMOTION ACT
SHORT TITLE : "Agriculture Innovation Promotion Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Department of Agriculture to set aside 10% of its annual budget to draw out, encourage, promote, support and replicate agricultural innovation programs of farmers and cooperatives to increase production in rice and high value crops.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SAN LUIS, EDGAR S.
DATE FILED : 2007-08-23
