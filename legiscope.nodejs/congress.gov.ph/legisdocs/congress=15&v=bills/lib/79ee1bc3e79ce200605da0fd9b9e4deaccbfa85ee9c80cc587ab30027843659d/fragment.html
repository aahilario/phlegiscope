HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00025
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING A PRE-SCHOOL PROGRAM IN THE PUBLIC EDUCATION SYSTEM AND INTEGRATING VALUES FORMATION INTO THE CURRICULUM OF PUBLIC PRE-SCHOOL, ELEMENTARY AND HIGH SCHOOL EDUCATION
ABSTRACT : Directs the Department of Education (DepEd) to design a pre-school program that shall be made part of the curriculum of public schools and shall be considered as a requisite to elementary education.
