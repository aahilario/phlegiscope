House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02780
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE USE OF GENERIC DRUGS AND MEDICINES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6675, OR THE GENERICS ACT OF 1988
ABSTRACT : The bill directs all medical, dental and veterinary practitioners to write prescriptions using generic names alone. It also provides penalties for violations hereof.
