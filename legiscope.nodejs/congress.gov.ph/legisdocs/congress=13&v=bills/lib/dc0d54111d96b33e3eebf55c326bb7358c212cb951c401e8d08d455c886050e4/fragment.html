House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01754
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REDEFINING ILLEGAL RECRUITMENT, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8042 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "MIGRANT WORKERS AND OVERSEAS FILIPINOS ACT OF 1995," AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Migrant Workers and Overseas Filipinos Act Amendment of 1998
ABSTRACT : "Migrant Workers and Overseas Filipinos Act Amendment of 1998." The bill seeks to distinguish acts or omissions by illegal recruiters, which constitue the crime of illegal recruitment, from the prohibited acts or omissions of legitimate and licensed placement or recruitment agencies.
