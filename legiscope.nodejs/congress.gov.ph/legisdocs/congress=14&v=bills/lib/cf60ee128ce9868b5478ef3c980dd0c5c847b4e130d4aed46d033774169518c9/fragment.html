HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02899
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6506, ALSO KNOWN AS "AN ACT CREATING THE BOARD OF EXAMINERS FOR CRIMINOLOGISTS IN THE PHILIPPINES," SECTION 23 AND 24 ON CRIMINOLOGY AND CRIMINOLOGISTS
ABSTRACT : The measure aims to provide the Philippine National Police, the Bureau of Jail Management and Penology and the Bureau of Fire Protection a pool of criminology graduates, professional criminologists and criminology experts who have complied with government policies and standards for criminology education program established by the Commission on Higher Education.
