FULL TITLE : AN ACT UPGRADING THE BENCHMARK POSITION FOR PUBLIC ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY SCHOOL TEACHERS FROM SALARY GRADE 10 TO 19 AND FOR THIS PURPOSE, AMENDING SECTION 9 OF REPUBLIC ACT 6758, AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to upgrade the benchmark position for teachers from salary grade 10 to 19 provided they pass the licensure examination for teachers.
