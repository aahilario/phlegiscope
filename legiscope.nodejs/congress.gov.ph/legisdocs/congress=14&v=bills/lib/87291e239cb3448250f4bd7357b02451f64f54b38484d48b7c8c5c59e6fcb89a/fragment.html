HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03155
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL NUCLEAR RADIATION SAFETY BOARD AND DEFINING ITS SCOPE, FUNCTIONS AND POWERS
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes the National Nuclear Radiation Safety Board that shall regulate all nuclear radiation research undertakings, developmental application for the safety and protection of the general public thereby preventing or decreasing the risk which such activities pose to life, property and ecology.
