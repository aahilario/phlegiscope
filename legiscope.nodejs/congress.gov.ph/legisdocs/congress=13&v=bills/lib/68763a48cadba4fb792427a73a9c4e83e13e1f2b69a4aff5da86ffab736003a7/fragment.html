House Bill/Resolution NO. HB06054
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A CREDIT RELIEF FUND FOR OVERSEAS FILIPINO WORKERS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE
SHORT TITLE : "The Credit Relief Fund for Overseas Filipino Workers Act"
ABSTRACT : "The Credit Relief Fund for Overseas Filipino Workers Act". The bill proposes the creation of the Credit Relief Fund for Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) which shall provide financial assistance to OFWs in their pre-employment requirements.
