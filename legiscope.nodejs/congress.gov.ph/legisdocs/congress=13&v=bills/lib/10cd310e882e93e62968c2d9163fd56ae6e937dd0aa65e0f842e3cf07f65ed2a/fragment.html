HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01285
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING PHILIPPINE EMBASSIES ANS CONSULATES TO CONDUCT CLASSES FOR CHILDREN OF FILIPINOS IN OTHER COUNTRIES, TO EDUCATE THEM ON PHILIPPINE HISTORY, CULTURE, LANGUAGES AND CIVICS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : This bill requires all Philippine embassies and consulates, particularly in countries where there are Filipino expatriates, to conduct special classes, free of charge, during Saturdays for children of Filipino parentage to educate them on Philippine history, languages, culture and civics.
