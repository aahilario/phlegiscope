House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01489
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE MANDATORY ALLOCATION OF FIVE PERCENT OF THE TOTAL BUDGET OF EVERY PROVINCES, MUNICIPALITIES, AND CITIES FOR THE HOUSING PROGRAM FOR THE URBAN POOR
SHORT TITLE : "Housing Program Funds Allocation Act"
ABSTRACT : "Housing Program Funds Allocation Act". The bills aims to ensure the implementation and sustainability of housing programs for the urban poor by allocating 5% of the total budget every province, municipality and city for this purpose.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : LAZATIN, CARMELO F.
