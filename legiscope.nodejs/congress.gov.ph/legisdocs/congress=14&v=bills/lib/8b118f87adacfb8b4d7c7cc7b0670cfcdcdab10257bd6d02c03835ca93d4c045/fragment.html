House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03128
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING CENTERS FOR STREET CHILDREN IN EVERY REGION OF THE PHILIPPINES AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill call for the establishment of centers for street children that shall be tasked to rescue, nurture and protect them, provide for their shelter and other rehabilitative measures.
