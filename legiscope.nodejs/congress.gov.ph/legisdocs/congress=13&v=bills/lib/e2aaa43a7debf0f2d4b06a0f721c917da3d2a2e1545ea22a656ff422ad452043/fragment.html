HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04198
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING MARCH TWENTY-TWO OF EVERY YEAR AS A REGULAR HOLIDAY THROUGHOUT THE ENTIRE PHILIPPINES IN ORDER TO COMMEMORATE THE BIRTH ANNIVERSARY OF GENERAL EMILIO AGUINALDO, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Declaring March Twenty-Two of Every Year As a Regular Holiday Throughout the Entire Philippines to Commemorate the Birth Anniversary of General Emilio Aguinaldo
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes to declare March 22 of every year as a regular holiday throughout the country to commemorate the birth anniversary of Gen. Emilio Aguinaldo, the first President of the Philippine Republic.
