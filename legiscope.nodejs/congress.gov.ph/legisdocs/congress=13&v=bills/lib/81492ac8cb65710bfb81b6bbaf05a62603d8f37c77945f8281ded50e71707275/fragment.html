House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00807
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING HAZARD PAY TO ALL DULY APPOINTED MEMBERS OF BARANGAY TANOD BRIGADES DURING THEIR INCUMBENCY, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill grants all duly appointed members of the barangay tanod brigades a hazard pay, during their incumbency, in the amount of Two Hundred Pesos (P200.00) per month.
