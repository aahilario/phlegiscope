House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05580
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING THE OPEN HIGH SCHOOL SYSTEM IN THE PHILIPPINES, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Open High School System Act of 2006."
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to make available the open high school system to all youth and adults who have finished elementary education and high school qualifiers under the Philippine Education Placement Test.
