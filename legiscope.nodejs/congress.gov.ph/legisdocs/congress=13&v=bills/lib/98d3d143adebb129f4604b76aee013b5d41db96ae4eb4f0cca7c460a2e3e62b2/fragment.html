HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00318
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING BENEFITS TO COLLEGE AND POST-GRADUATE STUDENTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Because of the economic crisis that plagued the country, many children quit school thus adding to the increasing number of out-of-school youth. The bill aims to provide some benefits to bona fide college and post-graduate students in public and private academic institutions. By providing some benefits to students, the economic pressure on parents are greatly reduced.
