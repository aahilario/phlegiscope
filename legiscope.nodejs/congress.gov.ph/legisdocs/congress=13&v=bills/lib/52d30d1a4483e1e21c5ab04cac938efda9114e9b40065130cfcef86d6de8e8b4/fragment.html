House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02367
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE REGULATORY FUNCTIONS OF THE PHILIPPINE OVERSEAS EMPLOYMENT ADMINISTRATION (POEA), AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8042, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'MIGRANT WORKERS AND OVERSEAS FILIPINOS ACT OF 1995'
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to repeal Sections 29 and 30 of RA 8042 which provide for the eventual deregulation of recruitment activities and the gradual phase-out of the regulatory functions of the Philippine Overseas Employment Administration over said activities.
