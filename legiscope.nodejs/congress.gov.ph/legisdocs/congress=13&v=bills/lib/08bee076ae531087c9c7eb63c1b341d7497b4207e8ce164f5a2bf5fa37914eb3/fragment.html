House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00118
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A PROVIDENT PERSONAL SAVINGS PLAN KNOWN AS THE PERSONAL EQUITY AND RETIREMENT ACCOUNT (PERA)
SHORT TITLE : Personal Equity and Retirement Account (PERA) Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Personal Equity and Retirement Account (PERA) Act of 2004". Generally, Filipinos view retirement with trepidation as it means lower or even loss of income because majority of Filipinos do not have retirement benefits to look forward to. This bill seeks to remedy the situation by institutionalizing the Personal Equity and Retirement Account (PERA) for all public and private employees. Under the proposed measure, people will be encouraged to save for their retirement years.
