HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01651
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING THAT AREAS WITHIN THE DECLARED TOURIST ZONES SUITABLE FOR AGRICULTURAL PURPOSES SHALL BE DECLARED ALIENABLE AND DISPOSABLE UNDER CERTAIN CONDITIONS
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the Department of Agrarian Reform, in coordination with the Philippine Tourism Authority, to determine the areas within tourist zones that are suitable for agricultural purposes and to segregate the same for eventual distribution to qualified beneficiaries under the Comprehensive Agrarian Reform Law.
