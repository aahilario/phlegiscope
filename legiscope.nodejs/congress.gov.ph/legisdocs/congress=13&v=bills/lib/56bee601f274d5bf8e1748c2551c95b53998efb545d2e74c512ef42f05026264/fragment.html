House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03165
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A SYSTEMATIC AND PERIODIC REVIEW OF THE ECONOMY, EFFICIENCY AND VIABILITY OF GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR -CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Sunset Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Sunset Act of 2004." The bill provides a mechanism for the systematic review and evaluation of government-owned and controlled corporations (GOCCs). It empowers the committees of the Senate and the House of Representatives having legislative jurisdiction over GOCCs to regularly conduct sunset reviews on these GOCCs.
