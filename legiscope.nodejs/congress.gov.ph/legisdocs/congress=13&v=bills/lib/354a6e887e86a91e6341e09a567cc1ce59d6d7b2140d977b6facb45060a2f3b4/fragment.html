House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02096
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMOTING THE WELFARE OF THE PHILIPPINE COAST GUARD BY INCREASING, INTEGRATING AND RATIONALIZING THE BASE PAY AND OTHER BENEFITS OF ITS OFFICERS AND ENLISTED PERSONNEL, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Magna Carta of the Philippine Coast Guard
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta of the Philippine Coast Guard." The bill provides the personnel of the Philippine Coast Guard (PCG) the following: a) rights, duties and responsibilities; b) hours of work, remuneration, welfare, health care and injuries benefits; and c) separation, retirement and death benefits.
