HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04370
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CHANGING THE REGULAR SCHOOL CALENDAR FROM JUNE THROUGH MARCH TO SEPTEMBER THROUGH JUNE
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to change the school calendar of elementary, high school and college levels from June through March to September through June to spare children from the hassles of the rainy season.
