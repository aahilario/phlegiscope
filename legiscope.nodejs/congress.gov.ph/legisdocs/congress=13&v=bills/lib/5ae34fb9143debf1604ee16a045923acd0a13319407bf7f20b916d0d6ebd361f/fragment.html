House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01499
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING PARENTS, ADOPTERS, GUARDIANS OR PERSONS EXERCISING SPECIAL OR SUBSTITUTE PARENTAL AUTHORITY OVER CHILDREN UNDER PARENTAL AUTHORITY FOUND TO BE ENGAGED IN BEGGING AND/OR PROSTITUTION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Parental Sanction Law for Child Begging and Prostitution
ABSTRACT : "Parental Sanction Law for Child Begging and Prostitution." The bill imposes the penalty of prision correccional to parents, adopters, guardians or substitute parents who shall use, coerce, intimidate or force their children to beg or become prostitutes.
