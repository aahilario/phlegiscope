HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00751
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE PRACTICE OF REAL ESTATE PROFESSION IN THE PHILIPPINES, CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE A PROFESSIONAL REGULATORY BOARD OF REAL ESTATE PROFESSIONALS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Real Estate Profession Act of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : "Real Estate Profession Act of the Philippines" The bill elevates the real estate service practice. i.e., real estate brokerage, appraisal and consultancy, to the category of a profession, thereby placing realty service practitioners under the jurisdiction of the Professional Regulatory Commission.
