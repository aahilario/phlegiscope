FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTY-NINE HUNDRED AND THIRTY-EIGHT, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE COOPERATIVE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill penalizes any public official who deprives, diminishes or restricts any duly registered cooperative from enjoying the tax exemption privilege granted under the Cooperative Code.
