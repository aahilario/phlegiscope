House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01363
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL TOURISM POLICY AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : National Tourism Policy Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "National Tourism Policy Act of 2004." The bill proposes the adoption of the following policies, among others: a) encourage activities and programs which promote tourism awareness; b) promote the preservation of traditional crafts; c) encourage the use of indigenous architectural designs and materials; and d) grant preferential treatment to the employment of Filipinos.
