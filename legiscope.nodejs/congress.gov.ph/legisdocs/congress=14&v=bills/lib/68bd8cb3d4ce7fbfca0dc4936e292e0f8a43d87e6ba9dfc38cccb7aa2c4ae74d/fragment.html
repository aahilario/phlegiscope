HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02496
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL LABOR RELATIONS COMMISSION DEVELOPMENT FUNDS THEREBY AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLE 277 OF PD 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill creates the National Labor Relations Commission (NLRC) Development Fund that shall be used to augment the allowances of the Chairman, Commissioners and labor arbiters of the NLRC and to finance the requisition, maintenance and repair of its office equipments and facilities.
