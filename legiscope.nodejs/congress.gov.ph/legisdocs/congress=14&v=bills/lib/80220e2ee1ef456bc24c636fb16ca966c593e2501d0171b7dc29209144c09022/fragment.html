HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01783
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE IMPOSITION OF OR EXACTION OF ANY FEE FOR THE USE OF PARKING SPACES AND FACILITIES LOCATED WITHIN SHOPPING MALLS, HOTELS AND SIMILAR BUSINESS AND/OR COMMERCIAL ESTABLISHMENTS AND PRESCRIBING PENALTIES THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits owners of shopping malls, hotels and similar business and commercial establishments from imposing, exacting, collecting and charging any fee for the use of parking spaces located within these establishments.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SANTIAGO, JOSEPH A.
