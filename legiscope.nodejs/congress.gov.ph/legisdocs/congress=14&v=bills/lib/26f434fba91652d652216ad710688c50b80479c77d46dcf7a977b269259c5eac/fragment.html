House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00118
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CREDIT INFORMATION SYSTEM AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Credit Information System Act"
ABSTRACT : "The Credit Information System Act". The bill provides for the creation of a Credit Information Bureau with the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas as the majority shareholder. The Bureau shall have the power to collect and disseminate credit-related information and maintain the credit history and track record of borrowers.
