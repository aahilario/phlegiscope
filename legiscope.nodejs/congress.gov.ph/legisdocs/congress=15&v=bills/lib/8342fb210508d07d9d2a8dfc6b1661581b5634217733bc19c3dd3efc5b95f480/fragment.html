HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00180
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING POOR LITIGANTS FROM POSTING BAIL WHEN CHARGED WITH NON-CAPITAL OFFENSES FOR THE FIRST TIME AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Poor Litigants Equalization Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Poor Litigants Equalization Act of 2010.” Seeks to exempt poor litigants from posting bail when charged with felony or crime in which the law attaches imprisonment of not more than six years for the first time and be released on cognizance.
