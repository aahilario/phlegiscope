HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00151
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE INCLUSION OF THE MINORITY FLOOR LEADER OF THE SANGGUNIAN CONCERNED IN THE MEMBERSHIP OF THE LOCAL SCHOOL BOARDS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE, THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to amend Section 98 of the Local Government Code by providing for the inclusion of the Minority Floor Leader of the Sanggunian concerned in the Local School Board (LSB). The Minority in the Sanggunian are the fiscalizers in the disbursement of funds and promulgation of laws in the Sanggunian and their presence in the LSB will give added assurance that the administration of the Special Education Fund will be subjected to scrutiny.
