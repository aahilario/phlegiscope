FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING AND ESTABLISHING THE CENTER FOR STUDIES ON INDIGENOUS CULTURAL COMMUNITIES, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Seeks to protect and promote the rights of indigenous communities to ensure their economic, social and cultural well-being and provide and opportunity to both Muslims and non-Muslims to participate in national life and development.
