HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01642
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PRESCRIBE, AS A PRIORITY PROGRAM, THE SCIENTIFIC PROPAGATION, CULTURE AND DEVELOPMENT OF INDIGENOUS RAW MATERIALS FOR HOMECRAFTS AND OTHER INDUSTRIES AND FOR OTHER RELATED PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes and prescribes, as a priority program, the scientific propagation, culture and development of indigenous raw materials such as bamboo, rattan, buri, anahaw, tambo and the like for homecrafts and other industries.
