House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01737
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REDEFINING THE THIRD LEVEL POSITIONS IN THE CAREER CIVIL SERVICE
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the following qualification requirements for third level positions: a) appropriate academic and professional background; b) career service executive eligibility; c) holds a comparable or next in rank position in government; d) meets age requirement; and e) not disqualified under any existing law.
