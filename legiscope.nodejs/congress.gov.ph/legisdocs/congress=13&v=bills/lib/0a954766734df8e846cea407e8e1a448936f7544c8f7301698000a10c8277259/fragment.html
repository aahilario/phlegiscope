HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03470
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PRESERVE AND REHABILITATE THE FOREST BY PERMANENTLY BANNING ALL LOGGING OPERATIONS IN PROTECTED FORESTLANDS, CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE A TASK FORCE FOR THE PRESERVATION AND REHABILITATION OF FORESTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Forest Preservation and Rehabilitation Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Forest Preservation and Rehabilitation Act of 2004." The bill proposes a permanent ban on all commercial logging activities in all protected forests. It prohibits the issuance of licenses, permits or agreements to cut any timber therein.
