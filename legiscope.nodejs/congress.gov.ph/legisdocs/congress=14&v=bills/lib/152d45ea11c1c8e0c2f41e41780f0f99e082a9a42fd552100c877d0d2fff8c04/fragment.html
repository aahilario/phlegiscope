HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02415
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING PRIVATE EMPLOYERS TO ESTABLISH AND PROVIDE SLEEPING QUARTERS AND/OR FACILITIES FOR THEIR FEMALE EMPLOYEES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates private employers to provide sleeping facilities for women employees for their use after working hours provided the working hours end between 8:00 pm to 6:00 am.
