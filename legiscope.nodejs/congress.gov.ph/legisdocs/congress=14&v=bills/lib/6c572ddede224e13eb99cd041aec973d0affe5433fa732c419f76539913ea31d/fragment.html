HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02058
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING ALL FORMS OF SMUGGLING AS ECONOMIC SABOTAGE AND PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill declares smuggling and technical smuggling as acts of economic sabotage and imposes a penalty of life imprisonment and a fine raning from the amount equivalent to the import duties due to triple the said amount which in no case shall be less than P500,000.
