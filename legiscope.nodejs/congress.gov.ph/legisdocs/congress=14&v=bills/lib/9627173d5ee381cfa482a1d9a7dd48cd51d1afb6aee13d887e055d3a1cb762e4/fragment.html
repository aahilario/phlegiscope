House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00977
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LEGALIZING JUETENG
SHORT TITLE : "Jueteng Act of 2004"
ABSTRACT : "Jueteng Act of 2004". The bill seeks to legalize jueteng and place its operation under the sole control and supervision of the local government unit where the game is conducted.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SOLIS, JOSE G.
