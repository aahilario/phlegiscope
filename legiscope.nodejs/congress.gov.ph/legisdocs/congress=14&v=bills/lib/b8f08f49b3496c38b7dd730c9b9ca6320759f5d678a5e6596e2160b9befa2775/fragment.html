HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02649
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A NATIONAL PROGRAM THAT WILL PROVIDE PREGNANT WOMEN WITH ALTERNATIVES TO ABORTION
SHORT TITLE : "Women and Children's Resources Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes a national program aimed at: a) promoting childbirth as a viable alternative to abortion and empower those facing unplanned pregnancies to choose childbirth; and b) extending support to entities and projects which provide information, counseling and support services that assist women to choose childbirth.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.
DATE FILED : 2007-09-24
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
