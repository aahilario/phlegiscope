HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04926
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE SHARE OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS (LGUs) IN THE GROSS INCOME EARNED BY ALL BUSINESSES AND ENTERPRISES WITHIN THE SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE (ECOZONE) AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE "THE SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE ACT OF 1995" AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to increase the share of local government units in the remittances to the national government by entities operating within economic zones from 1% to 2%.
