House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02644
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL DISASTER MANAGEMENT COMMISSION, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Comprehensive Disaster Management Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill creates the National Disaster Management Commission that shall address disaster issues in their totality, i.e., prevention, mitigation, preparedness, emergency operations, relief and rehabilitation.
