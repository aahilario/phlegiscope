FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE GRANT OF REWARDS AND PROTECTION TO GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES WHO WILL REPORT ANY ACT MALFEASANCE IN GOVERNMENT OFFICES, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for a system of reward and protection to individuals who shall report acts of malfeasance in government offices.
