HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02186
FULL TITLE : AN ACT BROADENING THE SCOPE OF COMPLIANCE AND EXPANDING THE CONDUIT NETWORK FOR AGRI-AGRA CREDIT, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE 717 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE AGRI-AGRA LAW
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to ensure the availability of more credit to farmers, fisherfolks and agrarian reform beneficiaries by removing the option of investing in government securities as a form of alternative compliance under the Agri-Agra Law.
