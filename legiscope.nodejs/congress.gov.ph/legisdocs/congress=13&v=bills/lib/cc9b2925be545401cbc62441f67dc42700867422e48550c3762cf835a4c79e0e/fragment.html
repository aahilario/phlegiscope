House Bill/Resolution NO. HB06009
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AMEND SECTION 2, PAR. 1, SECTION 3, PARS. 2 AND 3, SECTION 5, AND SECTION 6 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9227 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "AN ACT GRANTING ADDITIONAL COMPENSATION IN THE FORM OF SPECIAL ALLOWANCES FOR JUSTICES, JUDGES AND ALL OTHER POSITIONS IN THE JUDICIARY WITH THE EQUIVALENT RANK OF JUSTICES OF THE COURT OF APPEALS AND JUDGES OF THE REGIONAL TRIAL COURT, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Additional Compensation Act for Lower Courts of 2006"
ABSTRACT : "Additional Compensation Act for Lower Courts of 2006". The bill seeks to grant to judges of the Metropolitan Trial Court, Municipal Trial Court in cities, Municipal Trial Court, Municipal Circuit Court and Sharia Court the special allowances given to justices, judges and all other positions in the Judiciary with the equivalent rank of Justices of the Court of Appeals and judges of Regional Trial Courts.
