HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01136
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE INCLUSION IN THE HISTORY BOOKS OF ELEMENTARY, SECONDARY, AND COLLEGIATE CURRICULA THE LIVES AND HEROISM OF FILIPINO COMFORT WOMEN DURING THE JAPANESE OCCUPATION AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes to inform and educate our children on the lives, sacrifices and heroism of Filipino comfort women during the Japanese occupation. Their inclusion in history books would give due recognition to the courageous struggles of these women.
