House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01672
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A SCHOOL NUTRITION PROGRAM AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Child Nutrition Act of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : "Child Nutrition Act of the Philippines". The bill mandates the feeding of children enrolled in all public elementary schools and barangay day care centers in keeping with the State's policy of promoting and protecting the welfare of children.
