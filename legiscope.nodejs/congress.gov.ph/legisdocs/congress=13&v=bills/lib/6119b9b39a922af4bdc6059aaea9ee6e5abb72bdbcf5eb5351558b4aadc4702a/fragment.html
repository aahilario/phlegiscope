House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02692
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING PARAGRAPH THREE, ARTICLE TWENTY NINE OF ACT NUMBER THIRTY EIGHT HUNDRED FIFTEEN, AS AMENDED OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED PENAL CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the release of an accused who has undergone preventive suspension for a period equal to or more than the possible MINIMUM imprisonment of the offense to which he may be sentenced.
