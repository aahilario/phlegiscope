House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00690
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROMOTE AND REGULATE THE SUBSCRIBED TELEVISION SERVICE IN THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Subscribed TV Act
ABSTRACT : "Subscribed TV Act." The bill allows 40% foreign equity for operators to promote the growth of cable TV. It provides television service operators the following rights: easement, lease excess capacity, and to advertise. Repeals EOs 204 and 436.
