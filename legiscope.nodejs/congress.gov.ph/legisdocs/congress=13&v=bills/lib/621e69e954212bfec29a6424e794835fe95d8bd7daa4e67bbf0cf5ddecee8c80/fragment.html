House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04309
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREDITING TO MILITARY SERVICE, FOR PURPOSES OF RETIREMENT CIVILIAN GOVERNMENT SERVICE RENDERED AFTER RETIRING FROM ACTIVE MILITARY SERVICE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBERED SIXTEEN HUNDRED THIRTY-EIGHT, AS AMENDED BY PRESIDENTIAL DECREE SEXTEEN HUNDRED FIFTY
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to credit civilian government service rendered after retiring from active military service to the military service for retirement purposes.
