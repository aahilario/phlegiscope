HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02544
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR PUBLIC LIABILITY INSURANCE FOR THE PURPOSE OF PROVIDING IMMEDIATE RELIEF TO PERSONS AFFECTED BY ACCIDENT ARISING FROM THE OPERATIONS OR INCIDENTAL TO THE OPERATIONS OF ESTABLISHMENTS, ENTERPRISES, PUBLIC UTILITIES AND VEHICLES, BUILDINGS, MACHINERY AND THEIR ACCESORIES
SHORT TITLE : Public Liability Insurance Act of 2001
ABSTRACT : "Public Liability Insurance Act of 2001." The bill requires all owners and operators of business establishments, public utilities and services, buildings, machinery and their accessories to have public liability insurance to pay for injuries and damages arising from the operation of the above.
