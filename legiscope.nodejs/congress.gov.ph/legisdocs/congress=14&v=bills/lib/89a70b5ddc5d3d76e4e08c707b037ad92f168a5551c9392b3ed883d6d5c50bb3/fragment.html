HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02528
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROMOTE RURAL HEALTH BY PROVIDING FOR AN ACCELERATED PROGRAM FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF A POTABLE WATER SUPPLY SYSTEM IN EVERY BARANGAY IN THE COUNTRY WITHIN THREE YEARS
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Departments of Social Welfare and Development, Health, and Public Works and Highways to undertake a three-year program for the construction of potable water supply system in every barangay.
