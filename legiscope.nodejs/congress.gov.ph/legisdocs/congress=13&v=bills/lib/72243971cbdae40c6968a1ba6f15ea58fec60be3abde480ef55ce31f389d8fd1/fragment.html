House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01533
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ORDAINING THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE DOWNSTREAM NATURAL GAS INDUSTRY, CONSOLIDATING FOR THE PURPOSE ALL LAWS RELATING TO THE TRANSMISSION, DISTRIBUTION AND SUPPLY OF NATURAL GAS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Downstream Natural Gas Industry Development Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Downstream Natural Gas Industry Development Act of 2004". The bill seeks to provide a clear, comprehensive and integrated regulatory framework for the development of the nascent downstream natural gas industry. It defines the responsibilities of the various government agencies and the private entities concerned.
