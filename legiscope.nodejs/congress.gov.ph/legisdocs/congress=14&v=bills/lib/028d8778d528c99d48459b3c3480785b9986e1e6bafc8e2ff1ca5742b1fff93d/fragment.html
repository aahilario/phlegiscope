HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02802
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXTINGUISHING THE PENALTY OF IMPRISONMENT FOR LIBEL BUT INCREASING THE PENALTY OF FINE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3815, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED PENAL CODE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to abolish the penalty of imprisonment and to impose higher fines for offenders of the crime of libel. The proposed fines shall range from P20,000 to P50,000.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : NOGRALES, PROSPERO C.
