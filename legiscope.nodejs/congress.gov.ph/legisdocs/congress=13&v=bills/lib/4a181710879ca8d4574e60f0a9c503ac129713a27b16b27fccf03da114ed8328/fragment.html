HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01592
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THAT ALL EXPENDITURES FOR THE PAYMENT OF FOREIGN DEBTS BE INCLUDED IN THE ANNUAL GENERAL APPROPRIATIONS ACT
ABSTRACT : The bill allows Congress to review, assess and approve all budget items proposed by the Executive branch including the payment of foreign loans. The approval of the measure will put to an end to the vicious cycle of borrowing just to pay existing/maturing debts.
