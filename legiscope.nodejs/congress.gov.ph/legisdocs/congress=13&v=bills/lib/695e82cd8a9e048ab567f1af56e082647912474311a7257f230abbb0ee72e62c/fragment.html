House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03702
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING INCENTIVES AND PRIVILEGES TO FOSTER FAMILIES AND CHILDREN UNDER FOSTER CARE PROGRAM, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Foster Care Incentives and Privileges
ABSTRACT : "Foster Care Incentives and Privileges." The measure provides foster parents the following incentives: a) personal tax exemption, b) foster care susbsidy, c) respite care special leave, and d) supplies and other assistance. It also grants the following privileges to a foster child: a) health care privileges, b) free access to public education, c) civil service eligibility, and d) emergency fund for medical/hospitalization needs.
