HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03670
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REORGANIZING AND STRENGTHENING THE PUBLIC ATTORNEY'S OFFICE (PAO), AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PERTINENT PROVISIONS OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE CODE OF 1987 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill grants greater autonomy to the Public Attorney's Office by designating it into an attached agency of the Department of Justice, expanding its organization structure and granting it fiscal autonomy.
