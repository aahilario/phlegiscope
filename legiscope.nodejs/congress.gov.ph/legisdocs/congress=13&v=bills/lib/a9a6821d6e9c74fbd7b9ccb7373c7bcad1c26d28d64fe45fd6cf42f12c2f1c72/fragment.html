HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01291
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 4 AND 6, PARAGRAPH (c) OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6972, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'BARANGAY-LEVEL TOTAL DEVELOPMENT AND PROTECTION OF CHILDREN ACT', AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill increases the allowance of day care workers to PHP2,000.00 and mandates the Sangguniang Bayan or Sangguniang Panglungsod to create one mandatory position of Barangay Day Care Worker in every barangay.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ANTONINO-CUSTODIO, DARLENE MAGNOLIA R.
