House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02637
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROMOTE THE MANUFACTURE, IMPORTATION, SALE, DISTRIBUTION, USE AND DEVELOPMENT OF HYBRID AND ALTERNATIVE FUEL VEHICLES BY GRANTING TAX EXEMPTIONS AND PROVIDING INCENTIVES THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Alternative Fuel Vehicles Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Alternative Fuel Vehicles Act of 2004." The bill grants tax (duty, excise, value-added and income taxes) and non-tax exemptions and incentives for the manufacture, importation, sale, distribution, use of hybrid or alternative fuel vehicles.
