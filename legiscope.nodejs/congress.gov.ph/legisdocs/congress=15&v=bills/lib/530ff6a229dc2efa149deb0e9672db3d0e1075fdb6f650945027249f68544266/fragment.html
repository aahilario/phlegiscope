HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00127
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPOSING AN EXCISE TAX, TO BE KNOWN AS 'ENVIRONMENTAL LEVY' ON THE USE OF PLASTIC BAGS IN SHOPS, SUPERMARKETS, SERVICE STATIONS, STORES AND/OR SALES OUTLETS, CREATING THE ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION SUPPORT FUND, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Discourages the use of plastic bags through the imposition of an "environment" levy that shall be specifically used to fund efforts to protect the environment.
