FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR AN INTEGRATED AND COMPREHENSIVE NATIONAL POLICY ON RESPONSIBLE PARENTHOOD, POPULATION MANAGEMENT AND HUMAN DEVELOPMENT, CREATING A RESPONSIBLE PARENTHOOD AND POPULATION MANAGEMENT COUNCIL, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Responsible Parenthood and Population Management Act of 2005."
ABSTRACT : "Responsible Parenthood and Population Management Act of 2005." The bill sets in place a national policy that assures adequate and continuing information on reproductive health and a full range of family planning methods (excluding abortion).
