House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02895
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7877, ALSO KNOWN AS THE ANTI-SEXUAL HARASSMENT ACT OF 1995, SECTION 3 ON THE DEFINITION OF WORK, EDUCATION OR TRAINING-RELATED SEXUAL HARASSMENT
SHORT TITLE : Expanding The Definition Of Work Or Training-related Sexual Harassment
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to expand the definition of work or training-related sexual harassment to include customers, guests and clients who sexually harass employees of establishments like hotels, restaurants, department stores and hospitals.
