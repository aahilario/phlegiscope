House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04406
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MANNER AND DATE OF ELECTION OF SECTORAL REPRESENTATIVES IN THE LOCAL SANGGUNIANS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Local Sectoral Representation Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Local Sectoral Representation Act of 2005". The bill provides for the election of three sectoral representatives each in the Sangguniang Panlalawigan, Sangguniang Panlungsod and Sangguniang Barangay, to wit: one from the women sector; one from workers and one from any of the following - indigenous peoples, differently-abled persons, senior citizens, victims of calamities, and the urban poor.
