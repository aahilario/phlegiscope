House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03503
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE PAYMENT OF ARREARS IN THE VETERAN'S PENSIONS AND BENEFITS MANDATED PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7696, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS AN ACT STANDARDIZING AND UPGRADING THE BENEFITS FOR MILITARY VETERANS AND THEIR DEPENDENTS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes to utilize the proceeds from the development, lease or use of government properties to pay arrearages in the pension of veterans, military retirees and their dependents.
