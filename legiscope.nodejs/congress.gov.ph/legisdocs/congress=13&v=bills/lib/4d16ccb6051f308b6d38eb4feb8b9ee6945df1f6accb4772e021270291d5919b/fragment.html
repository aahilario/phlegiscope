House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02740
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A GUIDANCE COUNSELING CENTER IN EVERY BARANGAY IN THE PHILIPPINES, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Gabay Bahay Community Center Act of the Thirteenth Congress
ABSTRACT : "Gabay Bahay Community Center Act of the Thirteenth Congress." The Center shall provide guidance counseling to victims of harassment, spouse and parental abuse, rape and other forms of gender and family related violence.
