House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02671
FULL TITLE : A BILL TO PROTECT CHILDREN FROM EXPOSURE TO ENVIRONMENTAL TOBACCO SMOKE IN THE PROVISION OF CHILDREN'S SERVICE
SHORT TITLE : "Preventing Our Kids from Inhaling Deadly Smoke (PRO-KIDS) Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits the grant of government funding or financial assistance to any person with respect for the provision of children's service by such person unless such person establishes and makes a good faith effort to enforce a non-smkong policy.
