FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 4, 8, 21, 31, 34, 42, 43, 45, 47, 49, 51, 55, 58 AND 80 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9136 ENTITLED "AN ACT ORDAINING REFORMS IN THE ELECTRIC POWER INDUSTRY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
