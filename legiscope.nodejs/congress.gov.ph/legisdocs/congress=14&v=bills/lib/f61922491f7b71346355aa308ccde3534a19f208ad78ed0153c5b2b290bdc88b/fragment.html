House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03078
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TASK FORCE WITHIN THE DEPARTMENT OF SOCIAL WELFARE AND DEVELOPMENT (DSWD) TO GATHER INFORMATION ABOUT, STUDY, AND REPORT TO CONGRESS INCIDENTS OF ABANDONMENT OF INFANT CHILDREN
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Department of Social Welfare and Developments to look into cases of abandonement of infant children and to report such cases, with the corresponding recommendations, to Congress.
