HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02792
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING PUBLIC OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES TO SUBMIT A WRITTEN PERMISSION OR WAIVER IN FAVOR OF THE OMBUDSMAN TO LOOK INTO ALL DEPOSITS OF WHATEVER NATURE WITH BANKS OR BANKING INSTITUTIONS BOTH WITHIN AND OUTSIDE THE PHILIPPINES INCLUDING INVESTMENT BONDS ISSUED BY THE GOVERNMENT OF THE PHILIPPINES, ITS POLITICAL SUBDIVISIONS AND INSTRUMENTALITIES AND PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill requires all public officials and employees to submit within 30 days from assumption to office a waiver authorizing the Ombudsman to look into their bank deposits.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ESCUDERO, SALVADOR III H.
DATE FILED : 2007-10-02
