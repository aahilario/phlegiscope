House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01378
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING TAX REBATES TO INDIVIDUALS PURCHASING RENEWABLE ENERGY DEVISE OR SYSTEM
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Bureau of Internal Revenue to refund to taxpayers, upon their request, 50% of taxes paid for the purchase of renewable energy devices or components thereof.
