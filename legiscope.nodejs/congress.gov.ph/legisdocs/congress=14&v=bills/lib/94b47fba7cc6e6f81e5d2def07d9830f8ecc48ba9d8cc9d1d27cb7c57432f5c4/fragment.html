HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02733
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE ISSUANCE OF CERTIFICATE OF EMPLOYMENT TO THE RESIGNED OR DISMISSED EMPLOYEES AND IMPOSING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill directs all employers to issue a certificate of employment to an employee separated from employment, either by resignation or dismissed. The certificate of employment shall state the length of service, position held, job description and salary rate.
