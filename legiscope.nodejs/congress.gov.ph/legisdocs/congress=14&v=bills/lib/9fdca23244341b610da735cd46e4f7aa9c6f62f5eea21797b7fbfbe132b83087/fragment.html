House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02390
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE COMMISSION ON HIGHER EDUCATION (CHED), AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7722, ENTITLED "AN ACT CREATING THE COMMISSION ON HIGHER EDUCATION, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to iron out the seeming duplication and overlapping of functions performed by the Commission on Higher Education and other existing institutions such as the Professional Regulatory Commission, etc. It proposes to create offices for ladderized programs, for alternative learning systems and for review centers.
