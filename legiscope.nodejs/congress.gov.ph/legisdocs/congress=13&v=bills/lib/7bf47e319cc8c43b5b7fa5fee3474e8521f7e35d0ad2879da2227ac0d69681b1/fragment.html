HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00414
FULL TITLE : AN ACT BANNING FISH CULTURE PENS IN LAKES THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to prohibit the construction of fish culture pens in lakes and tributaries throughout the country. It also authorizes the summary abatement of existing ones as nuisance per set by the local government units that have jurisdiction over the fish culture pens concerned at the expense of the owners thereof without prejudice to their criminal liability.
