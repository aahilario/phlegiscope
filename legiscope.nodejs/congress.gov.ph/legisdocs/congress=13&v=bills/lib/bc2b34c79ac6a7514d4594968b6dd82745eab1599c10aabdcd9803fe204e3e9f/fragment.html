House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01761
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING ALL IRRIGATED AND IRRIGABLE AGRICULTURAL LANDS AS PROTECTED AREAS FOR AGRICULTURE, PROHIBITING ITS CONVERSION FOR USES OTHER THAN AGRICULTURAL AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 20 OF REPUBLIC ACT 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
SHORT TITLE : National Protected Areas for Agriculture (NPAA) Law
ABSTRACT : "National Protected Areas for Agriculture (NPAA) Law." To stop the indiscriminate conversion of agricultural lands to residential, commercial and industrial lands, the bill declares all irrigated and irrigable agricultural lands planted with rice, crops, sugar, coconut and other crops as NPAAs and as such, not subject to conversion for other uses.
