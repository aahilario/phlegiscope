House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01097
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER STRENGTHENING THE PAG-IBIG FUND AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Pag-IBIG Fund Law of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Pag-IBIG Fund Law of 2007". The bill shall rename the Home Development Mutual Fund into the Pag-IBIG which shall be the primary government financial institution charged with mobilizing provident funds for shelter finance. It calls for the mandatory membership to the Pag-IBIG Fund of all employees covered by the GSIS and the SSS.
