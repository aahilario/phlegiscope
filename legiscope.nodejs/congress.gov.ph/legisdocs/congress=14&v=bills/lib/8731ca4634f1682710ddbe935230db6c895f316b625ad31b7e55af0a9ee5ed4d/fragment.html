HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03016
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCLUDING THE ELECTED LEGISLATIVE DISTRICT REPRESENTATIVE AS MEMBER OF THE BOARD OF REGENTS OF STATE UNIVERSITIES OR THE BOARD OF TRUSTEES FOR STATE COLLEGES WITHIN THEIR RESPECTIVE CONGRESSIONAL DISTRICT, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8292, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "HIGHER EDUCATION MODERNIZATION ACT OF 1997"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to include members of the House of Representative as members of the board of regents or board of trustees of state colleges and universities.
