House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01414
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PREVENT THE USE OF TOBACCO PRODUCTS BY THE YOUTH
SHORT TITLE : Prevention of Youth Tobacco Use Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Prevention of Youth Tobacco Use Act of 2004." The bill prohibits any person under 18 years old to possess, smoke or consume tobacco in public. Imposes a fine of not more than P5,000 for violations of the proposed act.
