House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03786
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR BOARD, LODGING AND HOUSING BENEFITS TO EMPLOYEES AND PERSONNEL IN ECOZONE ENTERPRISES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 37 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE ACT OF 1995" AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Ecozone Housing Act"
ABSTRACT : "Ecozone Housing Act." The measure seeks to grant board, lodging and/or housing benefits to the employees and personnel of offices located inside economic zones.
