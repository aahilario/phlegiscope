House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01879
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MAGNA CARTA FOR PUBLIC SOCIAL WELFARE AND DEVELOPMENT WORKERS
SHORT TITLE : Magna Carta for Public Social Welfare and Development Workers
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta for Public Social Welfare and Development Workers." This bill declares is a State policy to: 1) promote and improve the social and economic well-being of public social welfare and development workers, their living conditions and terms of employment; 2) develop their skills and capabilities to make them more responsive and better equipped in delivering social services and programs, and 3) encourage those with proper qualifications and excellent abilities to join and remain in government service.
