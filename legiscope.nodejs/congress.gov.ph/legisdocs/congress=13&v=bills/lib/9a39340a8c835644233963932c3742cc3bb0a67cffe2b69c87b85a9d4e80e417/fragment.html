House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03385
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE PAYMENT OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE TOTAL DISABILITY PENSION TO SENIOR VETERANS OF WARS AND MILITARY CAMPAIGNS
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to start the implementation of the Administrative Total Disability Pension, provided under RA 7696, for all veterans who are at least 70 years old.
