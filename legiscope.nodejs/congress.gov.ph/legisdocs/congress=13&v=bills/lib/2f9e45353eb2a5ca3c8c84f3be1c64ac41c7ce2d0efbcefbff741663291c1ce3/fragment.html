House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04244
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE LEGITIMATION OF CHILDREN BORN TO PARENTS BELOW MARRYING AGE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE FAMILY CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED
SHORT TITLE : Legitimation of Children Born to Parents Below Marrying Age
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to allow illegitimate children born to parents below the marrying age to acquire legitimate status upon the subsequent valid marriage of the parents.
