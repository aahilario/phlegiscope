House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03108
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PHILIPPINE AIR FORCE ACADEMY (PAFA) AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine Air Force Academy Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes within the Philippine Air Force (PAF) the Philippine Air Force Academy that shall be tasked to teach and prepare aviation cadets for military service in the PAF.
