House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03014
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE AS A CLOSED CAREER SERVICE THEREBY AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 292 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "ADMINISTRATIVE CODE OF 1987"
ABSTRACT : The bill declares the Philippine National Police as a closed career authorized to develop its own merit system.
