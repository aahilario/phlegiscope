HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03259
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROTECT THE FILIPINO CONSUMER FROM UNCONTROLLED, UNJUSTIFIED AND ARBITRARY INCREASES IN THE PRICES OF GASOLINE, DIESEL, LIQUIFIED PETROLEUM GAS AND OTHER PETROLEUM PRODUCTS BEING IMPOSED BY THE MEMBERS OF THE DOWNSTREAM OIL INDUSTRY, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE, SECTION 43 OF R.A. 9136, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE ELECTRIC POWER INDUSTRY REFORM ACT 0F 2001
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to strengthen the oversight functions of the Energy Regulatory Commission (ERC). It allows the ERC to, moto propio or upon petition, compel any entity in the downstream oil industry to submit documents and records pertinent to the determination of the prices of domestic petroleum and crude oil products.
