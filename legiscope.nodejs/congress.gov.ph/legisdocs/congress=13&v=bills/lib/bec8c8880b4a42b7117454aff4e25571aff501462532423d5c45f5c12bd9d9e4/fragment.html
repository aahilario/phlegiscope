HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01270
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE AUTOMATIC ANNUAL INCREASE OF THE AMOUNT OF PENSION PAID TO ALL RETIRED GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES BY AT LEAST AN AMOUNT EQUIVALENT TO THE RATE OF ANNUAL INCREASE IN THE NATIONAL LIVING INDEX, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Rationale: Retired government officials, employees and workers deserve the concern of the government for their welfare and well-being, as they had devoted and dedicated the best years of their lives working under the government in the service of the people.
