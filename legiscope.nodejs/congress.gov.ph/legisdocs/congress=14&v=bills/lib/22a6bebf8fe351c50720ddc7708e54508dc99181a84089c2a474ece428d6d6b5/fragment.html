HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02455
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXTENDING FREE PHILHEALTH BENEFITS TO SURVIVING SPOUSE/BENEFICIARIES OF GOOD SAMARITANS WHO WILL DIE IN TRYING TO HELP OR HELPING OTHERS IN DANGERS OR IN RELATED CIRCUMSTANCES IN DEFENDING THE LIFE AND PROPERTY OF A FELLOWMAN AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill grants lifetime Philhealth benefits to the surviving spouse/beneficiaries of those who die doing heroic acts for their fellowmen.
