HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05915
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 43, ARTICLE XII OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9184, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "GOVERNMENT PROCUREMENT REFORM ACT"
ABSTRACT : The bill provides that when international treaties or agreements require suppliers, manufacturers and/or distributors be from the funding country, procurement from eligible suppliers/manufacturers/distributors from the funding country will still be subject to the principles and pertinent provisions of RA 9184.
