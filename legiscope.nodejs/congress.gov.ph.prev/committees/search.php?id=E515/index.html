HOUSE COMMITTEES

LAND USE

 

JURISDICTION: All matters directly and principally relating to land use issues inclusive of the enhancement of resource use and management of indigenous cultural communities, land valuation regulation, preservation of historical and cultural heritage sites, and public-private partnership as well as linkages among national and local agencies and stakeholders in land resource management.

CHAIRPERSON :

Matugas, Francisco Jose "Bingo" II F. CPA, CESO, LLB

District Representative
Surigao del Norte, 1st District
