House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00454
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING ALL OFFICIAL NON-WORKING DAYS TO FALL EITHER ON A MONDAY OR A FRIDAY (House passed its Senate version (SB02119) on Feb. 07, 2007)
SHORT TITLE : Declaring All Official Non-Working Days to Fall Either on a Monday or a Friday
ABSTRACT : It has been observed that when a holiday falls on a Tuesday, employees tend to be absent on the Monday preceding the Tuesday holiday and on the Friday following the Thursday holiday. This bill proposes that all official non-working holidays be mandatorily scheduled to be observed on either a Monday of a Friday. This bill aims to solve the problem of "intentional absenteeism" among workers.
