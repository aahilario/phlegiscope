FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING PRESCHOOL AND PUBLIC ELEMENTARY SCHOOL CHILDREN FEEDING PROGRAM AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "National Preschool and Public Elementary School Children Feeding Program Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "National Preschool and Public Elementary School Children Feeding Program Act of 2007". The bill seeks to provide a regular allotment of nutritious diet to preschool and public elementary school children below 12 years old.
