FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 286 OF REPUBLIC ACT 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, PROVIDING FOR THE AUTOMATIC APPROPRIATION AND RELEASE OF THE INTERNAL REVENUE ALLOTMENT OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS
ABSTRACT : The bill calls for the automatic appropriation and release of the internal revenue allotment of local government units without need of any further action.
