House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00108
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 285 AND 286 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, PROVIDING FOR AN EQUITABLE DISTRIBUTION OF THE INTERNAL REVENUE ALLOTMENT (IRA) TO LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS
ABSTRACT : The primary objective for distributing internal revene allotment (IRA) to local government units (LGUs) is to lessen the fiscal disparities among urban and rural communities. Unfortunately, the IRA distribution formula under the Local Government Code does not actually address the equity objective. In effect, the well-developed LGUs get bigger IRA shares than those which need them the most. To correct this inequity, the bill proposes to introduce poverty-sensitive factors in the IRA formula in lieu of population, land area and equal sharing. The poverty-sensitive factors are population density, poverty incidence and income class of the LGU. It also allows LGUs to automatically retain their IRA shares.
