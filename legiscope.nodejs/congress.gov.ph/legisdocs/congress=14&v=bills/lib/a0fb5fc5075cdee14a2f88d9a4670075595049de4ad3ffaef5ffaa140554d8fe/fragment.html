House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00127
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A RETIREMENT BENEFIT SYSTEM FOR PROSECUTORS IN THE NATIONAL PROSECUTION SERVICE OF THE DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE AND IN THE OFFICE OF THE OMBUDSMAN, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Expanded Retirement Law for Prosecutors in the National Prosecution Service and in the Office of the Ombudsman"
ABSTRACT : "Expanded Retirement Law for Prosecutors in the National Prosecution Service and in the Office of the Ombudsman". The bill seeks to grant prosecutors a retirement package commensurate to the demands and pressures of their job.
