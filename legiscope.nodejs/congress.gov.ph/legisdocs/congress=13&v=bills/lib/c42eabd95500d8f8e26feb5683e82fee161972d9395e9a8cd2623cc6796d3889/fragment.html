HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02677
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RENAMING THE COMMISSION ON HIGHER EDUCATION AS THE DEPARTMENT OF HIGHER EDUCATION, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to rationalize and strengthen the Commission on Higher Education (CHEd) to enable it to discharge properly and effectively its mandate and to rename the CHEd as the Department of Higher Education to be headed by a Secretary who shall have a cabinet rank.
