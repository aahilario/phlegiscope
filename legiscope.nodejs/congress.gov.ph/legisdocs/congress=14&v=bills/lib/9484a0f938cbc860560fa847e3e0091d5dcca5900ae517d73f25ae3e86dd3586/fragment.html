HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00547
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 20 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY TO BE KNOWN AS NATIONAL CHILDREN'S DAY
ABSTRACT : The bill declares November 20th of every year as a special nonworking holiday to be known as National Children's Day in consonance with the United Nations General Assembly's ratification of the Convention on the Rights of the Child.
