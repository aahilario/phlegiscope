HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02508
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STANDARDIZING PRESCHOOL EDUCATION PROGRAM PROVIDED IN BARANGAY DAY CARE CENTERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE BARANGAY-LEVEL TOTAL DEVELOPMENT AND PROTECTION OF CHILDREN ACT
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Department of Education, in coordination with the Department of Social Welfare and Development, to prepare a standard curriculum in all barangay day care centers for children 4 to 6 years old.
