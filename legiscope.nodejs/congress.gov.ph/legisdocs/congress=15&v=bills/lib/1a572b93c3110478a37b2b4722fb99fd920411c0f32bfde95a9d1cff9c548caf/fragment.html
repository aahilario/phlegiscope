House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00087
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ENSURE THAT EVERY FILIPINO IS GRANTED WITH BASIC HEALTHCARE SERVICES, PROVIDING FOR THE PURPOSE A MANDATORY UNIVERSAL HEALTHCARE COVERAGE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Mandatory Universal Healthcare Coverage Of Every Filipino Act
ABSTRACT : “Mandatory Universal Healthcare Coverage of Every Filipino Act.” Provides for automatic enrolment of all Filipinos which shall be funded by the national government through the inclusion of the required contribution in the annual General Appropriations Act.
