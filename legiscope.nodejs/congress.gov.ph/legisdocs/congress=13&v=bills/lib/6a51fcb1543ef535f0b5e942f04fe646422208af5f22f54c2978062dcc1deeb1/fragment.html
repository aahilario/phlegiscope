House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00129
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE SUBSIDY OVERSIGHT COUNCIL AND PROVIDING THE RULES FOR A RATIONAL MANAGEMENT OF THE GOVERNMENT SUBSIDY SYSTEM
SHORT TITLE : The Subsidy Oversight Council Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "The Subsidy Oversight Council Act of 2004". An important feature of the bill is the creation of the Subsidy Oversight Council that shall serve as a supervisory and recommendatory commission. The Council is tasked to monitor the grant of incentives, government loan guarantees, debt condonation and concessional loans. All government agencies which are empowered to grant subsidies, shall regularly report on such activities to the Council.
