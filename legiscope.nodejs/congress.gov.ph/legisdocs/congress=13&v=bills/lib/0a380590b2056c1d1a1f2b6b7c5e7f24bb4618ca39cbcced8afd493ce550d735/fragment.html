HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03663
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING ARTICLE NINETY-NINE OF ACT NUMBERED THIRTY-EIGHT HUNDRED FIFTEEN, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED PENAL CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to vest the authority to grant allowance for good conduct to prisoners to the following: a) the Director, Bureau of Corrections for insular prisoners; b) the Provincial Governor for provincial prisoners; and c) the Chief, Bureau of Jail Management and Penology for city and municipal prisoners.
