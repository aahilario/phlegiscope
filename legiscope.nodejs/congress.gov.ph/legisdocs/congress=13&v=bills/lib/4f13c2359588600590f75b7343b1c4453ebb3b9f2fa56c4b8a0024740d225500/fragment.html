House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01004
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STREAMLINING AND STRENGTHENING THE PHILIPPINE DISASTER PREPAREDNESS AND PREVENTION CAPABILITY, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The Philippines Disaster Preparedness and Prevention Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "The Philippines Disaster Preparedness and Prevention Act of 2004." For a faster and more effective response to natural and man-made disasters and emergencies, the bill seeks to streamline and strengthen the National Disaster Management Council. It provides a mechanism for the allocation, release and monitoring of the national calamity fund.
