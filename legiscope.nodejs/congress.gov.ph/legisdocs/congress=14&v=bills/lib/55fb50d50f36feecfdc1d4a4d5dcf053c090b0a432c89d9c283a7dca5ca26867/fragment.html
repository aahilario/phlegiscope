House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02739
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 209 KNOWN AS THE FAMILY CODE, ARTICLE 111
ABSTRACT : The bill replaces the term "spouse of age" with the more accurate term " either spouse" since a spouse must necessarily be of age.
