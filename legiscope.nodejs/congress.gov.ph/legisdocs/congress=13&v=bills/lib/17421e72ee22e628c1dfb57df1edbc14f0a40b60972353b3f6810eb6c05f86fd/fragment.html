HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03031
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO SPUR THE PLANTING OF A BILLION TREES THROUGH COMPLEMENTARY TREE PLANTING PROGRAMS OF THE DENR AND VARIOUS GOVERNMENT AGENCIES, LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS AND COMMUNITIES, SCHOOLS AND UNIVERSITIES, AND CIVIC AND NON-GOVERNMENTAL ORGANIZATIONS, PROTECTING THE REMAINING NATURAL FORESTS AND FOREST PLANTATIONS, AND CREATING A FUND THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Billion Trees Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Billion Trees Act of 2004." The bill seeks to enlist the participation of the different sectors of society in the reforestation of our denuded forest lands and idle alienable and disposable lands.
