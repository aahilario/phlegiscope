HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00400
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING A SYSTEM OF ALLOWING PARALLEL IMPORTATION OF MEDICINES INTO THE COUNTRY
ABSTRACT : To enable the Department of Health to perform its role in the development of public health, the bill proposes to allow the parallel importation of medicines into the Philippines. The parallel importation of medicines in competition with exclusive distributors would make medicines affordable and compel local manufacturers to lower their prices to more realistic levels.
