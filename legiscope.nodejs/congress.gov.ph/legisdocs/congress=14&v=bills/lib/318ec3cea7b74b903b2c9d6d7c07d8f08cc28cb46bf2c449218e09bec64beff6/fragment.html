HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00357
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE MONTHLY BENEFITS OF BARANGAY OFFICIALS AND PROVIDING ALLOWANCES TO BARANGAY TANODS, BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS AND BARANGAY SOCIAL WORKERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE, THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill increases the monthly honoraria of barangay officials and provides allowances to barangay tanods, members of the Lupong Tagapamayapa, barangay health workers and barangay social workers.
