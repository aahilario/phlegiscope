HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00319
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING AN ELECTIVE PUBLIC OFFICIAL FROM RESIGNING HIS ELECTIVE OFFICE TO ACCEPT AN APPOINTMENT TO ANY GOVERNMENT OFFICE AND PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill disqualifies an elective official who resigns his elective office from being appointed to any government position during the unexpired term of the office he resigned from.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : TAÑADA, LORENZO III M.
