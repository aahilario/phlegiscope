House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03198
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING A MAGNA CARTA FOR THE POOR
SHORT TITLE : "Magna Carta for the Poor"
ABSTRACT : The bill clearly defines the basic irreducible rights of the poor, to wit: a. the right to employment; b. the right to food; c. the right to free quality education; d. the right to shelter; and e. the right to free health services and medicine.
