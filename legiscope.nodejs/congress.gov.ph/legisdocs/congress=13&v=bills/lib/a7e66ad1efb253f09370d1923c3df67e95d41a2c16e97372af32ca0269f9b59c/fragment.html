HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02453
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXTENDING THE TERM OF BARANGAY AND SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN OFFICIALS AND SETTING THE DATE OF ITS ELECTION, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9164
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to postpone the synchronized election for barangay and sangguniang kabataan officials to the 2nd Monday of May 2007 to coincide with the national and local elections and to correspondingly extend their term of office to June 30, 2007.
