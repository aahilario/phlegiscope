House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03787
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THREE (3) SMALL FARMERS' REPRESENTATIVES IN THE NATIONAL IRRIGATION ADMINISTRATION (NIA) BOARD BY AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THIRTY-SIX HUNDRED AND ONE, AS AMENDED, "AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL IRRIGATION ADMINISTRATION" AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the inclusion in the Board of Directors of the National Irrigation Administration of 3 representatives from small farmers representing Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao.
