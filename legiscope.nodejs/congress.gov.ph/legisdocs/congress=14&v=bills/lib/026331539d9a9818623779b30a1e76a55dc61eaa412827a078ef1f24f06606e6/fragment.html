House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00123
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING THE AGRICULTURAL AND FISHERIES MODERNIZATION ACT (AFMA) OTHERWISE KNOWN AS REPUBLIC ACT 8435 BY PROVIDING FOR THE GRANT OF VAT EXEMPTION TO ALL ENUMERATED AGRICULTURAL INPUTS
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to exempt from the ambit of the value-added tax all agricultural inputs to lower the cost of agricultural products.
