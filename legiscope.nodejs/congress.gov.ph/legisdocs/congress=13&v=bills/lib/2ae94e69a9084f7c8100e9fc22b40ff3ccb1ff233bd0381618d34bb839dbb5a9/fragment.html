House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02723
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE TITLING AND REGISTRATION OF ALL SCHOOL SITES IN THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY WHERE THEY ARE LOCATED AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Department of Justice to promulgate the guidelines for the titling and registration of all school sites in the name of the municipality where the school is located.
