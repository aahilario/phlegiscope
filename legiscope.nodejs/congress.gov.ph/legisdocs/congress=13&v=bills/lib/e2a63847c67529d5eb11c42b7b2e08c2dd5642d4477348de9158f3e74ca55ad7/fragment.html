House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00406
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING LOCAL FISCAL AUTONOMY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : Various opinions rendered by national government agencies have unduly restricted the utilization of local government funds by the local government units (LGUs). This bill seeks to strengthen the fiscal autonomy of LGU in terms of disposition, allocation and utilization of local government funds from whatever source. It also aims to increase the share of LGUs in internal revenue taxes and other national taxes and fees.
