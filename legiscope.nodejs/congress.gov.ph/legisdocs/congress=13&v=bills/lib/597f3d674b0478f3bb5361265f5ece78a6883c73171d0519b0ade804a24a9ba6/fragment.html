HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00727
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING F0R THE INCLUSION OF BASIC ECONOMIC SUBJECTS AS PART OF THE CURRICULUM OF THE MILITARY AND POLICE COURSE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Coup d' etat, as an act of destabilization, gravely affects our political and economic stability. Therefore, it is crucial for our military and police personnel to understand the relevance of economic stability for the country. This bill seeks the inclusion of basic economic subjects in the curriculum of military and police courses to provide an understanding on the effects of destabilizing acts and activities on the country's economy.
