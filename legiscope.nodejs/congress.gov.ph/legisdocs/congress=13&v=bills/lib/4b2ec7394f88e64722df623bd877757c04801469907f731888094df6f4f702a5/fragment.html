House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01466
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE PROTECTION AND CONSERVATION OF ALL OBJECTS OF UNDERWATER CULTURAL HERITAGE IN PHILIPPINE WATERS
SHORT TITLE : Protection of Underwater Cultural Heritage Act of 2004.
ABSTRACT : "Protection of Underwater Cultural Heritage Act of 2004." Aims to protect and preserve underwater treasures by setting the requirement for archeology, exploration, excavation of shipwrecks and sunken vessels to those at least 100 years old.
