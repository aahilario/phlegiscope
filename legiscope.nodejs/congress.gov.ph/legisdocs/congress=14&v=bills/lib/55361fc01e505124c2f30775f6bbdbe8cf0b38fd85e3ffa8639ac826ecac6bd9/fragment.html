House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00663
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MORE RESPONSIVE SYSTEM OF CIVIL REGISTRATION
SHORT TITLE : "New Civil Registration Act of 2007
ABSTRACT : "New Civil Registration Act of 2007". The bill seeks to update the basic law on civil registration (Act No. 3753) to address present problems in civil registration and to conform with the latest laws and practices in civil registration.
