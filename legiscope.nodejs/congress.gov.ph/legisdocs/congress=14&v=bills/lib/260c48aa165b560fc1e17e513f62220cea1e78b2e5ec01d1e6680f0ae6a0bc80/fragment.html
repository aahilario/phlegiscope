House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01409
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING AND PENALIZING THE PILFERAGE AND THEFT, UNAUTHORIZED USE, INTERCONNECTION, OR RECEPTION OF ANY SIGNAL OR SERVICE OFFERED OVER A CABLE TELEVISION (CATV) OR CABLE INTERNET SYSTEM AND/OR NETWORK OR THROUGH ANY UNAUTHORIZED INSTALLATION, ACCESS OR CONNECTION THERETO THROUGH THE USE OF CABLE OR OTHER EQUIPMENT AND PRESCRIBING PENALTIES THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "the Anti-cable Television And Cable Internet Pilferage Act Of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The Anti-Cable Television and Cable Internet Pilferage Act of 2007". The bill declares the illegal interception of cable television as a crime and provides penalties for violation of the proposed act.
