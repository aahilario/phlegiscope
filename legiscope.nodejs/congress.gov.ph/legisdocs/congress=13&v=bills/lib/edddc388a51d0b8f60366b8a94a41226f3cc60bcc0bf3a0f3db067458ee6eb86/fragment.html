House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00701
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING THE CHARTER OF THE PHILIPPINE CHARITY SWEEPSTAKES OFFICE, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to redirect a portion of the charity fund of the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office (PCSO) to government institutions that implement social welfare and health programs. It grants the Department of Health and the Department of Social Welfare and Development 15% and 10% share, respectively, of the charity fund of the PCSO to finance their priority programs.
