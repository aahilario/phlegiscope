HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00073
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING EMPLOYERS IN THE PRIVATE SECTOR TO HAVE IN THEIR EMPLOY DISABLED PERSONS, AND PROVIDING ADDITIONAL INCENTIVE THEREFOR, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SEVENTY-TWO SEVENTY-SEVEN OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE MAGNA CARTA FOR DISABLED PERSONS
ABSTRACT : Requires private employers to hire disabled persons who have qualified in accordance with the provision of RA07277 and other applicable labor laws, and for such employed persons to constitute a minimum percentage of their total work forces.
