House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02888
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING REFUSAL TO ENROLL STUDENTS, RELEASE CARDS AND OTHER DOCUMENTS FOR CLEARANCE PURPOSES, IN PUBLIC SCHOOLS FOR FAILURE TO PAY MISCELLANEOUS AND OTHER FEES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to penalize any public school teacher or principal who refuses to enroll a student or release a report card because of non-payment of miscellaneous and other fees.
