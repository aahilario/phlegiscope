House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04322
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING FULL TRANSPARENCY IN TAX COLLECTION, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 71 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Instituting Full Transparency in Tax Collection
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to institute full transparency in tax collection by publishing the names, addresses, type of business and the amount of taxes (income and value-added) paid by an individual, corporation and/or partnership.
