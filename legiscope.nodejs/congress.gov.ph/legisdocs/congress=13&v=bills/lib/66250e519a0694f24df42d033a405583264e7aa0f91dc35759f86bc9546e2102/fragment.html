House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03534
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MODERNIZATION OF FIRE PROTECTION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Fire Protection Modernization Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Fire Protection Modernization Act of 2004." The bill proposes the Fire Protection Modernization Program that shall consist of: a) the establishment of fire protection services in all local government units; b) the upgrading of existing fire protection equipment and services; c) the establishment of specialized protection and related services; and d) the establishment of training facilities.
