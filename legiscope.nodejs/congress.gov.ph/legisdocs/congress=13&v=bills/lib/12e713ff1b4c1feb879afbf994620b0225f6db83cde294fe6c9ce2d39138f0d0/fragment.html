HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04382
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL PUBLIC SCHOOLS TO REGISTER ALL REAL PROPERTIES USED AS SCHOOL SITES IN THE NAME OF THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates all public schools to register land titles to real properties, public or private, used as school sites, in the name of the Department of Education to enable the school authorities to have direct control and supervision over land occupied by public schools.
