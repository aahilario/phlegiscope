HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00375
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CREATION OF TRAINING, LIVELIHOOD, AND EMPLOYMENT CENTERS IN CITIES AND MUNICIPALITIES NATIONWIDE, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to bring education to the grasroots level and promote employment opportunities by creating training, livelihood and employment centers in cities and selected municipalities nationwide. The centers will act as venues for skills development, job assistance and placement, employment generation and acquisition of technology and market information.
