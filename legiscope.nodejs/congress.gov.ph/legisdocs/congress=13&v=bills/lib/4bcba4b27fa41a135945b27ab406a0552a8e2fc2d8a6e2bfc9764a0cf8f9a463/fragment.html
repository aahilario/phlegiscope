HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00979
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO RESTORE THE TAX EXEMPTION PRIVILEGES OF ELECTRIC COOPERATIVES CREATED UNDER PRESIDENTIAL DECREE 269, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "NATIONAL ELECTRIFICATION ADMINISTRATION DECREE" AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE SECTIONS 193 AND 234(d) OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991"
ABSTRACT : This measure grants tax exemptions to electric cooperatives to sustain their financial viability and enable them to serve the poor areas in the countryside which the private corporations refuse to serve for being economically unviable.
