HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01126
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO INCLUDE LIQUEFIED PETROLEUM GAS (LPG) AND KEROSENE IN THE LIST OF BASIC NECESSITIES IN SECTION THREE (3), DEFINITION OF TERMS OF REPUBLIC ACT 7581, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "PRICE ACT"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to include LPG in the list of basic commodities so that its price can automatically be controlled or "frozen" during calamities, disasters and other emergency situations.
