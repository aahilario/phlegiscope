House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01393
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 32 (h) OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 4136 HEREBY REQUIRING THE MANDATORY COMPLIANCE BY ALL MOTORCYCLES TO AUTOMATICALLY TURN ON AND RIDE WITH THEIR HEADLIGHTS ON AT ALL HOURS OF THE DAY ON ALL ROADS
SHORT TITLE : Automatic Headlights on for Motorcycles Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Automatic Headlights On for Motorcycles Act of 2004." For the safety of all motorcycle drivers and passengers thereon and the commuting public, in general, all motorcycles are required to run on all roads with headlights turned on at all hours of the day. It requires motorcycle manufacturers to ensure that an automatic headlights on system is installed before sale.
