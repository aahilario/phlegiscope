HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03252
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE MONTHLY ALLOWANCES OF BARANGAY DAY CARE WORKERS FROM FIVE HUNDRED PESOS (Php500.00) TO ONE THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED PESOS (Php1,500.00) AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTY-NINE HUNDRED AND SEVENTY-TWO
ABSTRACT : The bill grants barangay day care workers in accredited day care centers a monthly allowance of not less than P1,500.
