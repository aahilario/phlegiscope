HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HR01508
FULL TITLE : RESOLUTION DIRECTING THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, THROUGH ITS APPROPRIATE COMMITTEES, TO CONDUCT AN IMMEDIATE INQUIRY, IN AID OF LEGISLATION, INTO THE RECRUITMENT RULES AND PROCEDURES GOVERNING THE CONSCRIPTION AND PLACEMENT OF OVERSEAS FILIPINO WORKERS, WITH THE END IN VIEW OF RECOMMENDING LEGISLATION THAT WILL, AMONG OTHERS, AFFORD MAXIMUM PROTECTION TO SUCH OVERSEAS WORKERS, PROTECT THE INTERGRITY OF THE RECRUITMENT PROCESS AND DETERMINE, DELINEATE AND FIX RESPONSIBILITY FOR THE MISTREATMENT, MALTREATMENT AND ABUSE OF FILIPINO WORKERS OVERSEAS
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SYJUCO, JUDY
