FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE SALE OF ALCOHOL AND ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES, CIGARETTES, CIGARS AND OTHER TOBACCO PRODUCTS IN LICENSED PREMISES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Tobacco and Alcohol Licensed Premises of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The Tobacco and Alcohol Licensed Premises of 2007". The bill establishes an integrated scheme for licensing premises that are used for the supply of alcohol, cigarettes, cigar and other tobacco products.
