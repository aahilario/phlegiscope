House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02132
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL PROGRAM FOR FILIPINO GIFTED CHILDREN, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Gifted Children Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Gifted Children Act of 2004." The bill aims to establish a national program for gifted Filipino children in the arts, humanities and the sciences. It establishes a Center for Gifted Children and Science Oriented Youth to implement the national program for gifted children.
