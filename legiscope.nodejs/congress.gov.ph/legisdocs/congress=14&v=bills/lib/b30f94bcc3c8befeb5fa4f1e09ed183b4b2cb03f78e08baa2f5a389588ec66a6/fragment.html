HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02832
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A SHORTER MATURITY PERIOD FOR LAND BANK OF THE PHILIPPINES BONDS GIVEN TO LAND OWNERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6657, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE COMPREHENSIVE AGRARIAN REFORM ACT OF 1988
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to shorten the maturity period of LBP (Land Bank of the PHilippines) bonds issued to landowners as compensation for their land falling under the coverage of the Comprehensive Agrarian Reform Program.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : GARAY, FLORENCIO C.
DATE FILED : 2007-10-04
