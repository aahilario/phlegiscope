House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03179
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REGULATE THE TRADE, MARKETING, ADVERTISING AND PROMOTIONS OF INFANT FORMULA AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits the advertising or promotions of infant formula and complementary food products intended for infants between 0-6 months. It seeks to promote breastfeeding during the first six months of infants.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : GUNIGUNDO, MAGTANGGOL T.
