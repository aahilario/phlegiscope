HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02276
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DISENGAGING PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS FROM COMPULSORY ELECTION DUTIES AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 13 OF REPUBLIC ACT No. 6646, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE ELECTORAL REFORMS LAW OF 1987
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to tap volunteer groups and individuals duly accredited by the Commission on Elections as members of the board of election inspectors. This proposal shall free public school teachers from compulsory election duties as is currently practiced.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : REYES, CARMENCITA O.
DATE FILED : 2007-08-28
