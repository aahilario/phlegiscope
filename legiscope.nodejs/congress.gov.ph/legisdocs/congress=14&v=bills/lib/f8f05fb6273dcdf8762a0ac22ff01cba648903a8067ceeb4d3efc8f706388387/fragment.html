House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01267
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CHAPTER VI, PROHIBITIONS AND PENALTIES AND PROVIDING AN ADDITIONAL CHAPTER ON LEGAL ACTIONS TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8550, ENTITLED 'AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE DEVELOPMENT, MANAGEMENT AND CONSERVATION OF THE FISHERIES AND AQUATIC RESOURCES, INTEGRATING ALL LAWS PERTINENT THERETO, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES' "
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to curb, if not totally eliminate, the illegal activities of commercial fishing operators in municipal fishing grounds by imposing higher penalties and stiffer fines.
