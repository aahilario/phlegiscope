House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00721
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE FULL IMPLEMENTATION OF THE NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE PROGRAM' COMPULSORY COVERAGE FOR ALL FILIPINO RESIDENTS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT 7875 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE ACT OF 1995", AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the compulsory health insurance coverage of all citizens of the Philippines under the National Health Insurance Program effective immediately.
