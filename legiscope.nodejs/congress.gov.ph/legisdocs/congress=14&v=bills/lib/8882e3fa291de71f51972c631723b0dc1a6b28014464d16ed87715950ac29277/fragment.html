House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01641
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO INCREASE THE PENALTY FOR THEFT OR ROBBERY COMMITTED WITHIN THE PREMISES OF CHURCHES, TEMPLES, AND MUSEUMS
SHORT TITLE : Increasing The Penalty For Theft Or Robbery Committed Within The Premises Of Churches, Temples, And Museums
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes for the penalty to be imposed on crimes committed within a church, temple or public museum to be higher by two degrees that that imposable for theft or robbery.
