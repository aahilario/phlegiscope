HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05062
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING DELINQUENT EMPLOYERS A ONE-TIME OPPORTUNITY TO PAY ALL UNREMITTED AND/OR UNPAID CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE SOCIAL SECURITY SYSTEM WITHOUT INCURRING THE PRESCRIBED PENALTIES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to grant delinquent employers a one-time opportunity to pay all contributions due and payable without incurring the prescribed penalties.
