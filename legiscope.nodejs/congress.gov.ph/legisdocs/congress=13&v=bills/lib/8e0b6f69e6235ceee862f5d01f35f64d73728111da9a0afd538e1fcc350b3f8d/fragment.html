HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02895
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING TAXPAYERS THE ANNUAL FILING OF STATEMENT OF ASSETS, LIABILITIES AND NETWORTH, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the mandatory annual submission to the Bureau of Internal Revenue of a Statement of Assets, Liabilities and Networth by all natural and juridical persons earning more than P200,000 or owning a real and/or personal properties valued at P500,000.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : REMULLA, JESUS CRISPIN C.
