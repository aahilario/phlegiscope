House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01123
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6938, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE COOPERATIVE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Cooperative Code of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Cooperative Code of 2004." The measure increases the members required to form a cooperative from 15 to 30 and raises the paid-up share capitalization to P30,000. It requires cooperatives to hire a bookkeeper.
