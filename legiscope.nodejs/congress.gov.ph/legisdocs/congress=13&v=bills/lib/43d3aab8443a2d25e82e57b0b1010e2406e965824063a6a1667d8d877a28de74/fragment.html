FULL TITLE : AN ACT LOWERING THE RETIREMENT AGE FOR PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS FROM SIXTY (60)TO FIFTY-FIVE (55) YEARS OLD AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Retires a 55-year old public school teacher who has served for 20 years with all the retirement benefits granted under existing laws.
