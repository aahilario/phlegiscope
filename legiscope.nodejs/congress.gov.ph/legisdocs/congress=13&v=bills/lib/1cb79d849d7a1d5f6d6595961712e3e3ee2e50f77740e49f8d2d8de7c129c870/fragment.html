House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03413
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXPANDING THE PROHIBITED ACTS OF DISCRIMINATION AGAINST WOMEN ON ACCOUNT OF SEX, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLES 135 AND 137 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : Expanding the Prohibited Acts of Discrimination Against Women on Account of Sex
ABSTRACT : The bill expands the prohibited acts of discrimination against women with respect to terms and conditions of employment to include the following: a) the hiring process; b) assignment of work; and c) dismissal of personnel.
