HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04989
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER PROMOTING FOREIGN INVESTMENTS IN THE AUTO INDUSTRY,AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9224, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to levy, access and collect 0% excise tax on vehicles powered by alternative fuels to promote foreign investments and also to address the increasing cost of fuel in the world market.
