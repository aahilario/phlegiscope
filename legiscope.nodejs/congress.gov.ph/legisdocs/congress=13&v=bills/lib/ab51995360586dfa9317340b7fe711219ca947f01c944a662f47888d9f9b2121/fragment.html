HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02102
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOWING LATE REGISTRANTS TO REGISTER THEIR BIRTH IN THE OFFICE OF THE LOCAL CIVIL REGISTRAR OF EITHER THE PLACE OF THEIR RESIDENCE OR THE PLACE OF THEIR BIRTH
ABSTRACT : The bill allows late registrants to register their birth at the Office of the Local Civil Registrar in either the place of their birth or their present residence. The later registrant must submit the following documents: a) a declaration by either parent, relative or any person who has knowledge of the birth of the late registrant; and b) an affidavit explaining the delayed registration.
