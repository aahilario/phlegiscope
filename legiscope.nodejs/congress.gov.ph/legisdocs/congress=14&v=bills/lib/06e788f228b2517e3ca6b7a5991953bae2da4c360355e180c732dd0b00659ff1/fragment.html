House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00510
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE WORKERS' COMPENSATION PROGRAM, THE WORK CONTINGENCY INSURANCE FUND, THE PHILIPPINE WORKERS' COMPENSATION AUTHORITY AND DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS AND AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE ARTICLES 166 TO 208-A OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE FOUR HUNDRED FORTY TWO (PD 442), AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Workers' Compensation Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Workers' Compensation Act of 2007". The bill provides a clear description of the Workers Compensation Program and its components, namely, coverage, benefits, rehabilitation of occupationally disabled workers, and work contingency prevention. It seeks the creation of a Work Contingency Insurance Fund from which payment of benefits shall be sourced.
