HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00427
FULL TITLE : AN ACT BANNING AND PROHIBITING THE USE, PURCHASE AND STORAGE OF ELEMENTAL MERCURY BY ALL PUBLIC AND PRIVATE SCHOOLS AND INSTITUTIONS; AND TO DIRECT THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES TO COMPILE AND DISTRIBUTE MATERIALS FOR SCHOOLS ON THE HAZARDS OF MERCURY AND ACTIONS WHICH MAY BE TAKEN TO REDUCE SUCH HAZARDS
SHORT TITLE : "Safe Schools Mercury Reduction Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Safe Schools Mercury Reduction Act of 2007". The bill prohibits the purchase, use or storage of mercury substances and mercury instruments by public and private schools and other educational institutions.
