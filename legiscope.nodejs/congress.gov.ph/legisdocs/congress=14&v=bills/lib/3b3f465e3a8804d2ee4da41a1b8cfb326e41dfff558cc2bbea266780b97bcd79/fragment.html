House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02287
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN THE UNIVERSITY OF THE PHILIPPINES AS THE PREMIER STATE UNIVERSITY
SHORT TITLE : "The University of the Philippines Charter of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The University of the Philippines Charter of 2007". bill aims to strengthen the University of the Philippines as the premier university in the country. It provides for a restructured Board of Regents and the creation of consultative assemblies and student councils.
