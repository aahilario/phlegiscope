House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04206
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ENABLING THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT TO ACQUIRE MAJORITY CONTROL OF THE EQUITY OF PETRON CORPORATION BY BUYING BACK EQUITIES FROM PRIVATE ENTITIES APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : An Act Gaining Majority Control of Petron Corporation
ABSTRACT : "An Act Gaining Majority Control of Petron Corporation". The bill authorizes the national government through the Philippine National Oil Corporation to buy the appropriate amount of stocks in order to gain majority control of Petron Corporation.
