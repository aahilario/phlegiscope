HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02438
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING A COMPREHENSIVE PROGRAM TO ADDRESS THE LONG-TERM CARE NEEDS OF SENIOR CITIZENS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Senior Citizens' Long-Term Care Act'"
ABSTRACT : The proposed long-term care program for senior citizens shall include the following components: a) social protection, b) developmental intervention/activities; c) advancement of health and well-being; and d) provision of an enabling and supportive environment.
