House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00695
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING APPROPRIATE CIVIL SERVICE ELIGIBILITY TO ELECTED LOCAL GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill grants eligibility to the following local officials who have served for 5 years: governor, vice-governor, mayor, vice-mayor and members of the sanggunian.
