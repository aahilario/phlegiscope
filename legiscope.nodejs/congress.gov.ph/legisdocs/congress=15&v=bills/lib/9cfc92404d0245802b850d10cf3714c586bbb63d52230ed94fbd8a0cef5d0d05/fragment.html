House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00018
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING THE PRESCHOOL EDUCATION INTO THE BASIC EDUCATION SYSTEM AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Preschool Education Act
ABSTRACT : “Preschool Education Act.” Seeks to provide equal opportunities for all children to avail of free and compulsory preschool education that effectively promotes physical, social, intellectual, emotional and skills stimulation and values formation to sufficiently prepare them for formal elementary schooling.
