House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00563
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MORE RESPONSIVE SYSTEM OF CIVIL REGISTRATION
SHORT TITLE : New Civil Registration Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "New Civil Registration Act of 2004". Documents concerning the civil status of persons such as birth certificate, death, marriage, and court decrees and orders have become essential instruments of every citizen. However, the basic law that governs this sytem is already outdated. The bill aims to create a new system of civil registration to make it more responsive.
