HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00626
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CREATION OF A SPECIAL EDUCATION FUND INTENDED FOR SCHOOL BUILDINGS AND FACILITIES, TO BE ALLOCATED FROM THE EXCISE TAX COLLECTIONS ON ALCOHOL AND TOBACCO PRODUCTS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure establishes the Special Education Fund to be funded from the excise tax collection on tobacco and alcoholic products. The Fund shall be earmarked for the construction of school buildings and facilities.
