House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01601
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REGULATE THE PRICES OF MATRICULATION, TUITION AND OTHER SCHOOL FEES IN ALL PUBLIC AND PRIVATE SCHOOLS NATIONWIDE, CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE A SCHOOL FEE BOARD IN ALL SCHOOL LEVELS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : School Fees Regulation Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "School Fees Regulation Act of 2004." The bill proposes the creation of the School Fee Board in all levels in public and private schools to monitor, study, evaluate and regulate matriculation, tuition and other school fees.
