House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03305
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING AND PENALIZING THE PRODUCTION, PRINTING, PUBLICATION, IMPORTATION, SALE, DISTRIBUTION AND EXHIBITION OF OBSCENE AND PORNOGRAPHIC MATERIALS, AND THE EXHIBITION OF LIVE SEXUAL ACTS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ART. 201 OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AS AMENDED
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Obscenity and Pornography Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The measure enumerates the list of punishable acts in relation to the production, printing, publication, importation, sale, exhibition and distribution of obscene or pornographic materials in all forms of mass media.
