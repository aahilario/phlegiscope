HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04766
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INTRODUCING FURTHER REFORMS AND MODERNIZATION IN THE ELECTORAL SYSTEM, INCREASING PENALTY FOR CERTAIN ELECTION OFFENSES AND FOR OTHER PUROSES
ABSTRACT : The bill introduces the following reforms in the electoral system: a) the issuance of electronic identification cards and biometric identification of voters; and b) the use of electronic and biometric machines in polling precincts.
