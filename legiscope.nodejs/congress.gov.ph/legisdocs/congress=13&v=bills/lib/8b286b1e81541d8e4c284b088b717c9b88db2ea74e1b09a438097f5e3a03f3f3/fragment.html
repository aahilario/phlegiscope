HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01295
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MANDATORY AUTOPSY OF ANY PERSON WHO DIED IN A VIOLENT, SUSPICIOUS, QUESTIONABLE, UNUSUAL AND/OR UNNATURAL MANNER, THE CONDITIONS FOR THE PROPER ISSUANCE OF THE DEATH CERTIFICATE OF SAID PERSON AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Autopsy Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Autopsy Act of 2004." Rationale: The autopsy shall be conducted in a manner that will ensure the proper determination of the cause, manner and other surrounding circumstances of death, and must rule out every other possible and conceivable contributing cause.
