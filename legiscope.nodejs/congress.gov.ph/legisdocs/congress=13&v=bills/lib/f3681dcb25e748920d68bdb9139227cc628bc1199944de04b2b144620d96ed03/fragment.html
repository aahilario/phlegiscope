FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 211 (2) OF BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 881, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE OMNIBUS ELECTION CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to expand the rules in the appreciation of ballots where, in case there is no incumbent, the vote shall be counted in favor of candidates who have already been elected and have served the position he is seeking.
