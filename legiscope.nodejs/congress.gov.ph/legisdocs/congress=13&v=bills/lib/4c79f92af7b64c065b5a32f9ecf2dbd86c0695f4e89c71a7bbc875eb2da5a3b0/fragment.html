HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01755
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING THAT SECURITY, INTELLIGENCE AND CONFIDENTIAL FUNDS BE SUBJECTED TO STANDARD AUDITING PROCEDURES, REQUIRING THE SUBMISSION OF THE AUDIT AND LIQUIDATION REPORTS ON SAID FUNDS TO THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF
ABSTRACT : To ensure that these funds are managed, expended and utilized in accordance with laws and regulations, the bill provides for the regular auditing by the Commission on Audit of all security, intelligence and confidential funds.
