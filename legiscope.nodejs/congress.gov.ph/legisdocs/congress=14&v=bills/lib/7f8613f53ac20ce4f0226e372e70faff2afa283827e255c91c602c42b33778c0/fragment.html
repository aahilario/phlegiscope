House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00287
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL COUNCIL FOR EDUCATION, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the creation of the National Council for Education that shall serve as an advisory body to both the executive and legislative branches of all issues and concerns affecting the educational system.
