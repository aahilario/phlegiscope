House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02366
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A BAYANIHAN HOMEBUILDING PROGRAM FOR LOW INCOME GROUPS THROUGH GRADUATED AMORTIZATION PAYMENT (GAP) SCHEME AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Bayanihan Homebuilding Program Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Bayanihan Homebuilding Program Act of 2004." The bill proposes the establishment of a self-help homebuilding program wherein beneficiaries shall be required to render hours of labor as part of their investment. It provides for the eligibility criteria for beneficiaries of the program and a graduated amortization payment scheme.
