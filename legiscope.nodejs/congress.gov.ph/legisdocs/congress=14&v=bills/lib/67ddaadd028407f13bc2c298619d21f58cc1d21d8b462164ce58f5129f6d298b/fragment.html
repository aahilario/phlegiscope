HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02419
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LEGALIZING CHURCH ANNULMENT OR DISSOLUTION OF CERTAIN MARRIAGES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill gives recognition and full force and effect to the annulment or dissolution of a marriage solemnized by a priest, minister, iman, rabbi or presiding elder of an established church or religion.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : GARCIA, PABLO P.
