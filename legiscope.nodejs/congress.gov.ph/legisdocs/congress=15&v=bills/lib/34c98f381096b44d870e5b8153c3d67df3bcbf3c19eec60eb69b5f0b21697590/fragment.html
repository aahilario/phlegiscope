House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00023
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MAGNA CARTA OF THE POOR
SHORT TITLE : Magna Carta Of The Poor
ABSTRACT : “Magna Carta of the Poor.” Defines the basic rights of the poor, to wit: a) the right to food; b) the right to employment and livelihood; c) the right to quality education; d) the right to shelter; and e) the right to basic health services and medicine.
