House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01878
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING GOVERNMENT AGENCIES TO RETAIN INCOME GENERATED BEYOND THE SPECIFIED TARGETS REFLECTED IN THE BUDGET OF EXPENDITURES AND SOURCES OF FINANCING, AND ALLOWING ITS DISBURSEMENTS AS PROVIDED BY LAW
SHORT TITLE : The Government Agencies' Income Rationalization Act
ABSTRACT : "The Government Agencies' Income Rationalization Act." This bill grants the different government agencies: a) the authority to retain a percentage of their agency income generated beyond the specified targets set in the Budget of Expenditures and Sources of Financing of the government; and b) a scheme to allocate a percentage of their excess agency income to the country's health and education programs.
