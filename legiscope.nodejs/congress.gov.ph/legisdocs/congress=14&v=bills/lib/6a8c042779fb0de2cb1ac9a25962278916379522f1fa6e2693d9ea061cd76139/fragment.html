HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03098
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING SPECIAL DRIVER'S EDUCATION FOR PROFESSIONAL AND TRUCK DRIVERS
SHORT TITLE : "Professional Drivers' Education Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill directs establishments employing professional drivers to require them to complete a special professional drivers' education program from a driving school accredited by the Land Transportation Office.
