House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00366
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO DEFINE THE PARAMETERS AND LIMITS OF AVAILABILITY OF CIGARETTES AND OTHER TOBACCO PRODUCTS TO MINORS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Non-smokers Health Protection Act
ABSTRACT : "Non-Smokers Health Protection Act". The bill prohinbits any person from supplying tobacco or tobacco-related products to children. It also prohibits the display and promotion of tobacco and tobacco-related products, where they can be seen by children, in places where tobacco and tobacco-related products are sold.
