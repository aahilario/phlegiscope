HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00233
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROVIDE THE REVIVAL OF ARBOR DAY BY VESTING IN PROVINCIAL, CITY AND MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENTS WITH THEIR COMPONENT BARANGAYS THE RESPONSIBILITIES FOR CELEBRATING THE DAY FOR THE TREE PLANTING AS AN ANNUAL EVENT
SHORT TITLE : Revival of Arbor Day
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates all municipalities and cities to celebrate an Arbor Day at a fixed date every calendar year as may be deemed suitable according to the proper time and season of planting trees.
