HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03142
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC WORKS AND HIGHWAYS (DPWH) TO SEEK THE CONCURRENCE OF THE REPRESENTATIVE ON THE APPOINTMENT, ASSIGNMENT OR DESIGNATION OF A DISTRICT ENGINEER WITHIN THE TERRITORIAL JURISDICTION OF HIS LEGISLATIVE DISTRICT
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Department of Public Works and Highways to secure the concurrence of Representative in the legislative district on the appointment of a district engineer in his district.
