HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01639
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN AND REFORM THE SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN, AMENDING FOR THAT PURPOSE CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The Sangguniang Kabataan Reform Law of 2004
ABSTRACT : "The Sangguniang Kabataan Reform Law of 2004." The bill seeks to broaden the base of the katipunan ng kabataan by amending the maximum age of membership from 21 years old to 23 years old. It likewise proposes to amend the age requirement for sangguniang kabataan officials from "at least fifteen (15) years but not over twenty one (21) years of age" to "AT LEAST EIGHTEEN (18) BUT NOT MORE THAN TWENTY THREE (23) YEARS OF AGE."
