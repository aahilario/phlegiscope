House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00131
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE DRINKING WATER BUSINESSES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Drinking Water Business Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Drinking Water Business Act of 2004". The bill seeks to regulate the business of producing and selling mineral, pure or distilled water. It also protects and safeguards the health of the citizens by insuring that the water they procure and consume are safe to drink as promoted and advertised by drinking water suppliers, distributors or sellers.
