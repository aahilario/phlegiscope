House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00243
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE CODE COMMISSION OF THE PHILIPPINES TO REVIEW AND CODIFY ALL PHILIPPINE LAWS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Code Commission Act of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : "Code Commission Act of the Philippines". The bill proposes the creation of a Code Commission which shall review and codify all existing laws, and publish the same in systematic form.
