FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A COMMUNITY ORCHARD IN EVERY CITY OR MUNICIPALITY OF EVERY PROVINCE OF THE PHILIPPINES, ALLOCATING FUNDS TEHREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes in every city and municipal a community orchard with an area of not less than 5,000 sq. meters.
