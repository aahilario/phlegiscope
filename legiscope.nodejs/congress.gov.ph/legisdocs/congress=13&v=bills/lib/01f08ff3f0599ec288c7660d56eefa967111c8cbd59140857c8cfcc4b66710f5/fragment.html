HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01343
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A SPECIAL FUND FOR THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL RED CROSS BLOOD SERVICES
ABSTRACT : The bill creates a special fund in the amount of P50.0 million which will be used by the Philippine National Red Cross Blood Services to ensure an adequate and safe supply of blood for charity patients. The fund shall be sourced from the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office, the Philippine Amusement and Gaming Corporation and the National Treasury through the Department of Health.
