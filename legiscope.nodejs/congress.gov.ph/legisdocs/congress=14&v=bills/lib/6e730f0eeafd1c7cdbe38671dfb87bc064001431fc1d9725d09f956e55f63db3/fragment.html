FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ESTABLISH AND MAINTAIN AN OFFICE OF VETERANS AFFAIRS IN THE PHILIPPINE EMBASSY IN THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA, PRESCRIBING ITS FUNCTIONS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes the Office of Veterans Affairs in the Philippine Embassy in the United States that whall be tasked to vigorously pursue the unresolved claims of Filipino veterans with the United States government.
