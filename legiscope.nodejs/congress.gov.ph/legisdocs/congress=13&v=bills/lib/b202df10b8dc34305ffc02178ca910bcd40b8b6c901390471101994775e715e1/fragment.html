House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04613
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING THE CRIME OF CHILD PORNOGRAPHY, IMPOSING PENALTIES THEREFORE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Child Pornography Act"
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Child Pornography Act". The bill imposes the penalty of reclusion perpetua on any person who solicits, proposes, induces, promotes, facilitates or engages in the conduct of child pornograpy or who produces child pornographic materials.
