House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04950
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 198, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE PROVINCIAL WATER UTILITIES ACT, AS AMENDED, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to, among others, increase the authorized capitalization of the Local Water Utilities Administration (LWUA) from P2 billion to P10 billion and to restore the tax exemption privilege enjoyed by the LWUA under PD 198.
