House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00739
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENTHENING AND REFORMING THE SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS 329, 433 AND 434 OF REPUBLIC ACT 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Sangguniang Kabataan Reform Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Sangguniang Kabataan Reform Act of 2004." To fully tap the potentials of youth empowerment, the existing organizational structure of the Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) needs to be strengthened and reformed. The bill calls on the sangguniang barangay to appropriate the SK fund in lump sum and grants the SK fiscal autonomy on its operations as to disbursements and encashment of their income and expenses.
