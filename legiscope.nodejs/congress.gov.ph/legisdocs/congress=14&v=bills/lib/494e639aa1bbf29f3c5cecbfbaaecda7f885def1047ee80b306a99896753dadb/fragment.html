House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03421
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LIBERALIZING THE CIVIL AVIATION INDUSTRY AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 776 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE CIVIL AERONAUTICS ACT OF THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Civil Aviation Liberalization Act of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the following amendments: a)inclusion of representatives from the Departments of Tourism and Foreign Affairs in the Civil Aeronautics Board; b) creation of the office of the Executive Director; c) increase of penalties for violation of the R.A. 776; and d) imposition of policy guidlelines and directions for the Board to observe in the exercise of its regulatory functions.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.
