House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03258
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MERGING THE LANDBANK OF THE PHILIPPINES AND THE DEVELOPMENT BANK OF THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the merger of the Landbank of the Philippines and the Development Bank of the Philippines, with the Landbank as the surviving entity.
