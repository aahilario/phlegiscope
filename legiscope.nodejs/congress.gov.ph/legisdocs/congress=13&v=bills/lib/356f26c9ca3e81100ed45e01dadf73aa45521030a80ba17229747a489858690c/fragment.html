HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02295
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE RATIONALIZATION OF SALARIES, ALLOWANCES AND BENEFITS OF OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES OF GOVERNMENT OWNED OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS AND GOVERNMENT FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS EXEMPTED FROM THE COMPENSATION AND POSITION CLASSIFICATION SYSTEM
ABSTRACT : This bill proposes the exemption of all officials and employees of government owned or controlled corporations and government financial institutions from the Compensation and Position Classification System provided that their compensation shall be no more than twice the salaries of equivalent ranks and positions in other government agencies.
