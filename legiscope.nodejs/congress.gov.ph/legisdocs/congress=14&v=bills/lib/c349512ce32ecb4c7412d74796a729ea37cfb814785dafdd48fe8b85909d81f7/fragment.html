House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00511
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AND SANITATION, INTEGRATING THE DIFFERENT GOVERNMENT AGENCIES RELATED TO ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AND SANITATION, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Department of Environment Protection and Sanitation Act"
ABSTRACT : "Department of Environment Protection and Sanitation Act". The bill creates the Department that will serve as the primary government agency to formulate, coordinate, regulate and integrate existing plans, policies and programs relative to environmental protection and sanitation.
