House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02584
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE REFUSAL OF HOSPITALS, MEDICAL INSTITUTIONS AND OTHER MEDICAL CLINICS TO RELEASE PATIENTS AND CORPSE OF PATIENTS DUE TO INABILITY TO PAY HOSPITAL EXPENSES AS SERIOUS ILLEGAL DETENTION AMENDING ART. 267 OF ACT NO. 3815 AS AMENDED OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED PENAL CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes the penalty of reclusion perpetua to death to owners of hospitals, medical institutions/clinics that detain or refuse to release patients confined due to non-payment of hospital expenses.
