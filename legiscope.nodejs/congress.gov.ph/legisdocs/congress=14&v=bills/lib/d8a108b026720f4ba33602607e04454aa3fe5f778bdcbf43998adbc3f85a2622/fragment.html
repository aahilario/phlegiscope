House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02059
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPLEMENTING THE RIGHT OF ACCESS TO INFORMATION ON MATTERS OF PUBLIC CONCERN GUARANTEED UNDER SECTION SEVEN, ARTICLE III OF THE 1987 CONSTITUTION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Freedom to Access of Information Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Freedom to Access of Information Act of 2007". The bill mandates all government agencies to make available for public information, scrutiny, copying and reproduction all information, in any form, in its custody or under its control.
