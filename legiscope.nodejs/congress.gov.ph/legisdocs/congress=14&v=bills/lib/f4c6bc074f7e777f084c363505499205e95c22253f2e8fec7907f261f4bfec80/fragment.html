House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00022
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE PRACTICE OF REAL ESTATE SERVICE IN THE PHILIPPINES, CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE A PROFESSIONAL REGULATORY BOARD OF REAL ESTATE SERVICE, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Real Estate Service Act of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : "Real Estate Service Act of the Philippines". The bill elevates the real estate service practice, that is, real estate brokerage, appraisal and consultancy, to the category of a profession and places realty service practitioners under the jurisdiction of the Philippine Regulatory Commission.
