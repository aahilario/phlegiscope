HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01961
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A FILIPINO IDENTIFICATION SYSTEM AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Filipino Identification System Act"
ABSTRACT : "Filipino Identification System Act". The bill provides for a tamper-proof National ID Card which may be used to transact with any government instrumentality without need of further proof as to his/her identity.
