House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00791
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING GOVERNMENT-OWNED AND/OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS TO SUBMIT THEIR ANNUAL BUDGETS TO CONGRESS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : In consonance with the Constitutional mandate granting Congress the power of the purse, the measure requires all government-owned and controlled corporations to submit their annual budget to Congress for scrutiny and approval. It also requires these entities to secure congressional approval before they incur any obligation or make payments for current operating or capital expenditures.
