HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02127
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE DETENTION OF PATIENTS OR THE REMAINS OF PATIENTS IN HOSPITALS AND MEDICAL CLINICS ON GROUNDS OF NONPAYMENT OF HOSPITAL BILLS OR MEDICAL EXPENSES
ABSTRACT : The bill declares as unlawful for any hospital or medical clinic to detain the following patients because of non-payment, in full or in part, of medical and hospital bills: a) patients who have fully or partially recovered and have been medically cleared for discharge; and b) the remains of patients.
