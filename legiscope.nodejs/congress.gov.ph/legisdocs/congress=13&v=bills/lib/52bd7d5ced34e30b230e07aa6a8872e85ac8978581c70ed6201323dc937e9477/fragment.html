HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00962
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8282, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "AN ACT FURTHER STRENGTHENING THE SOCIAL SECURITY SYSTEM THEREBY AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 1161, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SOCIAL SECURITY LAW" BY LOWERING THE MONTHLY RATE OF PENALTY IMPOSED TO EMPLOYERS ON DELAYED REMITTANCE OF CONTRIBUTION FROM THREE PERCENT (3%) PER MONTH TO ONE-HALF PERCENT (1/2%) PER MONTH OR SIX PERCENT (6%) PER ANNUM
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to reduce the penalty charges for delayed remittance of contributions to the Social Security System from 3% to 1/2% per month to be computed from the date the contribution falls due.
