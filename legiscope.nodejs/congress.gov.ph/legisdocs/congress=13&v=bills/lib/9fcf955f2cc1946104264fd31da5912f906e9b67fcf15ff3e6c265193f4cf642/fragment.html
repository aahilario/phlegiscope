House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02732
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE USE OF CELLULAR PHONE AND OTHER SIMILAR TELECOMMUNICATION DEVICES BY DRIVERS OF PUBLIC UTILITY VEHICLES (PUVs) AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits the use of handheld cellular phone or any similar telecommunication device by any motorist while driving a public utility vehicle, including public utility buses and public utility jeepneys.
