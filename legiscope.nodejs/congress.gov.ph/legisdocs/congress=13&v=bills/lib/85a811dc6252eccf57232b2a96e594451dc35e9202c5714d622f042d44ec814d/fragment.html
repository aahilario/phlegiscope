REPUBLIC ACT NO. RA09343
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETY-ONE HUNDRED AND EIGHTY-TWO (R.A. NO. 9182), OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SPECIAL PURPOSE VEHICLE ACT OF 2002
SHORT TITLE : Amending RA No. 9182, Otherwise Known As the Special Purpose Vehicle Act of 2002
ABSTRACT : The measure extends the deadline for: a) the establishment of a special purpose vehicle (SPV) from September 19, 2005 to 18 months from the effectivity of the amendatory law, and b) the duration for the availment of the tax benefits from April 2005 to two years from the effectivity of the amendatory law.
