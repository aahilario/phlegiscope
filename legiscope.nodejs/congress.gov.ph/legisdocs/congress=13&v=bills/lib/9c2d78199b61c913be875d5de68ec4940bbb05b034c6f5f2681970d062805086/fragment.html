HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02680
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PRESCRIBING THE RECRUITMENT OF PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS BASED ON MERIT AND FITNESS, REPEALING REPUBLIC ACT 8190, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the appointment and assignment of teachers for public elementary and high schools to be based exclusively on merit and fitness as determined by ranking in competency examinations and qualifications.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : BATERINA, SALACNIB F.
DATE FILED : 2004-08-31
