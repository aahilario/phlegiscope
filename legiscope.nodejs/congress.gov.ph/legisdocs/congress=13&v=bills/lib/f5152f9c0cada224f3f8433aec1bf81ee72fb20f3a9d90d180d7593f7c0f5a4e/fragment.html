HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03135
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING NON-SETTLEMENT OF UNLIQUIDATED CASH ADVANCE OF GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND ALLOWING LIMITED WRITE-OFF THEREOF, INCLUDING DORMANT AND UNCOLLECTIBLE ACCOUNTS
ABSTRACT : The bill criminalizes the non-settlement and non-payment of unliquidated cash advances. It provides for a limited write-off of unliquidated cash advances and outstanding/dormant/uncollectible accounts receivable.
