HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01214
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE PHILIPPINE ONION RESEARCH INSTITUTE, PRESCRIBING ITS POWERS, FUNCTIONS AND DUTIES AND PROVIDING FOR THE NECESSARY FUNDS FOR ITS OPERATION
ABSTRACT : This bill establishes the Philippine Onion Research Institute (PHILORINS), a corporation under the supervision of the Department of Agriculture, that shall be primarily tasked to formulate and maintain a comprehensive plan for the development of the onion industry in the country.
