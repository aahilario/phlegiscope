HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03213
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROFESSIONALIZING THE PRACTICE OF CIVIL ENGINEERING IN THE PHILIPPINES, REPEALING FOR THIS PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FIVE HUNDRED AND FORTY-FOUR (R.A. 544), AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to attain the following, among others: a) enable civil engineers (CEs) to cope with the challenges brought about by globalization and cross-border practice; b) upgrade the level of competence of CEs; and c) define the practice of civil engineering in the country by foreign nationals.
