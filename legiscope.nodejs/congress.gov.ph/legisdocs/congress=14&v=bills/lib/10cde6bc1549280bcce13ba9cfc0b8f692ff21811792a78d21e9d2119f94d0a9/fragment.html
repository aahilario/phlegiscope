HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01254
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE FIRST WORKING DAY OF EVERY YEAR, BEGINNING ON THE YEAR 2007, A NATIONAL DAY OF PEACE AND UNITY, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill declares the first working day of the year as a National Day of Peace and Unity to instill in the minds of our people a desire for lasting peace and unity.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SYJUCO, JUDY J.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-24
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
