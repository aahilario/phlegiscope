House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00015
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING POST-HARVEST FACILITIES TO RICE FARMERS
SHORT TITLE : Post-Harvest Facilities Support Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Post-Harvest Facilities Support Act of 2004". The bill provides for the government, in consultation with farmer cooperatives, to construct warehouses and rice mills in every rice-producing municipality and city, and to provide the equipment and machinery necessary to operate these warehouse and rice mills. It aims to raise farm incomes to a level high enough to allow farmers to satisfy their material needs and enhance their self-esteem.
