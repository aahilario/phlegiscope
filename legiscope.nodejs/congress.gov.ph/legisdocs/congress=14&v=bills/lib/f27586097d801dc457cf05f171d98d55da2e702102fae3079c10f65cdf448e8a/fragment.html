HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03461
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INTRODUCING DIVORCE IN THE PHILIPPINES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE TITLE II, ARTICLES 55 TO 66 INCLUSIVE AND ARTICLE 26 OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 209, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE FAMILY CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AND REPEALING ARTICLE 36 OF THE SAME CODE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill introduces divorce as an option for couples in failed marriages. It proposes five grounds for divorce. It also provides for the custody of minor children to be decided by the court in accordance with the best interest of the child.
