HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00180
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO FURTHER PROMOTE ENTREPRENEURSHIP AND SUPPORT THE DEVELOPMENT OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6977, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE MAGNA CARTA FOR SMALL ENTERPRISES
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to give recognition to the role of micro enterprises which has been tradionally lumped as part of the small enterprise sector. It further seeks to broaden the membership of the Small and Medium Enterprise Development (SMED) Council to include members from the executive and legislative branches. Among other major amendments to RA 6977, it proposes to rename the SMED Council to the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprise Development (MSMED) Council.
