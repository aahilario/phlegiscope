HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01483
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES STOCK EXCHANGE
SHORT TITLE : Small and Medium Enterprises Stock Exchange (SAEX) Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Small and Medium Enterprises Stock Exchange (SMEX) Act of 2004." The bill defines a small enterprise as "any business with a capitalization of P2 million to P20 million while a medium enterprise has a capitalization of P21 million to P100 million. Aims to provide funds for the SMEX by requiring their public listing at a stock exchange.
