HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01652
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE USE OF ENGLISH AS A MEDIUM OF INSTRUCTION IN THE PHILIPPINE SCHOOLS
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the use of English as a medium of instruction in all curriculum levels and in examinations for admission, accreditation, acceleration and promotion. It allows the inclusion of questions in Filipino provided the questions do not exceed 10% of the total points in the examination.
