House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01650
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING REFORMS IN LAND ADMINISTRATION
SHORT TITLE : "The Land Administration Reform Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The Land Administration Reform Act of 2007". The bill consolidates under the Land Administration Authority land administration functions such as surveying, mapping, land classification, titling, disposition and registration.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ROMUALDEZ, FERDINAND MARTIN G.
