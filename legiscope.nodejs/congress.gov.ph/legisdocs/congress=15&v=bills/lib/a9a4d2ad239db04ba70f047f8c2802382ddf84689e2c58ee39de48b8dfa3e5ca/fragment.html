House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00160
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING AND REDEFINING THE POWERS AND FUNCTIONS OF THE NATIONAL FOOD AUTHORITY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NOS. 4 AND 1770, AS AMENDED, RESPECTIVELY KNOWN AS THE NATIONAL GRAINS AUTHORITY ACT AND THE NATIONAL FOOD AUTHORITY ACT, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Food Authority Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “National Food Authority Act of 2010.” Seeks to financially strengthen the National Food Authority by wiping clean its books and transferring its loans and non-core assets to the National Government; inject fresh new capitalization; provide for a more transparent funding mechanism, and; streamline its organization by redefining the tasks of its individual units.
