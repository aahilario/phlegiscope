House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02347
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL COMMISSION ON MUSLIM FILIPINOS DEFINING ITS POWERS, FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Act Creating the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos"
ABSTRACT : The National Commission on Muslim Filipinos shall be tasked to preserve and develop the culture, traditions, institutions and well being of Muslim Filipinos; and to oversee the proper implementation of all laws and policies that affect them.
