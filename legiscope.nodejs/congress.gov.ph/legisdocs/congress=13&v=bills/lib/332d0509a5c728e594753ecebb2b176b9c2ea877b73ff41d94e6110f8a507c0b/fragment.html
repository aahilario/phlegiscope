House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01494
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE SOLAR ENERGY DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY DEFINING ITS POWERS AND DUTIES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill creates the Authority with a capitalization of P5 million to develop, utilize, promote, and research on solar energy as an alternative to oil, hydroelectric and geothermal energy.
