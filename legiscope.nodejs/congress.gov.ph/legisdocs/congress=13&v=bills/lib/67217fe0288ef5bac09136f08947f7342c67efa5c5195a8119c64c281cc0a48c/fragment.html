FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 154, ARTICLE XIII OF BATAS PAMBANSA 881 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS OMNIBUS ELECTION CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the provision of a separate voting place on the ground floor of a polling area to enable disabled individuals and senior citizens to cast their votes easily.
