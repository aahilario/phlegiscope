House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02151
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE USE OF THE PROCEEDS FROM THE DEVELOPMENT, LEASE OR USE, AS THE CASE MAY BE, OF GOVERNMENT PROPERTIES FOR VETERANS' PENSIONS AND BENEFITS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE EXECUTIVE ORDER NO.323
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the utilization of the proceeds from the development, lease or use of government properties to fund the pension and benefits of veterans and military retirees.
