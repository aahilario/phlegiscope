HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03171
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO RATIONALIZE THE SALARIES, ALLOWANCES AND OTHER BENEFITS OF OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES OF GOVERNMENT-OWNED AND -CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS AND GOVERNMENT FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS EXEMPTED FROM THE SALARY STANDARDIZATION LAW
ABSTRACT : The bill limits the salaries, allowances and other benefits being enjoyed the officials and employees of government-owned and controlled corporations and government financial institutions which are exempt from the salary standardization law not to exceed twice the salaries, allowances and benefits of officials and employees of other government agencies of equivalent ranks and positions.
