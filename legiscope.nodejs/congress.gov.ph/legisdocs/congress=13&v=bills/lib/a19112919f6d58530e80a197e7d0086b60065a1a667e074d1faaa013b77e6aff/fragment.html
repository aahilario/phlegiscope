HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01287
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING COMMERCIAL HANDLINE FISHING AND VESSEL AND PROVIDING REGULATIONS FOR UTILIZATION THEREOF, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE 'THE PHILIPPINE FISHERIES CODE OF 1998'
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to give weight to traditional fishing practices and small fishermen by defining handline commercial fishing as a traditional passive fishing method which utilizes a single fishing line and attached hook; and handline commercial vessel as a commercial fishing vessel which exclusively utilizes the commercial handline fishing method.
