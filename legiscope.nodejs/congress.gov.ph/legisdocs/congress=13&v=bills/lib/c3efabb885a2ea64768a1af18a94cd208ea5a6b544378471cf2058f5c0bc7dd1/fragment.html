HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03414
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MAKING DONATIONS OR CONTRIBUTIONS IN CASH OR IN KIND FOR VICTIMS OF TYPHOONS, FLOODS, LANDSLIDES, EARTHQUAKES, STORM SURGES, TIDAL WAVES OR OTHER NATURAL CALAMITIES, DEDUCTIBLE FROM TAXABLE INCOME WITHIN PRESCRIBED LIMITS
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to make donations and contributions given to victims of natural calamities deductible from the taxable income of persons, partnerships and corporations making such donations. The tax deductibility shall not be more than 60% of the actual amount or value of the donations or contributions.
