FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBER 8291 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS 'THE GOVERNMENT SERVICE INSURANCE SYSTEM ACT OF 1997'
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for an automatic increase in GSIS benefits every five years to realistically reflect the value of the buying power of the peso.
