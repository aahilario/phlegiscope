House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03042
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 31, 43, AND 80 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9136 ENTITLED "AN ACT ORDAINING REFORMS IN THE ELECTRIC POWER INDUSTRY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to lower the 70% threshold requirement for the privatization of the assets of the National Power Corporation (NPC) and for the transfer of NPC contracts to the administrators of the independent power producers to 40%, for both cases.
