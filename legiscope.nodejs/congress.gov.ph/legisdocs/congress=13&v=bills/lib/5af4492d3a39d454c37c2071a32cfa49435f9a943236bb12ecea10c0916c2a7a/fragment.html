HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02952
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE PHILIPPINE PORT AUTHORITY TO SHARE THE TEN PERCENT (10%) OF ITS INCOME TO THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS WHERE THEY ARE OPERATING
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the Philippine Ports Authority to share 10% of its actual income, based on receipts, with the local government unit where it operates.
