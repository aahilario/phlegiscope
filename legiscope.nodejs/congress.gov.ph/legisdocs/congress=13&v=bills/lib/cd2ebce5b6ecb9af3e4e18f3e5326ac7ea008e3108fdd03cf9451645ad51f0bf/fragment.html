House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00693
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING HARBOR PILOTAGE SERVICES AND THE CONDUCT OF HARBOR PILOTS IN PHILIPPINE PORTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Pilotage Services Act of 2001
ABSTRACT : "Pilotage Services Act of 2001." The measure defines harbor pilotage as the maneuvering of ships in and out of the port through tights channels. Provides for the functions of the Philippine Ports Authority, qualification of harbor pilots, their accreditation and disqualification.
