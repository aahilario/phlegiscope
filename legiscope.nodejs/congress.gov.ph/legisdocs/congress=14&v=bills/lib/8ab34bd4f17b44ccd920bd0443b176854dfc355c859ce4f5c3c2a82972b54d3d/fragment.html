House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03306
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING THE RIGHT OF REPLY AND PROVIDING PENALTIES IN VIOLATION THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : Granting the Right of Reply to All Persons Who Are Accussed Directly or Indirectly of Committing Any Crime or Offense Defined by Law
ABSTRACT : The bill gives an accused or a criticized person the right of reply in all forms of mass media, website or through any electronic device.
