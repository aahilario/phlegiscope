HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02502
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING WOMEN'S PARTICIPATION IN ELECTIVE AND APPOINTIVE POSITIONS IN GOVERNMENT AS WELL AS IN GOVERNMENT INSTITUTIONS UNDER THE EXECUTIVE DEPARTMENT
SHORT TITLE : Gender Balance in Political Participation and Representation Act
ABSTRACT : "Gender Balance in Political Participation and Representation Act." The bill proposes to implement a gender quota system to ensure that women are granted the following: a) access to appointive positions in the national and local government; and b) full participation in the electoral and political process.
