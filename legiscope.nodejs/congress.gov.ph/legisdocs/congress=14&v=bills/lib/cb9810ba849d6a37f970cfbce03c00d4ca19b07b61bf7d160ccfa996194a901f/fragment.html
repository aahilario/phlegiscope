HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02725
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PRESCRIBING FOR THE MANNER OF PROVIDING SECTORAL REPRESENTATION IN THE SANGGUNIANG BAYAN AND SANGGUNIANG PANLUNGSOD AND SANGGUNIANG PANLALAWIGAN AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS 446 AMD 457 OF THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the election of three sectoral representatives each in the Sangguniang Panlalawigan, Sangguniang Panlungsod and Sangguniang Bayan, to wit: one from the women sector; one from workers and one from the sector with sufficient membership in the locality to warrant representation.
