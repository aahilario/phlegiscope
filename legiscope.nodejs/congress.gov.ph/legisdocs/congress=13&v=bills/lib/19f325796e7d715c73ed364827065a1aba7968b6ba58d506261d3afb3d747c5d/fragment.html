House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04769
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ADOPTING A SALARY SCHEDULE FOR THE MEMBERS OF THE BENCH AND OTHER LAWYERS IN THE JUDICIARY, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : To raise the level of competence and to ensure the integrity of the justices, judges and lawyers in the judiciary, the bill proposes the adoption of a compensation scheme specifically designed for the justices, judges and lawyers in the judiciary.
