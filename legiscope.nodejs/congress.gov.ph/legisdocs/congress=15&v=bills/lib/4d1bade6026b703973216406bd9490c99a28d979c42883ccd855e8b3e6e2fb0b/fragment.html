House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00275
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A WELFARE AND SOCIAL ASSISTANCE PROGRAM TO FAMILIES LEFT BEHIND BY OVERSEAS FILIPINO WORKERS
SHORT TITLE : Left-behind Households Of Ofws Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Left-Behind Households of OFWs Act of 2010” Seeks to protect and strengthen the families left-behind by OFWs by establishing OFW Family Centers which shall provide welfare assistance and counseling services to them.
