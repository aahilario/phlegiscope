House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01962
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMOTING SMALL BUSINESS LOCATION AND INCUBATOR PROGRAM
SHORT TITLE : Small Business Location and Incubator Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Small Business Location and Incubator Act of 2004." Defines a business incubator as "an organization set up to nurture young enterprises by providing them with a common office and service facilities such as technology transfer, training, marketing, etc. Aims to promote the survival of new enterprises during their early years by giving them technical and managerial support.
