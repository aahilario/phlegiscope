House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00648
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING GRATUITY PAY TO ALL DULY ELECTED BARANGAY CAPTAINS AND BARANGAY COUNCIL MEMBERS, WHO HAVE COMPLETELY SERVED THREE CONSECUTIVE TERMS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREOF AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Barangay Council Gratuity Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Barangay Council Gratuity Act of 2004". Grants gratuity pay of P200,000 to barangay captains and P150,000 to council members who have served three consecutive terms.
