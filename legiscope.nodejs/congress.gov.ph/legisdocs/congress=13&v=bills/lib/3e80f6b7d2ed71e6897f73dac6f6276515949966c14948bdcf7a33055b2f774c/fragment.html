House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03017
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMOTING THE EXPLORATION, DEVELOPMENT AND PRODUCTION OF GEOTHERMAL RESOURCES
SHORT TITLE : Geothermal Resources Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Geothermal Resources Act of 2004." The bill grants geothermal service and technical and financial assistance contractors a package of incentives which translates to an increase of about 5 to 6 percentage points in the rate of return on their investments.
