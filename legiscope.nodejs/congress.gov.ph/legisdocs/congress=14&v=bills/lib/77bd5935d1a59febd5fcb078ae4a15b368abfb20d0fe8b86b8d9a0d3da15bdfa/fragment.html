House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03505
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LOWERING THE RATE OF AMUSEMENT TAX ON GROSS RECEIPTS FROM ADMISSION FEES OF THEATERS AND CINEMAS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 140 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to lower the rate of amusement tax from 30% to 10% of the gross receipts from the admission fees of theaters and cinemas.
