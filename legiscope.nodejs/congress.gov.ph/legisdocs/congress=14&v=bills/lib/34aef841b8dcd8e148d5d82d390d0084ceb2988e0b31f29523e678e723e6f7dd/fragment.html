FULL TITLE : AN ACT PUNISHING MILITARY COMMANDERS OR SUPERIORS FOR CRIMES OR OFFENSES COMMITTED BY THEIR SUBORDINATES UNDER THE PRINCIPLE OF COMMAND RESPONSIBILITY
SHORT TITLE : "Command Responsibility Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to enforce accountability on the part of military officers by penalizing their acts or omission in preventing , if not punishing, human rights offenses by their subordinates.
