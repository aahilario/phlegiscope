House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02657
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO MAXIMIZE THE COLLECTION OF REVENUES BY REQUIRING THE FILING OF STATEMENT OF ASSETS, LIABILITIES AND NETWORTH AS OF DECEMBER 31, 2003 AND BY GRANTING TAX AMNESTY ON ALL UNPAID INTERNAL REVENUE TAXES IMPOSED BY THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT FOR TAXABLE YEAR 2003 AND PRIOR YEARS
ABSTRACT : To raise revenues for the government, the bill proposes the implementation of a tax amnesty program covering all unpaid internal revenue taxes for taxable year 2003 and prior years.
