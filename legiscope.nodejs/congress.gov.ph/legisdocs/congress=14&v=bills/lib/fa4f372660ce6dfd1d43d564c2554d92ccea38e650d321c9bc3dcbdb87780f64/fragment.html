HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00594
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOCATING TO THE AFP MODERNIZATION ACT TRUST FUND THE SHARE OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT OF ALL TAXES, ROYALTIES AND CHARGES COLLECTED FROM THE MALAMPAYA NATURAL GAS PROJECT
ABSTRACT : The bill allocates and transfers to the AFP Modernization Act Trust Fund the share of the national government in the taxes, royalties and charges collected from the Malampaya Natural Gas PRoject.
