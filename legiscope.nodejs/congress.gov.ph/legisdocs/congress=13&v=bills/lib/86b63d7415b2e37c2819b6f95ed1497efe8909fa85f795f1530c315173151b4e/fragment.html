HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03118
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE MINIMUM WAGE OF HOUSEHELPERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill increases the monthly minimum wage of househelpers from P800 to P3,000 in highly urbanized cities; from P650 to P1,000 in other chartered cities and 1st class municipalities; and from P550 to P800 in other municipalities.
