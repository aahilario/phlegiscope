House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05270
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN LOCAL AUTONOMY BY EQUITABLY DISTRIBUTING TO THE LOCAL GOVERNMENTS THE CHARITY FUND OF THE PHILIPPINE CHARITY SWEEPSTAKES OFFICE
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to equitably and immediately distribute the charity fund of the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office to local units for their health programs.
