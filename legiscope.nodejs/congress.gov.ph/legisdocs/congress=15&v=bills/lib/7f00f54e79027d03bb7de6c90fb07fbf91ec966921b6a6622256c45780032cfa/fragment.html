HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00082
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING AT LEAST ONE (1) SPECIAL EDUCATION CENTER FOR EACH SCHOOL DIVISION AND AT LEAST THREE (3) SPECIAL EDUCATION CENTERS IN BIG SCHOOL DIVISIONS FOR CHILDREN WITH SPECIAL NEEDS, GUIDELINES FOR GOVERNMENT FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE AND OTHER INCENTIVES AND SUPPORT
SHORT TITLE : Special Education Act Of 1020
ABSTRACT : “Special Education Act of 1020.” Mandates the creation of at least one (1) special education (SPED) center in each city and province and three (3) centers in big school divisions in the country to ensure nationwide availability of special education services.
