House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01427
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL PROGRAM FOR FILIPINO GIFTED AND SCIENCE ORIENTED CHILDREN, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Gifted and Science-Oriented Children Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Gifted and Science-Oriented Children Act of 2004." The bill defines a gifted child as three to 16 years old exhibiting advance intelligence in the following areas: verbal/linguistic, logical/mathematical. body/kinesthetic, visual/spatial, musical/rhythmic and leadership. Establishes a Center for gifted children to support them, train their teachers and provide scholarships.
