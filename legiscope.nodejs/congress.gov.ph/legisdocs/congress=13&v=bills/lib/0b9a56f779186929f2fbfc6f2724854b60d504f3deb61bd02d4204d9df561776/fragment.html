House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02564
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE MANDATORY PRE-AUDIT OF GOVERNMENT DISBURSEMENTS AND USES OF FUNDS
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the Commission on Audit to conduct a pre-audit examination of all expenditures and uses of public funds pertaining to infrastructure projects and the procurement of goods and consulting services.
