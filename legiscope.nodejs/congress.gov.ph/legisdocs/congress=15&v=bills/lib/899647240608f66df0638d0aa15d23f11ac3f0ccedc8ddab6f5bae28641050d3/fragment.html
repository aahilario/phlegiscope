FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING TUITION FEE DISCOUNTS AND SCHOLARSHIP GRANTS TO QUALIFIED HOUSEHOLD HELPERS NATIONWIDE, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Household Help Scholarship Act Grants Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Household Help Scholarship Act Grants of 2010.” Institutionalizes the grants of tuition fee discounts and scholarships to qualified and deserving household helpers who can not possibly afford quality education with their meager and below minimum salaries.
