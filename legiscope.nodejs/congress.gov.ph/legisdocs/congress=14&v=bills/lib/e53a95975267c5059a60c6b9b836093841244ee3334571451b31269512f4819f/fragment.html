House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00775
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING THE ACQUISITION, USE, OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF GOVERNMENT MOTOR VEHICLES
SHORT TITLE : "Government Motor Vehicle Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Government Motor Vehicle Act of 2007". The measure identifies the government officials authorized to use government motor vehicles; and provides for their use, operation and disposal; repair and maintenance; and identification and markings. It prohibits government offices from acquiring or using luxury vehicles.
