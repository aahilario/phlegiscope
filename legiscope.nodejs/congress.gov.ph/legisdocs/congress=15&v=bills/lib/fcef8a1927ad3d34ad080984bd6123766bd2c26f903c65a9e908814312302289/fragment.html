HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00226
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING SECURITY OF TENURE UNDER CERTAIN CONDITIONS TO GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES WHOSE NATURE OF APPOINTMENT IS EITHER TEMPORARY OR CONTRACTUAL AND WHO HAVE RENDERED A TOTAL OF TEN YEARS OF EFFICIENT SERVICE
ABSTRACT : Seeks to give permanent appointment to temporary or contractual employees who have continuously and efficiently served the government for at least ten (10) years and promote efficiency in the government service as frequent turnover of personnel in the government will be avoided.
