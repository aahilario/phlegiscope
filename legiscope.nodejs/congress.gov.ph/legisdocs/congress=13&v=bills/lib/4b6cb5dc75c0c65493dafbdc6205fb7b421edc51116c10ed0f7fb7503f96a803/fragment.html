House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01965
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING ARTICLES 223 AND 224 OF CHAPTER FIVE TITLE SEVEN OF ACT NUMBERED THIRTY-EIGHT HUNDRED AND FIFTEEN, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill penalizes with prision mayor in its maximum period without pardon and parole and disqualification to hold office any public officer guilty of conniving or consenting with a prisoner to escape or through his negligence.
