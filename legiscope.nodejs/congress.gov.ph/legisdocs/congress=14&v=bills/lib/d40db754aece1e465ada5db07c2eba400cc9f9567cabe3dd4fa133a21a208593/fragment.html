House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03240
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING THE OFFENSES AND ABUSES AGAINST THE NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE PROGRAM (NHIP) AND PROVIDING PENALTIES AND SANCTIONS THEREFOR, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLE X OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7875 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE ACT OF 1995 AS AMENDED BY RA 9241 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure grants the PhilHealth enforcement power and authority to run after erring helath care providers, employers and members. It also expands the current representation of the self-employed to include the entire informal sector.
