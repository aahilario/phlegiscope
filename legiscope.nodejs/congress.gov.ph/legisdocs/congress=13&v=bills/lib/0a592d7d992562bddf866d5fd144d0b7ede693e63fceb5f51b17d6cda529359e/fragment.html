HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00111
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING ADDITIONAL INSURANCE BENEFITS TO ALL PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS, PROVIDING FUNDS FOR THE PAYMENT OF ITS PREMIUMS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Public School Teachers' Insurance Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Public School Teachers' Insurance Act of 2004". Teachers are the single most important factor in education. However, because of their not-so-attractive remuneration, teaching is perceived as a poorly esteemed profession that can not attract the best minds. This bill seeks to improve the plight of teachers by improving the benefits afforded to them to encourage the best minds to join the teaching profession. Specifically, this provides additional insurance benefits to teachers, over and above the benefits given by the Government Service Insurance System.
