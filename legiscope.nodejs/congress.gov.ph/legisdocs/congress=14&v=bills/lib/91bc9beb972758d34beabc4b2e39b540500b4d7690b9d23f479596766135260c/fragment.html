House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00472
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CRIMINALIZING VIDEO AND PHOTO VOYEURISM AND IMPOSING PENALTIES THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Voyeurism Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Voyeurism Act of 2007". The bill proposes the penalty of imprisonment from 18 months to 4 years and/or a fine of P200,000 to those who invade the privacy of others using a camera or a video camera.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : PUENTEVELLA, MONICO O.
