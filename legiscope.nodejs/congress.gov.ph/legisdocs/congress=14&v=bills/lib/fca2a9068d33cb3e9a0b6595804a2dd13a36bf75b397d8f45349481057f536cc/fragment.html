HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00459
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 220 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160 OTHERWISE AS 'THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE' AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill limits the power of local government units to re-assess the value of real property to be used as basis for the real estate tax. It provides that the assessment of real property shall not be increased oftener than once every five (5) years.
