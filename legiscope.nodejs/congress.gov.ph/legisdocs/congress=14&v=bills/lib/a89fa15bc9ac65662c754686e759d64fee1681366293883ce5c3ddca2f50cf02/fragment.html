HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02056
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING GREATER PROTECTION TO LOCAL INDUSTRIES THROUGH THE IMPOSITION OF ANTI-DUMPING DUTY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 301, PART 2, TITLE II, BOOK 1 OF THE TARIFF AND CUSTOMS CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8752
ABSTRACT : The bill restores the method/steps necessary in order to determine the normal value of the article/s subject of a dumping protest. It removes the posting of a surety bond by the affected industry or interested party upon filing of a complaint or petition against dumping.
