House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02378
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LIFTING THE TERM LIMITS OF BARANGAY OFFICIALS AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9164 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS AN ACT PROVIDING FOR SYNCHRONIZED BARANGAY AND SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN ELECTIONS
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to reset the barangay and sangguniang kabataan elections to the 2nd Monday of May 2005 and every three years therafter. It further lifts the term limits of barangay officials.
