HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02375
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING THAT ALL CITIZENS OF THE PHILIPPINES SHALL AUTOMATICALLY BE ENROLLED IN THE NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE PROGRAM AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE ACT OF 1995
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the automatic enrollment of all citizens in the National Health Insurance Program. It provides additional funding to the Program by allocating 5% of the annual earnings of the Philippine Gaming Corporation and the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office.
