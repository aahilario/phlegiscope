House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00212
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING REFORMS IN THE REAL PROPERTY VALUATION AND ASSESSMENT PRACTICES IN THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Real Property Valuation Reform Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Real Property Valuation Reform Act of 2010.” Seeks to promote the development and maintenance of a just, equitable, impartial and nationally consistent real property valuation system, based on internationally accepted valuation standards, concepts, principles and practices.
