House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04304
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE SALE, TRANSFER AND DISPOSITION OF PUBLIC LAND TO PRIVATE ENTITIES WITHOUT A COMPLETE EVALUATION BY THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES, DEPARTMENT OF LAND REFORM, DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE, NATIONAL HOUSING AUTHORITY AND OTHER LOCAL AGENCIES, PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits the transfer of ownership of public lands to private entities without the evaluation and approval of concerned agencies.
