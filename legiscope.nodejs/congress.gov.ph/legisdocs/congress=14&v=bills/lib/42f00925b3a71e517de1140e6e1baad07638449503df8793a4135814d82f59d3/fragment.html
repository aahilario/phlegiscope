House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00716
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A STUDENT FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE PROGRAM FOR STUDENTS OF ECONOMICALLY DISADVANTAGED FAMILIES AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS FOR THE PURPOSE
ABSTRACT : This bill establishes a student financial assistance program in the collegiate level which caters to students of underprivileged families who are willing to continue and finish a degree course. It entitles the beneficiaries under this program to a monthly stipend, free tuition, book allowance and other miscellaneous fees to be determined by the administering agency.
