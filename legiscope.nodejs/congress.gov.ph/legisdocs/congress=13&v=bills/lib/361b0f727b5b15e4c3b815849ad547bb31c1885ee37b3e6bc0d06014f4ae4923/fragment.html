FULL TITLE : AN ACT PRESCRIBING THE EFFECTIVE DATES OF BENEFITS FOR MILITARY VETERANS AND THEIR DEPENDENTS PRESCRIBED IN REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6948 AND REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7696
