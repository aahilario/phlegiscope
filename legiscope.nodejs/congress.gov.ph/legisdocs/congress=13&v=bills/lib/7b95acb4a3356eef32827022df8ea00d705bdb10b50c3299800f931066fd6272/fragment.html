HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05073
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING THE COMPENSATION BENEFITS AND OTHER PRIVILEGES AND INCENTIVES FOR THE MEMBERS OF THE NATIONAL PROSECUTION SERVICE AND THE OFFICE OF THE CHIEF STATE COUNSEL IN THE DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE, AND THE OFFICE OF THE OMBUDSMAN, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the salaries and grant additional services and privileges to the members of the National Prosecution Service, the Office of the Chief State Counsel and the Office of the Ombudsman to place them at par with those of the Judiciary.
