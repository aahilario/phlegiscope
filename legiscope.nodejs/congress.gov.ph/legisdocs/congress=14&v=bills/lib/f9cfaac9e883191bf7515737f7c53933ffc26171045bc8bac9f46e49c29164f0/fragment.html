House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01341
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPOSING A STIFFER PENALTY FOR THE CRIME OF ILLEGAL USE OF UNIFORMS OR INSIGNIA, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLE 179 OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes the penalty of prision correctional in its minimum period for the unauthorized use of the uniform or insignia of uniformed personnel.
