House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00682
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING AND PENALIZING CORPORAL PUNISHMENT ON CHILDREN, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7610, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS SPECIAL PROTECTION OF CHILDREN AGAINST CHILD ABUSE, EXPLOITATION AND DISCRIMINATION ACT, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Anti-Corporal Punishment Law of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The Anti-Corporal Punishment Law of 2007". The bill prohibits all forms of corporal punishment on children whether in the home, in schools, in private and public institutions, in the juvenile system and in all other alternative care system.
