HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00219
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING THE PARTICIPATION OF BONA FIDE PEOPLE'S ORGANIZATIONS, NON-GOVERNMENT ORGANIZATIONS, OR PRIVATE/CIVIC SECTOR GROUPS IN PUBLIC HEARINGS IN CONGRESS AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS' BUDGET DELIBERATIONS
SHORT TITLE : People’s Participation In Budget Deliberation Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “People’s Participation in Budget Deliberation Act of 2010.” Aims to institutionalize people’s participation in budget deliberations in various levels of government – national, provincial, municipal down to the barangay level, without underestimating and undermining the capabilities of elected representatives in crafting a budget that is most needed by a particular locality, giving full play to the people’s right to participate in at all levels of social, political and economic decision-making.
