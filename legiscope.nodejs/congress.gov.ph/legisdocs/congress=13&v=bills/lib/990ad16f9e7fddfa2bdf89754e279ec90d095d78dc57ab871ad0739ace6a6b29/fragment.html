House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03241
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RE-ESTABLISHING THE NATIONAL INVENTORS COMMISSION BY CONSOLIDATING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7459, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE INVENTORS AND INVENTION INCENTIVES ACT OF THE PHILIPPINES WITH REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3850, AS AMENDED, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Filipino Inventors and Inventions Incentives Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Filipino Inventors and Inventions Incentives Act of 2004." The bill re-establishes the National Inventors Commission, defines it powers, duties and responsibilities and provides for support mechansims and incentives for Filipino inventors.
