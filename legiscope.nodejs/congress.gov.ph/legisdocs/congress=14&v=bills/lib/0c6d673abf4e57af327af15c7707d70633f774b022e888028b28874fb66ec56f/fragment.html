HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00116
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL BRANCHES, SUBDIVISIONS, INSTRUMENTALITIES, AND AGENCIES OF THE GOVERNMENT, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR-CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS OR THEIR SUBSIDIARIES TO DIVULGE THE IDENTITY OF HIRED FOREIGN CONSULTANTS, THE NATURE OF THEIR WORK AND THEIR COMPENSATION, PERKS, AND OTHER PRIVILEGES, AND PROVIDING PENALTIES IN CASE OF VIOLATION THEREOF
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to require full disclosure of the identity, nature or work and compensation of foreign consultants hired or retained by the government, its agencies and instrumentalities. It aims: a. to inform the public of the amount being used to pay consultants hired by the government; b. to appraise the public of whether or not the services of these consultants are indispensable and cannot be rendered by our local experts; and c. to enlighten the public of the personalities providing expert advice and support to the government.
