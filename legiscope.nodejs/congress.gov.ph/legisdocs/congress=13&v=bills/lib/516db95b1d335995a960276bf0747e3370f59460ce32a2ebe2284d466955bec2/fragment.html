House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05525
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN AND PROPAGATE FOSTER CARE FOR ADANDONED AND NEGLECTED CHILDREN AND OTHER CHILDREN WITH SPECIAL NEEDS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Foster Care Act of 2006."
ABSTRACT : "Foster Care Act of 2006". The measure seeks to give assistance to foster parents which shall include support care services. It provides for the creation of the Foster Care Committee which shall be tasked to review and deliberate on issues affecting the placement of children.
