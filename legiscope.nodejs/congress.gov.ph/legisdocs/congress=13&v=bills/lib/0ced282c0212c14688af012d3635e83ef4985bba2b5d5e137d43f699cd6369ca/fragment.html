House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00378
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING FURTHER AND BROADENING THE COVERAGE OF THE SPECIAL PROGRAM FOR EMPLOYMENT OF STUDENTS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7323, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : To strengthen the Special Program for Employment of Students (SPES), the bill proposes to accomplish the following: a) adjust the income requirement for coverage under the law; b) provide a non-dimunition and automatic increase for its allocation; c) penalize those who dishonor the education vouchers; d) lessen the coverage of employment size of estalishments; and c) make the program a year-round activity.
