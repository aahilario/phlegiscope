FULL TITLE : AN ACT EMPOWERING LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS (LGUs) TO MONITOR THE ENFORCEMENT OF LAWS, RULES AND REGULATIONS ON ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AND CONSERVATION OF NATURAL RESOURCES
SHORT TITLE : Local Initiative on Environment Protection and Conservation of Natural Resources Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Local Initiative on Environment Protection and Conservation of Natural Resources Act of 2004." This measure aims to coordinate the efforts of the national and local governments to implement laws on environmental protection and conservation.
