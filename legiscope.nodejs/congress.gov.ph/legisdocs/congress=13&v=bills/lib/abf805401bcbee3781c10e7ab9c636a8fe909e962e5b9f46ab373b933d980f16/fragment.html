House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04375
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING GOVERNMENT HOSPITALS UNDER THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH (DOH) TO RETAIN AND UTILIZE ALL THEIR INCOME AND INTEREST DERIVED FROM DEPOSITS, FOR MAINTENANCE AND OTHER OPERATING EXPENSES, CAPITAL OUTLAYS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Authorizing Government Hospitals to Retain and Utilize All Their Income and Interest Derived From Deposits for Maintenance and Other Operating Expenses
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes government hospitals to retain and utilize 100% of their income (hospital fees from in-house services and facilities; income from non-patient related services such as rental fees, parking fees and the like; and income derived from deposits) for maintenance and other operating expenses.
