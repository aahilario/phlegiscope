HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00143
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE SUBMISSION TO CONGRESS OF THE CORPORATE BUDGET OF ALL GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, THEIR SUBSIDIARIES AND AFFILIATES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE, SECTION 13 OF PD 1177, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'BUDGETARY REFORM ACT OF 1977'
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the submission to Congress of the corporate operating budgets of all government-owned or controlled corporations to ensure that the same are consistent with the national development plan and also to assure transparency in the operations of said corporations.
