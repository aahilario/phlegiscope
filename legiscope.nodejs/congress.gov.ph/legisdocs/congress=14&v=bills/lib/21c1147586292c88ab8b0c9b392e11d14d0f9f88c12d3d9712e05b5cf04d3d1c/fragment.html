FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE INTEGRATION OF AN ADDITIONAL SUBJECT ON CULTURE, PEACE AND SOLIDARITY IN THE CURRICULA OF ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY SCHOOLS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
