HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00713
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CREATION OF A SPECIAL EDUCATION FUND INTENDED FOR SCHOOL BUILDINGS AND FACILITIES, TO BE ALLOCATED FROM THE EXCISE TAX COLLECTION ON ALCOHOL AND TOBACCO PRODUCTS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Classroom shortage compel teachers to hold their classes in make-shift shelters. The proposed measure seeks to augment the present budget for this purpose through the creation of a Special Education Fund for School Buildings and Facilities to be sourced from the excise tax collection on alcoholic products (distilled spirits, wines and fermented liquor) and tobacco products (cigars and cigarettes).
