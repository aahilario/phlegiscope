FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING DECEMBER 10 OF EVERY YEAR AS 'HUMAN RIGHTS DAY'
SHORT TITLE : Human Rights Day Act
ABSTRACT : "Human Rights Day Act." To instill awareness and foster promotion and respect for basic rights and individual freedom, the bill seeks to declare December 10 of every year as a special working holiday.
