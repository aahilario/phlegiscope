House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05215
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LIMITING THE INVESTMENT OF FUNDS OF THE SOCIAL SECURITY SYSTEM AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION TWENTY SIX OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED EIGHTY TWO HUNDRED EIGHTY-TWO, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SOCIAL SECURITY ACT OF 1997
SHORT TITLE : Limiting the Investment of Funds of SSS
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to trim down the areas of investments that the Social Security Service (SSS) may enter into to only those that assures predictability in order to protect SSS members from the vagaries of trade and commerce.
