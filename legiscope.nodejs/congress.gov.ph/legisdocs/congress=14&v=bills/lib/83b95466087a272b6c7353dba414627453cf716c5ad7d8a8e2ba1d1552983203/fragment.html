House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02444
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6971 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE PRODUCTIVITY INCENTIVES ACT OF 2007" AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill expands the coverage of the productivity incentives program to include all agencies and instrumentalities of the government. It also introduces a productivity gainsharing scheme whereby employers and workers share in the fruits of improvement in productivity.
