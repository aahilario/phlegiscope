House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00181
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 285 AND 286OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, PROVIDING FOR AN EQUITABLE DISTRIBUTION OF THE INTERNAL REVENUE ALLOTMENT (IRA) TO LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to correct the inequitable distribution of the internal revenue allotment (IRA) by introducing poverty-sensitive factors in the IRA formula in lieu of population, land area and equal sharing.
