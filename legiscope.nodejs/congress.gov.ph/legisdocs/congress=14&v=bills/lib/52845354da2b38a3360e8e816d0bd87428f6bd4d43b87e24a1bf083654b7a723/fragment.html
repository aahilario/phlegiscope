HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00825
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING A TEMPORARY MORATORIUM, THE EFFECTIVITY OF WHICH SHALL BE HEREIN PRESCRIBED, ON THE APPREHENSION, SEIZURE AND CONFISCATION OF PIRATED COMPACT DISCS, VIDEO COMPACT DISCS, AND CASSETTE AND VIDEO CASSETTE TAPES IN THE COUNTRY FOR THE PURPOSE OF ASSURING EQUITABLE ACCESS TO ENTERTAINMENT, PARTICULARLY OF THE POOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Emergency Equitable Access to Entertainment Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Emergency Equitable Access to Entertainment Act of 2007". The bill calls for a moratorium on the apprehension, seizure and confiscation of pirated compact discs, video compact discs, and cassette and video cassette tapes for a period of five (5) years.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SYJUCO, JUDY J.
