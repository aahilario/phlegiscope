HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01256
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE DISPOSAL AND/OR COMMERCIAL DEVELOPMENT OF CERTAIN MILITARY CAMPS AND RESERVATIONS, CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE THE MILITARY REAL ESTATE DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Military Real Estate Development Act
ABSTRACT : "Military Real Estate Development Act." To raise funds for the AFP Modernization Program, the bill authorizes the President of the Philippines to identify military real estate or parts thereof that could be disposed of and/or commercially developed based on established criteria for disposal.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : NICOLAS, REYLINA "NENENG" G.
DATE FILED : 2004-07-08
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
