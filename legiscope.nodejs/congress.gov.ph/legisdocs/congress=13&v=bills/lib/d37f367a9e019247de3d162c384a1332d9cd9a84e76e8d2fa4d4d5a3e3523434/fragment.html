FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING THE PROVISIONS OF ARTICLE 192, TITLE EIGHT, CHAPTER THREE, OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 603,AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CHILD AND YOUTH WELFARE CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the automatic suspension of sentence imposed on youth offenders thereby eliminating the need to pass through a rigorous application process to avail of said suspension.
