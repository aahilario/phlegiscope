HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02156
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 79 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 337, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE GENERAL BANKING ACT, AS AMENDED" AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates banks to allow the initial amortization for loans used for purposes that do not immediately produce revenues to be deferred until such time when said revenues are sufficient, but in no case shall the initial amortization be later that five years from the date the loan is granted.
