HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01790
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE ALLOWANCES/HONORARIA OF AND GRANTING RETIREMENT BENEFITS TO ALL BARANGAY OFFICIALS AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the monthly allowance/honorarium of the punong barangay from P1,000 to P6,000 and for the sangguniang barangay members, barangay treasurer and barangay secretary from P600 to P3,000. It also grants retirement benefits to barangay officials who have rendered nine years of cumulative service.
