FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MAGNA CARTA OF AGRICULTURAL OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES
SHORT TITLE : "Magna Carta of Agricultural Officers and Employees"
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta of Agricultural Officers and Employees". The measure prescribes the qualification standards, compensation and benefits, working conditions and performance evaluation and merit promotion program for agricultural officers and employees.
