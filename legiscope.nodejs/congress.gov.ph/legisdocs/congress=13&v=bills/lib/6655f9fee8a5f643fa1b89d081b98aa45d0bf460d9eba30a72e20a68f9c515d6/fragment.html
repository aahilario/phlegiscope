FULL TITLE : AN ACT UPGRADING THE MINIMUM SALARY GRADE OF PUBLIC SCHOOLS DISTRICT SUPERVISORS (PSDS) AND EDUCATION SUPERVISORS-1 (ES1), AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE 'COMPENSATION AND POSITION CLASSIFICATION ACT OF 1989'
