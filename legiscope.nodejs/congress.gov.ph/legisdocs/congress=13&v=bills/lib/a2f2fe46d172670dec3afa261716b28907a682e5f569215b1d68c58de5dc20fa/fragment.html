HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02674
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING APPROPRIATE CIVIL SERVICE ELIGIBILITY TO ELECTED LOCAL GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for a mechanism that will allow public servants from the Provincial Governor to the regular members of the Sangguniang Bayan to join the regular work force after the completion of their term of office by granting them appropriate civil service eligibility.
