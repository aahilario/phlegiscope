HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03949
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE INTERVENTION BY OR USE OF ANY UNIT OR MEMBER OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES IN LABOR MATTERS, PROVIDING PENALTY FOR VIOLATION THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER MATTERS
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits the use of military personnel in purely labor matters. It also prohibits the Armed Forces of the Philippines from interfering, disrupting or dispersing any concerted activity of employees in connection with any labor or industrial dispute.
