House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02773
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO FURTHER STRENGTHEN THE YOUTH DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM BY CREATING A LOCAL YOUTH DEVELOPMENT COUNCIL IN EVERY MUNICIPALITY, CITY AND PROVINCE, ESTABLISHING YOUTH CENTERS THEREIN, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Local Youth Development Council Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes a Local Youth Development Council in every city and municipality to formulate policies and implement youth development programs, projects and services in their respective areas of jurisdiction.
