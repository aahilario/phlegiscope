HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01405
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LOWERING THE COSTS OF MEDICINES AND HEALTH CARE IN THE PHILIPPINES BY EXEMPTING THE SALE, IMPORTATION, PROCESSING, REPACKING OR MANUFACTURING OF: MEDICINES; PHARMACEUTICAL PRODUCTS; MEDICAL, DENTAL AND HOSPITAL EQUIPMENT; AND RAW MATERIALS TO BE USED IN THE MANUFACTURE THEREOF; FROM ALL DUTIES AND TAXES
ABSTRACT : Amends Section 103 of PD 1994 exempting from value-added tax medicines and hospital equipment to help lower the cost of health care.
