HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00760
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE BENEFITS OF BARANGAY OFFICIALS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 393 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the minimum honoraria, allowances and other emoluments prescribed under Section 393 of the Local Government Code from Php1,000.00 to PhP5,000.00 per month for the punong barangay and from PhP600.00 to PhP4,000.00 per month for each SB member, treasurer and secretary.
