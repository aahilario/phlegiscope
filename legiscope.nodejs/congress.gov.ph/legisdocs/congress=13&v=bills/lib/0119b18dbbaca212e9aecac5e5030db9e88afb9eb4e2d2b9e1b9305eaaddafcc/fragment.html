HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00047
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE WAIVER OF THE RETIREMENT AGE REQUIREMENT OF GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEE, WHO HAS RENDERED AT LEAST TWENTY (20)YEARS OF SERVICE, FURTHER AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1146, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED GOVERNMENT SERVICE INSURANCE SYSTEM ACT OF 1977
ABSTRACT : To enable to government to get rid of employees who no longer have the drive to perform efficiently as they are merely waiting for the right age to retire and to maintain fresh legs and minds in the service, this bill proposes to allow government employees to retire after serving the government for a period of twenty (20) years. This will open windows of opportunities for retirees to venture into other fields using the amounts received from their retirement benefits while still in their productive years.
