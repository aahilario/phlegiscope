House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03257
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REGULATE THE PRACTICE OF CLINICAL PSYCHOLOGY AND PSYCHOMETRICS CREATING FOR THIS PURPOSE A PROFESSIONAL REGULATORY BOARD OF CLINICAL PSYCHOLOGY AND PSYCHOMETRICS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSE
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine Clinical Psychology and Psychometrics Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill defines the scope of the practice of psychologists and psychometricians and sets the criteria for the licensure of the practitioners of the profession. It creates a clinical and psycometrics board tasked to oversee the practice of the profession and the issuance of licenses.
