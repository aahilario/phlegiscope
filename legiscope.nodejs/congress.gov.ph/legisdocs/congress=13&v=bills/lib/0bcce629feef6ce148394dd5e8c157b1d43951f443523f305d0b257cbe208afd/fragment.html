House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05816
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 3 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL COMMEMORATIVE DAY TO BE CALLED THE 'NATIONAL VICTORY AND LIBERATION DAY OF THE PHILIPPINES'
SHORT TITLE : Declaring September 3 of Every Year As The 'National Victory and Liberation Day of the Philippines'
ABSTRACT : The bill declares September 3 of every year as a nojn-working holiday to commemorate the victory of Filipinos over the Japanese forces and the end of World War II.
