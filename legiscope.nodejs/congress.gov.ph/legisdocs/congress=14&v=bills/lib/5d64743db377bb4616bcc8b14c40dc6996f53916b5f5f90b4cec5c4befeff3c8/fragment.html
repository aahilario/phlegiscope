FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOCATING THE TRAVEL TAX COLLECTION TO THE PHILIPPINE TOURISM AUTHORITY, AMENDING THEREBY PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1183, AS AMENDED, AND REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7722, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Travel Tax reform Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the use of the proceeds of the travel tax to be used for the tourism projects of the Philippine Tourism Authority.
