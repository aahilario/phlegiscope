HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04144
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR LIMITATIONS ON THE TOLL SETTING POWER OF THE TOLL REGULATORY BOARD AND OTHER PURPOSES, AMENDING SECTION THREE OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBERED ELEVEN HUNDRED AND TWELVE, AS AMENDED, ENTITLED, "TOLL OPERATIONS DECREE"
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the Toll Regulatory Board to establish guidelines for setting toll rates. It proposes to subject the issuance, modification and promulgation of toll rates to extensive public hearings and consultations with toll users and other transport stakeholders.
