HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02643
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ACCELERATING THE DEVELOPMENT OF A NATIONAL RAILWAY SYSTEM, CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE THE NATIONAL RAILWAY AUTHORITY, PRESCRIBING ITS POWERS, FUNCTIONS AND DUTIES
SHORT TITLE : "National Railways Act"
ABSTRACT : The National Railways Authority (NRA) shall formulate and adopt a comprehensive national railways development plan and implement the same. The NRA shall control, develop, construct, operate and maintain the railway facilities.
