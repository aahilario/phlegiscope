FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING TUITION FEES OF PRIVATE EDUCATION INSTITUTIONS, THEREBY AMENDING BATAS PAMBANSA BLG 232 AS AMENDED, CREATING A PRIVATE SCHOOL FEE REGULATORY BOARD, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Private School Fee Regulation Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Private School Fee Regulation Act of 2005". The bill directs all private educational institutions to determine the tuition fee rates based on a reasonable return of investment of not more than 6%.
