HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02455
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING INDUSTRIES ENGAGED IN THE EXPLOITATION OF NATURAL RESOURCES TO GIVE PREFERENTIAL EMPLOYMENT TO LOCAL RESIDENTS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill compels corporations, partnerships associations or individuals engaged in the development and exploitation of natural resources to give employment preference to residents of the province or city where such activities are conducted.
