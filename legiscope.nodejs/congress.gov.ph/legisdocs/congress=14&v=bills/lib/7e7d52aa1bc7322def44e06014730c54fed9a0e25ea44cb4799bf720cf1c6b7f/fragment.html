HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03397
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CLASSIFYING TRANSACTIONS INVOLVING PETROLEUM PRODUCTS AS ZERO PERCENT (0%) VALUE-ADDED TAX (VAT) TRANSACTIONS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 108 (B) OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8424, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9337, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE TAX REFORM ACT OF 1997, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes to suspend the imposition of the 12% value added tax on crude oil and finished fuel products for a period of one year by classifying the same as zero-rated goods.
