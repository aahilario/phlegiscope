House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02121
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ENHANCING THE ADMINISTRATION OF JUVENILE JUSTICE, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER RELATED PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The Juvenile Justice Act of 2001
ABSTRACT : "The Juvenile Justice Act of 2001." The bill provides a just and more humane system of treating juvenile offenders with the aim in view of rehabilitating and reintegrating them into the mainstream of society. Creates the Office of the Juvenile Justice and Delinquency Prevention to oversee the implementation of the act, and the Office of the Child Ombudsman to check and monitor its implementation.
