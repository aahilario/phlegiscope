FULL TITLE : AN ACT RENAMING THE NATIONAL HISTORICAL INSTITUTE (NHI) TO NATIONAL HISTORICAL COMMISSION (NHC), INSTITUTIONALIZING REORGANIZATION THEREOF, PROVIDING FOR THE IMPROVEMENT AND STRENGTHENING OF ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : National Historical Commission Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "National Historical Commission Act of 2004." The bill prescribes the general mandate of the Commission, the composition and functions of the Board of Commissioners, and the organizational structure of the national secretarial of the National Historical Commission.
