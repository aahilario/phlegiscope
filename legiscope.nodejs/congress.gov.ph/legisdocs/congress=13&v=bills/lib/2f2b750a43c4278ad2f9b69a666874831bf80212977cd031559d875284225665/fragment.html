FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING TERRORISM AS HEINOUS CRIME, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AS AMENDED, PROVIDING FOR THE PENALTIES THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Terrorism Act
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Terrorism Act." Defines terrorism as the systematic use of terror, intense fear, violence, bombing committed bya group as a means of coercion and to intimidate a population or government into granting their demands.
