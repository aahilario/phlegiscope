House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02986
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE REGULATION OF THE PUBLIC LAND TRANSPORTATION SERVICE AND PRESCRIBING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION OF ANY PROVISION THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : Public Land Utility Transport Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Public Land Utility Transport Act of 2004." The bill requires all operators of public land utility vehicles traveling continuously for six hours to have a qualified alternate driver. It also requires the installation of comfort rooms in buses plying provincial routes with travel time of at least four hours.
