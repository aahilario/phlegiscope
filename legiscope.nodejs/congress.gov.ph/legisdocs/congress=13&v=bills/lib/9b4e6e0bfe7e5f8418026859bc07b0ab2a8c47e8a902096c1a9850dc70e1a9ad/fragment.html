HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00598
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING AUTOMATIC PROMOTION OF GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES UPON RETIREMENT FROM GOVERNMENT SERVICE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill aims to give due recognition to government officials and employees who have been in the government service for more than twenty (20) years despite their meager salaries, by promoting them one grade higher at the time of their retirement. The adjusted salary grade level shall be used as the basis in computing his retirement pay.
