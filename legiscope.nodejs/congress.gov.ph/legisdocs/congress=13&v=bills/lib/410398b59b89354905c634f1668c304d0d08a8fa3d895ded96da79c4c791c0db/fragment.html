HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03694
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT 7916, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE ACT OF 1995"
ABSTRACT : The bill provides that the levy (five percent of gross value) imposed on all business enterprises located within economic zones (ecozones) be allocated as follows: a) 2% to the national government; b) 2% to the city/municipality where the enterprise is located; and c) 1% to the province having territorial jurisdiction over the area where the ecozone is located.
