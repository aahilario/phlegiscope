HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00992
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE MONTHLY PENSION OF RETIRED PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS AND NON-TEACHING PERSONNEL OF THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION, CULTURE AND SPORTS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : This bill directs the GSIS to increase the monthly pension of retired teachers and the non-teaching personnel of the Department of Education by 100% and their beneficiaries by 50%.
