House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01954
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ENHANCING SAVINGS AND CREDIT OR FINANCIAL SERVICE COOPERATIVES, AMENDING THEREFOR REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 6938, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE COOPERATIVE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AND REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 6939, CREATING THE COOPERATIVE DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY
SHORT TITLE : Financial Service Cooperative Act
ABSTRACT : "Financial Service Cooperative Act." Provides for the FSCs' organization, registration, federation, articles of cooperation, purpose, share capital, membership, affiliation, management, administration, audit, ethics, officers, deposits, funds, credit, security, net surplus, assets, investments, merger, consolidation, dissolution, functions and powers of federations, inspections and investigations and government supervision and regulation.
