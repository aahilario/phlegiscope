House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00050
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE ESTABLISHMENT AND OPERATIONS OF HEALTH MAINTENANCE ORGANIZATIONS (HMOs), AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Health Maintenance Organization Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Health Maintenance Organization Act of 2010.” Encourages the establishment and operation of health maintenance organizations (HMOs) by granting them reasonable incentives; establishes the regulatory framework for HMOs; and recognizes and protects the rights of HMOs, health care providers and members.
