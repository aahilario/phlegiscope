House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01441
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING PROFESSIONAL LICENSE TO TEACHERS WHO HAVE RENDERED AT LEAST TWO (2)YEARS OF EFFICIENT SERVICE IN HIGH RISK AREAS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Special Professional License Privilege for Teachers in High Risk Areas
ABSTRACT : "Special Professional License Privilege for Teachers in High Risk Areas." The measure defines high risk areas as "those places where terrorism, kidnapping and insurrection are common and identified by authorities as a sanctuary of rebels or where there are military operations." Grants license to non-licensed teachers who have served for two years in such areas.
