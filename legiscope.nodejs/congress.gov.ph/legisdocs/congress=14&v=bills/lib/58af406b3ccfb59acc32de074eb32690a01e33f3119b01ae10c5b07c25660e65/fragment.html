HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00100
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE USE OF THE WORDS "MUSLIMS" AND "CHRISTIAN" IN MASS MEDIA TO DESCRIBE ANY PERSON SUSPECTED OF OR CONVICTED FOR HAVING COMMITTED CRIMINAL OR UNLAWFUL ACTS, AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : Prohibiting the Use of the Words "muslims" and "Christian" in Mass Media to Describe Any Person Suspected Of or Convicted For Having Committed Criminal or Unlawful Acts
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits any person to use in mass media the words "Muslim" and/or "Christian" or any other word that would denote religious, regional or ethnic affiliation to describe any person suspected of or convicted for having committed criminal or unlawful acts.
