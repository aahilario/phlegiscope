House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00948
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING RELIGIOUS OR RACIAL PROFILING AGAINST INDIGENOUS CULTURAL COMMUNITIES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Religious and Racial Profiling Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Religious and Racial Profiling Act of 2007". The bill prohibits acts that are commonly described as religious or racial profiling against Filipinos who are considered minorities by virtue of their cultural or religious background.
