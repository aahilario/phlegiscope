HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01127
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING FEBRUARY FOUR OF EVERY YEAR AS "ARAW NG PAGKADAKILA SA MGA PILIPINONG NAGBUWIS NG BUHAY NOONG DIGMAANG PILIPINO-AMERIKANO", PROVIDING FOR SUPPORT PROGRAMS, AND REQUIRING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Declaring February Four Of Every Year As "araw Ng Pagkadakila Sa Mga Pilipinong Nagbuwis Ng Buhay Noong Digmaang Pilipino-amerikano"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to declare February 4th of every year as a regular national holiday to commemorate the start of the Philippine-American War and to honor the courageous and indomitable spirit of the Filipino people who fought the American colonizers.
