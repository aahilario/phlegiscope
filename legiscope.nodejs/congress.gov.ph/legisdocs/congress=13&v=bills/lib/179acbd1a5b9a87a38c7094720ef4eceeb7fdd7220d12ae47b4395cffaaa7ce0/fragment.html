HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01945
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO RATIONALIZE THE COMPOSITION AND FUNCTIONS OF THE NATIONAL LABOR RELATIONS COMMISSION THEREBY AMENDING ARTICLES 213, 214, 215 AND 216, BOOK V, TITLE II, OF THE LABOR CODE, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Increases the members of the National Labor Relations Commission from 14 to 23 and requires the Labor Arbitration Officers to be members of the Bar. Provides that the chairman and members of the Commission shall have the same rank and salary as judges of the Regional Trial Court.
