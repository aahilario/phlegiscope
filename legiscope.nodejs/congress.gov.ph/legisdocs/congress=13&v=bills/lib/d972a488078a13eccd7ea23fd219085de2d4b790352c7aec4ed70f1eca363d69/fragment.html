HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01758
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCLUDING LIQUEFIED PETROLEUM GAS (LPG) IN THE LIST OF BASIC NECESSITIES AS DEFINED UNDER SECTION 3 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7581, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE PRICE ACT OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : Liquefield petroleum gas (LPG), being the most commonly used fuel in many households, is actually a basic need. The bill proposes to include LPG in the list of basic commodities so that its price can automatically be controlled or "frozen" during calamities, disasters and other emergency situations.
