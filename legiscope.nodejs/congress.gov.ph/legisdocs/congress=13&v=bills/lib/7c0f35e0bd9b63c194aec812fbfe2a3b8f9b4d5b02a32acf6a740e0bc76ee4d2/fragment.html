House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01478
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING A NATIONAL LAND USE POLICY, PROVIDING THE IMPLEMENTING MECHANISMS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Land Use Act of the Philippines
ABSTRACT : "National Land Use Act of the Philippines." The bill provides for national land use classification, planning, zoning guidelines and standards. Reconstitutes the National Land Use Committee into the Land Use Policy Administration and prescribes its powers and functions. Classifies lands into agricultural, alienable and disposable, forest, timber, watershed, coastal and mineral. Sets the criteria for the development of settlement, tourism and industrial areas and infrastructure.
