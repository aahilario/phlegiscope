HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03118
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR ADDITIONAL BENEFITS AND PRIVILEGES TO PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS IN THE BASIC EDUCATION SYSTEM, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FORTY-SIX HUNDRED SEVENTY, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE MAGNA CARTA FOR PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill grants public school teachers additional benefits, incentives and privileges, to wit: a) incentive allowance to those assigned outside of their locality; b) difficulty differential under certain circumstances; c) protection against any form of discrimination; and d) exemption from election duties, among others.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ANGARA, JUAN EDGARDO M.
