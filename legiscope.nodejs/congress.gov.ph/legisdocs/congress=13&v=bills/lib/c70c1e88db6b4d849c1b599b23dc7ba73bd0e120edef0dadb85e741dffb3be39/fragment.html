FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 31 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6770, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE OMBUDSMAN ACT OF 1989"' AS AMENDED, ALLOWING LAWYERS TO ACT AS SPECIAL INVESTIGATOR OR PROSECUTOR ON BEHALF OF THE OMBUDSMAN
ABSTRACT : The bill allows the Ombudsman to seek the assistance of private lawyers in handling cases against misfits in public office.
