HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03320
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR AN AUTOMATIC PAY INCREASE TO ANY MEMBER OF THE ARMED FORCES WHO IS DEPLOYED AWAY FROM THE MEMBER'S PERMANENT STATION OR, IN THE CASE OF A MEMBER OF A RESERVE COMPONENT OF THE ARMED FORCES, THE MEMBER'S HOME RECORD, ONCE THE DEPLOYMENT PERIOD EXCEEDS 180 DAYS OF CONTINUOUS DUTY
SHORT TITLE : "Extended Deployment Pay Increase Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for an automatic increase of P10,000 per month on the basic pay to any member of the Armed Forces who is deployed away from the member's permanent station once the deployment exceeds 180 days of continuous duty.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.
