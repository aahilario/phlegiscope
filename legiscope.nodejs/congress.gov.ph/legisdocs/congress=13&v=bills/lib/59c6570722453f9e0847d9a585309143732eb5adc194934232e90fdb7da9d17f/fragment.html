FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO INCREASE THE PENALTY FOR CRUELTY TO ANIMALS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 8 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8485
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes the penalty of imprisonment of not less than ONE YEAR but not more than FOUR years AND a fine of not less the FIVE thousand but not more than TEN thousand.
