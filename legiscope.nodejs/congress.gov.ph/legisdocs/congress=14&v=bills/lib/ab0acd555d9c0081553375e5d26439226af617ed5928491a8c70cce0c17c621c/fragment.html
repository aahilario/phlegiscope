HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00783
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE ESTABLISHMENT OF POLITICAL DYNASTY
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Political Dynasty Act of 2007
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Political Dynasty Act of 2007". The bill aims to disqualify a person who has a political dynasty relationship with an incumbent elective official from running for any elective public office within the same city and/or province where the incumbent elective official is also running.
