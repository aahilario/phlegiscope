House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00976
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING NIGHT SHIFT DIFFERENTIAL PAY TO GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES INCLUDING THOSE IN GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The measure grants government employees night shift differential not exceeding 20% of the hourly basic pay for work performed between 10:00 p.m and 6:00 a.m.
