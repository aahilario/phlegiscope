HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02745
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REQUIRE THE SECRETARIES OF HEALTH AND LABOR TO ISSUE REGULATIONS TO ELIMINATE OR MINIMIZE THE SIGNIFICANT RISK OF NEEDLESTICK INJURY TO HEALTH CARE WORKERS
SHORT TITLE : "Health Care Worker Needlestick Prevention Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Secretaries of Health and Labor and Employment to prescribe the following: a) bloodborne pathogens standard; b)exceptions to the bloodborne pathogens standard; c) exposure plan control; d) sharps injury log; and e) training on the use of needleless systems and sharps with engineered sharp injury protections.
