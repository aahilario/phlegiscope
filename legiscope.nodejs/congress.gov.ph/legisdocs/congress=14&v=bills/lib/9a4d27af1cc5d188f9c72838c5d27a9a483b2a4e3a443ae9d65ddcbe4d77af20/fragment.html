HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02203
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 4 OF REPUBLIC ACT 9184, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "GOVERNMENT PROCUREMENT REFORM ACT"
ABSTRACT : The bill clarifies that only treaties or international or executive agreements entered into by the government prior to the enactment of RA 9184 (Government Procurement Reform Act) shall be exempt from its coverage.
