HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00607
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ANY PERSON, ASSOCIATION, CORPORATION, OR ENTITY TO SECURE THE NECESSARY CLEARANCE OR PERMIT FROM THE NATIONAL HISTORICAL INSTITUTE (NHI) BEFORE COMMENCING ANY CONSTRUCTION OF REAL ESTATE DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS IN AREAS IDENTIFIED, DESIGNATED AND APPROPRIATELY MARKED AS HISTORICAL PLACES OR IN AREAS PROXIMATE THERETO, PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Historical Places PRotection Act
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits persons or firms from constructing projects or real estate development in historical places without a permit from the National Historical Institute. It provides stiff penalties for non-compliance with the permit requirement.
