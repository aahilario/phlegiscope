House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01053
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE COMMISSION OF ACTS OF TORTURE AND OTHER CRUEL, INHUMAN AND DEGRADING TREATMENT OR PUNISHMENTS, PRESCRIBING PENALTIES THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Torture Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Torture Act of 2007". The bill seeks to declare torture as a criminal act. It defines the acts of physical torture, mental/psychological torture and pharmacological torture and provides penalties therefor.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : HONTIVEROS, ANA THERESIA "RISA"
