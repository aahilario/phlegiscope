HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01997
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING DISCRIMINATION IN THE HIRING OF EMPLOYEES OR WORKERS IN GENERAL WITH REGARD TO THEIR AGE AND EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTION FROM WHICH THEY EARNED THEIR DEGREES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : the measure prohibits "entry-point discrimination" which is defined as the hiring of young applicants and graduates from renowned school. It imposes a fine of P10,000 or imprisonment of three months to three years to guilty employers.
