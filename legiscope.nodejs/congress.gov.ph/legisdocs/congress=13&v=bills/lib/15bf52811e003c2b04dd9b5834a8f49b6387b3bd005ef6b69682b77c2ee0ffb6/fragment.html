HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00451
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8189 ENTITLED, 'AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A GENERAL REGISTRATION OF VOTERS, ADOPTING A SYSTEM OF CONTINUING REGISTRATION, PRESCRIBING THE PROCEDURES THEREOF AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR' BY DELETING SECTION 44 THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : Amending RA 8189
ABSTRACT : Section 44 of RA 8189 mandates the reassignment of election officers every four years to a new station outside of the congressional district they presently serve. The provision is viewed to be onerous since it dislocates their families, affects the schooling of their children and entails additional expenses in establishing a new residence. This bill seeks to delete Section 44and revert back to Article 7, Section 56 of the Omnibus Election Code as the operative rule governing the reassignment of elections officers.
