HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01142
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE IMPORTATION, DOMESTIC SALE OR USE OF GOODS, THE MANUFACTURE, SALE OR USE OF WHICH IS BANNED IN THE COUNTRY OF ORIGIN
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits any person, corporation or entity to import, sell or use goods which are prohibited from being manufactured, sold or used in the country of origin of such goods.
