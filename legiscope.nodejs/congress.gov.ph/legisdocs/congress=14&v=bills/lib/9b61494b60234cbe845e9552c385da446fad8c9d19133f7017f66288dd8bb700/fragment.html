HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03106
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING ADDITIONAL FIFTEEN (15) DAYS SICK LEAVE AND FIFTEEN (15) DAYS VACATION LEAVE ANNUALLY TO ALL JUDGES OF THE METROPOLITAN TRIAL COURTS, MUNICIPAL TRIAL COURTS, MUNICIPAL TRIAL COURTS IN CITIES, MUNICIPAL CIRCUIT TRIAL COURTS AND SHARI'A CIRCUIT COURTS
ABSTRACT : The bill grants an additional fifteen (15) days sick leave amd fifteen (15) days vacation leave benefits to judges of the Metropolitan Trial Courts, Municipal Trial Courts, Municipal Trial Courts in cities, Municipal Circuit Courts and Shari'a Circuit Courts to put them at par with their colleagues in the Regional Trial Courts and Shari'a District Courts.
