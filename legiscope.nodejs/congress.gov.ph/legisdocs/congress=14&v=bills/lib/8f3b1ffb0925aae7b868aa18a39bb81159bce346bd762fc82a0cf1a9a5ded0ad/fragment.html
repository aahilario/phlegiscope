HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02221
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING MATERNITY LEAVE BENEFITS TO UNMARRIED PREGNANT WOMEN WHO ARE IN GOVERNMENT SERVICE
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to address discrimination against unmarried pregnant women in the government sector by granting them maternity leave benefits such as those enjoyed by unmarried pregnant women in the private sector.
