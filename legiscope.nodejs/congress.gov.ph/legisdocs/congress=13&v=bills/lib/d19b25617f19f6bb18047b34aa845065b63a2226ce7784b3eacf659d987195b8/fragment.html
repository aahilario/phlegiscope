House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01338
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MAGNA CARTA FOR SOCIAL WORKERS AND SOCIAL WELFARE AND DEVELOPMENT WORKERS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Magna Carta for Social Workers and Social Welfare and Development Workers
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta for Social Workers and Social Welfare and Development Workers". The highlights of the bill include: a) the appointment of social workers in all social welfare and development agencies; b) payment for overtime work rendered; c) reasonable workload; d) entitlement to additional benefits; and e) promotion of their rights and privileges.
