HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01703
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 1 OF EACH YEAR A NON-WORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE COUNTRY TO BE KNOWN AS "NATIONAL FILIPINO FAMILY DAY"
ABSTRACT : The bill declares September 1st of every year as a non-working holiday in honor of Filipino families.
