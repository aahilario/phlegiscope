House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03427
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROHIBIT ANY EMPLOYER, LABOR CONTRACTOR AND LABOR ORGANIZATION FROM DISCRIMINATING AGAINST ANY INDIVIDUAL BECAUSE OF THE INDIVIDUAL'S AGE
SHORT TITLE : "Prohibition Against Age Discrimination Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill penalizes employers who discriminate against a person for employment by reason of age.
