House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05579
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECENTRALIZING THE PAYROLL SERVICES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION, CREATING A REGIONAL PAYROLL SERVICES UNIT (RPSU) IN ALL REGIONAL OFFICES INCLUDING THE AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (ARMM), EXCEPT FOR THE NATIONAL CAPITAL REGION (NCR) AND REGIONS IV-A (CALABARZON) AND IV-B (MIMAROPA), AND PROVIDING FOR FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Decentralizing the Payroll Services of the Department of Education Thereby Creating a Regional Payroll Services Unit
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to decentralize the Payroll Services Division of the Department of Education by establishing a Regional Payroll Services Unit in all regions.
