House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02550
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMOTING RESPONSIBLE PARENTHOOD BY PROVIDING INCENTIVES TO COUPLES/INDIVIDUALS WHO PRACTICE SURGICAL FAMILY PLANNING METHODS PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Population Management Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Population Management Act of 2004." The bill seeks to encourage married couples and individuals to practice surgical family planning methods and to limit the number of their children to, at most, three.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : EDUARDO V. ROQUERO, M.D.
