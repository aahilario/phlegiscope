SHORT TITLE : Magna Carta of the Philippine National Police
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta of the Philippine National Police." The bill provides for the rights, duties and responsibilities of PNP personnel, their hours of work, salary, welfare, health, injuries, retirement and death benefits.
