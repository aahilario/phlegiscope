House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01090
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS TO CONDUCT A QUARTERLY BREAST CANCER DETECTION PROGRAM
SHORT TITLE : Breast Cancer Detection Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Breast Cancer Detection Act of 2004." Cancer ranks as the third leading cause of death in the Philippines with breast cancer as the most prevalent. The bill seeks to institutionalize a national screening program for the early detection of breast cancer by compelling local government units, in cooperation with the Department of Health, to conduct quarterly tests.
