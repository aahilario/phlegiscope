House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04868
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING TUITION FEE DISCOUNTS TO QUALIFIED STUDENTS BELONGING TO THE INDIGENOUS CULTURAL COMMUNITIES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to make quality education accessible to indigenous cultural communities. It institutionalizes the grant of tuition fee discounts to deserving college students from the marginalized, underprivileged and depressed sectors.
