HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03780
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPOSING STIFFER PENALTIES AGAINST POLICE OFFICERS, WHO, MALICIOUSLY REFRAIN FROM INSTITUTING PROSECUTION FOR THE PUNISHMENT OF VIOLATORS OF THE LAW OR FOR FAILURE TO TESTIFY IN COURT RESULTING TO THE DISMISSAL OF CASES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLE 208 OF ACT NO. 3815, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED PENAL CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The measure imposes the penalty of prision correccional in its maximum period and perpetual or temporal special disqualification on police officers who maliciously or negligently fail to testify in court resulting to the dismissal of a case.
