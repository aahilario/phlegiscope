House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01876
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CALLING FOR THE ORGANIZATION OF A CONSTITUTIONAL CONVENTION AND POSTPONEMENT OF THE 2007 BARANGAY ELECTIONS
SHORT TITLE : "Call for Constitutional Convention and Postponement of Barangay Elections Act"
ABSTRACT : "Call for Constitutional Convention and Postponement of Barangay Elections Act". The bill calls for a Constitutional Convention that will amend or revise the present Constitution in order to make it more responsive to the demands of the times.
