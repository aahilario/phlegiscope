HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03265
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 8(f) AND 22(a) OF THE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8282 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SOCIAL SECURITY ACT OF 1997
ABSTRACT : The bill excludes the mandated cost of living allowances and the cash value of other remunerations in the determination of the contributions to the SSS. It also proposes to reduce the rate of penalty for delays in remittances from 3% to 1% per month.
