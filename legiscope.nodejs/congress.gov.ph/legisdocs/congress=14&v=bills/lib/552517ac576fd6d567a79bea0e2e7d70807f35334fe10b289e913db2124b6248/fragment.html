FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1464, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE TARIFF AND CUSTOMS CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-smuggling Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Anti-smuggling Act of 2007". The bill seeks to curb smuggling, both technical and outright, by providing systemic solutions that will make it easier to detect smuggling.
