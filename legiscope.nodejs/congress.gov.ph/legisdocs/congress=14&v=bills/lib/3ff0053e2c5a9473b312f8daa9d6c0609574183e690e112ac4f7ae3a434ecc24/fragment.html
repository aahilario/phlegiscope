HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00320
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 10 OF REPUBLIC ACT 6728 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS GOVERNMENT ASSISTANCE TO STUDENTS AND TEACHERS IN PRIVATE EDUCATION (GASTPE)
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for a mechanism whereby the different sectors in our educational system can exercise their right to express their positions in any proposed increase in tuition and other school fees.
