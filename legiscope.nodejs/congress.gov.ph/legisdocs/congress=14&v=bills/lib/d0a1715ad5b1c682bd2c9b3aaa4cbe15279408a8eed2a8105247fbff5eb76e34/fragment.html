HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00202
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE POSITION OF TOURISM OFFICER IN ALL PROVINCES, FIRST CLASS MUNICIPALITIES AND CITIES, OR MUNICIPALITIES AND CITIES WHERE TOURISM IS CONSIDERED A MAJOR LOCAL INDUSTRY, AND DEFINING ITS QUALIFICATIONS, POWERS AND DUTIES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CERTAIN SECTIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE
ABSTRACT : This bill addresses the issues of accessibility to basic tourism information and services and the lack of a comprehensive approach to tourism development resulting in low tourism arrivals in the country by creating the position of tourism officer in all provinces, municipalities and cities where tourism is considered a major local industry.
