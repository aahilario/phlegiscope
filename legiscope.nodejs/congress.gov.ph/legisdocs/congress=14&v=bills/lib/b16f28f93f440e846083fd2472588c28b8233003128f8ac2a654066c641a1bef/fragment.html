HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02621
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 5 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9227, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS AN ACT GRANTING ADDITIONAL COMPENSATION IN THE FORM OF SPECIAL ALLOWANCES FOR JUSTICES, JUDGES AND ALL OTHER POSITIONS IN THE JUDICIARY WITH THE EQUIVALENT RANK OF JUSTICES OF THE COURT OF APPEALS AND JUDGES OF THE REGIONAL TRIAL COURT AS WELL AS THE MUNICIPAL TRIAL COURT AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to grant justices, judges and other court officials who retired before November 23, 2003 the same special allowances given under RA 9227.
