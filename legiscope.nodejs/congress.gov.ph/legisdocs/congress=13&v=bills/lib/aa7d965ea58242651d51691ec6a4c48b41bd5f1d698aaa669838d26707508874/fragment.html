House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02569
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A COMPREHENSIVE WATER RESOURCES MANAGEMENT TO ADDRESS THE NATIONAL WATER CRISIS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The Water Resources Management Act of 1998
ABSTRACT : "The Water Resources Management Act of 1998." The bill provides for the creation of the Water Resources Authority of the Philippines which shall be responsible for the formulation of a national framework for water resources management based on a decentralized, community-based and participatory approach. It defines the powers and functions and organizational structure of the Authority.
