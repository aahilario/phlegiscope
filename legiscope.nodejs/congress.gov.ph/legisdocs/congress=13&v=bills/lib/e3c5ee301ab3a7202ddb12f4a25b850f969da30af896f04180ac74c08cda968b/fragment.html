House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03705
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN SECTIONS OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Amending Certain Sections of the National Internal Revenue Code of 1997, As Amended
ABSTRACT : The bill expands the coverage of the valued added tax to include previously zero-rated and exempt transactions, as follows: a) sale of generated power by generation companies, b) sale or importation of petroleum products, c) sale or importation of raw materials used in the manufacture of petroleum products by the buyer/importer himself, d) importation of passenger/cargo vessels of more than 5,000 tons be used for international shipping, and e) services rendered in the exercise of the medical and legal professions.
