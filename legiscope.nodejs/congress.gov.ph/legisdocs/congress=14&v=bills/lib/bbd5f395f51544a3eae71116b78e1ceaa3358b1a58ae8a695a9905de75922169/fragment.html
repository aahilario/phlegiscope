HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00990
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING COMPULSORY LIFE INSURANCE COVERAGE TO ACCREDITED BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE "BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS' BENEFITS AND INCENTIVES ACT OF 1995"
ABSTRACT : The bill grants compulsory life insurance coverage to accredited barangay health workers. The monthly minimum contribution shall be shared by the national government and the local government unit concerned on a 50-50 basis.
