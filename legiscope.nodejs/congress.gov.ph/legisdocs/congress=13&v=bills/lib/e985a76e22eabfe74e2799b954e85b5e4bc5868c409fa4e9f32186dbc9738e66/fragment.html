House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01535
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE NATIONAL POWER CORPORATION TO ENSURE THE OPTIMUM UTILIZATION OF POWER SUPPLY WHICH THE GOVERNMENT HAS GUARANTEED TO PURCHASE FROM INDEPENDENT POWER PRODUCERS (IPPs), PROVIDING A POWER REBATE SYSTEM IN FAVOR OF THE CONSUMERS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Power Rebate Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Power Rebate Act of 2004". The bill mandates the Manila Electric Company (MERALCO), the electric power cooperatives and distributors to give their consumers a monthly rebate of unutilized kilowatts. The measure directs the Energy Regulatory Commission to formulate a system to determine the amount of rebate of utilized kilowatts based on the consumer's historical electric consumption.
