HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01398
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING CITY MAYORS, MUNICIPAL MAYORS AND PROVINCIAL GOVERNORS FROM MAKING APPOINTMENTS TWO (2) MONTHS IMMEDIATELY BEFORE THE NEXT LOCAL ELECTIONS AND PENDING ASSUMPTION TO OFFICE OF THEIR SUCCESSORS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits outgoing local elective officials from making casual, contractual, temporary or permanent appointments after the local elections and pending assumption to office of their successors.
