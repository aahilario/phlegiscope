HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04132
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 4 (PARAGRAPH 58) AND PROVIDING ADDITIONAL SECTION ON MUNICIPAL FISHERIES, CHAPTER 2, ARTICLE 1 OF R.A. 8550, ENTITLED: "AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE DEVELOPMENT, MANAGEMENT AND CONSERVATION OF THE FISHERIES AND AQUATIC RESOURCES, INTEGRATING ALL LAWS PERTINENT THERETO, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks the adoption of the archipelagic principle in the definition of municipal waters. It stipulates that "where the territory of a municipality include several islands, the outer most points of such islands shall be used as base points and connected by archipelagic baselines, irrespective of the length of such baselines from the main coastlines".
