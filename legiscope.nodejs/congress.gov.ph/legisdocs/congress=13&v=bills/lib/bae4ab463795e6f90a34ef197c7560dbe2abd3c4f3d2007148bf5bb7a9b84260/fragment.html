House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02930
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING ARTICLE 81 OF ACT NUMBERED THIRTY EIGHT HUNDRED AND FIFTEEN (ACT NO. 3815) OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill provides that in cases wherein the death sentence is not carried out within the prescribed period, the failure to carry out the death penalty shall not result to a prescription of the death sentence.
