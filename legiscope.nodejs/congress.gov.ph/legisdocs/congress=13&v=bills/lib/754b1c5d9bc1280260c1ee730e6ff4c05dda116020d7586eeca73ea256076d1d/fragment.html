HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00764
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING THE RIGHT TO FRANCHISES TO DEVELOP THE RURAL AIRPORT INFRASTRUCTURE NETWORK IN THE PHILIPPINES FOR COMMERCIAL AND OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to grant a francisee the right to construct, manage, operate and maintain a rural airport in any part of the country in order to eventually make each municipality regularly accessible or within easy reach. The establishment of a private rural airport infrastructure will encourage economic activity in the hinterlands by serving not only as transportation hubs but also as industrial, commercial and service centers.
