House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00027
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A FREE BREAKFAST PROGRAM FOR PUBLIC SCHOOL CHILDREN IN DAY CARE CENTERS, PRE-SCHOOLS AND ELEMENTARY SCHOOLS IN THE COUNTRY AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : Institutionalizes the free breakfast program which shall consist of fortified instant noodles or iron-fortified rice or fortified biscuits or any other appropriate form to address hunger primarily and malnutrition secondarily.
