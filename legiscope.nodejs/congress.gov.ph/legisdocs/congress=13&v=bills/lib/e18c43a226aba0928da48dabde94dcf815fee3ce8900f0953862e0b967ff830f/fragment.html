HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00381
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO DEFINE THE PARAMETERS OF RESPONSIBILITY OF AN EMPLOYER IN CASES OF SEXUAL HARASSMENT IN THE WORKPLACE
ABSTRACT : The proposed measure seeks to define the parameters of responsibility of an employer in cases of sexual harassment in the workplace. It obliges the employer to ensure that the company's policy on sexual harassment is known to all employees from the start of their employment. It proposes to strengthen the Sexual Harassment Law by adding in the definition of sexual harassment the notion of the act as "an unwelcome behavior of a sexual nature or other unwelcome behavior based on sex, that violated the employees integrity at work".
