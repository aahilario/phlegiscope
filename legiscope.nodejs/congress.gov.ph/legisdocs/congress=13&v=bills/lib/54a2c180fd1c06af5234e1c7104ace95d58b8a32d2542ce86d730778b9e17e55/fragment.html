House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01773
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE USE OF GOVERNMENT AMBULANCES, PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Regulating the Use of Government Ambulances
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits the use of government ambulances for: a) office service vehicle, for recreation or for personal use; b) to transport patients not in emergency situtation; and c) for private use for a fee or without.
