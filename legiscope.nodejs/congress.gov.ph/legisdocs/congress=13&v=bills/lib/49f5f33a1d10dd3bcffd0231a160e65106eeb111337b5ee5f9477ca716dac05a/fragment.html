FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PHILIPPINE PRE-NEED INSURANCE CORPORATION, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND DUTIES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Establishing the Philippine Pre-Need Insurance Corporation
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the establishment of the Philippine Pre-need Insurance Corporation and a pre-need insurance fund. It also seeks to prescribe sanctions against unsafe and unsound practices in the conduct of the business.
