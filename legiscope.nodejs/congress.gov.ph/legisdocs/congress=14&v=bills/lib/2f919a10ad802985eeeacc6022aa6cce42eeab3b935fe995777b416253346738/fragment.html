House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00171
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING REFORMS IN LAND ADMINISTRATION
SHORT TITLE : "The Land Administration Reform Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The Land Administration Reform Act of 2007". The bill consolidates under the Land Administration Authority all land administration functions such as surveying, mapping, land classification, titling, disposition and registration.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ANGARA, JUAN EDGARDO M.
