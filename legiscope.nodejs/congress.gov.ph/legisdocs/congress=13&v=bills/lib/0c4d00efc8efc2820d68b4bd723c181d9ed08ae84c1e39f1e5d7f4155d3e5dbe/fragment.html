House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01804
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL SEAFARERS ADMINISTRATION, PRESCRIBING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : National Seafarers Administration Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "National Seafarers Administration Act of 2004." This bill creates the Administration to be composed of full-time officials, not just an advisory group. The Administration will be charged with formulating policies and programs for the protection and promotion of the rights and welfare of seamen assigned aboard foreign vessels.
