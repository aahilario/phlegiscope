House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02317
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL LAND TRANSPORTATION AUTHORITY, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Land Transportation Authority (NLTA) Act
ABSTRACT : "National Land Transportation Authority (NLTA) Act." The bill provides for the creation of the NLTA to absorb the functions of the Land Transportation Office and the Land Transportation Franchising and Regulatory Board as the primary policy-making, programming, coordinating, implementing, regulating and administrative government agency in the nation's land transportation system
