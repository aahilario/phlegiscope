House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01100
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A COMPREHENSIVE HAZARDOUS AND RADIOACTIVE WASTES MANAGEMENT, PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF AND FOR OTHE PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Hazardous and Radioactive Wastes Management Act of 2003
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Hazardous and Radioactive Wastes Management Act of 2003." The bill establishes a system of waste storage, transport, treatment and disposal. Sets the criteria for identifying hazardous radioactive waste which contain carcinogens, mutagens or teratogens at levels dangerous to human life. Prohibits the entry and transit of radioactice wastes into the country.
