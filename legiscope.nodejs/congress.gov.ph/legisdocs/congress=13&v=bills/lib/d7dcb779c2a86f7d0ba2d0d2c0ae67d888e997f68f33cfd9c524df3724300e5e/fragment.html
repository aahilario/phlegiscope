FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING ALL FILIPINO EMPLOYEES AND WORKERS NATIONWIDE RECEIVING LESS THAN FIFTEEN THOUSAND PESOS (P15,000) MONTHLY WAGE AN EXEMPTION FROM INCOME AND WITHHOLDING TAXES
SHORT TITLE : Income and Withholding Tax Exemption Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Income and Withholding Tax Exemption Act of 2004." The bill exempts low-income earners from paying income taxes to alleviate their poverty.
