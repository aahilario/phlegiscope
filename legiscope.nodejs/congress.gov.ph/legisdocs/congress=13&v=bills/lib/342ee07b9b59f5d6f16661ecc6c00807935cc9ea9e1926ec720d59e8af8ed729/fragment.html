House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05457
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE MONTHLY COMPENSATION OF DAY CARE AND BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to grant day care workers and barangay health workers a monthly compensation of not less than P5,000; 50% of which shall be paid by the barangay and the balance by the city/municipality concerned.
