HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03412
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING GRANTS TO PUBLIC EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS IN ORDER TO ALLOW SUCH INSTITUTIONS TO PROMOTE CERTAIN EDUCATION INITIATIVES
SHORT TITLE : "Education Initiatives Act of 2008"
ABSTRACT : The bill awards grants to schools that promote the following: a) charter education program; b) early reading program; c) safe and drug-free environment; d) standardized testing program; e) curriculum development panels; f) increased teaching standards program; and g) teaching excellence program.
