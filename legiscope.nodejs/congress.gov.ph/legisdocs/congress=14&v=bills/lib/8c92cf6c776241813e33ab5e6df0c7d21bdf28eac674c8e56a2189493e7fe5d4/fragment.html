House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00375
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO SPUR THE PLANTING OF BILLION TREES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Billion Trees Act of 2007."
ABSTRACT : "Billion Trees Act of 2007". The bill targets the planting of a billion trees covering about 1.0 million hectares over a period of five years. It proposes to tap the police, local government units, military, upland communities, the private sector, NGOs and civic organizations in the reforestation program.
