HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02956
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING CIVIC SERVICE ELIGIBILITY UNDER CERTAIN CONDITIONS TO A GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEE WHOSE STATUS OF APPOINTMENT IS CASUAL OR CONTRACTUAL AND WHO HAS RENDERED A TOTAL OF TEN (10) YEARS OF EFFICIENT SERVICE
ABSTRACT : The bill grants civil service elegibility to casual and contractual employees who have rendered ten (10) years of efficient service in the government.
