House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00516
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING YOUTH CENTERS IN ALL FIFTEEN (15) REGIONS OF THE COUNTRY, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Youth Center Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Youth Center Act of 2004". The bill provides for a concrete program that will alleviate the plight of the youth by establishing an integrated youth center in every region in the country to be utilized as transient shelters, lodging facilities and skills training centers for those who are in need of immediate assistance and attention.
