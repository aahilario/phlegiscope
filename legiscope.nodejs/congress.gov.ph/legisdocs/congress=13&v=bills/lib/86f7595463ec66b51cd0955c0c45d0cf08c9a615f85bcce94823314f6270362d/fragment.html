HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02678
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AFFORD INTELLECTUAL COMPETENCE FOR THE MEN IN UNIFORM BY GIVING ACCESS TO ALL QUALIFIED SOLDIERS AND POLICEMEN THE PRIVILEGE TO ENROLL IN THE OPEN UNIVERSITY FOR INDEPENDENT STUDIES OF THE UNIVERSITY OF THE PHILIPPINES ASSISTING THEM IN THEIR INTELLECTUAL QUEST AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill provides an opportunity for qualified soldiers and policemen to secure a degree by allowing them to enroll in the Open University for Independent Studies of the University of the Philippines. It directs the Department of National Defense and the Department of the Interior and Local Government to prioritize the approval of the leaves of these soldiers/policemen.
