House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01067
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A COMPREHENSIVE JUVENILE JUSTICE SYSTEM AND DELINQUENCY PREVENTION PROGRAM, CREATING THE OFFICE OF JUVENILE JUSTICE AND DELINQUENCY PREVENTION UNDER THE DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The Juvenile Justice and Delinquency Prevention Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "The Juvenile Justice and Delinquency Prevention Act of 2004." Youth offenders should be treated differently from adult offenders. The bill intends to provide a just and humane system of treating juvenile offenders through a comprehensive approach toward rehabilitating and reintegrating them into society. It increases the age of criminal responsibility from nine to twelve years old.
