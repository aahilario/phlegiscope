House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03091
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING SCHOLARSHIP GRANTS TO PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS AND THEIR CHILDREN
SHORT TITLE : "Scholarship Grants to Public School Teachers and their Children Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill provides public school teachers in active service and tbeir legitimate children free education in the elementary, high school and college levels in any public school and state university and college.
