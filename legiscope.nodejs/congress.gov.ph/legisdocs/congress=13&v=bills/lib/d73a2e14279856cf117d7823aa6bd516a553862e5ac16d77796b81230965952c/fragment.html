House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03831
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE PENALTIES FOR THE USE AND OPERATION OF ANY VEHICLE AS COMMON CARRIER WITHOUT A CERTIFICATE OF PUBLIC CONVENIENCE ISSUED IN THE NAME OF ITS USER OR OPERATOR, DECLARING AS PUNISHABLE SOME ACTS SIMILAR OR RELATED TO SUCH OPERATION, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS OF THE PUBLIC SERVICE ACT AND THE LAND TRANSPORTATION AND TRAFFIC CODE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The Operation of Common Carriers Law of 2005
ABSTRACT : "The Operation of Common Carriers Law of 2005." The bill seeks to penalize grantees of certificates of public conveyance who allow, cause or tolerate another person to operate as a common carrier motor vehicles not owned, borrowed or leased by the grantee.
