HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04359
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CRIMINALIZING THE FAILURE OF OWNERS AND LESSORS OF RESIDENTIAL AND COMMERCIAL BUILDINGS TO COMPLY WITH NATIONAL AND LOCAL FIRE SAFETY LAWS
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to hold the owners and lessors of residential and commercial buildings primarily and criminally liable for fire that may cause death and damage to persons and properties resulting from their non-compliance with national and local fire safety laws.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ROBERT "DODOT" JAWORSKI, JR.
