HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02842
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING THE SALE OR IMPORTATION OF COMPUTER HARDWARE AND SOFTWARE FROM THE PAYMENT OF VALUE ADDED TAX AND DONATIONS THEREOF FROM DONOR'S TAX, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CERTAIN SECTIONS OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : In order to lower the price of computer hardware and software, the bill proposes to exempt their sale or importation from the ambit of value-added tax. The measure also exempts computer donations from the payment of donor's tax.
