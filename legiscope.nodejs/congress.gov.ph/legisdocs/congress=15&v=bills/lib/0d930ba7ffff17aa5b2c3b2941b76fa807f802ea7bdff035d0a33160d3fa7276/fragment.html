HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00230
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING FREE HOSPITALIZATION BENEFITS IN GOVERNMENT HOSPITALS TO ALL RETIRED PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS WHO HAVE RENDERED A MINIMUM OF TWENTY (20) YEARS IN SERVICE AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : Seeks to provide all retired government teachers who have rendered a minimum of twenty (20) years of service free hospitalization benefits in all government hospitals, in recognition of their significant role in nation building and the country's socio-economic progress and in molding the youth to become responsible and productive members of society.
