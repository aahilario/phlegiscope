HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02722
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE AUTOMATIC DEDUCTION OF 30% FROM EVERY TAXPAYER'S PAYMENT FOR FOREIGN DEBT SERVICING AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : To prevent the ballooning of our foreign debts, the bill authorizes the earmarking of 30% of the tax payments to pay our foreign debts.
