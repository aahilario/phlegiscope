House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00971
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING A LAND USE POLICY, PROVIDING THE IMPLEMENTING MECHANISMS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "National Land Use Act of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : "National Land Use Act of the Philippines". The bill provides the guidelines and criteria for land use based on the assessment of the development needs of various sectors especially the basic sectors who are bound to be displaced as a result of inappropriate or destructive land uses.
