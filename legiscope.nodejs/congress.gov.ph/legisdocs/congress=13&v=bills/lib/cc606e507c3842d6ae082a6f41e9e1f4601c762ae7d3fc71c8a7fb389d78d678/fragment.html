HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02159
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE THE PHILIPPINE CHARITY SWEEPSTAKES OFFICE AND THE PHILIPPPINE AMUSEMENT AND GAMING CORPORATION TO CREATE A MECHANISM AND PROVIDE FUNDS FOR THE IMMEDIATE RESETTLEMENT OF MIGRANT URBAN POOR IN METROPOLITAN CITIES TO THE COUNTRYSIDE OF THEIR ORIGIN
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office and the Philippine Gaming Corporation, with the assistance of the concerned government agencies and the local government units, to establish a mechanism and provide funds for the resettlement of the urban poor to their barangay of origin.
