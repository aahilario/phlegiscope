HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02919
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE TOTAL INDEMNITY CLAIM FOR DEATH OR SERIOUS PHYSICAL INJURY TO ANY PASSENGER OR THIRD PARTY WITHOUT THE NECESSITY OF PROVING FAULT OR NEGLIGENCE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 378 OF THE INSURANCE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill increases from P5,000 to P50,000 the total indemnity claim for death or serious physical injury to any passenger or 3rd party without the necessity of proving fault or negligence.
