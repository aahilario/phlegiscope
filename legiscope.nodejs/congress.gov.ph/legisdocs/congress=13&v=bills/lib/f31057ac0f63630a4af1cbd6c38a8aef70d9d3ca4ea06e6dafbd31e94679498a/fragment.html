HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02108
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING THAT ONLY PROOF OF OWNERSHIP SHALL BE REQUIRED IN SHIPPING ANY MOTOR VEHICLE, MOTOR VEHICLE ENGINE, ENGINE BLOCK, CHASSIS, OR BODY FROM ONE ISLAND TO ANOTHER, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTY-FIVE HUNDRED AND THIRTY-NINE, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "ANTI-CARNAPPING ACT OF 1972"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to eliminate the Philippine National Police clearance requirement for transporting a motor vehicle, motor engine, engine block, chassis or body from one island to another.
