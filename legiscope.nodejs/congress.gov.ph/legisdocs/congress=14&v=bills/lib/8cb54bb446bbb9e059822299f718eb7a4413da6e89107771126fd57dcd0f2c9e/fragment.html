House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00659
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 97 OF REPUBLIC ACT 8550 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE FISHERIES CODE OF 1998
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to prohibit the fishing, catching, gathering, sale, purchase, possession, transport and exportation of rare, threatened or endangered species listed in Appendix I of the CITES (Convention on the International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora).
