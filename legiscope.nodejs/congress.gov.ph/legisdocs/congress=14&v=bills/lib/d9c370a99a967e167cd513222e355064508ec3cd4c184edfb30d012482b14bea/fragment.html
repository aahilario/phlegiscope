House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00515
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AGENCY, DEFINING ITS POWERS, FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES; APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "National Environmental Protection Act"
ABSTRACT : "National Environmental Protection Act". The bill provides for the creation of the National Environmental Protection Agency that shall strictly enforce environmental laws and cause the prosecution of violators of our environmental laws to ensure the protection of our environment and our people.
