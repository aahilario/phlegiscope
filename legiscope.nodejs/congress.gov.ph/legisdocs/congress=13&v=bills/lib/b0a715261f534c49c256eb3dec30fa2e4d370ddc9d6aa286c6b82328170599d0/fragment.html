HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02896
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING THE MEMBERS OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES, PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE, BUREAU OF JAIL MANAGEMENT AND PENOLOGY, AND BUREAU OF FIRE PROTECTION A TWENTY PERCENT (20%) DISCOUNT ON PURCHASES OF FOOD, TRANSPORTATION AND MEDICINE, PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR A VIOLATION THEREOF AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill extends a 20% discount to all members of the uniformed services for purchases of food and medicines and for transportation provided they are members in good standing.
