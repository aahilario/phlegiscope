HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00397
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING THE STATUTORY PROVISION AUTHORIZING THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES TO MAKE NECESSARY ADJUSTMENT IN THE INTERNAL REVENUE ALLOTMENT OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT INCURS AN UNMANAGEABLE PUBLIC SECTOR DEFICIT, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE SECTION 284 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to protect the internal revenue allotment share of the local government units from funding restrictions or holdback in the event that the national government incurs an unmanageable public sector deficit. Any funding restriction or holdback imposed by the President will mean restraining the freedom of the local government units to operate and respond to the needs of their constituents.
