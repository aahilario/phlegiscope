HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02713
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROVIDE ASSISTANCE TO THE IMMEDIATE FAMILY OF A SOLDIER KILLED WHILE PERFORMING DUTIES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Victim's Family Assistance Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The measure grants the immediate family of a soldier killed while performing his duties the following assistance: a) funeral assistance; b) death benefit; c) living assistance; and d) dependent undergraduate education assistance.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.
DATE FILED : 2007-09-26
