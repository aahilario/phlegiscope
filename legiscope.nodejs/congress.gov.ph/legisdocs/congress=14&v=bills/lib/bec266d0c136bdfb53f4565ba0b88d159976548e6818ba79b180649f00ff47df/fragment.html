HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01164
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING A FEEDBACK MECHANISM IN THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES (AFP) AND THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE (PNP), CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE THE AFP/PNP WELFARE AND AFFAIRS OFFICE, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill institutionalizes a feedback mechanism in the Armed Forces of the Philippines and the Philippine National Police through the creation of the AFP/PNP Welfare and Affairs Office that shall serve as the channel for legitimate grievances and concerns of both the military and police personnel.
