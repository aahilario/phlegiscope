House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01255
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7898 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'AFP MODERNIZATION ACT'
ABSTRACT : This bill amends R.A. 7898 to attain the following, among others: 1) fix the annual appropriation for the AFP Modernization Act in an amount not less than 0.3% of the last officially reported gross national product; 2) establish a research and development program to upgrade existing AFP inventory of equipment and weapon systems; 3) expand the sources of funding for the AFP Modernization Act Trust Fund to include the proceeds from the lease and/or joint venture agreements and such other dispositions pertaining to military camps; 4) adopt a Defense Acquisition System which shall be separate and distinct from the procurement system applicable to other departments of government; and 5) create the office of the Undersecretary in the Department of National Defense for Resource Management and Capability Development.
