HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03090
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING SCHOLARSHIP GRANTS TO MEMBERS OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE (PNP) AND THEIR CHILDREN
SHORT TITLE : "Scholarship Grants for Members of the Philippine National Police (PNP) and their Children Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill provides active members of the PNP and their legitimate children free education in the elementary, high school and college levels in any public school and state university and college.
