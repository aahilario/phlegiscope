House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01844
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 20 AND 31, TITLE IV OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 957 REGULATING THE SALE OF SUBDIVISION LOTS, CONDOMINIUMS, PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF
ABSTRACT : Among others, this bill: 1) prohibits the disposition of any subdivision or condominium project until all the necessary improvements and infrastructure have been completed; 2) declares ipso facto as local government property abandoned roads in subdivisions that have been in existence for not less than ten (10) years.
