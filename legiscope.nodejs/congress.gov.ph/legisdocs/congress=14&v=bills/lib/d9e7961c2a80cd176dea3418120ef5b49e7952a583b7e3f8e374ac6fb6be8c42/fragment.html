FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING SHIPPING VESSELS COASTING PHILIPPINE WATERS FOR THE PROTECTION OF SHORES
SHORT TITLE : "Shore Protection Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits all vessels from transporting municipal or commercial wastes in coastal waters without a permit from the Secretary of the Environment and Natural Resources.
