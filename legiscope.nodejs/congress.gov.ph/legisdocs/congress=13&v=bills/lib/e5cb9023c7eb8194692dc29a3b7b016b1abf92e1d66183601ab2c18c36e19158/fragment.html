House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04081
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO SPUR THE PLANTING OF A BILLION TREES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "billion Trees Act Of 2005."
ABSTRACT : "Billion Trees Act of 2005". The bill allocates at least one million hectares of open and denuded forest lands, private lands and idle portions of alienable and disposable lands for planting with forest and fruit tree species in the first five years of implementation of the program.
