HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04387
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE REGULATION OF TUTION FEE INCREASES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE BATAS PAMBANSA BILANG 232 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "EDUCATION ACT OF 1982", AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill allows all private higher educational institutions to impose a fixed rate of allowable increase in their tuition and miscellaneous fees as determined and requlated by the Department of Education and the Commission on Higher Education provided that no increase shall be allowed for two consecutive school years.
