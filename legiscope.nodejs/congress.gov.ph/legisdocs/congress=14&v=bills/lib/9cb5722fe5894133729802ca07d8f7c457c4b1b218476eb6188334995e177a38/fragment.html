HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01627
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING TO RESIDENTS OF THE REGION WHERE A GEOTHERMAL ENERGY PLANT IS LOCATED A THIRTY PERCENT (30%) DISCOUNT ON THEIR POWER RATE CONSUMPTION
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to grant the residents in the region where a geothermal plant is located a 30% discount on their electric consumption.
