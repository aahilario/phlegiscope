House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00875
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE REGULATORY CAPACITY OF THE BUREAU OF FOOD AND DRUGS (BFAD) BY ESTABLISHING ADEQUATE TESTING LABORATORIES AND FIELD OFFICES, UPGRADING ITS EQUIPMENT, AUGMENTING ITS HUMAN RESOURCE COMPLEMENT, GIVING AUTHORITY TO RETAIN ITS INCOME AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES AMENDING CERTAIN SECTIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT 3720, AS AMENDED AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : "Bureau of Food and Drugs Empowerment and Strengthening Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Bureau of Food and Drugs Empowerment and Strengthening Act of 2005". This bill strengthens the Bureau of Food and Drugs (BFAD) by upgrading its equipment, creating a drug enfocement unit (DEU) under its operational and administrative control and by establishing adequate testing laboratories and field offices in all provinces and cities throughout the country.
