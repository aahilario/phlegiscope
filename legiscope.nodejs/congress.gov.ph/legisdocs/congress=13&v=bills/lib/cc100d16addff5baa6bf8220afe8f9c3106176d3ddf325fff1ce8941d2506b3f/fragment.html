House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02507
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MODERNIZATION OF THE PHILIPPINE ATMOSPHERIC GEOPHYSICAL AND ASTRONOMICAL SERVICES ADMINISTRATION (PAGASA) AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : PAG-ASA Modernization Act
ABSTRACT : "PAG-ASA Modernization Act." The bill proposes the adoption of a four-pronged approach for the modernization of the Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical and Astronomical Services Administration, to wit: a) modernization of physical resources and operational techniques; b) intensification of human resource development; c) upgrading of research and development capability; and d) establishment of social weather service centers in strategic cities.
