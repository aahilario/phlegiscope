House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01385
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN AND PROPAGATE FOSTER CARE FOR ABANDONED, NEGLECTED AND OTHER CHILDREN WITH SPECIAL NEEDS, PROVIDING APPROPRIATIONS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Foster Care Act of 2001
ABSTRACT : "Foster Care Act of 2001." The proposed act provides for a comprehensive legal farmework under which abused, neglected, surrendered, dependent and abandoned children can be provided with an alternative family. It seeks to systematize and enhance foster care in the country.
