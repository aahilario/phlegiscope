HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02126
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROTECTING THE FILIPINO CHILDREN FROM MALNUTRITION INSTITUTIONALIZING FOR THIS PURPOSE 'THE MILK FEEDING PROGRAM', APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Milk Feeding Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Milk Feeding Act of 2007". The bill proposes the distribution of locally produced fresh milk for direct intake of children ages 5-10 years of age nationwide, to be taken from the produce of local milk suppliers and sponsors from the government and private sectors.
