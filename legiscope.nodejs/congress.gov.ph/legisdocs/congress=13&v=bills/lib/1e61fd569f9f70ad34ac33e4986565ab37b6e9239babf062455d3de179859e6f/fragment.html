HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB06096
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE EXTENSION OF EXEMPTION FROM INCOME TAX ON INCOME DERIVED FROM PHILIPPINE OVERSEAS SHIPPING, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7471, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE PHILIPPINE OVERSEAS SHIPPING DEVELOPMENT ACT
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to exempt Philippine shipping enterprises from the payment of income tax on income derived from overseas shipping in order to strengthen our overseas shipping industry.
