House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01963
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CURTAILING THE ILLEGAL ACTIVITIES OF RACKETEERS AND POWERFUL SYNDICATES IN THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : Racketeer Influenced and Corrupt Organization (RICO) Act
ABSTRACT : "Racketeer Influenced and Corrupt Organization (RICO) Act." The bill imposes stiff penalties against criminal syndicates such as drug traffickers, money launderers, smugglers, kidnappers, etc., depriving them of their operational fund and denying them the money obtained from their illegal acts by forfeiting it in favor of the government.
