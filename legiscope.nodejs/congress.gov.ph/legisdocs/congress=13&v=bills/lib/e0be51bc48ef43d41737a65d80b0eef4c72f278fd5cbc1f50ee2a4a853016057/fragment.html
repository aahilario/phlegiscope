HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02133
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN SECTIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SEVENTY EIGHT THIRTY TWO, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'ANTI-ELECTRICITY AND ELECTRIC TRANSMISSION LINES/MATERIALS PILFERAGE ACT OF 1994'
ABSTRACT : The bill grants a monetary reward ranging from P5,000 to P50,000 to any person who reports to the proper authorities theft of electric power transmission lines/materials. It also increases the penalty for violations of Section 3 of RA 7882 to an amount ranging from P150,000 to P500,000.
