HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04217
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING COLLECTION OF ANY FEE FOR THE USE OF PARKING SPACES WITHIN THE BUILDING OF ANY BUSINESS ESTABLISHMENT AND PRESCRIBING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits the charging of any fee for the use of parking spaces in shopping malls, hotels, hospitals and similar commercial establishments.
