HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00793
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ACCORD GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES A COMMUTABLE FIVE WORKING DAYS LEAVE OF ABSENCE, WITH PAY, IN ADDITION TO EXISTING LEAVE BENEFITS GRANTED TO THEM, IN CASE ANY MEMBER OF HIS/HER FAMILY DIES
ABSTRACT : To give government employees adequate time to take care of the needs attendant to a death in the family, the bill grants a commutable 5-working day leave of absence, with pay in addition to existing leave benefits, in case an immediate relative such as a parent, spouse, brother, sister and child passes away.
