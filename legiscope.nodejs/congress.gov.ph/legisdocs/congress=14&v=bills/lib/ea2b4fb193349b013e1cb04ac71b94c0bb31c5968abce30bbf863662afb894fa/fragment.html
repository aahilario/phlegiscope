HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02743
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR AUTOMATIC MEMBERSHIP OF PROFESSIONALS IN THEIR RESPECTIVE ACCREDITED PROFESSIONAL ORGANIZATIONS
SHORT TITLE : "Automatic Professional Membership Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the accreditation by the Professional Regulation Commission of one professional organization for each profession that shall serve as the umbrella organization for all professionals in their respective professions.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SANTIAGO, NARCISO III D.
