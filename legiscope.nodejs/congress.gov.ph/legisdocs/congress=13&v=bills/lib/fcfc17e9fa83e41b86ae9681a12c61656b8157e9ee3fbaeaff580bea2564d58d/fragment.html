HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02290
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ABOLISHING THE DEATH PENALTY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill states that the death penalty law symbolizes the retributive justice that is adopted by the penal system and violates the Constitutional provision of cruel and excessive punishment. It further states that there is no clear indication, so far, that its imposition has deterred the continued commission of heinous crimes.
