House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01982
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 32 (h) OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 4136 HEREBY REQUIRING THE MANDATORY COMPLIANCE BY ALL MOTORCYCLES TO AUTOMATICALLY TURN ON AND RIDE WITH THEIR HEADLIGHTS ON AT ALL HOURS OF THE DAY ON ALL ROADS
SHORT TITLE : "Automatic Headlights On for Motorcycles Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Automatic Headlights On for Motorcycles Act of 2007". The measure requires all motorcycles to run or ride on all roads, as well as expressways, highways or limited access facilities with the headlights turned on at all hours of the day.
