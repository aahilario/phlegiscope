House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01410
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING THE MAGNA CARTA FOR THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE UNIFORMED AND NON-UNIFORMED PERSONNEL AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Magna Carta for the Philippine National Police Uniformed and Non-uniformed Personnel.
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta for the Philippine National Police Uniformed and Non-uniformed Personnel." The bill provides for the PNP organization, structure, prerequisite to entry, salaries, leave benefits, retirement, disciplinary measures and disability and death benefits.
