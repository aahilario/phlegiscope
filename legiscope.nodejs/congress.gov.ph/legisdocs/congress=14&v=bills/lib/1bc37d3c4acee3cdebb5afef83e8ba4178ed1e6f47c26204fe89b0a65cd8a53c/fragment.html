House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01492
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING CIVIL SERVICE SUB-PROFESSIONAL ELIGIBILITY TO ALL COTERMINUS, CONTRACTUAL AND CASUAL EMPLOYEES, WHO HAVE RENDERED NINE (9) STRAIGHT YEARS OF SERVICES
SHORT TITLE : "Civil Service Sub-Professional Eligibility for Non-Permanent Government Employees Act"
ABSTRACT : "Civil Service Sub-Professional Eligibility for Non-Permanent Government Employees Act". The measure confers sub-professional civil service eligibility to non-permanent government employees who have rendered at least nine (9) years of service.
