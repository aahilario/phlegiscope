House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01350
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE MANDATORY LABELLING OF FOOD AND FOOD PRODUCTS CONTAINING GENETICALLY-MODIFIED ORGANISMS AND FOOD PRODUCED BY GENETIC ENGINEERING TECHNOLOGIES, AMENDING FOR THAT PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THREE THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED AND TWENTY, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE FOOD, DRUGS AND DRUG DEVICES AND COSMETICS ACT, AND REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SEVEN THOUSAND THREE HUNDRED AND NINETY-FOUR, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CONSUMERS' ACT OF THE PHILIPPINES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Genetically Engineered Food Right to Know Act
ABSTRACT : Genetically Engineered Food Right to Know Act." Mandates all food and food products domestically sold, whether manufactured locally or imported, and which contain genetically modified organisms including those produced by genetic engineering technologies, to indicate such in the labels of their packaging followed by a government notice on the same.
