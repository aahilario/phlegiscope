HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00099
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE EXEMPTION OF FIXED INCOME EARNERS FROM THE FILING OF INCOME TAX RETURNS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 51(A)(2)(b) OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8424, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE TAX REFORM ACT OF 1997
ABSTRACT : Since employers already withhold the income taxes of fixed income earners, the bill seeks to exempt fixed income earners from filing income tax returns. The exemption of fixed income earners would help the Bureau of Internal Revenue meet its objective of simplifying and streamlining the country's entire tax collection system.
