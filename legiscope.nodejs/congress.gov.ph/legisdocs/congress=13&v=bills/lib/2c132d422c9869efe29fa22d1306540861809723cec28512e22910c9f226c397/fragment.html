House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04604
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PHILIPPINE NAVAL ACADEMY (PNA) AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to establish a naval academy to be known as the Philippine Naval Academy that will educate and prepare cadets to become naval officers of the Philippine Navy.
