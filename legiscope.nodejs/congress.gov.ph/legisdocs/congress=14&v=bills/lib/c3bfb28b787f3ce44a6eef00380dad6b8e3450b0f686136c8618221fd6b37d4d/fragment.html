FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MAGNA CARTA FOR WOMEN
SHORT TITLE : "The Magna Carta for Women"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to address the wide-ranging needs and sectoral concerns of women, specially those in the marginalized sector. It concretizes women's participation and empowerment in all aspects of the family, community and society.
