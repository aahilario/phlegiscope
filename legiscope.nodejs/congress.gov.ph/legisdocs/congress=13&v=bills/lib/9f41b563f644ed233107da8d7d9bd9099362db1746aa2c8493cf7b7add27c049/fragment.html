HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00449
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE PENSION PAY OF FILIPINO AMATEUR ATHLETES WHO HAVE WON MEDALS IN THE OLYMPIC GAMES AND PROFESSIONAL FILIPINO ATHLETES WHO HAVE WON WORLD TITLES OR CHAMPIONSHIPS IN DULY RECOGNIZED AND SANCTIONED PROFESSIONAL INTERNATIONAL SPORTING COMPETITIONS
ABSTRACT : Too often, we hear stories of Filipino former star athletes who brought honor and glory to the country only to succumb to poverty and obscurity in their old age. To duly recognize the sacrifices and efforts of old Filipino sports icons, this bill proposes the grant of a monthly pension to all Filipino athletes who, in the past, copped medals in the Olympic Games and won world titles or championships in duly recognized international professional sports competition.
