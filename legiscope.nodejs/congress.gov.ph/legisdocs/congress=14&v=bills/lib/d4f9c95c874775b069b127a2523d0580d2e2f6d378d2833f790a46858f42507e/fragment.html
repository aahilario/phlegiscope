House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00263
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN AND PROPAGATE FOSTER CARE FOR ABANDONED AND NEGLECTED CHILDREN AND OTHER CHILDREN WITH SPECIAL NEEDS, PROVIDING APPROPRIATIONS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Foster CAre Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Foster CAre Act of 2007". the bill seeks to systematize and enhance foster care in the country. It aims to provide every abused, neglected, surrendred, dependent and abandoned child with an alternative family.
