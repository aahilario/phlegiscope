House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04803
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6552, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REALTY INSTALLMENT BUYER ACT, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes, among others, to remove the obligation of the seller to refund to the buyer the cash surrender value of the total payments he has made whether or not he has paid an equivalent of two (2) years installments.
