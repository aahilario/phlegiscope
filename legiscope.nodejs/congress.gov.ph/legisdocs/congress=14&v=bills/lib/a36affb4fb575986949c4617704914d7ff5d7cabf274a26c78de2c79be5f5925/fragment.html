House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01767
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7941, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "PARTY-LIST SYSTEM ACT"
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes to a) raise the ceiling for party-list representatives in the House of Representatives from three (3) seats to six (6) seats, b) effectively lower the 2% threshold by introducing a methematically determined floating threshold, and c) craft a more proportional formula for the allocation of additional seats.
