HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04935
FULL TITLE : AN ACT UPGRADING THE MINIMUM SALARY GRADE LEVEL OF PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS IN THE ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY LEVELS FROM GRADE 10 TO GRADE 19, GRANTING ADDITIONAL LONGEVITY PAY ADJUSTMENT EVERY FIVE (5) YEARS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes to upgrade the salary grade of elementary and secondary public school teachers from grade 10 to grade 19. It also seeks to increase their longevity pay benefit by 10% of their basic pay every five (5) years of service.
