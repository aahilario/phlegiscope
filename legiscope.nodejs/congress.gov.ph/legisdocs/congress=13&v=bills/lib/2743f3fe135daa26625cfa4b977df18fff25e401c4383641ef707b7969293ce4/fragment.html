HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03222
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPOSING A TAX ON MOBILE CARRIERS FOR EVERY TEXT ENTRIES ON ALL TEXT GAMES, SPECIFICALLY ON TEXT POLLS, SURVEYS, BRIGADES AND OTHER GAMES OF CHANCE THROUGH TEXT MESSAGES USING CELLULAR PHONES OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE TEXT GAMBLING TAX OF 2004"
SHORT TITLE : The Text Gambling Tax of 2004
ABSTRACT : The Text Gambling Tax of 2004. The bill imposes on mobile carrier/cellular companies a tax on all text entries to text games. The tax, however, shall not be imposed on regular text messages.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : MAYO Z. ALMARIO
DATE FILED : 2004-10-28
