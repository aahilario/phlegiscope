HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00973
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REMOVING RESTRICTIONS ON THE TRANSFER AND OTHER CONVEYANCES OF TITLES ACQUIRED THROUGH FREE PATENT, AMENDING COMMONWEALTH ACT NO. 141, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE PUBLIC LAND ACT, AND OTHER PERTINENT LAWS, AND OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to update the obsolete provisions of the Public Land Act and facilitate the mass titling of public alienable and disposable lands in the Philippines. Among others, it provides for a) the reduction of the period of cultivation and occupation from 30 years to 10 years; b) the issuance of free patents without payment of oustanding taxes; c) extension of free patents to residential, commercial and industrial lands.
