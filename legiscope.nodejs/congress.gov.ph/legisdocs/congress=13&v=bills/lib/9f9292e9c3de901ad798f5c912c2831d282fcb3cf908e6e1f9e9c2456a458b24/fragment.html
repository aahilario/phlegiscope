HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00164
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING AS NATIONAL POLICY THE FREEDOM OF THE FILIPINO FROM ONEROUS, UNREASONABLE, AND UNCONSCIONABLE DEBT OR OBLIGATION, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 31 (b) OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1177 AND SIMILAR LAWS, DECREES, EXECUTIVE AND ADMINISTRATIVE ORDERS, AND CIRCULARS
ABSTRACT : The Filipinos have suffered for long from the burdens of onerous, unreasonable and unconscionable debts and obligation which have eaten up as much as thirty to forty percent of the annual budget as a result of the automatic appropriation provision under Section 31 (b) of PD 1117 and other similar laws, decrees, executive and administrative orders or circulars. This bill seeks to free the Filipino from the burdens of onerous and unreasonable debts by repealing the automatic appropriation provision under PD 1117.
