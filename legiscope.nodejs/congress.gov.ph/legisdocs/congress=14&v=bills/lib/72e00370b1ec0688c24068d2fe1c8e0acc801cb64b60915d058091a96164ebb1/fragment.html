House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03265
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING HIGHER PENALTIES FOR THE CRIME OF ROBBERY AND THEFT INVOLVING CELLULAR PHONES AND OTHER PORTABLE TELECOMMUNICATION DEVICES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Robbery and Theft Involving Cellular Phones and Other Portable Telecommunication Devices Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes a penalty of six (6) years imprisonment on any person convicted of the crime of robbery and theft of cellur phones and other portable telecommunication device.
