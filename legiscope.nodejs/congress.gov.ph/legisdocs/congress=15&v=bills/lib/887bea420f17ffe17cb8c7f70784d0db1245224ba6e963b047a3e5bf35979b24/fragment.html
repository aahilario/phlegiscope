House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00022
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPLEMENTING THE RIGHT OF ACCESS TO INFORMATION ON MATTERS OF PUBLIC CONCERN GUARANTEED UNDER SECTION TWENTY-EIGHT, ARTICLE II AND SECTION SEVEN, ARTICLE III OF THE 1987 CONSTITUTION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Freedom Of Information Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Freedom of Information Act of 2010.” Mandates for the electronic/digital publication of government documents considered as matters of public concern for the purpose of facilitating a more efficient communication between the people and government agencies and thus making easy access to information for students through the internet without having to go through all the red tape associated with government.
