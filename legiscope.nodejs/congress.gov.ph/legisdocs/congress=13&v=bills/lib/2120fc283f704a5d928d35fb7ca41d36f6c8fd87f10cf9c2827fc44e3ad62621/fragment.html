House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03242
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PRE-NEED PLAN CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : Pre-Need Plan Code of the Philippines
ABSTRACT : "Pre-Need Plan Code of the Philippines." The bill provides the regulatory framework to ensure the profitability and stability of the pre-need industry while providing enough safeguards for the Filipino family. It transfers the regulation of pre-need companies to the Insurance Commission.
