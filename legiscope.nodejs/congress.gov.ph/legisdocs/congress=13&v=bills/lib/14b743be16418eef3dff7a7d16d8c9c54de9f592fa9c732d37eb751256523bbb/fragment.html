House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04540
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING THE ADMINISTRATIVE DISCIPLINARY MACHINERY OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "PNP Administrative Disciplinary Machinery Act"
ABSTRACT : "PNP Administrative Disciplinary Machinery Act". The measure proposes the creation of the People's Law Enforcement Board that shall hear and adjudicate all citizens complaints filed against any uniformed member of the Philippine National Police (PNP). It concretely defines the exclusive administrative disciplinary machinery of the uniformed members of the PNP.
