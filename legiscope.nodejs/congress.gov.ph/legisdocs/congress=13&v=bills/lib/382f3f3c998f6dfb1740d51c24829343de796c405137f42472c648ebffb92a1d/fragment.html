HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01292
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REAPPORTIONING THE LEGISLATIVE DISTRICTS THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY AMONG PROVINCES AND CITIES
ABSTRACT : This bill envisions to achieve equality of representation, a basic principle of republicanism, through the reapportionment of districts which, following the constitutional requirement, shall, as far as practicable, be comprised of contiguous, compact and adjacent territories.
