HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00637
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING "FAKE DRUGS" (MEDICINES) AS DANGEROUS DRUGS AND ITS IMPORTATION, MANUFACTURE, SALE, ADMINISTRATION, DISPENSATION, DELIVERY AND DISTRIBUTION BE CLASSIFIED AS HEINOUS CRIME PUNISHABLE BY DEATH, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6425, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE DANGEROUS DRUGS ACT OF 1972, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to classify "fake drugs" as dangerous drugs punishable under RA 6425 (Dangerous Drugs Act of 1972). It also seeks to classify its importation, sale, administration, dispensation, delivery and distribution as a heinous crime punishable by reclusion perpetua to death.
