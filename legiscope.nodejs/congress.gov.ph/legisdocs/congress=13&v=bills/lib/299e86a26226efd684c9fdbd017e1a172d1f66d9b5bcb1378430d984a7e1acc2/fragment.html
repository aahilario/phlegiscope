HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00636
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR REASONABLE COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS FOR THE LOSS OF LIVES, INJURIES AND DAMAGES TO PROPERTY SUFFERED BY NON-COMBATANT INDIVIDUALS IN THE COURSE OF OPERATIONS BY THE MILITARY, POLICE AND/OR OTHER LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill compensates non-combatant victims of military and police operations, as follows:a) if the victim dies - PhP50,000.00 to his legal heirs; b) for physical, emotional and psychological injury - cost of hospitalization; and c) for loss or damage to property - assessed value of the lost or destroyed property.
