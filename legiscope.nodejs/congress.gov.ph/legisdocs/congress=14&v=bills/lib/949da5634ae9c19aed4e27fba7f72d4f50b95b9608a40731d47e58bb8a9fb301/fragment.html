House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01316
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOWING THE OMBUDSMAN TO EXAMINE ALL THE BANK DEPOSITS OF PUBLIC OFFICERS WITH THE LATTER'S WRITTEN CONSENT BY AMENDING SECTION 8 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6713, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'CODE OF CONDUCT AND ETHICAL STANDARDS FOR PUBLIC OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES'
ABSTRACT : The bill allows the Ombudsman to examine bank accounts and inquire into all investments and deposits of public officials, their spouses and children under the age of 18.
