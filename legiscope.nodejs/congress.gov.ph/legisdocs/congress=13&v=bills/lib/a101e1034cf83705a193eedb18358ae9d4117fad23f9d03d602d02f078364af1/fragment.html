House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01625
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ESTABLISH CERTAIN LIMITATIONS ON ADVERTISING RELATING TO CIGARETTES AND TOBACCO PRODUCTS
SHORT TITLE : Tobacco Products Control Act
ABSTRACT : "Tobacco Products Control Act." The bill seeks to impose certain restrictions in the advertisement of cigarettes and other tobacco products using billboards and periodicals in stadia and arenas, on trains, buses, train stations and terminals, in motion pictures, in video games and in family amusement centers.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : BARINAGA, ROSELLER L.
