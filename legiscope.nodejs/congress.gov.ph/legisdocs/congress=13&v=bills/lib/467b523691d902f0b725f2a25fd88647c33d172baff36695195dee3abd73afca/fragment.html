House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01933
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING ANY PERSON WHO SELLS ANY REAL PROPERTY TO THE GOVERNMENT OR ANY OF ITS POLITICAL SUBDIVISIONS OR INSTRUMENTALITIES FROM THE PAYMENT OF CAPITAL GAINS TAX AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Aims to encourage private owners to sell their real properties to the government by exempting them from capital gains tax.
