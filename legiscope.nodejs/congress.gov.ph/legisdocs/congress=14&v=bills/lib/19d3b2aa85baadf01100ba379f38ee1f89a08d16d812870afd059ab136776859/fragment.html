House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00129
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING MECHANISMS FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A CHILDREN'S HEALTH INSURANCE PROGRAM, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7875, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS 'THE NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE ACT OF 1995', AS AMENDED BY R.A. 9241, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase and expand the scope and coverage of the National Health Insurance Program to cover children of low-income families who cannot afford to insure the health needs of their children.
