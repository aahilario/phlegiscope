HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00660
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING GOVERNMENT SERVICES AND INSURANCE SYSTEM (GSIS) AND NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE CORPORATION (NHIC) MEMBERSHIP TO ACCREDITED BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS AND BARANGAY TANODS, AND OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the membership of barangay health workers and members of the barangay tanod to the GSIS and NHIC with the national and provincial governments sharing in their monthly contributions on a 50-50 basis.
