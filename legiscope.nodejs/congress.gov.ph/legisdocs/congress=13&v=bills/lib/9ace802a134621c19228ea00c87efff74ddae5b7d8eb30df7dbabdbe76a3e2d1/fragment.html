House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02205
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING REFORMS IN LAND ADMINISTRATION
SHORT TITLE : Land Administration Reform Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Land Administration Reform Act of 2004." This bill envisions to optimize and rationalize the contribution of an institutionalized land administration reform to the goals national development, the eradication of poverty and the attainment of social, economic and cultural justice.
