House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05479
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISMENT OF DRUG REHABILITATION CENTER IN EVERY REGION OF THE COUNTRY AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to establish a drug rehabilitation center in every region under the supervision of the Department of Health. It prescribes the functions and organizational structure of the center.
