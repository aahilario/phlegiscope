HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00728
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE FIRST WORKING DAY OF EVERY YEAR, BEGINNING ON THE YEAR 2003, A NATIONAL DAY OF PEACE AND UNITY, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : To inculcate in the minds and hearts of every Filipino a desire for lasting peace and unity, this bill seeks to declare the first working day of every year as "National Day of Peace and Unity" and a special non-working holiday.
