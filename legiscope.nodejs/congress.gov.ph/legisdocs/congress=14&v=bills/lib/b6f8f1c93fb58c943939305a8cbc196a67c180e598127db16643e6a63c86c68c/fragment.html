HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00175
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING MAY 7 OF EVERY YEAR AS 'HEALTH WORKERS' DAY'
SHORT TITLE : "Health Workers' Day Act"
ABSTRACT : "Health Workers' Day Act". The bill seeks to establish May 7 of each year as "Health Workers' Day" to give due respect and appreciation to the services rendered by the main pillars of the country's health care system.
