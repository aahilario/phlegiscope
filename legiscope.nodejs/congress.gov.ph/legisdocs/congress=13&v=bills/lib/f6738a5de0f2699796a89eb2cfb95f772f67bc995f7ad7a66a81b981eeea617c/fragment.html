HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00873
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXPANDING THE LIABILITIES OF INDIRECT EMPLOYERS IN THEIR JOB CONTRACTING, COVERING OTHER BENEFITS DUE THEIR EMPLOYEES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLE 106 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : This bill directs the companies and employers that hire workers through job contractors to grant all the benefits due them. However, in cases where the workers are not able to receive the benefits due them, they can seek redress from the company they are working for.
