House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00269
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO FURTHER STRENGTHEN THE YOUTH DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM BY CREATING A LOCAL YOUTH DEVELOPMENT COUNCIL IN EVERY MUNICIPALITY, CITY AND PROVINCE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Local Youth Development Council Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Local Youth Development Council Act of 2007". The bill calls for the creation of a Local Youth Develoment Council in every municipality, city and province that shall be responsible for the formulation of policies and the implementation of youth development programs, projects and activities.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ANTONINO-CUSTODIO, DARLENE MAGNOLIA R.
