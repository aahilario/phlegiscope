House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00424
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING REAL PROPERTY AND TRANSFER TAX EXEMPTIONS TO REAL ESTATE DEVELOPERS, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE SECTIONS 135 AND 234 OF THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill exempts from real property and transfer taxes the donation of roads, alleys, sidewalks amd open spaces for parks, playgrounds and recreational facilities to local government units and homeowners associations of subdivision projects.
