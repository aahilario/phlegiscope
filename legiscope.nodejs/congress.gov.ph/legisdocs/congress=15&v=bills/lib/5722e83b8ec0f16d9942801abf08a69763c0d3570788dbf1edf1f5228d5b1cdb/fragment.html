House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00278
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING FROM TAXATION ALL DONATIONS, GRANTS OR GIFTS TO NON-STOCK AND NON-PROFIT EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS
ABSTRACT : Encourages the non-government sectors to fully support and provide the much needed relief to the non-profit and non-stock educational institutions by granting full tax exemption and tax credits to these willing benefactors.
