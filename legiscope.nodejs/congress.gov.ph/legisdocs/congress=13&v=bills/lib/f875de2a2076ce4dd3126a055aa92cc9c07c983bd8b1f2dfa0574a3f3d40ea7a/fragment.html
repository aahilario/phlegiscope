HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02999
FULL TITLE : AN ACT POSTPONING THE BARANGAY AND SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN ELECTIONS FROM OCTOBER 2005 TO THE SECOND WEEK OF MAY 2007 TO COINCIDE WITH THE LOCAL ELECTIONS
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the holding of the synchronized barangay and sanggunian kabataan elections on the 2nd Monday of May 2007 and every three years thereafter.
