House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05903
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REORGANIZING THE MOVIE AND TELEVISION REVIEW AND CLASSIFICATION BOARD, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Movie and Television Review and Classification Board or MTRCB Reorganization Act."
ABSTRACT : "The Movie and Television Review and Classification Board or MTRCB Reorganization Act". The bill defines in unambiguous terms the nomenclature, words and phrases commonly used in the classification of motion pictures and cinematographic works. It also seeks to effect the reorganization of the present MTRCB.
