HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00230
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN AND ENHANCE THE USE OF ENGLISH AS THE MEDIUM OF INSTRUCTION IN PHILIPPINE SCHOOLS
SHORT TITLE : "Strengthening and Enhancing the Use of English as the Medium of Instruction Act"
ABSTRACT : "Strengthening and Enhancing the Use of English as the Medium of Instruction Act". The bill mandates all public elementary and secondary schools to use English as the medium of instruction in all subjects except in Pilipino and other language subjects.
