          -Committee on Appropriations Director Julieta Apostol suggested that the implementation of the measure be done in phases given its complexity. She requested the DBM to recommend amendments albeit its unfavorable position such as (1) payment of separation pay to be paid either by local and national government, (2) reverting back the positions to the national government, (3) delineating shares of LGU and the NG in terms of funding, and (4) insert provision for an IRR. She further suggested that the term "personnel" on Section 2 of the bill be amended as "POSITIONS" to establish the fact that plantilla positions are the one to be reverted back not the warm bodies. The TWG also agreed that instead of creating an Agricultural and Fishery Review Panel, the existing COCOFM shall perform its function.
