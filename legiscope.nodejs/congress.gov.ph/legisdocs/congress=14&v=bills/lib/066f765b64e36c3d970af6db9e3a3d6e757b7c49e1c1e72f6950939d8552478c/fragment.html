HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01716
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMOTING THE DEVELOPMENT AND USE OF FREE/OPEN SOURCE SOFTWARE (FOSS) IN THE PHILIPPINES, AMENDING RA 8293 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES", PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "free/open Source (foss) Act Of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Free/Open Source (FOSS) Act of 2007". The bill mandates the use of FOSS in all government agencies and encourages the use and development of FOSS in the private and public sectors.
