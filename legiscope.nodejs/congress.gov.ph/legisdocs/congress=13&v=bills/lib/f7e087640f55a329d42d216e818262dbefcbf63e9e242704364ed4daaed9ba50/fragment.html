House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01421
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A DEPARTMENT OF FISHERIES AND AQUATIC RESOURCES, PROVIDING FOR ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Departmnet of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Department of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Act of 2004." The bill creates the Department to manage, develop, protect, conserve and utilize all fisheries and aquatic resources except for municipal waters which shall be under the local government. It prescribes the jurisdiction of the Department, its powers and functions, structural organization, functions of its officials and creation of bureaus.
