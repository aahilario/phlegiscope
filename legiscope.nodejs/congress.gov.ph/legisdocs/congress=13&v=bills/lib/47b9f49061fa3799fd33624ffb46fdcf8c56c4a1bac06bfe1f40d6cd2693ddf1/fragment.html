House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00249
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 463, CHAPTER 2, TITLE FOUR, BOOK III OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991 AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : Many of our forests have been turned into virual savannas due to illegal logging activities. Recent events have also proven that uncontrolled and unregulated cutting of logs is the major cause of flooding. To preserve our precious forest reserves and to avoid flooding, the bill seeks the mandatory, instead of optional, appointment of the Provincial Environment and Natural Resources Officer (PENRO) who shall ensure that the forest lands in every province are protected from wanton and illegal cutting of logs and that reforestations are undertaken in areas where needed.
