House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03455
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING AND EXPANDING THE BENEFITS GRANTED TO SENIOR CITIZENS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NOS. 7432 AND 9257, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the discount granted to senior citizens from 20% to 30% and to expand the grant of such benefit to include cost of electric and water services, payment of real estate taxes on family homes and payment of withholding tax on interest income from bank deposits.
