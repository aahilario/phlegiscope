HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05459
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE MINES AND GEO-SCIENCE BUREAU OF THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES (DENR) BY ENCHANCING ITS KNOW-HOW IN GEOLOGICAL SURVEY, PURPOSELY, TO IDENTIFY GEOLOGICAL POWERS TO MAKE KNWON ITS STUDY ON GEOLOGICAL HAZARDS THAT SURROUND THE PHILIPPINE ARCHIPELAGO
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Mines and Geo-Science Bureau of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources to pursue studies/surveys on geological hazards in the country and to immediately inform the concerned agencies and local government units on the implication of such danger to the community.
