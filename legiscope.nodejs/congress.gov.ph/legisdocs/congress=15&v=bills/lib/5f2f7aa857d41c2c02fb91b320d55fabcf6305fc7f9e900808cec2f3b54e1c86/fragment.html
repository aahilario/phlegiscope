ACTIONS TAKEN BY THE COMMITTEE
COMMITEE HEARINGS/ACTIONS:
      1. Under study by the Technical Working Group(TWG) on 2011-01-24
          -The SSS representatives were not convinced of granting the subsidy since it only covers the first 3 monthly SSS contribution of self-employed PWDs. They fear that after the firts 3 months, the PWDs will no longer continue their SSS contributions to their detriment. The PWD representatives on the other hand wanted a longer period of subsidy for their SSS contribution to ensure continuity.
          -A TWG was created to discuss House Bill Nos. 1685 and 3192, both referring to employment of PWDs.
