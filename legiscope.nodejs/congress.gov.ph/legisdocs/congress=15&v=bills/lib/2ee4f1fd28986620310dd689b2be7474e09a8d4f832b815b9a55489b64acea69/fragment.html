HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00130
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING TITLE VI OF E.O. No. 2009 AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE FAMILY CODE BY REMOVING AND/OR ERASING DISTINCTIONS BETWEEN LEGITIMATE, ILLEGITIMATE AND LEGITIMATED FILIATION, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Grants all children the following rights: 1) to bear the surname of the father and mother in conformity with the provisions of the Civil Code and special laws; 2) to receive support from their parents, their descendants and in proper cases, their brothers and sisters; and 3) to be entitled to legitimate and other successional rights without any distinction whatsoever.
