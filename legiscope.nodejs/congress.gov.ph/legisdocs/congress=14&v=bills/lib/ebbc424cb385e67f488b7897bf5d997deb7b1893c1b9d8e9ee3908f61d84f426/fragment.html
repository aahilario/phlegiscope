House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03323
REPUBLIC ACT NO. RA09499
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOWING FILIPINO WORLD WAR II VETERANS TO CONTINUE RECEIVING PHILIPPINE GOVERNMENT PENSIONS AND BENEFITS NOTWITHSTANDING SIMILAR PENSIONS AND BENEFITS PROVIDED BY THE UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT THEREBY AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6948, AS AMENDED
SHORT TITLE : "Filipino World War II Veterans Equity Act of 2007."
ABSTRACT : The bill allows Filipino World War II veterans and their surviving spouses and children to receive the benefits/pensions granted by the United States government notwithstanding similar benefits/pensions granted by the Philippine government.
