House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00811
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING TWENTY PERCENT (20%) DISCOUNT ON THE PURCHASE OF CELLCARDS BY A SENIOR CITIZENS WHOSE INCOME IS NOT MORE THAN ONE HUNDRED TWENTY THOUSAND PESOS (P120,000.00) PER ANNUM, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7432, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'LAW MAXIMIZING THE CONTRIBUTION OF SENIOR CITIZENS TO NATION BUILDING, GRANT BENEFITS AND SPECIAL PRIVILEGES'
ABSTRACT : This bill grants a twenty percent (20%) discount on the purchase of prepaid cellcards by a senior citizen whose income is not more than ONE HUNDRED TWENTY THOUSAND PESOS (P120,000.000.00) per annum, provided that the senior citizen shall purchase only one cellcard a month.
