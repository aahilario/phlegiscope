HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01683
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A COMPREHENSIVE PSYCHOLOGICAL AND SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM FOR CHILD SOLDIERS, CREATING A SPECIAL TASK FORCE ON CHILD SOLDIERS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Child Soldiers' Act of 2003
ABSTRACT : "Child Soldiers' Act of 2003". To lead them back to the mainstream of society and to enable them to live normal and productive lives, the bill provides for a rehabilitation and scholarship program to children involved in armed conflicts.
