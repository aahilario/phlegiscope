HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02702
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 9 AND 45 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9136, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE ELECTRIC POWER INDUSTRY REFORM ACT (EPIRA) OF 2001, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure allows TRANSCO to put transmission lines in missionary areas that can be connected to the grid or those already connected to TRANSCO's main inter-island cable in order to spur economic development in these areas.
