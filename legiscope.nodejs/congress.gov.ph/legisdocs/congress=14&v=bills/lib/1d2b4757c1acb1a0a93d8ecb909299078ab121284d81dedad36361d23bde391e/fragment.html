House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00979
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A DEPARTMENT OF MARITIME AFFAIRS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Department of Maritime Affairs Act of 2002"
ABSTRACT : "Department of Maritime Affairs Act of 2002". The bill creates the Department of Maritime Affairs which shall be tasked to recommend and implement the government's policies, plans and programs for the promotion of safety and development of ports, shipping, shipbuilding, seafaring and other activities in the maritime industry.
