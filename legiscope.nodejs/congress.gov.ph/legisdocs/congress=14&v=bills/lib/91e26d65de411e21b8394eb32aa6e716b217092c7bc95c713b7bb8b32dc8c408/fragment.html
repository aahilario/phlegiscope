HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02473
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE DEPARTMENT OF HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT, RATIONALIZING THE ORGANIZATION AND FUNCTIONS OF GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND CORPORATIONS RELATED TO HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Omnibus Housing and Urban Development Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to rationalize the shelter and urban development program of the government. It aims to streamline the bureaucracy and the process of formulating human settlement programs to meet the demand of our increasing population and decreasing space.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : BIAZON, ROZZANO RUFINO B.
