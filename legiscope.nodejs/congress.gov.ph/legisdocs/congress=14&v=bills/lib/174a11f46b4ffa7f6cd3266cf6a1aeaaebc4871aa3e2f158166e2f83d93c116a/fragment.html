House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01460
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PHILIPPINE EMERGENCY AND DISASTER MANAGEMENT AUTHORITY AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine Emergency and Disaster Management Authority Act of 2004"
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Emergency and Disaster Management Authority Act of 2004". The bill creates the Philippine Emergency and Disaster Management Authority that shall develop a comprehensive national emergency and disaster prevention and preparedness plan. It also provides for the establishment of emergency and disaster field office in every province.
