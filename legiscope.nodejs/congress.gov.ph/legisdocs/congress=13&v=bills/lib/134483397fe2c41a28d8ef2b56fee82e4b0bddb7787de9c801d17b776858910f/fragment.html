HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02881
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ENCOURAGE SAVINGS MOBILIZATION BY GRANTING TAX PRIVILEGES TO SMALL DEPOSITORS
ABSTRACT : The bill entitles depositors whose aggregate savings account do no exceed P500,000 per year, to apply the 20% final withholding tax paid on their interest income as a tax credit for their income tax liability or to file for a refund thereof in case there are no other tax liabilities.
