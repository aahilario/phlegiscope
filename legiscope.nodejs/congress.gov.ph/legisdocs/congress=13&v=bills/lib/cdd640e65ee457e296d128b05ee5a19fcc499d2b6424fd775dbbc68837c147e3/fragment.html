House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01928
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A PROVIDENT PERSONAL SAVINGS PLAN KNOWN AS THE PERSONAL EQUITY AND RETIREMENT ACCOUNT (PERA)
SHORT TITLE : Personal Equity and Retirement Account (PERA)
ABSTRACT : "Personal Equity and Retirement Account (PERA)." The bill defines PERA as a "voluntary account by and for the contributor, a long-term savings for his retirement." Aims to provide retirement plans to nonmembers of GSIS, SSS and Pag-Ibig to boost the capital, equity and bonds markets.
