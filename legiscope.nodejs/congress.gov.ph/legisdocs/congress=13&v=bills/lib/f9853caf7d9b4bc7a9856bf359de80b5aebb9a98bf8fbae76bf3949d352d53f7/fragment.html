House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03523
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 17, 18, 20, 22, 25, 27, 38 AND 39 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 957, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SUBDIVISION AND CONDOMINIUM BUYER'S PROTECTIVE DECREE
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to clarify the gray areas in certain provisions of PD 957. Among others, it provides for greater protection to buyers, includes condominium units and pre-selling scheme within the ambit of the law and provides protection to developers who fail to meet the completion schedule for reasons beyond their control.
