House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03291
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE FRAMEWORK PROGRAM FOR CLIMATE CHANGE, CREATING THE CLIMATE CHANGE COMMISSION, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine Climate Change Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill creates the Climate Change Commission that shall: a) monitor the implementation of the Clean Air Act of 1999; b) recommend legislation, policies, programs and budgets on climate change adaptation and mitigation; and c) disseminate information on climate change, among others.
