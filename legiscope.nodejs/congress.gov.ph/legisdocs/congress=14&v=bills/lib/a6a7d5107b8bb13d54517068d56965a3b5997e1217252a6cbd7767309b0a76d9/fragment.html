HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00356
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING RETIREMENT BENEFITS TO ALL ELECTIVE OR APPOINTIVE BARANGAY OFFICIALS WHO HAVE RENDERED A CONSECUTIVE PERIOD OF NINE (9) YEARS OR THREE TERMS OF PUBLIC SERVICE IN THE BARANGAY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to grant retired barangay officials a monthly pension amounting to not less than 65% of their average monthly honoraria, allowances and other emoluments.
