House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02804
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROVIDE AT LEAST ONE (1) HEALTH WORKER IN EVERY BARANGAY, INCREASING THEIR SALARIES AND BENEFITS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the appointment in every barangay of one (1) health worker with a salary grade not lower than Salary Grade 10.
