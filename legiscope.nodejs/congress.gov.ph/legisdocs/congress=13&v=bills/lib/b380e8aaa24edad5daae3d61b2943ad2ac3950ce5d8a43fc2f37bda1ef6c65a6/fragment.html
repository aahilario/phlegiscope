House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00619
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING TO RESIDENTS OF A REGION WHERE A GEOTHERMAL ENERGY PLANT IS LOCATED A THIRTY PERCENT (30%) DISCOUNT ON THEIR POWER RATE CONSUMPTION
SHORT TITLE :
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to compensate residents for pollution caused by geothermal plants located in their region by giving them a 30% discount on their electric bill.
