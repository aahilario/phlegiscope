HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02916
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE BANGKO SENTRAL NG PILIPINAS (BSP) TO REQUIRE ALL IMPORTERS AND OTHERS WHO NEED TO BUY DOLLARS TO POST IN ADVANCE A PESO DEPOSIT EQUIVALENT TO THEIR DOLLAR APPLICATION OR PESO ADVANCE IN DOLLAR REQUIREMENT (PAID R) AND THAT ALL DOLLAR REMITTANCES OF OVERSEAS FILIPINO WORKERS AND EXPORTERS MUST BE CHANNELED THROUGH GOVERNMENT BANKS
ABSTRACT : The bill requires importers and others who buy dollars to post a peso deposit equivalent money to buy dollars. Furthermore, the bill requires exporters and overseas Filipino workers to channel their dollar remittances through government banks to control the circulation of dollars.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : PANCHO, PEDRO M.
