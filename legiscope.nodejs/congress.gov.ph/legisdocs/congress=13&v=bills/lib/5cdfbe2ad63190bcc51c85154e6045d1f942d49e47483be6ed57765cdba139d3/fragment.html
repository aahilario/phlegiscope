HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02336
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE ARTISTS CAREER SERVICE, RECOGNIZING THE SPECIAL QUALIFICATIONS OF ARTISTS IN GOVERNMENT, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to encourage artists to pursue civil service careers by: a) setting the criteria for appointment and advancement based on talent, education and productivity, and artistic standing; and b) the creation of the Artists Career Service.
