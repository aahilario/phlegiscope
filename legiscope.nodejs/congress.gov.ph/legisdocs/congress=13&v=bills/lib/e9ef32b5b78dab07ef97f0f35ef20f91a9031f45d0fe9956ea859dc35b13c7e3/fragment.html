HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03232
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROVIDE THE REVIVAL OF ARBOR DAY BY VESTING IN PROVINCIAL, CITY AND MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENTS THE RESPONSIBILITIES FOR CELEBRATING THE DAY FOR TREE PLANTING AS AN ANNUAL EVENT
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates all municipalities and cities to celebrate an Arbor Day at a fixed date every calendar year as may be deemed suitable according to the proper time and season of planting trees.
