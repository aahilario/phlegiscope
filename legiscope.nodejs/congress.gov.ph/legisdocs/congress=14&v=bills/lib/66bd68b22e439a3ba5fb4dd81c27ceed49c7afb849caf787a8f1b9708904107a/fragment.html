HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00988
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING THAT ALL CITIZENS OF THE PHILIPPINES SHALL AUTOMATICALLY BE ENROLLED IN THE NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE PROGRAM AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE ACT OF 1995
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to cover the entire populace with the basic health insurance benefits. It provides for the earmarking of 5% of the annual earnings of the Philippine Gaming Corporation and the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office as additional funding for the program.
