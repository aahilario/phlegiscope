House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03249
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A SOCIALIZED AND LOW-COST HOUSING LOAN RESTRUCTURING PROGRAM, PROVIDING THE MECHANISM THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Socialized and Low-Cost Housing Loan Restructuring Act of 2003
ABSTRACT : "Socialized and Low-Cost Housing Loan Restructuring Act of 2003." The bill seeks to help owners of socialized housing a chance to restructure their loans and help them get back on their feet by providing them some "breathing space" in their payment scheme for a specific period.
