House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01914
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER AMENDING ARTICLE 81 OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AS AMENDED BY SECTION 24 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7659 BY LIMITING THE WAITING PERIOD IN CARRYING OUT CAPITAL PUNISHMENT AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill limits the waiting period to carry out the death sentence from six months to one year instead of the present period of one year to 18 months.
