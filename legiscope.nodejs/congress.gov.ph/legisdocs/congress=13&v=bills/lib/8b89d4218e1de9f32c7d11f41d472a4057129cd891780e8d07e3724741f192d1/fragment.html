House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01590
FULL TITLE : AN ACT SUBJECTING THE PHILIPPINE CHARITY SWEEPSTAKES OFFICE (PCSO) AND THE PHILIPPINE AMUSEMENT AND GAMING CORPORATION (PAGCOR) TO THE CORPORATE INCOME TAX, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 27(C) OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OR 1997, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to eliminate the exemption of the Philippine Charity Sweesptakes Office and the Philippine Gaming Corporation from corporate income taxes.
