HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02479
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR PEOPLE'S INITIATIVE TO AMEND THE CONSTITUTION
SHORT TITLE : "People's Initiative to Amend the Constitution Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to amend the Constitution through a people's initiative upon a petition of at least 12% of the total number of registered voters of which every legislative district is presented by at least 3% of the registered voters therein.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ABLAN, ROQUE JR. R.
DATE FILED : 2007-09-11
