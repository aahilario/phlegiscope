HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01756
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPOSING A LAND CONVERSION TAX, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the imposition of a 15% land conversion tax on all agricultural lands converted for residential purposes and a 30% tax on all agricultural lands converted for commercial, industrial and other non-agricultural purposes.
