HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01886
FULL TITLE : STUDENT HOUSING ACT OF 2004
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to encourage the private sector to invest in the estalishment of student dormitories or housing projects by providing them incentives. It mandates the National Housing Authority and the Commission on Higher Education to take the lead in ensuring accessibility and afforadability of living quarters to students.
