House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03220
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING THE VISION AND POLICIES OF GOVERNMENT IN THE HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT SECTOR, CREATING THE DEPARTMENT OF HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT, DEFINING ITS MANDATE, POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Department of Housing and Urban Development Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Department of Housing and Urban Development Act of 2004." The bill provides for the powers and functions of the Department proper, the Plans, Policies and Monitoring Office, the Urban Development and Housing Operations Office, the regional offices and Housing and Urban Development Adjudication Commission and prescribes their jurisdiction and composition.
