House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00762
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 27 AND 29 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9280, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CUSTOMS BROKERS ACT OF 2004, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to clarify, once and for all, that RA 9280 does not intend to prohibit corporations from engaging in the business of customs brokerage prvided that they hire the services of at least one customs broker.
