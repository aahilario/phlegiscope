HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02283
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE USE OF THE CONTRACTOR'S CERTIFICATE AS A LEGAL PAPER THAT CAN BE USED AS COLLATERAL AND REDISCOUNTING IN ANY WINDOW OF A PUBLIC OR PRIVATE BANK
ABSTRACT : To protect the credit standing of the contractor and the agency, the bill proposes the acceptance of the contractor's certificate as a collateral to secure loans and be rediscounted at any bank.
