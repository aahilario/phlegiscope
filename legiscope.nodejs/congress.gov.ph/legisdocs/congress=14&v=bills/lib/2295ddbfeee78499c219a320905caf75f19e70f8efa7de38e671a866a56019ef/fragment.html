HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03298
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO HASTEN TITLING OF RESIDENTIAL LANDS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN BY EXTENDING THE GRANT OF FREE PATENT TO RESIDENTIAL LANDS UNDER CERTAIN CONDITIONS
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the following: a) extend the grant of free patent to residential lands classified as such by the local government in its land use plan and not needed for public service and use; b) reduce the period of occupation to ten (10) years; and c) remove the restriction on encumbrance in the title.
