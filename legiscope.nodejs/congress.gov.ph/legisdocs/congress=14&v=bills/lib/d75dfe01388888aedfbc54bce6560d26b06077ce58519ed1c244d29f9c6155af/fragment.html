HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00469
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING CONSUMER PROTECTION IN THE PURCHASE OF BRAND NEW MOTOR VEHICLES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Lemon Law of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Lemon Law of 2007". The bill mandates manufacturers to fix all defects covered by the warranty of new motor vehicles within the lemon law rights period or within certain mileage as reflected by the odometer of the vehicle.
