HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01033
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE INTERNAL REVENUE ALLOTMENT FOR LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS AND LIKEWISE INCREASING THE ALLOCATION FOR BARANGAYS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the share of local government units in the internal revenue allotment (IRA) from 40% to 45% starting in FY 2007. The proposed 5% increase shall be allocated to barangays in addition to the 20% allocation provided under Section 285 of the Local Government Code.
