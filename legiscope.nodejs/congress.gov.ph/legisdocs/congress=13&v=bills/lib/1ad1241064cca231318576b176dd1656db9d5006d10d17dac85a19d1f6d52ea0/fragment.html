HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02846
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE USE OF BASIC ENGLISH AS THE MEDIUM OF INSTRUCTION IN PRE-SCHOOL, ELEMENTARY AND HIGH SCHOOLS AND PRESCRIBING THE TEACHING IN SPECIALIZED ENGLISH IN TERTIARY LEVELS OF THE PHILIPPINES EDUCATIONAL SYSTEM
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the use of English as the medium of instruction in pre-schools, elementary and high schools. In the college level, the teaching of English shall focus on the language and terminology of business and industry.
