REPUBLIC ACT NO. RA09418
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING A STRATEGY FOR RURAL DEVELOPMENT, STRENGTHENING VOLUNTEERISM AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Volunteerism Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Volunteerism Act of 2005". The bill strengthens volunteerism as an effective instrument for sustainable development and as a catalyst for mobilization, positive action and social change. It promotes the participation of various sectors in the country and foreign volunteer organizations in public and civic affairs.
