House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00650
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER STRENGHTENING THE HOME DEVELOPMENT MUTUAL FUND AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Home Development Mutual Fund Law of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Home Development Mutual Fund Law of 2004". Proposes the following amendments to PD 1752: 1. Restate the Fund's tax exemptions, 2. Expand the Board of Trustees, 3. Exempt the Fund from the Salary Standardization Law, and 4. Strengthen the enforcement power of the Fund to sanction delinquent borrowers.
