HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00117
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN SECTIONS OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBERED SIX HUNDRED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE MARINE POLLUTION DECREE OF 1974
ABSTRACT : Presidential Decree No. 600 declares as unlawful the throwing, discharge or deposit of any refuse matter of any kind or description. However, the penalty of a fine of five hundred pesos (P500.00) or imprisonment of not more than one (1) year is not at all parallel to the concomitant damage to our waters. This measure seeks to increase the penalty for unlawful dumping and holds the president, manager or director of a corporation or partnership liable for such acts. It sets the guidelines under which dumping may be allowed.
