HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01952
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THAT EDUCATIONAL CURRICULUM AND THE CONSEQUENT PREPARATION OF TEXTBOOKS AND RELATED MATERIALS SHOULD HAVE CONTINUITY AND STABILITY, AND THAT THE SAME SHOULD NOT BE AFFECTED FOR A PERIOD OF AT LEAST SIX (6) YEARS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE R.A. NO. 8047, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE BOOK PUBLISHING INDUSTRY ACT
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates that textbooks and related materials should not be changed for a period of at least six (6) years.
