FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING THE FARMERS' AND FISHERFOLK ASSISTANCE ACT OF 2007 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Farmers' and Fisherfolk Assistance Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes the adoption of a voucher system in the requisition, release and disbursement of funds to farmers to be used in the cultivation, development, improvement or fertilization of farmlands.
