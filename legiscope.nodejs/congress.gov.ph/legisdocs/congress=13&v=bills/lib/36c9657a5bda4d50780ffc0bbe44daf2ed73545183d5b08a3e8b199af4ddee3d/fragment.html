House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05897
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING THROUGH A SOCIAL INTEGRATION PROGRAM LEGAL RESIDENCE STATUS TO CERTAIN ALIENS IN THE PHILIPPINES UNDER CERTAIN CONDITIONS
SHORT TITLE : "The Alien Social Integration Act of 2006."
ABSTRACT : "The Alien Social Integration Act of 2006". The bill seeks to provide legal residence status to certain overstaying or illegal aliens in the country through a social integration program. It also provides for the imposition of integration fees and charges.
