House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05157
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 3 OF EVERY YEAR AS SPECIAL COMMEMORATIVE DAY TO BE CALLED THE NATIONAL VICTORY AND LIBERATION DAY OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to declare September 3 of every year as a non-working holiday to commemorate the National Victory and Liberation Day of the Philippines.
