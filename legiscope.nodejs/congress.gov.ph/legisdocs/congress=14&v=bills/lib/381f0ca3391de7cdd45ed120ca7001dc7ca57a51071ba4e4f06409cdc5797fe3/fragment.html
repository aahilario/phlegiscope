House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01099
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE LIVELIHOOD AND TECHNOLOGY RELATED LOAN CONDONATION ACT OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to condone all penalties and surcharges on livelihood or technology-related loans from any government institution involved in livelihood and technology-related programs of the government.
