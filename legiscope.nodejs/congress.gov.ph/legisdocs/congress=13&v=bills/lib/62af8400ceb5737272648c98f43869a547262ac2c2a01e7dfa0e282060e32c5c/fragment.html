HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00810
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING EQUAL RIGHT TO WIVES AND HUSBAND, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLES 333, 334 AND 344 OF ACT NUMBERED 3815, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED PENAL CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill substitutes the terms "adultery" and "concubinage" under Articles 333 and 334 of the Revised Penal Code with the term "infidelity" in order to attune the Code to the fundamental equality of women and men before the law.
