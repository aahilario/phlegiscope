FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A FRAMEWORK FOR RESPONSIBLE AND ACCOUNTABLE TRADE NEGOTIATIONS 'BY CREATING THE PHILIPPINE TRADE REPRESENTATIVE OFFICE, APPROPRIATNG FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Charter Of The Philippine Trade Representative Office
ABSTRACT : “Charter of the Philippine Trade Representative Office.” Provides for the establishment of the Philippine Trade Representative Office and for the duties and responsibilities of the Philippine Trade Representative who principally handles the country’s trade and investment agreements.
