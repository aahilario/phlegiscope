HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03178
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TRANSFERRING THE BUREAU OF FISHERIES AND AQUATIC RESOURCES FROM THE DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE TO THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES
ABSTRACT : The bill places the Bureau of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources including its personnel, applicable appropriation, records, equip,emt, properties, and assets and liabilites under the jurisdiction of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources.
