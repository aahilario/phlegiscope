HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03015
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING POLITICAL TURNCOATISM AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill defines political turncoatism as a "change of political party affiliation, determined at the time of filing of certificate of candidacy, by any elected public official, except a barangay official, after proclamation and during his term of office."
