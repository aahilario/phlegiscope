HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02683
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROHIBIT DISCRIMINATION AND PREFERENTIAL TREATMENT ON THE BASIS OF SEX, ETHNICITY, PHYSICAL CONDITION, RELIGIOUS BELIEF OR POLITICAL AFFILIATION IN CONNECTION WITH ADMISSION TO AN INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION PARTICIPATING IN ANY PROGRAM AUTHORIZED UNDER THE COMMISSION ON HIGHER EDUCATION
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Discrimination in College Admission Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits discrimination and preferential treatment on the basis of sex, ethnic consideration, physical condition, religious belief or political affirmation with regard to college admission.
