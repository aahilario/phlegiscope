House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03018
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING ENERGY CONSERVATION, ENHANCING THE EFFICIENT USE OF ENERGY, AND GRANTING INCENTIVES TO ENERGY CONSERVATION PROJECTS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Energy Conservation Act of 2002
ABSTRACT : "Energy Conservation Act of 2002." The bill prescribes the duties and functions of the Department of Energy, other government agencies and private and non-government agencies in the overall energy conservation program of the government. It also grants incentives for energy conservation projects.
