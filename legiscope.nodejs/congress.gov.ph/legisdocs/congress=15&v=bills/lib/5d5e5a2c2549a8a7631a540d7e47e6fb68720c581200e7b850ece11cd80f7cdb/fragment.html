HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00074
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING PARENTS, ADOPTERS, GUARDIANS OR PERSONS EXERCISING SPECIAL OR SUBSTITUTE PARENTAL AUTHORITY OVER CHILDREN UNDER PARENTAL AUTHORITY FOUND TO BE ENGAGED IN BEGGING AND/OR PROSTITUTION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Parental Sanction Law For Child Begging And Prostitution
ABSTRACT : “Parental Sanction Law for Child Begging and Prostitution.” Seeks to elevate the dignity of the child as well as the parents who nurture the upbringing of the child, measured in terms of he capacity to give love and care without exploitation.
