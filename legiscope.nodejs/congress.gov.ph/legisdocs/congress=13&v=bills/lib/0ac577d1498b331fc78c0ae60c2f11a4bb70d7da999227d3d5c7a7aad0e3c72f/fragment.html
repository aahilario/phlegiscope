House Bill/Resolution NO. HB06063
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9280, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CUSTOMS BROKERS ACT OF 2004, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Amending RA09280, Otherwise Known as the Customs Brokers Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to amend RA 9280 which disallows firms, companies or associations from being registered or licensed as "professionals" under the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) since only individuals who passed the licensure examination for customs brokers are covered by the PRC.
