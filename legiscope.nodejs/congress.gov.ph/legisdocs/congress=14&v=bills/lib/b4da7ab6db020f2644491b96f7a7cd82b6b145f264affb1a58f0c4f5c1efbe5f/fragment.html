HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03072
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL PUBLIC ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY SCHOOL PRINCIPALS AND SUPERVISORS TO HAVE TEACHING LOADS ESPECIALLY ON MAJOR SUBJECS IN THE CURRICULUM AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill requires all public elementary and high school principals and supervisors to have teaching loads in major subjects such as Math, Science and English.
