HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00557
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE DEPARTMENT OF INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATIONS TECHNOLOGY, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Department of Information and Communications Technology Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Department of Information and Communications Technology Act of 2004". Merges the communications aspect of the Department of Transportation and Communications (DOTC) with the National Computer Center (NCC), which is presently under the Office of the President (OP), with the purpose of streamlining its inherent functions to address the new phenomenon of converging and emerging information technologies.
