House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01752
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CONSTITUTING A CONSTITUTIONAL CONVENTION TO AMEND OR REVISE THE PRESENT CONSTITUTION, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Constitutional Convention Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Constitutional Convention Act of 2007". The bill seeks to constitute a constitutional convention for the purpose of amending the present Charter in order to make it more responsive and more relevant to the times.
