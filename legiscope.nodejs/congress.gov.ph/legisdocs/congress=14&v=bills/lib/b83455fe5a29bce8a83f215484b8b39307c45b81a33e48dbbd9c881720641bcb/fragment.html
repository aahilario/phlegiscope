REPUBLIC ACT NO. RA10087
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER CHANGING THE NOMENCLATURE OF THE NATIONAL LIBRARY INTO THE NATIONAL LIBRARY OF THE PHILIPPINES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS 1 AND 2 OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THREE THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED SEVENTY THREE, ENTITLED: AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BUREAU OF PUBLIC LIBRARIES TO THE NATIONAL LIBRARY
SHORT TITLE : Further Changing The Nomenclature Of The National Library Into The National Library Of The Philippines
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to change the name of the National Library to the National Library of the Philippines to properly identify it as belonging to the Philippine state.
