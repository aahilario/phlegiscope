House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02834
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ACCELERATING THE DEVELOPMENT OF A NATIONAL RAILWAY SYSTEM, CREATING FOR ITS PURPOSE THE NATIONAL RAILWAY AUTHORITY, PRESCRIBING ITS POWERS, FUNCTIONS AND DUTIES
SHORT TITLE : National Railway Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "National Railway Act of 2004." The bill provides for the consolidation of our national rail systems to encourage the use of railways. It prescribes the powers, functions and organizational structure of the National Railway Authority.
