House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00515
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN AND PROPAGATE FOSTER CARE FOR ABANDONED AND NEGLECTED CHILDREN AND OTHER CHILDREN WITH SPECIAL NEEDS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Foster Care Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Foster Care Act of 2004".The bill seeks to sytematize and enhance foster care in the country by providing incentives to qualified foster familites such as subsidy from the government, health insurance and tax exemptions. If this bill is enacted into law, it will also accord a legal framework under which every abused, neglected, surrendered, dependent and abandoned child can be provided with an alternative family.
