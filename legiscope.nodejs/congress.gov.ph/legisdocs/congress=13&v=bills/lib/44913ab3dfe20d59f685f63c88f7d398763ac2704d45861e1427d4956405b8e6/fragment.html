HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01280
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBERED SIXTEEN HUNDRED TWO, PRESCRIBING STIFFER PENALTIES FOR JUETENG, AND OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Jueteng Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Jueteng Act of 2004." Declares it the policy of the State to eradicate jueteng, promote local revenues through honest, legitimate, lawful and ethical means and impose heavier sanctions and penalties on local administrators and law enforcers who provide protection to jueteng operators and syndicates.
