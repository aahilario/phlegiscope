HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01546
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXPANDING THE LIABILITIES OF INDIRECT EMPLOYERS IN JOB CONTRACTING, COVERING OTHER BENEFITS DUE THEIR EMPLOYEES, FOR THIS PURPOSE AMENDING ARTICLE 106 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the indirect employers to ensure the payment of all benefits due employees hired through job contractors. It provides that if the employees can not get their benefits from their agencies, they can turn to the company for relief.
