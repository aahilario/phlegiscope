FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ACCELERATE THE DEVELOPMENT OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY STARTUP COMPANIES BY PROVIDING AN IT VENTURE CAPITAL FUND AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : It Venture Capital Fund Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “IT Venture Capital Fund of 2010.” Seeks to strengthen the global competitiveness of the country as well as maximize the unlimited potential of capable Filipinos to develop breakthrough products in the information Technology (IT) field that can become multi-million or even multi-billion dollar bestsellers.
