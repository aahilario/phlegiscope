HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00120
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A NATIONAL JUNIOR POLICE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Establishes a National Junior Police Force which shall be composed of abled Filipino citizens between the age of fifteen (15) and twenty (20) years old who will assists the regular policemen, local officials and even school officials in the enforcement of laws and ordinances.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : LOPEZ, CARLO V.
DATE FILED : 2010-07-01
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
