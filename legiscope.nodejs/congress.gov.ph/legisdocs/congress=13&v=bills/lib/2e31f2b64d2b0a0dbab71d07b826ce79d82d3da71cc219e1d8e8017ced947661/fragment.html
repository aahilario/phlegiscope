FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MODERNIZATION OF THE NATIONAL MAPPING AND RESOURCE INFORMATION AUTHORITY (NAMRIA), AMENDING CERTAIN PORTIONS OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 192, SECTION 22(a) AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : NAMRIA Modernization Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "NAMRIA Modernization Act of 2004." The bill cites the need for fresh funds to upgrade NAMRIA's facilities. Prescribes the objectives, mandate, responsibilities, powers and functions of the NAMRIA and the components for its modernization.
