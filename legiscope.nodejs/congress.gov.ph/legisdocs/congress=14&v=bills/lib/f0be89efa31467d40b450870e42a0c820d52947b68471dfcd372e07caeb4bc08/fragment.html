House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01722
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A P125.00 DAILY ACROSS-THE-BOARD INCREASE IN THE SALARY RATES OF EMPLOYEES AND WORKERS IN THE PRIVATE SECTOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The P125.00 Daily Across-the-Board Wage Increase Act"
ABSTRACT : "The P125.00 Daily Across-the-Board Wage Increase Act". The current minimum wages are grossly inadequate to support a decent standard of living hence the proposal to grant a P125.00 daily across-the-board increase for all employees in the private sector, whether agricultural or non-agricultural.
