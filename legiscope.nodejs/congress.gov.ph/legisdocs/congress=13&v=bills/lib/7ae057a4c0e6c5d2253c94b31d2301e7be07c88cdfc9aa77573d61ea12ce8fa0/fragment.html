House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04451
REPUBLIC ACT NO. RA09396
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REDEFINING THE TERM 'VETERAN' THEREBY AMENDING FURTHER REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6498 AS AMENDED
SHORT TITLE : Redefining the Term 'Veteran'
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to retionalize the conferment of a veteran status to military personnel by extending the total cumulative active service in the Armed Forces of the Philippines from six years, as provided in the present law, to the time of his retirement or sooner separated due to death or disability.
