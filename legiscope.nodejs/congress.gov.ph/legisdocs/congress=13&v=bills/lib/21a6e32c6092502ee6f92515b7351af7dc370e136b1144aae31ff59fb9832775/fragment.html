HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03082
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE RATES OF EXCISE TAX ON ALCOHOL AND TOBACCO PRODUCTS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS 141, 142, 143, 144 AND 145 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to restructure the specific tax rates for distilled spirits and wines and to revise the excise tax system for fermented liquor, cigars and cigarettes by adopting the ad valorem tax scheme.
