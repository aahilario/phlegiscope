House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02484
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING INCOME EXTENDERS TO PUBLIC ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY SCHOOL TEACHERS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Teachers� Income Extenders Act of 2006�
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to grant public school teachers non-monetary incentives in the form of tuition fee exemption for their children enrolled in state universities and colleges, a 25% discount on the price of rice/corn bought directly from the National Food Authority and a GSIS loan interest rate 3% lower than the regular rate.
