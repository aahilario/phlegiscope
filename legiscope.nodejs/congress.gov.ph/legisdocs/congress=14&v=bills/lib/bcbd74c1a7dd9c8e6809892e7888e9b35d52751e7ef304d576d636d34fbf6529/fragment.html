House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00128
FULL TITLE : AN ACT UPGRADING THE MINIMUM SALARY GRADE LEVEL OF TEACHERS FROM SALARY GRADE 10 TO 19
ABSTRACT : To motivate teachers to strive for excellence in their field and to improve the quality of education in the country, the bill calls for the upgrading of the salary grade (SG) of public school teachers from SG 10 to SG 19.
