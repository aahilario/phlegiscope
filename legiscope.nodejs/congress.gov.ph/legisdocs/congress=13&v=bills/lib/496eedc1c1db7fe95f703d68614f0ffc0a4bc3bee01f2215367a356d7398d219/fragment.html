House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04548
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING ARTICLES 60, 97, 107, 159, AND 164 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7394, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CONSUMER ACT OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : To deter unfair and unconscionable sales acts and practices and to safeguard the welfare of buyers, the bill seeks to increase the penalties for violation of the Consumer Act of the Philippines.
