House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00338
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE PROVINCE OF CAVITE AS THE HISTORICAL CAPITAL OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to declare the province of Cavite as the historical capital of the country to give recognition to the important role the province played in out nation's history.
