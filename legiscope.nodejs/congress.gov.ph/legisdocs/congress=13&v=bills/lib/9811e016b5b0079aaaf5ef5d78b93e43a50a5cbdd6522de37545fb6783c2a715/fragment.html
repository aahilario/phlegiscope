HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02109
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPOSING STIFFER PENALTIES TO DRUG STORES WHICH REFUSE TO HONOR SENIOR CITIZEN CARDS ISSUED BY THE GOVERNMENT FOR AVAILMENT OF BENEFITS AND PRIVILEGES GRANTED TO SENIOR CITIZENS
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to penalize drug stores, which refuse to honor the senior citizen's card, with a fine ranging from P1,000 to P5,000 or imprisonment from one month to six months.
