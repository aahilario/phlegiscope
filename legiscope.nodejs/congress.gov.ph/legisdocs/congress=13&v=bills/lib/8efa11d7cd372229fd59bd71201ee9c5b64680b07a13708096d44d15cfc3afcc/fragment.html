House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05002
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE BOT AUTHORITY FURTHER AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6957, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7718, ENTITLED AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE FINANCING, CONSTRUCTION, OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS BY THE PRIVATE SECTOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to set a reasonable rate of return for solicited and unsolicited or negotiated projects; institutionalize a fair, honest and competitive procurement process and establish a Build-Operate-Transfer Authority to rationalize program implementation.
