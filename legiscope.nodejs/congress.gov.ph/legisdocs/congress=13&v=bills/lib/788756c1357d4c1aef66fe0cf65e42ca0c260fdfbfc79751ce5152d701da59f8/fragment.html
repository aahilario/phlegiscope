HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00679
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A SYSTEM OF PAYING THE ARREARS OF PENSION TO WORLD WAR II VETERANS, PENALIZING ANY FRAUD COMMITEED IN RELATION TO THIS ACT.
ABSTRACT : The bill grants a one-time lump sum P30,000 gratuity pay to all 70-year old World War II veterans in lieu of the monthly P1,700 total disability pension.
