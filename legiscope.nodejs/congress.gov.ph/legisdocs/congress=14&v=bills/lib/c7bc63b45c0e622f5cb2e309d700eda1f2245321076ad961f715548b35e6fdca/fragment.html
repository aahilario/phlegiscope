HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00188
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO FURTHER STRENGTHEN THE CONSUMER PROTECTION BY AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7394, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CONSUMER ACT OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to make the National Consumer Affairs Council a truly independent body by placing it under the Office of the President. It also redefines the composition of the Council to include representatives from the Department of the Interior and Local Government and the Philippine Information Agency.
