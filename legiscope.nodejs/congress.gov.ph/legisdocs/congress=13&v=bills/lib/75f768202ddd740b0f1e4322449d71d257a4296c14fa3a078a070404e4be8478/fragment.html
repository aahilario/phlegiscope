HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00359
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ENSURE THE AFFORDABILITY OF MEDICINES AND HEALTH CARE IN THE PHILIPPINES BY EXEMPTING FROM ALL DUTIES AND TAXES THE SALE, IMPORTATION, PROCESSING, REPACKING OR MANUFACTURING OF ALL KINDS OF PHARMACEUTICAL PRODUCTS AND THEIR RAW MATERIALS USED IN THE MANUFACTURE THEREOF
ABSTRACT : In order to reduce health care costs to an affordable level, this bill seeks to exempt from all duties and taxes the importation, sale, manufacturing, repacking or processing of all kinds of pharmaceutical products and raw materials used in the manufacture thereof. If duties and taxes of these products are eliminated, the cost of goods directly related to health care will be lowered and the manufacturers and distributors can pass on the benefit to consumers.
