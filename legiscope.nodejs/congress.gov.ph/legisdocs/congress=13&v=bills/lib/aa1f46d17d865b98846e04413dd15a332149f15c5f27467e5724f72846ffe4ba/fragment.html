HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01846
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 41 PARAGRAPH 4 OF RA 4850 AS AMENDED BY SECTION 2 PARAGRAPH 2 OF EXECUTIVE ORDER 927
ABSTRACT : This bill amends the Laguna Lake Development Authority Law, as amended, and restores to the local government of San Pablo City the jurisdiction over the Seven Lakes. The condition of the seven lakes in San Pablo City deteriorated due to the failure of the LLDA to closely manage and develop it.
