HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04960
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6847, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "PHILIPPINE SPORTS COMMISSION ACT"
ABSTRACT : The measure empowers the Philippine Sports Commission (PSC) to discredit national sports associations violating the state policy on sports development. It also directs the PSC to formulate rules and regulations to govern the selection of trainees, athletes and coaches and design their training regimen.
