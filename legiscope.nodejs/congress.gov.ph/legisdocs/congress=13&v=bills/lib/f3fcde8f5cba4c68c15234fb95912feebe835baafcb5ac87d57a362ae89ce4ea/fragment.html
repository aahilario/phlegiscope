House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04752
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE MONTH OF AUGUST OF EVERY YEAR AS "NATIONAL BREASFEEDING AWARENESS MONTH."
SHORT TITLE : "National Breastfeeding Awareness Month Act"
ABSTRACT : "National Breastfeeding Awareness Month Act". The measure seeks to reestablish the culture of breastfeeding and instill public awareness about its superiority and benefits vis-a-vis bottle feeding.
