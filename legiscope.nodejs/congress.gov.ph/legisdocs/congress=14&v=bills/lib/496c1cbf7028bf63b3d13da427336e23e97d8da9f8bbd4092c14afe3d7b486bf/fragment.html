House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00160
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FURTHER AMENDING SECTION THREE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THREE HUNDRED AND FORTY, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE ARMED FORCES RETIREMENT LAW
ABSTRACT : The measure authorizes the payment of benefits to the widow of a deceased officer or enlisted man whose marriage to the officer or enlisted man was contracted after his retirement from the military service.
