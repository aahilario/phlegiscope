House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01774
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 4 OF THE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7161, FURTHER AMENDING SECTION 71 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 705, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE REVISED FORESTRY CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The Revised Forestry Code of the Philippines strictly prohibits the cutting of mangrove species. The bill proposes to allow the cutting of mangrove species only in cases where such would spur economic activities in the area like the construction of a seaport.
