HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00215
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE PROVISION OF FIREARMS, AMMUNITIONS, EXPLOSIVES, CHEMICAL, BIOLOGICAL OR NUCLEAR WEAPONS OR ANY PARTS OR COMPONENTS USED IN THE MANUFACTURE THEREOF TO ENEMIES OF THE STATE
ABSTRACT : There has been an upsurge of crimes affecting public order and safety due to the proliferation of illegally possessed and manufactured firearms, ammunition and explosives. The bill renders as unlawful for any person, natural or juridical to knowingly sell, transfer, or in any manner provide, directly or indirectly, firearms, ammunitions, explosives, chemical, biological or nuclear weapons or any part or component used in the manufacture thereof, to enemies of the State. It also proposes a penalty of reclusion perpetua to death and a fine ranging from Php 500,000 to PhP 1.0 million for any violation of the proposed law.
