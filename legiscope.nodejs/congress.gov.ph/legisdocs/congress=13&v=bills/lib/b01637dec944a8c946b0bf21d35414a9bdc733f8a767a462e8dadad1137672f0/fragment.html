House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04291
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING AND STRENGTHENING THE PROBATION SYSTEM, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE PERTINENT PROVISIONS OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 968, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE PROBATION LAW OF 1976", AS AMENDED
SHORT TITLE : Rationalizing and Strengthening the Probation System
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to allow offenders who have been sentenced to a probationable penalty and have already perfected their appeal to apply for probation. It also seeks to allow those sentenced to 6 years and 1 day to apply for probation.
