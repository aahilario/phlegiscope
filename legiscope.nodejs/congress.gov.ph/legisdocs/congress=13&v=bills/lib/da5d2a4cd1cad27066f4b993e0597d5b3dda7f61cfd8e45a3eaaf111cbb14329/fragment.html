House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04074
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING THE SUBDIVISION GREEN PARKS PROGRAM, GRANTING INCENTIVES FOR THE PLANTING OF TREES AND THE DEVELOPMENT AND MAINTENANCE OF FORESTED AREAS IN SUBDIVISIONS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Subdivision Green Parks Act of 2005."
ABSTRACT : "Subdivisions Green Parks Act of 2005". The bill mandates the owner or developer of a subdivision project to plant trees in the open spaces reserved for the common use and enjoyment of lot owners and along roads. It directs the Department of Natural Resources and Environment and the Housing and Land Use Regulatory Board to determine the number of trees to be planted and the size of the green park.
