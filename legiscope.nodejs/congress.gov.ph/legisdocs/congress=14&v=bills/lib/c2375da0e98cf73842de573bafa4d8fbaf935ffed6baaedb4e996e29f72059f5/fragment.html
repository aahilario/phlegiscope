House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00883
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 13 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates local government units to undertake prior consultation with and favorable recommendation from the National Historical Institute for any change in the name of a street and other public place in their respective territorial jurisdiction.
