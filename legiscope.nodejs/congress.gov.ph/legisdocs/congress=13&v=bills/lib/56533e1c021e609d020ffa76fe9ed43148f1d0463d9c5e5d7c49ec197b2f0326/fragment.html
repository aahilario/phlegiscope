House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02639
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING TERRORISM, PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Terrorism Act of 2003
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Terrorism Act of 2003." The bill aims to define the crime of terrorism and impose the corresponding penalty. It seeks to bring the crime of terrorism within the coverage of the Anti-Wire Tapping Law and the Anti-Money Laundering Law. It provides for the creation of an Anti-Terrorist Action Council to coordinate, supervise and monitor the counter-terrorism efforts of the government.
