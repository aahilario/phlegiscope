HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02474
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING TOBACCO MANUFACTURERS TO USE A MINIMUM OF SEVENTY FIVE PERCENT (75%) LOCALLY PRODUCED TOBACCO IN THE MANUFACTURE OF CIGARETTES IN THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The measure penalizes cigarette manufacturers who fail to comply with the local tobacco component mandated under the proposed measure. The proposed penalties are - a fine of P10 million for the first offense, P30 million for the second offense and P50 million for the 3rd offense plus closure of the cigarette plant.
