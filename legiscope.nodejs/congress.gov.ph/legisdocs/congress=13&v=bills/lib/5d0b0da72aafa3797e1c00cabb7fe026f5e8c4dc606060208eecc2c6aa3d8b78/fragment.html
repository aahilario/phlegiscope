House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02591
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE SECURITY OF TENURE OF FARMERS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Farmers Security of Tenure Act
ABSTRACT : "Farmers Security of Tenure Act." The bill requires the preliminary determination of cases involving disputes or controversies between farmers and landlords by the Department of Agrarian Reform before a regular court can take cognizance of such cases.
