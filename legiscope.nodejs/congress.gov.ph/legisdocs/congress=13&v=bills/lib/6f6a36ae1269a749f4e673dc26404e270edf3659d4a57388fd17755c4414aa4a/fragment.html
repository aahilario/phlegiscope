House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03203
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PRESCRIBING ENGLISH AS THE MEDIUM OF INSTRUCTION IN ALL CURRICULAR SUBJECTS, EXCEPT IN FILIPINO LANGUAGE SUBJECTS IN ALL PUBLIC ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY SCHOOLS
SHORT TITLE : English as the Medium of Instruction Act
ABSTRACT : "English as the Medium of Instruction Act." The bill mandates all public elementary and secondary schools to use English as the medium of instruction in all subjects except in Pilipino and other language subjects.
