HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01212
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE TITLING, APPROPRIATION, ENCROACHMENT AND REGISTRATION OF CREEKS, ESTEROS, STREAMS, CANALS, RIVER BANKS AND OTHER PUBLIC WATERWAYS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes the penalty of imprisonment of not less than six (6) months and one (1) day to six (6) years or a fine of not less than P5,000 or both on any person who appropriates and encroaches upon any creek, canal, estero, stream, and other similar public waterways; and who registers or otherwise acquires a title thereto.
