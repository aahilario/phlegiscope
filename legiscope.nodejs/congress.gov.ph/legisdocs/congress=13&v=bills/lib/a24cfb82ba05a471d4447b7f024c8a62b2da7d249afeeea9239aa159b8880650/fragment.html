House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03123
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO UPGRADE THE SALARY GRADE LEVEL OF PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS FROM SALARY GRADE 10 TO SALARY GRADE 20, AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6758, AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREOF
ABSTRACT : To improve the economic plight of teachers, the bill seeks to upgrade the salary grade (SG) of public school teachers from SG 10 to SG 20.
