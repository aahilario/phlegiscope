HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03085
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PRESCRIBING THE PROPER LABELING OF PHARMACEUTICAL PRODUCTS WHICH ACT ON THE BRAIN AND CENTRAL NERVOUS SYSTEM SO AS TO IMPAIR DANGEROUSLY THE CONSUMER'S ABILITY TO CARRY OUT CERTAIN ACTIVITIES AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Pharmaceutical Labeling (Warning of Cognitive Function Impairment) Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill requires pharmaceutical companies to properly label drugs that cause impairment of certain cognitive functions thus affecting the ability to perform certain activities.
