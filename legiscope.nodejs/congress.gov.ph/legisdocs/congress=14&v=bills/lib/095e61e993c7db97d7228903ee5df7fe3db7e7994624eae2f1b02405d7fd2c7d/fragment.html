HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00553
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A NATIONAL SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM FOR COLLEGE STUDENTS IN SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY COURSES IN EVERY MUNICIPALITY OF EVERY PROVINCE OF THE COUNTRY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to grant annually a scholarship in science and technology course to a poor but deserving student in every municipality of every province. The fund assistance shall cover tuition and registration fees, book allowance, board and lodging fees or transportation allowance, and a monthly stipend for living expenses.
