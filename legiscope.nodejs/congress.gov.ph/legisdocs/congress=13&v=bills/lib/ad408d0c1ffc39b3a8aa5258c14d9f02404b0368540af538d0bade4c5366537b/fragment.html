House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02963
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 21 OF EVERY YEAR AS A "NATIONAL DAY OF REMEMBRANCE" IN MEMORY OF THE STRUGGLES AND SACRIFICES OF THE HEROES AND MARTYRS OF THE MARTIAL LAW ERA
SHORT TITLE : National Day of Remembrance Act
ABSTRACT : "National Day of Remembrance Act." In recognition of the struggles and sacrifices of the heroes and martyrs of the martial law era, the bill declares September 21 of every year as a special working holiday to be known as the "National Day of Remembrance."
