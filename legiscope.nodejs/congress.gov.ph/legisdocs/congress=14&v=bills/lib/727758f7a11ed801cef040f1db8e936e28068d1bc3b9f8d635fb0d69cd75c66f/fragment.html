House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00056
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING THE MAGNA CARTA FOR DAY CARE WORKERS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Magna Carta for Day Care Workers"
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta for Day Care Workers". The proposed bill seeks to promote, protect and improve the social and economic welfare of day care workers. It also aims to professionalize the sector that provides substitute parenting to our children.
