House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03334
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPROVING PHILIPPINE COMMITMENT TO HUMAN RIGHTS PROMOTION AND PROTECTION BY PROVIDING THE NECESSARY MECHANISMS FOR THE PREVENTION OF THE OCCURRENCE AND PROTECTION FROM THE ADVERSE EFFECTS OF INTERNAL DISPLACEMENT AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Internal Displacement Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Internal Displacement Act of 2004." The bill seeks to protect citizens from being arbitrarily displaced from his/her home. It establishes a mechanism of support to victims of internal displacement so that damage and injury can be minimized in cases when they cannot be prevented.
