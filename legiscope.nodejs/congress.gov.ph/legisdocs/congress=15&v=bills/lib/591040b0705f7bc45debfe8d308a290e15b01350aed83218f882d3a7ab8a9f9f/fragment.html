HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00239
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REMOVING THE INTERNAL REVENUE ALLOTMENT FROM THE METROPOLITAN MANILA DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY (MMDA), AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7924, ENTITLED, "AN ACT CREATING THE METROPOLITAN MANILA DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFORE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Removes the entitlement of the Metropolitan Manila Development Authority (MMDA) to a share of the internal revenue allotment for the reason that MMDA is not created like a province, city, municipality and barangay as contemplated in the Local Government Code but it is a public corporation.
