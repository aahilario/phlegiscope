HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00103
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING GOVERNMENT HOSPITALS TO UTILIZE ALL ITS INCOME FOR THEIR MAINTENANCE AND OTHER OPERATING EXPENSES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The practice of disallowing government hospitals from using their income for maintenance and other operating expenses has limited their capacity to expand and to deliver quality and efficient basic health services to the people. This bill seeks to authorize government hospitals to utilize their income from pharmacy, fees from in-house services and facilities, including but not limited to laboratory, radiology, operating room and nursery facilities; income derived form nonpatient related services such as parking fees, interview fees and tour fees, for maintenance and other operating expenditures including capital outlay.
