HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01926
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING EXEMPTION TO THE PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS FROM THE COVERAGE OF REPUBLIC ACT 6758, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SALARY STANDARDIZATION LAW AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to upgrade the salary of teachers to enable them to cope with the rising cost of living by exempting them from the Salary Standardization Law and revising it like those received by employees in government corporations.
