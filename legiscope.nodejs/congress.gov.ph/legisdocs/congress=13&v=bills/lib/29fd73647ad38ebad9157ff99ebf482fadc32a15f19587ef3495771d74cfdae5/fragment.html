FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A THREE THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED PESOS (P3,500.00) ACROSS-THE-BOARD MONTHLY SALARY INCREASE IN THE WAGE AND SALARY RATES OF EMPLOYEES AND WORKERS IN THE PUBLIC SECTOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
