HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00606
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE PAYMENT OF RETIREMENT BENEFITS TO GOVERNMENT RETIREES ON THE DATE OF THEIR RETIREMENT
ABSTRACT : The bill requires all government agencies to process the papers of a retiree three months before retirement to ensure payment of benefits on the day he retires.
