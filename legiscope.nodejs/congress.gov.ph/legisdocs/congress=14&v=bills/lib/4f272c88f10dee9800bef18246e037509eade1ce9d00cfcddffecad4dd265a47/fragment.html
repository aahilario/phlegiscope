HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03052
FULL TITLE : AN ACT SETTING ASIDE IN FAVOR OF THE NATIONAL PROSECUTION SERVICE TEN PERCENT (10%) OF THE TOTAL AMOUNT AWARDED TO AND RECEIVED BY THE OFFENDED PARTY AS JUDGMENT ON THE CIVIL ASPECT OF A CRIMINAL CASE HANDLED BY A PUBLIC PROSECUTOR
SHORT TITLE : "Public Prosecutor's Incentive Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill grants incentives to public prosecutors who handle the civil aspect of a criminal case when the offended party does not engage the services of private prosecutor.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.
DATE FILED : 2007-11-12
