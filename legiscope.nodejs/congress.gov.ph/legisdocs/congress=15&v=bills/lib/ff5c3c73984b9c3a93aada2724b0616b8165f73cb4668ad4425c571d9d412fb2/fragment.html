House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00245
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE WORKERS COMPENSATION PROGRAM, THE WORK CONTINGENCY INSURANCE FUND, THE PHILIPPINE WORKERS COMPENSATION AUTHORITY AND DEFINING THEIR POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE ARTICLES 166 TO 288-A OF TITLE 2 BOOK IV OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES", AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE
SHORT TITLE : Workers Compensation Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : "Workers Compensation Act of 2010" Renames the Employee's Compensation Program (ECP) to Workers Compensation Program thereby enhancing the effectiveness and efficiency of the implementation of the said program in it the work contingency prevention, as well as the rehabilitation of occupationally disabled workers.
