House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00231
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 110 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1529 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE PROPERTY REGISTRATION DECREE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to amend Section 110 of PD 1529 (Property Registration Decree) to relieve property owners of the burdensome and onerous task of resorting to judicial actions for the reconstitution of their Torrens Certificate of Title that were lost or destroyed while in the custody of the Register of Deeds. It identifies the Register of Deeds as the proper party to initiate the filing of petitions for the reconstitution of lost or destroyed owner's certificate of title.
