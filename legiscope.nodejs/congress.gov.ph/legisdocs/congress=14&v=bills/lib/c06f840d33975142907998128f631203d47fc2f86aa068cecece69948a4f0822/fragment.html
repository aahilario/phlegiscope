HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02471
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROTECT THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES FROM WIRETAPPING OR OTHER SIMILAR ACTIONS IMPOSING HEAVIER PENALTY UPON OFFENDERS, IF THEY ARE MEN OR WOMEN IN GOVERNMENT, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill penalizes with imprisonment of not less than six (6) years of prision correctional but not more than twenty (20) years of reclusion temporal any public official or employees who wiretaps or bugs the Office of the President.
