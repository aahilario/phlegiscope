HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04705
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 26 OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 292, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER OF 187
ABSTRACT : In order to foster unity and political and economic stability, the bill seeks to declare as regular holidays the Iglesia ni Kristo Foundation Day on June 12th of every year and the Last Day of the Ramadan on December 30th of every year.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ALVAREZ, ANTONIO C.
