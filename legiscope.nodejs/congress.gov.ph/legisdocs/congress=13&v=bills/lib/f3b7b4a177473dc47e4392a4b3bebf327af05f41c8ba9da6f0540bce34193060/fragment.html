House Bill/Resolution NO. HB06074
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING AND MAINTAINING LACTATION STATIONS IN WORKPLACES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Workplace Lactation Station Act of 2007
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the creation of a standard lactation station in every workplace and grants an additional 30 minutes of work break for milk expression.
