HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01343
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING STIFFER PENALTIES FOR CUTTING, GATHERING OR COLLECTING TIMBER, OR OTHER FOREST PRODUCTS WITHOUT LICENSE, AND FOR OTHER VIOLATIONS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE REVISED FORESTRY CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes the penalty of prision mayor in its minimum to medium period and a fine of not less than P500,000 but not exceeding P1.0 million for the systematic cutting, gathering, collecting of timber for commercial purposes.
