HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03100
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING THAT PROCEEDS OF THE TRAVEL TAX AND PERCENTAGE TAX ON HOTELS EARMARKED FOR OR ADMINISTERED BY THE PHILIPPINE TOURISM AUTHORITY, AND NET PROFITS DERIVED FROM MERCHANDISING OPERATIONS OF DUTY AND TAX-FREE SHOPS SHALL ACCRUE ENTIRELY TO THE UNAPPROPRIATED GENERAL FUND OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks the preservation and/or accrual to the general fund of the proceeds of the travel tax and the percentage tax on hotels earmarked for or administered by the Philippine Tourism Authority and of the net profits from the merchandising operations of the duty and tax-free shops accruing to the Department of Tourism.
