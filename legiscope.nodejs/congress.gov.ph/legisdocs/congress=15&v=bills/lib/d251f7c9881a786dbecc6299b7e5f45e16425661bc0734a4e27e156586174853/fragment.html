HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00038
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING RETIREMENT, HEALTH CARE AND DEATH BENEFITS TO PROFESSIONAL FILIPINO ATHLETES WHO WIN WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP TITLES IN INTERNATIONAL PROFESSIONAL SPORTS COMPETITIONS OR IN OTHER EQUALLY PRESTIGIOUS INTERNATIONAL CHAMPIONSHIP GAMES AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Professional Filipino Athletes Retirement, Health Care And Death Benefits Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Professional Filipino Athletes Retirement, Health Care and Death Benefits Act of 2010.” Seeks to extend monthly pensions and other privileges to Filipino professional athletes who became world champions at one time in their lives.
