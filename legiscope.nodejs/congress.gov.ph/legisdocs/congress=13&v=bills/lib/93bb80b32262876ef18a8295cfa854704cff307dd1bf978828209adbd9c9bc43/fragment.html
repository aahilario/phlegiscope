HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02356
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CLARIFYING THE COVERAGE OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7837, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "AN ACT GRANTING PERMANENT RESIDENT STATUS, OTHER RIGHTS AND PRIVILEGES TO FILIPINO VETERANS OF WORLD WAR II WHO ACQUIRED AMERICAN CITIZENSHIP UNDER THE UNITED STATES IMMIGRATION ACT OF 1990 AND ANY OTHER PRIOR ACTS FOR THESE PURPOSES"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to insert the phrase "AND ANY OTHER PRIOR ACTS" in Sections 5, 6 and 7 in RA 7837 to allay the fears of Filipino veterans, who acquired American citizenship before 1990, that they may not be included among the benefiticaries of RA 7837.
