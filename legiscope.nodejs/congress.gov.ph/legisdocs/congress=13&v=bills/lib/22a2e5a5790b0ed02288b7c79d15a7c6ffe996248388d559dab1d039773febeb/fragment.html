HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05808
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING ARTS. 83, 87, 100, 128, 131, 280 AND 282 OF PD 442, THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the following amendments, among others: a) compressed workweeks/flextime arrangement, b) exemption from the night work prohibition on women industries/establishments operating on a continuous 24 schedule, c) recognition of fixed-term employment, and d) inclusion of gross inefficiency or poor performance as a just cause for dismissal
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : FERJENEL G. BIRON, M.D.
DATE FILED : 2006-09-25
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
