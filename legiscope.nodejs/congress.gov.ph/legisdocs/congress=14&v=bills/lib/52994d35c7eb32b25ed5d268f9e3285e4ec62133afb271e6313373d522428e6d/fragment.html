HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02527
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 46 OF REPUBLIC ACT 8189 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE VOTER'S REGISTRATION ACT OF 1996" BY INCREASING THE PENALTY PROVIDED AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure penalizes a person found guilty of any election offense with imprisonment of not less than six (6) years but not more than twelve (12) years. He shall also be disqualified to hold public office and deprived of his right to vote.
