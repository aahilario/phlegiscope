House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01321
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MAGNA CARTA FOR PUBLIC SOCIAL WELFARE AND DEVELOPMENT WORKERS
SHORT TITLE : Magna Carta for Public Social Welfare and Development Workers
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta for Public Social Welfare and Development Workers." The measure aims to institutionalize social work as a distinct profession. It provides for the code of conduct, work hours, compensation and other benefits of social workers.
