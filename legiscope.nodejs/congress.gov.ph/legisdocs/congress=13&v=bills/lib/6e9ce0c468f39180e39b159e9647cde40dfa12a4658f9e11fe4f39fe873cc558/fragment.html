HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01354
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MANNER AND DATE OF ELECTION OF SECTORAL REPRESENTATIVES TO THE LOCAL SANGGUNIANS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Local Sectoral Representation Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Local Sectoral Representation Act of 2004." The bill provides for the election of three sectoral representatives each in the sangguniang panlalawigan, sangguniang bayan and sangguniang panlungsod - one from the women sector, one from the workers and one from any of the following: the urban poor, indigenous cultural communities, disabled persons, and the elderly. Provides that the first election shall be held on the second Monday of May 2007 and every three years thereafter.
