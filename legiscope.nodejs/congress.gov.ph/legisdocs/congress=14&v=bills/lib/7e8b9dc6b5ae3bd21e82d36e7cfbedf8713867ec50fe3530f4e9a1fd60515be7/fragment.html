HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01951
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXPANDING THE PURPOSES AND APPLICATION OF THE SPECIAL EDUCATION FUND (SEF), AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 272 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to include in the Special Education Fund coverage the budget for the payment of electric and water bills; payment of salaries, allowances and other benefits of non-teaching personnel; procurement of service vehicles, establishment and maintenance of extension classes; and purchase of prescribed books, teaching aids and other instructional materials.
