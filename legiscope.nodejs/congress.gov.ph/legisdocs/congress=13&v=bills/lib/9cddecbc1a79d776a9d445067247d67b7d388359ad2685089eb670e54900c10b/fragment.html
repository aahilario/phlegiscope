House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05273
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE MONTH OF FEBRUARY OF EVERY YEAR AS "LIVER CANCER AND HEPATITIS-B AWARENESS AND PREVENTION MONTH"
SHORT TITLE : "The Liver Cancer and Hepatitis-B Awareness and Prevention Month Act"
ABSTRACT : "The Liver Cancer and Hepatitis-B Awareness and Prevention Month Act". The bill designates the month of February of every year as National Liver Cancer and Hepatitis-B Awareness Month.
