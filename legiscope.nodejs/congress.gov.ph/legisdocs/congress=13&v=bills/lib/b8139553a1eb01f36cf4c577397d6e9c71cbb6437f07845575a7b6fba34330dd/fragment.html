House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00307
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE FIELD OF LAND TITLE INSURANCE AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 612 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE INSURANCE CODE OF 1978 AS AMENDED BY PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1455 AND 1460 AND BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 874
ABSTRACT : In recent months, the Registry of Deeds in many cities and municipalities were rocked by widespread incidents of faking of land titles. These incidents have shaken the very foundation of the Torrens System of land titling. This bill seeks to amend the Insurance Code by creating the field of land title insurance. Title insurance protects whoever is dealing with land titles from any loss which may be incurred should the title later on turn out to be fraudulent or fake. With the introduction of this insurance, those who transact in real estate are compelled to declare the true value of their transaction.
