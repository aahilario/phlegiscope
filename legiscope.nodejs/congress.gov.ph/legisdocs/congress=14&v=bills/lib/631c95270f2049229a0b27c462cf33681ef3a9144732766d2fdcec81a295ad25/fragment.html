HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02833
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING STIFFER PENALTIES FOR THE ILLEGAL USE OF AFP (ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES) AND PNP (PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE) UNIFORMS, INSIGNIA OR DRESS AMENDING FOR THAT PURPOSE ARTICLE 179 OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill prescribes stiffer penalties for the unauthorized use of the prescribed insignia, uniform or dress of the Armed Forces of the Philippines or the Philippine National Police.
