House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01736
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CURTAILING THE ILLEGAL ACTIVITIES OF RACKETEERS AND POWERFUL SYNDICATES IN THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : Racketeer-Influenced and Corrupt Organizations (RICO) Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Racketeer-Influenced and Corrupt Organizations (RICO) Act of 2004." The bill aims to prevent organized crime syndicates from using money and power to subvert and corrupt legitimate businesses by facilitating the prosecution of and imposing stiffer penalties for criminal offenders.
