HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00699
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE SELECTION OF ADDITIONAL MEMBERSHIPS IN THE BOARDS OF THE POEA AND THE OWWA, AMENDING FOR SUCH PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT 8042 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'MIGRANT WORKERS AND OVERSEAS FILIPINO'S ACT OF 1995'
ABSTRACT : The bill provides that the selection of the additional members to the Boards of the OWWA and the POEA be made through open nomination by the general membership of the women, sea-based and land-based sectors.
