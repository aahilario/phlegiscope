REPUBLIC ACT NO. RA09853
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9280, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CUSTOMS BROKERS ACT OF 2004, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Amending Ra 9280, Otherwise Known As The Customs Brokers Act Of 2004
ABSTRACT : The measure explicitly provides that corporations shall not be registered by the Professional Regulation Commission as customs brokers but as business which can engage in customs brokerage activities and register with the Bureau of Customs provided that they hire the services of a customs broker.
