HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01023
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INTEGRATING EDUCATION ON WOMEN, GIRLS AND GENDER RIGHTS, EQUALITY AND WELFARE IN ALL LEVELS OF PUBLIC AND PRIVATE SCHOOLS
SHORT TITLE : Women, Girls and Gender Education Act of 2003
ABSTRACT : "Women, Girls and Gender Education Act of 2003." The bill requires all public and private educational institutions, including computer, technical and vocational schools, to integrate in their curriculum women's rights, welfare, development and gender equality, RA 7877 (Anti-Sexual Harassment Act of 1995) and RA 9208 (Anti-Trafficking in Persons Act of 2003).
