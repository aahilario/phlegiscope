House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00166
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE RULES FOR A COMPETITIVELY NEUTRAL GOVERNMENT, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Government Competitive Neutrality Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The Government Competitive Neutrality Act of 2007". The bill compels the government to adopt the following competitive neutrality arrangements, among others: a) prohibition of fiscal and legislative powers to the advantage of government business over the private sector; b) prohibition of subsidies; and c) prohibition of exemption from any form of taxes.
