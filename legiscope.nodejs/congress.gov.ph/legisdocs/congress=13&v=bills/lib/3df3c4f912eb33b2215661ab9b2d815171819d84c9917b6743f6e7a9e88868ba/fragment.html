House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00704
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING POLICIES AND MEASURES FOR THE PREVENTION, CONTROL AND TREATMENT OF SEVERE ACUTE RESPIRATORY SYNDROME (SARS), PROVIDING FUNDS THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS) Prevention, Control and Treatment Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS) Prevention, Control and Treatment Act of 2004." The proposed bill seeks to institute policies and measures to control, prevent and treat SARS through an information and education campaign, the establishment of a SARS Isolation Center and Airport Quarantine Area and the creation of a Philippine National SARS Council. It also seeks the establishment of a comprehensive SARS monitoring program or "Bantay SARS".
