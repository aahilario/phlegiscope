House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02212
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RENAMING THE NATIONAL IRRIGATION ADMINISTRATION TO "NATIONAL IRRIGATION AND DRAINAGE ADMINISTRATION", AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3601, AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL IRRIGATION ADMINISTRATION, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The National Irrigration and Drainage Administration Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the inclusion of the construction, management and administration of drainage component to our irrigation facilities to prevent the unequal and imbalanced flow of waters to farmlands.
