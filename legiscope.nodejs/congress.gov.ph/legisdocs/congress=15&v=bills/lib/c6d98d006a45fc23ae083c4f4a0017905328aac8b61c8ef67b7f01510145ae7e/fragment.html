COMMITEE HEARINGS/ACTIONS:

      4. Approved by the Committee on Ways and Means on 2012-11-13
          -with the following amendments: the second paragraph of section 4 of the bill be deleted and a new section shall be inserted which shall read as follows: 1. "SECTION 5. THE CENTER SHALL BE EXEMPT FROM THE FOLLOWING FORMS OF TAXATION: B. ALL DONATIONS IN ANY FORM TO THE CENTER SHALL BE EXEMPT FROM THE DONOR'S TAX AND THE SAME SHALL BE CONSIDERED AS ALLOWABLE DEDUCTION FROM THE GROSS INCOME IN THE COMPUTATION OF THE INCOME TAX OF THE DONOR, IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE PROVISIONS OF THE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8424, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED; AND C. IMPORTATION OF SCIENCTIFIC, PHILOSOPHICAL, HISTORICAL AND CULTURAL BOOKS, SUPPLIES AND MATERIALS FOR THE USE IN THE CONSERVATION OR PRESERVATION WORK OF THE CENTER SHALL BE EXEMPT FROM CUSTOMS DUTIES." 2. THE SUCCEEDING SECTIONS SHALL BE RENUMBERED ACCORDINGLY.
      5. Approved by the Committee on Appropriations on 2012-11-19
          -Approved pursuant to Section 44 of the Rules of the House of Representatives, 15th Congress, with amendment.
