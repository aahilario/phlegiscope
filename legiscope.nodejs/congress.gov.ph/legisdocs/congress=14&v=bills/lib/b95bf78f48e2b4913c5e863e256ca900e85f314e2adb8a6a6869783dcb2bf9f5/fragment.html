HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00278
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING RETIREMENT INCENTIVE AND HEALTH CARE BENEFITS TO PROFESSIONAL FILIPINO ATHLETES WHO HAVE WON WORLD TITLES OR WORLD CHAMPIONSHIPS AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Filipino Professional Athletes Retirement Incentive and Health Care Benefits Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Filipino Professional Athletes Retirement Incentive and Health Care Benefits Act of 2007". The bill seeks to grant a monthly pension of P15,000 to former world champions upon reaching the age of fifty. Likewise, it grants members of a world champion team a monthly lifetime retirement incentive of P10,000 upon reaching the age of fifty.
