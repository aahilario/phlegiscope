House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02156
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PRESCRIBING THE INTEGRATION AND INSTITUTIONALIZATION OF PRESCHOOL EDUCATION INTO THE PUBLIC ELEMENTARY SCHOOL SYSTEM, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Preschool Education Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Preschool Education Act of 2007". the bill aims to integrate and institutionalize preschool education within the public elementary school system. The preschool education program shall be offered for free to children between three (3) to five (5) years old.
