House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02861
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REFORMING THE ADMINISTRATIVE TITLING PROCESS BY AMENDING CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF COMMONWEALTH ACT NO. 141 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE PUBLIC LAND ACT AS AMENDED, AND OTHER RELATED LAWS
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to reduce the period of elgibility for free patent from 30 years to 10 years and to extend the applicability of free patent to cover residential and commercial lands.
