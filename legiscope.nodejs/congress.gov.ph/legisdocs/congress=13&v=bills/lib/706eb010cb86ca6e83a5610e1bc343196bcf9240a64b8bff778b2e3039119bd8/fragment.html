House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01068
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO FURTHER PROMOTE THE DEVELOPMENT, UTILIZATION AND COMMERCIALIZATION OF RENEWABLE ENERGY SOURCES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Renewable Energy Act of 2003
ABSTRACT : "Renewable Energy Act of 2003." The country has immense indigenous energy resources that can be utilized to mitigate our dependence on imported fuel. These renewable energy resources include biomass, solar, wind, geothermal, hydro and ocean energy and other emerging energy sources. The bill aims to accelerate the exploitation, development and utilization of these renewable energy sources to enable the country to attain energy self-sufficiency, energy afforability and rural electrification.
