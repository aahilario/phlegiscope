HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01609
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING ALL MOBILE AND LANDLINE TELEPHONE COMPANIES OPERATING IN THE PHILIPPINES TO REGISTER UNDER THE DEPARTMENT OF TRADE AND INDUSTRY (DTI) AND THE BUREAU OF INTERNAL REVENUE (BIR) ALL MANUFACTURED OR IMPORTED PREPAID CARDS PRIOR TO THEIR DISTRIBUTION AND/OR SALE AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF
ABSTRACT : At present, the sale of prepaid cards for cellular and landline telephones are not subjected to any taxes. The bill requires all telephone companies to register with the Department of Trade and Industry and the Bureau of Internal Revenue all prepaid cards prior to sale to enable the BIR to collect the correct taxes.
