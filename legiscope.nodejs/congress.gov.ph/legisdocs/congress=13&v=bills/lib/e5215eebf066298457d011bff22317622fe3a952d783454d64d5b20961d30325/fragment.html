HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01769
FULL TITLE : AN ACT BANNING CHAIN DISTRIBUTION PLANS OR PYRAMID SALES SCHEMES AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SEVENTY-THREE HUNDRED AND NINETY-FOUR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Pyramid Sales Ban Act
ABSTRACT : "Pyramid Sales Ban Act." The bill proposes to increase the fine for engaging in chain distribution plans or pyramid sales schemes from not more than P10,000 to not more than P1 million.
