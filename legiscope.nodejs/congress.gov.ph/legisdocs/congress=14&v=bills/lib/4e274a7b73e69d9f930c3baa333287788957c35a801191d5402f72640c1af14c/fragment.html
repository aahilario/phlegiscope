House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01894
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PHILIPPINE AIR FORCE ACADEMY (PAFA) AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes the Philippine Air Force Academy within the Philippine Air Force (PAF). The Academy shall be tasked to teach and prepare aviation cadets for military service in PAF.
