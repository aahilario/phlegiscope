HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01209
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE RELEASE OF RETIREMENT, SEPARATION PAY AND BENEFITS WITH INTEREST FOR RETIRING OR RESIGNING GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES WITH ADMINISTRATIVE, CRIMINAL AND CIVIL CASES WHO ARE EVENTUALLY EXONERATED OR CLEARED THEREFROM
ABSTRACT : This bill provides for retiring and resigning government officials and employees with administrative, criminal or civil cases, upon exoneration or clearance therefrom, to receive their retirement or separation pay and benefits with compounded interest computed from the period of their retirement or separation date up to date of the promulgation of the decision on their cases.
