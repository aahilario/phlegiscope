House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01098
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROVIDE AND PROMOTE NUMBER PORTABILITY FOR CELLULAR MOBILE TELEPHONE SERVICE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Cellular Mobile Telephone Number Portability Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Cellular Mobile Telephone Number Portability Act of 2004." Cellular mobile telephone number portability (CMTNP) is defined as the right of a consumer to retain his cellular phone number when changing to another service provider. The bill requires service providers to give consumers the option to port telephone numbers between service providers by implementing the CMTNP throughout the network of cellular phone service providers.
