House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01267
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING ARTICLE 13 (a), TITLE V, BOOK III OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : This bill protects the rights of Filipino Interior Designers by setting forth conditions under which foreigners may perform professional services as interior designers or consultants in the country. Requires foreigners to secure a special temporary permit from the Professional Regulation Commission to practice their profession
