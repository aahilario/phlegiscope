HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00392
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE, GIVING MUNICIPALITIES MORE EQUITABLE SHARE OF NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE TAXES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to amend the Local Government Code in order to remove the disparity in the share of local government units and grant municipalities a more equitable share in the collection of national taxes. The proposed increase in the internal revenue allotment to municipalities shall transfer to projects that would generate employment and livelihood, ensure food security, conserve the environment, increase agricultural productivity and improve health and sanitation.
