House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04431
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPOSING A NATIONWIDE COMMERCIAL LOG BAN IN NATURAL FORESTS, SUBJECT TO EXEMPTIONS IN SELECTED PRODUCTIVE AREAS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Commercial Log Ban Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Commercial Log Ban Act of 2005". The bill imposes a nationwide commercial log ban in all forestlands for a period of 30 years except in tree plantations and private lands and production forests covered by timber license agreements and individual forest management agreements.
