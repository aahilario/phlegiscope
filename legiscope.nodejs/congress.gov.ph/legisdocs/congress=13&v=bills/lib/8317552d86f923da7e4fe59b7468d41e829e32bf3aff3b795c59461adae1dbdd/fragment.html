HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02635
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING 'CLANDESTINE LABORATORY' AND PENALIZING THE OWNERSHIP, ESTABLISHMENT, MAINTENANCE AND OPERATION THEREOF, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9165, THE 'COMPREHENSIVE DANGEROUS DRUGS ACT OF 2002', AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill clarifies that a "clandestine laboratory" refers to any facility used for the illegal manufacture of any dangerous drug situated not only on land or real property but also those in a boat, ship, airplane, van or on any location possible. It imposes the death penalty on the person who owns, establishes, maintains and operates a clandestine laboratory.
