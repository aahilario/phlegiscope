House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01000
FULL TITLE : AN ACT UPGRADING THE MINIMUM SALARY GRADE LEVEL OF PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS IN ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY LEVELS FROM GRADE 10 TO GRADE 18 AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : Claims that teachers are overworked and underpaid and half of their time they sell goodies to survive. The bill seeks to help them by upgrading their salary from Grade 10 to 18.
