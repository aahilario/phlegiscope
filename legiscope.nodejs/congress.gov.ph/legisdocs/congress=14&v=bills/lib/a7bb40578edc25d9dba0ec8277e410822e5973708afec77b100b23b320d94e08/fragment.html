FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING MATERNAL AND INFANT HEALTH HOME VISITING PROGRAM
SHORT TITLE : "Maternal And Infant Care Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill directs health centers to designate a health professional to deliver maternal and child health services in the home of indigent pregnant adolescents and adolescent parents.
