HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04658
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC WORKS AND HIGHWAYS (DPWH) TO SEEK THE CONCURRENCE OF THE REPRESENTATIVE ON THE APPOINTMENT, ASSIGNMENT OR DESIGNATION OF A DISTRICT ENGINEER WITHIN THE TERRITORIAL JURISDICTION OF HIS LEGISLATIVE DISTRICT
ABSTRACT : The bill requires the Department of Public Works and Highways to seek the concurrence of the concerned Congressmen on the appointment, assignment or designation of a District Engineer within the territorial jurisdiction of his legislative district.
