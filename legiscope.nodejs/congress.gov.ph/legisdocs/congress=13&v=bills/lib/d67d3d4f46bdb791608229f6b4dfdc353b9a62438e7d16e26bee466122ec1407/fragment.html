HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03979
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FIXING THE RETIREMENT AGE OF THE OFFICERS AND ENLISTED PERSONNEL OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES AND MEMBERS OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE TO SIXTY-FIVE
ABSTRACT : The bill sets the compulsory retirement age of the officers and enlisted personnel of the Armed Forces of the Philippines and the Philippine National Police at 65 years and upon completion of a minimum of 15 years of active service.
