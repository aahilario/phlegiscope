House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02190
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REORGANIZING THE COAST AND GEODETIC SURVEY DEPARTMENT OF THE NATIONAL MAPPING AND RESOURCE INFORMATION AUTHORITY, AN ATTACED AGENCY OF THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES, AND TRANSFERRING IT TO THE DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION AND COMMUNICATIONS TO BE KNOWN AS THE BUREAU OF COAST AND GEODETIC SURVEY, DEFINING ITS ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE, POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the creation of the Bureau of Coast and Geodetic Survey (BCGS) and defines its powers, functions and organizational and command structure.
