HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02863
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EQUALIZING THE LONGEVITY PAY OF PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS AND GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES WITH THOSE PAID UNIFORMED PERSONNEL OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES AND THE INTEGRATED NATIONAL POLICE AND, FOR THIS PURPOSE, AMENDING SECTION 16 OF REPUBLIC ACT 4670 AND SECTION 13 (c) OF REPUBLIC ACT 6758, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for public school teachers and all government employees to receive a longevity pay computed at 10% of their basic pay for every five years of continuous satisfactory service.
