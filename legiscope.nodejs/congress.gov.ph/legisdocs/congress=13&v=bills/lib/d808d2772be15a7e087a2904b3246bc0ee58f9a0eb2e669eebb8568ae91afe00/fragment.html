HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02940
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING THE USE OF HIGH SCHOOL CAMPUS GROUNDS BY ALLOWING ACCESS FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF STATE UNIVERSITY OR COLLEGE (SUC) WITHIN THE SAME CAMPUS GROUNDS MAXIMIZING ITS USE FOR THE STUDENTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Campus Land Use Rationalization Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Campus Land Use Rationalization Act of 2004." The bill directs public high schools to allow the establishment of a state university and college (SUC) building within the property of such high school provided that only 30% of the total area shall be occupied by the SUC.
