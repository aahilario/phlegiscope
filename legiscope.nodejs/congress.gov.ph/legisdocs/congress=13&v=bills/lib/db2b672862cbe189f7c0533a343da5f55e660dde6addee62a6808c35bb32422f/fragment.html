House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02744
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL INDEPENDENT POWER PRODUCERS, GENERATION COMPANIES AND/OR ENERGY RESOURCE DEVELOPERS TO REMIT THE AMOUNT THEY ARE REQUIRED TO SET ASIDE AS FINANCIAL BENEFIT TO HOST COMMUNITIES DIRECTLY TO THE LATTER
SHORT TITLE : Requiring All Independent Power Producers, Generation Companies and/or Energy Resource Developers to Remit the Amount They Are Required to Set Aside as Financial Benefit to Hose Communities Directly to the Latter
ABSTRACT : In order to expedite the remittance of the financial benefit (one centavo per kilowatt hour of the total electricity sales) that independent power products, generation companies and energy resource developers are required to set aside for the affected host communities, the bill directs said entities to remit the benefits directly to the communities concerned.
