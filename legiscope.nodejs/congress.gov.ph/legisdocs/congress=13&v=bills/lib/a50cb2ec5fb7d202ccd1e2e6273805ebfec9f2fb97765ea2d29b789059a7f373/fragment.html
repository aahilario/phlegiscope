House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00554
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR OPTIMUM PERFORMANCE IN REVENUE COLLECTION THROUGH THE GRANT OF SPECIAL INCENTIVES AND REWARDS FOR EXEMPLARY SERVICE AND THROUGH LATERAL ATTRITION IN REVENUE-GENERATING AGENCIES OF GOVERNMENT AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill establishes a syatem of special incentives and rewards in revenue collection to motivate collection officials, officers and employees to render exemplary service while upholding the highest ethical standards in the performance of their tasks.
