HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02327
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AFFORD PROTECTION TO WORKING WOMEN AND TO STRENGTHEN THEIR RIGHTS TO EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITIES IN BOTH PRIVATE AND PUBLIC SECTORS, INCLUDING THE PROHIBITION ON DISCRIMINATION AGAINST WOMEN PERTAINING TO THEIR EMPLOYMENT
ABSTRACT : The bill expands the prohibited acts of discrimination against women with respect to terms and conditions of employment to include the following, among others: a) the hiring process; b) apprenticeship and learnership programs; and c) promotion.
