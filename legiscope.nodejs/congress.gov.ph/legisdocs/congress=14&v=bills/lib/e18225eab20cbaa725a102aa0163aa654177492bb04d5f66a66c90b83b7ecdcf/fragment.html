HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00422
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE FIELD OF LAND TITLE INSURANCE AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 612 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE INSURANCE CODE OF 1978 AS AMENDED BY PRESIDENTIAL DECREES NO. 1455 AND 1460 AND BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 874
ABSTRACT : The bill creates the field of "title insurance" to protect land buyers from fake or forged titles, reimburse them on fraudulent transactions and save the integrity of the Torrens Titling System from attack by crime syndicates.
