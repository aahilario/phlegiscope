House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03890
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTILLING FISCAL DISCIPLINE IN THE PUBLIC SECTOR BY SPECIFYING PRINCIPLES OF RESPONSIBLE FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT AND PROMOTING FULL TRANSPARENCY AND ACCOUNTABILITY IN GOVERNMENT REVENUE, EXPENDITURE AND BORROWING PROGRAMS
SHORT TITLE : Fiscal Responsibility Act
ABSTRACT : "Fiscal Responsibility Act." The measure proposes the adoption of the following: a) debt cap, b) a deficit-neutral measure to address the proliferation of unfunded laws, and c) a Personal Services cap or PS cap. It highlights the importance of transparency and accountability in the government's fiscal operations.
