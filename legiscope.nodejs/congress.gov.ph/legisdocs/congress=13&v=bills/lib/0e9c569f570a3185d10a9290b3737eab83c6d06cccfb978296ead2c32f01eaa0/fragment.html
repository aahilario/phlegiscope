House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03840
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING THE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7305 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE MAGNA CARTA OF PUBLIC HEALTH WORKERS PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to promote the welfare of public health workers coming from the 4th, 5th and 6th class municipalities and provinces by directing the national government to entirely shoulder their salaries, allowances and other benefits.
