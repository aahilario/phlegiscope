HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00092
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE REGISTRATION OF PRE-PAID SIM CARD USERS AND MOBILE PHONE UNITS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill directs public telecommunications entities to require users/purchasers of pre-paid SIM cards and mobile phones to present a valid identification card to establish their identity and their addresses.
