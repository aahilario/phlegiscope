House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02523
FULL TITLE : THE PUBLIC ASSEMBLY ACT OF 2007
SHORT TITLE : "Public Assembly Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The measure institutionalizes the creation of freedom parks in the entire archipelago, provides rigid penalties against erring public officials and peace-keeping contingent in the conduct of dispersal and spells out clear-out rules in the issuance of rally permits.
