HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04698
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING SECURITY OF TENURE TO GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES AND TEACHERS WHO HAVE RENDERED SEVEN YEARS OF SERVICE
ABSTRACT : The measure grants government employees and teachers, appointed in a temporary or probationary status, a permanent appointment after rendering seven (7) years of satisfactory service.
