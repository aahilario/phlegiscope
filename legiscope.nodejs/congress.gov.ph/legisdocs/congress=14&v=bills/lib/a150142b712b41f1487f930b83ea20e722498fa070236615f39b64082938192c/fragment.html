HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01284
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE COMMISSION ON PRE-SCHOOL EDUCATION, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "The Commission on Pre-School Education Act of 2004"
ABSTRACT : "The Commission on Pre-School Education Act of 2004". The bill creates the Commission on Pre-School Education, defines its powers, functions, and appropriates the necessary funding for its establishment and operations.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CHIPECO, JUSTIN MARC SB.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-24
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
