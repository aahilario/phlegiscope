House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05824
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING THE ESTABLISHMENT AND OPERATIONS OF LOCAL COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Local Colleges and Universities Act of 2006"
ABSTRACT : "Local Colleges and Universities Act of 2006". The bill requires local government units to consult and coordinate with the regional office of the Commission on Hegher Education with regard to the establishment of a local college or university created through an ordinance of the Sanggunian.
