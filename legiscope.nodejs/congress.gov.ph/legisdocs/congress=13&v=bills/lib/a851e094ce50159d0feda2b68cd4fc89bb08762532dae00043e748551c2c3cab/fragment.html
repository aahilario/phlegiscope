House Bill/Resolution NO. HB06104
FULL TITLE : RICE INDUSTRY DEVELOPMENT ACT OF 2006
SHORT TITLE : The Rice Industry Development Act
ABSTRACT : "The Rice Industry Development Act". The bill seeks to address the underdevelopment and underproductivity of our rice industry as well as the poverty of our rice farmers by building, through strong government intervention, the necessary policy and program structure.
