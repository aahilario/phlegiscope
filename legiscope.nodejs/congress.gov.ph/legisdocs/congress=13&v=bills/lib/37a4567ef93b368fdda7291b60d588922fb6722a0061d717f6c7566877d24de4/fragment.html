HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03168
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PRESERVE AND REHABILITATE THE FOREST BY BANNING ALL LOGGING OPERATIONS IN PRIMARY AND SECONDARY GROWTH FORESTS AND RAINFORESTS, CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE A TASK FORCE FOR THE PRESERVATION AND REHABILITATION OF FORESTS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Forest Preservation and Rehabilitation Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Forest Preservation and Rehabilitation Act of 2004." The bill proposes a total log ban on primary and secondary forests and rainforests for a period of 35 years. It provides for the establishment of the Task Force for the Preservation and Rehabilitation of Forests, its functions and composition.
