House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01240
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING MANDATORY SCHOOL INSPECTIONS TO PROTECT THE HEALTH AND SAFETY OF STUDENTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Healthy Learning Environment Act"
ABSTRACT : "Healthy Learning Environment Act". The bill provides for a yearly inspection of school facilities to ensure that basic amenities for the health and safety of the students and the teaching and non-teaaching personnel are provided.
