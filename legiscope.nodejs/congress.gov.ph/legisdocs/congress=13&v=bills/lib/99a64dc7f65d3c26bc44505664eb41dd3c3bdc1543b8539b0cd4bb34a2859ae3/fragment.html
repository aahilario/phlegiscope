House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03201
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING PRE-SCHOOLS AS AN INTEGRAL PART OF BASIC EDUCATION
SHORT TITLE : National Preparatory Education Act
ABSTRACT : "National Preparatory Education Act." The bill proposes a two-level compulsory preparatory public education, namely: a) Kindergarten One for children 3 to 4 years old, and b) Kindergarten Two for children 5 years old.
