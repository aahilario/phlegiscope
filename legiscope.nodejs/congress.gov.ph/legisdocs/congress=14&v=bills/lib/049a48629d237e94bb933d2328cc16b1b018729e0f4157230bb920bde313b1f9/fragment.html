House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02789
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO GRANT MONTHLY PENSION TO GOVERNMENT RETIREES UNDER R.A. 1616 WHO HAVE REACHED THE AGE OF SEVENTY (70) YEARS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the granting of a monthly pension to government retirees who retired under RA 1616 when they reach the age of seventy (70) years.
