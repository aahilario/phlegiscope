House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00276
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1185, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "FIRE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES", PROVIDING FOR EFFECTIVE IMPLEMENTATION THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides administrative sanctions and criminal penalties for acts or omissions of a public officer when the same, either through negligence or malice, results to or fails to prevent the loss or damage of property due to fire. It provides stringent measures against the owner, administrator, occupant or person who violates the Code.
