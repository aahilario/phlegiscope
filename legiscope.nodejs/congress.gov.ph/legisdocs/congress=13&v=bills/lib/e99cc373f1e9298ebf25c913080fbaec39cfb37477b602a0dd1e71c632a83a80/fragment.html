House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01407
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PRESCRIBING FOR A SYSTEM OF CREDITING THE UNITS OF ACADEMIC COURSES OFFICIALLY ENROLLED AND PASSED BY A TRANSFERRING STUDENT TO THE INSTITUTION OF HIGHER LEARNING WHERE HE INTENDS TO ENROLL
SHORT TITLE : Borderless Course Credit Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Borderless Course Credit Act of 2004". The bill provides that in case a student transfers from one institute of higher learning to another, whether public or private, the admitting school, after evaluation, shall credit all subjects enrolled and passed by the transferring student.
