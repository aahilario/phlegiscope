House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01255
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MANDATORY CLEARANCE FOR ALL GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS UPON THEIR RETIREMENT FROM PUBLIC OFFICE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Honorable Dismissal of Government Officials Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Honorable Dismissal of Government Officials Act of 2007". The bill requires all government officials to secure a certificate of honorable dismissal from the Civil Service Commission, Office of the Ombudsman and the Department of Justice prior to the release of their retirement benefits.
