HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01859
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXTENDING THE MANDATED FUNDING SUPPORT FOR AGRICULTURAL AND FISHERIES MODERNIZATION AND PROVIDING SPECIFIC FUNDING ALLOCATION FOR STRATEGIC AGRICULTURAL AND FISHERIES DEVELOPMENT ZONES (SAFDZ), AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8435, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE AGRICULTURAL AND FISHERIES MODERNIZATION ACT (AFMA)
ABSTRACT : This bill: 1) provides specific funding allocation for the development of Strategic Agricultural and Fisheries Development Zones (SAFDZ), and 2) mandates the Department of Budget and Management (DBM) to include annually in the next nine (9) years, in the President's program for expenditures for submission to Congress an amount not less than Seventeen Billion Pesos (Php17,000,000,000.00) for the implementation of this Act.
