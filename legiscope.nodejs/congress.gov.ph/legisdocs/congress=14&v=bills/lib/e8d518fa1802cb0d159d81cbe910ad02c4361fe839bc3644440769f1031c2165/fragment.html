FULL TITLE : AN ACT DIRECTING THE USE OF COMPRESSED NATURAL GAS (CNG) FOR PUBLIC UTILITY VEHICLES (PUVs) IN METRO MANILA, ESTABLISHING THE NATURAL GAS VEHICLE PROGRAM FOR PUBLIC TRANSPORT, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "CNG for Public Utility Vehicles Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill institutionalizes the use of compressed natural gas (CNG) for the public transportation sector to reduce the country's dependence on imported crude oil.
