House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01924
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE INTERNAL REVENUE COMMISSION, IN ORDER TO REVITALIZE TAX ADMINISTRATION, PROVIDING THE MECHANICS FOR ITS IMPLEMENTATION, ALLOCATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : IRC Act of 2003
ABSTRACT : "IRC Act of 2003." The bill creates the Commission under the Department of Finance to administer taxes and implement internal revenue laws. Provides for the power and functions of the Commission, qualifications of the commissioners, composition of the Board and qualifications of its members.
