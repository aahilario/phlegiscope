HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02129
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING GOVERNMENT ASSISTANCE TO MADRASAH EDUCATION SYSTEM, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Fund Assistance to Madrasah Education Act of 2002"
ABSTRACT : "Fund Assistance to Madrasah Education Act of 2002". The bill extends government assistance to the Madrasah Education System by providing the following: infrastructure development, nutrition and feeding programs, classroom facilities, textbook assistance funds, scholarship grants, education loan fund, in-service training fund for teachers and scholarship grants to children of teachers.
