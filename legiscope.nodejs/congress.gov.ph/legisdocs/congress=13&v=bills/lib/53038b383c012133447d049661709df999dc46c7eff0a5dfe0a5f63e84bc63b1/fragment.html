House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01721
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A CEILING ON ALL PUBLIC DEBTS OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Public Debt Ceiling Act
ABSTRACT : "Public Debt Ceiling Act." The bill proposes to set a ceiling on the total public debt of the country at US$1.2 trillion which may not be exceeded except with the concurrence of two-thirds of all members of Congress voting separately.
