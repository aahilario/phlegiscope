House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04848
REPUBLIC ACT NO. RA09484
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REGULATE THE PRACTICE OF DENTISTRY, DENTAL HYGIENCE AND DENTAL TECHNOLOGY IN THE PHILIPPINES, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 4419, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS 'AN ACT TO REGULATE THE PRACTICE OF DENTISTRY IN THE PHILIPPINES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES', AND REPUBLIC ACT NO. 768, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS 'AN ACT TO REGULATE THE PRACTICE OF DENTAL HYGIENISTS IN THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES', APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "The Philippine Dental Act of 2005."
ABSTRACT : "The Philippine Dental Act of 2005". The bill enumerates and defines the scope of practice of dentists, dental hygienists and dental technologists.
