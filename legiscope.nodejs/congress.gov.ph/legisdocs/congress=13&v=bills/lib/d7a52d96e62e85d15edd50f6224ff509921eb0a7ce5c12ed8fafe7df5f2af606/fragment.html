HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB06094
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PUNISHING THE CRIME OF KIDNAPPING-FOR-RANSOM WITH AMPUTATION OF UPPER AND LOWER EXTREMITIES THEREBY AMENDING ARTICLE 267 OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The perpetrators of kidnap-for-ransom should be dealt with punishment as severe as the crime they commit. As a psychological deterrent, the measure proposes that the punishment of amputation of the upper and lower extremities be imposed.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SYJUCO, JUDY
