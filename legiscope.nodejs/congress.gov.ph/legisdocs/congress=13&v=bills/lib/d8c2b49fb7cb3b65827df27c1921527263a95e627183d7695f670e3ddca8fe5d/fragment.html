House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03272
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE POSSESSION OF TEN (10) GRAMS OF METHAMPHETAMINE HYDROCHLORIDE BY LIFE IMPRISONMENT TO DEATH, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE "COMPREHENSIVE DANGEROUS DRUGS ACT OF 2002", AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : To minimize, if not eradicate, the proliferation of illegal drugs, the bill penalizes the mere possession of 10 grams of shabu by life imprisonment to death.
