House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01254
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXTENDING PREFERENCE OF EMPLOYMENT TO ANY ONE CHILD OF VETERANS IN THE GOVERNMENT, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED AND/OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Extending Preference of Employment to Any One Child of Veterans in the Government
ABSTRACT : This bill declares it a policy of the State to enhance the socio-economic security and well-being of veterans and their families, thus all government agencies, including government-owned and/or controlled corporations are directed to hire or employ any child of a veteran. In the absence or disqualification of said child, a grandchild shall be hired or employed in his stead.
