House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02199
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MODERNIZATION OF THE NATIONAL MAPPING AND RESOURCE INFORMATION AUTHORITY (NAMRIA)
SHORT TITLE : NAMRIA Modernization Act
ABSTRACT : "NAMRIA Modernization Act." The bill seeks to improve the capabilities of the NAMRIA in the following areas: a) mapping; b) information technology; c) data acquisition; and d) research and development.
