HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01930
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3680 AND INSTITUTING MANDATORY GOVERNMENT SERVICE TO GRADUATES OF PHILIPPINE MERCHANT MARINE ACADEMY (PMMA) AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill exempts graduates of the PMMA who are holders of a Bachelor of Science in Marine Transportation from taking the Operational Level Deck Watchkeeping Officer Examination. It also exempts holders of a Bachelor of Science in Marine Engineering from taking the Operational Level Engine Watchkeeping Examination.
