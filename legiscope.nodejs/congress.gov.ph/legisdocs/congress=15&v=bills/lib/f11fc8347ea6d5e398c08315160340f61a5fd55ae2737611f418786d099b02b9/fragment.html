House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00093
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN AND ENHANCE THE USE OF ENGLISH AS THE MEDIUM OF INSTRUCTION IN PHILIPPINE SCHOOLS
SHORT TITLE : Strengthening And Enhancing The Use Of English As The Medium Of Instruction Act
ABSTRACT : “Strengthening and Enhancing the Use of English as the Medium of Instruction Act.” Aims to correct the defects of the current Bilingual Education Program of the Department of Education (DepEd) thus improving the learning process in school to ensure quality inputs in the educational program.
