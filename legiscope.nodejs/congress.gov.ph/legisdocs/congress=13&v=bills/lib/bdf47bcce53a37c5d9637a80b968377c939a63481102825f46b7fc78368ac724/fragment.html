House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04450
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING PRIVATE EDUCATION INSTITUTIONS TO ALLOT A CERTAIN NUMBER OF STUDENTS AS SCHOLARSHIP GRANTEES
SHORT TITLE : Private Educational Institutions Scholarship Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Private Educational Institutions Scholarship Act of 2005". The bill mandate all private educational institutions to grant scholarships to 10% of the student population per academic program and per branch or satellite maintained by the institution.
