HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01745
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE DETAIL OR ASSIGNMENT OF MILITARY PERSONNEL IN CIVILIAN OFFICES AND PUBLIC UTILITIES AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to terminate the services of military personnel in all civilian and public offices and to prohibit their detail or reassignment.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : PICHAY, PROSPERO JR. A.
