HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00313
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE RELEASE OF FUNDS DIRECTLY TO THE SANGGUNIANG BARANGAY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The Sangguniang Barangays, more often than not, consider it wiser to use scarce resources on projects and activities that are deemed "of greater benefit to the barangay" than Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) concerns. To correct the situation, the bill seeks to give the SK a free hand in the administration of its funds by authorizing the release of funds directly to the Sangguniang Barangay provided that the share of the SK shall likewise be released directly to the SK Treasurer. Releasing the funds directly to the SK give our youth some measure of autonomy that will empower them to implement projects and engage in activities that will build their character, mold them into becoming responsible citizens and equip them with the necessary skills to prepare them for their future role as community leaders.
