House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01614
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FOR THE PRESERVATION OF PHILIPPINE ANTIQUITIES
SHORT TITLE : The Antiquities Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "The Antiquities Act of 2004." The country's artistic and historic wealth constitute our cultural treasure. The bill seeks to protect and regulate the disposition of such cultural treasure. It grants the National Museum jurisdiction over ruins, archaeological sites, historic and prehistoric monuments and structures, and objects of antiquity.
