House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00052
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO CREATE A LOCAL HOUSING BOARD IN EVERY CITY AND MUNICIPALITY, PROVIDING FOR ITS POWERS AND DEFINING ITS DUTIES AND FUNCTIONS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Local Housing Board Act"
ABSTRACT : "Local Housing Board Act". The measure mandates the creation of local housing boards that shall formulate, develop, implement and monitor policies on the provision for housing and resettlement areas; and on the observance of the rights of the underprivileged and homeless to a just and humane eviction and demolition.
