HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03066
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE MAXIMUM AMOUNT FOR THE CONSTITUTION OF A FAMILY HOME UNDER THE FAMILY CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLE 157 THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill increases the maximum amount for the constitution of a family home from P300,00 to P2.0 million in urban areas, and from P200,00 to P1.5 million in rural areas in order to, at least, approximate the cost of building a decent house under prevailing market prices.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ABAYA, JOSEPH EMILIO A.
DATE FILED : 2004-10-04
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
