House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04892
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE IMPOUNDING OF MOTOR VEHICLES INVOLVED IN A VEHICULAR ACCIDENT THAT RESULTS IN THE DEATH OR SERIOUS PHYSICAL INJURY OF A PEDESTRIAN, COMMUTER OR BY-STANDER, UNLESS A CASH BOND IS POSTED
SHORT TITLE : Requiring the Impounding of Motor Vehicles Involved in a Vehicular Accident
ABSTRACT : The bill calls for the impounding of any motor vehicle involved in a vehicular incident resulting in the death or serious physical injuries to a pedestrian, commuter or by-stander pending the outcome of any criminal/civil case unless a cash bond of not less than P50,000 is posted.
