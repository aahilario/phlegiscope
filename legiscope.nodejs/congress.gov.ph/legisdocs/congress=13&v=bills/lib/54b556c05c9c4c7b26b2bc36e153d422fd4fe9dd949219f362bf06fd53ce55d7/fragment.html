HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00365
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING GOVERNMENT HOSPITALS TO UTILIZE ALL ITS INCOME FOR THEIR MAINTENANCE AND OTHER OPERATING EXPENSES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Government hospitals are often critized for being poorly maintained. The bill proposes to authorize government hospitals to utilize their income from pharmacy, fees from in-house services and facilities including but not limited to laboratory, radiology, operating room and nursery facilities and income derived from non-patient related services such as parking fees, tour fees, and interview fees for their maintenance and other operating expenditures including capital outlay and for the acquisition of necessary equipment.
