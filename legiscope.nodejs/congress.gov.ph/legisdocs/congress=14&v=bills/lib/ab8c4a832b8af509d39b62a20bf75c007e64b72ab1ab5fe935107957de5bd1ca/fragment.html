HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01348
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LIMITING THE AUTHORITY OF THE DEPUTY OMBUDSMAN FOR THE ARMED FORCES TO THE INVESTIGATION AND PROSECUTION OF COMPLAINTS AGAINST UNIFORMED MEMBERS OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6770, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS 'THE OMBUDSMAN ACT OF 1989'
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to delineate and limit the authority of the Deputy Ombudsman for the Armed Forces to the investigation and prosecution of complaints against uniformed members of the Armed Forces of the Philippines.
