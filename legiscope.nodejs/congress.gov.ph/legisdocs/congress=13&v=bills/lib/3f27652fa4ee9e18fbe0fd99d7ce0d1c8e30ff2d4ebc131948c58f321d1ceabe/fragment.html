House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02394
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING PROSTITUTION, IMPOSING PENALTIES ON ITS PERPETRATORS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Prostitution Act of 2002
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Prostitution Act of 2002." The bill seeks to penalize any person (not just women) who indulge in sexual or lascivious acts for money or profit. It also imposes penalties and sanctions on pimps, operators, agents who control, perpetuate and benefit from taking advantage, degrading and exploiting the vulnerability of others.
