HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01219
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING THE OPENING OF NIGHT CLASSES USING EXISTING HIGH SCHOOL AND ELEMENTARY SCHOOL FACILITIES ALL OVER THE COUNTRY AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : This bill directs all public elementary and high schools to allow their school facilities to be used as a venue for the holding of night high school classes. The compensation of the teachers of the night high school shall be computed on an hourly basis, taking into account the premium compensation rates provided by law for night time service and overtime work.
