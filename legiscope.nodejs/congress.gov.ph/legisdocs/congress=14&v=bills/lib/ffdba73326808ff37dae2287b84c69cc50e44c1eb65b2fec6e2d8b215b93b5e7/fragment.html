House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01152
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING THE MAGNA CARTA FOR DAY CARE WORKERS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Magna CArta for Day Care Workers"
ABSTRACT : "Magna CArta for Day Care Workers". The bill seeks to protect, promote and improve the social and economic status of day care workers by providing for their: a) salary; b) working hours; c) additional compensation and allowance; d) transfer and tenure; e) leave credit benefits; f) medical, insurance and retirement benefits; g) freedom to organize; and h) code of conduct.
