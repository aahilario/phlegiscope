FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING EQUAL MATERIAL BENEFITS TO UNWED FEMALE GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES
ABSTRACT : The measure grants unwed female government employees the same maternity benefits and privileges as those enjoyed by married employees.
