HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02446
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A NATIONAL SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM FOR COLLEGE STUDENTS IN SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY COURSES IN EVERY MUNICIPALITY OF EVERY PROVINCE OF THE COUNTRY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to grant annually scholarships in science and technology course to poor but deserving students. The fund assistance shall cover tuition and registration fees, book allowance, board and lodging fees or transportation allowance, and a monthly stipend for living expenses.
