HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03760
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING MINING ROYALTIES TO INDIGENOUS PEOPLE AND OTHER COMMUNITIES AFFECTED BY MINING OPERATIONS AND ANY OTHER ACTIVITY ARISING FROM THE EXPLORATION, UTILIZATION, EXTRACTION AND DEVELOPMENT OF ANY MINERAL RESOURCE FROM ANY MASS OF LAND OR PROPERTY INCLUDING BODIES OF WATER AND ANY NATURAL RESOURCE THAT THESE PEOPLE AND COMMUNITIES OWN, OCCUPY AND UTILIZE FOR THEIR SOCIAL, ECONOMIC, CULTURAL AND COMMON WELFARE, ESTABLISHING MECHANISMS FOR ITS PROPER PAYMENT, COLLECTION AND ADMINISTRATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Mining Profits Withholding Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Mining Profits Withholding Act of 2005." The bill prescribes the rules to be observed in the repatriation of the profits and capital of foreign mining companies. It prescribes the mining royalty rates to be granted to indigenous people and other communities affected by mining operations.
