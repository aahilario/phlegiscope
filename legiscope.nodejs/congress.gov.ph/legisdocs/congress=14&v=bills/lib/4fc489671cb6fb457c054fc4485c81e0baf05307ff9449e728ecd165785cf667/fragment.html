House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00818
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE PHILIPPINE COAST GUARD AUXILIARY, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREOF AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Philippne Coast Guard Auxiliary Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Philippne Coast Guard Auxiliary Act of 2007". The bill seeks to establish the role and mandate of the PCGA, to define the administrative powers of its officers over the organization and to institute mechanisms for a democratic procedure of selecting officers and commanders.
