HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02966
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING THE MAGNA CARTA FOR FILIPINO SEAFARERS
SHORT TITLE : "Magna Carta for Filipino Seafarers"
ABSTRACT : The measure establishes mechanisms for the enhancement of administrative, adjudicative, social and welfare services for Filipino seafarers and for their families. It also aims to maintain and develop of a pool of competent and world class seafarers of all grades and ratings through a system of education and training and of accreditation and licensing.
