HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00109
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE YEARLY ALLOCATIONS FOR SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNOLOGICAL ACTIVITIES (STA)
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for an increase in the allocation for scientific and technological activities (STAs), particularly research and development (R&D), to at least one percent (1%) of the gross domestic product (GDP), the prescribed UNESCO norm for developing countries.
