House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02767
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROFESSIONALIZING THE PRACTICE OF CIVIL ENGINEERING IN THE PHILIPPINES, REPEALING FOR THIS PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FIVE HUNDRED AND FORTY-FOUR (RA 544), AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Civil Engineering Law of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Civil Engineering Law of 2004." The bill seeks to achieve the following, among others: a) strengthen the profession and enable civil engineers to cope with the challenges brought about by globalization and cross-border practice, b) define the practice of civil engineering in the country by foreign nationals.
