HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02639
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXTENDING THE HOURS COVERED IN THE TERM 'NIGHT SHIFT', AMENDING SECTION 2 OF RULE II, BOOK III OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBER 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill extends the coverage of the night shift from eight (8) o'clock in the evening to six (6) o'clock in the morning.
