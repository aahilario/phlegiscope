House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03315
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING COMPENSATION TO VICTIMS OF HUMAN RIGHTS VIOLATIONS DURING THE MARCOS REGIME, DOCUMENTATION OF SAID VIOLATIONS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Human Rights Compensation Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Human Rights Compensation Act of 2004." The bill defines the terms "compensable human rights violation", "human rights violation", "compensable human rights violation victim", "agent of the State/person acting in an official capacity", as well as the source and manner of awarding compensation.
