HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00422
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR SCHOOLS, COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES WHICH REFUSE ADMISSION OF QUALIFIED SCHOLARS OF THE PHILIPPINE VETERANS AFFAIRS OFFICE
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to penalize schools that refuse enrolment of qualified Philippine Veterans Office scholars due to non-payment of school bills, with a fine at P10,000.00 for the 1st offense, P20,000.00 for the 2nd offense and P50,000.00 for the 3rd offense.
