House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00126
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PRESCRIBING STRICTER PENALTIES ON THE CRIME OF DELIVERING PRISONERS FROM JAIL AND INFIDELITY IN THE CUSTODY OF PRISONERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLES 156, 223 AND 224 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3815, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to increase the penalties on public official/s and employee/s who, during his/their tour of duty, deliberately allows or connives in the escape of prisoner/s in his/their custody.
