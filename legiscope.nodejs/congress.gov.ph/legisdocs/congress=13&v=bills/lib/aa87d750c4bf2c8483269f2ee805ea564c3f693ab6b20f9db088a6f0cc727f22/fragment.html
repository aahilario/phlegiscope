House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00121
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR PROTECTION TO CONSUMERS IN THEIR CREDIT CARD TRANSACTIONS
SHORT TITLE : Credit Card Consumer Protection Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Credit Card Consumer Protection Act of 2004". In view of continuing complaints raised against credit card companies for double charging, high interest, penalties, finance and other charges, there is a need to review the issuance of credit cards to protect the interests of consumers. This bill seeks to protect consumders from deceptive price increases for goods and services by prohibiting unfair credit card surcharges. It also aims to encourage the availability of discounts by retailers who wish to offer lower prices for goods and services purchased through the use of credit cards.
