House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04999
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 30 OF EVERY YEAR AS A DAY OF CELEBRATION TO COMMEMORATE PHILIPPINE FEMINISM
SHORT TITLE : Declaring June 30 of Every Year As a Day of Celebration to Commemorate Philippine Feminism
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to commemorate on June 30th of every year Philippine Feminism and the founding of the Asociacion Feminista Filipina, the first formal national feminist organization in the country.
