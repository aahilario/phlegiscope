House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01582
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE PRACTICE OF MINING ENGINEERING IN THE PHILIPPINES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO.4274
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Mining Engineering Act of 2003
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Mining Engineering Act of 2003." The bill seeks to amend and update the Mining Engineering Law, which was enacted in 1965, to make it more realistic, relevant and practicable to current professional practice of mine engineers. It also embodies provisions to reduce or eliminate illegal mining practices in mines and quarries.
