House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00740
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL HEALING, RECONCILIATION AND UNITY COMMISSION AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Healing, Reconciliation and Unity Act of 2003
ABSTRACT : "National Healing, Reconciliation and Unity Act of 2003." The bill proposes the establishment of a National Healing, Reconciliation and Unity Commission which shall have the power to resolve political conflicts and to implement a national reconciliation and unity program, formulate a mechanism to recover ill-gotten wealth of certain individuals and implement a mechanism for redress of victims of political conflicts and determine who shall be entitled to a political amnesty.
