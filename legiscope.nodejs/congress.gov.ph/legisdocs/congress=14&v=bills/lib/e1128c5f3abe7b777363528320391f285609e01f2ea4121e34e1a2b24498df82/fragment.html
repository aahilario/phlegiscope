House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00154
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING REFORMS IN REAL PROPERTY APPRAISAL AND ASSESSMENT PRACTICES IN THE PHILIPPINES, CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE THE NATIONAL APPRAISAL AUTHORITY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Real Property Appraisal and Assessment Reform Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The Real Property Appraisal and Assessment Reform Act of 2007". The measure calls for the establishment of a National Appraisal Authority that shall set valuation standards, regulations and specifications consistent with generally accepted valuation standards for real property appraisal; provide technical assistance on real property appraisal matters; and review and approve the schedule of market values; among others.
