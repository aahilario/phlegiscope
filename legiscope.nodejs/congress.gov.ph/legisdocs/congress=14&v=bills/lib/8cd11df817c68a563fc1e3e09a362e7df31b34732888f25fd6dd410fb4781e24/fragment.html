HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03127
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING GRATUITY PAY TO ALL DULY ELECTED BARANGAY CAPTAINS AND BARANGAY COUNCIL MEMBERS, WHO HAVE COMPLETELY SERVED THREE CONSECUTIVE TERMS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Barangay Council Gratuity Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to grant elected barangay officials who have served for three (3) consecutive terms gratuity pay amounting to P200,000 for barangay captains and P150,000 for barangay council members.
