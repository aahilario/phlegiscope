House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00274
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING YOUTH CENTERS IN ALL FIFTEEN (15) REGIONS OF THE COUNTRY, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Youth Center Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Youth Center Act of 2007". The bill calls for the establishment of an integrated youth center in every region of the country that shall serve as a transient shelter, lodging facility and skills training center for the youth who are in need of immediate assistance and attention.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ANTONINO-CUSTODIO, DARLENE MAGNOLIA R.
