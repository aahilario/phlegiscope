House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04714
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY 23 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE ENTIRE COUNTRY TO COMMEMORATE THE DECLARATION OF THE FIRST PHILIPPINE REPUBLIC
SHORT TITLE : Declaring January 23 of Every Year a Special Nonworking Holiday in the Entire Country to Commemorate the Declaration of the First Philippine Republic
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to declare January 23 of every year as a special non-working holiday to be known as "The Declaration of the First Philippine Republic Day".
