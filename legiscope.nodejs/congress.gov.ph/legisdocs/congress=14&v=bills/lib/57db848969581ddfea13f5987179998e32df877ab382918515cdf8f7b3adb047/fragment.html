House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00247
FULL TITLE : CARPARK REGULATION LAW OF 2007
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to regulate the operation of carparks to assure the public of free and easy access to parking spaces. It will also guarantee that vehicles will be well-protected from theft and other premeditated damages.
