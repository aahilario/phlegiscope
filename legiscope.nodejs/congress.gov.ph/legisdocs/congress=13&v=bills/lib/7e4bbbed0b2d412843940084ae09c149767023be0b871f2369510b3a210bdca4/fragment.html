House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00107
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE TAX ON IDLE LANDS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE, SECTION 236 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The country has a total land area of 30 million hectares, 47% of which are classified as alienable and disposable. The bill seeks to increase the ceiling tax on idle lands from five percent (5%) to ten percent (10%) to give local government units more flexibility in the imposition of the tax. The current maximum of five percent (5%) has not proven to be deterrent against the non-utilization of agricultural lands for productive purposes. The idle land tax is in addition to the basic real property tax.
