HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03813
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING INDEPENDENT HEALTH AND ENVIRONMENT ASSESSMENT FOR ALL MINING PROJECTS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE PHILIPPINE MINING ACT OF 1995
ABSTRACT : The bill requires that an independent health and environmental assessment of the communities surrounding a mine site be conducted every 5 years from the start of mining operations until 10 years after its closure. An independent Assessment Fund shall be created for the purpose and deposited as a trust fund in a government depository bank.
