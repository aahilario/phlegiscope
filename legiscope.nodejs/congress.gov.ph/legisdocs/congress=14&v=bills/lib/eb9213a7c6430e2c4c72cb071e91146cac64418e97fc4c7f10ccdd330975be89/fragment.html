HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02431
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING THE CREATION OF A SCHOLARSHIP FUND IN EVERY PRIVATE SCHOOL, COLLEGE AND UNIVERSITY WHICH HAS BEEN OPERATING PROFITABLY AT LEAST FOR THE LAST 10 YEARS FOR THE BENEFIT OF POOR BUT DESERVING STUDENTS
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes a scholarship program in every private school and university to be funded from at least 5% of the school's annual net profit.
