HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01251
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE RECORDING, BROADCASTING, AIRING AND/OR PUBLICATION OF A CONFESSION OR ADMISSION BY A PERSON ACCUSED OF A CRIME WITHOUT AN EXPRESSED WAIVER IN THE PRESENCE OF COUNSEL, PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits a person from recording by means of video, compact disc, tape and other devices any confession and/or admission to a commission of a crime by a person arrested or under investigation, detention or custodial interrogation for the same crime unless such person waives or consents to the recording in writing in the presence of his counsel.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : BICHARA, AL FRANCIS D.
