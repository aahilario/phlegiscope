HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01964
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DIRECTING ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES, ENTITIES, INSTRUMENTALITIES, AND SUBDIVISIONS, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT OWNED OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS TO TAKE APPROPRIATE ACTION WITHIN THE PRESCRIBED PERIOD HEREIN PROVIDED, ON ALL KINDS OF APPLICATION FOR PERMITS, LICENSES, CERTIFICATIONS, CLEARANCES, AND OTHER SIMILAR ISSUANCES RELATING TO AND NECESSARY FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT AND OPERATIONS OF BUSINESSES AND INVESTMENTS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Red Tape Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Red Tape Act of 2004." The bill prescribes the following waiting period for the issuance of business permits: a) barangay-one day to 30 days; b) city or municipality-one day to 45 days; c) province-15 days to 60 days ; and d) national agencies-15 days to 120 days.
