FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR COMPENSATION TO THE FILIPINO VICTIMS OF WARTIME SEXUAL COERCION, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "FILIPINO COMFORT WOMEN," DURING THE JAPANESE OCCUPATION IN THE PHILIPPINES IN THE SECOND WORLD WAR, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Filipino Comfort Women Compensation Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Filipino Comfort Women Compensation Act of 2005." The bill authorizes the appropriation of P250 million to fund, in advance, the monetary compensation for women.
