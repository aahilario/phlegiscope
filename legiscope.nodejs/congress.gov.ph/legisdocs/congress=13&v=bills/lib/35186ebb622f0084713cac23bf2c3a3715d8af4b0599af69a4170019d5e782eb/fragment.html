House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01820
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A FARM MACHINERIES MANUFACTURING PROGRAMS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Farm Machineries Manufacturing Program (FMMP) Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Farm Machineries Manufacturing Program (FMMP) Act of 2004." This bill provides for the establishment of a comprehensive program that shall introduce mechanized farming to boast production. It encourages local manufacturers to enter into a joint venture with foreign companies that shall assist and train them with the end in view of enabling them to maximize the utilization of local materials in their products.
