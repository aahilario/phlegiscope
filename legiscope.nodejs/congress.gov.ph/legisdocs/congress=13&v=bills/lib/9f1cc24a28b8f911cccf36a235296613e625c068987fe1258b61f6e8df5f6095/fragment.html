House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03208
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF THE INDETERMINATE SENTENCE LAW, AS AMENDED, BY PROVIDING FOR THE MANDATORY PAROLE OF PRISONERS WHO SHALL HAVE SERVED AT LEAST FIVE (5) YEARS OF HIS/HER SENTENCE WITH GOOD BEHAVIOR
ABSTRACT : To decongest our penal institutions, the bill provides for the mandatory parole of prisoners who have served at least five years of his/her sentence with good behavior.
