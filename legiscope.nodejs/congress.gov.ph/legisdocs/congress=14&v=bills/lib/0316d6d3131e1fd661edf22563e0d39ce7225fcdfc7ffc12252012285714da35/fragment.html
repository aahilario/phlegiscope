House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01711
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE FILIPINO ACADEMY OF ARTS, LETTERS AND PHILOSOPHY, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Filipino Academy of Arts, Letters and Philosophy Act"
ABSTRACT : "Filipino Academy of Arts, Letters and Philosophy Act". The bill establishes the Filipino Academy of Arts, Letters and Philosophy that shall plan, formulate and implement programs for the conservation, development, enhancement and propagation of Filipino arts, letters and philosophy.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ESCUDERO, SALVADOR III H.
