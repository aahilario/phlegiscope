House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03824
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CALLING FOR A CONSTITUTIONAL CONVENTION TO STUDY AND APPROVE PROPOSED AMENDMENTS TO, OR REVISIONS OF, THE 1987 CONSTITUTION, PROVIDING FOR PROPORTIONAL REPRESENTATION THEREIN AND OTHER DETAILS RELATING TO THE HOLDING OF THE CONVENTION, SETTING THE DATE OF ELECTION OF DELEGATES, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The Constitutional Convention Act
ABSTRACT : "The Constitutional Convention Act." The measure prescribes the composition of the Constitutional convention, the qualifications and election of delegates, their compensation, rules and quorum.
