HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00525
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING RETIREMENT BENEFITS TO ALL ELECTIVE OR APPOINTIVE BARANGAY OFFICIALS WHO HAVE RENDERED A CONSECUTIVE PERIOD OF NINE (9)YEARS OR THREE (3) TERMS OF PUBLIC SERVICE IN THE BARANGAY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill entitles all elective and appointive barangay officials, who have rendered a consecutive period of nine (9) years or three (3) terms of honorable service, to receive a monthly pension for life amounting to not less than 65% of the monthly average honoraria, allowances and other emoluments authorized by law or barangay, municipal or city ordinance.
