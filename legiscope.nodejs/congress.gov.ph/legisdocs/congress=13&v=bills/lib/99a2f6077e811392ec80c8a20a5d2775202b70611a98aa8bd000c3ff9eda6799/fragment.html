HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00266
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LIMITING THE PRACTICE OF COSMETIC DERMATOLOGY TO LICENSED AND QUALIFIED PHYSICIANS WITH RESIDENCY TRAINING IN DERMATOLOGY
ABSTRACT : A significant number of facial care clinics and salons have sprouted and many of these do not have qualified cosmetic surgeons to perform services like facial restructuring and breast augmentation. There have been reports that the practice has resulted to death or caused facial contusions to patients. To protect and promote the right to health of the people, the bill seeks to limit the practice of cosmetic dermatology to licensed and qualified physicians with residency training in dermatology or plastic surgery.
