House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01333
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN AND PROPAGATE FOSTER CARE FOR ABUSED, ABANDONED, NEGLECTED AND OTHER CHILDREN WITH SPECIAL NEEDS, PROVIDING APPROPRIATIONS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Foster Care Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Foster Care Act of 2007". The bill seeks to institutionalize foster caring in the country. It provides for a comprehensive legal framework within which the welfare and interests of those needing foster care and those who can provide it can be harmonized.
