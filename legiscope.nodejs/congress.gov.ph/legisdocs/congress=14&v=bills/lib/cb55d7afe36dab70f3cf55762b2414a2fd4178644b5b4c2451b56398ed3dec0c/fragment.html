HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00279
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ENSURING OBJECTIVITY AND THE PROTECTION OF WITNESSES IN THE PROSECUTION OF CASES BY THE OMBUDSMAN AND ALLOWING PRIVATE LAWYERS TO ACT AS PROSECUTORS ON ITS BEHALF, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 15 AND 31 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6770, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE OMBUDSMAN ACT OF 1989," AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to allow the Office of the Ombudsman to deputize qualified private lawyers as prosecutors. It further provides that in appropriate criminal cases, the Ombudsman may petition the Supreme Court to ensure the protection of the witnesses.
