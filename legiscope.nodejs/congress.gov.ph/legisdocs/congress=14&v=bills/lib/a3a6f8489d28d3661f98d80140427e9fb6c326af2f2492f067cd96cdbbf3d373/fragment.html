HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02536
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING CIVIL SERVICE PROFESSIONAL ELIGIBILITY TO ALL PERMANENT EMPLOYEES, WHO HAVE RENDERED 15 STRAIGHT YEARS OF SERVICES BY YEAR 2008
SHORT TITLE : "Civil Service and Professional Eligibility for Permanent Government Employees Act"
ABSTRACT : To reward them for their hardwork and dedication to public service, the bill grants professional civil service to all permanent government employees who have rendered 15 straight years of service by the year 2008.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : LAZATIN, CARMELO F.
