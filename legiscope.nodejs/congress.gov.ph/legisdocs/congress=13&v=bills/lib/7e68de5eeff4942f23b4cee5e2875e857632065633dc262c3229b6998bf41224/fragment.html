House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02978
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING A NATIONAL LAND USE POLICY, PROVIDING THE IMPLEMENTING MECHANISMS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : National Land Use Act of the Philippines
ABSTRACT : "National Land Use Act of the Philippines." The bill provides guidelines and criteria for land use based on an assessment of the development needs of various sectors. It provides for the establishment of crucial priorities for land use, especially areas to be protected for ecological purposes and areas to be developed and protected from conversion to ensure food security.
