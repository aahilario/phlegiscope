HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01487
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AND PROVIDING FOR THE RATIONALIZATION OF WAGE LEVELS ON A NATIONAL OR INDUSTRIAL BASIS
ABSTRACT : The bill abolishes the Regional Tripartite Wages and Productivity Board and replaces it with the National Wages and Productivity Commission which shall determine and fix national minimum wage rates according to industries and issue the corresponding wage order.
