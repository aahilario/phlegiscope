House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01937
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING SENIOR CITIZENS WARD IN PRIVATE AND PUBLIC HOSPITALS OPERATING IN THE PHILIPPINES AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill establishes the Senior Citizens' Ward in all hospitals that shall provide the elderly, who barely have the means, access to health and medical services.
