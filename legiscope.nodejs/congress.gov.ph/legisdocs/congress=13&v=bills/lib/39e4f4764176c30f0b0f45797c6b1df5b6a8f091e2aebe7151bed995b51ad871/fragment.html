House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02224
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE POSTPONEMENT OF THE SYNCHRONIZED BARANGAY AND SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN ELECTIONS SET FOR THE YEAR 2005 TO THE SECOND MONDAY OF MAY 2007 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill provides for the postponement of the synchronized barangay and sangguniang kabataan elections set for the year 2005 to the second Monday of May 2007, and every three (3) years thereafter.
