House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03251
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A FREE MEDICINE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM FOR THE POOR AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Free Medicine Assistance Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Free Medicine Assistance Act of 2004." The bill seeks to establish the program to address the health care problem of the country. The beneficiaries of the program shall be identified by the Department of Health through the government hospital in coordination with the Department of Social Welfare.
