FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE WORKERS' CONSTITUTIONAL RIGHT TO SELF-ORGANIZATION, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : Strengthening the Workers' Constitutional Right to Self-Organization
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to protect the rights of workers to self-organization by preventing undue interference and expediting the process of registration and certification of labor organizations. Allows rank and file and supervisory employees to join the same federation or national union, provides that the co-mingling of rank and file and supervisory employees in the same federation is not a ground for the cancellation of registration and encourages affiliation with federations and national unions.
