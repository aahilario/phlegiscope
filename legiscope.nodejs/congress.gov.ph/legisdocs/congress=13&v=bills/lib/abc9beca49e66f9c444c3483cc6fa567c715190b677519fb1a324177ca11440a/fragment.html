House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03202
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PRESCRIBING REQUIRED MINIMUM KNOWLEDGE AND COMPETENCIES (RMKC) FOR EVERY CURRICULAR SUBJECT IN EVERY GRADE AND YEAR LEVEL IN BASIC EDUCATION IN ALL PUBLIC ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY SCHOOLS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Required Minimum Knowledge and Competencies in Basic Education or 'RMKC' Act
ABSTRACT : "Required Minimum Knowledge and Competencies in Basic Education or 'RMKC' Act." The bill mandates the administration nationwide of a standardized test to determine and assess the competency of students.
