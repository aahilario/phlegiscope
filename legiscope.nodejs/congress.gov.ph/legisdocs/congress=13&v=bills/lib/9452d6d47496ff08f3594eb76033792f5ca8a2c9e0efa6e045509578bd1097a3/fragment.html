HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01936
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING LOCAL CHIEF EXECUTIVES FROM MAKING TEMPORARY APPOINTMENTS AND/OR DESIGNATIONS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Provides that appointments made by local chief executives shall be valid only for six months to prevent them from extending it indefinitely in violation of the career service rule.

DATE FILED : 2004-07-30
