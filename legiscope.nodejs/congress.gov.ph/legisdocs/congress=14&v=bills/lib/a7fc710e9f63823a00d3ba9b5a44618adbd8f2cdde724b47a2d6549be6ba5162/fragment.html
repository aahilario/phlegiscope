HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01870
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING A PROGRAM FOR THE CONTINUING EDUCATION FOR COLLEGE TEACHERS, AMENDING FOR SUCH PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7784, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "AN ACT TO STRENGTHENING TEACHER EDUCATION IN THE PHILIPPINES BY ESTABLISHING CENTERS OF EXCELLENCE, CREATING A TEACHER EDUCATION COUNCIL FOR THE PURPOSE, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill requires all primary, secondary and college teachers to undergo training in professional education, general education and teaching methods at least once every five years.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : DEFENSOR, MATIAS JR. V.
DATE FILED : 2007-08-07
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
