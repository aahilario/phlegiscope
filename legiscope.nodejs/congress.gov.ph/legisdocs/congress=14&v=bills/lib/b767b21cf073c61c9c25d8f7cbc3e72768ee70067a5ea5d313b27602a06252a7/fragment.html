House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03388
FULL TITLE : AN ACT UPHOLDING THE RIGHT TO TRAVEL AND PROVIDING GROUNDS FOR TRAVEL RESTRICTIONS
SHORT TITLE : "Right to Travel Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill explicitly provides that the right to travel can only be restricted upon a clear showing of reasonable and compelling reasons that the proposed travel is harmful to national security, public safety and public health.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.
