HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00698
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REPEALING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 217, AS AMENDED, ENTITLED, 'ESTABLISHING BASIC POLICIES FOR THE TELEPHONE INDUSTRY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE PERTINENT PROVISIONS OF COMMONWEALTH ACT', THE FRANCHISE OF THE PHILIPPINE LONG DISTANCE TELEPHONE COMPANY UNDER ACT NO. 3426, AS AMENDED, AND ALL INCONSISTENT LEGISLATIVE AND MUNICIPAL FRANCHISE INCLUDING OTHER EXISTING LAWS
ABSTRACT : The bill repeals the Subscriber Investment Plan (SIP)establsihed under PD 217. The SIP is deemed redundant considering that the country now has have quite number of telephone service providers. With the advent of the mobile phone industry, consumers have a wide choice for their telecommunication needs.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ROMUALDO, JURDIN JESUS M.
