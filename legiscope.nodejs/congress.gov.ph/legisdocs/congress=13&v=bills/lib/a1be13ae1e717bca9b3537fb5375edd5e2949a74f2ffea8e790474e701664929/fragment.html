House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04489
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE TITLING, APPROPRIATION, ENCROACHMENT, AND REGISTRATION OF CREEKS, ESTEROS, STREAMS, CANALS, RIVER BANKS AND OTHER PUBLIC WATERWAYS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Penalizing the Titling, Appropriation, Encroachment, and Registration of Creeks, Esteros, Streams, Canals, River Banks and Other Public Waterways
ABSTRACT : The measure imposes a penalty of imprisonment of not less than six months and one day to six years or a fine of not less than P5,000 or both on any person who encroaches upon and/ or registers a title over any creek, canal, estero, stream and other similar public waterways.
