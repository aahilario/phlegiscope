House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01827
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE PROMOTION OF BREASTFEEDING PRACTICES AND INSTITUTING MEASURES THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Breastfeeding Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Breastfeeding Act of 2007". The bill seeks to promote breastfeeding to improve the health of children. It provides for lactation support programs and rooming in provision in every healthcare facility. It also prohibits the advertising or promotions of infant formula for those between 0-12 months.
