House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00159
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PRE-NEED CODE OF 2007
SHORT TITLE : "Pre-Need Code of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Pre-Need Code of 2007". This bill seeks to provide the legislative framework that will govern the pre-need industry, provide the regulatory standards within the industry to level the playing field, strengthen the Insurance Commission's regulatory capabilities to insure that the industry does not become a forum for abuse and provide for the effective protection of planholders.
