House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02754
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING POLITICAL TURNCOATISM OF ELECTED PUBLIC OFFICIALS AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits the changing of political party affiliation by any public official, except barangay officials, during his term of office except upon merger of political parties, abolition of political party or expulsion of a member from his political party.
