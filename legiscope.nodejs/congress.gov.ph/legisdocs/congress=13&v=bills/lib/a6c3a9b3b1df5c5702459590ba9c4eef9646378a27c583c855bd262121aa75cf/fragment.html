HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00448
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PRESCRIBING THE GEOGRAPHICAL REQUIREMENTS IN THE BIDDING AND AWARDING OF CONTRACTS FOR GOVERNMENT INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1594
ABSTRACT : In addition to the legal, technical and financial prequalification requirements of prospective contractors spelled out in PD 1594, the bill prescribes certain geographical requirements such that project contractors based in Luzon may only be qualified to undertake government infrastructure projects within Luzon. The same requirement shall apply to contractors based in the Visayas and Mindanao.
