HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02912
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 857, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED CHARTER OF THE PHILIPPINE PORTS AUTHORITY (PPA), AS AMENDED, TO STRENGTHEN AND STREAMLINE THE AUTHORITY, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to: a) enhance competition and efficiency in port infrastructure development and services; b) promote private sector participation in the development of seaports; c) encourage decentralization of local port management offices; and d) decouple the Philippine Port Authority's regulatory and commercial functions.
