HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00486
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE PRE-BLEND OF LOCALLY-PRODUCED METHYL ESTER INTO DIESEL FUEL TO PROMOTE CLEAN AIR AND BOOST RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT FOR RENEWABLE FUELS TO REDUCE IMPORT DEPENDENCE, OPEN A NEW EXPORT REVENUE, AND PROVIDE FUEL SECURITY FOR THE COUNTRY IN TIMES OF GLOBAL FUEL SHORTAGE
SHORT TITLE : "Biodiesel Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Biodiesel Act of 2007". The bill requires a minimum blend of 1% coconut methyl ester in all locally refined and imported diesel fuel sold in the country.
