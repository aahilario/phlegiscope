House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01628
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR SCHOLARSHIPS AND OTHER BENEFITS FOR PUBLIC AND PRIVATE SCHOOL TEACHERS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the creation of a scholarship fund to cover the tuition and other regular school fees, including textbooks, of public and private school teachers who wish to enroll in graduate and post graduate courses within the country.
