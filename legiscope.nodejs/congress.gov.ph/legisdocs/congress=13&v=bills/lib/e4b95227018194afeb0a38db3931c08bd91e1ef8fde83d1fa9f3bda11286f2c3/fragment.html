House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02900
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE OVERSEAS SEAFARERES WELFARE FUND, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Seafarers' Welfare Fund of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Seafarers' Welfare Fund of 2004." The bill creates the Welfare Fund to provide welfare services to Filipino seafarers working abroad. It seeks to grant seafarers working abroad life and personal accident insurance amounting to P1.0 million and P300,000, respectively.
