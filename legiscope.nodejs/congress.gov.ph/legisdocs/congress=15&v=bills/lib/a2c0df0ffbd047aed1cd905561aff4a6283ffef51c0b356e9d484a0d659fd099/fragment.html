House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00149
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE INTERNAL REVENUE ALLOTMENT OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS (LGUs) AND AUTHORIZING ITS AUTOMATIC RETENTION, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 284 AND 286 OF R.A. No. 7160 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : Increases the Internal Revenue Allotment (IRA) from forty (40%) per cent to sixty (60%) per cent to stimulate economic and social progress in the countryside.
