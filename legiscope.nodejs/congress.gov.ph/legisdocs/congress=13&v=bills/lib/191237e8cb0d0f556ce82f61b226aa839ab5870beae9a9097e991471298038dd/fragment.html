House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03599
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MODERNIZATION OF THE PHILIPPINE ATMOSPHERIC, GEOPHYSICAL AND ASTRONOMICAL SERVICES ADMINISTRATION, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : PAG-ASA Modernization Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "PAG-ASA Modernization Act of 2005." The bill seeks to: a) establish regional weather service centers in strategic areas; b) modernize its physical resources and operational techniques; c) intensify human resource development; and d) upgrade its research and development capability.
