House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04637
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE OPERATION AND MANAGEMENT OF CANTEENS, SUPPLY AND BOOKSTORES, AND OTHER SERVICES BY TEACHERS OR GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES' COOPERATIVES IN PUBLIC SCHOOL, OR GOVERNMENT INSTITUTIONS OR AGENCIES, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT OWNED OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREOF AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Teachers and Government Employees' Cooperatives Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Teachers and Government Employees' Cooperatives Act of 2005". The measure provides a vehicle for employees and teachers to organize themselves into viable economic groups and augment their income through the benefits and privileges derived from cooperatives.
