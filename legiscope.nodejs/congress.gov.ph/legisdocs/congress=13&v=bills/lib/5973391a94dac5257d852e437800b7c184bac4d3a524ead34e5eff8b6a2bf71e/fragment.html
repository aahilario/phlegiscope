HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01297
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING PRE-AUDIT ON ALL GOVERNMENT PROGRAMS AND PROJECTS WITH FUNDING REQUIREMENTS BEFORE IMPLEMENTATION AND PRESCRIBING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF
ABSTRACT : This bill declares that any official or employee who approves or allows the implementation of a certain program or project with government funding without undergoing pre-audit shall for the first offense, suffer the penalty of suspension from office for a period of not less than fifteen (15) days nor more than sixty (60) days; for second of succeeding offenses, the penalty of imprisonment of not less than six (6) months but not more than six (6) years shall be imposed on the offender, plus perpetual disqualification to hold public office.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ANTONINO-CUSTODIO, DARLENE MAGNOLIA R.
