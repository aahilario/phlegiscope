House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00784
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ANNUAL MANDATORY DRUG TEST FOR ALL ELECTED GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION THIRTY-SIX OF REPUBLIC ACT NINETY-ONE HUNDRED SIXTY-FIVE, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'COMPREHENSIVE DANGEROUS DRUGS ACT OF 2002'
ABSTRACT : The bill requires all elective government officials except the President and the Vice President to undergo a mandatory annual drug test.
