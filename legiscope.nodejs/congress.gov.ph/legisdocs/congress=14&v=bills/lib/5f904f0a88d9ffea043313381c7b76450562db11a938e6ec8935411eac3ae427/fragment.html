HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00277
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING TUITION AND OTHER SCHOOL FEES IN ALL HIGHER, BASIC AND TECHNICAL-VOCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS OF LEARNING, THEREBY AMENDING BATAS PAMBANSA 232, AS AMENDED, CREATING A TUITION FEE REGULATORY BOARD, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to regulate tuition fee increases in all schools. It provides for the creation of a Tuition Fee Regulatory Board that shall study, conduct consultation and thereafter recommend tuition and other school fee adjustments.
