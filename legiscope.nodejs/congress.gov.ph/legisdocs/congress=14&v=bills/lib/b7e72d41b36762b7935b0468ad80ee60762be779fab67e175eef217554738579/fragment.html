HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02490
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING ARTICLE 66 OF THE CIVIL CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES IN ORDER TO PRESCRIBE ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS FOR MALE CITIZENS OR SUBJECTS OF A FOREIGN COUNTRY DESIRING TO MARRY A FILIPINO WOMAN
ABSTRACT : The bill requires a foreigner who intends to marry a Filipina to provide himself with a certificate of good moral character and a certificate that he has a gainful trade, business or employment issued by his country's diplomatic or consular official, in addition to the usual certificate of legal capacity.
