HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01252
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN EDUCATION TRUST FUND FOR GRANTEES OF GSIS AND SSS MEMBERS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Education Trust Fund Act
ABSTRACT : "Education Trust Fund Act." This bill defines the fund as the accumulated money deducted from GSIS and the SSS members and paid to the system for the education of the grantees, then paid to colleges or universities for a vocational, technical or baccalaureate program as provided in the policy contract.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : EDUARDO V. ROQUERO, M.D.
DATE FILED : 2004-07-07
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
