House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00117
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE REGULATION OF INVESTMENT COMPANIES
SHORT TITLE : Investment Company Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Investment Company Act of 2004". The measure seeks to amend RA 2629 to provide for a more conducive regulatory environment for the development of investment companies including mutual funds. It also proposes the establishment of the Investment Company Investors Protection Fund which shall compensate investors for financial injury they may suffer due to reasons other than market decline in the investment portfolio of the company.
