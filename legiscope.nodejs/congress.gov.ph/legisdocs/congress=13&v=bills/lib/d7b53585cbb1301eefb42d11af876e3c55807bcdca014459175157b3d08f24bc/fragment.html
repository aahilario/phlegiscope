HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01645
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CENTER FOR TEACHER'S EXCELLENCE ALL THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the Department of Education, in coordination with the Commission Higher Education, to establish, operate and maintain a Center for Teachers' Excellence that shall be charged to develop and maintain a continuing program to upgrade the skills of teachers.
