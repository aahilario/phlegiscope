HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05068
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOCATING TO THE AFP, BFP, BJMP AND PNP FOR MODERNALIZATION PURPOSE THE SHARE OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT IN ALL TAXES, ROYALTIES AND CHARGES COLLECTED FROM THE MALAMPAYA NATURAL GAS PROJECT
ABSTRACT : The bill provides that the proceeds of taxes, charges, royalties collected from the Malampaya Natural Gas Project due the national government be directly remitted to the Armed Forces of the Philippines, the Bureau of Fire Protection, the Bureau of Jail Management and Penology and the Philippine National Police.
