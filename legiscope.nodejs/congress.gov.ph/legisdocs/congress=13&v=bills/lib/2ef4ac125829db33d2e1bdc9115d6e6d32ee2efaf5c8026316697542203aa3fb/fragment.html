House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01480
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE NEGATIVE PORTRAYAL OF A PERSON WITH A DISABILITY IN MOVIES, TELEVISION, STAGE AND OTHER FORMS OF MASS COMMUNICATION
SHORT TITLE : Media Protection for the Disabled Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Media Protection for the Disabled Act of 2004." The bill declares it unlawful to mock, embarass, humiliate, or insult a disabled person in mass media.
