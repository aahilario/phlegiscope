House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04796
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE LEGITIMATION OF CHILDREN BORN TO PARENTS BELOW MARRYING AGE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE FAMILY CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the legitimation of children born out of wedlock to parents disqualified by law to marry by reason of minority in age.
