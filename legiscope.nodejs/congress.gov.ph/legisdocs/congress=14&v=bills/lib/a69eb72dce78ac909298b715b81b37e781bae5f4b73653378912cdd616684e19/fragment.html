FULL TITLE : AN ACT CONVERTING IDLE PUBLIC LANDS INTO TREE PLANTATIONS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to convert idle lands into tree plantations to protect our people from floods and landslides, water shortages, loss of bio-diversity, air pollution and drought, among others.
