House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00012
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR STIFFER PENALTIES FOR CRIMES RELATING TO FIREARMS AND LIGHT WEAPONS, FURTHER AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1866, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8294, AND FOR RELEVANT PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Seeks to regulate the unlawful possession, manufacture, dealing in, acquisition or disposition, of firearms, ammunition and provide stiffer penalties of the same in order to effectively deter the commission of crimes with the use of firearms.
