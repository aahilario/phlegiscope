House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00061
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A COMPREHENSIVE REVISION OF THE CHARTER OF THE BANGKO SENTRAL NG PILIPINAS (BSP)BY AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SEVENTY-SIX HUNDRED AND FIFTY-THREE ENTITLED "THE NEW CENTRAL BANK ACT"
ABSTRACT : Directs the National Government to pay in cash available to the Government or through the issuance of readily marketable government securities of equivalent market value to settle the balance of P40 Billion unpaid subscription of the Government to the Bangko Sentral, among other proposed amendments in the measure.
