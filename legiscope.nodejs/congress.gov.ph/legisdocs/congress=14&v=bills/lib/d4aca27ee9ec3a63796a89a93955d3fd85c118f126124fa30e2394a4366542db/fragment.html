House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02785
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR MANDATORY HEPATITIS B VACCINATION OF ALL INFANTS WITHIN A REASONABLE PERIOD OF TIME AFTER BIRTH, AND BASIC IMMUNIZATION AND VACCINATION
SHORT TITLE : "Mandatory Infant-Health Immunization Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes a mandatory basic immunization service for infants, to wit: a) BCG vaccination against tuberculosis; b) inoculation against diphtheria, tetanus and pertussis; c) oral poliomyetitis immunization; d) protection against measles; e) immunization against hepatitis B; f) immunization against rubella; and g) other basic immunization services.
