House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01431
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PHILIPPINE CEREALS RESEARCH, DEVELOPMENT AND EXTENSION INSTITUTE (PHILCEREAL), AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Cereals Research, Development and Extension Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Cereals Research, Development and Extension Act of 2004." The measure creates the Institute "to transform the cereals sub-sector industry from being resource-based to technology-based." It seeks to expand the coverage of the Philippine Rice Research Institute to include other grains.
