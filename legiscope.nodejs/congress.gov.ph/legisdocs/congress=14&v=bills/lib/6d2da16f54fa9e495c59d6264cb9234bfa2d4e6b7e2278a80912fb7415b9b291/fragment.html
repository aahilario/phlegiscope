House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02962
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE PRACTICE OF MINING ENGINEERING IN THE PHILIPPINES, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOUR THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED SEVENTY FOUR (R.A. NO. 4274) AS AMENDED OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "MINING ENGINEERING LAW OF THE PHILIPPINES", AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Mining Engineering Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for: a) the examination, registration and licensure of mining engineering; b) the supervision, control and regulation of the practice of mining engineering (ME); c) the development and upgrading of the ME curriculum; and d) continuing professional education for mining engineers.
