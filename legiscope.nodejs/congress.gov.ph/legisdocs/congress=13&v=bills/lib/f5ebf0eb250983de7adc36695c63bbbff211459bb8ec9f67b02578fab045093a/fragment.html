House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01497
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROMOTING VOLUNTARY BLOOD DONATION, PROVIDING FOR AN ADEQUATE SUPPLY OF SAFE BLOOD, REGULATING BLOOD BANKS AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : Blood Services Act of 1992
ABSTRACT : "Blood Services Act of 1992." The bill aims to make blood readily available to hospitals and patients by creating the National Blood Services Program under the Department of Health, Philippine National Red Cross, and the Philippine Blood Coordinating Council which shall establish blood collection centers nationwide.
