HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00165
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING MECHANISMS FOR THE REDUCTION OF THE DISTRIBUTION OF MERCURY-ADDED PRODUCTS AND ITS PROPER DISPOSAL THEREOF IN ORDER TO REDUCE THE INTRODUCTION OF MERCURY INTO THE ENVIRONMENT
SHORT TITLE : "Mercury Reduction Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Mercury Reduction Act of 2007". The bill seeks to address the global issue of mercury pollution, to wit: a) switch of mercury-using products and processes to non-mercury alternatives; b) control of mercury release; and c) mercury waste management.
