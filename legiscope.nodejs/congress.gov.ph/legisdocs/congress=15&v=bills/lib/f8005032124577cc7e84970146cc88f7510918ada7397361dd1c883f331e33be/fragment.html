HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00095
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING INCENTIVES TO ALL BARANGAY OFFICIALS, INCLUDING BARANGAY TANODS AND MEMBERS OF THE LUPONG TAGAPAMAYAPA, AND BARANGAY EMPLOYEES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Barangay Incentives Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Barangay Incentives Act of 2010.” Seeks to give due recognition to the important job being performed by the barangay official s and employees as well as to acknowledge the vital role being played by the smallest government unit and political subdivision, the barangay.
