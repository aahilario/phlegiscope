House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02753
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROTECT THE RIGHT OF THE PEOPLE TO INFORMATION ABOUT REPRODUCTIVE HEALTH CARE SERVICES
SHORT TITLE : "Women's Right to Know Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill directs all government instrumentalities that provide health care services to disseminate information on the availability of reproductive health care services including family planning, prenatal care and adoption services.
