House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04197
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REQUIRE ALL BUILDING OWNERS TO INSTALL, OPERATE AND MAINTAIN SECURITY CAMERA SYSTEM IN ALL ENCLOSED PARKING SPACES PROVIDING FOR PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Mandatory Installation of Security Cameras System in all Enclosed Parking Spaces of Buildings by their Respective Owners Law
ABSTRACT : "Mandatory Installation of Security Cameras System in all Enclosed Parking Spaces of Buildings by their Respective Owners Law". The bill exempts from the value-added tax all contracts of sale for the purchase or rental of security cameras and other equipment. It allows the purchase of security cameras and other equipment to be treated as "Ordinary and Necessary Expenses".
