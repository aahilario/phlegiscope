FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REGULATE AND MODERNIZE THE PRACTICE OF CHEMISTRY IN THE PHILIPPINES, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SEVEN HUNDRED FIFTY FOUR (R.A. NO. 754)
SHORT TITLE : "Chemistry Law of 2006."
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to upgrade the practice of the chemistry profession to reflect changes in the global practice of chemistry and include the practice of chemical technology in the profession.
