HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02609
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A NATIONAL CENTER FOR INFORMATION AND TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE RELATING TO ALL TYPES OF FAMILY RESOURCE AND SUPPORT PROGRAMS
SHORT TITLE : "Family Resource Act"
ABSTRACT : The measure establishes a National Center on Family Resource that shall serve as a national information, training, technical assistance and material development source for family resource and support programs.

DATE FILED : 2007-09-19
