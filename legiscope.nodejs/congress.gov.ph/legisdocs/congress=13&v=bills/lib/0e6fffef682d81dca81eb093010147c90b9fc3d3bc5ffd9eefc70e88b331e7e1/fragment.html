House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03859
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PUNISHING CULPABLE INSOLVENCY FOR NATURAL AND JURIDICAL PERSONS
SHORT TITLE : Culpable Insolvency Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Culpable Insolvency Act of 2005". The bill seeks to impose the death penalty on incorporators, principal shareholders, board of directors and officers of a corporation or partnership who divest his/her shares from the corporation or partnership involved in a major incident, disaster or catastrophe immediately after such incident, disaster or catastrophe or while an investigation is on-going.
