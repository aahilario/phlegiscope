HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03304
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A SELF-SUSTAINING FOREST MANAGEMENT PROGRAM TO BE KNOWN AS TREE FOR LEGACY PROGRAM, BY GIVING ECONOMIC AND OTHER INCENTIVES TO TREE PLANTERS ON PRIVATE AND FOREST LANDS IN THE FORM OF TREE OWNERSHIP OR USUFRUCT
SHORT TITLE : Tree for Legacy Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Tree for Legacy Act of 2004." The bill prescribes the objectives of the Program; the incentives and privileges; the obligations of grantees, the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and the provincial local government units; and the rights of the grantees, among others.
