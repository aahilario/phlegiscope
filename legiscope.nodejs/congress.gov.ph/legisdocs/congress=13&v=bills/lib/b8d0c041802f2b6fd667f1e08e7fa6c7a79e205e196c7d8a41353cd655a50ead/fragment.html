House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01748
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP IDENTIFICATION SYSTEM
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Citizenship Identification System Act of 2001
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Citizenship Identification System Act of 2001." The bill requires all Filipino citizens, who have reached the age of majority, to secure a citizen's ID from the Bureau of Immigration, which shall have the sole responsibility of issuing the ID.
