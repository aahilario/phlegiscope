HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03428
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING DISOBEDIENCE TO THE WRIT OF HABEAS CORPUS AND OTHER ACTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Compliance to the Writ of Habeas Corpus Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to punish disobedience to the writ under the following instances: a) a person who refuses or neglects to obey the writ; b) a member of the court who knowingly recommits to prison a person already discharged; c) a clerk of court who refuses without reason to issue a writ; and d) a person who intently transfers residence or transfers a person under his custody of restraint to elude the service of the writ.
