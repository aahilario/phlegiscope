HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01576
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CENTER FOR THE TEACHER'S EXCELLENCE IN ALL THE LEGISLATIVE DISTRICTS THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks the establishment of a Center for Teacher's Excellence in all legislative districts. The Center shall be tasked to develop and maintain a continuing program to upgrade the teaching competence and skills of teachers under the Department of Education.
