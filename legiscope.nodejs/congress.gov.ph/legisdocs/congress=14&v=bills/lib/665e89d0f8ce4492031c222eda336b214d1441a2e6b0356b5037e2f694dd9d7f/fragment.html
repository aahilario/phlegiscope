House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03288
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RECOGNIZING THE OBSERVANCE OF NOVEMBER 17 OF EVERY YEAR AS THE INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS' DAY IN THE PHILIPPINES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill declares November 17th of every year as the International Students' Day to commemorate the execution of nine Czechoslovakian students who fought against the Nazi occupation in Czechoslovakia.
