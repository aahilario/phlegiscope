HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03161
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LIMITING THE HOURS OF CASINO OPERATIONS FROM TEN (10) A.M. TO TWO (2) A.M., AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 14 SUBPARAGRAPH 2 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1869
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for casino operations to start from 10:00 a.m. up to 2:00 a.m. In places where curfew hours are imposed, all players and personnel of the casinos must leave the casino premises before the curfew hours start.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ABAYON, HARLIN CAST
