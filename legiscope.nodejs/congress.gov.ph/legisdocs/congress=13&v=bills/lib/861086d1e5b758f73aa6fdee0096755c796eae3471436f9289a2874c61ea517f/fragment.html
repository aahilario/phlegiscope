House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02985
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8424, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "TAX REFORM ACT OF 1997"
SHORT TITLE : Tax Exemption Act for Those Earning Not More Than the Prescribed Cost-of-Living
ABSTRACT : "Tax Exemption Act for Those Earning Not More Than the Prescribed Cost-of-Living." The bill exempts all workers whose total annual compensation does not exceed P219,000 from the payment of income taxes and filing of income tax returns.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RENATO "KA RENE" B. MAGTUBO
