HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00638
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE OVERSEAS FILIPINO WORKERS BANK, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the establishment of the Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) Bank, its powers, functions and organizational structure. It further sets the authorized capitalization for the Bank at PhP1.5 billion of which PhP500 million shall be offered to OFWs for subscription.
