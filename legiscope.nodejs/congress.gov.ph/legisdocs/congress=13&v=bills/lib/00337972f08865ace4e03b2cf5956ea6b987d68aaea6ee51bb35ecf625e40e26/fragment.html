HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00495
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LIMITING THE BORROWING POWER OF THE PRESIDENT BY PRESCRIBING CERTAIN REQUIREMENTS FOR ALL FORMS OF FOREIGN AND DOMESTIC INDEBTEDNESS AND ALL MATTERS RELATING TO THE COUNTRY'S DEBT MANAGEMENT STRATEGIES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the prior approval of the Philippine Congress in negotiating and contracting any loan, whether foreign or domestic, in the name and liability of the national government, including those undertaken by government-owned and controlled corporations and local government units.
