HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00412
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TOTALLY BANNING LOGGING OPERATIONS FOR THE NEXT 25 YEARS
ABSTRACT : Our forests are being denuded at an alarming rate. In order to save our remaining forests and to ensure the successful reforestation of badly denuded areas, the measure proposes to impose a total log ban in forests, timberlands, forest reserves and watersheds for the next 25 years.
