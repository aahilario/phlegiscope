HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02293
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPLEMENTING THE FREEDOM OF ACCESS TO INFORMATION ON MATTERS OF PUBLIC CONCERN GUARANTEED UNDER SECTION SEVEN ARTICLE III OF THE 1987 CONSTITUTION BY REQUIRING ELECTRONIC PUBLICATION OF ALL GOVERNMENTAL AGENCIES' RESOLUTION, REGULATION, CIRCULARS, DECISIONS, AND CONTRACTS, AND THE LIKE
SHORT TITLE : "Electronic Access to Information Act"
ABSTRACT : "Electronic Access to Information Act". The bill aims to put information, such as rules, regulations, decisions, orders, circulars, letters of intent and government contracts and concession agreements electronically available to the public.
