HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01507
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A STAGGERED WORKING HOUR SCHEME IN THE PRIVATE SECTOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to encourage employers in the private sector to adopt a flexible time scheme that would allow their employees to work for more than eight hours a day but not more than forty hours a week. It provides for the payment of overtime for work rendered beyond forty hours a week.
