HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02299
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL PUBLIC AND PRIVATE SCHOOLS, COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES THE TEACHING OF BASIC COMPUTER PROGRAMMING AS PART OF THE CURRICULUM OF ALL SECONDARY LEVEL STUDENTS IN THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Computer Programming Literacy Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the inclusion of basic computer programming courses in the curriculum of all high schools in the country to make the youth computer literate.
