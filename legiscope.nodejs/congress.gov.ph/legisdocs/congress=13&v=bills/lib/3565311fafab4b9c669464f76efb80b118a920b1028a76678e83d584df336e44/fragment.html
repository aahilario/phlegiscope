HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00715
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN SECTIONS OF BATAS PAMBANSA BILANG 881 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE OMNIBUS ELECTION CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : It can not be denied that barangay elections are used by various political parties and political organizations to provide them with a political machinery. The bill proposes the deletion of Section 38 of the Omnibus Election Code which provides for a non-partisan barangay election. The proposal would finally adapt the law into the political reality prevalent in this country.
