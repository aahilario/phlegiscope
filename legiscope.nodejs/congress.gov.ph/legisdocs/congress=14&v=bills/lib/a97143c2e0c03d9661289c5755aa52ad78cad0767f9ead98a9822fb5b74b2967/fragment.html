House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00187
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EMPOWERING WOMEN BY PROVIDING THEM WITH FINANCIAL EDUCATIONAL AND INFORMATION ASSISTANCE IN ORDER FOR THEM TO EXCEL IN COMMERCE AND TRADE
SHORT TITLE : "Women Empowerment in Enterprise Act"
ABSTRACT : "Women Empowerment in Enterprise Act". The bill seeks to establish a Women in Enterprise Development Program that will provide women financial, education and information assistance in the field of commerce and trade.
