HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02401
FULL TITLE : AN ACT QUALIFYING THE KILLING OF MEMBERS OF BROADCAST AND PRINT MEDIA IN THE LAWFUL EXERCISE OF THEIR FUNCTIONS AS SUCH, AS A CRIME OF MURDER PUNISHABLE UNDER ARTICLE 248 OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill classifies as a crime of murder the slaying of members of the media while in the performance of their duties.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.
DATE FILED : 2007-09-04
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
