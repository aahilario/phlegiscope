HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00609
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING NATIONAL HEALTH RESEARCH INSTITUTES APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "National Health Research Institute Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "National Health Research Institute Act of 2007". The measure creates the National Health Research Institute that shall be responsible for the following, among others: a) encourage research and development on indigenous health care systems; b) promote research and development of drugs from indigenous and non-indigenous sources; and c) encourage basic and clinical research on diseases peculiar to our country and ecology.
