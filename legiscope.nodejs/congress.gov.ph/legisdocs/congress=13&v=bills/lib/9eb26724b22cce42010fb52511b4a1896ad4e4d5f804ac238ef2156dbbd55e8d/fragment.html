HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01976
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING DISCRIMINATION IN THE RECRUITMENT, EMPLOYMENT, PROMOTION AND DISMISSAL OF ANY MEMBER OF THE THIRD SEX BOTH IN PUBLIC AND PRIVATE EMPLOYMENT AND PROVIDING PENALTIES THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill declares it unlawful and imposes a fine of P10,000 or three months imprisonment to an employer who discriminates against any member of the third sex.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ESPINA, GERARDO JR. J
DATE FILED : 2004-08-02
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
