House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03263
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR STIFFER PENALTIES FOR LAW ENFORCEMENT OFFICIALS AND MILITARY PERSONNEL AMENDING ACT NUMBER 3815, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED PENAL CODE
SHORT TITLE : "Amendments to the Penalties of the RPC for 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to mete the maximum imposable period of the prescribed penalty, regardless of any mitigating or alternative circumstance, on any law enforcement officer or military official found guilty of an offense charged under the Revised Penal code.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CELESTE, ARTHUR F.
DATE FILED : 2007-12-10
