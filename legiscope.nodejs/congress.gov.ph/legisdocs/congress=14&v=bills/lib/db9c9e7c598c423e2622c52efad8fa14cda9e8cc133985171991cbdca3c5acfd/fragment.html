House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01891
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING PROTECTION TO PERSONS WHO DISCLOSE CONDUCT CONSTITUTING GRAFT AND CORRUPTION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Whistleblower Protection Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The Whistleblower Protection Act of 2007". The measure seeks to create a positive whistleblowing culture in government by providing whistleblowers protection from reprisal and harassment and by identifying the remedies available to whistleblowers.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : GONZALEZ, RAUL JR. T.
