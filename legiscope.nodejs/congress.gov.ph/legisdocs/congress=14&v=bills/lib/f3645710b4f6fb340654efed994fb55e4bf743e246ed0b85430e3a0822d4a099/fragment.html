FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE USE OF THE PROCEEDS FROM THE DEVELOPMENT, LEASE OR USE OF CERTAIN GOVERNMENT PROPERTIES FOR PAYMENT OF PENSIONS AND OTHER BENEFITS OF THE VETERANS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to utilize the proceeds from the development, lease or use of government properties assigned for veterans� use to fund the pension and benefits of veterans and military retirees.
