HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00299
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL OWNERS OR OPERATORS OF TELEVISION NETWORKS OR STATIONS AND PRODUCERS OF TELEVISION NEWS PROGRAMS TO BROADCAST THEIR NEWS PROGRAMS WITH SUBTITLES AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF
ABSTRACT : Television is the most popular means of communication. Unfortunatley, it denies hearing-impaired individuals the full audio-visual experience to be informed of current happenings in the world. This bill seeks to require all franchise holders, operators of television networks or stations and producers of television programs to broadcast their newscast or new programs, which are national and regional in scope, with subtitles in order to benefit persons with hearing disabilities.
