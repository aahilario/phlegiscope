House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02530
FULL TITLE : THE INVESTMENTS AND INCENTIVES CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : "The Investments and Incentives Code of the Philippines"
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to harmonize and simplify the grant of fiscal and non-fiscal incentives in order to promote foreign and domestic investments in the country. It strengthens the Board of Investments and creates a separate industry development agency, the Industrial Development Board.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CUA, JUNIE E.
