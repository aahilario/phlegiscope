House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02837
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE PAYMENT OF RETIREMENT BENEFITS TO GOVERNMENT RETIREES ON THE DATE OF THEIR RETIREMENT
ABSTRACT : The bill drects all government agencies, bureaus and corporations to complete the processing of the papers of their employees under compulsory and optional retirement three (3) months before the retirement date.
