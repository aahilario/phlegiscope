HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02859
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR STIFFER PENALTIES FOR ILLEGAL TRADING OF PETROLEUM PRODUCTS AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 33 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes a penalty of prision correccional and/or a fine of not less than P50,000 nor more than P200,000 for the illegal trading of petroleum products.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ABALOS, BENJAMIN JR.
