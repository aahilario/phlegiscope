HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01751
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE AMOUNT OF INSURED BANK DEPOSITS FROM ONE HUNDRED THOUSAND PESOS (P100,000) TO ONE MILLION PESOS (P1,000,000), AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THREE THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED NINETY-ONE, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CHARTER OF THE PHILIPPINE DEPOSIT INSURANCE CORPORATION
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the maximum amount of insured deposits to P500,000 and to establish a deposit insurance coverage plan for deposits in excess of P500,00 which a depositor, as an option, may enroll in at his cost.
