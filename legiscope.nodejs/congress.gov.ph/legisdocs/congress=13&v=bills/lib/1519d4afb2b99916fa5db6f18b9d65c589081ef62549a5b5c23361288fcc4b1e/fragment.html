HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02304
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE COMPULSORY INCLUSION IN THE CURRICULUM OF ALL SECONDARY SCHOOLS, PUBLIC OR PRIVATE, OF A SUBJECT ON 'LOCAL GOVERNANCE AND THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE', AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : To introduce to high school students the concept of local governance, the bill requires the inclusion of a subject on "Local Governance and the Local Government Code" in the curricula of private and public high schools.
