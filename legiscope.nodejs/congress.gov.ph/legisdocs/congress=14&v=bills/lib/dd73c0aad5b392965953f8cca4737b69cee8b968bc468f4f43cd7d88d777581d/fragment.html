House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00327
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE COMMISSION OF ACTS OF TORTURE AND OTHER CRUEL, INHUMAN AND DEGRADING TREATMENT OR PUNISHMENT, PRESCRIBING PENALTIES THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Torture Act of 2007".
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Torture Act of 2007". The bill defines and classifies torture into physical, mental or psychological torture. It also provides penalties for acts of torture.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : LAGMAN, EDCEL C.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-02
