House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01634
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE NATIONAL COMMISSION ON MUSLIM FILIPINOS, DEFINING ITS POWERS, FUNCTIONS, AND RESPONSIBILITIES AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : An Act Creating the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos
ABSTRACT : "An Act Creating the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos." The bill prescribes the Commission's organizational structure, powers, functions and responsibilities. The Commission is envisioned to take over the functions of the Office of Muslim Affairs.
