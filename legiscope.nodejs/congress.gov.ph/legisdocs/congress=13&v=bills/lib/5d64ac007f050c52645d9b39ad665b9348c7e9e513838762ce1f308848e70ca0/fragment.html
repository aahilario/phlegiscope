House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02815
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE DISPOSITION OF SMUGGLED AGRICULTURAL GOODS, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF TARIFF AND CUSTOMS CODE, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the mechanics in the disposition of smuggled agricultural products, to wit: a) 10% to the exporter; b) 25% to the apprehending government agency; c) 25% to the competitive enhancement fund; and d) 40% to the national government.
