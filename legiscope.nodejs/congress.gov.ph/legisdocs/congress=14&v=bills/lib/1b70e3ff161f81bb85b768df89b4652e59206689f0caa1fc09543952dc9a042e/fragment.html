House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02623
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CHAPTER VI SECTION 87 ON PROVISIONS AND PENALTIES OF R.A. NO. 8550 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE PHILIPPINE FISHERIES CODE OF 1998, PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A FISHERIES LAW ENFORCEMENT SUPPORT FUND AND PROVIDING FOR THE CREATION ON SPECIAL COURTS FOR FISHERIES
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes stiffer penalties on illegal fishing activities in the country. It creates the Fisheries Law Enforcement Support Fund that shall be used to augment BFAR's (Bureau of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources) law enforcement activities, among others.
