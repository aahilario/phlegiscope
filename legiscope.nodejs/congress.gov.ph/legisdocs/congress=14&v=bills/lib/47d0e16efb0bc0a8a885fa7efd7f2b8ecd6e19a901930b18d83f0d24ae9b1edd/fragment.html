HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00963
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RESTORING TO CONGRESS THE SOLE AND EXCLUSIVE AUTHORITY TO REORGANIZE THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT, REPEALING FOR THIS PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1416, AS AMENDED, AND SECTION 31, CHAPTER 10,TITLE III, BOOK III OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 292 SERIES OF 1987 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE ADMINISTRATIVE CODE OF 1987
ABSTRACT : The bill calls for the revoke of the continuing authority granted to the President of the Philippines to reorganize the national government in whole or in part.
