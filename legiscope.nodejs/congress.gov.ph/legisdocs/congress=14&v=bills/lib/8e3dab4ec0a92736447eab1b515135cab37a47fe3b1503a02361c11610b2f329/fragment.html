House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01944
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A BUREAU OF CORRECTIONS TECHNICAL INSTITUTE WITHIN THE NEW BILIBID PRISONS (NBP) RESERVATION AND THE CORRECTIONAL INSTITUTION FOR WOMEN (CIW), AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Bureau of Corrections Technical Institute Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Bureau of Corrections Technical Institute Act of 2007". The measure establishes the Bureau of Corrections Technical Institute that shall provide relevant and high-quality programs in skills development and technical and vocational training to all prisoners serving their sentences in the New Bilibid Prisons and the Correctional Institution for Women
