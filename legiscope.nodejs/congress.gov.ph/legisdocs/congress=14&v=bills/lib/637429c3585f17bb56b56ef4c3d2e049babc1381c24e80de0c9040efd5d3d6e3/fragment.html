HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00519
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING THE SALE OR IMPORTATION OF COMPUTER HARDWARE AND SOFTWARE FROM THE PAYMENT OF VALUE ADDED TAX AND DONATIONS THEREOF FROM DONOR'S TAX, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 109 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill exempts the sale or importation of computer software and hardware from the ambit of the value-added tax. It also seeks to exempt from the donor's tax donations of computer hardware and software to private and public schools.
