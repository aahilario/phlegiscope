HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02716
FULL TITLE : AN ACT UPGRADING THE MONTHLY BENEFITS OF WORLD WAR II VETERANS, THEIR SPOUSES, AND UNMARRIED MINOR CHILDREN
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes that the monthly pension of veterans be based on age, to wit: a) from 75 to 79 - P6,700; b) from 80 to 89 - P7,000; and c) from 90 and above - P8,700.
