House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02615
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO DETER AND PUNISH TERRORIST ACTS, TO ENHANCE LAW ENFORCEMENT, TO AUTHORIZE PEN REGISTERS AND TRAP AND TRACE UNDER CERTAIN CONDITIONS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Peace Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Peace Act of 2004." The bill defines the offense of terrorism and provides penalties therefore. It allows court authorized wiretaps in terrorism investigations and wiretaps without court authorization in cases of concensual monitoring.
