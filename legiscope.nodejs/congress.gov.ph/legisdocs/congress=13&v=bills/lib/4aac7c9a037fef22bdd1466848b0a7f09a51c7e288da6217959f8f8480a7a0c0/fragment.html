HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01560
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE REIMPOSITION OF THE FRANCHISE TAX ON TELEPHONE AND TELEGRAPH COMPANIES, AND RADIO AND TELEVISION BROADCASTING COMPANIES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS 108 AND 119 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to remove telecommunications and broadcast companies from the ambit of the value-added tax system and to subject them to a franchise tax equivalent to 3.5% of the gross receipts on the first year of effectivity at this act and to 7% thereafter.
