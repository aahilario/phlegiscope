House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02941
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING ALL FRANCHISES GRANTED TO DIFFERENT RADIO AND/OR TELEVISION BROADCASTING STATIONS FOR THE PURPOSE OF MANDATING THEM TO ALLOCATE TIME FOR PUBLIC INFORMATION PROGRAM OF THE GOVERNMENT
SHORT TITLE : Government Information Program Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Government Information Program Act of 2004." The bill mandates all operators/owners of radio and television broadcasting stations to allot at least 60 minutes of their airtime, daily, for free, for the use of the government for its public information program.
