House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05846
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE COMMISSION OF ACTS OF TORTURE AND OTHER CRUEL, INHUMAN AND DEGRADING TREATMENT OR PUNISHMENTS, PRESCRIBING PENALTIES THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Torture Act of 2006."
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Torture Act of 2006". The bill defines and classifies torture into physical, mental or psychological torture. It criminalizes acts of torture and provides victims or their families legal assistance.
