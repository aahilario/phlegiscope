FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FURTHER BENEFITS TO PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS PROVIDING THE MECHANICS FOR ITS IMPLEMENTATION, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Kaguruan Kabayanihan Act
ABSTRACT : "Kaguruan Kabayanihan Act." Grants public school teachers a 15% discount on restaurants, supermarkets, theaters, airlines, ships, buses, taxia and medicines.
