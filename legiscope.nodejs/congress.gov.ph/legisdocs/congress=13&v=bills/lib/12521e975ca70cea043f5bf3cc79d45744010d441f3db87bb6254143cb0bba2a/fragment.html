HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00853
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MAKING THE POSITION OF THE MUNICIPAL AGRICULTURIST MANDATORY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 482 OF REPUBLIC ACT 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991"
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the appointment of a MUNICIPAL AGRICULTURIST so that the agricultural municipal government is assured that it can rely on the expertise of an agriculturist at all times.
