House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03472
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PHILIPPINE EMERGENCY AND DISASTER MANAGEMENT AUTHORITY AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Emergency and Disaster Management Authority Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Emergency and Disaster Management Authority Act of 2004." The bill provides for the composition, powers and functions of the Philippine Emergency and Disaster Management Authority. It also provides for the establishment of emergency and disaster field offices in every province.
