House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04184
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO GOVERN OCCUPATIONAL SAFETY AND HEALTH IN THE CONSTRUCTION INDUSTRY
SHORT TITLE : Occupational Safety and Health in the Construction Industry Act
ABSTRACT : "Occupational Safety and Health in the Construction Industry Act." The bill requires every construction project to implement a suitable construction safety and health program which shall state the specific safety policies to be observed and maintained in the construction site.
