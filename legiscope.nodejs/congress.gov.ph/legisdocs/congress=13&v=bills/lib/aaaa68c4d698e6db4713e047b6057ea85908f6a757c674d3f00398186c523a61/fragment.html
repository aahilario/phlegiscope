HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01504
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL PUBLIC UTILITY AGENCIES AND COMPANIES OR CORPORATIONS, BOTH PUBLIC AND PRIVATE, TO REPAIR ANY DIGGING OR DESTRUCTION MADE IN THE COURSE OF THEIR INSTALLATION OR MAINTENANCE WORK
ABSTRACT : The bill requires all utility agencies to restore roads, streets, passageways, pathways or similar places to their prior status within 72 hours from completion of any work in the area. It further requires the punong barangay or the barangay secretary to issue a certificate that the repair work has been completed.
