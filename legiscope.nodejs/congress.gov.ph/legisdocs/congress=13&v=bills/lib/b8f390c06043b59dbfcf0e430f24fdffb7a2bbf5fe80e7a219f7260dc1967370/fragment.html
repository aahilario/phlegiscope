House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01925
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PREVENT AND PENALIZE ACTS OF TERRORISM AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Anti-terrorism Act of 2002
ABSTRACT : "Anti-terrorism Act of 2002." The bill defines terrorism as a "cause, attempt or threat of destruction of properties or death by showing terror to the public, disturbing peace and order internationally or domestically due to ideological, political, religious, ethnic, or cultist belief."
