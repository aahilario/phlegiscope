HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00393
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE RE-ALLOCATION OF THE INTERNAL REVENUE ALLOTMENT OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 285 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to provide that starting 1999, the internal revenue allotment to be distributed shall be net of the cost of devolved functions and personnel, including personnel hired by local government units before December 1997 to perform the devolved functions, and the cost of city-funded hospitals and legislated benefits, excluding capital outlay. It provides that the cost deducted shall be distributed and remitted directly to the local government units concerned.
