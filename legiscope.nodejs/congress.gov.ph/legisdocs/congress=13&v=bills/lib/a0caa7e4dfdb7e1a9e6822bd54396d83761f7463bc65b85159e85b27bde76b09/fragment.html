HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03069
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL HOLDERS OF STUDENT'S PERMIT OR DRIVER'S LICENSE TO OBTAIN ACCIDENT AND DEATH INSURANCE AS PREREQUISITE FOR APPLICATION AND RENEWAL, AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates that no student's permit and driver's license shall be issued or renewed without proof of insurance coverage within the lifespan of the permit or license. The minimum insurance coverage shall be P100,000 per year.
