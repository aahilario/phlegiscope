FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7610, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SPECIAL PROTECTION AGAINST CHILD ABUSE, EXPLOITATION AND DISCRIMINATION
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes the penalties of reclusion temporal in its medium term to reclusion perpetua for acts that are either preparatory to or per se amounts to child pornography and prostitution.
