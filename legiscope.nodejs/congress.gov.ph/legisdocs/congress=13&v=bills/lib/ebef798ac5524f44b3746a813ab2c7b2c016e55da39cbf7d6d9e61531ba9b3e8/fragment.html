HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01789
FULL TITLE : AN ACT UPGRADING THE MINIMUM SALARY GRADE LEVEL OF PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS IN ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY LEVELS FROM SALARY GRADE 10 TO SALARY GRADE 18 AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to upgrade the salary of public elementary and high school teachers from salary grade 10 to salary grade 18 to compensate them commensurately to their qualifications and to the role they play in educating our youth.
