House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00424
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE POSITION OF BARANGAY POPULATION WORKER, GRANTING BENEFITS THERETO, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE 'LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991', AS AMENDED, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The Barangay Population Worker shall be tasked to conduct studies on reproductive technologies currently in use; and collect, analyze and disseminate basic data on population principles and development.
