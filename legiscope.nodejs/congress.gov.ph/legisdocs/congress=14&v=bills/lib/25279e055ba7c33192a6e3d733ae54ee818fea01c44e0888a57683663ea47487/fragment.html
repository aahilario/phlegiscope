House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00882
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING ADDITIONAL RETIREMENT BENEFITS TO MEMBERS OF THE JUDICIARY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 910, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the retirement benefits of retired justices to be automatically increased whenever there is an increase in the salary of incumbent justices and judges of the same rank.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : NOGRALES, PROSPERO C.
