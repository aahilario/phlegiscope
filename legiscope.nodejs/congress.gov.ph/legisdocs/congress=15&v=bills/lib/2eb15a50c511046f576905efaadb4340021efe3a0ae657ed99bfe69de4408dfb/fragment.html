FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING SUBSISTENCE ALLOWANCE TO PERSONS UNDERGOING VOCATIONAL AND/OR TECHNICAL TRAINING, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
