HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00136
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING REFORMS IN THE CRIMINAL JUSTICE SYSTEM BY ENHANCING DNA TECHNOLOGY ANALYSIS AS A POTENT INVESTIGATIVE TOOL, CREATING THE DNA ADVISORY BOARD UNDER THE NATIONAL BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "DNA Analysis Enhancement Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "DNA Analysis Enhancement Act of 2007". The bill calls for the establishment of a National DNA Index System that shall assist law enforcement agencies in the identification, detection or exclusion of persons who are the subjects of investigations.
