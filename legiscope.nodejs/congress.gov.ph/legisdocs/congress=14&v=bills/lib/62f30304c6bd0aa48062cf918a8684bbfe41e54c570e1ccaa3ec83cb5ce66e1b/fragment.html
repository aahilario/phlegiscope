HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01810
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING EACH CITY OR MUNICIPALITY TO CREATE A RIVER DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY FOR THE PRESERVATION, PROTECTION AND DEVELOPMENT OF ALL RIVERS, RIVER SYSTEMS AND NATURAL WATERWAYS WITHIN ITS JURISDICTION, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates each city or municipality to create, within its jurisdiction, a River Development Authority that shall be tasked to preserve, protect and develop the rivers, river systems and natural waterways.
