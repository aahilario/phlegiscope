House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03093
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE SECURITY OF SENSITIVE DATA MAINTAINED BY THE GOVERNMENT
SHORT TITLE : "Government Data Privacy Protection Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill allows the use of encryption standards recognized by the Department of Science and Technology to secure the integrity and confidentiality of sensitive data maintained by the different government agencies.
