HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01386
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXPANDING THE COVERAGE OF AND THE CONDUIT NETWORK FOR AGRI-AGRA CREDITS, PRESCRIBING A FIXED PERCENTAGE OF THE FUNDS FOR LENDING TO SMALL FARMERS AND FISHERFOLK, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES THEREBY AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 717 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE AGRI-AGRA LAW
ABSTRACT : This bill requires banks to grant 25% of their loanable funds to the agriculture and fisheries sectors, 50% of which shall be lent to small farmers and fisherfolk. It prohibits the diversion of the 10% agrarian reform share to government securities.
