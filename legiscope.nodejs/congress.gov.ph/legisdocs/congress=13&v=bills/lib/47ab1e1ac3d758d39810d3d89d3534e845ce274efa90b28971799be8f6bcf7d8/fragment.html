House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05468
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO DEVELOP ENTREPRENEURSHIP AMONG GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES FOR AN INNOVATION-INSPIRED CORPS OF CIVIL SERVANTS AND A PRODUCTIVE POST-SERVICE CITIZENRY
SHORT TITLE : "Government Employees' Entrepreneurship Development Act of 2006."
ABSTRACT : "Government Employees' Entrepreneurship Development Act of 2006". The measure encourages all government agencies to establish coperatives and savings and loans associations in their respective offices as an outlet for entrepreneurial skills in order to generate financial resources for their employees.
