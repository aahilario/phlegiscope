HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02459
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING ADDITIONAL BENEFITS AND INCENTIVES TO ALL BARANGAY TANODS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Barangay Tanod Incentives Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill grants barangay tanods the following incentives: a) GSIS coverage; b) health benefits; c) educational benefits; d) income tax exemption; and e) legal representation.
