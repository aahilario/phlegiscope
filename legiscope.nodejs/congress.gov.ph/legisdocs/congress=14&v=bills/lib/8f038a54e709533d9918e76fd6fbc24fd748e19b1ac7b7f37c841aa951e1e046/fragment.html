HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02919
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING CIVIL SERVICE ELIGIBILITY TO GOVERNORS, VICE GOVERNORS, MAYORS, VICE MAYORS AND THE MEMBERS OF ALL THE SANGGUNIANG BAYAN, PANLUNGSOD AND PANLALAWIGAN WHO HAVE RENDERED A TOTAL OF FIVE YEARS OF EFFICIENT SERVICE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill grants civil service eligibility to the following local officials who have served for 5 years: governor, vice-governor, mayor, vice-mayor and members of the sanggunian.
