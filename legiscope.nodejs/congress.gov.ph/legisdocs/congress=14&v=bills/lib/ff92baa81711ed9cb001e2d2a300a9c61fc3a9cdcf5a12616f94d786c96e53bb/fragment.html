HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02819
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOWING AN ACCUSED TO AVAIL OF PROBATION WHEN IMPOSED UPON APPEAL IS LOWERED TO SIX YEARS AND BELOW, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 968, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE PROBATION LAW OF 1976, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill extends the benefits of probation to cases where the sentence imposed by the trial court is, upon appeal, reduced by the appelate court to six (6) years or below.
