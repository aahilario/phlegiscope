House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02315
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL RAILWAY SYSTEM AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine National Railway Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to consolidate the different railway agencies into a single entity. The Philippine National Railway shall be mandated to establish a national railway system covering the islands of Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao.
