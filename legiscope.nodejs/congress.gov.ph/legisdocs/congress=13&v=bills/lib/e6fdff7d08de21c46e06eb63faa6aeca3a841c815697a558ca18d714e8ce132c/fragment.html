House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02700
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL FILIPINO REGISTERED NURSES TO RENDER AT LEAST TWO (2) YEARS SERVICE WITHIN THE COUNTRY PRIOR TO ANY EMPLOYMENT ABROAD
SHORT TITLE : Mandatory Two-Year Domestic Service for All Filipino Registered Nurses Act
ABSTRACT : "Mandatory Two-Year Domestic Service for All Filipino Registered Nurses Act." The bill requires Filipino registered nurses who wish to apply for foreign employment to secure an eligibility status with the Professional Regulatory Commission and a clearance from the Secretary of Labor and Employment.
