HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02640
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE INCLUSION OF SEXUAL AWARENESS AS A SUBJECT IN THE SCHOOL CURRICULA OF ALL PRIVATE AND PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOLS IN THE COUNTRY
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to include sexual awareness in the curricula of all secondary schools to instill a "culture of vigilance and responsibility towards sex" among the youth.
