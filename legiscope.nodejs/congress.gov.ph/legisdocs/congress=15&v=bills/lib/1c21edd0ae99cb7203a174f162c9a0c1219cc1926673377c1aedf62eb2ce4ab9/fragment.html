COMMITEE HEARINGS/ACTIONS:

          -HB Nos. 88 and 519 were deliberated jointly with the CHTE.
      2. Under deliberation by the Mother Committee on 2010-09-07
          -Joint meeting with CHTE. TWG was created to consolidate the provisions of HB Nos. 88, 519 and 3223.
      3. Under deliberation by the Mother Committee on 2010-09-07
          -• A technical working group (TWG) was created to study and consolidate the provisions of the three (3) house bills and incorporate the comments and recommendations of various stakeholders; The TWG will be composed of representatives from the Department of Education, Commission on Higher Education, Technical Education and Skills Development Authority, National Economic and Development Authority, Fund for Assistance to Private Education, Coordinated Council for Private Education Association and Department of Budget and Management, and to be headed by the proponents of the measures and supported by the secretariat of both Basic Education and Culture and Higher and Technical Education Committees
      4. Under study by the Technical Working Group(TWG) on 2010-11-23
          -Joint TWG meeting with CHTE to consolidate the provisions of HB Nos. 88, 519 and 3223.
