HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04196
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PENALIZE ANY PERSON WHO HIRES, PERSUADES, COERCES, INDUCES OR OTHERWISE ENGAGES CHILDREN FOR THE PURPOSE OF PERFORMING LASCIVIOUS ACTS CONSTITUTING INDECENT SHOWS OR EXHIBITION WITH OR WITHOUT THE USE OF SEX TOYS AND SIMILAR PARAPHERNALIA BROADCASTED OVER THE INTERNET WHETHER FOR PROFIT OR NOT AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBER 7610 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SPECIAL PROTECTION OF CHILDREN AGAINST ABUSE, EXPLOITATION AND DISCRIMINATION ACT PROVIDING FOR PENALTIES THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill imposes the penalty of reclusion temporal in its medium period on any person who engages a child to perform lascivious acts constituting indecent shows for broadcast over the internet.
