House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03396
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR STRICTER PENALTIES FOR CRIMES COMMITTED AGAINST PERSONS WITH DISABILITY, FURTHER AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE, REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7277 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "MAGNA CARTA FOR DISABLED PERSONS"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to impose a penalty one degree higher that that prescribed under the law on any person who commits a crime against a person with disability.
