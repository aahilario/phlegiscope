FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR ROUTINE HEPATITIS B IMMUNIZATION OF ALL INFANTS AT BIRTH
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the giving of routine hepatitis B vaccination to all infants at birth in order to prevent the risk of acquiring the viral infection.
