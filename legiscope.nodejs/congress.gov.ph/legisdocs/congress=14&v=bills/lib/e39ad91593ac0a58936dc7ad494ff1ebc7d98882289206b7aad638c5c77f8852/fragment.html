House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00365
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 9 OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THIRTY HUNDRED AND NINETEEN, AS AMENDED, ENTITLED 'ANTI-GRAFT AND CORRUPT PRACTICES ACT'
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to penalize persons who benefit from the unlawful act of a public officer with imprisonment of six months to five years and forfeiture in favor of the government of all proceeds derived from such act.
