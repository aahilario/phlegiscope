HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02658
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN LOCAL AUTONOMY BY EQUITABLY DISTRIBUTING TO THE LOCAL GOVERNMENTS THE CHARITY FUND OF THE PHILIPPINE CHARITY SWEEPSTAKES OFFICE
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for an amount equivalent to the gross receipts of sweepstakes tickets from each province to be given to the respective provincial government as its provincial charity fund.
