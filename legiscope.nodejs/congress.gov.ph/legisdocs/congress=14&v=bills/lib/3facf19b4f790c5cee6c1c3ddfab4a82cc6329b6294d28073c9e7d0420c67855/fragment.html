HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03115
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE DEPLOYMENT OF FILIPINO DOMESTIC HELPERS IN CERTAIN PLACES ABROAD PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Overseas Domestic Helpers Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to determine the countries where maltreatment and abuse of overseas Filipino workers is rampant and to ban their development to these countries.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ABANTE, BIENVENIDO JR. M.
DATE FILED : 2007-11-21
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
