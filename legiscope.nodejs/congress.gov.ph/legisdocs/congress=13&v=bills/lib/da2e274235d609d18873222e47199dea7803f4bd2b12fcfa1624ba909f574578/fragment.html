House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00382
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO MANDATE THE ESTABLISHMENT OF POEA REGIONAL OFFICES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 247, ENTITLED 'REORGANIZING THE PHILIPPINE OVERSEAS EMPLOYMENT ADMINISTRATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES'
ABSTRACT : The proposed measure mandates the establishment of Philippine Oberseas Employment Agency (POEA) Regional Offices in all regions of the country. The enactment of the bill is seen to enahnce the effectiveness and efficiency of the POEA in responding to changing market and economic conditions and to the call of the national development plan to strengthen the worker protection and regulation components of the overseas employment program.
