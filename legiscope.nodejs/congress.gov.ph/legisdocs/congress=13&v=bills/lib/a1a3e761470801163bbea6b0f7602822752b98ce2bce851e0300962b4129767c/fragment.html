HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00297
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR ADDITIONAL BENEFITS TO SENIOR CITIZENS AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SEVENTY FOUR HUNDRED AND THIRTY-TWO
SHORT TITLE :
ABSTRACT : This bill seeks to provide additional benefits to senior citizens by granting them a 20% discount on purchases of funeral services (on a pre-need basis) to lessen the burden on the family left behind when the elderly passes on. It proposes the establishment of the Office of the Senior Citizens Affairs (OSCA) at the office of the Governor and city Mayor to be headed by a provincial board member and a city councilor, respectively.
