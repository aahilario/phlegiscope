House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00348
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A ROAD DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Road Development Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Road Development Act of 2007". The bill seeks to create the Road Development Authority that shall be responsible for the construction and rehabilitation, and maintenance and operation of major highways, roads, bridges and public thoroughfares.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ABAYA, JOSEPH EMILIO A.
