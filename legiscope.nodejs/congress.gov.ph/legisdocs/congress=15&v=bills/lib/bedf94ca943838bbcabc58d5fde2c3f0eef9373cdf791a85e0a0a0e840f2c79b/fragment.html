HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00208
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CHAPTER VI, PROHIBITIONS AND PENALTIES AND PROVIDING AN ADDITIONAL CHAPTER ON LEGAL ACTIONS TO R.A. 8550, ENTITLED: "AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE DEVELOPMENT, MANAGEMENT AND CONSERVATION OF THE FISHERIES AND AQUATIC RESOURCES, INTEGRATING ALL LAWS PERTINENT THERETO, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES"
ABSTRACT : Seeks to strengthen the law to curb if not totally eliminate the illegal activities of the commercial fishers in municipal waters and to institutionalize the right of every citizen to file suit and receive remunerations for violations to their person and to the provision of this act.
