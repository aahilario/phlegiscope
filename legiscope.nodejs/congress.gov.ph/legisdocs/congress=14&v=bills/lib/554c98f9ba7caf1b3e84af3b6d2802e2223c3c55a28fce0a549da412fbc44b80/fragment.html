House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00070
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PHILIPPINE COAST GUARD AS A SEPARATE AND DISTINCT SERVICE ATTACHED TO THE DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION AND COMMUNICATIONS, FURTHER AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5173, AS AMENDED AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to place the Philippine Coast Guard (PCG) under the Department of Transportation and Communications with its own organizational set-up of uniformed and non-uniformed personnel and the authority to establish civilian volunteer organizations or the PCG auxilliary.
