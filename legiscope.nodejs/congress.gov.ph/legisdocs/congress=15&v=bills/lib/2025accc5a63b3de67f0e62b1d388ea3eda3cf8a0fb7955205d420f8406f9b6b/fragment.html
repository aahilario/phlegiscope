House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00062
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9340, RESETTING THE SYNCHRONIZED BARANGAY AND SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN ELECTIONS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Resets the synchronized barangay and sangguniang kabataan elections scheduled on last Monday of October 2010 to the second Monday of May 2011 and every three (3) years thereafter.
