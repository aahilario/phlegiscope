HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00704
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE BENEFITS OF AND PROVIDING INCENTIVES TO BARANGAY OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES INCLUDING BARANGAY TANODS, AND MEMBERS OF THE LUPONG TAGAPAMAYAPA AND PANGKAT NG TAGAPAGKASUNDO, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS 393 AND 406 OF THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
SHORT TITLE : "Barangay Officials and Employees Incentives Act of 2004"
ABSTRACT : "Barangay Officials and Employees Incentives Act of 2004". The bill grants the following benefits to barangay officials: a) honoraria and allowances - P2,000 per month for the punong barangay; P1,000 per month for sanggunian barangay members, treasurer and secretary; and P300 per month for barangay tanods; b) Christmas bonus - P1,500; and c) hazard pay - P150 per tour of duty for every barangay tanod.
