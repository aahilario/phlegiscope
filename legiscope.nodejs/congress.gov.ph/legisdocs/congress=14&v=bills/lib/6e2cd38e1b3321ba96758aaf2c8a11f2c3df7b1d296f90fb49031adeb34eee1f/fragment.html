House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02472
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A MECHANISM FOR THE NATIONAL IDENTIFICATION SYSTEM IN THE PHILIPPINES, PROVIDING CORRESPONDING OBLIGATIONS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "National Identification Card System (NICS) Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the adoption of a national identification system that shall serve as the official identification card of Filipino citizens when dealing with government institutions, financial institutitons and others.
