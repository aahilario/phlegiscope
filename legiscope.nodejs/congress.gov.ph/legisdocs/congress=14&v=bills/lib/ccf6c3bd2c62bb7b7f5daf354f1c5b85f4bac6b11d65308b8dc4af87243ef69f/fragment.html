House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01347
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE MONTHLY ALLOWANCES OF BARANGAY DAY CARE WORKERS FROM FIVE HUNDRED PESOS(P500.00) TO THREE THOUSAND PESOS (P3,000.00) AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTY-NINE HUNDRED AND SEVENTY-TWO
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to increase the monthly allowance of accredited barangay day care workers from P500 to a minimum of P3,000.
