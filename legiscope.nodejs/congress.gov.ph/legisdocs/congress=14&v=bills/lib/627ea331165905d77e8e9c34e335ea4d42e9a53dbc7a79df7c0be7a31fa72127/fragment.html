House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02349
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL PROVINCES AND HIGHLY-URBANIZED CITIES TO ESTABLISH A SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL
SHORT TITLE : "Science High School Establishment Act"
ABSTRACT : The measures proposes the establishment of a science high school in all provinces and highly-urbanized cities in order to uplift the quality of education in the country and to produce globally-competitive graduates.
