House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00707
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING RADIO, TELEVISION, NEWSPAPER, MOVIE HOUSE, POSTER AND BILLBOARD ADVERTISEMENT ON CIGARETTE AND ALCOHOLIC DRINKS
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Cigarettes and Alcoholic Drinks Advertisement Act"
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Cigarettes and Alcoholic Drinks Advertisement Act". The bill prohibits and penalizes the showing, airing or printing of advertisement of cigarettes and alcoholic drinks in radio or television programs, movie houses, newspapers, billboards, posters or flyers.
