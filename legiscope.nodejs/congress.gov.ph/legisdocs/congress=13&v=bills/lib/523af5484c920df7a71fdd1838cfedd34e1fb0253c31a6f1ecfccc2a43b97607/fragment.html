HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03125
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REDUCING BUREAUCRATIC RED TAPE BY PROVIDING A MAXIMUM OF TEN WORKING DAYS PERIOD FOR EVERY GOVERNMENT TRANSACTION AND PROVIDING PENALTIES IN VIOLATION THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Red Tape Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Red Tape Act of 2004." The bill seeks to reduce, if not totally eliminate, red tape by limiting the action the government may take in any official transaction to a total of 10 working days.
