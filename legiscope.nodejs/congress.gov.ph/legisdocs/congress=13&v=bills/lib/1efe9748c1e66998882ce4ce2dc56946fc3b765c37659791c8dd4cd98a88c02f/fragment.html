HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00322
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8293, ENTITLED 'AN ACT PRESCRIBING THE INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY CODE AND ESTABLISHING THE INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY OFFICE, PROVIDING FOR ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to amend the Intellectual Property Code through the integration of comprehensive, efficient and adequate strategies designed to respond to internet piracy. It gives as much concern to the rights of performers, phonogram producers and broadcasters. It also seeks to recognize their right to distribution and rental, and their right to receive payment for certain forms of broadcasting or communication.
