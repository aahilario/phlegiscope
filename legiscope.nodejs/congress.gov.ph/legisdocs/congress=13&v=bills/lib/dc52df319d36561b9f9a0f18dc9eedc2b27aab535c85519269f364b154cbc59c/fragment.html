HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02275
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING INSURANCE BENEFITS TO BARANGAY TANODS AND INCREASING THE AMOUNT OF THE COVERAGE OF INSURANCE BENEFITS OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6942 AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : This bill increases by at least fifty percent (50%) but not more than one hundred percent (100%), the insurance benefits accorded to all local officials who do not receive fixed salaries during their incumbency as per R.A. 6942.
