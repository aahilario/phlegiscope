HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03246
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES TO PROVIDE SCHOLARSHIP GRANTS TO AT LEAST FIVE PERCENT (5%) OF THEIR STUDENT POPULATION WHO ARE POOR BUT DESERVING STUDENTS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Scholarship for the Poor Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Scholarship for the Poor Act of 2004." The measure extends scholarship grants in the form of free tuition fee to at least 5% of the student population who are poor but deserving students.
