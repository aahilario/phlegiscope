House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04534
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE UTILIZATION AND MANAGEMENT OF NATURAL RESOURCES, ENVIRONMENTAL ASSETS OR SOCIAL AND ECOLOGICAL ENDOWMENTS FOR SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Environment Policy Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Environment Policy Act of 2005". The bill institutionalizes a policy that would integrate and harmonize sectoral environmental laws and issuances. It seeks to update environmental policy and keeps our system of environment management abreast with current trends in sustainable development policies.
