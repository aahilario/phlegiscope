ACTIONS TAKEN BY THE COMMITTEE
COMMITEE HEARINGS/ACTIONS:
      1. Under deliberation by the Mother Committee on 2010-12-07
          -TWG was created to consolidate the provisions of the Open High School System bills (HB Nos. 524, 648, 1524, 2343 and 3430)with the Alternative Learning System measures (HB Nos. 630 and 3637). Using HB 3637 as the reference bill.
      2. Under deliberation by the Mother Committee on 2011-08-02
          -Agreements reached: • The Director of the Bureau of Alternative Learning System (BALS) shall seek the favorable endorsement of the Department of Education (DepEd)) on their funding proposal, which reads: THE AMOUNT NECESSARY FOR THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THIS ACT SHALL REPRESENT NOT LESS THAN TEN PERCENT (10%) OF THE CURRENT YEAR’S BUDGET OF THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION. THEREAFTER, THE AMOUNT NECESSARY FOR THE CONTINUED OPERATION OF THE BALS AND THE ALS PROGRAM SHALL BE INCLUDED IN THE DEPED’S BUDGET IN THE GENERAL APPROPRIATIONS ACT. • Inclusion of a provision, which reads: THE ALTERNATIVE LEARNING SYSTEM (ALS) TEACHER - THE ALS TEACHER SHALL BE KNOWN AS MOBILE TEACHER. AS SUCH, THE MOBILE TEACHER SHALL FOLLOW A RELEVANT CAREER PATH UNIQUE TO THE CONDITIONS OF ALS AND IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE STANDARDS AND GUIDELINES OF THE CIVIL SERVICE COMMISSION. LIKEWISE, THE PROFESSIONAL REGULATION COMMISSION SHALL FORMULATE A LICENSURE EXAMINATION RELEVANT TO THE CONDITIONS OF ALS. • Include Representatives Piamonte, Escudero, Espina, S. Garin, Flores, and Palatino as co-authors of the substitute bill.
      3. Recommendations submitted by the TWG on 2011-08-02
          -1. Substitute Bill for House Bill No. 630 by Rep. Angara and House Bill No. 3637 by Rep. Escudero re the INSTITUTIONALIZATION OF THE ALTERNATIVE LEARNING SYSTEM
