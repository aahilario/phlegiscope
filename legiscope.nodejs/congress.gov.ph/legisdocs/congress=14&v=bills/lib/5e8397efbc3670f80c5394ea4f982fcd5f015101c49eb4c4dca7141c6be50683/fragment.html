HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03249
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING CYBER-TRAFFICKING AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Cyber Trafficking Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to impose the following penalties: a) imprisonment of two (2) to five (5) years and a fine of not less than P500,000 but not more than P1 million on any performer, actor or exhibitor of an indecent act; b) imprisonment of 15 to 20 years and a fine of not less than P1 million but not more than P2 million on the internet service provider or website operator; and c) imprisonment of 20 to 30 years and a fine of not less than P2 million but not more than P5 million on the producer or promoter of cyber-trafficking.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : UNGAB, ISIDRO T.
DATE FILED : 2007-12-06
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
