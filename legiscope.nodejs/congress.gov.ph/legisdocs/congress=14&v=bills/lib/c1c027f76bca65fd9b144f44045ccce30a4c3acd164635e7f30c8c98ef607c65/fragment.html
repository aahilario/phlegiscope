HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01008
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROMOTE AND DEVELOP THE SEED INDUSTRIES OF THE PHILIPPINES AND TO INSURE THE AVAILABILITY OF HIGH QUALITY SEEDS OF SUPERIOR CROP VARIETIES FOR NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTION
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine Seed Act"
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Seed Act". This bill seeks to develop the seeds industry in the country in order to produce, through intensive research and study, high quality varieties of seeds and, most importantly, to make them available in sufficient quantities to all farmers throughout the country.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : GARIN, JANETTE L.
