FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING MANDATORY BASIC IMMUNIZATION SERVICES FOR INFANTS INCLUDING HEPATITIS B VACCINATION
SHORT TITLE : �Basic Immunization Services for Infants Act�
ABSTRACT : �Basic Immunization Services for Infants Act�. The measure proposes a mandatory basic immunization service for infants, to wit: a) BCG vaccination against tuberculosis; b) inoculation against diphtheria, tetanus and pertussis; c) oral poliomyetitis immunization; d) protection against measles; e) immunization against hepatitis B; f) immunization against rubella; and g) other basic immunization services.
