House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00566
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 5173, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS AN ACT CREATING A PHILIPPINE COAST GUARD, PRESCRIBING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, APPROPRIATING THE NECESSARY FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES BY ESTABLISHING A SEPARATE AND DISTINCT PHILIPPINE COAST GUARD SERVICE ATTACHED TO THE DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION AND COMMUNICATIONS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Philippine Coast Guard Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Coast Guard Act of 2004". The bill intends to transfer the Philippine Coast Guard from the Philippine Navy to the Department of Transportation and Communications to enable it to concentrate on its functions of marine safety administration, marine environmental protection, marine search and rescue and maritime law enforcement. It is expected that said transfer would result in a unified and integrated approach to maritime safety and water transport development.
