HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00250
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO IMPROVE EFFICIENCY IN THE DELIVERY OF GOVERNMENT SERVICES TO THE PUBLIC BY REDUCING BUREAUCRATIC RED TAPE AND PROVIDING GRAFT AND CORRUPTION
SHORT TITLE : Red Tape Reduction Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Red Tape Reduction Act of 2004". In order to expeditiously and effectively deliver basic government services to the public, the proposed measure limits the number of signatories required in government transactions and streamlines procedures to attain a smooth flow in the processing of papers and documents.
