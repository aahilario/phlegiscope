HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00637
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING RESPECT FOR AND FOSTERING HUMAN RIGHTS IN THE SYSTEM OF FORMAL AND INFORMAL EDUCATION IN THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Department of Education and the Commission on Higher Education to include the study of human rights in the curricula of all levels of education in all schools in the country.
