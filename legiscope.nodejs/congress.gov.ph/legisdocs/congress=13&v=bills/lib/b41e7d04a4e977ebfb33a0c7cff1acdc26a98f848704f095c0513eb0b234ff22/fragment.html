House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01249
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ABOLISHING THE DEATH PENALTY REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7659 ENTITLED, 'AN ACT TO IMPOSE THE DEATH PENALTY ON CERTAIN HEINOUS CRIMES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AS AMENDED, OTHER SPECIAL PENAL LAWS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill abolishes death penalty and, in lieu thereof, imposes the penalty of reclusion perpetua. Justice must likewise be tempered with mercy in order for genuine repentance and change to have a chance to prosper. Hence, emphasis must be placed on restorative justice rather than retributive justice.
