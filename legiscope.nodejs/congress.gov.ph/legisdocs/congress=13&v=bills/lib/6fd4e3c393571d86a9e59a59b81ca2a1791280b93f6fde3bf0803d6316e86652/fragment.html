HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01491
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING AN AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTIVITY ENHANCEMENT FUND, IN EACH PROVINCE, CITY AND MUNICIPALITY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the proceeds generated from the impositionn of an additional real property tax on idle lands to accrue to the Fund and provides that 50% of the 20% development fund allotted to cities, province and municipalities to be used for the construction of roads, irrigation, post harvest facilities and other agricultural infrastructure.
