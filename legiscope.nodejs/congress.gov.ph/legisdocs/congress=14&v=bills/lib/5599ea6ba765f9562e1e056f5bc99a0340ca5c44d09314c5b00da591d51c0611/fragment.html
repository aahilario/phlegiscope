HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01073
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A FLOATING MEDICAL CENTER TO BE KNOWN AS THE PHILIPPINE FLOATING MEDICAL CENTER AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the Philippine Navy and the Department of Health to jointly maintain and operate the Philippine Floating Medical Center which shall visit the different provinces, municipalities and cities in the country.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : PICHAY, PHILIP A.
