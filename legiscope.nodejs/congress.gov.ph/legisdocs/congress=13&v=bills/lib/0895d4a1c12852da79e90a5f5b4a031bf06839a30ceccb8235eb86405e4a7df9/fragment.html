House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03978
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CLARIFYING APPLICABILITY OF SECTION 1, ARTICLE V OF THE CONSTITUTION TO DETENTION PRISONERS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The Detention Prisoners Voting Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "The Detention Prisoners Voting Act of 2005". The measure extends to persons under preventive detention, who are not otherwise disqualified by law, the right to vote for the president, vice-president, senators and party-list representatives.
