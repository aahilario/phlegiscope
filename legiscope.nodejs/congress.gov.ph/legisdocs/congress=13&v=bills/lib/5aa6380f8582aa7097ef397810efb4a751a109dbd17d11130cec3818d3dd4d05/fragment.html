House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01610
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MAGNA CARTA FOR SOCIAL WORKERS AND SOCIAL WELFARE AND DEVELOPMENT WORKERS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Magna Carta for Social Workers and Social Welfare and Development Workers
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta for Social Workers and Social Welfare and Development Workers." The bill provides for their compensation and benefits, the payment of overtime, defines their rights and prohibits their reassignment without justification.
