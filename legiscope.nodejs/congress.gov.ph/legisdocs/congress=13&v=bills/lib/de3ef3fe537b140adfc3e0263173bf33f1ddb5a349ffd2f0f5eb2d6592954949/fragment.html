FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SEC. 31 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1177, AS AMENDED, IN ORDER TO INSTITUTIONALIZE THE AUTOMATIC APPROPRIATION OF THE FUNDS REQUIRED TO COVER THE YEARLY SHORTAGES OF TEACHERS AND CLASSROOMS
ABSTRACT : The bill sets the teacher-student ratio at one teacher to forty-five students and the classroom-student ratio at one classroom for every forty five students.
