          -The Committee was informed that an exact version of the bill (HB 1483 DEFINING DISCRIMINATION ON THE BASIS OF SEXUAL ORIENTATION AND GENDER IDENTITY AND PROVIDING PENALTIES THERFOR/Rep. Teddy Casiño of Bayan Muna Party-List) has been referred to the Committee on Justice. The CWGE will facilitate HB 1483 change of referral from the Committee on Justice to the Committee on Women & Gender Equality (CWGE). All members present (Reps. M. Amante-Matba, M. Alvarez, E. de Jesus, J. Castro, L. Mercado-Revilla, S. Yap, E. Aglipay, J. Lacson-Noel, Herrera-Dy, C. Bagasina, R. Obillo, M. Bonoan-David) agreed to co-author a consolidated version of HBs 515 & 1483.
      2. Change of committee referral requested on 2011-10-11
          -The Committee on Justice requested for a change of referral of HB No.1483, a similar bill to HB No. 515, to the Committee on Women and Gender Equality (CWGE) and was conformed by the Chair of CWGE. Said request letter was submitted to the Committee on Rules on 13 October 2011.
