House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01089
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE STANDARDS FOR THE TRAINING AND PRACTICE OF REHABILITATION MEDICINE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Medical Rehabilitation Standards Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Medical Rehabilitation Standards Act of 2004." To ensure that rehabilitation centers function with utmost competence, the bill seeks to set standards for the practice, training and certification of rehabilitation medicine to ensure the highest quality of professional rehabilitation service to the public. It also sets standards for the technical and operational aspects of rehabilitation centers.
