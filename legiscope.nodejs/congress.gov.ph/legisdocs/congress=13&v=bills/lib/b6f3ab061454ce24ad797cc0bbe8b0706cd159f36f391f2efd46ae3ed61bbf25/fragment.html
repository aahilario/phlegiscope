House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04356
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CREDIT INFORMATION SYSTEM, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Credit Information System Act
ABSTRACT : "Credit Information System Act". The measure proposes the establishment of the Credit Infomation Bureau (CIB) and the Credit Information System. The CIB shall have the power to collect and disseminate credit-related infomration.
