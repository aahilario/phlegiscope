House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05371
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN WITHIN SMALL ISLANDS AS AGRICULTURAL LAND OPEN TO DISPOSITION FOR COMMERCIAL ECO-TOURISM AND OTHER PRODUCTIVE PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Declaring Certain Parcels of Land of the Public Domain Within Small Islands as Agricultural Land Open to Disposition for Commercial Eco-Tourism
ABSTRACT : The bill declares certain parcels of land located in small islands that have already been subject to a cadastral survey as agricultural lands open to disposition for eco-tourism and other productive purposes.
