House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00436
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A SYSTEM OF DISPOSITION OF SEIZED/CONFISCATED ILLEGALY CUT, GATHERED, REMOVED, AND POSSESSED LOGS, LUMBER, FLITCHES, AND OTHER FOREST PRODUCTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : System of Disposition of Seized/Confiscated Illegally Cut, Gathered, Removed and Possessed Logs and Other Forest Products
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to prohibit the auctioning of seized/confiscated illegally cut, gathered, removed or processed logs, lumber, flitches and other forest products by the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) through the Natural Resources Development Corporation. It provides a system whereby the DENR shall dispose and apportion confiscated forest products.
