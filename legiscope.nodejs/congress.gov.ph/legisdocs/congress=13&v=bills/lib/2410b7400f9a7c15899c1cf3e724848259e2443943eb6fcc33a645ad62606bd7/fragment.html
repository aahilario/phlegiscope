House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03133
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN SECTIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 2640, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "AN ACT TO CREATE A PUBLIC CORPORATION TO BE KNOWN AS THE VETERANS FEDERATION OF THE PHILIPPINES, DEFINING ITS POWERS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES"
ABSTRACT : The bill relieves the Secretary of National Defense of the responsibility of micro-managing the Veterans Federation of the Philippines, a purely civilian organization composed of voluntary members.
