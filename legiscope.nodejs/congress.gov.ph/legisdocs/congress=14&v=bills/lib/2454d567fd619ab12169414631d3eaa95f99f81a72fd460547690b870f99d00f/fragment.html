House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00063
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE TOLL REGULATORY BOARD TO CONDUCT PUBLIC HEARINGS PRIOR TO THE APPROVAL OF TOLL RATES OR ANY INCREASE THEREIN, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBERED EIGHTEEN HUNDRED NINETY-FOUR
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the Toll Regulatory Board to conduct public hearings prior to the approval of initial toll rates at any infrastructure still to be constructed or any proposal to increase the toll rates of existing ones.
