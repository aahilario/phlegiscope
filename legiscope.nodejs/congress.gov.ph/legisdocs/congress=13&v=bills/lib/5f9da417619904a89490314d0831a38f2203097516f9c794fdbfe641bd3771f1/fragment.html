HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00845
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH TO PROVIDE AT LEAST ONE (1) DENTIST IN EVERY MUNICIPALITY OF EVERY PROVINCE, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to provide one (1) dentist in every municipality of every province under the control and supervision of the Department of Health. Directs the Department of Health to issue rules and regulations for the effective implementation of this Act.
