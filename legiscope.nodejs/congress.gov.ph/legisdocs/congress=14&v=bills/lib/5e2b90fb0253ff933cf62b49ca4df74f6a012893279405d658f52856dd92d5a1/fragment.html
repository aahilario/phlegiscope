HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03458
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CLASSIFYING TRANSACTIONS INVOLVING PETROLEUM PRODUCTS AND RAW MATERIALS AS VALUE-ADDED TAX EXEMPT TRANSACTIONS AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 109 (1) OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8424, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE TAX REFORM ACT OF 1997, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9337, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to exempt the sale or importation of petroleum products (except lubricating oil, grease, wax, petrolatum, and coal and natural gas) and raw materials used in the manufacture of the same from the reformed value-added tax.
