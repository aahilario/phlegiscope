FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE COMMISSION ON PRE-SCHOOL EDUCATION, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : The Commission on Pre-School Education Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "The Commission on Pre-School Education Act of 2004." The bill seeks to create the Commission on Preparatory School Education, define its powers and functions and appropriate the necessary funding for its establishment and operation.
