HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03051
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING LOCAL GOVERNMENTS TO EARMARK A PORTION OF THEIR INTERNAL REVENUE ALLOTMENTS FOR FREE MEDICINES TO INDIGENT PATIENTS IN THEIR LOCALITIES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill directs local government units to set aside a percentage of their internal revenue allotment budget for the acquisition of medicines for distribution through public hospitals, clinics, dispensaries or centers to indigent patients for free.
