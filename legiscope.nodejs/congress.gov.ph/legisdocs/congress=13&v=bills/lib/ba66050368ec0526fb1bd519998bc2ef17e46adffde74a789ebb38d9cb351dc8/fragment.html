HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00798
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CIVIL SERVICE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill updates all civil service related laws to conform with the 1987 Constitution and with present day needs and realities. It addresses the problems of graft and corruption, red tape, violations of employees' rights, violations of merit and fitness principle, among others. It codifies the various laws and other issuances into one comprehensive law.
