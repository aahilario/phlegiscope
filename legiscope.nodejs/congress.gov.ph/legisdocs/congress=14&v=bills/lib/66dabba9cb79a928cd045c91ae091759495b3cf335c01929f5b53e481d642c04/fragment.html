HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02542
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REPEALING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 217, AS AMENDED, ENTITLED, "ESTABLISHING BASIC POLICIES FOR THE TELEPHONE INDUSTRY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE PERTINENT PROVISIONS OF COMMONWEALTH ACT NO. 146, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE PUBLIC SERVICE ACT", THE FRANCHISE OF THE PHILIPPINE LONG DISTANCE TELEPHONE COMPANY UNDER ACT NO. 3426, AS AMENDED, AND ALL INCONSISTENT LEGISLATIVE AND MUNICIPAL FRANCHISES INCLUDING OTHER EXISTING LAWS
ABSTRACT : In view of the efficient and effective telecommunications system now existing in the country, the bill proposes the repeal of PD 217 and Act No. 3426 to even the playing field among industry players.
