House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04386
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE PROLIFERATION OF INDECENCY USING THE DIFFERENT FORMS OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
SHORT TITLE : Internet Indecency Act
ABSTRACT : "Internet Indecency Act". The bill prohibits any person from engaging in any form of pornographic exploitation using any information technology such as cyberspace, satellites and mobile phones.
