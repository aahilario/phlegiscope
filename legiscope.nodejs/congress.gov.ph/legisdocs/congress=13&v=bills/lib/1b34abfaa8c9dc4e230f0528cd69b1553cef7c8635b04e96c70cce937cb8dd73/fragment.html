House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03363
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING AS CONDITION PRECEDENT A HUMAN RIGHTS CLEARANCE IN EVERY APPOINTMENT AND PROMOTION OF ALL OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES IN THE GOVERNMENT
SHORT TITLE : Human Rights Clearance Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Human Rights Clearance Act of 2004." The bill requires a human rights clearance from the Commission on Human Commission prior to the appointment or promotion of all public servants regardless of rank and employment status and whether elective or appointive.
