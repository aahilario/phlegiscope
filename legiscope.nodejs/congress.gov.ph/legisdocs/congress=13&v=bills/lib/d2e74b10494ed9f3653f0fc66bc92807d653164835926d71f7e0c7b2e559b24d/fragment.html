House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04521
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPROVING THE SOCIALIZED HOUSING PROGRAM, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7279 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "URBAN DEVELOPMENT AND HOUSING ACT OF 1992"
SHORT TITLE : "Revised Socialized Housing Act of 2005".
ABSTRACT : "Revised Socialized Housing Act of 2005". The bill proposes to give local government units a free hand to recommend to developers the schemes for the acquisition and disposition of lands within their localities for socialized housing purposes.
