HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02994
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE RETIREMENT BENEFITS OF MEMBERS OF THE JUDICIARY, FURTHER AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 1 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 910, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the retirement benefits of retired justices to be automatically increased whenever there is an increase in the salary of incumbent justices and judges of the same rank.
