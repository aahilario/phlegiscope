House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03077
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3815, ALSO KNOWN AS THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AS AMENDED, ARTICLE 186 ON MONOPOLIES AND COMBINATIONS IN RESTRAINT OF TRADE
SHORT TITLE :
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to penalize monopolies and combinations in restraint of trade with prision mayor or a fine not exceeding P1,000,000 if a corporation or P500,000 if a person, or both.
