House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02889
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR A NATIONAL FOLIC ACID EDUCATION PROGRAM TO PREVENT BIRTH DEFECTS
SHORT TITLE : "Folic Acid Promotion and Birth Defects Prevention Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Secretary of Health to launch a program promoting the consumption of a dietary supplement that would provide the daily folic acid requirements of pregnant women.
