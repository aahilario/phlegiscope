House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02081
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING DISCRIMINATION AGAINST A PERSON BY REASON OF AGE UPON HIRING FOR AND DURING EMPLOYMENT, PENALIZING ANY VIOLATION THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Age Discrimination in Employment Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Age Discrimination in Employment Act of 2007". The measure seeks to stop discrimination against individuals with respect to compensation and terms and conditions of employment on account of age.
