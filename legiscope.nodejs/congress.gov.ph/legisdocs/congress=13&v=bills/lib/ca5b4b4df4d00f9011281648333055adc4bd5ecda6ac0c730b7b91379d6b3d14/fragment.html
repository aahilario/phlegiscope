House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03716
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A LOCAL TOURISM OFFICE IN EVERY MUNICIPALITY THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes to establish a local tourism office in every municipality so that local folks who know the place best will be able to advertise their own culture and heritage.
