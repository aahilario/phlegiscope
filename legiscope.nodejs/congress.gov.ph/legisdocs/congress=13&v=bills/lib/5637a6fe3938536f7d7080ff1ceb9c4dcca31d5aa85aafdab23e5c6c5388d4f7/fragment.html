House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02719
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A MARITIME TRANSPORT SAFETY COUNCIL IN EVERY PORT OF ORIGIN THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY, PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Maritime Transport Safety Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Maritime Transport Safety Act of 2004." The bill prescribes the powers, functions and organizational structure of the Council and penalizes negligent and abusive government officials and employees and owners and unseaworthy passenger ships and transport vessels.
