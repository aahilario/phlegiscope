HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00002
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PRESCRIBING A FIXED TERM FOR THE CHIEF OF STAFF AND MAJOR SERVICE COMMANDERS OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to fix the term of office of the Chief of Staff and the Major Service Commanders of the Armed Forces of the Philippines to give them sufficient time to complete their programs.
