House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01903
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AMEND REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8239, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE PHILIPPINE PASSPORT ACT OF 1996
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the requirements in the issuance of passports and the grounds for amendments, suspension or revocation. Enumerates the individuals qualified to file applications in behalf of the applicants. Increases the penalties to persons faking passports.
