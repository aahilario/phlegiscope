House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03728
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE COCONUT LEVY FUNDS AND ASSETS ACQUIRED AND ESTABLISHED THEREFROM AS PUBLIC FUNDS IN THE NATURE 0F GENUINE SMALL COCONUT FARMERS' FUND AND CREATING THE COCONUT INDUSTRY DEVELOPMENT COUNCIL FOR PURPOSES OF ADMINISTRATION AND MANAGEMENT THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : "Genuine Small Coconut Farmers' Funds Act of 2005"
ABSTRACT : "Genuine Small Coconut Farmers' Funds Act of 2005." The bill provides a mechanism so that the rights and interests of small farmers to the coconut levy funds can be protected. It establishes the Coconut Industry Rehabilitation and Development Council and provides for its composition, terms of office, powers and functions.
