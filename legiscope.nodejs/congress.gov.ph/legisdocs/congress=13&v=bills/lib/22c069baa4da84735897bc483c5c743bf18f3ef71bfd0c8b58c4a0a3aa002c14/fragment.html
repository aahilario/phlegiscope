HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01908
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CONSERVING, PROMOTING AND POPULARIZING THE NATION'S HISTORICAL AND CULTURAL HERITAGE AND RESOURCES, AS WELL AS ARTISTIC CREATIONS BY EXACTING A PERFORMANCE FEE FROM FOREIGN PERFORMERS AND ALLOCATING THE PROCEEDS THEREOF TO THE NATIONAL COMMISSION ON CULTURE AND ARTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Singers Equity Act
ABSTRACT : "Singers Equity Act." To protect local entertainers, the bill seeks to charge a foreign performer an equity fee equivalent to 15% of his total talent fee which shall be remitted to the National Commission on Culture and Arts.
