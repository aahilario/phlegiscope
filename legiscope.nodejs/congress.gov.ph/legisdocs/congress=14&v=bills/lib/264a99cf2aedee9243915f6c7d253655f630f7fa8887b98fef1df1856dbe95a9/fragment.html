HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00713
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING GOVERNMENT AGENCIES TO AWARD NATIONALLY FUNDED PROJECTS COSTING FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND PESOS BELOW TO NON-GOVERNMENTAL ORGANIZATIONS
SHORT TITLE : "Empowering NGO's in Government Declared Calamity Areas and Facilitating Participation on Projects Funded by the National Government"
ABSTRACT : "Empowering NGO's in Government Declared Calamity Areas and Facilitating Participation on Projects Funded by the National Government". The bill authorizes government agencies to award nationally-funded projects costing P1.5 million and below to non-government organizations engaged in the promotion of livelihood and community projects in calamity devastated areas.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : LIM, RENO G.
