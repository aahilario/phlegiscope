House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00379
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE WILLFUL ACT OF CAUSING FORCE OR INTIMIDATION OR HARRASSMENT OR INSENUATION OR ANY SORT OF PROPOSAL TO COMPEL AND CONVINCE ANY PERSON UNDER ONE'S CHARGE TO RENDER SERVICES IN PAYMENT OF DEBTS, PROVIDING STIFFER PENALTIES THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Household Services Protect Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Household Services Protection Act of 2004". The law prohibits involuntary servitude in any form. The bill attempts to establish a mechanism to stop the prevailing involuntary servitude especially among women and children. It proposes the imposition of the penalty of arresto mayor in its maximum period to prision correctional in its minimum period on any person who compels a debtor to work for him against the debtor's will without any due compensation.
