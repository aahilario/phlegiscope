House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02873
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 4 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 968, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE PROBATION LAW OF 1976
ABSTRACT : The bill gives the accused the right to apply for probation despite his appeal from the judgment of conviction if, after the appeal, the decision of the trial court is affirmed and the penalty imposed by the appellate court is still probationable.
