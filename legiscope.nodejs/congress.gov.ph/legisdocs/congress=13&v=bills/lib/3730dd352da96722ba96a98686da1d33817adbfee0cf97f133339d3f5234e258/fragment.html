HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02821
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL PERSONNEL OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES AND THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE TO UNDERGO A TWO-MONTH BASIC COURSE ON THE PHILIPPINE CONSTITUTION AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill requires all military and police personnel to undergo a two-month basic course on the Constitution to inculcate in them respect for human rights and a high regard for established laws.
