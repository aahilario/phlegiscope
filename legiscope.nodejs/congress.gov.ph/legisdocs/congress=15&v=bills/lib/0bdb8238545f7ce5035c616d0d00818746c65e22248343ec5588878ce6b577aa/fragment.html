HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00249
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ADOPTING A SALARY SCHEDULE FOR THE MEMBERS OF THE BENCH AND OTHER LAWYERS IN THE JUDICIARY, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Aims to: a) raise the level of competence and ensure the integrity of the justices, judges and lawyers in the Judiciary, and; b) improve the compensation system for the members of the bench, including the court personnel, to be able to attract the best and the brightest to the Judiciary.
