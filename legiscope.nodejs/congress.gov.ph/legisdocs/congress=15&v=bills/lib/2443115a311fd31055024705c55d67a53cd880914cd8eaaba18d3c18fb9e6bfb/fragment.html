HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00105
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 4 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9184, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'GOVERNMENT PROCUREMENT REFORM ACT'
ABSTRACT : Removes any ambiguity in the interpretation of the scope and application of RA09184 by amending Section 4 thereof to make it very clear that it applies to all government procurement activities, regardless of source of funds, whether local or foreign, and that only treaties or international or executive agreements entered into by the government prior to its enactment shall be exempt from its coverage.
