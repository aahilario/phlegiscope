House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01409
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE POLITICAL PARTY SYSTEM
SHORT TITLE : Political Party Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Political Party Act of 2004." The bill aims to transform political parties from being personality-oriented to issue-oriented organizations. Provides that any member of the party changing his affiliation after being elected on his party's ticket should first resign from his elective position and seek a fresh mandate.
