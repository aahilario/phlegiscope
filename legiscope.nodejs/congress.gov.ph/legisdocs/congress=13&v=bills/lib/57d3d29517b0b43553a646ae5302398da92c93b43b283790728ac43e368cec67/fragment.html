HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01637
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE PROMOTION OF BIO-ORGANIC FARMING IN THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to establish a comprehensive bio-organic farming program through the propagation of bio-organic cultivation and production methods. It also seeks the creation of a Bio-Organic Farming Commission that shall be tasked to implement the program.
