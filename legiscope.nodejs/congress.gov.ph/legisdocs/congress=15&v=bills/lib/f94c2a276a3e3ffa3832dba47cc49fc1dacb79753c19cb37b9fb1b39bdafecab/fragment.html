ACTIONS TAKEN BY THE COMMITTEE
COMMITEE HEARINGS/ACTIONS:
      1. Scheduled for first meeting/hearing on 2011-05-31
          -Suggestions: • To provide a provision on when the tests would be administered and at what level in high school should it be administered. • To provide a provision for a more personal, one-to-one counseling to high school students for their career choice. • Leniency with the requirements of the Guidance Counselor profession in order to entice students to pursue a Guidance Counseling career. • Rewording of Section 10 to be clear that the system will not be mandatory but shall serve as guidance to students in choosing a career path. • Impose a punitive provision on ranking and advertisement of schools as mentioned in Section 14. • Make private educational institutions part of the system. • For DepEd to study the German Dual Education System which may be replicable and appropriate to the Philippine educational setting considering the commencement of Enhanced K to 12 Basic Education System. • For DepEd to comment on the cut-off score as provided in Section 10. Agreements reached: • Technical Working Group was created to fine-tune the provisions of the bill. • Committee shall await the endorsement of the DepEd prior to its final action on HB 1753.
