House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01631
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO GRANT PUBLIC SHCOOL TEACHERS FREE OF CHARGE X-RAY AND OTHER FORMS OF LABORATORY EXAMINATIONS IN ANY GOVERNMENT AND PRIVATE HOSPITALS DURING THEIR REQUIRED ANNUAL PHYSICAL EXAMINATION AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Free Physical Examination for Public School Teachers Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Free Physical Examination for Public School Teachers Act of 2004." In order to relieve teachers of additional costs during the mandatory annual physical examination, the bill seeks to grant teachers free x-ray and other laboratory examinations in government and private hospitals.
