House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01454
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING MEASURES T0 ENSURE PEDESTRIAN SAFETY AND CONVENIENCE
SHORT TITLE : Pedestrian Safety Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Pedestrian Safety Act of 2004." The bill declares that all obstructions along roads and sidewalks are nuisances subject to summary abatement without need of judicial proceedings. Aims to solve road traffic, sidewalk congestion and hazards to pedestrians.
