HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01978
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MANDATORY INCREASE IN THE MINIMUM WAGE RATES OF HOUSEHELPERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the minimum wage for househelpers (kasambahay) in highly urbanized cities from P800 to 3,000 per month, in chartered cities and first class municipalities from P650 to P1,000 per month, and in other municipalities from P550 to P800 per month.
