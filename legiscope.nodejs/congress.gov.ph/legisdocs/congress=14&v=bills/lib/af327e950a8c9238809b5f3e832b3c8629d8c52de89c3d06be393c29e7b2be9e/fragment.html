HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00590
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE PROVISION OF FIREARMS, AMMUNITIONS, EXPLOSIVES, CHEMICAL, BIOLOGICAL OR NUCLEAR WEAPONS OR ANY PARTS OR COMPONENTS USED IN THE MANUFACTURE THEREOF TO ENEMIES OF THE STATE
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits any person, natural or juridical, to knowingly sell, transfer, or in any manner, provide, directly or indirectly, any firearms, ammunitions, explosives, chemical, biological or nuclear weapons or any parts or components used in the manufacture thereof, to enemies of the State.
