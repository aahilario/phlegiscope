House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01387
REPUBLIC ACT NO. RA10070
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE PERSONS WITH DISABILITIES AFFAIRS OFFICE IN EVERY PROVINCE, CITY AND MUNICIPALITY, AMENDING SECTION 40 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7277, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS 'AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE REHABILITATION, SELF-DEVELOPMENT AND SELF-RELIANCE OF DISABLED PERSONS AND THEIR INTEGRATION INTO THE MAINSTREAM OF SOCIETY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Creating The Persons With Disabilities Affairs Office In Every Province, City And Municipality
ABSTRACT : To ensure that persons with disabilities enjoy the services provided under RA 7277, the bill mandates the establishment of a Persons with Disabilities Affairs Office in every province, city and municipality.
