House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01349
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 99 AND 262 OR BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 881, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE OMNIBUS ELECTION CODE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : RA 7166 failed to decriminalize acts with respect to persons making contributions to candidates and political parties. The bill seeks to remedy the situation by decriminalizing violations to Sections 98 and 99 of the Omnibus Election Code. Nevertheless, it imposes on persons who violate these sections administrative fines similar to those provided in Section 14 of RA 7166 which are imposed on candidates and political parties who fail to file a report on campaign contributions and expenditures. In addition to the fines, a contributor who fails to comply with Sections 98 and 99 will not be able to claim exemption from donor's tax.
