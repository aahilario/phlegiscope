HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00083
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE PAYMENT THROUGH VETERANS BONDS OF ARREARS IN THE VETERANS PENSIONS MANDATED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7696, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the Treasurer of the Philippines to issue to veterans Certificates of Indebtedness in payment of arrearages in their pensions. The certificates may be used as payment for goods and services such as rice from National Food Authority, real estate taxes, tuition fees, amortization of housing loans, utility bills, among others.
