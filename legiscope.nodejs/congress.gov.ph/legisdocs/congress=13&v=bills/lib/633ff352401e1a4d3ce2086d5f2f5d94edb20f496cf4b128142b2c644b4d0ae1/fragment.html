HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01612
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO RATIONALIZE THE COMPOSITION AND FUNCTIONS OF THE NATIONAL LABOR RELATIONS COMMISSION, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE ARTICLES 213, 214, 215, AND 216 OF P.D. 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to increase the members of the National Labor Relations Commission from fourteen to twenty three members with eight members each coming from the nominees of the workers and employers organizations. Likewise, the existing five divisions of the Commission shall be increased to eight.
