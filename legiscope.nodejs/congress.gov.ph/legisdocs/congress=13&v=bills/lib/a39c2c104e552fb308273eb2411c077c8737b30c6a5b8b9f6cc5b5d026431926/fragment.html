House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02449
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A COLLEGE EDUCATION TRUST FUND FOR CHILDREN OF GOVERNMENT SERVICE INSURANCE SYSTEM (GSIS) AND SOCIAL SECURITY SYSTEM (SSS) MEMBERS
SHORT TITLE : College Education Trust Fund Act
ABSTRACT : "College Education Trust Fund Act." The bill seeks to establish in the Government Service Insurance Corporation and the Social Services System a special trust fund to ensure the college education of the children (legitimate, legitimated, legally adopted, illegitimate or grandchild) of the members of both systems.
