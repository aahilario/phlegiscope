HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01746
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO STRENGTHEN LOCAL ENVIRONMENT PROTECTION BY MAKING THE APPOINTMENT OF AN ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES OFFICER MANDATORY FOR PROVINCIAL, CITY AND MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENTS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 484 (A) OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991"
ABSTRACT : Under the Local Government Code, the appointment of an environment and natural resources officer is merely optional for local government units. To prevent the degradation of our ecosystem, the bill makes the appointment of the environment and natural resources officer mandatory.
