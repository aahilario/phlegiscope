HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01269
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR FIREARMS REGULATION AND EXEMPTING SELECTED PERSONS AND ENTITIES FROM THE GENERAL PROHIBITION AGAINST CARRYING OF FIREARMS
ABSTRACT : This bill identifies the persons who are exempted from the general prohibition to carry firearms in public. It also provides the standards for the issuance of a firearms license and permit to carry firearms.
