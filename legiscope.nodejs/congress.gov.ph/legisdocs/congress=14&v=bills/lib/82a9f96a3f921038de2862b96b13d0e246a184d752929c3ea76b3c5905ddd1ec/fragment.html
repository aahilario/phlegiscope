House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01128
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING WOMEN'S AND GIRLS' HUMAN RIGHTS AWARENESS PROGRAMS IN MEDIA, PROVIDING INCENTIVES THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Women's and Girls' Human Rights Awareness Program in Media Act of 2003"
ABSTRACT : "Women's and Girls' Human Rights Awareness Program in Media Act of 2003". The measure requires all broadcasting networks to allot 15% of its daily total air time to the promotion, protection and advocay of women's and girls' human rights, welfare, development and gender sensitivity and equality.
