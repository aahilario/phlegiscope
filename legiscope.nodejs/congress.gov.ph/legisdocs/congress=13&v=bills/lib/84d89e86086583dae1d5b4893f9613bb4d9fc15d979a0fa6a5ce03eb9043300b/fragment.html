House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03319
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE PRACTICE OF REAL ESTATE PROFESSION IN THE PHILIPPINES, CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE A PROFESSIONAL REGULATORY BOARD OF REAL ESTATE PROFESSION, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Real Estate Profession Act of the Philippines
ABSTRACT : "Real Estate Profession Act of the Philippines." The bill aims to professionalize and upgrade the real estate practice in the country to make it globally competitive. It provides for the creation of the Professional Regulatory Board which shall be tasked to supervise and monitor all real estate transactions, stabilize the prices of land and construction materials, and ensure the collection of the correct amount of taxes.
