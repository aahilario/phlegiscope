HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00253
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING ORGANIZATION AND COOPERATIVES OF PERSONS WITH DISABILITY DULY REGISTERED WITH THE SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE COMMISSION AND THE DEPARTMENT OF SOCIAL WELFARE AND DEVELOPMENT, TO OPERATE LIVELIHOOD ACTIVITIES, BOOTHS OR FACILITIES IN GOVERNMENT BUILDINGS AND THEIR PREMISES FOR REMUNERATIVE EMPLOYMENT, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure provides organizations and cooperatives of persons with disability the authority to operate livelihood facilities, activities, commercial booths and other related remunerative employment opportunities in government buildings and their premises.
