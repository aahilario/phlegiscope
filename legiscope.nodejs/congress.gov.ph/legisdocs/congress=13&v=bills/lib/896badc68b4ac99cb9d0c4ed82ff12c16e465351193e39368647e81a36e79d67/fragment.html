HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02682
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROMOTE PUBLIC SAFETY EDUCATION AND AWARENESS AMONG ELEMENTARY, HIGH SCHOOL AND COLLEGE STUDENTS TO DEVELOP SAFETY AND SECURITY AWARENESS IN OUR CITIZENRY, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to establish a Public Safety Education and Awareness Program to be included in the Department of Education, Technical Education and Skills Development Authority and the Commission on Higher Education curriculum standards for all public and private schools.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : EULOGIO "AMANG" R. MAGSAYSAY, Ph.D.
