House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02158
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE LESSOR TO DEPOSIT IN BANK THE CASH DEPOSIT OF A LESSEE IN A LEASE AGREEMENT, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION THREE OF BATAS PAMBANSA BILANG WALONG DAAN PITUMPOT PITO ENTITLED "AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE STABILIZATION AND REGULATION OF RENTALS OF CERTAIN RESIDENTIAL UNITS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES", AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill requires the lessor to deposit in any bank the cash deposit tendered by the lessee. It directs the lessor to turn over to the lessee any and all interest earned from the deposit at the expiration of the lease contract.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CATALINO V. FIGUEROA
