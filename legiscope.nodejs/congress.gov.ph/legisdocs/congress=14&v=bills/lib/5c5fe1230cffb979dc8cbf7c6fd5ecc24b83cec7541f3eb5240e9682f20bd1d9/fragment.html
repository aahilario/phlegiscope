HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00111
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING THE PRE-SCHOOL EDUCATION, INCREASING THE MONTHLY COMPENSATION OF DAY CARE WORKERS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Pre-School Development Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Pre-School Development Act of 2007". This measure aims to institutionalize pre-school education as part of compulsory basic education. The barangay day care centers shall constitute the public sector pre-school institutions.
