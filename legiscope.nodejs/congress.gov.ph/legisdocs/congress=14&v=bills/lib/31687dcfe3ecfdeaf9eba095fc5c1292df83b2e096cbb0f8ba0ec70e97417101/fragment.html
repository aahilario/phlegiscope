House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00069
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING CIVIL SERVICE ELIGIBILITY TO CONTRACTUAL AND CO-TERMINUS GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES WHO HAVE RENDERED A TOTAL OF FIVE (5) YEARS OF EFFICIENT SERVICE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Contractual and co-terminous employees are part of the civil service. However, they can not be given permanent appointment due to lack of appropriate civil service eligibility. HB00069 proposes to grant civil service eligibility to contractual and co-terminous government employees who have rendered a total of five (5) years of efficient service. The proposal would promote efficiency in the service considering that these employees will have the assurance of security of tenure and prevent the frequent turnover of personnel.
