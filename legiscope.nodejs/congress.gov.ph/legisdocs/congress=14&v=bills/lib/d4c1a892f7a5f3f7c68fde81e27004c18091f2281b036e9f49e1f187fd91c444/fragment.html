FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT 9064, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE SPORTS INCENTIVES ACT AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure grants cash rewards to differently-abled athletes who win gold, silver and/or bronze medals in paralympic games and special olympics world games.
