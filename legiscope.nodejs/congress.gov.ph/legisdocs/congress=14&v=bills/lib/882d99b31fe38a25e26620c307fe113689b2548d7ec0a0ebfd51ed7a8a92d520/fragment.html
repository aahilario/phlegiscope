HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01847
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REGULATE HOMES ESTABLISHED FOR THE RESIDENTIAL CARE OF PERSON WHO HAVE ATTAINED THE AGE OF 60 YEARS OLD, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Residential Care Homes for Elderly Persons of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The Residential Care Homes for Elderly Persons of 2007". The bill seeks to increase the service capacity of home-based services for the elderly. It directs residential care homes to provide the elderly economic activities according to their needs and capabilities.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : LIM, RENO G.
DATE FILED : 2007-08-07
