HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00993
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE LAND MANAGEMENT BUREAU TO CONDUCT THE CADASTRAL SURVEY OF THE ENTIRE COUNTRY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Cadastral Survey Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Cadastral Survey Act of 2004". The bill authorizes the conduct of a cadastral survey of the entire country to identify bona fide landowners or claimants and to eradicate the proliferation of fake or spurious titles.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SOLIS, JOSE G.
