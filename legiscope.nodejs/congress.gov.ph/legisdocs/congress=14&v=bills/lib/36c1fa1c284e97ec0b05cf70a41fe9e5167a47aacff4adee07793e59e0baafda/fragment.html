HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02165
FULL TITLE : ISANG BATAS NA NAGTUTURING SA PANDARAYA BILANG KRIMEN, PAGPATAW NG PARUSA DITO AT IBA PANG LAYUNIN (AN ACT QUALIFYING CHEATING AS A CRIME, IMPOSING A PENALTY THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES)
SHORT TITLE : "Batas Laban sa Pandaraya" ("The Anti-Cheating Law")
ABSTRACT : "Batas Laban sa Pandaraya" ("The Anti-Cheating Law"). The bill criminalizes the act of cheating and penalizes perpetrators thereof with imprisonment of three (3) years and a fine of P100,000.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : JOSON, EDUARDO NONATO N.
DATE FILED : 2007-08-21
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
