HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03497
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE DELINEATION OF THE SPECIFIC FOREST LIMITS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill directs the Department of Environment and Natural Resources to undertake the classification of the remaining unclassified lands of the public domain and to conducts a ground delineation of all classified and/or reclassified forestlands that shall constitute as permanent forestlands.
