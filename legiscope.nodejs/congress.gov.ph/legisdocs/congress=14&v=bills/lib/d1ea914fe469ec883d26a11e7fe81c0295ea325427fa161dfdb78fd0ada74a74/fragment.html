HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00490
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 21 (b) OF REPUBLIC ACT 8749, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CLEAN AIR ACT OF 1999, FOR THE PURPOSE OF EMPOWERING LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS TO CONDUCT SMOKE EMISSION TESTING
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes cities and municipalities to put up emission testing centers within their jurisdiction to be managed and operated by the local government unit concerned.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : PUENTEVELLA, MONICO O.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-02
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
