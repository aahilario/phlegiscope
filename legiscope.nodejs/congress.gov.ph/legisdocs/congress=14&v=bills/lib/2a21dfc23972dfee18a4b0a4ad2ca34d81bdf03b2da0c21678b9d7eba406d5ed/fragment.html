House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00958
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 24 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED, REDUCING THE INCOME TAX RATES OF INDIVIDUALS
SHORT TITLE : "Individual Income Tax Reduction Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Individual Income Tax Reduction Act of 2007". The bill proposes to exempt from income taxation the first P50,000 of the taxable income of taxpayers. It also proposes to reduce the marginal tax rates.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : HONTIVEROS, ANA THERESIA "RISA"
