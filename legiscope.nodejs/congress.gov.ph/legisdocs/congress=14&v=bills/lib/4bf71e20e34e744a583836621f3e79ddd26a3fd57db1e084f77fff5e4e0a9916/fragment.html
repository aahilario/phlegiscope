House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03161
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR AN AGRICULTURAL AND AGRARIAN REFORM CREDIT AND FINANCING SYSTEM THROUGH BANKING INSTITUTIONS, REPEALING PD 717 THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The measure directs all banks to set aside at least 25% of their loanable funds for agriculatural and agrarian reform credit for small farmers, fisherfolks, agrarian reform beneficiaries, farmers and fisherfolks organizations and cooperatives and agri-related micro enterprises.
