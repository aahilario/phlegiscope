HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03549
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DIRECTED AT RAISING THE QUALITY OF THE LABOR FORCE AND INCREASING LABOR PRODUCTIVITY BY INSTITUTIONALIZING THE PROVISION OF BASIC EDUCATION TO WORKERS
ABSTRACT : The bill directs employers to draw up a work schedule that would allow their employees who are under 40 years old and have not completed high school time to attend school or do home study to complete their basic education (elementary and high schools).
