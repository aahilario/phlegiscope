House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01979
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THE MANDATORY COVERAGE OF ALL HOUSEHELPERS IN THE NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE PROGRAM (NHIP) UNDER THE PHILIPPINE HEALTH INSURANCE CORPORATION (PHILHEALTH)
SHORT TITLE : Requiring the Mandatory Coverage of All Househelpers in the National Health Insurance Program (NHIP) Under the Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (PHILHEALTH)
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the enrollment of all househelpers (kasambahay)in the National Helath Insurance Program or Philhealth. It also provides for the employers of the househelpers to pay the month premiums to Philhealth.
