House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05895
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE AS A CLOSED CAREER SERVICE THEREBY AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 292 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'ADMINISTRATIVE CODE OF 1987'
SHORT TITLE : Declaring the Philippine National Police as a Closed Career Service
ABSTRACT : The bill declares the Philippine National Police as among those classified as a closed career service enabling it to establish and maintain its own merit system.
