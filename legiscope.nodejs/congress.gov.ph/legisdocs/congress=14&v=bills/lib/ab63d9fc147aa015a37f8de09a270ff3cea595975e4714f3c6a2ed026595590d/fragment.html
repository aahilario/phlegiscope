House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01426
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROTECT, PROMOTE AND SUPPORT BREASTFEEDING THROUGH ENCOURAGING THE ESTABLISHMENT AND MAINTENANCE OF BREASTFEEDING FACILITIES, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "working Mothers Breastfeeding Act"
ABSTRACT : Seeks to encourage and provide incentives to firms, establishments and institutions that provide lactation facilities where mothers can express milk or breastfeed in privacy.
