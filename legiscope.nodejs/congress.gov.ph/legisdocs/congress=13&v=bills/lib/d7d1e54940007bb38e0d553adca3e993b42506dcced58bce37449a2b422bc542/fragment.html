House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03689
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO REGULATE AND MODERNIZE THE PRACTICE OF CHEMISTRY IN THE PHILIPPINES, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE R.A. 754
SHORT TITLE : Chemistry Law of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Chemistry Law of 2005." The measure seeks to upgrade the definition of chemistry and the scope of the professional practice of chemistry and of chemical technicians. It introduces measures to make the professional practice of chemistry more globally competitive.
