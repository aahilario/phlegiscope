House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01979
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MAKING APPLICABLE TO LAWYERS IN THE PUBLIC ATTORNEY'S OFFICE OF THE DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE THE RETIREMENT BENEFITS UNDER REPUBLIC ACT NO. 910, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill upgrades the retirement, disability and survivorship benefits of public attorneys by putting their benefits at par with those being received by the members of the judiciary.
