House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00108
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE PHILIPPINE LEGISLATIVE ACADEMY, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Institutionalizes the guidelines needed to maintain a high level of technical and professional personnel in the Legislative Branch by establishing the Philippine Legislative Academy with the aim of providing a system of training and continuing education program for the legislative officers and employees.
