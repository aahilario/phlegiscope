HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00844
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO HELP INSURE THE SAFETY OF BANK DEPOSITS AND FUNDS THEREBY STRENGTHENING THE OPERATIONS OF BANKING INSTITUTIONS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill helps in ensuring the safety of bank deposits and funds thereby strengthening the operations of banking institutions. It authorizes all banking institutions recognized and accredited by the Banko Sentral ng Pilipinas to provide special security services of their banking activities and operations and to procure and possess firearms for security purposes.
