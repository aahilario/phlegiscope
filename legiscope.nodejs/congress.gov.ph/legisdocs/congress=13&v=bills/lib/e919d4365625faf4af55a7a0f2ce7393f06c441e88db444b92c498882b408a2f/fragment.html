FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXTENDING THE EFFECTIVITY OF THE RENT CONTROL LAW FOR CERTAIN RESIDENTIAL UNITS FOR ANOTHER FIVE YEARS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE "RENTAL REFORM ACT OF 2002"
