House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03127
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR SYNCHRONIZED ELECTIONS IN 2007, EXTENDING THE TERM OF OFFICE OF BARANGAY OFFICIALS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
SHORT TITLE : Synchronized Elections Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Synchronized Elections Act of 2004." The bill provides for the holding of a synchronized barangay, national and local elections on the 2nd Monday of May 2007. It further allows block voting for barangay positions.
