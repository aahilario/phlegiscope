HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00595
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPOSING A TEN PERCENT (10%) AD VALOREM TAX ON FLAVORED OR COLOURED SYRUPS EXCLUSIVELY USED IN THE MANUFACTURING OF SOFTDRINKS BY INSERTING A NEW SECTION, THEREBY AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CHAPTER VI, TITLE VI OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes the imposition of a 10% ad valorem tax on flavored or colored syrups exclusively used in the manufacture of softdrinks to be imposed on the manufacturer's selling tariff and customs duty, including customs duty and all other charges, net of value-added tax.
