HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00614
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING PRE-SCHOOLS AS AN INTEGRAL PART OF BASIC EDUCATION
SHORT TITLE : "National Preparatory Education Act"
ABSTRACT : "National Preparatory Education Act". The bill proposes a two-level compulsory preparatory public education, namely: a) Kindergarten One for children 3 to 4 years old, and b) Kindergarten Two for 5-year old children.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : VILLAFUERTE, LUIS R.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-02
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
