House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04352
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING CERTAIN BUILDINGS, INSTITUTIONS, ESTABLISHMENTS AND PUBLIC UTILITIES TO RESERVE SUFFICIENT AND SUITABLE PARKING AND SEATING SPACES FOR THE USE OF EXPECTANT MOTHERS
SHORT TITLE : Requiring Certain Buildings, Institutions, Establishments and Public Utilities to Reserve Parking and Seating Spaces for Expectant Mothers
ABSTRACT : The measure mandates public and private buildings for public use such as schools, airports, sports and recreation centers, shopping malls and other similar establishments to reserve suitable parking and seating spaces for the use of expectant mothers.
