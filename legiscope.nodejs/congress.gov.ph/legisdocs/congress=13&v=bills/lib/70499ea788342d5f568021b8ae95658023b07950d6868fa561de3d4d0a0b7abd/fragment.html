House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01691
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A SPECIAL REGISTRATION OF ALL LANDS BEING OCCUPIED AS SCHOOL SITES UNDER THE NAME OF THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Special Land Registration Act of 2001
ABSTRACT : "Special Land Registration Act of 2001." The bill directs the Land Registration Authority, in coordination with the Department of Education (DepEd), to conduct a special registration, in the name of the DepEd, of the lands being used as public schools sites acquired by the DepEd either by purchase, donation or exchange.
