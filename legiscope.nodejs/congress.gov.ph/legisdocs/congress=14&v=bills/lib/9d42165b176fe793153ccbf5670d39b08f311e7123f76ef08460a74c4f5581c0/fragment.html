House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02812
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 6, 16M 17 AND 18 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9189, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE OVERSEAS ABSENTEE VOTING ACT OF 2003
ABSTRACT : In order to safeguard the secrecy and sanctity of the ballot, the bill seeks to revoke the authority granted to consulates and other foreign service establishments under the Overseas Absentee voting Act of 2003.
