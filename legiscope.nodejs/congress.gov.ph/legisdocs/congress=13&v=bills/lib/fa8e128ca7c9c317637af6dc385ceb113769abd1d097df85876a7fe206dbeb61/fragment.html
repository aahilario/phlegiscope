House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01452
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AND SANITATION, INTEGRATING THE DIFFERENT GOVERNMENT AGENCIES RELATED TO ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AND SANITATION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Department of Environment Protection and Sanitation Act
ABSTRACT : "Department of Environment Protection and Sanitation Act." The bill creates the Department to coordinate different government programs on waste disposal and air, water or land pollution to prevent confusion, duplication and overlapping of functions. It provides for the powers and authority of the Commission.
