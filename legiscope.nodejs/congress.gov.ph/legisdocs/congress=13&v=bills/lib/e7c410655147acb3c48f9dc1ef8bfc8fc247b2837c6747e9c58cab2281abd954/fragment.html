HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02090
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE MONTHLY HONORARIA AND ALLOWANCES OF THE PUNONG BARANGAY, SANGGUNIANG BARANGAY MEMBERS, BARANGAY TREASURER, BARANGAY SECRETARY AND SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN CHAIRMAN AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE SECTION 393 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160 AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the monthly honoraria and allowances to P5,000 for the punong barangay and to P3,000 for the sangguniang barangay members, treasurer, secretary and the sangguniang kabataan chairman. It also grants a Christmas bonus of P3,000 each to the above officials.
