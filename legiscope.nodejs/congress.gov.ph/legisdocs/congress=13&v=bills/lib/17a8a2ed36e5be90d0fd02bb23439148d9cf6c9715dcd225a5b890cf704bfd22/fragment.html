HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00113
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING BONDS ISSUED BY THE LAND BANK OF THE PHILIPPINES FOR LAND ACQUISITIONS FROM PAYMENT OF THE TWENTY PERCENT (20%) FINAL TAX ON INTEREST INCOME, AMENDING FOR THE SAID PURPOSE SECTION 24 (B)(1) OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8424, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE TAX REFORM ACT OF 1997
ABSTRACT : In the implementation of the law, the contemplated protection of the just share for the landowners did not actually materialize. In most cases, only the productive portions of the lands were bought, leaving the landowners with unproductive and undesirable portions of the land. To redress a perceived social wrong, this bill proposes to exempt interest earnings on land bank notes paid to landowners from the payment of withholding tax.
