House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01972
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE TRANSFER OF SENTENCED PERSONS TO OR FROM FOREIGN COUNTRIES
SHORT TITLE : Transfer of Sentenced Persons Act of 2001
ABSTRACT : "Transfer of Sentenced Persons Act of 2001." The bill aims to allow convicted persons to serve his sentence in his home country where his rehabilitation may be properly monitored and within the reach of his family and relatives.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : CUENCO, ANTONIO V.
