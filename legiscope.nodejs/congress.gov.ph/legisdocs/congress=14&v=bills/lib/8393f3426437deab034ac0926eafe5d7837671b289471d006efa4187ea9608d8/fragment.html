House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02403
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE ESTABLISHMENT AND OPERATIONS OF HEALTH MAINTENANCE ORGANIZATIONS (HMOs), PROVIDING THEM INCENTIVES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Health Maintenance Organizations Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill encourages the establishment and operation of health maintenance organizations (HMOs) by granting them reasonable incentives. It estabishes the regulatory framework for HMOs. It recognizes and protects the rights of HMOs, health care providers and members.
