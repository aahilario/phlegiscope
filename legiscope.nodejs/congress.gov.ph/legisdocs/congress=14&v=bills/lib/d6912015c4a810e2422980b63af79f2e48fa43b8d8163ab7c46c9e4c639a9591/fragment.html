HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00833
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING CERTAIN BENEFITS AND INCENTIVES TO ALL BARANGAY TANODS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to grant barangay tanods the following benefits: a) a monthly honorarium of P600.00; b) 1,000 Christmas bonus; and c) free tuition and matriculation fees for their legitimate children attending state colleges and universities.
