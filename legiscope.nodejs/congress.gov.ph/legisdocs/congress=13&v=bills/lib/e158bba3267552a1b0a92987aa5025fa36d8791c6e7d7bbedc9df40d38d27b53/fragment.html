House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04689
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 470 (b) OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the temporary assignment of provincial, city and municipal treasurers who have pending cases, whether administrative or criminal, and whose preventive suspension have already expired, to the Bureau of Local Government Finance until their cases have been resolved with finality by the court or agency concerned.
