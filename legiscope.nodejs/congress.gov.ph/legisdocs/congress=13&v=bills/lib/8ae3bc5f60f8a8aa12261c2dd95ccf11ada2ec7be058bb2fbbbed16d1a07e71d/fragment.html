House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02797
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EMPOWERING WOMEN BY PROVIDING THEM WITH FINANCIAL EDUCATIONAL AND INFORMATION ASSISTANCE IN ORDER FOR THEM TO EXCEL IN COMMERCE AND TRADE
SHORT TITLE : Women Empowerment in Enterprises Act
ABSTRACT : "Women Empowerment in Enterprises Act". The bill seeks to empower women in enterpreneurship by giving them financial assistance in setting up business endeavors and educational assistance to prepare them for involvement in government or in business.
