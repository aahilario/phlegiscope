HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02338
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOWING LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS TO RETAIN FIFTY PERCENT (50%) OF THE PROCEEDS FROM ALL TAXES, FEES AND FINES COLLECTED UNDER PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBERED ELEVEN HUNDRED AND EIGHTY FIVE, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE FIRE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the local government units to retain 50% of the proceeds from the fire inspection fees for the upgrading of equipment and facilities of their respective fire departments.
