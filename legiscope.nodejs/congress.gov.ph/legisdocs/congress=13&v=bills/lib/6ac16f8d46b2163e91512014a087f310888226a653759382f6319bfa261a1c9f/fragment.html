House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02259
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AUTHORIZING HEADS AND CHIEFS OF HOSPITALS TO APPROVE PURCHASE OF MEDICINES AND OTHER MEDICAL SUPPLIES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 344 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991", AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : This bill mandates that in the procurement of medicines and other medical supplies necessary for the delivery of basic health services, the heads of departments and chiefs of hospitals, duly authorized by concerned local chief executives, may approve such disbursements.
