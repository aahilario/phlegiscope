House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00931
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING THAT THE APPROPRIATION FOR FUNDED PROJECTS BE FULLY UTILIZED FOR THEIR INTENDED PURPOSES TO DO AWAY WITH THE PRACTICE OF WITHHOLDING A PERCENTAGE OR PART THEREOF FOR GOVERNMENT BUDGETARY RESERVES
ABSTRACT : This bill prohibits the vicious budgetary practice of past administration to generate savings by withholding thr appropriation for funded projects.
