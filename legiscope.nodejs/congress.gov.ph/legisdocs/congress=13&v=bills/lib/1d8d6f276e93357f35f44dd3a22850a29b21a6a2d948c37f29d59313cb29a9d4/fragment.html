HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03856
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REVISING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9136 ENTITLED "AN ACT ORDAINING REFORMS IN THE ELECTRIC POWER INDUSTRY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE CERTAIN LAWS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES"
ABSTRACT : The measure proposes certain amendments to RA 9236 (Electric Power Industry Reform Act of EPIRA) in order to afford protection to end-users of electicity, to level the playing field and to recognize the rights of the industry players to a just and reasonable return on their investments.
