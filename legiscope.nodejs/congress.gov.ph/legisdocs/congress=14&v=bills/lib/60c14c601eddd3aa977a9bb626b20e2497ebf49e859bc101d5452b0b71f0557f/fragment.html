HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03005
FULL TITLE : AN ACT RATIONALIZING THE PROCEEDINGS IN AND RESOLUTION OF ELECTORAL PROTESTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Fair Electoral Protest Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the following, among others: a) reasonable filing fees to cover only the administrative costs for accepting and docketing the protest; b) for the proclaimed winner who is eventually ousted in the protest to shoulder all costs; c) the imposition of a time limit in the resolution of protests; and d) disqualification of an ousted winner from running for any elective position.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : RODRIGUEZ, RUFUS B.
DATE FILED : 2007-11-05
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
