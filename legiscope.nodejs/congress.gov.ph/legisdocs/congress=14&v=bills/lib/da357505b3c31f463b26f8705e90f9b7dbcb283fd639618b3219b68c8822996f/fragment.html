House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02740
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CREATION OF MUNICIPAL SPECIAL EDUCATION CENTERS FOR THE DEAF-MUTE AND BLIND CHILDREN APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : "Municipal Special Education Centers Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the Secretary of Education to award grants to public or private non-profit agencies, institutions or organizations for the establishment of municipal special education centers for deaf-mute and blind children.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SANTIAGO, NARCISO III D.
