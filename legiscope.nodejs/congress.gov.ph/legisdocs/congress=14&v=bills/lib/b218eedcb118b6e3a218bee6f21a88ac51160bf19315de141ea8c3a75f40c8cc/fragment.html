HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02805
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 387 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE AND SECTION 39 OF BATAS PAMBANSA BILANG 881, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE OMNIBUS ELECTION CODE AS AMENDED TO SIMPLIFY THE HOLDING OF BARANGAY ELECTIONS BY MAKING THE BARANGAY KAGAWAD WITH THE HIGHEST VOTES AS THE PUNONG BARANGAY, WHO WILL SERVE AS SUCH, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill provides for the barangay kagawad with the highest number of votes in the election for sangguniang barangay members to serve as the punong barangay.
