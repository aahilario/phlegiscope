HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00353
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING CIVIL SERVICE ELIGIBILITY UNDER CERTAIN CONDITIONS TO A GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEE WHOSE STATUS OF APPOINTMENT IS CASUAL OR CONTRACTUAL AND WHO HAS RENDERED A TOTAL OF TEN (10) YEARS OF EFFICIENT SERVICE
ABSTRACT : The bill grants civil service eligibility to contractual or casual employees in the government who have rendered at least ten (10) years of continuous service.
