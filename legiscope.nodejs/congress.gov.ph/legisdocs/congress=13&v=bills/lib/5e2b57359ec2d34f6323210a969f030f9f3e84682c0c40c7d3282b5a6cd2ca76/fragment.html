House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02197
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A DEPARTMENT OF MARITIME AFFAIRS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Department of Maritime Affairs Act of 2002
ABSTRACT : "Department of Maritime Affairs Act of 2002." The bill provides for the creation of a separate and distinct Department of Maritime Affairs that will absorb the existing bureaus, offices and agencies involved in the management and development of the country's shipping, shipbuilding and seafaring industries, and the exploration, exploitation and protection of our marine resources.
