HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03148
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING THE COMPULSORY COVERAGE OF BARANGAY HEALTH WORKERS TO THE NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE PROGRAM, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7883 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill grants accredited barangay health workers compulsory coverage in the National Health Insurance Program (Philhealth). Their monthly minimum contribution shall be shared by the national government and the local government unit concerned on a 50-50 basis.
