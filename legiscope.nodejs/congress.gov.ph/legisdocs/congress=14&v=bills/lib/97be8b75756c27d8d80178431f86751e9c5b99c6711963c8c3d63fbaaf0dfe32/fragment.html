HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02747
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TASK FORCE TO RECOMMEND A UNIFORM STRATEGY TO PROTECT WOMEN AGAINST VIOLENT CRIME
SHORT TITLE : "Violence Against Women Research Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The measure establishes the National Task Force on Violence Against Women that shall develop a uniform law enforcement strategy aimed at protecting women against violence, punish persons who commit such crimes, and enhance the rights of victims.
