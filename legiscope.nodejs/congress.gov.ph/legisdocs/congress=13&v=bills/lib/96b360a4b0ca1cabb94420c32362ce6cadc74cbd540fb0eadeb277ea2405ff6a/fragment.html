HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02536
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING THE PROBATIONARY PERIOD OF EMPLOYMENT OF ACADEMIC PERSONNEL IN PRIVATE SCHOOLS, THEREBY AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 442 (AS AMENDED), OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill sets the probationary employment period of academic personnel as follows: a) one (1) calendar year of very satisfactory service for those in the elementary and secondary levels; b) two consecutive regular semesters of very satisfactory service for those in the tertiary levels; and c) three consecutive trimester of very satisfactory service for those in the tertiary level with a trimester program.
