HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03812
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROMOTE AND HASTEN THE DEVELOPMENT OF MICROFINANCE NGOs IN THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : Microfinance NGOS Development Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Microfinance NGOs Development Act of 2005." The measure grants donee institution status to NGOs to enable donors to claim their donations as a deduction from gross income for a period of 10 years from the effectivity of the proposed act.
