House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00130
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE RULES FOR A COMPETITIVELY NEUTRAL GOVERNMENT AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Despite its policy of privatization and deregulation, the State continues to compete with private enterprises for available economic resources. This bill seeks to establish a competitively neutral government. Competitive neutrality arrangements are intended to minimize resource allocation distortions arising out of public ownership of significant business activities.
