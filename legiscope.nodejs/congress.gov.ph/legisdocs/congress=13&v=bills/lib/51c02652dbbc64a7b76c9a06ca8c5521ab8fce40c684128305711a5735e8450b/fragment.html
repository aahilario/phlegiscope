House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03117
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE BOARD OF AGROFORESTRY UNDER THE PROFESSIONAL REGULATIONS COMMISSION, PROVIDING FOR MECHANISMS OF IMPLEMENTATION, APPROPRIATING FUNDS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Board of Agroforestry Act of 2003
ABSTRACT : "Board of Agroforestry Act of 2003." The bill defines the scope of the practice of agroforestry and prescribes the powers, functions and composition of the Board of Agroforestry. The Board shall, among others, conduct a national board examination to all graduates of a four-year degree program on agroforestry
