HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01557
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING THAT ELECTIVE OFFICIALS RUNNING FOR ANY OFFICE OTHER THAN THE ONE WHICH THEY ARE HOLDING SHALL BE CONSIDERED IPSO FACTO RESIGNED UPON THE FILING OF THEIR CERTIFICATE OF CANDIDACY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE OMNIBUS ELECTION CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to do declare all elective officials running for any office other than one he is holding in a permanent capacity as ipso facto resigned from his office upon filing of the certificate of candidacy.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : UMALI, ALFONSO JR. V.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-31
