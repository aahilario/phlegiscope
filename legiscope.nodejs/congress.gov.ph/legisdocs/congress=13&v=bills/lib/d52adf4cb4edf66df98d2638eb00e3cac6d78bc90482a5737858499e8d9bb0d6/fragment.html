HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03781
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GOVERNING THE REMITTANCES TO THE PHILIPPINES OF FOREIGN EXCHANGE EARNINGS OF FILIPINO WORKERS ABROAD TO ENSURE THAT THE FOREIGN EXCHANGE EARNINGS OF THESE WORKERS ARE REMITTED THROUGH AUTHORIZED FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS OF THE PHILIPPINE GOVERNMENT OR THEIR AUTHORIZED AGENTS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill mandates the remittance of the foreign exchange earnings of overseas Filipino workers to their families, dependents and/or beneficiaries through official financial institutions of the government.
