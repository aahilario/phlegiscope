House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00247
FULL TITLE : COLLECTIVE INVESTMENT SCHEMES LAW
SHORT TITLE : Collective Investment Schemes Law
ABSTRACT : "Collective Investment Schemes Law" Seeks to balance the interests of the investors, by incorporating mechanisms that would safeguard the pubic interest, at the same time recognizing the interests of the other parties involved in the CIS industry, by giving them flexibility to expand their CIS businesses.
