House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00102
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE LEGITIMATION OF CHILDREN BORN TO PARENTS BELOW MARRYING AGE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE FAMILY CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : Today, the incidents of children born out of wedlock to parents who are disqualified under the law to marry each other because of the issue of minority are still very common. To uphold the rights and dignity of these children, this bill provides for the legitimation of children born to parents below marrying age.
