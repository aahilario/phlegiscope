HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00317
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MAGNA CARTA OF STUDENTS
ABSTRACT : The bill aims to promote the students' rights and welfare and to enhance their role and potentials towards social change and development. The proposed Magna Carta of Students guarantees the students' right to be represented in policy-making bodies of the school, to govern their own activities, to discipline their own rank and to be accorded due process in disciplinary proceedings.
