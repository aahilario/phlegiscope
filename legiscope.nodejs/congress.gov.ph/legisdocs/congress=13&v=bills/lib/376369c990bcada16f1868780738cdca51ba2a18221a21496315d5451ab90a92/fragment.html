House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01543
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING MARINE POLLUTION IN THE PHILIPPINES
SHORT TITLE : Marine Pollution Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Marine Pollution Act of 2004." The bill seeks to prevent or limit the dumping into ocean waters of any material which would adversely affect human health, welfare or amenities or the marine environment or the ecological system.
