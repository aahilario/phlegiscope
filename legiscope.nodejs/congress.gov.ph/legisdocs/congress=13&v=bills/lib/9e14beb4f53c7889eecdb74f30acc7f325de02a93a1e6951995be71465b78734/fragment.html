House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01847
FULL TITLE : "AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MAGNA CARTA FOR BARANGAY TANODS"
SHORT TITLE : Magna Carta of Barangay Tanods
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta of Barangay Tanods." This bill grants barangay tanods similar privileges and benefits received by other appointive barangay officials to improve their economic welfare and to enhance their skills and capabilities to make them better equipped in the maintenance of peace and order in the barangay.
