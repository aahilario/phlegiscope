House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00941
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE CONFERMENT OF BACHELOR OF LAWS DEGREE AS EQUIVALENT TO A MASTERS DEGREE
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to declare the conferment of a Bachelor of Laws degree as equivalent to a masters degree for purposes of appointment to positions requiring such educational attainment.
