HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01603
FULL TITLE : AN ACT LIFTING THE RESTRICTIONS ON ELECTRICITY GENERATING COMPANIES TO BUILD ELECTRIC TRANSMISSION FACILITIES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE ELECTRIC POWER INDUSTRY REFORM ACT OF 2001
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to lift the restrictions which disallows electricity generating companies from owning and operating electric transmission facilities in order to create a more conducive business environment for prospective investors in the electric power industry.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : MERCADO, ROGER G.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-31
