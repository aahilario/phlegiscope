House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02419
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ADDRESSING THE SYSTEM OF PROSTITUTION, IMPOSING PENALTIES ON ITS PERPETRATORS, PROVIDING PROTECTIVE MEASURES AND SUPPORT SERVICES FOR ITS VICTIMS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Prostitution Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Prostitution Act of 2004." The bill seeks to shift the accountability for prostitution from the persons exploited in prostitution to those who really gain from the system of prostitution, i.e., the business establishments (sauna, bath and massage parlors; motels, hotels and lodging houses; bars, etc.) and the buyers who are predominantly men.
