House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02831
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT 8424 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE, AS AMENDED AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to subject to excise taxation not only non-essential goods but also non-essential services. It further expands the list of non-essential goods to include those with qualities such as antique, handmade by artisan, rare, etc., that make them luxurious items.
