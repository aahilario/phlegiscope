House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00708
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING AND PENALIZING THE PRODUCTION, PRINTING, PUBLICATION, IMPORTATION, SALE, DISTRIBUTION AND EXHIBITION OF OBSCENE AND PORNOGRAPHIC MOVIES AND MATERIALS, AND THE EXHIBITION OF LIVE SEXUAL ACTS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLE 201 OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Obscenity and Pornography Act of 2004"
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Obscenity and Pornography Act of 2004". The bill penalizes the following acts, among others: a) the production, importation, distribution, showing and sale of obscene or pornographic movies; b) the printing, publication, importation, sale or distribution of any magazine or comic book containing obscene or pornographic materials; c) the writing of obscene or pornographic articles; and d) the performance of live sex acts for public viewing.
