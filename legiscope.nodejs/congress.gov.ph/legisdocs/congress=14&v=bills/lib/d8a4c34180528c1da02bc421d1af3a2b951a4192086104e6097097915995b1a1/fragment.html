House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00841
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE DEPARTMENT OF MARITIME AFFAIRS PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Department of Maritime Affairs Act"
ABSTRACT : "Department of Maritime Affairs Act". The bill creates the Department of Maritime Affairs which shall be tasked to recommend and implement the government's policies, plans and programs for the promotion of safety and development of ports, shipping, shipbuilding, seafaring and other activities in the maritime industry.
