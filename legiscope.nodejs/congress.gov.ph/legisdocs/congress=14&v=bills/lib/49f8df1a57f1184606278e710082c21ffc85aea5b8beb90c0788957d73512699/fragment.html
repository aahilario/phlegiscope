House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00972
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING THAT ANY NATIONAL AND LOCAL ELECTIVE OFFICIALS EXCEPT PRESIDENT AND VICE-PRESIDENT, RUNNING FOR ANY OFFICE OTHER THAN THE ONE TO WHICH HE IS HOLDING IN A PERMANENT CAPACITY, SHALL BE IPSO FACTO RESIGNED FROM HIS OFFICE UPON THE FILING OF HIS CERTIFICATE OF CANDIDACY
SHORT TITLE : "The Election Mandate Act"
ABSTRACT : "The Election Mandate Act". The proposed bill seeks to declare any national or local elective official, except the position of President and Vice President, running for any public office than the one which he is holding in a permanent capacity as ipso facto resigned from his office upon the filing of the certificate of candidacy.
