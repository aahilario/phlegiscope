House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01468
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE RATE OF THE VALUE-ADDED TAX, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS 106, 107, 108 AND 114 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : To help the government meet its revenue target, the bill proposes to increase the value added tax to be paid by the seller from 10% to 12% of the gross selling price of goods or services.
