House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01276
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXTENDING THE TERM OF BARANGAY OFFICIALS AND SETTING OF THE DATE OF THE ELECTION OF BARANGAY OFFICIALS, AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9164
ABSTRACT : This bill extends the term of all barangay officials from three (3) years to five (5) years so that election for these offices shall be held every five (5) years.
