HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00735
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROPOSING THE CREATION OF THE PHILIPPINE BAMBOO AND RATTAN CENTER (PBRC) TO CONSERVE, PROPAGATE AND PROMOTE BAMBOO AND RATTAN SPECIES
SHORT TITLE : "Bamboo and Rattan Bill of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Bamboo and Rattan Bill of 2007". The bill creates the Philippine National Bamboo and Rattan Center that shall be tasked, among others, to promote bamboo and rattan as sources of food, furniture, light construction materials and other industrial products.
