HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00342
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING A PERSON WHO IS A PERMANENT RESIDENT OR IMMIGRANT OF A FOREIGN COUNTRY FROM BEING APPOINTED TO ANY PUBLIC OFFICE OR POSITION AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBERED EIGHT HUNDRED SEVEN (PD 807) OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CIVIL SERVICE DECREE OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The Constitution explicitly provides that any public officer who acquires the status of an immigrant of another country during his tenure shall be dealt with by the law. Resident aliens of foreign countries are deemed incapable of placing 100% devotion to the interest and welfare of their homeland since they must also see to their duties as permanent residents of that country. The bill seeks to amend the Civil Service Decree of the Philippines with a provision that no person who is permanent resident or immigrant of a foreign country shall be eligible for appointment to any public office.
