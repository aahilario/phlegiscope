House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02688
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR PROTECTION OF FAMILY PRIVACY
SHORT TITLE : "Family Privacy Protection Act"
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits any person, while conducting a program or activity funded by the government, from seeking the response of a minor to a survey question eliciting incriminatory information concerning the minor's family.
