FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN SECTIONS OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to remove the taints of unconstitutionality that infect some provisions of RA 9337. It also aims to ease the impact of the value-added tax on the prices of goods and services.
