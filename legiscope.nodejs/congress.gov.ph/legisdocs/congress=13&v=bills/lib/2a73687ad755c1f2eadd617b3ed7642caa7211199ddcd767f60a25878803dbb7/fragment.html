HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02358
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REVERTING THE TERM OF OFFICE OF BARANGAY AND SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN OFFICIALS TO FIVE (5) YEARS, AND RESETTING THE DATE OF ELECTION OF BARANGAY OFFICIALS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to revert the terms of office of barangay and sangguniang kabataan official to five years, as previously envisioned under RA 8524.
