House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01384
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CONTROL AND ERADICATION OF RABIES, PRESCRIBING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Rabies Act of 2002
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Rabies Act of 2002." The bill mandates the establishment of a National Rabies Prevention and Control Program which shall be a multi-agency effort to control and eradicate rabies in the country. Its activities shall include, among others, mass vaccination of dogs, impounding of stray dogs, and information and education campaign on the prevention and control of rabies.
