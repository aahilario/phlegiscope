House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01069
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9164, TO EXTEND THE TERMS OF OFFICE OF BARANGAY OFFICIALS AND RESET THE DATE OF BARANGAY ELECTIONS
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to extend the term of office of barnagay and sangguniang kabataan officials from three years to five years. Elections for these offices shall be held every five years.
