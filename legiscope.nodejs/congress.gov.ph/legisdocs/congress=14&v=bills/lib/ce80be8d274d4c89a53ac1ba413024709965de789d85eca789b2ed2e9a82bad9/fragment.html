House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03188
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING STIFFER PENALTIES FOR THE CRIME OF THEFT AND ROBBERY OF PORTABLE TELECOMMUNICATION DEVICES
SHORT TITLE : "Anti-Theft and Robbery of Portable Telecommunication Devices Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to penalize, with the penalty next higher in degree, any person convicted of the crime of theft and robbery of cell phones, personal digital assistants, portable computers, two-way very/ultra high frequency (VHF or UHF) radios and other portable telecommunication equipments.
