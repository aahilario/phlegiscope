House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02290
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ADDRESSING THE SYSTEM OF PROSTITUTION, IMPOSING PENALTIES ON ITS PERPETRATORS, PROVIDING PROTECTIVE MEASURES AND SUPPORT SERVICES FOR ITS VICTIMS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE REVISED PENAL CODE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "The Anti-Prostitution Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "The Anti-Prostitution Act of 2007". The bill seeks to define the punishable acts of prostitution and provides for its penalties and sanctions. It provides for the rights of persons exploited in prostitution and the responsibilities of government agencies in anti-prostitution efforts.
