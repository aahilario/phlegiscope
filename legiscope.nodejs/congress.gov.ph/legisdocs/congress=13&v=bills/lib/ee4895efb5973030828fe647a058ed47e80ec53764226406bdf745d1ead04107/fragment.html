House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01419
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE YEARLY ALLOCATION FOR SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNOLOGICAL ACTIVITIES (STA)
SHORT TITLE : Science and Technology Funding Act of 2001
ABSTRACT : "Science and Technology Funding Act of 2001." The bill provides for the allocation for scientific and technological activities to be gradually increased until it reaches the prescribe UNESCO norm of one percent of total GNP.
