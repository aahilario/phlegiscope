FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AMEND SECTION FOUR (4) PARAGRAPH (a) OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETY TWO HUNDRED FIFTY SEVEN (9257)
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to treat the cost of discounts granted to senior citizens as tax credits based on the net cost of the goods sold or services rendered instead of as a mere deduction from gross income.
