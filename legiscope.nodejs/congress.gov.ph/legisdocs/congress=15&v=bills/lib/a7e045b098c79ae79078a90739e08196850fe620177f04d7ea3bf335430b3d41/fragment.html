House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00197
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MODERNIZATION OF THE BUREAU OF CORRECTIONS (BuCor) PROVIDING THE FUNDS THEREOF AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Bureau Of Corrections (bucor) Modernization Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “Bureau of Corrections (BuCor) Modernization Act of 2010” Aims to ease the inhuman conditions of confinement and degrading treatment of prisoners brought about by crowded, decrepit, or dilapidated buildings and penitentiary facilities in the country.
