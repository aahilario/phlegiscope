HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB06038
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE AUTOMATIC RETENTION BY THE BARANGAY OF ITS SHARE OF FIFTY PERCENT (50%) IN THE COMMUNITY TAX COLLECTED BY SAID BARANGAY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 164 OF R.A. NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill authorizes the barangay treasurer to automatically retain the 50% share of the barangay in the community tax collection and remit the share of the city or municipality within 10days after the end of every month.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : EDGAR M. CHATTO
