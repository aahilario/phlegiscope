HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01763
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE TAX ON IDLE LANDS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 236 OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SEVENTY ONE HUNDRED AND SIXTY OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE OF 1991
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to increase the tax on idle lands from 5% to 10% of the assessed value of the property in addition to the basic real property tax.
