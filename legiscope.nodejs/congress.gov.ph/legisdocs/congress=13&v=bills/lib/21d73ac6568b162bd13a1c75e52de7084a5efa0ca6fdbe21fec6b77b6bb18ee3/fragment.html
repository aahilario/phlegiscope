House Bill/Resolution NO. HB06093
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO AMEND REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9189 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE OVERSEAS ABSENTEE VOTING ACT OF 2003
SHORT TITLE : Amending RA No. 9189, Otherwise Known as the Overseas Absentee Voting Act of 2003
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the following amendments, among others: a) for counting to be fully automated; b) for the scope of voting by mail to be widened; c) for the adoption of a continuing system of registration for Filipinos overseas; and d) for the adoption of a registration and voting mechanism adaptable to seafarers' situation.
