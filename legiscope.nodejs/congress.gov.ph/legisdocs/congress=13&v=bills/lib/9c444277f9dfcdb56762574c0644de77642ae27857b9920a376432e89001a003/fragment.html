HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02867
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING THAT THE ACADEMIC CURRICULA FOR ALL SCHOOLS, COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES SHALL BE ESTABLISHED BY LAW
ABSTRACT : Under the present system, the academic curricula of schools, colleges and universities are established by the Department of Education. Thus, the curricula is more often than not influenced by the thinking of the Secretary of Education. For a more meaningful direction, the bill provides for the academic curricula to be established by law.
