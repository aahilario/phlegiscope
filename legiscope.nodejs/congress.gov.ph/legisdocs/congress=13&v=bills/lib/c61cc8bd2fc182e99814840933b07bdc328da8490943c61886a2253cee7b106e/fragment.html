House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00128
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ENHANCING ECONOMIC COORDINATION, ADOPTING THE APPROPRIATE MECHANISM THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Economic Coordination Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Economic Coordination Act of 2004". This bill is anchored on the dictum that economic goal settling should properly involve Congress, the Governor and Monetary Board of the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP) as well as the National Economic Development Authority headed by the President. It also gives flesh to the mandate of accountability and transparency of the BSP. It specifically sets a feedback loop mechanism on the state of the economy.
