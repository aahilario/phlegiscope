House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03830
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING DRUG PRICES, CREATING FOR THE PURPOSE THE DRUG PRICES REGULATION BOARD, DEFINING ITS POWERS AND FUNCTIONS, AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
SHORT TITLE : Drug Prices Regulation Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Drug Prices Regulation Act of 2005." The measure imposes price controls on medicines to bring down prices to reasonable and affordable levels. It establishes the Drug Prices Regulation Board and provides for its composition and powers.
