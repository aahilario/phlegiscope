HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00308
FULL TITLE : AN ACT EXEMPTING SAVINGS DEPOSITS AMOUNTING TO FIFTY THOUSAND PESOS OR LESS FROM WITHHOLDING TAX, AMENDING SECTION 21 (C)(1) OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE THEREBY, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Small Deposits Exemption Law
ABSTRACT : "Small Deposits Exemption Law". The bill seeks to help low income depositors neutralize the effects of inflation by exempting them from the imposition of withholding taxes so that they are not unduly deprived of the use of the interests earned by their deposited money. If no deduction is done, more people can put their extra money in the banking institutions. Consequently, more money will be available for the banks to invest in worthwhile projects to boost our economy.
