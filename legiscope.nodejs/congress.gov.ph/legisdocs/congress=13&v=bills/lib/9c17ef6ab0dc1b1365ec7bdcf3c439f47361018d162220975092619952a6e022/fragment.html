HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01885
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL ELEMENTARY AND HIGH SCHOOLS NATIONWIDE TO INTEGRATE IN THEIR SUBJECTS AND LESSONS LECTURES ON SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT PRACTICES AND PROCESSES
ABSTRACT : This bill requires all elementary and high schools nationwide to integrate lectures on solid waste management in existing subjects such as Science, Sibika at Kultura or Araling Panlipunan, and the like.
