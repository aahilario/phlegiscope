House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01547
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REGULATING THE EDUCATION AND LICENSURE OF PHYSICIANS AND THE PRACTICE OF MEDICINE IN THE PHILIPPINES, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 2382, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Physicians Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Physicians Act of 2007". The bill provides for the following: a) the standardization, upgrading and regulation of medical courses including internship training; b) the examination for registration and licensure of physicians; c) the supervision, control and regulation of the practice of medicine; d) the integration of the medical profession ; and e) the development of the professional competence of physicians through a continuing professional education.
