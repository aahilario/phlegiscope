HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04252
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE PENALTIES FOR NON-COMPLIANCE OF THE PRESCRIBED INCREASES AND ADJUSTMENTS IN THE WAGE RATES OF WORKERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6727, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "WAGE RATIONALIZATION ACT', AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill increases the penalty on any person, corporation, partnership and other business entity for non-compliance with the prescribed wage increases from P25,000 to P50,000 plus moral damages not exceeding P30,000 for each affected worker and the cost of litigation.
