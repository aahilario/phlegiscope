House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00120
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GOVERNING THE ESTABLISHMENT, OPERATION AND REGULATION OF LENDING COMPANIES
SHORT TITLE : The Lending Company Regulation Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "The Lending Company Regulation Act of 2004". In view of the significant impact of lending companies (LCs) in the financial sector, there is an urgent need for a law that would regulate the establishment and activities of LCs to prevent and mitigate practices of LCs that are prejudicial to public interest.
