HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB05404
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPOSING PENALTIES ON PHILIPPINE CONSULAR OFFICIALS AND OTHER GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL ASSIGNED TO OVERSEAS POSTS FOR FAILURE TO ACT ON COMPLAINTS OF, GIVE ASSISTANCE TO OR RENDER SERVICES TO MIGRANT WORKERS, THEIR FAMILIES AND OVERSEAS FILIPINOS IN DISTRESS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE THE "MIGRANT WORKERS AND OVERSEAS FILIPINOS ACT OF 1995"
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to penalize any government official in the consular, labor and social welfare services who fail or refuse to give assistance to Filipino migrants.
