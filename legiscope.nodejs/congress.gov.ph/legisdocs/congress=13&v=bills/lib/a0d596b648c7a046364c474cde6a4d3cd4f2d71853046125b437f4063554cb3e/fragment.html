House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01550
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TOTALLY BANNING LOGGING OPERATIONS FOR THE NEXT TWENTY-FIVE YEARS
ABSTRACT : To save our remaining forests, the bill seeks to impose a total log ban on forests, timber lands, forest reserves or watersheds within the next 25 years.
