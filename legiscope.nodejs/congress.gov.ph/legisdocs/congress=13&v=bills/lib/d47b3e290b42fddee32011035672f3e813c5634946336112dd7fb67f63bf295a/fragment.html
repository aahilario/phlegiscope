HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02926
FULL TITLE : AN ACT REQUIRING ALL NURSES TO SERVE IN A PRIVATE OR GOVERNMENT HOSPITAL FOR AT LEAST THREE (3) YEARS AS A PREREQUISITE FOR EMPLOYMENT IN FOREIGN COUNTRIES AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION THEREOF
ABSTRACT : The bill requires all nurses applying for employment abroad to submit a certification that he/she rendered three years of nursing service in a private or government hospital.
