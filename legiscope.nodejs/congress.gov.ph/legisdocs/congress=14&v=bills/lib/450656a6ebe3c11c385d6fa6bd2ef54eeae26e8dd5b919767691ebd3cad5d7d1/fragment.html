House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00403
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A MAGNA CARTA FOR HOMEOWNERS' ASSOCIATIONS
SHORT TITLE : "Magna Carta for Homeowners' Associations"
ABSTRACT : "Magna Carta for Homeowners' Associations". The bill seeks to solve various problems pertaining to the relationship of a homeowners association with its members, with the local government unit that has jurisdiction over it, with the regulatory body that exercise quasi-judicial powers over it and the immediate community it interacts with.
