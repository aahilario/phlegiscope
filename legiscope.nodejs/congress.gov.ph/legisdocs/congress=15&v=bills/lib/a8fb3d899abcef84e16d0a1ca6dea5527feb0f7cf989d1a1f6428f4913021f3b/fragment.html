HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00234
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR BARANGAY EMPOWERMENT, AMENDING VARIOUS PROVISIONS OF THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Barangay Empowerment Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : "Barangay Empowerment Act of 2010" Provides for the creation of a barangay building official in all barangays with a population of at least 3,000 inhabitants and it also proposes to give the barangay a bigger share in the proceeds of tax collected on extracted sand, gravel and other quarry resources.
