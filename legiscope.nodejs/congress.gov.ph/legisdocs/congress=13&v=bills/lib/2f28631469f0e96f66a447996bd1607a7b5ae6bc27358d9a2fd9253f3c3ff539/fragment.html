House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04407
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROHIBITING THE ESTABLISHMENT OF POLITICAL DYNASTIES
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Political Dynasty Act of 2005
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Political Dynasty Act of 2005". The bill aims to disqualify a person who has a political dynasty relationship with an incumbent elective official from running for any elective public office within the same city and/or province where the incumbent elective official is also running
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : DEFENSOR, ARTHUR D.
