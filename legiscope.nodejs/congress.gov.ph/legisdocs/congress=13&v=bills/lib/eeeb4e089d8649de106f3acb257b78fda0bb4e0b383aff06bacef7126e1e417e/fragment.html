HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01469
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE REIMPOSITION OF THE FRANCHISE TAX ON TELECOMMUNICATION COMPANIES, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTIONS 108 AND 119 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill re-imposes a five percent franchise tax on gross receipts on telephone and telegraph services in lieu of the value added tax.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SINGSON, ERIC D.
