House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01556
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING AND PENALIZING THE CRIME OF ENFORCED OR INVOLUNTARY DISAPPEARANCE
SHORT TITLE : An Act Defining and Penalizing the Crime of Unforced or Involuntary Disappearance
ABSTRACT : "An Act Defining and Penalizing the Crime of Enforced or Involuntary Disappearance." The bill grants the victim of an enforced or involuntary disappearance the right to inform his family, lawyer or human rights organization of his whereabouts and condition. It also provides for penalties for the commission of enforced disappearance and for compensation and rehabilitation for the victims and their families.
