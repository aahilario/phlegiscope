House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03028
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY TWENTY-TWO OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY TO BE KNOWN AS NATIONAL FARMER'S DAY
SHORT TITLE : Declaring January Twenty-Two of Every Year As National Farmer's Day
ABSTRACT : To honor the heroism of the peasants who died in the "Mendiola massacre" while fighting for genuine land reform, the bill declares January 22nd of the every year as a special working holiday.
