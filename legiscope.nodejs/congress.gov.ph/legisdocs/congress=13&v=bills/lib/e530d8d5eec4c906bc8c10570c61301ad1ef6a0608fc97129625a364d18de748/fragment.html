House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00801
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING CERTAIN BENEFITS AND INCENTIVES TO SCIENCE AND MATHEMATICS TEACHERS
SHORT TITLE : Science and Mathematics Teachers Development
ABSTRACT : "Science and Mathematics Teachers Development." The bill provides measures that will attract bright students in mathematics and science to pursue a career in science teaching, enable them to advance in their specialization, sustain their interest in the field, and encourage them to stay on teaching science.
