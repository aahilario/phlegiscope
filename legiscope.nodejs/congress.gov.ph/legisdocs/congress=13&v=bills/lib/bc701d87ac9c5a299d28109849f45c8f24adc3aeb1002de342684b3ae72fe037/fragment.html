HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01677
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTIONS 447 AND 458 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7160, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT CODE
ABSTRACT : The bill defines "filcab" as a four-wheel vehicle with a seating capacity of 7-14 persons. The bill proposes that filcabs be regulated by the local government units (LGUs) instead of the Land Transportation Franchising Regulatory Board and the Land Transportation Office since the LGUs are in a better position to determine the transportation needs in their locality.
