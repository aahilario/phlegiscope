House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01097
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE LICENSURE OF ALL HEALTH FACILITIES AND SERVICES IN THE PHILIPPINES, REPEALING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FORTY-TWO HUNDRED TWENTY-SIX (R.A. NO. 4226), OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'HOSPITAL LICENSURE ACT,' AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Health FAcilities and Services Licensure Act
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to strengthen the Bureau of Health Facilities and Services (BHFS) by vesting it with quasi-judicial powers, allowing it to use its revenues to improve its capabilities and by streamlining the procedures and requirements in the issuance of licenses.
