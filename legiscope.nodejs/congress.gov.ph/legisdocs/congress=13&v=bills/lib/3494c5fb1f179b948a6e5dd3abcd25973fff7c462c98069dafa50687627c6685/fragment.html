House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02625
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING A NATIONAL POLICY ON TOURISM AS THE PRIMARY ENGINE OF INVESTMENT, EMPLOYMENT, GROWTH AND NATIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND STRENGTHENING THE DEPARTMENT OF TOURISM
SHORT TITLE : Tourism Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Tourism Act of 2004." The bill provides for the adoption of a national tourism policy to a) guide the course and pace of tourism development; b) coordinate the functions and resources of government agencies in tourism programs; and c) improve the quality of life of the people by promoting ecotourism.
