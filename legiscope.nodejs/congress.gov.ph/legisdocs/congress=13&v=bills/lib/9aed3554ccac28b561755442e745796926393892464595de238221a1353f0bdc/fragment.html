HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01323
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE EXCISE TAX RATES ON PETROLEUM PRODUCTS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION 148 OF THE NATIONAL INTERNAL REVENUE CODE OF 1997, AS AMENDED
ABSTRACT : Proposes to increase the excise tax being imposed on petroleum products by PhP2.00. The tax rate on liquefied petroleum gas (LPG), which is a basic household need, has been excluded from the proposed increase and remains at zero.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SUAREZ, DANILO E.
