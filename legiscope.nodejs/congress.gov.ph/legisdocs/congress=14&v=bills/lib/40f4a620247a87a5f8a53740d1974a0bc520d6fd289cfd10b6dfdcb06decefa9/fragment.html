HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03088
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO PROVIDE STRONGER PROTECTION TO PARENTS REGARDING THEIR CHILDREN'S ACCESS TO SEXUALLY EXPLICIT MATERIAL OVER THE INTERNET
SHORT TITLE : "Cyber Safety for Kids Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : The bill prohibits commercial websites from placing materials that are harmful to minors. It directs the National Telecommunications Commission to develop a content description tag that will warn users that the website contains materials harmful to minors and allow them to block or filter access to such website.
