HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03120
FULL TITLE : AN ACT ALLOWING CONTRACTORS WITH RECEIVABLES FROM THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT TO USE THE CERTIFICATE OF INDEBTEDNESS AS COLLATERAL FOR LOANS AND OTHER CREDIT ACCOMMODATIONS IN ANY GOVERNMENT FINANCIAL INSTITUTION
ABSTRACT : The bill directs all government financial institutions to accept as collateral for loans and other credit accommodations any duly executed certificate of indebtedness issued by any government agency to an eligible contractor.
