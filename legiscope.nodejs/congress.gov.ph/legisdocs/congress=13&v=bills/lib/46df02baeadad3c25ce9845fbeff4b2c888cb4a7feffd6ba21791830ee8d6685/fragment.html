HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04380
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE NUMBER OF SANDIGANBAYAN DIVISIONS AND STREAMLINING THE JURISDICTION OF THE SANDIGANBAYAN, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1606 AS AMENDED BY RA NOS. 7975 AND 8249 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The measure seeks to increase the number of associate justices in the Sandiganbayan from 14 to 44 and, likewise, to increase the number of Sandiganbayan divisions from 5 to 15 with three justices sitting in each division.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : PUENTEVELLA, MONICO O.
