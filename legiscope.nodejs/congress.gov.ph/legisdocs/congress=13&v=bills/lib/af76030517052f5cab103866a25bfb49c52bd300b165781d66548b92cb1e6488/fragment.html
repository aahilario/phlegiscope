HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03219
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO INCLUDE LIQUIFIED PETROLEUM GAS (LPG) AND KEROSENE IN THE LIST OF BASIC NECESSITIES IN SECTION THREE (3), DEFINITION OF TERMS OF REPUBLIC ACT 7581, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "PRICE ACT"
ABSTRACT : The bill includes LPG and kerosene in the list of basic commodities so that their prices can be automatically controlled during calamities and other emergency situations.
