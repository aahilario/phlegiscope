HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03073
FULL TITLE : AN ACT FIXING THE COMPULSORY RETIREMENT AGE OF OFFICERS OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES
ABSTRACT : The bill sets the compulsory retirement of an officer of the Armed Forces of the PHilippines at 35 years from the date of commission or upon reaching the age of 58 years old, whichever comes first.
