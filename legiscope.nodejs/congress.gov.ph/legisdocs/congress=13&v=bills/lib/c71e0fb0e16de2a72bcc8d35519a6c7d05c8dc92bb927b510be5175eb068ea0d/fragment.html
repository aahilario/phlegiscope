House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00346
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE REGULATORY FUNCTIONS OF THE PHILIPPINE OVERSEAS EMPLOYMENT ADMINISTRATION (POEA) AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8042, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'MIGRANT WORKERS AND OVERSEAS FILIPINOS ACT OF 1995'
ABSTRACT : Deregulation of the labor export industry, where "migration of workers becomes strictly a matter between the worker and his foreign employer", places migrant workers at greater risk of their rights being violated. This bill seeks to repeal the entire deregulation and phase-out provisions of Sections 29 and 30 of RA 8042 (Migrant Workers and Overseas Filipinos Act of 1995). It will prove the firm commitment of the government to uphold the interest and welfare of overseas Filipino workers.
