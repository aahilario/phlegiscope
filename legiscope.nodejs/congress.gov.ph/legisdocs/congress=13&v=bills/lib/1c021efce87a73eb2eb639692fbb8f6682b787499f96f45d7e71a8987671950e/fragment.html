House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00105
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING PRE-SCHOOL EDUCATION, INCREASING THE MONTHLY COMPENSATION OF DAY CARE WORKERS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Pre-School Development Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Pre-School Development Act of 2004". It is the declared policy of the State to provide for a universal early childhood or pre-school education that promotes effective social, intellectual and skills stimulation and value-formation for children from three to five years old with the end in view of preparing them for formal elementary schooling. To implement this policy, this measure aims to institutionalize pre-school education as part of compulsory basic education. The barangay centers shall constitute the public sector pre-school institutions.
