House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03176
FULL TITLE : AN ACT STRENGTHENING THE FUNCTIONAL AND STRUCTURAL ORGANIZATION OF THE COMMISSION ON HUMAN RIGHTS, APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Commission on Human Rights Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Commission on Human Rights Act of 2004." The bill provides for an expanded structural and functional organization to enable the Commission to meet the demands of human rights cases here and abroad. It grants the Commission prosecutorial powers over delineated forms of human rights violations for an effective and speedy resolution of human rights cases.
