House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04667
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1185, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'FIRE CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES', PROVIDING FOR EFFECTIVE IMPLEMENTATION THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Amending PD 1185, Otherwise Known as the 'Fire Code of the Philippines'
ABSTRACT : The measure places the responsibility of implementing the Fire Code on the Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP). It prescribes the functions of the BFP and the protection to be followed for violation of the Fire Code.
