FULL TITLE : AN ACT REPEALING THE AUTOMATIC APPROPRIATION FOR DEBT SERVICE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 292 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE REVISED ADMINISTRATIVE CODE OF 1987, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to strengthen the power of Congress over the purse by deleting the provision on automatic appropriation for debt service on public debts.
