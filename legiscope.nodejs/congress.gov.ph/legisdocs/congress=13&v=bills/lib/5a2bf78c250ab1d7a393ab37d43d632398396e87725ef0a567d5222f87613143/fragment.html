HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02673
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING CAREER SERVICE PROFESSIONAL OR SUBPROFESSIONAL ELIGIBILITY TO THE PUNONG BARANGAY, THE SANGGUNIANG BARANGAY MEMBERS, THE BARANGAY TREASURER AND SECRETARY, THE SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN CHAIRMAN AND THE SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN MEMBERS
ABSTRACT : The bill extends career service professional and sub-professional eligibility to all punong barangay, sangguniang barangay members, barangay treasurers and secretaries, and to sangguniang kabataan chairmen and members who have served for at least three years.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : VICENCIO, ROMUALDO T.
DATE FILED : 2004-08-31
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
