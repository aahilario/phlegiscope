House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00934
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INSTITUTIONALIZING THE AIR NEGOTIATING COMMISSION OF THE PHILIPPINES AND DEFINING ITS POWERS, DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : "Philippine Air Negotiating Commission Act of 2007"
ABSTRACT : "Philippine Air Negotiating Commission Act of 2007". The bill creates the Air Negotiating Commission that shall represent the country in all negotiations or consultations with foreign civil aeronautical authorities leading to the conclusion of air services agreements or air tranportation agreements and succeeding negotiations/consultations regarding the renegotiation, renewal or extension of these air services agreements of air transport agreements.
