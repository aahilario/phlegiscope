HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01595
FULL TITLE : AN ACT GRANTING THE MAGNA CARTA FOR DAY CARE WORKERS, PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Magna Carta For Day Care Workers
ABSTRACT : Seeks to protect, promote and improve the social and economic status of day care workers by providing for their: a) salary; b) working hours; c) additional compensation and allowance; d) transfer and tenure; e) leave credit benefits; f) medical, insurance and retirement benefits; g) freedom to organize; and h) code of conduct.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ARROYO, IGNACIO T.
DATE FILED : 2007-07-31
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
