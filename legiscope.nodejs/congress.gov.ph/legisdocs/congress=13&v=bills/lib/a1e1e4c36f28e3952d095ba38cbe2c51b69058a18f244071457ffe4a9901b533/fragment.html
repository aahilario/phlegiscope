House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03359
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE COMMISSION OF ACTS 0F TORTURE AND OTHER CRUEL, INHUMAN AND DEGRADING TREATMENT 0R PUNISHMENTS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : Anti-Torture Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Anti-Torture Act of 2004." The bill defines the acts of torture, declares torture as a non-derogable right, grants every person arrested the right to physical and psychological examination and provides compensation to victims of torture.
