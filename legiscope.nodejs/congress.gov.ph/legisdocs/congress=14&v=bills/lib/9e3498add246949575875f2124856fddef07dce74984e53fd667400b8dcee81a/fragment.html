House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01775
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING A SPECIAL TASK FORCE ON GLOBAL WARMING UNDER THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES
SHORT TITLE : "Global Warming Task Force Act"
ABSTRACT : "Global Warming Task Force Act". The bill creates the Global Warming Task Force that shall conduct an urgent inquiry, in aid of legislation, on the alarming consequences of global warming.
