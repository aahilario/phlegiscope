HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00579
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM MAY 1 TO 7 OF EVERY YEAR AS THE NATIONAL OLDER WORKERS WEEK
ABSTRACT : In recognition of the contributions of workers aged 50 years and above, the bill seeks to declare May 1-7 of every year as the "National Older Workers Week".
