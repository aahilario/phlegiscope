House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01544
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING THE FIELD OF LAND TITLE INSURANCE AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 612, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE INSURANCE CODE OF 1978, AS AMENDED BY PRESIDENTIAL DECREES NOS. 1455 AND 1460 AND BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 874
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to create a land title insurance system that would protect owners of real estate against title defects, forgery, falsification, double titling and other similar acts.
