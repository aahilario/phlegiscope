HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01225
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE TEACHING OF ECOLOGY IN ALL ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY LEVELS, WHETHER PUBLIC OR PRIVATE OF THE PHILIPPINES AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The inclusion of ecology in the curriculum of elementary and secondary levels aims to inculcate in the youth ecological awareness and vigilance and make them understand more the dynamic relationships which support and maintain the bio-system.
