House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02086
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING SECTION 59, CHAPTER 7, SUBTITLE A, BOOK V OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 292, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE 'ADMINISTRATIVE CODE OF 1987'
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to clarify that the exemption to the rule on nepotism refers only to persons being appointed as teachers, physicians or those in confidential positions.
