House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01413
FULL TITLE : AN ACT DEFINING 'APPROVED, CERTIFIED AND ACCREDITED COURSES/SUBJECT' OPENED AND/OR OFFERED BY ACADEMIC INSTITUTIONS AND IMPOSING STIFFER PENALTY FOR VIOLATIONS THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : "Courses Accreditation Act of 2004."
ABSTRACT : "Courses Accreditation Act of 2004." The measure mandates that before any academic course is offered by a school, approval shall be obtained from proper government authorities.
