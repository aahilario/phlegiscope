HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02279
FULL TITLE : AN ACT AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 442, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE LABOR CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, AND PROVIDING FOR THE RATIONALIZATION OF WAGE-FIXING ALONG NATIONAL AND INDUSTRIAL LINES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes the abolition of the Regional Tripartite Wage and Productivity Boards and creates the Natonal Wage and Productivity Commission that shall determine and fix the national and industry minimum wage rates. It establishes Industry Tripartite Wage and Productivity Board in industries where it is deemed necessary.
