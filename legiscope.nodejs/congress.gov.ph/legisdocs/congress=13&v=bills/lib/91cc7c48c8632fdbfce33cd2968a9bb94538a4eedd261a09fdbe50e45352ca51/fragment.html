House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00268
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PENALIZING THE APPROPRIATION, ENCROACHMENT, REGISTRATION AND TITLING OF CREEKS, ESTEROS, STREAMS, CANALS, RIVER BANKS AND OTHER PUBLIC WATERWAYS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : Numerous statutes and rulings have been issued to the effect that canals, esteros, creeks and similar waterways are of public domain and as such, may not be acquired by prescription and therefore not registrable. However, despite these laws, encroachment, titling and/or registration of these waterways are prevalent. The bill seeks to penalize the appropriation, encroachment, titling, registration of creeks, esteros, streams, canals, river banks and other public waterways in order to prevent or curtain these anomalous acts.
