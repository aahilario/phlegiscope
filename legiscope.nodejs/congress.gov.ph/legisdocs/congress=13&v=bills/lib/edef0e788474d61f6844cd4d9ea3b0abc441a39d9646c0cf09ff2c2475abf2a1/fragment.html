House Bill/Resolution NO. HB04194
FULL TITLE : AN ACT IMPOSING STIFFER PENALTIES FOR THEFT AND PURCHASE OR PROCUREMENT OF STOLEN ELECTRICAL DEVICES AND IMPLEMENTS UTILIZED IN THE CONVEYANCE OF ELECTRICITY TO THE PUBLIC AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE ARTICLE 310 OF THE REVISED PENAL CODE
SHORT TITLE : Imposing Stiffer Penalties for Theft and Purchase or Procurement of Stolen Electrical Devices
ABSTRACT : The bill seeks to consider as qualified fencing the acts of buying, receiving, keeping, acquiring, selling or disposing of stolen electrical and telephone devices and implements used for the conveyance of electricity and communication services.
