HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02590
FULL TITLE : AN ACT INCREASING THE PENALTIES FOR THE PILFERAGE OF ELECTRICITY AND THEFT OF ELECTRIC POWER TRANSMISSION LINES AND MATERIALS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7832, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE "ANTI-ELECTRICITY AND ELECTRIC TRANSMISSION LINES/MATERIALS PILFERAGE ACT OF 1994"
ABSTRACT : The bill icnreases the penalties for: a) pilferage of electricity - imprisonment of reclusion temporal or a fine ranging from P100.000 to P200,000; b) theft of electric power transmission lines and materials - imprisonment of reclusion perpetua or a fine ranging from P300,000 to P500,000.
