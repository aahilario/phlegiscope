HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00139
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING BENEFITS TO DEPENDENTS OF PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS AMENDING FOR THESE PURPOSES REPUBLIC ACT NUMBER FORTY-SIX HUNDRED SEVENTY (R.A. NO. 4670) OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE MAGNA CARTA FOR PUBLIC SCHOOL TEACHERS, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
ABSTRACT : The bill proposes to grant additional benefits to public school teachers by giving their dependents the right to scholarship grants, free medical treatment and pension in case of disability of the teachers.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : ANGARA, JUAN EDGARDO M.
