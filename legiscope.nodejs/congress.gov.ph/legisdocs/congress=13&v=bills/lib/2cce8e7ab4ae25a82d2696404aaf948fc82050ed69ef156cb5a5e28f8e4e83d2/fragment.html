HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB03408
FULL TITLE : AN ACT MANDATING MOBILE PHONE SERVICE PROVIDERS TO GIVE THEIR SUBSCRIBERS THE OPTION TO RETAIN THEIR NUMBERS
SHORT TITLE : Telephone Portability Act of 2004
ABSTRACT : "Telephone Portability Act of 2004." The bill grants mobile phone subscribers the option to retain their numbers if and when they switch to another service provider.
PRINCIPAL AUTHOR/S : SANTIAGO, JOSEPH A.
DATE FILED : 2004-12-06
SIGNIFICANCE: NATIONAL
