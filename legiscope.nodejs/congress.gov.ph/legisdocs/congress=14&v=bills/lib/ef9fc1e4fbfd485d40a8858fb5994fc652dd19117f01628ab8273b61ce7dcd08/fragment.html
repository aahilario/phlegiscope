HOUSE BILL/RESOLUTION HISTORY
House Bill/Resolution NO. HB01458
FULL TITLE : AN ACT CREATING TRI-SERVICE ACADEMIES FOR THE MAJOR SERVICES COMMAND OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREOF
SHORT TITLE : "Tri-Service Academies Act"
ABSTRACT : "Tri-Service Academies Act". The bill proposes the creation of the Philippine Army Military Academy (PAMA), the Philippine Air Force Military Academy (PAFMA) and the Philippine Naval Military Academy (PNMA) to meet the requirement for officers of the Major Services Command of the Armed Forces of the Philippines.
