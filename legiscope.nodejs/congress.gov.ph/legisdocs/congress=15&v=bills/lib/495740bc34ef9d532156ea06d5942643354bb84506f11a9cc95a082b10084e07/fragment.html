House Bill/Resolution NO. HB00096
FULL TITLE : AN ACT PROVIDING FOR A NATIONAL POLICY ON REPRODUCTIVE HEALTH, RESPONSIBLE PARENTHOOD AND POPULATION AND DEVELOPMENT, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
SHORT TITLE : The Reproductive Health And Population And Development Act Of 2010
ABSTRACT : “The Reproductive Health and Population and Development Act of 2010.” Seeks to provide the enabling environment for couples and individuals to enjoy the basic right to decide freely and responsibly the number and spacing of their children and to have the information, education and access to safe, effective, affordable and acceptable methods of family planning of their choice.
