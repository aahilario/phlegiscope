House Bill/Resolution NO. HB02682
FULL TITLE : AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A PRIVACY PROTECTION COMMISSION
SHORT TITLE : "Privacy Protection Act"
ABSTRACT : The measure establishes the Primary Protection Commission that shall be tasked to: a) ensure that privacy rights of citizens regarding electronic date, and fair information practices and policies are not violated; and b) provide advisory guidance on matters related to electronic data storage, communications and usage, among others.
